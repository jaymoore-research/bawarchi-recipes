Coconut Rice

Ingredients

2 cups fresh coconut scraped
2 dry chillies
1 pinch hing powder
cashew nuts - 6 or 7
Few Curry leaves
1/2 tea spoon each of Urad daal & chana daal
2 table spoons of Ghee
1/2 tea spoon mustard seeds

Method

  1. In a pan take ghee and put in mustard seeds and let it splutter. Lower the flame and add cashews, urad daal, chana daal, chillies, curry leaves and hing powder. Let cashews and daals become light reddish in color.
  2. Now add scraped coconut and fry for 7 to 8 minutes.
  3. Boil the rice separately. Do not over boil the rice and let it cool down.
  4. Now add the above fried ingredients to the rice and add salt according to your taste and mix it very well. Coconut rice is ready to eat.
