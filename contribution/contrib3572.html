Masala Lobster

Ingredients

2 Lobsters
1 big Onion finely diced
1 big Onion chunky pieces
5 Garlic cloves (mashed)
1" Ginger (mashed)
2 Tomatoes (cut into slices)
2 Green Chillies
1 tsp Chilli powder (as you like)
1 tsp Turmeric powder
Chopped Coriander leaves
3 tbsp Oil (or according to your choice)
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Boil the Lobsters with 1/2 tsp salt, for about 12-15 minutes in medium flame.
  2. Clean the boiled Lobster and remove the unwanted shells.
  3. Cut the Lobster into 6 slices and drain the water. Marinate with 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder.
  4. Heat 2 tbsp oil in the pan. Add sliced Lobster and medium fry.
  5. Remove the Fried Lobster slices to another bowl.
  6. Heat 1 tbsp oil in the pan. Add garlic, ginger, onions and keep frying till they are brown. Add Turmeric powder, cinnamon powder & chilli powder. Stir well.
  7. Add Fried Lobster slices, chunky onion pieces and fry for couple of minutes. Add 1-cup water & Salt to taste. Let it boil for 3 to 4 minutes. Remove the pan from the flame.
  8. Garnish it with sliced tomato, green chillies & coriander leaves.
  9. Serve hot with any main course.
