Fresh Corn Pakodas

Ingredients

Fresh Corn Kernels - 2 cups
Ginger- 1 inch
Garlic - 5/6 cloves.
Green Chillies 2/3
Coriander leaves
Curry leaves
Coriander seeds 2 tsp
Cummin seeds -1 tsp
Red Chilli powder - 1 tsp
Turmeric powder - half tsp
Plain Flour - 2/3 tbsp
Oil to deep fry

Method

  1. Take a medium size bowl, mix fresh corn greated or kernels 2 cups. Grind with ginger, garlic, green chillies, coriander seeds & cummin seeds. Do not make a fine paste but grind roughly.
  2. Add red chilli powder, turmeric powder, plain flour, salt to taste, mix well.
  3. Heat oil, add 1 tbsp hot oil to the mixture, add coriander leaves & curry leaves finely chopped in the above mixture .
  4. Mix well and fry pakodas of medium shape till light brown.

Crispy crunchy snack is ready. Serve hot with Pudina Chutney or Tomato Sauce.
