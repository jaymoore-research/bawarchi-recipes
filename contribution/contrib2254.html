South Indian Vegetables

Raw Plantain Curry

Ingredients

2 Raw Plantains
1/4 Coconut
A few Green Chillies
1 Onion
Turmeric Powder
Salt to taste
1tbsp. Oil
Curry Leaves
1/2 tsp Mustard Seeds

Method:

  1. Cut the plantains into small bits with oily hands.
  2. Grind coconut, green chillies and onion with little water.
  3. Cook plaintain in little water adding salt and turmeric powder. After the plantains are cooked and cooled add them to the ground masala.
  4. Take oil in a pan put mustard seeds, and curry leaves and when they splutter add the masala plaintains and fry until the raw flavour is lost.
  5. Serve hot with Phulkas.

Basic Ingredients for a tasty spicy Rasam [Indian Soup]

Dhania [coriander seeds] 1 cup
Jeera [cummin seeds] 1/4 cup
Pepper corns 1/4 cup
Methi [fenugreek seeds] 1/4 cup
Mustard Seeds 1/8 cup
Asafoetida 1/2 tsp
Turmeric stick 1/2 tsp
Curry Leaves a small bunch.
Red Chillies 3/4 cup

Roast all the ingredients separately except red chillies. Roast red chillies with some oil and add to the others. When cooled powder them and store. Use whenever required.

Andhra Pesarittu:

Ingredients:

Whole Green Gram with skin [moong dhal] 1 heaped cup
Onion 1 [med. size]
Ginger 1/4"
Green Chillies 5
Raw Rice 2tbsp [heaped]
Salt to taste.

For topping:

Finely cut Onions 3/4 cup,
Finely cut Chillies 1tbsp
Cummin seeds1/2 tsp
Oil 1 tbsp

Method:

  1. Soak green gram and rice together for 8-10 hrs.
  2. Grind with onion ginger and chillies. Add salt at the end.
  3. Heat oil and fry cummin seeds, green chillies with diced onions for 2-3 min.
  4. Make dosas sprinkle little onion mixture and press.
  5. Fold and serve with Coconut Chutney. Do not turn over.
  6. Add Ghee or Butter on top before serving.
