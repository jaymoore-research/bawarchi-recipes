Cabbage Rice

Ingredients

Finely chopped Cabbage - 3 cups
Green peas (fresh or frozen) - 1/2 cup (optional)
Basmati Rice - 2 cups
Vangibath powder - 1 1/2 teaspoons
Turmeric powder - 1/4 teaspoon
Coriander leaves - 2 teaspoons
Salt - to taste
Ghee - 1 teaspoon
Grated coconut - 2 tablespoons (optional)

For seasoning:
Oil - 3 teaspoons
Mustard - 1 teaspoon
Urad dal - 1 teaspoon
Channa dal - 1 teaspoon
Curry leaves - 1 sprig

Method:

  1. Cook the rice so that each grain is separate. Spread on a plate to cool, sprinkling some oil or ghee.
  2. Wash chopped cabbage and peas. Drain water and keep aside. If you are using fresh peas, microwave it for 2 minutes or until it is semi-done.
  3. Heat oil in a wide kadai on low heat. Add the ingredients for seasoning. When the mustard starts to splutter, add the turmeric powder and immediately add the cabbage. Stir well.
  4. Let the cabbage cook on low heat for 3-5 minutes. Add the peas and salt. Mix well and let it cook on low heat for 5 more minutes, until the water evaporates.
  5. Now add the vangibath powder. Mix well and turn off the stove.
  6. Add cooked rice to it and mix well.
  7. Garnish with grated coconut and coriander leaves. Serve hot with raita and papad.
