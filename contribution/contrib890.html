Pineapple Gojju

Ingredients

20 oz canned pineapple in unsweetened syrup
Lemon sized tamarind or 2 tsps of dry tamarind Powder
Salt to taste
2 tsps brown cane sugar

For Ground paste :

2 tsps udad dal
2 tsps sesame seed (til)
2 tsps methi seeds
10 red chillies
1/2 cup grated coconut (dry will do)

For seasoning :

16 curry leaves
1/2 tsp mustard seeds
A pinch of asafoetida
1 tsp oil

Method :

  1. Soak the tamarind for half an hour in hot water. If using powder, you can skip soaking (And save 1/2 an hour).
  2. Add around 1/2 a cup water to the pulp and add the salt and pineapple pieces without the syrup and keep to boil.
  3. Boil for around 5-7 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile roast all the ingredients for the ground paste until light brown and make a thick paste.
  5. Add this paste to the boiling mixture of pineapple and tamarind.
  6. Cook for another 10 minutes until gravy is thick.
  7. Now add the sugar and mix well.
  8. Prepare the seasoning with the oil in a small dekchi.
  9. Add the mustard seeds. When the mustard seed splutter add curry leaves and hing.
  10. Add this to the gojju. Serve with rice and dal.
