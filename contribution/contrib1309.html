Eggplant fry

Ingredients

Eggplant-1 (medium)-cut it into 2 inch slices
Tomato-1 (medium) cut it into long slices
Onion-1 (medium) cut it into long slices
Oil-1 tbsp
Mustard seeds-1/2 tsp
Cumin seeds-1/2 tsp
Chilli power-1/2 tsp
Turmeric power-a pinch
Cumin power-1/2 tsp
Salt
Coconut -1 tbsp (optional)

Method:

  1. Pour the oil in a frying pan.
  2. Then add the mustard seeds and the cumin seeds.
  3. After spluttering add the onion slices and fry them for a while.
  4. Add the tomato now. Then add the eggplant slices. Add the salt and turmeric power now and fry them for sometime.
  5. Now add the chilli powder, coconut (optional).
  6. Finally add the cumin powder. Now the dish is ready.
