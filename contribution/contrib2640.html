Onion Dosas

Ingredients

Rava 1 cup (Upma Rava)
Maida 1/2 cup
Rice Flour 2 cups
Onions 1
Green Chillis 5-6
Coriander leaves
Salt

Method:

  1. Add rava, maida , rice flour, salt mix well.
  2. Grind green chillis with water and add it to the above.
  3. Add the finely chopped Onions.
  4. Add coriander leaves.
  5. Add more water to the above than required for dosa consistency.
  6. Heat a pan on a medium flame. preferably, the pan should be nonstick.
  7. Take a big spoon full of batter of the dosa and pour it I repeat just pour it on the pan you cannot spread it as ordinary dosas.
  8. Spread a spoon of ghee/oil over the dosa.
  9. Serve hot with any Chutney or no chutney.
