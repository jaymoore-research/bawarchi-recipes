Coconut Horse in Microwave

Ingredients

Coconut 1 freshly grated
Sugar 10 tbs or to taste
Instant coarse Rice Beverage 60 gms (available in the super market)
Dry Fruits 1/2 cup
Milk 1 cup

Method:

  1. Mix all the dry ingredients in a bowl thoroughly.
  2. Add one cup of milk and mix well.
  3. Put the bowl in microwave oven for 7 min. Mix again.
  4. Put the bowl back in the oven for 8 min.
  5. Take out and spread in the greased dish evenly.
  6. Let it cool. Enjoy after it is cooled.
