Quick Uthappam

Ingredients

1 cup Urad dal powder
1 cup fine Sooji or Semolina
2 cups Rice Sooji
1 cup Curds or Buttermilk
Salt 1 1/2 tsp
Hing Powder 1/4 tsp
Finely chopped Green Chillies as per taste or 1-2 medium hot flavour.
Finely chopped Onions 1/2 cup
chopped cabbage 1/4 cup
Chopped Coriander & Curry leaves 1 tablespoon
Baking Soda or Baking Powder 1/2 tsp.
Oil for shallow frying

Method :

  1. Mix all the ingredients except the oil well. Add enough water to make a `dropping dough' like cake or idli batter. Beat mixture well. Keep aside for 5- 10 minutes for it to soak in the moisture. Add more water if the dough is too thick.
  2. Heat a flat tawa or frying pan and spread a teaspoon of oil on it and let it remain on low heat for 3-4 minutes for the pan to absorb the oil so that the dough does not stick to it.
  3. Take a ladle of dough and spread it on one side of the pan into a 4" round. Similarly you can make two or three uthappams of this size in a large tawa. Now spoon a little oil all round the dough and a few drops on the top also. Cook covered over medium heat till the underside is golden brown. You can see the edges browning. Turn the uthappams to lightly brown the other side, and remove.
  4. Serve with Coconut Chutney, or Sambhar or the traditional Idli-Dosa Red Chutney Powder.
