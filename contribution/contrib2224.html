Besan Wali Capsicum

Ingredients

500 gms of Capsicum
2 Table spoon besan (gram floor)
Little ginger chopped
3-4 small green chiii finely chopped
Pinch of asafoetida (hing)
Corriander leaves finely chopped
Salt to taste
1 tea spoon ajwain
1/3 tea spoon turmeric powder
1/3 tea spoon corriander powder
1/3 tea spoon amchoor
1/3 teaspoon garam masala
1/3 teaspoon aniseed
Oil for frying

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a pan and add hing and ajwain.
  2. Mix chopped capsicum in pan and cook it till capsicum gets soft.
  3. Add salt, haldi, corriander powder, aniseed and let it cook for further 5 mins.
  4. Then add besan, garam masasla and amchoor.
  5. Mix it well and let it be on heat for about 5 mins.

Serve with chapatis.
