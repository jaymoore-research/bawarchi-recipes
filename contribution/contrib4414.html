Chatpatta Tomato Chutney

Ingredients

Tomatoes - 4 nos.
Green Chillies - 3-4
Panch Poran Masala - 1 tspn
Salt to taste
Jaggery - 1 tspn
Chilly Powder - 1/2 tspn
Oil-1 tspn

Method:

  1. Heat oil. Splutter the panch poran. Add green chillies. Add chopped tomatoes. Add salt, jaggery and chilly powder. Cook on a low heat till tomatoes are cooked.
  2. Serve hot with parathas.
