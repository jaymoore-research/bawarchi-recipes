Mango Rice

Ingredients

Raw Mango --- 2 (Peeled and grated)
Rice --- 2 Cups
Green Chillies --- 2
Red Chilli --- 1
Ginger --- Mustard seeds --- teaspoon
Chana dal --- teaspoon
Urad dal --- teaspoon
Oil --- 3 tablespoon
Turmeric powder--- teaspoon
Curry leaves --- Few sprigs
Cashew nuts
Groundnuts
Coriander leaves --- 1 bunch
Salt to taste

For the masala

Coriander seeds --- 1 teaspoon
Chana Dal --- 2 teaspoon
Red Chillies --- 2
Grated Coconut --- 2 teaspoon
Oil --- 1 teaspoon

Method:

  1. Take 1 spoon of oil in a pan add coriander seeds, chana dal and red chillies. Once they turn brown, remove from fire allow to cool and make a powder of it.
  2. Wash the rice add 4 cups of water and cook.
  3. Now take 3 tablespoons of oil in the pan add mustard seeds. Once they splutter, add Chana dal, Urad dal, Groundnuts, Cashew nuts, Red chilli, green chillies, ginger, Turmeric powder and lastly Curry leaves. Remove from fire and add it to the cooked rice.
  4. Squeeze out juice from the grated mango and add it to the rice. Add some salt to taste and mix thoroughly, taking care to see that the rice doesn't become too sticky and lumpy. Lastly add the powdered masala. If need be, add some oil to the rice while mixing to avoid lumpiness. For garnishing, use the squeezed out grated mango pieces and coriander leaves.
