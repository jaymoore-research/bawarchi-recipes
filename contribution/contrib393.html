This is for all you people who want to make a very authentic telugu style tomato chutney. It is very simple and very tasty.

Telugu Style Tomato (pacchadi) Chutney

Ingredients

Tomatoes - 1 1/2lbs - (6-8 Roma) or (4-5 big round Sandwich tomatoes)
Vegetable oil (1 1/2 tb Spoons)
Tamarind paste (1 tb spoon)
Haldi (Turmeric powder) - 1/2 tea spoon
Salt

For Seasoning (Tadkha/Popu/Tiragamota):
2 Tb spoons Oil
Hing (Asoefatida/ Inguva) - 1 generous Pinch
Chana dal 1 1/4 tb spoon
Urad dal 1 tb spoon
Red Chillies - 5 for medium spiciness (according your spice meter)
Green Chillies - 6-7 for medium spiciness (less or more according to your spiciness)
Rai seeds (Mustard seeds)
Green Coriander (at the end) - lots of it (1/8 bunch)
Garlic - Optional - 3 big cloves

Method

Heat oil in kadai and add cut tomatoes (Big pieces), once the tomatoes are well cooked and the juice of the tomatoes thickens in the pan add tamarind paste and haldi and salt cook for 10 more minutes on medium heat, now the tomato mixture should be pretty viscous and hot so cool it down by shutting of the stove and just letting it sit while you make the tadka.

Tadka heat oil add hing, add dals and saute them until they start turning golden then add red chillies and green chillies and mustard (rai seeds) in the last so that they splutter less add garlic cloves if you like- When you add green chillies to the tadka be careful they might split and explode on your face so that is why cover it with a lid (a see through lid is preferable).

let the tadka cool down for 5-10 mins, then grind the tadka first and add the tomato paste to the tadka paste and whip for few seconds, add coriander as it is on top or whip it together, adjust your salt according to taste. Any way this stuff tastes great with Idlis, Dosas or with just plain rice. Enjoy
