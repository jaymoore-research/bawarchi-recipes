Siru Payiru Kootu

Ingredients

1. Siru Payiru - 1/4 KG(soak for 1 hour)
2. Onion(Big) - chopped(cut into vertical pieces)
3. Tomato (medium) -2 cut in to pieces
4. Mustard- 1 spoon
5. Oil
6. Sambar Powder--- 3 spoons

For grinding:

Coconut - 8 -10 spoons
Red Chillies -- 5
Cumin seeds ---1 and 1/2 spoons
Ginger - 1 finely chopped

Method:

  1. Soak siru payiru in water for 1 hour. wash and drain the water.
  2. Heat kadai and pour some refined oil. Add mustard. When it splutters, add chopped onions, saute it till turns golden brow. Keep fire low. Then add the tomatoes cut into pieces to that and make it a gravy. Do not add water. If needed add a little more oil. Fry till the tomatoes turn watery. To this mixture add, 2- 3 spoons of sambar powder and salt as to your taste.
  3. In the meanwhile, boil the sirupayiru in water. (DO NOT MASH IT). When it is done, keep this aside and prepare for the griniding. Grind the ingredients as said and make it a fine paste with some water. Add this mixture to the gravy and mix it well. Fry for 1 or 2 minutes. now add the sirupayiru which you have cooked to this mixture and mix it well.

This kootu should be a bit thing and not so thick. So add a little water to it and mix it well. This goes well with Rice, Chapatis or Rotis.
