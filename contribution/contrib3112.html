Instant Dhokla

Ingredients

For the Dhokla Batter:-

1 cup Sooji (semolina)
1 1/2 cup Curds
A small piece of Ginger grated
Green Chillies cut fine (1 or 2 - your choice)
Less than 1/4 tsp of Soda Bicarbonate
2 tbsp Oil
2 tbsp Lemon Juice
Little Yellow Powder (turmeric)
Salt to taste

For Green Chutney:-

1 bunch of Coriander
2-3 Green Chillies
4-5 flakes Garlic
Tamarind water/Lime juice
Few roasted Peanuts (remove skin)
Salt to taste
Pinch of Sugar

For Tadka:-

2 tsp Mustard seeds (rai)
Few leaves of Curry Patta
Green Chillies (slit)

For Garnishing:-

Coriander Leaves (kotmir) - chopped fine

Method:-

  1. First of all keep water to boil in a large pan. Grease a thali preferably in a steel thali and keep aside.
  2. Now take all the ingredients of the dhokla batter and mix it nicely in a dish. Do not put the soda.
  3. Mix all the ingredients nicely till the mixture is smooth. Then mix the soda. Wait for five minutes. The batter will rise.
  4. Now put this mixture into the greased thali and steam for 10 minutes on high fire and then keep it for another 5 minutes on low fire. Now remove it and keep to cool.
  5. Lastly, cut into square or diamond shape. Then heat little oil and put mustard seeds till they crackle and also the green chillies and the curry patta and put on top of the dhokla. Its ready now.
  6. Garnish with kotmir and if you are fond of coconut, just grate a small piece of fresh coconut and garnish along with the kotmir.
  7. For the green chutney, just grind all the above mentioned ingredients for the chutney in a blender and your chutney is ready.
