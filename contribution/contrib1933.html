Kathrikka Masala Kozhambu

Ingredients

1. Eggplant - 1 big (approx 200 gm)
2. Chilli powder - 2 tsps
3. Dhania - 2 tsps
4. Cumin seeds - 1 tsp
5. Aniseed - 1 tsp
6. Channa Dhal - 1 tsp
7. Urud Dhal - 1/2 tsp
8. Red chilli - 1
9. Poppy seeds - 2 tsps
10. Coconut scrappings - 4 tsps
11. Cinnamon - 1 small stick
12. Clove - 1
13. Oil - to fry the eggplant
14. Tamarind paste - 1/2 tsp or lemon sized tamarind
15. Tomato - 1 medium sized cut into cubes

Method:

  1. Cut the eggplant into 2 inch long cubes.
  2. Wash them well in running water and dry them well using a paper towel.
  3. Pour some oil in a kadai and shallow fry the cut eggplant pieces till they turn golden brown.
  4. Meanwhile dry roast the ingredients 3 to 9 and then add the coconut scrappings and grind them well to a smooth paste.
  5. Heat a little oil in a vessel and add mustard, cinnamon, cloves and allow it to splutter.
  6. Add the tomatoes and fry them for a while
  7. Add some water and the tamarind paste (or the tamarind water if the paste is not used)
  8. Add the chilli powder and salt (according to taste) and the ground masala
  9. When the kozhambu starts to boil add the fried eggplant pieces and allow it boil for some more time.
  10. Garnish it with curry leaves and cilantro.

This can be served with Plain Hot Rice or Ghee Rice.
