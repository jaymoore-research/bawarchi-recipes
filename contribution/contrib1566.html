Cauliflower Manchurian

Ingredients

For Floret Paste:

1 Big Cauliflower divided into small florets
3/4 tbsp.Cornflour
2 tbsp. Maida
1 tsp. Sugar
1 tsp. Ajinomoto
1 tsp. Soya Sauce
2 cup Oil
Pinch of Khana Soda
Salt to taste
Water to make paste

For the Curry:

Chopped Coriander
8-10 flakes of Garlic
2 pcs of Ginger (1" each finely chopped)
5-6 Green Chilli chopped finely

For the Manchurian:

2 Tbsp. Cornflour
1/2 cup Water
1 Tbsp. Vinegar
1 Tsp. Ajinomoto
1 Tsp. Chilli sauce
1 Tbsp. Soya Sauce
1 Tbsp. Tomato Ketchup
Salt to taste

Method :

  1. Dip the cauliflower florets in the paste and deep fry.
  2. Keep them aside on a paper towel.
  3. Make a paste of cornflour with water, vinegar, ajinomoto, chilli sauce, soya sauce, salt, ketchup and put in fried curry paste.
  4. When it is little thick gravy, put fried cauliflower in it & put off the flame.
