Low Fat Dessert

Ingredients

Angel food cake - small
Jello instant pudding- small
Fresh strawberries- 30
Cool whip- whipped cream
A large dessert bowl

Method

  1. Make small pieces of the angel food cake and put these In the dessert bowl.
  2. After preparing the pudding pour it on these pieces.
  3. Then put another layer of the sliced angel food cake.
  4. On top of it pour some more pudding.
  5. Top it off with a layer of fresh sliced strawberries.
  6. Then repeat steps 3 and 4.
  7. Then put some whipped cream as a topping and decorate with some fresh strawberries.

Some suggestions
Strawberries can be used with vanilla flavoured jello pudding.
Slivered almonds can be used with chocolate flavoured pudding.
