Banana Flower Paatholi

Ingredients

Banana flower 1
Arhar Dal 1 small cup
Red chillies 5 or 6
Jeera half tea spoon
Salt as per taste
A pinch of turmeric
A pinch of Asafoetida (hing)
Oil

Method:

  1. Soak arhar daal and red chillies for half an hour.
  2. Peal the banana flower- In each layer you have many flowers take out the pistil and the small white cover at the base. As you remove the layers you will find no more pistils. The last bit inside can be chopped.
  3. Chop the peeled flowers and boil it in salt water for five minutes (or you may put it in the pressure cooker)
  4. Grind the soaked daal coarsely.
  5. In a thick bottom pan, put 2 table spoons of refined oil, and put jeera, Then add the coarse daal and red chilli mixture.
  6. Fry on medium flame continuously adding little oil whenever needed, so that it doesn't stick to the pan. When it is half fried, add the banana flower, and fry.At this point you may add salt, hing and turmeric. When it is dry and leaving the sides it is ready to be served.
  7. Serve hot with rice and rasam.

Instead of banana flower you may put beans, methi leaves, or even make paatholi without any veges. Try it out and let me know if you like it.
