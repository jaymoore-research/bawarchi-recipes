Pesarattu

Ingredients

Pesallu (green gram)-1cup(Moong beans)
Rice-1tbsp
Ginger-1 inchpiece
Green chillies-6 (or) according to taste
Onion-1 big (finely chopped)
Jeera-1tsp
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Soak green gram ,rice and jeera overnight in water.
  2. Drain the water and grind them with all the above ingrediends into a fine paste.
  3. Heat the tawa and spread 1-2 tbsps of the above dough as dosa.
  4. Pour one tsp of oil on it.
  5. Then spread some finely chopped onions on it.(optional)
  6. Fry on both sides or only oneside.
  7. Serve hot with Rava Upma (optional).

Note: Rice grains are used for crispiness.
