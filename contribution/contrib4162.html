Palak Makke Capsicum Wali

Ingredients

500 gm Palak washed repeatedly in water and cut in samll pieces
50 gms fresh Makka boiled on low heat for 10 minutes
100 gms Capsicum chopped in thin lengths
4/5 Tomatoes finely mashed or Tomato Puree
50 gms Green Chilli finely chopped
1 tsp Garam Masala
Onion Paste 50 gms or fresh onion mashed in food processor

Method:

  1. Boil palak in cooker or purchse canned palak (alternatively try sag also). If using freh palak then mash after boiling and drain excess water. Even after mashing separate water out.
  2. Put pan on fire and put cooking oil in it. Now put mashed onion (onion paste) and mashed tomato (tomato puree 150 gms) and keep frying for 10 minutes till the mixture leaves oil.
  3. Now put makka and cut capsicum and fry for 5 minutes by putting salt as per taste and garam masala followed by green chilli.
  4. Lastly put mashed palak or canned sag and keep on low fire for 3 minutes. Dish is ready for serving. Preferably serve with Tandoori Roti or Lachedar Paratha.

Serves: 3 persons
