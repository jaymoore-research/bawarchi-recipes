Topping Coconut Dosai

Ingredients

Raw rice- 300 gms
Blackgram dhal - 100 gms
Fenugreek seeds- 1 tablespoon
Grated coconut - 1 cup
Salt - to taste
Green chillies- 2
Tomato - 1
Yeast -1/4 teaspoon

Topping:

Capsicum-1, Carrot-1, Cabbage -2 or 3 Leaves, Cauliflower- 3 Buds, Onion - 2 or 3, Corriander Leaves , Cheese Layers or Grated Cheese.

Procedure:

  1. Wash the raw rice , black gram dhal and fenugreek seed well together.
  2. Then keep it in water for half-an-hour.Half an hour later take the mix out from the water and grind it in mixie well with less water.
  3. Then add coconut, greenchillies, salt and grind it again. After this, put the yeast in warm water and mix it well and add it to grinded mix.
  4. Do all this in the morning around 10 o'clock. In the evening cut all the vegetables into small pieces keep in microwave for 2 minutes then add salt to taste.(without water).
  5. Now mix all the vegetables well with a big spoon. Now if you see the rice mixture flour , it will be double in size. Mix it well.
  6. Then put a tawa on the stove and heat it. Spread 1 or 2 spoons of cooking oil on the tawa and spread the flour evenly but in a thick layer.
  7. Then spread the vegetables mix on the dosal and cover the lid for 2 to 3 minutes.
  8. Flame should be below medium but not low. If the dosai is cooked well then it take it out and spread the corriander leaves and cheese on the top. Serve it with Chillie or tomato sauce or cocunut chutney.

Chutney:
Grated coconut should be grinded with green chillies and salt according to the taste. Very little water should be added.
