Butter Chicken

Ingredients

1/2 Kg Boneless Chicken
2 medium size Onions
1/2 cup Cashewnut
6 Green Chillies
2 tsp Ginger/Garlic Paste
5 Tomato (made into puree)
2 tsp Jeera powder
1 tsp Red Chilli Powder
4 tbsp Butter
4 tbsp Fresh Methi Leaves
1 Bay Leaf
1 tsp Cardamom powder
1/2 tsp Garam Masala
2 tbsp Tomato Sauce
4 tbsp Milk Cream
A pinch of Red Colour
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Grind Onions, Cashewnut, Green chillies (make a fine paste)
  2. Cut Chicken into Small pieces and fry untill cooked.
  3. In a pan add Butter, bay leaf and methi leaves, saute until crisp. Then add jeera powder, red chilli powder, garam masala powder and then add the onion cashewnut paste. Saute for 2 min on slow gas.
  4. Add the tomato puree, tomato sauce, colour and salt. Cook for 5 min then add the cream and cardomom powder along with the fried chicken. Cook for 20 min on slow gas.
  5. Garnish with butter and coriander leaves.
  6. Serve with hot naans/roti/paratha/rice.

Preparation Time: 30 min
Serves: 4-6 people
