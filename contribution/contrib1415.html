Dahi Vada

Ingredients

Urad dal - 1 cup
Green chili - as per your taste
Salt - as per your requirement
Kadi patha - a couple of them finely chopped
Coriander leaves - finely chopped
Carrot - finely grated
Yogurt - 2 cups
Oil for frying the vada

Method :

  1. Soak the urad dal for an hour. If it is soaked for more time it would absorb lot of oil.
  2. Grind it into a fine paste along with green chili and salt.
  3. Add kadi patha and coriander leaves to it.
  4. Heat oil in a kadai.
  5. Take small balls of the dal and flatten it in your hand and make a small hole with your thumb and put it in the oil and deep fry until golden brown.
  6. once all the vada is done place them in a open tray and pour the yogurt on top of it and granish it with more coriander leaves and the finely grated carrot.
  7. Leave this for an hour and then have it. It it a good evening snack.
