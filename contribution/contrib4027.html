Channa Dal Paat Vadi

Ingredients

2 cups Channa dal.
Green Chillies.
Ginger.
Jeera.
Sabbakki Soppu. (Shepu in marathi) or coriander.
Garlic-2-3 flakes.
Salt to taste.
Haldi

Method:

  1. Soak channa dal for 2 hrs.
  2. Grind the dal with the other ingredients.
  3. Grind to a coarse paste & add Shepu or lots of coriander.
  4. Grease some oil or ghee on Dhokla stand or on a flat plate, and put the paste on the plates, steam it for 10-12 mins.
  5. Serve it hot with sauce or green chutney.
