Naroo

Ingredients

1 fresh Coconut Powdered (bit thick)
1 cup Sugar
1 small cardamom powder.

Method:

  1. Heat a pan or kadai, put all the ingredients together, and keep on frying till sugar and coconut are mixed well and are light brown in colour and till it sticking onto your finger.
  2. Remove from heat. And put it on a plate, let it cool for a minute and make lemon size ball with hand.

Don't cool it too much other wise the NAROO won't be made. You can keep it for 10-15 days. Happy cooking.
