Pepper Chicken

Ingredients

Chicken - 1 kg or 1/2 lbs
Onions - 2
Green Pepper - 4
Ginger paste - 1 tsp
Garlic paste - 1 tsp
Cloves 4-5
Cinnamon 4-5
Pepper powder 1/2-1 tsp to suit your taste,
Coriander leaves

Method:

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add green pepper, onion, ginger-garlic paste. Fry for sometime.
  2. Add chicken and salt and cook till it is 3/4th cooked, Then add powdered cloves and cinnamon pieces. Then add pepper powder to your taste.
  3. Garnish with chopped coriander. Hope everyone enjoys this.
