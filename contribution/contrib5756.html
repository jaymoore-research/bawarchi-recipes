Masala Kadubu

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients

For filling:

Channa dal:1 cup
Onion. 2
Green chillies.6
Ginger 1"
GarlicĀĀ 3 pieces
Cloves 2
Cinnamon 1/2"
Salt to taste.
Lemon juice 1 spoon
Sugar 1 sp
Fresh coconutĀpowder 1/2 cup.
Dill 1/4 bunch.
Coriander 1/4 bunch.

For dough:

Rice flour 2 cups
. Pinch of salt.

Method

  1. Soak dal for 2 hrs.
  2. Wash thoroughly and strain.
  3. First grind chillies, cloves, cinnamon, ginger and garlic.Ā
  4. ĀAdd soaked dal to it and grind coarsly. Don't make it nice.
  5. Add chopped onion,coriander and dill.
  6. Add grated coconut, lemon juice, salt and sugar.
  7. Mix everything well and keep aside.

For Dough:

  1. Keep water in a vessel and add a pinch of salt.
  2. When water boils add rice flour. cook well.
  3. Mix well with spoon without lumps.
  4. Take out small ball of cooked flour and roll intoĀ smallĀpuris.(if you have chappathi maker. press it. it will be easier).
  5. Then take the filling, put it in the center as much as it holds.
  6. Stick both the ends which looks semi circular in shape.(leaveĀ two ends little open).
  7. Take idli stand or colander. Arrange kadubu. steamĀ forĀabout 25-30 minutes.
  8. Serve hot with green chutney or any chutney of yourĀ choice.
