Sambharam

Ingredients

Curd: 1 cup
Water: 3 cups
Green chilles: 2-3 diced
Ginger: 1" (Grated)
Curry leaves: 10-12 leaves
Asofetida(Hing): 1 pinch
Salt: As per your taste

Method:

  1. Add all the ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend it for a minute.
  3. Pour the Sambharam to a tall glass.
  4. Add some ice cubes and decorate it with mint leaves.

Preparation time: 5 minutes
Serves: 2
