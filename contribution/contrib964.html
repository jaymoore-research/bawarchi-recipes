Mexican Cheese Shashlik

Step :1

1/2 kg Basmathi Rice

Step : 2

Marinate:

100 gms Onions
100 gms Capsicum
50 gms Tomatoes
10 Green Chillies
1 Cup Curds

Chop all these and marinate in curds for 1/2 hour. To above add 1/4 tsp of Pepper, 1/4 tsp Mustard Powder, 1 tbs Salad Oil or double refined oil and Salt to taste.

Step : 3

200 gms Onions
100 gms Capsicum
50 gms Garlic
10 Green Chillies

All these to be finely chopped and do not soak it in water.

Heat oil in a kadai. Fry whole garlic till brown. Then saute onions and then fry green chillies, capsicum and tomatoes till it is pulpy and then remove from flame.

Step : 4

Separately Heat 1 tbs of oil and fry 2 tbs of Maida till light brown. Then to that add 100 gms Butter, 25 gms Grated Cheese, 100 gms Fresh Cream, Salt to taste, Red Chilli Powder and a few drops of Tandoori Color and remove from flame.

Step : 5

Separately in 1 tbs of oil cook the marinated mixture till 3/4 done. Then mix this with Step 3 (finely chopped) curry. Then mix the Maida mixture Step 4, Tomato Sauce as required (about 1tsp) and then little sugar and continue cooking on low flame for few minutes.

For The Rice:

Boil rice and keep aside. Add 50 gms of Butter, 200 gms of Onions, Salt to taste, Pepper and finally Coriander Leaves to the Rice, mix all these and heat.

To Serve:

Press rice in to a katori and turn it on to a Plate. Make a hole in the center and put some Cheese Cubes. Next put some Curry over/on the rice. Sprinkle grated Cheese on the top and serve.
