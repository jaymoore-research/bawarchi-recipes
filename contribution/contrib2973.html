Stuffed SnakeGourd

Ingredients

Snake Gourd cut into 2 to 3inch circular pieces. 10-12 pieces
Grated Coconut 1/2
1 big Onion finely chopped
Cilantro finely chopped
Chilly Powder
Turmeric Powder
Gramflour(besan) 3tbsp
Salt to taste
Oil for cooking

Method:

  1. Mix all the above ingredients except the snake gourd pieces (once the snake gourd is cut the inner seeds and all can also be chopped finely and mixed with the other ingrdients). Stuff the gourd pieces.
  2. In a pan keep oil for heating and layer the pieces, sprinkle the leftover filling on top of the pieces. Reduce the heat to medium and cover the pan. After fifteen minutes flip the pieces over but check to see if they are golden brown.
  3. Repeat the process on the other side and check for doneness.
  4. Serve hot with Rice and Dal or Rasam. Enjoy!
