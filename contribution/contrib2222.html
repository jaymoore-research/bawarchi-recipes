Spicy Chicken Curry

Ingredients

1 chicken cut into small pieces
1 tbsp chilli powder
1 tbsp dhania powder
2 cups yogurt
5-6 elaichi,
4-5 lavanga,
half cinnamon stick (grinded into a mixture)
1 tbsp ginger and garlic paste
2 tsp soya sauce
1tsp chilli sauce
1 onion cut into small pieces
Salt

Method:

  1. Place 6 spoons of oil in a pan and add onions to it.
  2. Fry the onions till golden brown and add ginger, garlic paste.
  3. After a while, when the onions and the paste blend together, add chilli powder, salt, grinded masala mixture, yogurt, soya sauce, chilli sauce and let it simmer. Then add chicken.
  4. Let the chicken cook and in the end add dhania powder and remove it from the stove.
  5. Place some coriander leaves on top and it's ready to enjoy!
