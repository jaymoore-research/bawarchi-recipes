Red Pepper Chutney

Ingredients

2 Red Peppers (Red Capcicum - you can even try with other colours..)
2 red chillies
2 tbl spoons/ oil
1 1/2 tsp Chana Dal
1 tsp Urad Dal
Fist ful of peanuts
1/2 tsp mustard seeds
1/2 tsp methi seeds
Pinch of Asafatoeda
Pinch of turmeric
Salt
1 tsp lemon juice

Method

  1. Cut the peppers into small pieces
  2. Heat 1 1/2 tbl spoons of oil in a sauce pan add all the seeds and fry in order (so that all get fried evenly) - peanuts, chana dal, uraddal, methi seeds, red chillies, mustard seeds, asafatoeda and turmeric then add the cut pepper pieces to it and add salt and cover the pan with a lid and let it fry for 5 min (till the pepper is moderately soft)
  3. Let this cool down.
  4. When it is cool enough to be put in a blender, blend the mixture together with the lemon juice.

Serve with rice.
