Doodh-Peda

Ingredients

2 cups of Milk Powder
3 tbsp of unsalted butter
1/2 cup milk - sufficient enough to make a soft dough
1/2 cup sugar + 1/2 cup water
3 or 4 cardamom (powderedor crushed)
1 tbsp pista crushed

Method:

  1. Mix butter and milk powder, and add milk to make a soft dough. Refrigerate for 15 minutes. Take out the dough after 15 minutes and crumble it with your finger breaking all the lumps.
  2. Make sugar syrup of one strand and put the crumbled dough and keep stirring till it starts leaving the pan. Remove it when it starts leaving the pan and cool it lightly.
  3. Make small balls and flatten it into a shape of Peda and decorate it with cardamom and pista powder on the top.

Enjoy making prasad!!!
