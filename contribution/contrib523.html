Badam Halwa

Ingredients

Almonds - 1 cup
Sugar- 2 1/2 cups
Ghee- 2 cups
Kesari(yellow) - 1 pinch

Method :

  1. The previous night itself, soak 1 cup of almonds, in boiling water.
  2. The next morning peel off the skin of the almonds.
  3. Grind the almonds in a mixer/food processor till a fine paste-like consistency is obtained.
  4. Prepare sugar syrup by boiling the sugar with cup of water taken in a pan(kadai).
  5. When the sugar syrup starts boiling, add the almond paste and mix well.
  6. Mix the kesari powder with a little water and add to the boiling mixture.
  7. Immediately, mix in 1 cup of ghee.
  8. As the mixture starts becoming paste-like , gradually pour in spoonfuls from the remaining cup of ghee.
  9. When the mixture reaches a batter-like consistency, remove from the fire and serve piping hot, or refrigerate and serve chilled.
