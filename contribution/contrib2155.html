Chicken in Spicy Honey Sauce

Ingredients

1 Kg Chicken legs
3 tsp Ginger Garlic paste
3 tsp Green Chilli Paste
2 Limes
2 tbsp Soya Sauce
2 tbsp Worschestire Sauce
Salt as per taste
3 tbsp Honey
2 tbsp Port Wine
1 tsp Red Chilli Powder
2 tsp Butter

Method

For Marination

  1. Wash the chicken and make slits.
  2. Marinate the chicken in Ginger Garlic paste, green chilli paste, lime, soya suace, worschestire sauce and salt.
  3. Leave for at least 6 to 7 hours.
  4. Then roast or grill the chicken pieces.

For the Sauce

  1. In a pan heat butter. Add honey, port wine and the red chilli powder and cook for 4 to 5 minutes.
  2. Place the grilled chicken pieces on a plate and pour the sauce over the pieces.
  3. Serve hot.
