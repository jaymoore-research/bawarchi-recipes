Kesar Badam Kulfi

Ingredients

1 Ltr milk
1/2 tin Milkmaid
1 Cup sugar
1/2 cup Badam Pista blended to a coarse paste
1tbsp corn flour powder
Strings of Saffron
1 spoon powdered cardamom

Procedure

  1. Boil the milk and reduce to half the quantity.
  2. Make sure you heat in a saucepan with a heavy base.
  3. Then add sugar to it and stir well.
  4. Now add the milkmaid to the mixture and stir well.
  5. In a cup mix corn flour with plain cold milk and mix well.
  6. Add this mixture to the milk and keep stirring.
  7. Now add the paste of dry fruits. (To make a paste soak Badam and Pista in warm water for 20 minutes. Peel the skin and blend it into a coarse mixture)
  8. Finally add the powdered cardamoms and strings of saffron and keep stirring.
  9. Always stir the mixture and u will notice that slowly it will start thickening.
  10. Your delicious Kulfi is ready.
  11. Cool it and freeze in refrigerator for 2 to 3 hours.
  12. This can be served in small earthern pots.
  13. In that case you pour the ingredients and keep in fridge for another half an hour. Try it out.
