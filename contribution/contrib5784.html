Easy Chicken Fry

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients

Chicken quarters            6 pcs (cut into desired sizes)
Red chilly pd.                2 tblsp.
Salt                                to taste
Sugar                             a pinch
Hung curd                      3 tblsp
Garam masala pd         2tsp
Chopped coriander      1 bunch
Leaves Saffron threads             few
Egg                                2 nos.
Maida                            5tbsps

Method

  1. Mix all the ingredients except the egg and maida.
  2. Marinate the chicken in the mixture and keep in refrigerator for two hours.
  3. Take out chicken pieces from the batter and put on a rack and place in fridge for another one hour.
  4. Then put each piece in maida flour and coat in egg and again in maida .
  5. Dust the extra maida.
  6. Heat oil in a kadai and deep fry the pieces till golden brown.
  7. Drain on paper towel and serve hot with dhaniya chutney.
