Masala Eggplant

Ingredients

Small Brinjals (Indian store) 7 or 8
Dhalia Gram 1/2 cup
Garlic cloves 3 or 4
Cumin seeds 2 tsp
Shreded Coconut 1/4 cup
Red Chilli Powder 2 tbsp
Salt according to your taste
Oil 1 cup
Onion 2 large

For seasoning

Cumin seeds 1/2 tsp
Urad Dal 1 tsp
Mustard 1 tsp
One spring Curry Leaves

Method:

  1. Make dhalia gram into powder, add garlic cloves, cumin seeds, shreded coconut, chilli powder and salt. Make that into a fine powder.
  2. Wash brinjals and make slits like + from top view. Sprinkle the salt inside the slits.
  3. Heat the oil in the khadai, add brinjals and fry them till they are very soft. Remove the brinjals and strain them.
  4. Stuff the brinjals with the powder you made.
  5. In another khadai add the left over oil and heat it. Add cumin, mustard after spluttering add urad and curry leaves.
  6. Add chopped onions and fry them till they are very soft and turns into light brown. Add the stuffed brinjals to the onions and fry for 3 minutes and sprinkle the left over powder and fry for half minutes and remove it.

It goes well with plain hot rice. Enjoy it.
