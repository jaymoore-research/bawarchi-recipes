Potato Balls In Spinach Gravy

Ingredients

For the gravy:

1 bunch fresh Spinach leaves
2 tbsp Oil
2 Green Chillies.
Salt to taste

For the batter:

2 tbsp Maida Flour
Salt to taste
Water to make a paste.

For the potato balls:

2 Potatoes
1 Onion finely chopped
2 tbsp Cheese
2 Green Chillies cut finely
1/2 tsp Chilli powder (optional)
1/2 tsp Garam Masala (optional)
2 tsp Coriander
Salt to taste.
Oil for fying.

Method

For the spinach gravy:

  1. Wash the spinach leaves well until its well cleaned.
  2. Fry them in 1/2 tbsp oil with the green chillies until the spinach gets cooked well.
  3. Once cooled, grind them to a smooth paste in a mixer sprinkling some water.
  4. Fry again in the remaining oil till the spinach gets cooked well.

For the batter:

Make a paste (not so watery and not so firm like for poori) with maida, salt and water so that the potato balls can be dipped and covered with it.

For the potato balls:

  1. Boil the potatoes. Remove skin and mash the potatoes.
  2. Add the chopped onions, green chillies, coriander, cheese and salt.
  3. Add garam masala and chilli powder (optional). Mix well. The mix should be stiff. (If the mix is not stiff, it would break once it starts frying.)
  4. Make small balls with the mix.
  5. Dip the mix in the batter as prepared above and fry in oil till browned. Put it in a kitchen towel to drain out the excess oil. Just before serving put the balls in the spinach gravy.
  6. Serve hot with chappathi/nan.
