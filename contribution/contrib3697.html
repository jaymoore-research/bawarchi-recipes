Tasty Sambar

Ingredients

Toor dal (as required)-pressure cooked with haldi powder & a pinch of asafoetida)
Dried Red Chillies-5-6 (depending on your taste)
Onions-1 chopped
Garlic 15-20 cloves(peeled)
Mustard seeds
Curry Leaves
Tamarind- (lemon size, soak in water)

Method:

  1. Put the mustard seeds in a kadai & wait until it splutters.
  2. Then add onions fry them until light brown, then add curry leaves & dried chillies.
  3. Add the cloves, fry them in oil. Pour some water so that the redness in the chillies comes out.
  4. Add the presure cooked dal, cook until the garlic becomes soft.
  5. Add the tamarind juice, give 4-5 boils and its all done.
