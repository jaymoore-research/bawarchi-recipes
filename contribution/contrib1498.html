Carrot Peas Bhath

Ingredients

Raw Rice - 1 cup
Carrots - 4 (big)
Shelled Peas (1 cup)
Tomato - 1
Red Chillies - 2 or 3 (according to taste)
Green Chillies - 5 (according to desired taste)
Onions - 3
Coconut Scraping - half cup
Ghee - 2 tablespoons
Cashew nuts - 10 (optional)
Saffron powder - a pinch
Khus Khus - 1 tsp
Curry Leaves - few
Coriander Leaves - few
Salt to taste
lime - 1

Cut tomato into bits. Squeeze lime on it. Place this in a bowl and keep aside.

To be ground into a paste:

Fry the red chillies and khus khus in a small quantity of ghee. Grind this into a paste with coconut and onions.

Method :

  1. Boil 3 cups of water and add the rice. When it is three fourths cooked, add the salt. When cooked add a tbsp of ghee.
  2. Using the remaining ghee, fry the carrots, green chillies and peas. Fry also the cashew nuts. When half cooked, add the ground masala and half cup water. Stir well and keep on fire till the gravy thickens. Remove from fire, when the carrots and peas are well cooked.
  3. Add the rice. Mix the gravy well with the rice. Now add the tomato-lime mixture which has been kept aside.
  4. Add the curry and coriander leaves on top. When the rice is cooking, pinch of saffron may also be added.

Note: The lime may be added as per taste. Initially a bit of lime may be squeezed on the tomato. If not sufficient, some more lime may be added after the rice has been mixed with the carrot.

This rice has a new, and great taste. Happy Cooking.
