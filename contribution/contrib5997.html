Pasta Piselli

Ingredients

1 packet of frozen fresh peas OR 400 gms approx. of fresh green peas
1/4 cup olive oil
1Āsmall onion finely chopped.
1/4 cup of marinara sauce ( recipe of sauce given below)
1 pound of small shells OR Datali pasta ( You can even change the pasta as per your taste)

Serves: 4 Adults

Method

  1. Boil water in a small potĀandĀcook the peas until justĀdone.
  2. At the same time, heat olive oil in largeĀPan over Medium heat.
  3. Add onion and cook until golden brown.
  4. Add the marinara sauce and the cooked peas with 1/4 cup of the water (Use the same water that you used for cooking the peas).
  5. Season with salt & pepperĀand cook for another 15 minutes.
  6. In aĀlarge pot, bringĀwater to a boil.ĀĀAdd 1 tablespoon of salt and the pasta.
  7. Cook uncovered over high heat until done.
  8. Drain pasta, put back in pot add some of the sauce to the pot and mix it up.
  9. Dish out pasta and pour in remaining sauce over top.
  10. Serve hot.

Marinara Sauce

Ingredients

1/4 cup of olive oil
4 cloves of garlic sliced in half
1 35oz can ofĀĀtomatoes ( Italian tomatoes preferred)
3 basil leaves, washed, patted dry and chopped
1/2 teaspoon of oregano
salt & pepper to taste

Method

  1. Heat olive oil in large sauce pan.
  2. Turn heat to medium and cook garlic untilĀ it is soft andĀgolden brown.
  3. Crush the tomatoes or makeĀcoarse puree.
  4. Add about 15 to 20 oz ofĀwaterĀto theĀtomatoes.
  5. Add basil, oregano,Āpepper & salt to taste.
  6. Bring to a boil, then lower heat to a simmer and cook until thickened. It would take approximately 20 to 30 minutes.
