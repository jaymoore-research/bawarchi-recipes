Bread Bonda

Ingredients
3 medium sized potatos
2 medium sized onions
1 table spoon boiled green peas
2 green chilles
1 tea spoon of lemon juice
1/2 tea spoon sugar
1 tea spoon mustard seeds
1 tea spoon cumin seeds
1/2 tea spoon turmeric powder
1 table spoon oil
Salt to taste
Fresh coriander leaves
Fresh curry leaves
For covering bondas, 7 to 8 bread slices

Method

  1. Boil potatos, peel and mash with hands.
  2. Chop onion into thick pieces, green chilles and coriander.
  3. Heat oil, add mustard and cumin seeds.
  4. When it splutters, put turmeric powder and then add chopped onions, green chilles and curry leaves.
  5. Fry well till golden brown.
  6. Add boiled green peas, lemon juice, sugar, salt and coriander leaves, and fry for seconds.
  7. Add mashed potatos, mix well and fry on a low fire for a minute.
  8. Take from fire, mix well and cool it.
  9. Make small balls and keep it.
  10. Take one slice of bread, put it in water for a second and squeeze out the water nicely.
  11. Put one aloo ball in the bread and cover it nicely.
  12. Boil oil in a frying pan.
  13. Fry the bread stuffed with aloo until the bonda becomes golden brown.
  14. Serve hot with tomato ketchup.
