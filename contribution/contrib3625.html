Chicken Fry

Ingredients

1) Chicken 1/2 KG
2) Red Chili powder
3) Coriander leave
4) Mint Leave
5) Garlic
6) Salt
7) Vegetable oil

Procedure :

  1. Cook the cut chicken with salt in a cooker or can be done in open container, boil the oil in different pan or something (Non stick is preferred) add the cooked chicken and add red chili powder (2-3 spoons) and keep stirring it.
  2. Add garlic (1 tb spoon), less coriander leave and more (15-20 leaves).
  3. Keep adding the water which you got while cooking chicken at some interval as chicken dries. It really tastes good when chicken burns a little.

Duration : Just Half an hour.
