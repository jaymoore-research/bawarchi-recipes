Brinjal Chutney

Ingredients

Green brinjals 4
Green chillies 4
Sesame seeds 1 tspoon
Dhaniya seeds 1 tspoon
Jeera 1 tspoon
salt to taste
oil 1 table spoon
mustard seeds & curry leaves a few

Method:

  1. Wash and chop brinjals into medium sized pieces.
  2. Take a kadai, pour oil and when it is hot, put the green chillies and fry for a minute.
  3. Also fry dhaniya and jeera in a little oil.
  4. Grind to a paste, the fried green chillies, sesame seeds and the fried dhaniya and jeera. Keep aside.
  5. Now in the remaining oil, fry the brinjal pieces till they are soft.
  6. Grind this with some salt and the masala paste made out of dhaniya, jeera, sesame seeds and green chillies.
  7. Heat little oil in a pan and add mustard seeds and after it splutters, the curry leaves. Garnish the chutney with this.

Goes well with chapathi and rice.
