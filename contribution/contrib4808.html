Mint Rice

Ingredients

Rice 2 cups
Tomato 3
Green chilies 6
Onion 3
Mint 3 big bunches
Patta 1/2 inch
Cloves 2
Fennel Seeds 1 tsp.
Peas 100 GMS
Water 3-1/2 cups

Method:

  1. Wash rice and drain it.
  2. Fry rice slightly.
  3. Grind mint into a smooth paste.
  4. In a pressure cooker heat oil and then add onions, green chilies.
  5. Fry till the onions turn golden yellow.
  6. Then add peas, grinded mint, patta, cloves, fennel seeds, salt and then add required amount of water.
  7. When boiling add rice.
  8. Cool well and serve hot with raitha.
