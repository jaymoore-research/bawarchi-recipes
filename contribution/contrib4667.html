Okra Stuffing (Special)

Ingredients

1 LB Okra
1/4th cup Oil (total)
4 TBSP. Chana powder*

Seasoning Materials:

1 tsp. Raw Chana Dhal
1 Red Chilli (2 pieces)
1 tsp. Urad dhal
1/ tsp. Cumin seeds
1/2 tsp. Mustard seeds
4-5 Curry leaves

Method:

  1. Wash okras. Place them in a colander and drain water. Remove stems and slice them into four parts leaving 1/4 th at the end. Divide them in 3 portions and fry them in oil in skillet one portion after another until they are a little bit tender (Takes half an hour for frying). Keep the rest of the oil separately.
  2. Take 2 TBSP. oil from the total oil. Season it. Add fried okras to the seasoned material at low temperature. Sprinkle the chana flour* all over okras and keep for 2 minutes at low-medium heat. Serve these delicious okras with rice.
  3. Make powder from roasted chana dhal. Take 4 TBSP. powder out of this. Add 3/4 th tsp.salt, 1/2 tsp. hot chili powder, 1 tsp. cumin seeds and 4 garlic cloves (grind cumin seeds and garlic pieces before adding). If necessary, add salt and chili powder.
