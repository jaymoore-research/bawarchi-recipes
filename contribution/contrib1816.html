Kodubalay

Ingredients

All Purpose Flour 1 cup
Rice Flour 1 cup
Crisco Butter little
Salt and Chilli Powder accordingly
Oil for frying

Method:

  1. Mix all the above with little water and make it into a soft dough consistency not too soft.
  2. With this dough make small round like shapes you can make 10-15 at a time.
  3. Meanwhile heat oil in a pan and deep fry them until they are brown.

You can have this with hot coffee. Hope you will like it.
