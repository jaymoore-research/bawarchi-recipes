Bitter Gourd Curry

Ingredients

Bitter Guords-2
Onion 1 big finely chopped
Sesame powder (Til)- 4tsp (roasted)
Ginger Garlic paste
Oil
Green Chillies-5
Salt to taste
Mustard seeds
Jeera
Jaggery or sugar - 2 tsp

Method:

  1. Cut the Bitter guord into long thin pieces, apply salt and keep aside for half an hour.
  2. Drain out excess water from the Bitter guord.
  3. Put oil in the pan tamper with mustard seeds, jeera seeds, ginger garlic paste then add bitter guord pieces and fry.
  4. Add onion, salt, jaggery to it.
  5. Close with the lid and cook till the onions and the bitter guord is soft, now add sesame powder to it, leave it for a minute, remove from fire.

Enjoy this curry with rice and ghee!
