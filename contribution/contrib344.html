Chicken Maharaja

Ingredients

1 Small chicken (400 Grms ),
150 gm fresh cream ,
200 gm fresh tomoto puree,
50 gm butter,
5 gm kasturi methi,
1 tsp garam - masala,
salt to taste,
red food colouring,
Curd,
Garlic Paste, Ginger paste

Method :

  1. Wash & clean chicken prepare a marinade of curd, ginger and Garlic paste, garam masala & a pinch of red colouring marinate the chicken for at least one hour.
  2. Grill the chicken on charol till it is cooked then debone the whole chicken add fresh tomato puree to the frying pan along with methi, chopped corainder leaves, chopped green-chillies salt and butter cook the tomato puree for at least five minutes
  3. Now add the deboned chicken and the cream, mix gently on a slow fire.
