Low calorie Mysore Pak

Ingredients

1 cup ladoo Besan
2 cups Sugar
1/2 cup Ghee
1/2 cup Water

Method:

  1. Mix the besan and sugar in water till mixture resembles dosa batter. Stir in a large wok on low heat.
  2. Add the ghee, stirring all the while, a tablespoonful at a time.
  3. When the mixture turns frothy, and starts to turn at the end of the spoon, pour the mixture on a greased plate.
  4. Gently shake the greased plate till the mixture settles uniformly.
  5. Cut into pieces (approximately 20 1-inch square pieces).

Time taken: Approx. 20 minutes
