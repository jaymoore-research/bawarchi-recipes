Garlic Chutney

Ingredients

1 cup grated Coconut (fresh or dry)
5 Red Chillies
10 cloves of Gralic
1/2 table spoon of Jeera
5 table spoon Toor Dal
2 fresh Tomatoes
1/2 table spoon Tamarind Paste
5 table spoon of cooking Oil
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Heat oil in a small pan.
  2. Roast garlic, jeera, red chillies, toor dal one at a time.
  3. Cut tomatoes into small pieces and cook for 2 minutes in the above pan after roasting all the ingredients.
  4. Mix coconut, roasted stuff, tomatoes, tamarind and salt to taste in a blender with small amount of water so that chutney can be a fine thick blend.
  5. Enjoy with Yogurt Rice, Chapati, Dosa and Idli.
