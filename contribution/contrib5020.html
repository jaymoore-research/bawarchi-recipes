Dahi Wada

Ingredients

Urad dal (washed) : 1 cup
Ginger: 1 inch
Green chilli : 2 nos.
Oil/Ghee: to fry
Curd/Yoghurt: 4 cup
Milk: 1 cup
Sugar: 1 tsp.
Salt: to taste
Black salt: 1/2 tsp.
Pepper: 1 pint
Zeera powder: 1/2 tsp.
Chilli powder: 1 pint

Method:

  1. Wash dal and soak in 3cup of water for 1-2hrs.
  2. Drain water.
  3. Grind dal with ginger and green chilli with adding sufficient amount of water to make a thick batter. (don't keep batter for more than 20 mins)
  4. Heat the oil, and keep the gas on medium-high.
  5. Make balls and fry.
  6. Drain the extra oil on soak paper.
  7. Mix milk to the curd and stir it well.
  8. Add all the ingredients and soak fried wada into the mixture for at least half an hour.
  9. Garnish with coriander leaves, and serve with tamarind chutney.
