Ingredients

1 cup : Green gram dhal
2 cups : Sugar
1 cup : Ghee
1 cup : Milk
1 tbs : All purpose flour (golden fried in ghee)
10 Cashews (golden fried in ghee)
1 pinch of Nutmug

Method:

  1. Pressure cook the dhal in 1:1 (milk:water). Cool it. Then smash the dhal.
  2. In tawa, put sugar with little amount of water and keep it till it comes to the texture of 2 thin-lines. Add the mashed dhal and stir it continuosly. Simultaneously, add ghee while stirring. When it comes like dough (that is in the position of not sticking with the vessel), add the fried flour, fried cashews and the pinch of nutmug powder. We can have it just like this or can make pieces by putting it in a greased plate.
