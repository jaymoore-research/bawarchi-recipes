Hariyali Chawal

Ingredients

COOKEDBasmati Rice - 3cups
Carrot - 1
French beansá- 10
Capsicum small - 1
Shelled Green peas - 1/2 cup
Spring onion - 1bunch
Green chillies - 2 or 3nos (depending on how much spicy you want this to be)
Ginger - 1inch piece
Garlic {optional}-4 pods
Cut Coriander leaves - 3/4th cup
Salt - to taste
Oilá- 2 table spoon

Method

á

  1. Cut carrots, beansá, capsicum, spring onionáinto small pieces. á
  2. Grindá greená chillies, ginger, garlic and coriander leaves to a paste {not very smooth} á
  3. Heat oil in a kadai and fryácut spring onion bulbs {white potion of the spring onion} for a minute á
  4. Add the ground paste to the onions and fry for a minute.
  5. Also add the cut vegetables and cook for 5 minutes after adding salt. á
  6. Add the cooked basmati rice to the cooked vegetable and toss well. á
  7. Lastly, add the green stalks of spring onion also and sautÚ® for a while. á
  8. The beautiful looking aromatic rice is ready to be served. á

Believe me, it is yummy!!!!!! and children will enjoy the rice very much.

P.S: Remember to reduce the salt if you have already added salt with the rice while cooking.

This tastes very good with any raita.
