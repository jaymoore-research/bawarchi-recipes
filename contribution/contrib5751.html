Jhatpat Snack

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients

All you need is:
Slices of white/wheat bread
Salsa (you get them in regular grocery store..if you want to make it at home, see the recipe below)
Shredded cheese (mozzarella, cheddar or combo)
1 small onion Cilantro and sour cream for garnish

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 400oF
  2. Slice off the edges of the bread and cut 4 squares of each slice.
  3. Arrange them on a cookie sheet or any other oven-proof tray
  4. With a small spoon, put little bit of salsa on each piece.
  5. Sprinkle shredded cheese
  6. Put in the oven for about 12-15 min or till the cheese melts and brownsĀa bit..the bread should have also become toasty..
  7. Take out and garnish with finely chopped onion, cilantro and a dash of sour cream(optional)
  8. Enjoy with wine, beer or soda.

Recipe of salsa:

2 tomatoes
1 onion
2 cloves garlic
5-6 green chillis
Cilantro
Salt

  1. Mix everything in the blender and store in refrigerator for upto a week.
