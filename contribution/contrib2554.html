Cabbage Manchurian

Ingredients

4 cups grated Cabbage
1 cup all purpose flour (maida)
Salt to taste
2 tspn. Garam Masala
Pinch of Turmeric
Oil for frying

Method:

  1. Take a wide mouthed container and put grated cabbage, maida, salt , turmeric and garam masala and mix well. Use as little water as possible.
  2. The dough should be of consistency for making small balls.
  3. Convert the dough into small balls
  4. Deep fry them in the oil till they become golden brown
  5. Serve them hot with Tomato Ketchup.
