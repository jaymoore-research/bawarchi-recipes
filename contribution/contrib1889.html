Bitter Gourd Curry

Ingredients

Bitter Gourd (Karela)- 3-4 (Big)
Moong Dal - A Handful
Coconut - Half Shell Green Chillies - 3 To 4 Accdg To Taste
Red Chilli Powder - 1 Tsp
Tumeric Powder - 1 Tsp
Sugar Or Jaggery - 1 Tsp (Accdg To Taste)
Tamarind - A Small Ball
Hing - A Pinch
Mustard Seeds - 1 Tsp
Urad Dal - 1 Tsp
Salt As Per Taste

Method

  1. Dry roast the moong dal.till the raw smell disappears.
  2. Cut the bitter gourd in small bits. Keep it in the pressure cooker along with the roasted moong dal, salt, tumeric powder, chilli powder, hing and the ball of tamarind.
  3. Pressure cook it. Once it is done. Remove all the water.
  4. In another vessel transfer the contents and extract juice from the tamarind which had been kept with the bitter gourd.
  5. Dry grind the coconut with the green chillies. In a kadai. Splutter mustard seeds. Add urad dal and fry. Now add the cooked bitter gourd alongwith the tamarind extract. Add sugar and let it cook further till all the water is removed. In the end add the ground coconut.
