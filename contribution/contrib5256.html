Cracked Wheat Khichdi

Makes : 2

Ingredients

2 cup wheat cracked
1/2 cup corn kernels
1/2 cup green peas
1/2 cup French beans finely chopped
1/2 cup carrot diced
peanuts (optional)
garlic paste
ginger chilli paste
salt
red chilli powder
dhaniya powder
turmeric
a pinch of hing
1 tea spoon ghee or oil

Method:

  1. First in rice cooker vessel add all the ingredients & add 3 glass of water.
  2. Mix well.
  3. Allow to cook.
  4. Serve hot.

Variation:
You make this khichdi in microwave also. follow the same procedure. cook for 15-20 min. stirring occasionally.
You can also add cauliflower if you like.
You can also make this khichdi with baingan & potato.
