Hi guys .....this is Beena Shubham Chopade. I shall be sharing a recipe unknown to me until recently. I learnt it from my mom in law. These are like sweet chapaties meant to last long and to be had with groundnut chutney. They are fantastic and I thank my mother in law for this wonderful dish.

Email: beenasc@yahoo.com

Dashmi

Ingredients

2 cup Wheat flour
1 cup milk
1 cup sugar
4 tbsp oil/ghee
Salt for taste

Method:

  1. Add the entire oil to the flour and rub it on the flour for 1 min.
  2. To the milk add entire sugar and stir.
  3. Entire sugar does not dissolve.....its a super saturated solution.
  4. Add this to the flour along with salt and knead as U knead for making chapaties.
  5. Make small balls and roll as U would for chapaties.
  6. Bake it on the pan....just as for chapaties.
