Sayang's Mutton Curry

Ingredients

500 gm boneless mutton
12 - 15Ābig green chillies (add more or less depends on your taste)
4 cloves of garlic (bruised)
4 slices of ginger (bruised)
1 stalk of lemongrass (cut into 2 and bruised)
Mutton curry powder (i prefer to use fish curry powder because its more spicy)
1 tbsp turmeric
Oil for cooking
Salt to taste
Aji-no-moto or MSG
Water

Method

  1. Cut mutton into small pieces and wash. Slice chillies about 1cm thick (do not remove the seeds).
  2. TheĀ curry powder must be mix with the turmeric powder with a little bit ofĀwater to make aĀpaste.Ā
  3. Heat the pan and add oil.
  4. When its hot enough add the garlic, lemongrass and ginger and fry until they turn a little brown and fragrant.
  5. Add curry paste and fry it for about 1 minute.
  6. Then add the green chillies, stir it and leave it for a while until the curry paste coated the chillies.
  7. After that add the boneless mutton and stir it for a couple of minutes.
  8. For the first few minutes do not add water, let the mutton cook in its own juicesĀandĀlet theĀpasteĀabsorb the meat.
  9. When the mutton gets dry addĀwater and leave it covered for a while.
  10. If you are using pressure cooker the meat should be tender and cooked after 4 wistles.Ā
  11. Season it with salt and msg according to your taste.
  12. Add water everytime it gets dry, stir it aroundĀand leave it until its cooked.
  13. Serve it with hot rice, chapatis, etc.
