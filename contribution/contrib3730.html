Doodhi No Moothio

Ingredients

1 medium size Bottle Guard
1 1/2 cup Wheat flour
1 tsp Chilli powder
1 tsp Turmeric
2 pinch Asafoetida
Salt to taste
1 tbsp Oil
1/2 tsp Mustard seeds
1/2 tsp Cumin seeds

Method:

  1. Grate the bottle guard, drain water into a vessel and keep aside.
  2. Mix wheat flour with chilli powder, turmeric, asafoetida and salt
  3. Add the grated bottle guard into it. Mix well while adding the remaining water till it becomes soft dough.
  4. On a greased plate flatten the dough and switch it into pressure cooker for 20 minutes. Donot put whistle.
  5. After it is pressure cooked well cut the dough into small pieces.
  6. Take a frying pan. Put oil, mustard seeds, cumin seeds When it splutters add the pieces of dough and mix well for 5 minutes.
  7. Take out from the fire and now it is ready to serve with sause or pickle.

This dish is basically meant for breakfast. It is filling too. I hope you will enjoy it. Write to me your comments to my email address mentioned above.

Note: Quantity of Chilli powder can be increased as per your requirements.
