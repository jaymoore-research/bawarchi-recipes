Stuffed Bitter Gourd

Ingredients

Bittergourd 8
Vegetable Oil 4 tbsps

For stuffing

Boiled Potatoes (medium) 4
Coriander leaves
Anar dana 1 tbsp
Turmeric powder 1/2 tsp
Salt 1/2 tsp
Red Chilli powder 1/2 tsp

Method:

  1. Boil bitter gourd for five minutes, slit them lengthwise.
  2. Mash the potatoes and mix all the ingredients with spoon.
  3. Stuff the bitter gourds and fry them in a pan on low flame. They are ready to be served with hot chapati.
