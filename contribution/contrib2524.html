Semya (Vermicelli) Payasam

Ingredients

Milk 1 litre
Sugar 1/2 Kg
Vermicelli (Semya) 1/2 Kg
Ghee 1/4 Kg
Cashewnut 25 nos
Cardamom 5 nos
Dry Grapes 25 nos
Water 2 cups

Method:

  1. Pour 50 gm of Ghee in a frying pan. Fry Vermicelli in ghee until it becomes brown color. Keep the vermicelli in a separate plate.
  2. Then fry cashewnut, cardamom and dry grapes in ghee for a little while. After frying, add water and vermicelli to this mixture and let it to boil. After 10 minutes add milk. Wait until milk is boiled.
  3. Add ghee and sugar to this mixture. Take after 2 minutes.
  4. Serve in cups.

This is a nice sweet after tiffin/lunch. If you want this to be a bit thick add more vermicelli. This will stay for 3 days in refrigerator. Whenever you want, boil and eat.
