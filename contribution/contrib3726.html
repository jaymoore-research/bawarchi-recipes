Kathirikai (Eggplant) Chutney

Ingredients

Brinjal - 2 big one [please make sure that you get brinjal from Indian grocery store that too Indian eggplant]
Coconut - 3 to 4 small pieces
Onions - 1/2
Tomato - 2 in no small one
Red Chilly - 2 [according your spice you can add more]
Urad dhal - 1 tbsp
Tamarind - little bit [ if you use paste put 1/4 th in spoon]
Curry leaves - 4 to 5 leaves.

For Seasoning:

Mustard seed - little bit
Urad dhal - 1 tsp
Curry leaves - 4 to 5 leaves

Method:

  1. First you should remove the cap of the brinjal and put it on a stove top in a medium flame make them cook while turning sides and top and bottom , brinjal shold be turned into a black and it will get shrink. Then you remove from the fire. Remove the black skin, wash it and keep it separately.
  2. Secondly put the kadai in a fire pour little oil and then put some urad dhal and the urad turns red add red chilly and then add the coconut pieces fry till the coconut turns to brown. Remove from the fire and keep it separately once they done.
  3. Thirdly put some oil in a kadai and add the chopped onions and fry till they turn into brown, then keep it separately. Then add some oil and put the tomatoes and fry till the skin shrink. After this put all these cooked brinjal, coconut pieces, red chillies, onions, tomatoes, then salt tamarind... all these fried ones should be in a mixer and grind them to a paste. Then atlast seasoned with mustard seeds, urad dhal and curry leaves.
  4. Eat with dosa it will be really fantastic.
