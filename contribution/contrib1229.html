Keerai Kootu

Ingredients

1 bunch spinach
1 small onion
2 tbsp coconut
2-3 green chillies
1/2 spoon tamarind
Required amount of salt and oil

Method:

  1. Heat oil in a pan, then add spinach and fry for few minutes. Till the spinach shrinks.
  2. Add coconut, chillies and onion fry for another two to three minutes till the raw smell goes.
  3. Add all of these along with tamarind and salt to the blender and grind to a smooth paste.
  4. Again bring it to boil. Serve hot with rice.
