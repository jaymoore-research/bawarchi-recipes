Pudina Chutney

Ingredients

2 1/2 cups cleaned Coriander leaves
1 1/2 cups Mint (pudina) leaves
6 to 8 Green Chillies
1 tsp Salt
A small piece of Tamarind or little Lemon juice to taste.

Method :

  1. In the jar, add the cleaned leaves, green chillies, salt and tamarind (if used). Add one tablespoon water. Run mixer at whipper speed for 30 second, switch off.
  2. Push the chutney down the sides of jar and run mixer at speed 1 for 60 seconds, adding a little water if required for proper circulation.
  3. Add lemon juice to taste if required.
  4. A little quantity of fried channa dal or cashewnut can be added before grinding.
  5. If required, fry a little mustard, channa dal, curry patta leaves and asafoetida in ghee as topping on chutney.
  6. Serve with idli and dosas
