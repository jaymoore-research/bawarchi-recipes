Jackfruit Aloo Masala Curry

Ingredients

Raw jackfruit (small) - One
or
young jackfruit can - one
potato- one medium sized
onion- 1 medium sized
garlic paste- 1 tea spoon
ginger paste- 1 1/2 tea spoon
cloves- 4 no's
green chillie- 2 no's
tomato puree- 2 table spoon
or half medium sized tomato cut into small cubes
oil - 2 tablespoon
salt to taste
jeera powder- 1 teaspoon
meat powder- 1/2 teaspoon (optional)
or curry powder- 1/2 teaspoon
Turmeric powder- small pinch
coriander leaves for garnishing

Method

  1. Peel the jackfruit & cut into long thin slices. If you are using jackfruit can then open the can & wash the jackfruits pieces thoroughly.
  2. Peel potato & cut into long cube shaped.
  3. Cut the onion into thin slices & grind to make a thick paste.
  4. Heat oil in a pan & fry cut potatoes till light golen brown. Keep it aside.
  5. Then to the remaining oil add cloves & onion paste. Fry till onion becomes light brown. Add chillies, turmeric powder & thin sliced jackfruit.
  6. Mix the jackfruit well with the masala & stir in between for 5 - 7 mins.
  7. Then add fried potatoes to it & mix well for another 3 mins. & Add tomato puree or cube shaped tomatoes, meat masala or curry powder
  8. Add water & salt as desired.
  9. Keep in fire till jackfruit & potatoes becomes tender & gravy becomes slightly thick. You can pressure cook as well all above upto one whistle.
  10. After gravy becomes thick, take off from fire & garnish with coriander leaves.

Serve hot with plain or steamed rice & have a bon apetit..

Preparation time - 15 mins
Cooking time - 10 mins
Serves 4
