Paneer-Radish Paratha

Ingredients

Grated Radish-1 cup.
Grated Paneer- 1/2 cup
Wheat flour- 1 1/2 cup
Red Chilli powder- 1 tbspn
Salt -as per taste
Ajwain or Sounf-1/2 tspn
Water- to make dough
Ghee- for roasting parathas

Method:

  1. First make dough out of wheat flour and keep it aside (consistency as Chapathi dough) for 3 to 4 hours.
  2. Remove water from the grated Radish. Mix it with grated paneer. Add salt, chilli powder and ajwain. Mix well.
  3. Now make small balls out of dough (medium size). Take each ball and roll it into a round roti using rolling pin. ( Roti should not be rolled very thin or very thick. It should be medium thick)
  4. Add the mixture (made out of Radish, paneer, red chilli powder etc.,) to the rolled roti.
  5. Now fold the roti into a half moon shape and press the edges. Now roll it again using the rolling pin.
  6. Heat tawa under low flame and roast both side of the half moon shaped, stuffed roti using ghee until golden brown.
  7. Serve hot parathas with Curds or Yogurt.
