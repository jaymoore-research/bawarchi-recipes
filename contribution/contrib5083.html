Cabbage Thoran

Ingredients

Cabbage 1/4 kg chopped
Onion 1 finely chopped
Coconut freshly grated 1/4 cup
Turmeric powder 1/2 tsp
Green chillies 2-3
Mustard seeds 1 tsp
Urad dal 1 tsp
Curry leaves few
Salt to taste
Oil 1 tsp

Method:

  1. Mix Cabbage,chopped green chillies & onion well with hand.
  2. Boil the mixture with turmeric powder & salt.
  3. In the mean time grind to paste (coarsely) coconut.
  4. Once the vegetables are boiled, heat oil in pan, add mustard seeds, urad dal, curry leaves & allow it to splutter.
  5. Then pour the above over the boiled vegetables& mix it well. Fry it for a minute or so then add the paste & mix well.
  6. Cook it for some time say 2-3 mts. Serve hot with hot rice & sambhar.
