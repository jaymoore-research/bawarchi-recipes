Kothamalli Rice

Ingredients

Coriander leaves - 1 bunch (cleaned n chopped)
Basmati rice - 2 cups
green chillies - 2-3
small onions - 4-5
garlic - 1 pod (optional)
tamarind pulp - 1 tsp
jeera - 1tsp
salt to taste
sunflower or any cooking oil - 1 tbsp
gingelly oil - 1/2 tbsp

Method:

  1. Soak rice in water for 15 minutes.
  2. Cook with 4 cups water al dente.
  3. Meanwhile grind together-coriander, green chilli, onion, garlic , salt and tamarind pulp.
  4. Heat sunflower oil.splutter jeera seeds.
  5. Add the ground mixture and saute till the raw smell starts disappearing.
  6. When a nice aroma starts then switch off the stove.
  7. In a wide plate spread the rice, add gingelly oil.
  8. Then add the ground mixture and mix uniformly.

Garnishing suggested: 1 tbsp fresh coriander chopped or 1 tbsp carrot grated
