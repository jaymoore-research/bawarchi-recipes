Milk Sweet With Ricotta Cheese

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients 1 cup ricotta cheese (Fat free or whole milk) (You can use the ricotta cheese container itself)
1 cup Milk Powder
1 cup Sugar

Method

  1. Mix all together. Brush the microwave vessel with ghee.
  2. Pour the mixture in the vessel and keep it for 5 mints in the microwave and take it out and stir it and keep again in the microwave.Ā
  3. Again set it for 5 min. and it will come to a consistency of making burfies.
  4. Brush the plate with ghee and pour it in the plate and let it free for 2 min and make burfi pieces.
  5. And allow it to cool and then take the milk sweet out of the plate.
  6. You can decorate it with saffron, nuts etc.
