Thoran

Ingredients

1/4 kg French Beans
(you can also use any other vegetables - String beans,carrot, palak,s pring onion, red palak, okra etc.)
1 medium Onion
2-3 Green Chillies
1/2Cup Grated Coconut
A pinch Jeera Powder
2 tbsp Oil
1 tsp Mustard Seeds
2 Red Dry Chillies
4 - 5 Curry Leaves

Method

  1. Chop the vegetable, onion and green chillies.
  2. Put the grated coconut and jeera powder in the above and mix all.
  3. Heat oil in a kadai.
  4. Put mustard seeds in the oil.
  5. When the seeds splutter, put dry chillies and curry leaves.
  6. Add the vegetable (step 2) in the above and mix well.
  7. Pour a little water and close the lid.
  8. Cook for 8 -10 minutes in low flame.

It is good with rice.
