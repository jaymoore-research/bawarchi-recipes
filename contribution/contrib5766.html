Tofu Ka Paratha

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients

Firm tofuĀĀĀ- 1 cake
OnionĀĀ - 1 large (finely chopped)
Green chilli(chopped)Ā to taste
Cumin seeds -Ā 1 tsp
coriander powder - 11/2 tsp
Salt as per taste
Green coriander(hara dhaniya) to garnish
Oil to make paratha and fry onion
Wheat flour - 2 cup
Water to knead

Method

  1. Heat 2 tbsp oil in thick pan.
  2. Put cumin seeds and add chopped onions and fry till they are golden brown.
  3. Add chilli.
  4. Mash tofu and add. Put salt and sprinkle coriander powder.
  5. Keep stirring till tofu gets dry enough to fill in the dough.
  6. Mix hara dhaniya. Set a side to get cold.
  7. Mix salt and wheat flour in a bowl. Knead with water to make soft pliable dough.
  8. Take a small ball of dough and roll a thick roti about 4"diameter.
  9. Place a handful of filling at center and bring the ends to center from 4 to 5 points.
  10. Overlap ends so they do not leave an opening and then press gently, dust with dry flour, and proceed to roll.
  11. Heat a heavy griddle, place paratha on it and allow one side to cook have brown golden patches.
  12. Turn repeat for other side. Drizzle some oil and flip. Drizzle oil on other side. Sizzle till done.
  13. Serve hot with Tomato Ketchup or Chutneys.
