Hi, I' m Aarti P. Amonkar from India. Try this chicken out. its very tasty.

Email: aarti_p_a@rediffmail.com

Ingredients

1/2  kg boneless  chicken
2 1/2  tsps  maida
5 tbsp cornflour
1 egg
salt, pepper to taste
a pinch of ajinomoto
a few drops of red colour
oil for frying
Sauce; 2tbsp oil
2tbsp ginger-garlic paste
4green chillies
2tsp soya sauce
2tsp white vinegar
1 1/2 tbsp chilly sauce
a pinch of ajinomoto
few drops of  red colour

Method

  1. Wash  and cut chicken into small pieces.
  2. Mix flour, cornflour, salt, pepper, and ajinomoto
  3. Beat the egg and add  to the flour mixture.
  4. Blend  well, till there are no lumps.
  5. Add  the  red colour.
  6. Put the chicken pieces into the mixture  and marinate  for 15  minutes.
  7. Deep fry in hot oil.
  8. Simmer and fry till  done.
  9. Remove the pieces and set aside.
  10. Heat two tbsp of oil in a  pan.
  11. When  the oil heats up,add ginger - garlic  paste and green chilles finely cut.
  12. Saute for a  couple of  minutes.
  13. Now add the  sauces, vinegar, ajinomoto and red colour
  14. Cook the sauce  for a few  minutes  on a  low  flame.
  15. Add the kept aside chicken pieces.
  16. Cook on low flame till the sauce completely coats the chicken pieces.
