Spiced Rice

Ingredients

2 cups Rice (uncooked).
5 tea-spoons grated Coconut
2 tea-spoons Lemon Juice
6 tea-spoons cooking Oil
2 tea-spoons Toor Dal
2 tea-spoons Udad Dal
1 tea-spoon Mustard
3 Red Chillies
5 Curry Leaves
A pinch of Turmeric and Asafoetida
Salt to taste.

Method:

  1. Cook rice and add coconut, lemon juice, 5 tea-spoons cooking oil and salt.
  2. Roast rest of the above mentioned spices in 1 tea-spoon of oil and dry grind in the blender. Add this ground powder to rice and mix thoroughly.
  3. Serve when rice is warm. Left over should be refrigerated.
