Zesty Coriander Rice

Ingredients

Rice - 2 cups precooked, any variety
Coriander leaves - 3/4 bunch.
Onion - 1 medium , diced
raw ginger - a small piece
garlic - 4 or 5 plump pods
green chillies - 2 medium sized ones
garam masala powder - 2 teaspoons
salt - to taste
peas - 1/2 cup
bay leaf - 1 or 2 (optional)
Oil - 4 tablespoons

Method

  1. Grind together the onion pieces, ginger, garlic and green chillies with the coriander leaves in as little water as possible.
  2. In about 4 tablespoons of oil, add the bay leaf and the ground paste. Fry till the rawness disappears and the oil starts to separate out.
  3. Then add the peas and stir for a few minutes. Then add the garam masala powder and the salt. Stir and leave on fire on a small flame for a few minutes. Then add the precooked rice and stir well.

Serve with Curd or Raita and Pappad.

Preparation time: 15 minutes.
