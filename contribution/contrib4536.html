Besan Pudala

Ingredients

2 cups besan
1 tbsp red chilli powder
1 finely cut green chilly
Salt to taste
Coriander Leaves
1-2 onions( optional)
2 finely cut tomatoes

Method:

  1. Take Besan in a vessel and add water to it. Make a thick batter of it.
  2. Add the red chilli powder, green finely cut chillies, salt, onions, tomatoes and coriander leaves to the batter and mix it well
  3. Take a frying pan and let it heat for some time. Once heated, pour the mixture on to it and spread it to a medium size like a dosa.
  4. Let it cook for some time on one side before flipping it on the other.Cook both the sides till golden brown. Your pudala is ready to eat!

Serve hot with ketchup, coriander chutney or any other chutneys or ketchups! I hope you enjoy it!
