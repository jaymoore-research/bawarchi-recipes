Green Chicken

Ingredients

1 Coriander Leaves - 1 small bunch
2 Chicken pieces - 1/4 kgs
3 Onion [small] - 2
4 Tomatoes - 1
5 Green chillies - 3 to 4 [add more for very hot]
6 Ginger small pieces
7 Garlic 6 to 7"
8 Cinnamon - small pieces
9 Cloves - 2
10 Poppy seed - little
11 Fennel seed- little
12 Cashew - 2
13 Salt as per your taste
14 Turmeric powder - 1/4 tsp

Seasonings:
Oil
Mustard seeds - little
Curry leaves - 4to 5 in no.
Cardamom 2 to 3 piece.

Method:

  1. First Grind all the above raw ingredients in a blender excluding turmeric powder and chicken pieces.
  2. Keep the paste aside now take the pressure pan put some oil and add chicken to it and also add the turmeric powder and fry it little bit then add the paste to it and preesure cook for 3 sounds [for the chicken pieces to cook nicely].
  3. Then take a non-stick pan pour little oil and all the seasonings and then add the cooked chicken so that the pasty water will evaporate so the juice will go into the chicken [stir fry].

    Now it is ready to eat with rice.
