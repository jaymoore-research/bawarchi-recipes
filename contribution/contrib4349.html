Aloo Paneer Sandwich

Ingredients

Bread 4 slices
Potato (boiled) 1
Paneer small pieces 7-8
Onion chopped 1
Green Chillies 2
Red chilli powder 1/2 tsp (as per taste)
Chat Masala 1/2 tsp
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Smash the potatoes, cut the paneer to smaller piece and mix the above ingredients well (except bread).
  2. Place the mixture between two slices of bread, add some butter and toast it in a toaster.
  3. Tasty sandwich tayar! Enjoy it with garma garam Chai and Tomato Ketchup.
