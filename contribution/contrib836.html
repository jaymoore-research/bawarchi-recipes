Cabbage Tomato Curry

Ingredients

1 cabbage whole
1 onion finely chopped
2 small tomatoes or 1 big
1/2 ginger garlic paste
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1 1/2 tsp Red chilli powder
1 tsp Cumin seeds (jeera)
2 1/2 tsp Cumin-Coriander powder (dhaniya-jeera powder)
Oil
Coriander
Salt.

Method :

  1. Cut the cabbage into small pieces.
  2. Tomatoes should be cut finely into small pieces.
  3. Take 1 tablespoon oil into a frying pan.
  4. To this add Jeera seeds, mustard seeds, ginger garlic paste, turmeric and when they splutter add the onions and let them fry well.
  5. Add cabbage to it and fry it nicely.
  6. Add Water if required and let it cook well.
  7. Then add the tomato, mix well.
  8. Then add jeera-coriander powder and salt to it.
  9. Leave it, to cook for a while.
  10. Garnish this with coriander .
