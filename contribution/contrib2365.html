Shrimp Sandwich

Ingredients

1 cup medium sized shrimps (shelled & deveined & cooked)
1 small Onion (chopped finely)
Ginger, Garlic paste (1 tsp each)
Chilli powder
Some Mayonnaise
Lettuce leaves
Dinner rolls or Buns

Method:

For the filling:

  1. Take 2-3 tsp oil in a pan.
  2. Saute chopped onion till golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste & again stir for 1 minute.
  4. Add cooked shrimps & chilli powder, salt & stir. Cover for 2 minutes. Remove from the heat. The filling for sandwich is ready.

Now Spread Mayo, above mixture & put one lettuce leave on the inner side of the roll. Serve.

Shrimp sandwich is very quick & delicious meal. Bon Appetite!
