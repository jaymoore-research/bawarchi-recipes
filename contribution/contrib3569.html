Lentil Rajma

Ingredients

1/4 cup Ghee or Olive Oil
1 cup Lentils
4-6 Tomatoes or 1 can of whole tomatoes
2 Onions, chopped
2 tablespoons Ginger
3 cloves Garlic
1/4 tsp Cumin seeds
1/4 tsp Tumeric
1/4 tsp Salt
1/4 tsp black Pepper
Pinch of Hing

Method:

  1. Boil the lentils separately.
  2. In a large pot, fry the onions ginger and garlic in the Ghee or olive oil.
  3. Drain the lentils and add them to the pot.
  4. Put in the tomatoes and spices. Cook till the tomatoes form a thick sauce
  5. Serve over white or brown rice (an apple chutney is good with it).
