Easy Tomato Saar

Ingredients

3 Large Tomatoes (chopped)
1 Large Onion (cut lengthwise)
4 Green Chillies (cut lengthwise)
1 Sprig Curry Leaves
A small bunch of chopped Coriander Leaves
1/2 tsp. Mustard
Salt to taste
2 tbsp. oil

Method:

  1. Heat oil in a sauce pan. Add mustard and let it splutter.
  2. Then add curry leaves, onion, green chillies, tomatoes, coriander leaves and salt, saute for a while.
  3. Add 1 and half cups of water and let it boil for 5 minutes.
  4. Finally u may garnish with more coriander leaves.
