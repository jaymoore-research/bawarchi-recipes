Quick Basin Cutlet

Ingredients

Basin 1 cup
Water 1/4 cup
Green chillies finely chopped
Curry leaves 5-6 finely chopped
Cilantro finely chopped
Hing a pinch
Mustard 1/4 tea spoon
Jeera 1/4 tea spoon
Oil 3 tea spoons to fry
Salt to taste
Coconut grated

Method:

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard and jeera to it.
  2. When it is done add green chillies to it then add water and let it boil for some time.
  3. Add basin to the boiled water slowly so as to avoid lumps (keep mixing the ingredients while adding). Add salt.
  4. Mix it well for a while and close the container for about a minute till it is well cooked.
  5. Grease the plate and pour the cooked ingredients evenly onto that plate.
  6. Spread grated coconut, cilantro and curry leaves evenly over it.
  7. Cut it into small squares and your cutlet is now ready to be served as a snack or with Chapatis.
