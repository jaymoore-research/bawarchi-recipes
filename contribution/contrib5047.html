Gobi Ki Kheer

Ingredients

Milk 1 litre
Cauliflower 1
Sugar 1 cup
Ghee 1 tsp
Cashew 25 gms
Almonds 25 gms
Raisins 25 gms
Cardamom 3-4

Procedure

  1. Take the Cauliflower and wash it properly. Now grate the cauliflower.
  2. Boil the cauliflower (by adding in water) at least 5-6 times ,till it looses its smell.
  3. Drain it and keep aside.
  4. Take the milk and boil it till it becomes half of its original volume.
  5. Now add the boiled cauliflower and boil for 5-7 minutes.
  6. Add the sugar and cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Now heat the ghee and add all the dry fruits till golden brown and transfer it into the kheer.
  8. Garnish it with cardamom.
  9. Keep in refrigerator after it gets cool .Now its ready to eat.
