Golden Surprise

Ingredients

150 gms Carrots
150 gms Green Peas
150 gms Beans
8-10 small Onions
5-6 small Potatoes
3/4 kg Tomatoes
1 big Onion 4-5 Garlic cloves
1/2 tsp Jeera
3 tsp Corriander seeds
5-6 Red Chillies
1/2" Ginger
Pinch of Turmeric Powder
3 Cloves
3 Elaichi
3 Cinnamon

Method:

  1. Powder cloves, cinnamon and elaichi and keep aside.
  2. Make a paste of ingredients from 1 big onion to turmeric powder. Keep it aside.
  3. Boil tomatoes and puree it.
  4. Cut beans and carrots in long pieces.
  5. Peel the potatoes and the small onions. Keep all the vegetables aside.
  6. Heat ghee in a pan. Put bay leaf and fry a little. Add the paste and fry for full 5 mins.
  7. Add onions and potatoes and fry a little. Then add all the other vegetables and fry for sometime. After about sometime add a little water and allow to boil for a while.
  8. Then add tomato puree to it. Add a little sugar and salt to taste and sprinkle a little garam masala.
  9. Garnish with finely chopped corriander leaves.

Serve hot. Goes very well with Rotis or Naans.
