Leafy Ghugni

Ingredients

Green masala:

1. Half of curry leaves.
2. Four green chillies.
3. Mint leaves

1. One cup of Chana.
2. Half cup of Green peas.
3. One large potato-cut into pieces.
4. 20 Persley leaves; one onion-sliced.
5. A bunch of Curry leaves.
6. A handful of Mint leaves.
7. 1/2tbsp.of Turmeric powder.
8. 1tbsp.of Curry powder.
9. 1/2tbsp.of Sambar masala.
10. 1/2tbsp.of Ginger paste.
11. 1/2tbsp.of Garlic paste.
12. Salt,1/2tbsp.of Sugar,oil.

Method

  1. Pressure cook Chana, peas, and potato, turmeric powder, persley leaves, sugar and half of the curry leaves. Don't drain the water.
  2. Heat oil. Add onion. Light fry. Add green masala. Fry for few minutes.
  3. Mix curry powder, sambar powder, and ginger-garlic paste in half cup of water. Add it.
  4. Remove persley leaves from cooker.Add the other ingredients with the water to the onion masala.
  5. Let the gravy thicken on medium flame for about 10 minutes. Keep stirring. Test the salt. The gravy should be very thick. Remove from oven, and keep covered for some times. Serve hot with Dahi.

Tips: It's not necessary to soak the chana overnight when you're boiling them in pressure cooker. It may melt then. I prepare all the ghugni dishes without soaking the chana overnight.
