Rice in Green Masala

Ingredients

1/2 kg. rice
1 bunch of corriander leaves
8-10 cloves of garlic
8-10 green chillies
salt to taste
1 teaspoon haldi powder
mustard seeds
2 teaspoons oil.

Method

  1. Soak the rice. Grind into a paste - corriander leaves, garlic and green chillies.
  2. In a thick bottom vessel put 2 teaspoons of oil when the oil is hot add the mustard seeds, when the mustard seeds stop spurting add the ground masala, salt and haldi powder.
  3. Fry the masala till golden brown.
  4. Add the rice and cook till done.
  5. Vegetables to go with the rice

    1-2 brinjals (cut in fours)
    1-2 potatoes (cut in cubes)
    4-5 small size onions (peeled)

  6. Masala is the same as for the rice, except the mustard seeds.
  7. In a kadia put oil and fry the masala till golden brown.
  8. Add the vegetables and little water and cook till the vegetables are done.

Eat with the rice.
