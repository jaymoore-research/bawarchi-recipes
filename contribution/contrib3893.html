Meetha Suji Ka

Ingredients

Suji/Bombay Rawa 2 cups
Ghee 2-3 cups
Raisin
Cashewnut
Elaichi/Cardamom (Green) 4 (powdered) for flavour
Sugar 2cups
Banana 1 mashed
Saffron Pinch

Method:

  1. Mash banana and mix pinch of saffron to it and leave it aside.
  2. Roast cashewnut, raisin in ghee in a thick bottomed pan. Add suji and sugar to it and mix thoroughly add little milk to make the consistency. Cook on a low fire. Take care to see that suji does not stick to the sides of the pan. When the suji cooked add mashed banana, powdered cardamom and mix well. Serve hot or cold. It is delicious and easy and quick to prepare.
