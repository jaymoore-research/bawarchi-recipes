Araichu Vitta Sambar

Ingredients

Thuvar dhal: 1 cup
Tamarind : 1 small lime size
Vegetable: Pumpkin or chow-chow or drum-stick or small-onion
Coconut: fresh - 1 handful
Onion: 1 big (optional if using small-onion as vegetable)
Tomato: 1 big
Chana dhal : 1 tbsp
Red-chillies : 4/5
Coriander-seeds: 1 tsp
Fenugreek : 1 tsp
Mustard, Cumin seeds: 1 tsp each
Curry leaves : 1 string
Turmeric powder(optional) : 1 pinch
Hing powder
Oil
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Cut pumpkin / chowchow and boil with salt and turmeric.
  2. Cook Thuvar dhal and keep aside.
  3. Take extract from the tamarind.
  4. In little oil - fry red chillies, chana dhal, fenugreek, coriander seeds and little curry leaves. When they turn brown add the coconut and take off the fire.
  5. Grind the above mixture to a smooth paste with water.
  6. In a vessel - heat oil - then splutter mustard and cumin seeds.
  7. Fry (small/big) onions till golden followed by tomatoes till pulpy.
  8. Pour tamarind extract with a pinch of turmeric powder. Boil till the raw smell goes off.
  9. Add the dhal and vegetables.
  10. When the mixture comes to boiling-point add salt and the ground-paste.
  11. When sambar becomes thick(after a boil)take off from fire.
  12. Garnish with curry leaves and cut corriander leaves.
  13. Serve hot with rice, Chips, appalam or Benas curry would be a great combination. Coconut is optional for the ground-paste.
