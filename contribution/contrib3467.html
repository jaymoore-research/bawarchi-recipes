Cream Chicken

Ingredients

1 Chicken cut in big pieces
Turmeric Powder-1/2 tsp
Red Chilli powder-1 tsp
Ginger-Garlic paste-1 tsp
1 Onion chopped
5-6 Green Chillies cut longitudinally
Salt to taste
Oil for frying

For gravy: Sour Cream -300 gm
Tomato Hunts/Paste

Mix sour cream and tomato paste together and make a smooth paste by adding water. The colour should be pinkish-red. The consistency should be neither too thick or thin.

Method:

  1. Marinate chicken pieces in turmeric powder, red-chilli powder, ginger-garlic paste and salt for 15-20 mins.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and shallow fry the chicken pieces till reddish brown in colour.
  3. Take out the chicken pieces from the pan and add onions to the remaining oil. Fry for sometime and then add the green chillies.
  4. When the onions turn golden brown add the sour cream and tomato hunts paste. Mix it. Add water to thin the gravy.
  5. When it boils add chicken pieces and cover and cook for 5-7 min. on a medium flame.
  6. Serve with Paratha.

Note:You can also use chicken legs or thighs instead. For better taste keep the gravy thick.
