Quick South Chicken Gravy

Ingredients

Chicken - 1 kg (Chopped, cleaned and broken into small cubes.)
Ginger/garlic - 1/4 cup (Chop equal amounts of garlic and ginger)
Onion finely chopped - 1/2 cup
Tomato finely chopped - 1 cup.
1/4 tsp. Red Chilli Powder
1/2 tsp of Turmeric Powder
1/2 cup of Coriander leaves finely chopped
1/2 tsp Pepper Powder
1 tsp of Chinnamon, Cloves and Cardamom paste
Salt to taste.

Method :

  1. Grind the ginger/garlic, chinnamon, colves and some onions and half of the mint-coriander mixture. All these should be grinded separately.
  2. First pour 2 tsp of oil in a pan and boil the Chopped, cleaned and broken chicken with a small cup of water and salt (Just one vapour). Remove it.
  3. Then pour the required oil in pan for gravy. Allow the oil to heat.
  4. Then Fry the chinnnamon/clove paste first.
  5. Then add ginger/ garlic paste. Then add onions and fry till it gets mixed up with the paste.
  6. Then add the chopped tomatoes and mix it well with the other paste, keep mixing it till the tomato is properly cooked with the paste.
  7. Now add a little salt as per required and also the turmeric /chilli powder and mix it well till the raw smell is out.
  8. Now finally add the chicken to it and mix it well, see to it that the chicken is mixed in such a way that all the garvy gets mixed well with the chicken.
  9. Now add a cup of water to the gravy and allow the water to get strain or thicken.
  10. Finally at the time of removing add the remaining coriander leaves and off the fire.

Time : 30 minutes

It goes well with Rice, Chappathi or Parota
