7 Cups Sweet

Ingredients

1 cup- Semolina
1 cup -Gram Flour (besan)
1 cup-Green Moong Dal Flour
1 cup-fresh Coconut
1 cup-Milk
1 cup-Sugar
1 cup-Ghee
4-5 stings of Saffron dissolved in water
1/2 tsp - Nutmeg Powder
20 Cashewnuts to decorate

Method:

  1. Take a deep frying pan put ghee and fry each dry ingredients separately well until nice aroma comes out then keep all the dry ingredients in a bowl together.
  2. Now boil milk and water of same quantity.
  3. Take a round plate grease it with little ghee keep it ready.
  4. Now put remaining ghee when it is little hot transfer the dry ingredients to the pan fry for a second or two on medium then add the boiling milk to the dry mixture and mix well. Here you have to be careful so that lumps are not formed, keep stirring until halwa consistency or thick sauce consistency then switch of the stove.
  5. Then add sugar to it mix well, add half a teaspoon of salt, dissolved saffaron in the sweet, nutmeg powder. Mix well and pour into the plate
  6. Decorate it with cashewnuts.
  7. When cool cut it and enjoy it.
