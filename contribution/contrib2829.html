Bharvan Karela

Ingredients
(For 4 persons)

Karela - 1/2 kg (Peeled)
Onions - 3 medium nos (Finally chopped)
Garlic - 1 Full pc (Finally chopped)
Green Chilli - 2 nos
Salt as per taste
Coriander Powder: 2 table spoon
Red Chilli powder: 2 tea spoon
Cumin Powder - 1 table spoon
Mango Powder - 2 tea spoon
Garam Masala - 1 tea spoon (optional)

Method:

  1. Make slit the Karelas lengthwise & peel out the inner stuff.
  2. Boil Karelas in salted water.
  3. Fry oninons, garlic, green chillies & the peeled out stuff and put all the spices. Fry them untill fully cooked.
  4. Put this filling into the boiled Karelas.
  5. Put oil in a pan & shallow fry Karelas.

You can also try Bharvan Parval by the same method.
