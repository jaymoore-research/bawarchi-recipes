Sal Phulka

Ingredients

Chappati 3-4 or more
Onion 1
Tomatoes 2-3
Green Chillies 3-4
Garlic paste 1 tsp
Coriander leaves
Turmeric pwd 1/2 tsp
Red chilly pwd 1/2 tsp
Coriander pwd 1 tsp
Garam Masala 1/2 tsp
Salt
Oil
Mustard seeds 1/2 tsp
Cummin seeds 1/2 tsp

Method:

  1. Break the chappatis into small pieces.
  2. Take a tsp of oil & add the mustard & cummin seeds into it...once it splutters add the chopped onions & green chillies.
  3. Add the garlic paste & fry the mixture till the onion is pink in colour & loses its raw smell.
  4. Add the chopped tomatoes & cook till it is smashed.
  5. Now add on the masalas & fry for 2 minutes.
  6. Next add 2 cups of water & add salt (as per taste & be careful as the chappati already has salt in it)....... & let the water start boiling.
  7. Then add the chappati pieces & mix properly in the gravy.
  8. Cook in medium heat & keep mixing or else the chappati pieces might get stuck to the botom of the pan.
  9. Once the water is soaked & dry keep for 2 more minutes. Sprinkle the chopped coriander leaves on top & serve immediately.
