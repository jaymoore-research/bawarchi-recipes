Bread Uppma

Ingredients

5 to 6 slices of bread cut into 1/2 inch squares or better still cut crisp-ends of sandwiches (2 1/2cups aprox.) This taste better!!!
3 medium onions cut into small pieces
5 tomatoes cut into small pieces
5-6 curry leaves
1 teaspoon each cummin, saunf,coriander powder, cummin powder, pav-bhaji masala.
1 Tablespoon chilli powder
A pinch of Turmeric
Salt to taste.
2 tablespoon corn oil.
2 table spoon fresh coriander

Method:

  1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
  2. Add cummin seeds, Saunf, Turmeric, Onions and fry for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Now add tomatoes, fry for a minute and then add the cummin+ corriander+ pav bhaji masalas. Stir for a while and keep on low fire untill this "sauce" cooks say 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add the bread pieces and mix very well such that the cooked sauce covers the bread pieces. Add salt and keep on a low fire for a minute or two UNCOVERED.

Garnish with 2 table spoon fresh corriander Serve Hot!

Note: You can vary the chilli/ spices to your taste.
