Spinach Curry

Ingredients

1/2 bag washed and ready to eat Spinach
6 big Garlic pods
1/2 inch Ginger
4 tbs of Tomato puree
2 tsp of Cumin powder
1 tsp of Chilli powder
1/2 tsp of Garam masala
1 pinch of Hing
1 pinch of Cumin seeds
Salt to taste
4 tbs of oil

Method:

  1. Finely chop the spinach
  2. Grate garlic and ginger
  3. Heat oil. Put the hing and then the jeera seeds
  4. When it splutters, put the grated garlic /ginger in the oil together.
  5. When light brown put the tomato puree.
  6. Let fry. Now put the cumin powder, chilli powder.
  7. Now put the chopped spinach. Fry at high heat.
  8. When the water from the spinach dries up and the spinach becomes soft, put the salt and garam masala
  9. Serve hot with roti or rice.
