Bombay Chutney

Yield: 12 Servings

Ingredients

Mustard seeds - 1 tsp
Urud Dal - 1 tsp
Green chillies - 4
Onions - 2 big
Besan Gram Flour - 3 tsp
Curry leaves -Ā10
Tomatoes - 2
Oil - 1 tsp
Salt - as desired

Method

  1. Chop Onions , tomatoes, green chillies finely.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, urud dal & let it splutter, then add onion, green chillies, curry leavesĀand tomatoes to it & fry it
  3. Mix the besan gram flour with water addĀsalt to it and add this mixture to the pan , allow it to boil till it becomes like a pasteĀ.
  4. Garnish with Coriander leaves.
  5. Total cooking time would be 15-20 minutes, you can also add boiled potatoes to it.
