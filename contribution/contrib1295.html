Cilantro Baath

Ingredients

Rice -2 cups
Cilantro - 1 bunch
Green chillies - 3 or 4 (or as per your taste)
Cumin seeds -1 1/2 tsp
Cloves -4
Cardomom -1/2 tsp
Onion -1 medium
Tej patha -a few.
Juice of a Lemon

Method :

  1. Cook rice with a tsp. of oil and allow it to cool.
  2. Grind cilantro, green chillies, 1 tsp cumin seeds, 2 cloves, and onion together.
  3. Take little oil in a frying pan and season it with cumin, cloves, cardomom and patha.
  4. Then add the ground mixture and fry everything till the raw smell goes and oil separates.
  5. Add this to the cooked rice and add salt to taste and mix every thing together.
  6. Add lemon juice and mix well.

Hope you all like it! You can also add vegetables like peas and carrots when you are frying the masala.
