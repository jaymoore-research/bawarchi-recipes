Corn Cobs in Yogurt Sauce

Ingredients

4 nos. fresh Corn boiled in salty water (yellow or white)
Green chili paste (according to your taste)
Ginger paste
Salt to taste
1 cup yogurt mix with about 1 and 1/2 cup of water or use buttermilk mix with 2 tbsp of Gram flour
Dhana Jira powder
Sugar to taste
2 tbsp Oil
Cumin seeds 1 tsp
Mustard seeds 1/2 tsp
Turmeric powder
Cilantro 2 tblsp.

Method :

  1. In a deep pan, heat oil then add cumin and mustard seeds. As the seeds splutter, add chili paste and ginger and saute for about 1 to 2 minutes
  2. Then add yogurt mix with all other ingredients with corn pieces. Boil until the mixture looks like gravy. Remove from stove then add cilantro when serve.

Serve with Rice or Paratha/Chapati.
