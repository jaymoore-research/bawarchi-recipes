Ingredients

1 cup mixed vegetables (include broccoli, asparagus, celery etc if you wish)
1 large red onion(finely chopped)
1/2 bunch cilantro
3 tomatoes or 1/4 cup tomato puree

Method

  1. (I grind my own masala at home) Combine 2 tsp.coconut, 1 tsp. poppy seeds, 2-3 garlic cloves, 1"ginger, 5 red chillies, 1/2"cinnamon stick, 4 peppercorns, 1/5 tsp.
  2. Dhania seeds, 4 cloves,a little mace and nutmeg.
  3. (Dry roast and powder) cook the vegetables.
  4. Heat oil in your pan. Temper with Jeera and slit green chillies.
  5. Add onion and fry till brown. Add masala pdr. Fry for 30 seconds.
  6. Add tomatoes/puree.
  7. Add salt and cook till oil floats on top (about 10-15 minutes).
  8. Add cooked vegetables and simmer for 10 minutes.
  9. To make the gravy creamy, I prefer to add 1/2 cup of milk!!
  10. Garnish with cilantro leaves and serve piping hot with phulkas, puris or rice!!
