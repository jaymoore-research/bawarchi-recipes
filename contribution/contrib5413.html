Stuffed Karela with Onion Masala

Ingredients

Karela - 6
Oil - For frying

For Masala:
Onion - 1
Garlic cloves - 3
Coriander seeds(dhania) - 2 tsp
Cumin seeds(jeera) - 2 tsp
Dry/red chilli - 5 to 6
Salt - for taste

Method:

  1. For the masala, put all the ingredients(onion, garlic, dhania, jeera, dry chillies) in the blender and grind them to a fine paste. Take the paste into a bowl. Fry the paste in a pan using required amount of oil. Add salt to the paste as per ur taste. Put the fried paste aside.
  2. Wash karelas properly and cut them at both ends in such a way that you can stuff the masala. Be careful, the karelas should not get cut totally, they should remain as whole karelas. After cutting, keep the karelas in microwaveable plate and microwave them for about 5 minutes. They will become little tender. If you don't have a microwave, it is okay... you can directly start frying the karelas. Now take a pan and place it on the stove. Put oil and fry the karelas till they are cooked. (You can also fry them deeply, if you don't mind the saturated fat!!) Take out the karelas from the pan and let them cool for some time. Now, stuff the fried masala paste in the fried karelas. Be careful, so that you don't break the karelas!! Fry the stuffed karelas again for about 5 to 10 minutes on a low flame, depending on how deeply you want to fry them. Be careful while stirring, turn the karelas slowly. If you have some more paste remaining after stuffing into the karelas, you can put it! he paste over these fried karelas and fry till they are done.

Note: Measurements of the ingredients can be changed as per your taste.
