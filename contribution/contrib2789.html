Rava Uppama

Ingredients

250 grams Rava
4 Green Chillies
1 tablespoon Curd
1 piece Ginger
4 small Bay Leaves (tej patta)
1 teaspoon Mustard seeds, Urad and Chana Dal
25 grams fried Cashewnuts
A few Curry Leaves
1 Onion chopped finely
Coriander Leaves for garnishing
Salt to taste.

Method:

  1. Fry rava to a golden colour in 2-3 teaspoon ghee.
  2. Heat 2 teaspoon oil and put in the mustard seeds and the dals. When the dals become red add ginger, chillies, bay and curry leaves and onion and fry.
  3. Add curd and mix well. Then pour 4 cups water, put in salt and bring to a boil, reduce heat and add rava.
  4. Cook and stirring constantly till the rava is thick and dry.
  5. Garnish with coriander leaves, mix in cashewnuts and serve immediately with Coconut Chutney.
