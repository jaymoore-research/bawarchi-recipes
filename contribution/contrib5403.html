Peach Cake

Ingredients

1 tablespoon butter -- softened
1 cup sugar
2 1/2 cups flour
3 teaspoons baking powder
1 1/2 cups milk
3/4 cup sugar
mixed with
2 teaspoons cinnamon
5 large peaches -- peeled and sliced
2 tablespoons butter -- melted

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. With electric mixer, blend first five ingredients.
  3. Spread in greased and floured 15x10" jelly roll pan, or 13x9" plus one 8" cake pan.
  4. Sprinkle half of cinnamon/sugar mixture lightly over dough base.
  5. Arrange peach slices by rows on top of base.
  6. Sprinkle with remainder of cinnamon/sugar mixture and drizzle melted butter over all.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes. (For thicker dough, omit 8" cake pan - use 13x9" pan only.)
