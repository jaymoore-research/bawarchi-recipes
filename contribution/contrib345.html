Potato Curd Curry.

Ingredients

Potatoes - 4 if med.sized, 2 if big(red,yellow or brown potatoes).
Tomatoes - 2 if med.sized, 1 if big.
Coconut - 5 or 6 tablespoons.
Green chillies - 5 if med.sized, 6or 7 if small, 2 if big.
Pottukadalai - 1med.sized cup or 2 small(1/8 measuring cups) cups.
cloves - 2.
ghee to season.
Kariveppilai or curry leaves - 6 leaves or acc:- to your satisfaction.
Home made curd - 1 med.sized bowl. (don't substitute it with sour cream or yoghurt. preparing home made curd is very easy).
salt to taste.

Method

  1. Boil the potatoes and mash them after they cool down.
  2. Grind the pottukadalai, coconut and the green chillies in the mixer.
  3. Then add water and chopped tomatoes to a copper bottomed or any heat resistant bowl and let it boil.
  4. After 2 or 3 minutes, mix the ground mixture, mashed potatoes with the curd and add them to the boiling tomatoes.
  5. Let the whole dish simmer for about 7 to 9 minutes in very low flame because, the curd will separate if kept on high or med.flame.
  6. Season the cloves in hot ghee and add it to the simmering dish.
  7. Switch the burner off and add the curry leaves. Serve while it is hot. It is so palatable and goes very well as a side dish for dosas( ordinary dosas).
