Dhoodhi Muthias

Ingredients

1 cup grated dhoodhi (bitter gourd)
1 cup besan atta (gram flour)
1/2 cup rice flour
2-3 green chillies cut into fine pieces
a pinch of haldi (tumeric)
red chilli powder
1 tablespoon curd or juice of 1 lemon
salt to taste
1 tablespoon sugar
oil
mustard seeds
dessicated coconut to garnish
coriander leaves

Method

  1. Squeeze the grated dhoodhi of water.
  2. Mix the gram and the rice flour with the dhoodhi, adding salt, sugar, green chillies, red chilli powder, a little curd or juice of lemon. Without adding water, knead the mixture into a dough.
  3. You can sprinkle a little water. In an oiled pan, flattten the dough and steam it. This can be done quickly in a microwave, or a cooker.
  4. After the dough is cooked, let it cool, then cut it lengthwise or into squares.
  5. Put a little oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds. After they splutter, add the cut pieces of the dough, dessicated coconut and lastly coriander leaves.
  6. Serve it with tomato sauce or chutney.
