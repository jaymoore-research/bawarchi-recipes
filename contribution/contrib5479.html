Tappale dose

Ingredients

1 Cup Rice
1 Cup Toor Dhal
Finely grated coconut
4 Green chillies
Salt to taste
Oil

Method:

  1. Soak equal quantities of rice and toor dhal (1 cup each) in water for about 5 hours.
  2. Grind them to a paste, not too fine, but not too coarse to make the dose paste.
  3. Grind the chillies and the coconut together and add this paste to the dose paste.
  4. Add Salt to taste.
  5. Keep for 4 to 5 hours.
  6. Then make dose on a hot tavva with 1 tsp oil for each dose.

Serve with Coriander coconut chutney or any chutney or with Saagu (a wet curry).
