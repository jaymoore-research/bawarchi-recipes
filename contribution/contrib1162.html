I am contributing a favourite Kerala dish with Tapioca, originated from central Kerala. This is called as 'Kappa Puzhukku' in Kerala. It is difficult to find this age-old recipe nowadays in Recipe columns. I want to keep-alive the age old wonderful recipes. This is eaten as the main dish served with some curry -Labby

Tapioca

Ingredients(Serves 2)

Tapioca - 2 rootstems
Turmeric powder - 1/2 teaspoon
Green chillies - 2
Red chilli powder - 1/2 teaspoon
Curry leaves - 1 sprig
Coconut shredded - one half
Coconut oil - 1 table spoon
Mustard seeds - 1/2 teaspoon
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Peel and cut the tapioca into 1/2 inch pieces
  2. Boil Tapioca pieces. When half boiled, strain the water (this is to take off the bitterness found in tapioca skin)
  3. Boil again by adding water, and after the water comes to a boil, strain the water again. The tapioca should only be 3/4th cooked.
  4. Crush the green chillies and shredded coconut.
  5. Pls be careful only to crush it and not make a paste.
  6. Add turmeric powder, red chilli powder.
  7. Mix 1/2 cup of water to this mixture, add salt and boil tapioca to a very semi-soft consistency.
  8. Let the water gets completely evaporated.
  9. Heat coconut oil in a pan, splutter mustard seeds and curry leaves. Add it to the tapioca mixture and mix it.

Serve with fish, chicken or beef curry.
