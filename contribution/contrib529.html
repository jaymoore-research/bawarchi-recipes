Spinach Chutney

Ingredients

Bunch of Spinach leaves thoroughly washed
Chana dal 1 tbs.
Urad dal 1tbs.
Tamarind juice, paste (According to taste)
Salt
Red Chilies (According to your level of tolerance)
Coconut (Optional)

Method:

  1. Boil the spinach and drain the excess water, don't throw away excess of water you can use it while grinding. Roast the Chana dal, Urad dal , Red Chilies with or without oil.
  2. Allow it to cool, now in the grinder add this roasted mixture, boiled spinach, coconut, tamarind paste and salt, as far as adding the water while grinding is concerned you can use the drained water.

And here you go the chutney is ready. It goes well with rice/chappaties.

You can have little variations here, instead of tamarind paste you can add tomatoes.

Cooking time is less than 15 min.
