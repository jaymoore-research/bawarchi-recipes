Chatpata Moong Dal

Ingredients

1 cup yellow Moong daal
2 cups of water
2-3 teaspoon of cooking oil
1 teaspoon jeera
2-3 crushed red chillies
1 pinch hing
salt to taste
1/2 teaspoon turmeric
1 teaspoon lemon juice

Method

  1. Pressure cook yellow moong dal with water, salt and turmeric powder.
  2. Cook till it becomes a paste.(Infact after 2-3 whistles, stir it to make a paste like consistency).
  3. Spread dal on a serving plate to make about 1 cm thick layerĀand allow itĀto rest for about 2-3 minutes.
  4. In the mean time heat oil in small pan and put hing, crushed red chillies and jeera. As soonĀas they start sputtering, takeĀpan off the fire and spread this tadka on the dal.
  5. Serve hotĀby garnishing with lemon juice in the end. ( Could also use a little bit of mint chutney).
  6. Enjoy your snack.
