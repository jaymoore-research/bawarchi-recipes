Makai (Corn) Khichdi

Ingredients

corn kernels (you get these in any american grocery store)
1 palmful of yellow moong
haldi
mirchi powder
salt as per taste
1 tsp. sugar (if required)

Method

  1. Take the corn (you can also use the cream style sweet corn) and grind it in the mixie so as to make a rough paste.
  2. Put this in a microwavable bowl and add the moong to it.
  3. Add some water and microwave it for around 10 minutes.
  4. Now, put this in a vessel.
  5. Add some haldi powder, salt, sugar (if you are not using the cream style sweet corn) and mirchi powder to this and steam it for around 10 minutes.
  6. Once it's steamed, take some oil in a kadai.
  7. Put some rai and allow it to sputter.
  8. Add the steamed corn to this and keep on stirring.
  9. Once, it starts to thicken; put off the gas and add a dash of lime to it. Serve hot.

Making time: around 25 minutes
