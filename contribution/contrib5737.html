Palak ki kadhi

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients

1/4 bunch of spinach (Palak), cut into fine strips
1/2 kg thick and slightly sour curd
11/2 cup of water
1 tablespoon besan (bengal gram flour)
3 red chillies
Turmeric 1 pinch
Curry leaves 2 sprigs
3 green chillies, 1/2 inch ginger,6 cloves garlic and few sprigs of coriander ground to a fine paste
Salt to taste
Few coriander leaves for garnishing
1 tablespoon oil

Method

Ā

  1. Heat the oil in a kadhai, add red chillies, curry leaves and let the tempering crackle a little.Ā
  2. Add the palak leaves and stir for 1/2 a minute and cover the kadhai on a slow flame.Ā
  3. Meanwhile beat the curd along with the masala paste turmeric and besan into a homogenous paste and slowly add water.Ā
  4. The palak would be half cooked in about 5 min and then add the curd mixture and stir well.Ā
  5. Now add the salt according to your taste.Ā let it be on the flame till a boil comes.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander.
  7. Serve boiled rice and papad, makes an exciting meal. Ā
