Green Filler Salad

Ingredients

Cottage Cheese - 2 cups
Cabbage leaves - 5-6
Cucumbers - 1 small sized
Tomatoes - Half cup
Onions - 1/4 th cup
Green Chilli - 1
Lime Juice - 2 tea spoons
Green Peas - 1/4 th cup
Button Mushrooms - 1/4 th cup
Grated Cheese - 3 teaspoon
Coriander & mint leaves - 1/4 th cup
Salt & pepper to taste

Method:

  1. Boil the green peas till nearly done
  2. Grate the cottage cheese
  3. Chop cabbage leaves, tomatoes, onions, Mushrooms, cucumbers & chilli
  4. Mix together all the ingredients in a bowl
  5. Add the seasoning & lemon juice & refrigerate for 15-20 minutes

Serving: serve with grated cheese & chopped coriander & mint eaves

Serves 2 people with good appetite
