Tomato - Dahi Chutney

Ingredients

Cut Tomatoes (medium) 4
Green chilies chopped 2 (as per your taste)
Curd 2 - 1/2 Cups
Turmeric (haldi) 1/2 tbsp
Chili powder 1/2 tbsp (as per your taste)
Mustard/Cumin seeds 1/2 tbsp each
Curry leaves few
Oil 1 tbsp
Salt As per your taste

Method:

  1. Add oil to the skillet, when it is hot enough, put mustard/cumin seeds.
  2. Allow them to splutter, then add tomato pieces, green chilies and curry leaves in it.
  3. Add turmeric/red chili powder and salt.
  4. Let it cook for 5-10 minutes till the tomatoes are soft and entire water is evaporated.
  5. Once done, allow it to cool for sometime, say3-4 minutes. (For fast cooling, you can put the tomato gravy in a steel container and put this container in another big container having the cold water.
  6. Beat curd a little bit. Add the above mixture to this curd and mix well.
  7. You can serve this either hot or cold. Serve it with rotis or rice.

Note: Please do not heat it a whole lot, as the curd consistency would become watery on high heat.
