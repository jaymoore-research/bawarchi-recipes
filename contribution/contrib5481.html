Chingri Macher Paturi

Ingredients

1/2 kg -1kg shelled shrimps.
Salt to taste
Mustard oil 25gms
Yellow Mustard seed-25gms for Grinding
Coconut 1/2.
5-8 Green Chillies.
Banana Leaf or Pumpkin leaves.
Normal thread to Tie.

Method:

  1. Grind the chillies, mustard seeds, coconut into smooth paste.
  2. Clean and rub the shrimps dry.
  3. Mix it with the paste with 1tablesppon of Mustard oil also with salt and turmeric.
  4. Keep it aside for 15minutes then fold it in bed of Banana leaf or in bed of Pumpkin leaves. It should be tied nicely with thread.
  5. Heat the Non stick Pan with Mustard oil which is already there.
  6. Keep the tied shrimps on the Pan and over slow flame of Gas.

Both the sides should be fried till Brown. Remove it from fire take it out and serve it with Hot Rice.
