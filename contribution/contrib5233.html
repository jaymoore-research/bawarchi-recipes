Tomato Morkootan

Ingredients

2 cups curd (beaten)
2 ripe and firm tomatoes(med sized)
1tsp haldi
1/2 cup grated coconut
1 or 2 green chillies
1tsp raw rice
salt -to taste

For tadka:
2 tsp oil
1tsp mustard
1tsp fenugreek
1 red chill-made into 3-4 pieces
curry leaves

Method:

  1. Cut tomatoes into med. sized pieces and cook with haldi and salt for 1-2 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile grind coconut, green chilli and raw rice with 1 tbsp of curd and little water to make a fine paste
  3. Add this paste to tomatoes and cook till raw smell disappears.
  4. May stir in-between
  5. Once cooked add beaten curd and keep the flame in simmer for 2 minutes.
  6. It should not boil.
  7. Switch of flame and add tadka and curry leaves.
