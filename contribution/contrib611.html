Spicy Chana Dal

Ingredients

1cup Chana dal
1 large onion, sliced
a one inch piece of ginger, crushed
2-3 cloves of garlic, crushed
1 tsp cumin powder
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp tumeric powder
1/2 tsp black pepper powder
2 bay leaves
3-4 cloves
2 large cardomoms
1tbsp oil
salt to taste

For tempering(Tadka)

1 small onion chopped finely
2-3 red chillies
1tbsp ghee

Method:

  1. Wash the the dal and soak for about 15 mins.
  2. Heat oil in the pressure cooker.
  3. Add the garam masalas (bayleaves, cloves and cardomoms).
  4. Once they exude their aroma add the onions.
  5. When the onions are translucent add the ginger and garlic.
  6. Fry a little more, then add the remaining spices and fry with a little sprinkle of water.
  7. Add the dal.
  8. Fry a little more then add 3 cups of water.
  9. Season with salt.
  10. Bring to a boil.
  11. Then reduce heat and let it simmer. (Or let it cook in a pressure cooker on low heat for about 20 mins.)
  12. The dal is ready when it is of a thick consistency and the onion slices are no longer visible.
Tadka

Heat ghee add the onion and red chillies. When the onions are red and crisp , pour the mixture over the dal.

Enjoy with hot Phulkas or steamed rice.Tastes good even with Pulao and is a good dal dish for parties.
