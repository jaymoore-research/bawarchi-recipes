Vegetable Sandwich

Ingredients

Two slices of Wheat Bread,
9-10 slices of Cucumber,
2 slices of Tomato,
One slice of Cheese,
Few layers of Lettuce,
One whole spoon of Mayonease,
One spoon of Tomato Ketchup
Two pinches of Chat Masala

Method:

  1. Dry toast the slices of bread.
  2. Apply mayonease on one of the slices and tomato ketchup on the other.
  3. On the side where the tomato ketchup is applied, place the sliced cucumbers, put a pinch of chat masala. Then place the tomato.
  4. Put another pinch of chat masala. Then place the slice of cheese and top it with luttuce.
  5. Cover the sandwich with the slice of bread which has mayonease on it.

This forms a healthy and tasty lunch or even a snack. Complete it with a glass of orange juice.
