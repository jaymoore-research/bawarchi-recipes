Rava Idli

Ingredients

Samolina-2 cups
Curds-1 cup
Salt to taste
Little Cooking Soda
2 teaspoons Coconut
1 grated Carrot
25 grams Peas (frozen is also fine)
Cashewnuts for taste
Cilantro leaves to taste

Method:

  1. Fry samolina a little until little warm and mix it with the curds and little bit of water for this mixture add grated carrot, coconut, peas and about 2 green chillies slit and add salt and cooking soda and keep this mixture for about an hour or so before steaming add little bit of tadka.
  2. For the tadka add little musturd seeds, urad dal and channa dal and cashews.
  3. Put the tadka to the mixture and mix it well and steam the idlis in a idli plate for about 18 minutes and serve hot with Chutney or Tomato Ketchup.
