Mixed Vegetable Sagu

Ingredients

For the Masala:

1) Fresh Coconut - A handful
2) Poppy Seeds - 1 tspn
3) Jeera 1 tspn
4) Hing/Asafoetida a pinch
5) Pepper Corns 5-6 pcs
6) Dalia 3 tspn
7) Chakke one small piece
8) Ginger one inch piece
9) Green Chillies 10 pieces
10) Coriander a few strands
11) Onion one small onion cut into pieces
12) Turmeric powder one pinch

Vegetables

1) Lima Beans 1/2 a cup
2) Peas 1/2 acup
3) Beans 50grams
4) Carrot one medium size carrot chopped into rings.
5) Potato one medium sized chopped into cubes
6) Chayyote one medium sized cut into medium sized pieces
7) Cauliflower one medium sized cut into medium sized florets
8) Tomato one medium sized cut into fine pieces.
9) Capsicum one medium sized cut into 3 inch long pieces.
10) Onion one medium sized cut into small pieces.

Method:

  1. Boil the vegetables (not in a pressure cooker because it gets overdone) except capsicum, tomatoes and onion.
  2. Add all the ingredients for the masala into the blender and make a fine paste.
  3. In a heavy bottom vessel add 4-5 tsps of oil and fry the onions till light brown. Now add tomotoes and cook it for a while. Later add capsicum and toss it.When it is cooked add the masala and fry it for a minute.
  4. To this mixture add boiled vegetables and stir. Add salt and allow this mixture to boil for a while.
  5. The sagu is now ready can be served with hot Chappatis/Puris. Happy Cooking!!!
