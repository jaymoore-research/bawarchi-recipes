Fish Fry

Ingredients

Any kind of Fresh Fish 1 lb (rainbow trout, catfish etc.)
Plain Red Chilli Powder 1 tsp or (according to taste)
Salt
Ginger Garlic paste 2 tsp
Corn Flour 1 tsp
Garam Masala 1 tsp
Oil to deep fry.

Method

Add the red chilli powder, salt, garam masala powder and corn flour to the ginger-garlic paste and mix well and add this to the fish pieces and deep fry in any cooking oil until golden brown.

The fish can be mixed this way and left to marinate in the refrigerator the previous day itself or it can be fried right away. both ways will be equally good.
