The recipe I am going to write is called the Moong Dhal Dosa. Instead of Rice I use moong Dhal for this dosa. I learnt this recipe from my co-sister. It is very easy to make.

Moong Dal Dosa

Ingredients

Moong Dhal 2 cups
Urad Dhal 1 cup
Green Chilli 4 or 6
Ginger 1 spoon grated
Salt 2 spoons

Method

  1. Soak both the dhal previous day or soak it for 2 hours in warm water.
  2. Then use your blender and blend all the ingredients together and add salt to it according to your taste.
  3. You can also garnish it with fresh coriander leaves or curry leaves or both. And you can add chopped onions to it.
  4. Take a pan and make your dosa.

Enjoy it with a Chutney or Ketchup or Sambar.
