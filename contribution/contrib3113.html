Pudina Chutney

Ingredients

1. Mint leaves - 1 bunch
2. Cilentro [coriander leaves] -1 bunch exactly equal amount of mint leaves
3. Dry Red chillies - 2 to 3 according to your taste
4. Tamarind paste - 1/2 teaspoon
5. Salt - to taste
6. Onion - 1 small onion
7. Tomatoes - 1 small
8. Urad Dal -1 teaspoon

Seasonings:

Mustard seeds, Urad Dal, Curry Leaves.

Method:

  1. Keep the kadai and put some oil and then put 1/2 teaspoon of urad dal and fry till it turns brown and then put chopped onions red chillies and fry till they turns golden brown and keep it aside.
  2. Again keep kadai and put those mint and cilentro fry them till the leaves shrink then keep it aside.
  3. Then put some oil in kadai and fry the tomatoes till the outer coverings shrink, and keep it aside.
  4. Now grind all those fried ones and put tamarind paste, salt.
  5. Now it is ready to grind in a blender. Don't add water while grinding because tomatoes leave a juice if needed add little water.
  6. Now put the kadai pour some oil and put the mustard seed wait till it splutters and add urad dal while it turns brown put those curry leaves and add all these ones to our chutney. Really it will be tasty when you have with your dosa.
