Stuffed Karela

Ingredients

Karelas (bitter gourd ) -8-10 nos.
Raw Mango -1 med sized
Chili powder -1 tps
Amchoor powder -1tsp
Salt - to taste
Turmeric powder -1/3 tsp
Coriander powder - 1 tsp
Garam Masala -1/2 tsp
Oil for shallow frying..

Method:

  1. Peel the karels lightly and rub some salt to it and leave for about 1/2 an hour.
  2. Peel the raw mango and grate it with a grater.
  3. Mix the above dry ingredients to it and blend well.
  4. Stuff the karelas with this mixture and heat oil in a wok and shallow fry till it becomes golden brown in color.
  5. Serve hot with Chappati and your favorite Daal

Note:

You can also stuff baby potatoes and baby onions with this filling and fry as above. Tastes very good when served hot.
