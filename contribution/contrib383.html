Capsicum special

Ingredients

Capsicum (medium size) 2 nos
Potato, Onion, Tomato (medium) 1 nos each
Cottage cheese 1/2 cup
Makhana (puffed lotus seeds) 1/2 cup
Cashew nuts 1/4 cup
Curd 1/2 cup
1- teaspoon red chilli powder, salt to taste, ginger to garnish, Oil to fry

Method

  1. Dice Potato & cheese in cube form & chop Capsicum, Onion , tomato in small pieces.
  2. Deep fry - potato , cottage cheese cubes & Makhana and keep aside.
  3. Saute the onions to golden brown.
  4. Add tomato pieces and saute till done.
  5. Add curd to above, and stir continuously till oil comes up.
  6. Add salt & chilly powder
  7. Add fried potato, cheese, makhana, capsicum, cashew nuts & mix well.
  8. Cover and cook for two minutes on sim flame.
  9. Garnish with ginger and serve medium hot with nan or rice

Serve For 4 Persons
