Bise Bela Bath

Ingredients

6 cups of water
thick tamarind paste 1 1/2 spoons
urud dhal 1 1/2 spoons
split peas dhal 1/2 spoons
corriander seeds 1 1/2 spoons
fenugreek - a pinch
2-3 small pieces of cinnamon sticks
cloves 5-6
2-4 spoons of grated coconut
10 dry chillies
2 medium sized onions(chopped)
one big tomato
1 1/2 cups of rice
1/2 cup of toordhal
2 pinches of turmeric
salt 2 1/4 spoons
Mixed vegetables (like peas , carrot, beans, brinjal, potato, etc)

Method:

  1. First fry the onions and keep it separately.
  2. Then fry the items in no 3 - 10 and make a thick paste of masala out of it.
  3. Now make a diluted paste of tamarind with 6 cups of water, now add all the vegetables, salt and turmeric, so that it is half cooked.
  4. Now add the masala to this mixture, allow it to cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Now add the chopped tomatoes, rice, toordhal.
  6. Let it cook for 5 minutes such that the stove is in it's minimum heat, now add the onions and stir well and pressure cook it.
  7. That's it. Garnish with corriander.

Serve hot with potato chips or fryums. Add ghee (optional)
