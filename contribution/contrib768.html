Fish (Sardines) Curry

Ingredients

10 Sardines - cleaned and washed thoroughly
3 medium onions - chopped
6 flakes garlic - chopped finely
1 inch piece ginger - chopped finely
5 - 6 green chillies - slit lengthwise
2 medium tomatoes - chopped
1 tsp. turmeric powder.
1/2 tsp. red chilli powder (optional)
Oil for frying - 4 -5 tbsp.
Coriander leaves finely chopped
Salt to taste

The fish:

Sardines are easily available at the local market and best to taste if fresh. Canned fish is a poor substitute. Ensure that the head and the internals are removed. It is important to remove the scales too. The fish should be thoroughly cleaned and washed if it is to be enjoyed. (This is the most painstaking part of the entire recipe. Initially, you may mess it up but hey, if I can master it so can you!!!

Method:

  1. Heat oil in a wok and then add the green chillies, garlic and ginger.
  2. Stir for 15 seconds and then add the chopped onions.
  3. Fry till onions are glossy and transparent.
  4. Add the turmeric powder, chilli powder, and the salt.
  5. Stir for 1 minute and then add the chopped tomatoes.
  6. Keep frying till the tomatoes become slightly mushy.
  7. Add 2 to 3 cups water and bring the curry to a boil for 5 min.
  8. Switch it to sim mode and then gently slip in the sardines into the curry.
  9. Let the fish curry simmer for a while (around 5 mins) and then garnish with chopped coriander leaves.

The curry + rice combination is excellent.
