Moong Dal Vadee (Moongodee)

Ingredients

1 cup moong dal
1 finely chopped onion (divide chopped onion into 2 parts)
1 1/2 large tomatoes
5 cloves of garlic (peeled)
1/2 inch ginger root (peeled)
1/4 tspoon cumin seeds
1/2 tspoon turmeric powder
1/2 tspoon coriander powder
1/2 tspoon garam masala powder
Salt to taste
Oil for frying

Method:

  1. Soak Moong dal in 1 cup water for atleast 2 hours. Then drain the water and grind it in the Mixer/grinder into a paste. (The consistency of the paste must be slightly thicker than the cake batter.)
  2. Mix 1/2 of the chopped onions and very little salt (1/2 tspoon) to the dal paste.
  3. Heat oil in a Kadai or pan and make small round vadees of the paste and drop them in the heated oil. Deep fry them till they are golden brown. Do not over fry. Keep them aside and prepare the gravy.

Gravy:

  1. Heat the oil (about 2 tablespoons) in a pan and add cumin seeds in it. Wait till they splutter and then add the other half of chopped onions in it. Saute the onions till golden brown or soft.
  2. In the mean time grind Tomatoes, garlic and ginger in the mixer/grinder. Then add this paste to the golden brown onions.
  3. Add salt (to taste), Turmeric powder, corriander powder, garam masala to the pan and cook for about 15 minutes.
  4. Once the tomato paste and spices are cooked add 1 1/2 cup of water to it and bring it to a boil.
  5. Dip the moong vadee in the gravy and cook for another 5 -10 min. Now its ready to serve with either rice or chapaties.
