Aviayal

Ingredients

Coconut - 1/2 - grated,
Jeera - 3 tbsp.,
Green chillies - 10,
Curry leaves - little

Seasoning :

Coconut oil - 2 tbsp.
Mustard Seeds - 3 tbsp.
Curry leaves - little

Vegetables :

Pumpkin - 1 bowl
Beans - 1 bowl
Carrot - 1 bowl
Aloo - 1 bowl
Drumstick - little
You can also use chou-chou (Bangalore kathirikai)
Curd - preferably sour - 3 bowls
Salt to taste.

Method :

  1. Cut all the vegetables into long thin pieces and cook them with 1 or 2 tbsp. of salt. in a pressure cooker.
  2. Grind all the ingredients
  3. Heat the oil in a deep fry pan and add seasoning items.
  4. Add boiled vegetables and ground coconut, and boil for 5 min.
  5. Add curds and boil for 5-10 min in medium flame.

Aviyal is ready to serve. Serve hot with papad.
