Vadai Curry

Ingredients

Bengal gram dhal - 1 cup
Onion - 2 (cut into small pieces)

For the gravy :

Onion - 4 (cut into thin long pieces)
Garlic - 2 (separate into pods and peel)
Tomatoes - 1/4 kg (blanch in hot water, remove the skin and make it into a paste)

Grind together :

Onion - 1
Ginger - 4 flakes
Red chillies - 5
Green chillies - 5
Ani seeds - 1/2 tsp.
Cardamoms - 3
Cinnamon - 1 inch piece
Cloves - 3
Roasted bengal gram dhal - 1 tbl.sp.

For making vadai :

  1. Soak dhal for 2 hours. Drain grind it Coarsely with enough salt. Mix finely cut onions.
  2. Heat oil in pan make the dough into small round and deep fry. Keep aside. (Do not fry till deep brown, Remove when it turns golden).

Method :

  1. Heat 3 tablespoons of oil in a pan and fry garlic and then onions. When it turns into golden brown in colour, add the ground mixture, fry till oil separates.
  2. Mix tomato paste and fry till good smell comes. Pour 3 cups of water with turmeric powder, Sugar and salt When it starts boiling, add the fried vadas in it and let it boil for a few minutes.
  3. If the water is not enough add little more, otherwise the koftas will absorb the water and gravy will become very thick.
  4. When gravy is quite thick and flowing consistency remove from fire and garnish with cut coriander leaves.
  5. This goes very well with appam, dosai and idlis.
