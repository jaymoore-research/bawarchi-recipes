Paalak-Tofu Curry

Ingredients

Spinach 1 lbs
Tofu 1 pocket
Onion 1 medium size
Tomato 1 medium size
Red Chilli powder to taste
Turmeric 1 pinch
Salt to taste
Cumin seeds 1 pinch
Garlic 2 flakes(optional)
Ginger 1 small piece (optional)
Oil 2 tbsp

Method:

  1. Wash the Spinach thoroughly and add garlic and ginger then let it boil for 5-8 mts or until its soft.
  2. Cool it and then grind to a fine paste alongwith the tomato.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds, when its done, quickly add lengthwise chopped onions , fry them until it turns golden brown.
  4. Add little turmeric powder , chilli powder and salt and then add the spinach-tomato paste. add little water to give it a curry consistency. Let it cook for sometime.
  5. cut Tofu into small square pieces and finally add to the above prepared curry. let it simmer for a while and enjoy the taste.....u can decorate it with coriander or mint leaves if u wish.....
