Potato Pancakes

Ingredients

Potatoes - 500 gms.
Cooking Oil - 1 tbsp.
Pepper powder - 1/2 tsp.
Salt to taste.

Method:

  1. Wash, peel the potatoes & grate them.
  2. Sprinkle pepper and salt over them.
  3. Heat some oil in a frying pan, spread the grated potatoes in the pan in a circular shape
  4. Put a little oil on the sides with a spoon & cook on medium heat.
  5. Turn the sides with the help of a spatula and fry until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Transfer the pancake to a serving dish, serve hot with Tomato Ketchup, any Chutney, Pickles or Sauce.

I am sure you will like it. Happy Cooking!!
