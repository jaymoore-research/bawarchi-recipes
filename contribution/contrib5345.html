Daddhojanam

Ingredients

1 cup rice [any variety]
coriander [also called cilantro]
8-10 tablespoons of yoghurt [or plain curd]
2-3 tablespoons oil
4 green chillies
1/4 tablespoon jeera
1/4 tablespoon mustard seeds
a pinch of asfoetida
salt to taste
a pinch of turmeric powder [optional]
a little butter [optional]
a small piece of ginger
2-3 cloves of garlic [optional]

Method:

  1. Cook the rice,adding the required amount of water [generally 2 cups water for 1 cup rice],either in a rice cooker or in a pressure cooker.
  2. After the rice is done ,take a bigger bowl or a plate with edges or an aluminum foil dish and then collect the cooked rice into it.
  3. Take a small pan and to it add 2-3 tablespoons oil and set the heat to medium.
  4. To it add 4 cut green chillies,a pinch of asfoetida,2-3 cloves of cut garlic [optional],small pieces of cut ginger,1/4 tablespoon jeera,1/4 tablespoon mustard seeds,a pinch of turmeric powder [optional] and a little butter [optional].Leave it on the stove for about 2 minutes or till the mustard seeds begin to crackle.
  5. Take this hot mixture and add it to the rice which we have already cooked.Then add 8-10 tablespoons of plain yoghurt or curd] and finally salt to taste.
  6. Then take a spoon or use your clean hand to mix all the contents till the salt mixes properly.Decorate it with cut cilantro [coriander].
  7. Daddhojanam is ready to be served.It is a full meal in itself ,otherwise it can be served as a side dish.
