Butter Chicken

Ingredients

Chicken 2 thighs or 4 drumsticks.
Onion 1 large
Garlic 3-4 cloves
Ginger a small piece
Butter half stick
Tomato 3-4 big
Whole/whipped Cream one small
Salt, Red Chili powder, Dry Corriander, Garam masala (according to taste).

Method :

  1. Thoroughly clean chicken and keep aside.
  2. Cut the garlic and ginger into very fine pieces (or make a paste).
  3. Cut the onion half and then length wise (like you make for bhindi).
  4. Make a paste of the tomatoes.
  5. Now heat the butter in a non-stick pan and add onion, garlic, ginger, chicken and all masalas.
  6. Cover it with the lid and let it simmer for about 20-25 minutes.
  7. Once you see that the chicken is done (it will have it's own water in which it will be cooked) add the tomato paste to it.
  8. Let the tomato paste get fully absorbed (that means no water left).
  9. Now add a little water (if you wish to eat with rice).
  10. After about 5-6 boils add whole cream to it.
  11. Wait for about 5 minutes and put off the gas.

Enjoy eating with nan or matar pulao.
