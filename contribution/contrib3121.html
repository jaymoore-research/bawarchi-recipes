Mee Curry (Noodle)

Ingredients

600 gm Yellow Noodles
1 cup Vegetarian Curry Paste (see recipe below)
2 sprigs Curry Leaves
2 stalks Lemon Grass
60 gm Tofu puffs (tofu pok) sliced
500 ml Coconut Milk
300 ml Water or stock
1/2 tsp sugar
salt to taste

Garnishing:

100 gm Cucumber, shredded
100 gm Pineapple, shredded
50 gm Bean sprouts,
2 fried firm Tofu, cut thinly
Fried Shallots
Chopped Spring Onions

Method:

  1. Combine curry paste, curry leaves, lemon grass, coconut milk and water in a pot. Bring to a boil in low heat. Season with salt and sugar before adding tofu puffs. Simmer for 30 minutes or gravy thicken abit.
  2. Meanwhile cut and prepare the vegetables for garnishing.
  3. To serve assemble individual portions of noodles about 150 gm in a bowl top with the various vegetables then scoop enough hot gravy over it.

Vegetarian Curry Paste:

100 gm Red Fresh Chillies, 20 gm Dried Chillies, 50 gm Candlenuts, 30 gm Coriander seeds, 10 gm fresh Turmeric Root, 4 gm Curry Leaves, 2 stalks Lemongrass and 1/4 cup Oil.
Blender all the ingredients into a fine paste. Pour into a wok and fry into a fragrant, adding oil if too dried.
