Ginger Fish

Ingredients

1 pound Fish (Salmon or Catfish) cut into 1 inch cubes
1 large Onion
3 tablespoons Soya Sauce
1/2 cup finely ground Ginger
1 tsp Red Chilli powder
4 Green Chillies slit lengthwise
1/4 tsp Cumin
1/4 tsp Turmeric
1 tsp finely cut Coriander leaves
Salt to taste
Oil for frying - 4 tablespoons

Method:

  1. Wash and Clean fish cubes.
  2. Marinate fish with 2 tablespoons soya sauce, 3 tsp ginger, salt and turmeric. Set aside for 1/2 hour.
  3. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin, once it splutters add finely cut onion.
  4. Fry onion till its light brown. Now, add red chilli powder, slit green chillies and the remaining ground ginger. Fry till oil oozes out.
  5. Add soya sauce and the marinated fish. Cover and Cook on medium flame till fish is well done. Add salt to taste.
  6. Garnish with coriander leaves.

Serve with Chappathi or Rice.

This recipe can be alternated with Chicken or plain Potatoes. Enjoy.

Note: Soya sauce has some amount of sodium , keep this in mind before adding salt.
