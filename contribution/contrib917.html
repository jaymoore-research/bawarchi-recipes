Paruppu Usili

Ingredients

Beans - 150 gms
Thoor dal -1/2 cup
Chana dal - 1/4 cup
Salt
Red chillies - 3 or 4
Asafoetida
Oil - 2 teaspoons

Method:

  1. Soak the dals in water for one hour
  2. Cut the beans and cook it.
  3. Drain and grind the dals with salt, red chillies and asafoetida. It need not be a smooth paste.
  4. In a kadai, heat up the oil. Add mustard. When it sprinkles, add the ground dals and fry till they turn slightly red. Add more oil when it begins to stick to the vessel.
  5. Add the cooked beans and mix well.

Vegetables like snow peas, cabbage, cauliflower can be used in place of beans. Have as side-dish with rice.

Cooking time: 20 minutes excluding soaking.
