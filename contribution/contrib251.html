Green Chicken

Ingredients

Chicken : 1/2 kg
Green onions : 4 bunch, finely chopped
Tomatoes : 3 finely chopped
Green chillies : 3-4 depends on you, finely chopped
Red onion : one
Ginger-Garlic paste : 1/2 tsp
Coriander : 1/4 of bunch
Oil : 3tbsp
Salt&Red; chilly powder according to taste

Ingredients to marinade:

Soya sauce : 4 tsp
Vinegar : 3 tsp
Ginger-Garlic paste : 1 tsp
Salt

Method

  1. Marinade chicken for 2 hrs.
  2. In a pan heat oil and add thinly sliced onions, when onions becomes brown add chicken and ginger-garlic paste.
  3. And fry chicken until all juice dries out.
  4. Add green onions, tomatoes, green chillies, salt & red chilly and cook on medium heat. You can put the lid but keep looking the chicken.
  5. When you see that chicken is done and onions, tomatoes are well mixed add coriander and cook for more 5-7 min.
  6. Serve with chapati.
