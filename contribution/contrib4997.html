Vegetable Stuffed Chapatti

Ingredients For Chapatti:

200 gm Wheat Flour
1 tsp Oil
125 ml Water

Ingredients For Filling:

1 tsp Fennel powder
1 Onion thinly sliced
2 cm Ginger, minced
2 Garlic, minced
2 Green Chillies, sliced
3 tsp Meat Curry Powder / curry powder
350 gms Cauliflower, cut into small pieces
3 medium Potatoes, boiled, peeled 7 diced
100 gms Peas, softened in hot water
Salt to taste

Method to make Chapatti:

  1. Put flour, oil & water in a bowl. Knead to form a smooth dough, leave aside for one hour.
  2. Divide into 8 balls, roll each ball into chapatti shape.
  3. Heat the tava, and cook the chapatti from both the sides with little bit of oil. Keep aside.

Method to make the Filling:

  1. Heat 2 tsp of oil and fry fennel for about 30 seconds.
  2. Add onion, ginger, garlic, chillies and fry for 30 seconds.
  3. Add all other ingredients and stir-fry for 15 minutes, until mixture is quite dry.
  4. Put tbsps of filling on chapatti end, roll up tightly. Serve.
