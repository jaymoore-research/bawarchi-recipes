Seven Cups

Ingredients

Take a cup or bowl o your choice.Be sure to use the same cup for all the ingredients!

1 cup Besan (bengal gram flour).
1 cup Milk (previously boiled)
2 cups Sugar.
1 cup Ghee (clarified butter).
1 cup grated Coconut.

Method:

  1. Place a heavy bottom pan on stove top. First put ghee to it. After a minute, put milk and sugar. After a minute, put besan and coconut powder.
  2. Keep stirring the mixture on a medium flame until you get a thick consistency like for a mithai.
  3. Put off the fire and immediately pour the mixture to a greased plate (greased with a spoon of ghee).
  4. Cut them to squarish or diamond shape. Let it cool.
  5. Garnish with dry fruits (optional). And that's it. Your seven sup (6 cup) is ready. Isn't it delicious? Enjoy it with your family.
