Prawns in butter

Ingredients

1/2 kg prawns cocktail
1 cup chopped onions
1 cup peeled and chopped tomatoes
2 tablespoon butter
1-2 bay leaves
1 teaspoon cumin seeds
1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
1/4 teaspoon black pepper powder
1/4 teaspoon gharam masala
1/2 teaspoon tabasco sauce
1/2 teaspoon ginger paste
1/4 teaspoon pepper powder
1/4 teaspoon dhania powder
1/4 teaspoon chilly powder
1/4 teaspoon vinegar
2 green chillies
fresh corriander for garnishing
salt to taste
1 lemon

Method

  1. Peel and clean the prawns very well and then put some salt, tabasco sauce,pepper powder 1/2 lemon juice 1/4 garlic paste and vinegar keep aside for 20 - 30 minutes.
  2. In a pan heat butter and fry the cumin seeds, bay leaves and slitted chillies add onions fry till light put 1/4 teaspoon of garlic paste, add marinated prawns cook for a while then add tomatoes cook till it softenes then add turmeric powder,chilli powder garam masala and dhania powder cover and cook till water from prawns dries and prawns are ready squeeze juice of 1/2 lemon and garnish with corriander leaves.

Serve with rice or parathas

Cooking time approx: 25 minutes
