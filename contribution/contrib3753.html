I am Shubha. This is a recipe for Green Mango Rice. It is usually prepared on Ugadi (Telugu New Year) day for lunch in our home. It is a mixed rice dish, so you can prepare the masala and mix it with cooked rice as and when required.
Note: The masala, if fried with enough oil and refrigerated properly, will keep for almost a year, so you can make a lot of this when green mangoes are in season and enjoy it throughout the year!

Green Mango Rice

Ingredients

1 medium Green Mango grated (you can leave the skin)
10 - 12 Green Chillies slit in half (increase or decrease depending on taste)
1 tsp Mustard seeds
2 tsps Urad Dhal
2 tsps Channa Dhal
10-12 Curry leaves
3 tblsps Oil
1 cup Cooked and Cooled White Rice.
Cilantro/Coriander for garnish

Method:

  1. Heat oil in a kadai. Add the mustard seeds. When the seeds stop popping, add the Urad Dhal and Channa Dhal and fry till they turn golden brown in color.
  2. Add the grated green mango the slit green chillies and curry leaves to the kadai at this point. Fry this mixture till the mango is well cooked, and the oil starts to leave the sides of the kadai. Add more oil if required.
  3. Once this masala is done, you can store this in the refridgerator for use when required.

To proceed:

Mix in about 2 tblspns of this masala into the cooked rice. Add some lime juice if the mango mixture is not tangy enough for you. If the green chillies have lost their punch, you can fry some green chillies in oil and add to this. If the mixed rice is very dry, add some hot oil to it. You can add a pinch of sugar to enhance the flavor. Mix well and garnish with finely chopped cilantro/coriander leaves.
