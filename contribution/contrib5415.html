Bagare Baingan

Ingredients

tbsp. oil
2 tbsp. ginger garlic paste
1/4th tsp. turmeric powder
3/4th tsp. chilli powder
1/2 cup thick tamarind pulp
1 large onion roasted - peel and grind

Roast separately :
1/2 cup copra
1 tbsp. groundnuts
1 tbsp. sesame seeds
1 tbsp. corriander seeds
1/2 tsp. cummin seds
3 green chilies
and a bunch of corriander leaves to be include when grinding Method:

  1. Make two slits in each brinjal from base towards stalk end without separating quarters.
  2. Leave the calyx and a bit of the stalk end on. Soak the brinjals in salted water.
  3. Heat oil, sqeeze the brinjals to remove excess water, drop into hot oil, shake the pan from time to time and lift out brinjals when they change colour.
  4. Reheat oil and seasoning of a pinch of summin seed and a spring of curry leaves.
  5. Add ginger garlic paste abd fry without burning.
  6. Add ground masala powdered spices, ground onion and fry till masala leaves the pan and oil separates.
  7. Add the brinjals and fry for a few minutes, then add salt and tamarind pulp and sufficient water to cook the brinjals till tender but firm. Simmer till oil floats on surface.

Serve hot or cold.
