Capsicum Rice with Pecan Nuts

Ingredients

1 cup rice (washed and soaked in 2 cups water, for at least 2 hours in a heatproof pan)
1 medium red capsicum chopped small
1 small onion finely chopped
Optional 1 tbsp curly leaf parsley finely chopped
2 tbsp oil
Jeera 2 tsp
3 tbsp Pecan nuts coarsely powdered
2 tbsp Soya sauce
Salt and pepper to taste

Method

  1. Heat the soaked rice till water is nearly boiling
  2. At same time, heat oil in a hob/microwave proof casserole
  3. Put in jeera, add onions and saute
  4. Add capsicum and optional parsley, lower flame, cover the casserole with lid and half cook the capsicum
  5. Add warmed rice with the same water to the mixture, add very little salt (as soya sauce contains salt) and pepper to taste
  6. Cook on fast flame, till boiling, then lower heat, cook till half the water is absorbed
  7. Add the pecan nuts, soya sauce, mix gently, cover the casserole and continue cooking for few more minutes
  8. Remove from hob and microwave the rice for 2-3 minutes, till cooked and fluffy.

Serve hot.

Tastes absolutely delicious on its own, with the herbal flavours smelling wonderful and the red capsicum imparting a pleasing colour. Or serve with Raita or Tomato Chutney. All in all, it takes about 20 minutes to cook.
