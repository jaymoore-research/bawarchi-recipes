Tasty Brinjal Chutney

Ingredients

Brinjal- one large
Green chillis - 4
Tamarind - a small lime size
A pinch of hing
Salt to taste
Coriander leaves - one small bunch

For Tempering:

Oil- 3 tsp.
Mustard seeds- 1/2 tsp.
Red chilli (optional)- 2 or 3
A pinch of asafoetida (optional)

Method:

  1. Cut brinjal into small pieces and cook in very little water.
  2. Cool and grind with tamarind, coriander leaves, green chillis, hing and salt to a smooth paste.
  3. Temper with mustard, red chillis and asafoetida in oil.
