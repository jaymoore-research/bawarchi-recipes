Tangy Yam

Ingredients

500 gms Yam (sooran)
1/4 cup grated fresh Coconut
3 tblsps concentrated Tamarind Juice
1/2 tsp Mustard seeds
1/2 tsp Chana dal
1 tsp Jeera
3 Green Chillies
1/2" Ginger piece
3 tblsps of Cooking Oil
A few Curry Leaves

Method:

  1. Remove skin from yam, cut into small cubes and wash thoroughly.
  2. Use a steel sieve and steam it in a pressure cooker - keep only till one whistle.
  3. Remove and cool in a plate for 10 minutes.
  4. Grind coconut, jeera, ginger and chillies coarsely, without adding water.
  5. In a hard bottomed pan, pour oil, put in the mustard seeds and after it starts spluttering, add the chana dal. Add curry leaves. Add ground coconut paste and fry for some time. Then add the tamarind concentrate. Let it get cooked for about two minutes.
  6. Add the steamed Yam and pour in sufficient water to make a gravy of required consistency. Add salt to taste and turmeric powder.

It can be had with Rotis or with hot steamed Rice.
