Ingredients

Yoghurt 2 kg.
Saffron 1 tsp.
Milk, cold 2 tbsps.
Green Cardamom power 1 1/4 tsp.
Pistachois 1/4 cup.
Almonds 1/4 cup.
Sugar To taste.

Method:

  1. Hang the yoghurt in a muslin cloth, in a cool place until all the liquid from the yoghurt has drained off.(approx. 3 hours)
  2. Dissolve the saffron in the milk and set aside.
  3. Blanch the almonds, cool abd cut into slivers.
  4. Cut the pistachois into slivers and power the sugar.
  5. Tie a cleen muslin cloth tighttly over the mouth of a large clean vessel. The muslin cloth thus acts as a sieve, and should not sag in the middle. Use a clean thick string to tie the cloth over the vessel. Transfer the thick yoghurt along with the sugar over the muslin cloth, in small quantities.
  6. For every cup of thick yoghurt, add 3/4 cup of sugar. Rub the above mixture firmly over the cloth, till the whole mixture is sieved.
  7. Add the dissolved saffron, almonds, pistachois and cardamom power to the sieved mixture. Mix well and transfer to a glass bowl and refrigerate till cold.
