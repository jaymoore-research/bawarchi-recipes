Cabbage Rice

Ingredients

Basmati Rice : 1 1/2 cup
Cabbage : 1 (medium)
Green Peas : 1 cup
Onions 2 (medium)
Tomato : 1 (medium)
Green chillies : 2
Garlic 5 cloves
Ginger : a small piece
Jeera : 1 tsp
Dhania (coriander) powder : 2 tblsp
Tumeric powder : a pinch
Red chilli Powder : 1 tsp
Peanuts 1 tblsp
Cilantro (coriander leaves) a small bunch
Oil 2 tblsp
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Cook the Rice and place it in a plate, allow it to cool.
  2. Shred the Cabbage and add water, a pinch of salt, a pinch of turmeric powder and boil it.
  3. Soak the peas overnight and cook it.
  4. Chop onions, tomatoes, garlic, chillies and ginger.
  5. In a Big Frying pan, add oil and jeera, chillies, ginger, allow it to splutter.
  6. Add the chopped Onions, when it turns light golden brown in color add tomatoes and Garlic. Add now the cooked Cabbage, peas.
  7. Add Dhania powder, red chilli powder and toss it together and add Salt. When it is almost done, in a small frying pan, add 1 tsp of oil, and fry the peanuts add it.
  8. Add the Cabbage Masala to the rice and mix well, Garnish with Cilantro.

Onion Raita will be combination for this Rice.

Time for preparation: 30 minutes (excluding cooking the rice)
Servings: 4
