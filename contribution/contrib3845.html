Rasila Karela

Ingredients

Karela 1 lb.
Besan  1/2 cup
Sugar 1/2 teaspoon
Salt as per taste
Chilli powder 1 teaspoon(or per taste)
Haldi (Turmeric powder)
Coriander powder 4 spoon
Oil

Method:

  1. First of all peel karela and make one cut in it and boil just for 2 minutes in boiling water. Drain water. Let it cool and then gentely squeez the water out.
  2. On other side in kadai heat oil to fry besan. When you smell aroma, put all dry masala (chilli powder, haldi-dhania-salt-sugar) in besan and mix it very well. Since besan is already hot, you don't need to fry this masala for too long. Let this masala cool.
  3. Now stuff this masala in karela.
  4. Take kadai, heat oil (about 1/2 cup) and fry stuffed karela in it. When skin is brown take it out. Serve hot.
