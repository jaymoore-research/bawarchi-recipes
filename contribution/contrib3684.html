Seviyan (Bombino Vermicilli) Upma

Ingredients

Vermicilli (Bombino) / Seviyan - 1 Pkt
Ghee - 2 tbsps
Salt - as per the taste
Potatoes - 2 (Cut into small peices)
French Beans - a handful
Green Peas - a handful
Carrot - 1 (Cut into small pieces)
Tomatoes - 2 (Cut into very fine pieces)
Mustard Seeds (Rai) - 1 tsp
Ulud Dal - 1 tsp
Chana Dal - 1 tsp
Red Chilli - 2 nos
Kadi Patta - a few
Green chillies - 4 (Cut finely)
Turmeric - a pinch
Oil - 1 1/2 tbsp
Hot Water - 2 glasses

Method:

  1. Roast Vermicilli in a Kadhai with some salt till a little red in colour.
  2. Add 2 tbsp of ghee to it. Roast for some more time.
  3. Once it gets reddish in colour add 3/4 cup of hot water. Mix it very well. (Remember that the water should be less than the vermiclli other wise it will become very soggy and sticky). The seviyan will be seperate. Remove it from the gas in one minute.)
  4. Keep it aside in a flat plate.
  5. In the same kadhai add oil till it becomes hot. Now add mustard seeds, after they burst add Urud dal, Chana dal, Red Chillies, Kadi Patta and mix well. When its pale red in colour, remove it.
  6. Add all the vegetables, except tomatoes. Mix well. Now add the green chillies, turmeric, salt, and mix well. Add the tomatoes and fry for 1 min. Then add water, the water should be just as much as the vegetables, not less. A little more is alright. Keep it for the 2 mins, by then all the vegetables would have been cooked.
  7. Now add the Seviyan. Mix very well. Keep it for a minute and then put it aside. YUM! YUM! Its tastes great! It can be eaten with any Chutney or Sauce.

Time: 20 mins
Serves: 4 people.
