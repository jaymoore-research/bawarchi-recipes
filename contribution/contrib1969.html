Vegetable Pulao

Ingredients

Basmati rice or any other Good quality rice cooked and allowed to cool. See to it that each grain is seperate - 2 cups

To Grind :

Cilantro or coriander leaves - 1/4 bunch
Mint leaves - 3 to 4 strands (Optional, this is for the typical aroma for pulao)
Green chillies - 5 to 6
Ginger - 1/2 inch piece
Jeera - 1/2 Tsp
Coriander seeds - 4 to 5
Cinnamon - 2 sticks
Cloves - 3 to 4
Cardamom - 2 to 3
Fresh grated Coconut - 1/2 cup
Ghee - 3 Tbsp
Salt to taste

Vegetables include Beans, Carrots, Cauliflower florets, Green Peas and Onions - 1

Method :

  1. Cook the veggies except onions till soft.
  2. Crush a stick of Cinnamon, 2-3 cloves.
  3. Heat ghee in a kadai, add the crushed cinnamon and cloves, add chopped onions and saute till the onions turn transculent.
  4. Now, boil the cooked veggies with required amount of salt, add the ground masala and the sauted onions and boil the mixture on a medium flame till the raw smell of the masala is gone.
  5. Pour this mixture on the cooled rice and mix properly and heat this on a very low flame just to make the pulao warm.

Tomato Raitha goes very well with this pulao.
