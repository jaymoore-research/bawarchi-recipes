Pittore

Ingredients

1 cup gram flour
2.5 cups water
0.5 tsp red chilly
Turmeric powder 1 pinch
0.25 tsp salt
1 tsp oil
Mustard seeds

Method

  1. Mix flour with water in a pan.
  2. Avoid lumps.
  3. Add red chilly + turmeric + salt in it & mix.
  4. Heat on medium flame stirring well continuously.
  5. Cook till it is semisolid.
  6. Grease a plate and apply the hot paste evenly on it.
  7. On cooling cut into long strips 4" wide & roll.
  8. Cut the strip rolls into 1" wide pieces.
  9. Heat oil in a pan & splutter mustard seeds.
  10. Add the rolls in it 7 mix gently.
  11. Serve with chapaties hot

Serves : 4
Shelflife : best fresh
Can last three days in a refrigerator.

PS : To shorten the cooking time cut into square pieces directly without rolling
