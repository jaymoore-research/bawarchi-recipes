Seven Cups

Ingredients

Basin flour 2 cup
Sugar 2 cup
Coconut grated 2 cup
Ghee 1 cup
Cardomom Powder 1 tsp
Raisins 1/4 cup
Cashew nuts 1/4 cup

Method

  1. Take sugar in a container and add water to it and keep for boiling. After 5 minutes put little bit ghee to it. When sugar and water mixture calmest like thread add basin flour to it.
  2. After 2 minutes add coconut and cardamom powder to it. Mix it well and add some more ghee.
  3. After mixture separates from bottom of the container, take out the mixture and spread it on a tray greased with ghee.
  4. Cut it into diamond shaped pieces and for decoration you can use raisins and Cashew nuts.
