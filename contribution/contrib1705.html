Mint Pulao

Ingredients

Grind to a paste the following 4 ingredients with a little water
1 large bunch of fresh Mint
1 cup of freshly grated Coconut
A few Green Chillies according to taste
3 - 4 Garlic pods
2 cups of Basmati rices washed and cooked (the grains should be separate and not sticky, for 2 cups rice use 3 cups water to cook after soaking in water for 1/2 an hour)
1 large Potato peeled and cubed
1 large Onion thinly sliced lenghtwise
Whole Garam Masalas for seasoning
Bay leaves, Red chilli powder, garam masala powder 1/2 teaspoon each
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. In a heavy bottomed pan put a little oil and add bay leaves and the whole garam masalas.
  2. When it is slightly brown add the onion and fry till golden brown, then add the potatoes and cook it on slow fry till done (cover if necessary)
  3. Now Add the mint paste and fry for a few minutes till slightly dry.
  4. Add the chilli powder, garam masala, salt and mix gently.
  5. Leave it to cool. Now spoon by spoon add in the rice and mix with a light hand. Be careful that the grains do not break.

Serve with any of your favourite Raitas and Pappads.
