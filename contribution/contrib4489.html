Kerala Appam

Ingredients

Raw Rice One and a half glass
Coconut half, or equivalent amount of coconut powder
Yeast 10 to 12 numbers
Sugar one teaspoon
Salt half a teaspoon

For Seasoning:

10 to 12 Mustard seeds
4 to 5 Curry Leaves
Small piece of Onion chopped finely
2 dry Red Chilli pieces
Fry all this in 2 teaspoon of coconut oil

Method:

  1. Grind one and a half glass of raw rice with half a coconut or one cup coconut powder or coconut milk. Add yeast , one spoon sugar and salt with the paste and mix well with the paste. Keep it overnight or till it ferments. Too much of fermentation will spoil the taste.
  2. Then make the appam in a pan. Keep it covered. Do not turn the appam on the pan as we do in the case of Dosa. Keep the flame in the sim. Kadala (chick pea) curry, Chicken curry, Egg curry and dry coconut chammanthy (chutney) go well with appam. Season the chutney before serving.
