Methi Pappad Sabjee

Ingredients

1. Methi 2-3tsp
2. masala pappads(big) 2nos.
3. Jeera 1/2 tsp
4. Tomato 1 large
5. Curd 1/2 cup
6. Haldi 1/4 tsp
7. Coriander powder 1/2 tsp
8. Chilly powder 1/2 tsp

Method:

  1. Soak methi in water for one hour. Break the pappads into pieces and keepaside.
  2. Heat oil in a frying pan add jeera fry for a while and add the methi after the water is drained out. Fry for 2-3 min. Now add the tomatoes and curd. Fry very well until the water of the cud is completely absorbed.
  3. Then add 6, 7 and 8 fry for one sec. Now add water one cup and add papppad pieces and salt according to the taste. Boil for sometime until the pappad is done.

Serve with hot basmati rice or hot ghee rice.
