Broccoli Rice

Ingredients

1 small Broccoli cleaned and chopped
1 cup Rice, soak for 10 minutes and drain well
1 medium size Onion, chopped into thin slices
1 Bay Leaf
1 tea spoon Ghee OR Butter
2-3 tea spoons Oil
5-6 Green Chilies (more if you want it spicy)
1 tea spoon Ginger paste
1 tea spoon Garlic paste
Salt to taste

Powder the following:

2-3 Cloves
1 inch Cinnamon stick
1 pod Cardamom

Method:

  1. Heat oil in a vessel, large enough to cook the rice. Drop the bay leaf in it.
  2. Add the sliced onions, ginger paste and garlic paste. Fry till the onions are soft, and the raw smell of the ginger garlic paste is gone.
  3. Add the chopped broccoli, salt, masala powder and slit green chilies. Fry about 5 minutes. Add the ghee OR butter at this point.
  4. Add the drained rice and fry till the rice starts to turn brown. Add 2.5 cups water to the rice and cover. Keep stirring once in a while. Add more water if you feel the rice needs to cook some more. When the rice is done, remove from fire. You can eat this dish with Onion, Tomato and Cucumber Raita.
