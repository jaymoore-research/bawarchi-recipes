Mushroom Peas Combo

Ingredients

1 tin Mushroom
200 gms Peas
2 or 3 Onions
Paste of Ginger Garlic and Green Chillies
Dhania Powder
Jeera Powder
1 small tin Tomato Puree or pulp of 4 tomatoes
1/2 cup Curd
Paste of 10 to 15 Cashewnut

Method

  1. Boil mushroom and peas and keep aside.
  2. Cut onions finely and cook till golden brown.
  3. Add the paste of ginger garlic and green chilies.
  4. Add haldi, jeera and dhania powder.
  5. Add tomato puree and salt cook for a while. Add a little water when the water starts boiling, add all the ingredients are well mixed add the boiled mushroom and peas.
  6. Add 1/2 cup curd fresh curd. Simmer for a while.
  7. Add a paste of 10 to 15 cashewnuts. Simmer for another 5 mins.
  8. Garnish with coriander and finely chopped green chillies [optional]

Enjoy!
