Masala Rice

Serves: 4,
Cooking time: approx. 20 min,
Preparation time: 5 min.

Ingredients

3 cups rice (cooked)
3-4 tomatoes chopped into small peices
1/4 tsp Cumin seeds
1/4 tsp Mustard seeds
1/4 tsp Urad dal
1/4 tsp Chana Dal
1 dried red chilli
Ginger/garlic paste (optional) Salt
Turmeric powder
Garam masala/curry powder or any masala mix that you have (e.g vangibath masala etc)

Method:

  1. In a large pan, heat some oil.
  2. When the oil is hot, add the cumin, mustard, urad dal, chana dal and red chilli until sputtering.
  3. Add the ginger/garlic paste , the chopped tomatoes, Masala powder and turmeric powder.
  4. Cover and cook for about 3 minutes.
  5. Now add the cooked rice and some salt and mix it so that the tomato base coats the rice fully.
  6. The tomato mixture should not be too watery otherwise the rice will become soggy.
  7. If you have some cilantro, chop and add for extra taste.

This goes really well with some onion or potato raita.
