Carrot Rice

Ingredients

1) Basmati rice - 2 cups
2) Carrots - 4
3) Onion - 1, medium sized
4) Coconut - grated, 4 tablespoons
5) Green Chillies - 5
6) Poppy seeds - 1 tablespoon.
7) Turmeric powder - A pinch.
8) Mustard and oil for seasoning
9) Ghee (Optional)
10) Cashewnuts (Optional)

Method:

  1. Boil rice (with salt) separately and cool it in a large tray.
  2. In a pan, take a little oil, and fry poppy seeds and chopped onions.
  3. Make a paste of the fried poppy seeds, onions, coconut and green chillies.
  4. Grate the carrots.
  5. Heat oil in pan, and add mustard seeds. Add the grated carrots, the paste, turmeric and salt. Cover it and cook till the carrots are done (approx. 10 minutes). You can add ghee if desired.
  6. Mix the cooked carrots and rice. You can fry cashewnuts in ghee and add to rice if desired.
