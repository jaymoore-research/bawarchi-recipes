Dal Halwa

Ingredients

1 cup moong dal
1 cup sugar
1 cup ghee
2 cups Half and Half(thick milk)
1 big spoon besan
1 cup water
Almonds
Cardammom powder
cashews

Method:

  1. soak dal for 4-5 hours and then grind it well.
  2. Make a syrup of sugar and water.Add saffron flakes to it.
  3. Heat ghee in a heavy saucepan and add dal paste.
  4. Add Gram Flour
  5. Fry till ghee separates
  6. Add Half and Half. when it dries up add sugar syrup little by little.
  7. When it dries up add cardammom powder, cashews and almonds.

Sounds delicious. isn't it?
