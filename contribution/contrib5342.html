Tamarind Rice

Ingredients

Rice 2 cups
chana dal 3 tbsp
urad dal 2 tbsps
Mustard seeds 2 tsp.
Sesame seeds 3 tbsp
Tamarind pulp 1/4 cup
Sugar 2tsp
Oil 1 cup
Butter 3tsp
Green chilies. 6 nos
Ginger 1" piece
Whole red chilies 6 nos..
peanuts 1/4 cup .
Turmeric powder 1/2 tsp
Salt To taste

Preparation Method:

  1. Wash the rice and add 4 cups of water and cook the rice.
  2. Take tamarind pulp add salt to taste and 2tsp sugar and 1/2 tsp turmeric powder and put it on the stove in low than the tamarind comes like paste that paste add to the rice.
  3. Grind it separately 1 tsp Mustard seeds, 3tsp sesame seeds like thick paste.. Chop it Ginger, Green chilies, finely.
  4. Heat 1cup of oil and add red chilies,1tsp mustard seeds, 2tsp chana dal and 2 tsp urad dal and peanuts saute for 2-3 minutes till dals have a brown colour. And than add chopped ginger& chilies and stir fry for = a minute. And than add the paste of mustard seeds and sesame seeds.
  5. Than add all these to rice and add 3tsp butter.Tamarind rice is ready.
  6. This dish is one of the favorites for East Indians. My husband like this dish
