Badam Milk Shake

Ingredients

3 cups of milk
1 cup of badam
1 cup of yogurt/curds
1 cup of sugar
5 cinnamon
2 cups of water

Method:

  1. Grind cinnamon, badam, sugar all together
  2. After put milk & curds into the mixie, grind well, along with the above powder.
  3. Put water if u do not want it served thick.
  4. Serve the chilled badam milk shake.
