Vegetable Mushroom

Ingredients

One LB fresh mushroom
1 medium capsicum
1 medium onion
1 medium tomato
1 green chilli in small pieces
1 tsp oil
2 tsp red chill powder
2 tsp dry coriander powder
1 tsp cumin-mustard seeds
1/2 tsp Turmeric
salt to test

Method:

  1. Wash mushroom neatly. If mushroom is small keep them like that.other wise cut into two pieces.
  2. cut onions, capsicum, tomato in square pieces
  3. put oil in a pan.
  4. Add cumin and mustard seeds
  5. Add green chills, onions
  6. Fry onions till golden brown
  7. Add capsicum cook until soft
  8. Add tomato cook little bit
  9. Now add mushroom, put turmeric, red chill powder, coriander powder, salt
  10. Mix it well.
TIP:

Mushroom does not take long time to cook.

If you would like to make this dish for lunch /dinner, make this vegetable before half an hour.

Mushroom contains water and this will come out as you cook and hence it should be prepared before half an hour.
