Murmura Snack

Ingredients

Murmura
Onion : 1
Green Chilli : 4
Coriander Leave: 1/2 to 1 bunch depending on the quantity of Murmura
Grated Coconut: 2 tbsp
Fried Channa Dal: 4 tbsp
1/4 ball size Tamarind juice.

Seasoning Items:

3 tbsp Oil
1/4 tsp Mustard Seeds
1/4 tsp Jeera
Curry Leaves
Red Chilli (optional)
Turmeric powder: 1/4 tsp
Salt To taste

Method:

  1. Soak Murmura in water for about 10 minutes and star in the water uisng a collander. Do not over soak. They will become a paste if over soaked.
  2. Chop onions into small pieces and keep aside.
  3. Grind together Green Chillies, Coriander leaves, grated coconut, fried channa dal. Don't put water. It should be dry like a powder. (You can use less chilli if you don't want it to be spicy!)
  4. Take Oil in a kadai and when it is hot, put the seasoning items. After they splutter add onions and saute them well till they are golden brown. Now add the grinded powder from the step#3 and fry for 1 minute. Add Salt and turmeric powder to the mixture. Fry it for 2 minutes. Now add the drained Murmura and tamrind juice. Mix it well so that the murmura gets coated well with the powder. Add little more oil if necessary. Close the lid and Cook for 5 miutes.
  5. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.

Shelf Life: Fresh

This is a very light dish for breakfast or evening snack. Enjoy!
