Shawarma

Ingredients

For Humus:

1/2 cup boiled chick peas
2 table spoon of white sesame seeds
Salt to taste
1 tbsp Vinegar
Grind the above to make a smooth paste

For Garlic Mayonise

2 whole eggs
1 cup canola/veg oil
2 pods of garlic crushed
salt to taste
2 tbsp vinegar
1/4 tsp black pepper powder
1/4 tsp mustard powder

Whip eggs in a blender till frothy. Add all dry ingredients & vinegar. Whip again for 1 minute. Slowly add the oil & keep whipping till it becomes a fluffy smooth paste & thickens. Never dump the oil.

For Picked Cucumber

1 medium cucumber
1/2 cup vinegar
2 pinches salt
2 pinches mustard powder
Cut cucumber length wise & mix in all the other ingredients. Keep in air tight container for 2 days.

For Frenchfries

1 large potato washed, peeled, cut into long strips & deep fried.

For Filling

2 breasts of chicken deboned
salt to taste
1 tbsp of vinegar
1 tsp pepper
1 tbsp gingergarlic paste
Marinate the chicken in the above over night in the refrigerator. Bake or stem till tender. Shred & grill it or saute in nonstick pan with very little oil. Use a pita bread or phulka.

Method:

  1. Put cooked chicken in the centre length wise.
  2. Keep pickeled cucumbers length wise. Roll it.
  3. Serve hot with french fries, humus, garlic mayonise.

It sounds lengthy but mayonise, cucumbers & humus can be prepared & stored in fridge a week ago. The rest is very easy. I know once you eat it u cant resist making another time. It is a whole meal. When served with fresh mixed fruits juice it's simply great! Happy cooking & happy eating.
