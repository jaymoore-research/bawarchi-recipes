Quark Cake

Ingredients

Base:

60 gms Butter
60 gms Sugar
150 gms Refined Flour
2 tsp. Baking Powder
1 Egg

Filling:

150 gms Butter
150 gms Sugar
3-4 Eggs
1 Kg Quark
1/2 Pack Pudding /Custard Powder
Raisins (Optional)
1 tsp. Lemon Juice (Optional)

Method :

Base:

  1. Melt butter and mix it with sugar.
  2. Add egg and mix thoroughly. Melt butter
  3. Sieve the flour and baking powder and add to the above. Mix well to form a soft dough.

Filling :

  1. Melt butter and mix it with sugar.
  2. Separate the egg yolks and egg whites.
  3. Add the egg yolks to the butter and sugar.
  4. Add lemon juice (Optional).
  5. Add Pudding /Custard and mix well.
  6. Add the Quark and mix thoroughly.
  7. Beat the egg whites nicely and add to the above.
  8. Add the raisins and mix (Optional).

Baking :

Spread the base in the cake pan (circular).
Fill the pan with the filling over the base.
Bake it at 175o (preheated) for 1 1/4 hrs.

Serves: 10-12 pieces (medium)
Suits with Tea
