Quick Chole

Ingredients

Channa 1 cup pressure cooked with salt
Onion 1 small sliced
4-5 cloves
1-2 cinnamon
1 tsp chillie pwd
1/2 tsp turmeric pwd
2 tbsp corriander pwd
1/2 tsp garam masala
Jeera 1 tsp
Ginger garlic paste 1 tsp
Oil 2 tbsp
3-4tbsp tomato puree
Salt to taste

For garnishing
green chillies 4
ginger few cut into long pieces
Corriander leaves - few

Method:

  1. Place a kadai on the stove & add oil into it, when it gets hot add the jeera and ginger garlic paste into it.
  2. Add onion pieces and saute till it gets brown. Pour in the puree and to it add chillie powder, turmeric powder, corriander powder, salt cloves, cinnamon and mix well.
  3. Stir till the oil leaves the sides. Then add the boiled channa and some water to it.
  4. Keep boiling it for 5 mts. Add the garam masala to it. Remove from fire.
  5. Fry the ginger pieces in some oil and garnish Channa with the ginger pieces, chillies & corriander leaves.
