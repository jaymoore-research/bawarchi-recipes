Spicy Methi Puri

Ingredients

1. Wheat Flour - 2 cups
2. Fresh Methi - 1 bunch (finely chopped)
3. Fresh Cilantro Leaves - 1/4 cup (finely chopped)
4. Red Chilli Powder - 2 tsps
5. Cumin Seeds - 1 tsp
6. Asafoetida - 2 pinches
7. Turmeric Powder - 1/4 tsp
8. Salt - according to taste
9. Oil - for deep frying

Method:

  1. Mix the wheat flour with ingredients 2-8.
  2. Add water and make puri dough
  3. Knead well for consistent dough
  4. Keep the dough closed in a container for 15 minutes
  5. Make puris from the dough
  6. Deep fry till light brown
  7. Serve hot spicy puris with Coconut Chutney.
