Egg Chicken

Ingredients

8-10 Chicken pieces
One large Onion : chopped in semi-circular way.
'30 minute Marinade' paste: 6tbsp. OR Garlic paste : 1 1/2 tbsp, juice of half fresh Lemon
Ginger paste: 1 tbsp
Two Eggs
Turmeric powder:1 tbsp.
Dry Red Chilli :4/5
Coriander powder: 3 tbsp.
Fenugreek seeds: 1 tbsp.
Poppy seeds :1 tbsp.
Garam Masala: 3 Cloves, 3 Cardamoms, 3/4 small pieces of Cinnamon.
Sugar : 1 tbsp.
Salt and Ghee.

Method:

  1. Marinate the chicken with the '30 minute Marinade' paste and with ginger, salt, egg and 1 tbsp coriander powder. Or, replace the '30 min..' with garlic paste and lemon juice; add the other mentioned things.
  2. Heat ghee in pressure cooker. Add chopped onion, dry red chilli and cardamoms.
  3. Fry until light brown; add marinated chicken pieces. Fry for 3 minutes.
  4. Mix turmeric powder, rest of the coriander powder, sugar, poppy seeds and fenugreek seeds and garam masala in half cup water. Add it to the chicken. Fry for 2/3 minutes until water is dried up.
  5. Add sufficient water. Pressure cook. When it is done, keep on low flame to thicken the gravy and also add salt as per taste.

Chicken is ready. Happy cooking.
