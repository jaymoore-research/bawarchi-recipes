Garlic Mushroom Appetizer

Ingredients

10-12 button mushrooms
2-3 cloves finely chopped garlic
1tbsp garlic paste
1 large egg
Pinch of salt
1/2Ātsp crushed black pepper
1 teaspoon chili powder
1 cup suji or bread crumbs
Oil to deep fry

For Garlic Sauce

1 cup mayo
3-4 tbsp of Hot n Sweet Tomato Chili Maggi Sauce
Chopped spring onions or parsley for garnishing

Method

  1. Clean mushrooms, pat dry and leave them aside for a few minutes.
  2. For the marinade, beat egg.
  3. Mix garlic paste, salt, pepper, and chili powder with the beaten egg.
  4. Marinate mushrooms in this marinade for about 3-4 hours.
  5. One by one, roll mushrooms in suji or bread crumbs.
  6. Mushrooms should be nicely covered with suji.
  7. Avoid touching the mushroom while rolling it.
  8. For this, take suji in a tall round Ziploc container.
  9. Now put one marinated mushroom in it and briskly roll the container in a circular motion.
  10. This will not only cover mushroom with suji, but also roll the mushroom in the container.
  11. Deep fry these mushrooms by gently placing each one in preheated oil.
  12. Water from mushrooms will sputter. So be careful while frying. Mushrooms should be nicely cooked and the coating should be crispy.
  13. Set aside the mushrooms for a few minutes on a paper to drain oil.
  14. To make the garlic sauce, add finely chopped garlic in mayo. You can also add grated garlic, if you desire.
  15. Further, add Maggi sauce as desired. (If this sauce is not available, add 1 tsp red chili powder to 3 tbsp of tomato ketchup). Check if mayo contains salt.
  16. Your garlic sauce is now ready.
  17. In a serving platter, place fried mushrooms.
  18. On the top of each mushroom put one tbsp of garlic sauce.
  19. Garnish with some finely chopped spring onions on each mushroom or fresh parsley.
