Bhaat Dhokla

Ingredients

Boiled rice- 1 Cup
Gram flour- 3/4 -1 cup
Sour Buttermilk- 2 cups
Green chillies- 4-5 (as per your req.)
Mustard seeds- 1tbsp.
Cumin seeds- 1 tbsp.
Turmeric powder
salt to taste.
Oil- 5-6 big spoon.

Method

  1. Soak the boiled rice in buttermilk for 1/2 hour.
  2. Put oil in a thick bottom vessel.
  3. Then add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, green chillies cut into halves, turmeric powder. Let it splutter.
  4. Then add rice with buttermilk. Let it boil for 2 minutes.
  5. Now keep the flame low & add Gram flour slowly & go on stirring in one direction add salt to taste.
  6. Consider this as done when the mixture loses its side & oil seperates out.
