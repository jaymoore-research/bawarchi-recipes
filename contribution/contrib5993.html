Martabak

Ingredients

1 pkt frozen Spring Roll pastry
Deep or shallow frying facilities.
3 tspns lemongrass finely chopped
1 cup of spring onions or chives (both if you want)
6 tspns of chopped parsley (flat leaf)
2-3 eggs

Combine the following ingredients to a frypan

500 grams mince
3 tbspns peanut oil
2 finely chopped onions
1 tspn coriander
1/2Ātspn ground cumin
1 tspn chilli powder
1/2 tspn ground turmeric or curry powder (both if you want)
2 tspns of salt (or more to taste)

Method

    Ā
  1. Fry till mince is nice and browned then add the following: lemon grass, spring onion, parsley and eggs
  2. Cut Spring roll pastry into four squares. The pastry is very fine. (wet fingers a little but not too much for better working)
  3. Add small amounts of mixture to the centre of squares and fold to triangle sealing the joins with moist fingers.
  4. Deep or shallow fry the little triangle till golden brown. They Brown quickly. (Note: Do not touch them till they have browned or they may fall apart)
  5. Serve straight away. A sweet chilli sauce can add to the delight these little parcels bring.

Enjoy!
