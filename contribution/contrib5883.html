Shimoga Rasam

Ingredients

2 roma tomatoes, and 2 any other kind of tomatoes.
3 hand fullĀcoriander leaves.
1 hand full curry leaves.
1 tea spoon cumin seeds and half tea spoonĀblack pepper freshly powdered.
1 pinch of turmeric and half tea spoon chili powder.
1 clove garlic and one small marble size tamarind.
2 pinch asafoetida powder.

Method

  1. Boil tomatoes with tamarind,Ātwo cups of water, little curry leaves and coriander leaves [you can boilĀ this in micro-oven]
  2. When it isĀcooked then cool it and take off the coriander and curry leaves then make tomato puree from little boiled water and keep it aside.
  3. In a vessel put little oil and put mustard and little zeera,Āfry a little andĀthen turn off Āthe heat
  4. Put crushed garlic and curry leaves and turmeric, chili, pepper, zeera and asafoetida powders
  5. Then turn on the heat fry a little then put puree and all water [you can put more water if you want] and salt, boil for 5 minutes then put finely chopped coriander leaves
  6. If it becomes very sour you can put little jaggery.
