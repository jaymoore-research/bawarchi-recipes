Rock Toast and Quick Dosa

Ingredients

Rock Toast

Sooji - 1/2 cup
Onion - 1 small (diced finely)
Tomato - 1 (diced)
Coriander - finely chopped
Green Pepper - 2-3 (finely cut)
Milk Fat (malai) - 1-1/2 tbsp
Rai or Mustard Seeds
Red Pepper, Salt, Garam Masala - to taste
Bread
oil

Ratio of the veggies and spices can be altered according to taste.

Method :

  1. Mix all the veggies, spices and milk fat in sooji and with the help of water make a thick paste. Keep it for about 10 minutes. Since sooji will rise a bit mix little bit more water if needed.
  2. Take a bread slice and cover one side of it with the batter.
  3. Heat the griddle or frying pan (preferably nonstick to avoid too much usage of oil). Pour 1 tsp of oil and when the oil is hot enough, put 1 pinch of rai in it. When the rai starts sputtering put the bread slice with paste side faced down.
  4. Cook both the sides till done using little bit more oil (if necessary).
  5. Cut the peices in half triangularly and serve hot with Coriander Chutney or Tomato Sauce.

Quick Dosa

Ingredients

Wheat Flour - 1 cup (Ratio should be 1:1 between both the flours)
Rice Flour - 1 cup
Onion - 1 (finely chopped)
Green Pepper - 2 (finely chopped)
Curd/Yogurt - 2 tbsp
Salt, Black Pepper - according to taste.
Oil - to cook

Method :

  1. Mix both the flours, curd, spices (not the veggies) and make a dosa like smooth batter. Keep it for 5 - 10 minutes
  2. Heat the gridle or the frying pan and when hot enough, pour the batter (make the dosa) and immediately put onion and pepper on it when the top side is still uncooked.
  3. With the help of some oil cook both the sides.

Serve hot. Tastes great with any Chutney even with Tomato Sauce. Can use other vegetables (like tomatoes and carrots) too along with onions.
