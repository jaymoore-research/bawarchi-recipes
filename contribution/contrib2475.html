Double Decker Sandwich

Ingredients

Bread slices 36; Amul Butter 1/2 cup.

1st layer:
onion 1 chopped;
grated Cheese 1cup;
Green Chillies 2 chopped;
Coriander Leaves 2 tablespoon;
Salt to taste.

Method: Mix all the ingredients.

2nd layer: Tomato 1 chopped;
Cabbage 1/2 cup;
Cheese Spread 2 tablespoon
Mashed Boiled Peas 1/2 cup;
Mashed Boiled Carrots 1/2 cup;
Salt to taste.

Method: Mix all the ingredients together.

Method for Sandwich:

  1. Apply butter on one side of the bread slices. Take one slice spread the mixture of the 1st layer.
  2. Place the 2nd slice on top. Spread the mixture of the second layer. Place the second layer. Place the third layer. Heat the equity toaster. Put the sandwiches. Lightly toast for 3-4 minutes.
  3. Serve hot with Ketchup and Hot French Fries.
Serves : 12 people.
