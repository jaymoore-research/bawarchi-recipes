Spicy Roast Chicken

Ingredients

1. 50 g cup ground Almonds.
2. 50 g cup Desiccated (shredded) Coconut.
3. 150 ml/ 2/3 cup Oil.
4. 1 medium Onion, finely chopped.
5. 1 tsp fresh Ginger root, chopped.
6. 1 tsp fresh Garlic, crushed.
7. 1 tsp Chilli powder.
8. 1 1/2 tsp Garam Masala.
9. 1 tsp Salt.
10. 150 ml/ 2/3 cup Yogurt.
11. 4 Chicken quarters, skinned.
12. Green Salad Leaves to serve.

To Garnish

13. Fresh Coriander.
14. 1 Lemon, cut into wedges.

Method:

  1. In a heavy-based saucepan, dry roast the ground almonds & coconut & set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a frying pan & fry the onion, stirring, until golden brown.
  3. Place ginger, garlic, chilli-powder, garam-masala & salt in a bowl & mix with the yogurt. Add the almonds & coconut & mix well.
  4. Add onions to the spice mixture, blend & set aside.
  5. Arrange the chicken quarters in the bottom of a heat proof dish. Spoon the spice mixture over the chicken sparingly.
  6. Cook in a pre-heated oven, 160o C/ 425 oF/ Gas Mark 3, for 35-45 minutes. Check that the chicken is cooked thoroughly by piercing the thickest part of the meat with a sharp knife or a skewer- the juices will run clear when the chicken is cooked through.
  7. Garnish with coriander & lemon wedges & serve with salad.

Cook's Tip

If you want a more spicy dish, add more garam-masala & chilli-powder.
