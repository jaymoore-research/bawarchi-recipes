Stuffed Fish

Yield: 12 Servings

Ingredients

Tilapia - 4 boneless and skinless slab
Mustard - 6 tsp paste, not watery
ginger grated - 2 tsp
onion - 1 medium cut into small pieces
gram flour paste with ! tsp of turmeric powder and salt - for dipping stuffed fish
green chillies - as preferred small pieces
salt - according to taste
oil -Ā6 tbsp
some toothpicks

Method

  1. Take the fish slabs ,place them on a cutting board and beat them smoothly.
  2. Next mix mustard paste,ginger,onion,salt n chillies.
  3. Put the pasteĀat one endĀof fish slab and fold it or roll it.
  4. Then deep them in gram flour paste and fry them in low flame with a lid half open.,if full covered steam will form and water may splutter out.
  5. Serve when done.
  6. Best when taken withĀ basmati rice,should eat by mixing mustard paste with rice.
  7. Can add 2 tsp of mustard oil in mustard paste , if want more pungent.
