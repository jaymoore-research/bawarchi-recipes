Chicken Stew

Ingredients

Chicken- 1 kg
Onion - 5 big
Potato - 2 medium
Tomato - 1
Green Chillies-10
Coconut - half
Salt- to taste
Ginger, Garlic- little.
Turmeric powder- 1/2 teaspoon

Method:

  1. Clean and chop the vegetables.
  2. Put oil in the pan.
  3. Fry onion, green chilly, ginger and garlic.
  4. Then add tomato, chicken, and turmeric powder fry well for 5 minutes.
  5. Then put chopped potato fry for 3 minutes. Add water. When these ingredients are cooked well , at last mix coconut milk. Sim the gas stove for 5 to 7 minutes then off.
