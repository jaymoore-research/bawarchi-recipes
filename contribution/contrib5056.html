Ammani Kozhukattai

Ingredients

1 cup rice flour
1 cup water
salt as desired
2 no. red chillies
some mustard seeds
3 tbs oil
1 tsp cummin seeds

Method:

  1. Boil the water and put the rice flour stir it to form a dough .
  2. while forming the dough use 2 tbs of oil.
  3. Keep this dough aside for an hour.
  4. Make small round balls out of the dough.
  5. In the seasoning(mustard seeds, cummin seeds, red chillies) place the balls and mix it gently. Ammani kozhukattai is ready.

Coconut chutney will be a very good side dish for this.
