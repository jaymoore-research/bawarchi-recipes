Potato's Sukhi Bhaji

Ingredients

2 lb (1 kg approx) Potatoes.
1-1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/4 cup green shreaded peppers
2 tsp Ginger shreaded
1/2 tsp garlic powder
3 tsp cup Mustard seeds
2 tsp Jeera seeds
1/4 cup sesme seeds (Tal)
3 tsp salt or to taste
2 tsp Sugar
1 tsp garam Masala
1/4 bunch (cut in small) Coriander to sprinkle

Method:

  1. Boil potato in pressure cooker for 2 whistle (otherwise it will over cook) only.
  2. When cooker is cool, get potatos out and skin be removed cut potatoes in pieces as yoo like or as you see in reastaurant
  3. In seperate deep steel pan or non-stick pan, warm the oil. Add to this oil, garlic powder, ginger, green papers unitl soft.
  4. Add to the same oil mix, mustard seeds, Jeera seeds and semse seeds
  5. After about 2 minutes, add to the oil mix(item 5 & 6), Cut potatoes and gently well mix potatoes..
  6. After well mixed potatoes, add salt, sugar & garma masala and hladi powder(optional), if you like yellowish colo on top of mix and gently mix again for few times.
  7. Sprinkle, fresh Coriander. The sukhi bhaji is ready to eat with Puri, Chapati. This is our popular dish on catering, which we do for Wedding. We also add rasin & cut castrews on top for decoration. my kids, like it on Satuaday breakfast with tiki. puri (which you can make it, using bassan flour, Jeera, oil, salt & water for Dough) .
