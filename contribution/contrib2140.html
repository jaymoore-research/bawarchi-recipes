Crispy and Yummy Chicken

Ingredients

Chicken cut into 2" pieces - 2 lbs
Ginger and Garlic paste - 2 tsp each
Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp
Red chilly powder 4 tsp
Lemon - 1
Corn flour - 3 tsp
Salt as per taste
Oil to fry

For seasoning

  1. Mix chilly powder, corn flour, turmeric powder, garlic, ginger paste, lemon and salt.
  2. Marinate chicken with the above mix and keep in refrigerater for 1-2 hours.

Method

  1. Take a Non-stick pan.
  2. Pour little oil and let it warm up.
  3. Once the oil comes to boiling level put the marinated chicken pieces. Fry it till golden brown.

Relish it with rice and a curry. The left out oil can be used for any other fry be it veg or non-veg.
