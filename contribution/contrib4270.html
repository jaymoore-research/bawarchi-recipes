Melon Huli

Ingredients

Ash Gourd cut into cubes - 2 cups
Boiled Toor Dal - 3/4 cup
Coconut Gratings - 1/3 cup
Fried Red Chillies - 3 to 4 (or as per your taste)
Tamarind Paste - 1 tsp
Powdered Jaggery or gud or brown sugar 1 tblsp
Jeera - 1 tsp
Urad - 3/4 tsp
Turmeric - 1/2 tsp
Mustard seeds, a pinch of Hing and Curry Leaves for the Tadka
Vegetable Oil - 2 tsp
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Boil the gourd pieces in a little water with a little salt and Turmeric. When the vegetable is almost cooked add the boiled dal to the vegetable and simmer.
  2. Grind the chillies, tamarind and gur with a little water added to a very fine mixture. Then add the urad dal and Jeera and grind for a few seconds. At the end add the coconut gratings and run the mixie for just a few seconds. The coconut gratings should not be too fine. Add this mixture to the vegetable-dal mixture and Bring to a boil and turn off the heat.
  3. Heat the oil in a pan, add the mustard seeds, when they begin to splutter, Add the hing and curry leaves. Pour it over the Huli. Serve with Rice or Idlis.

This is the type of sambhar which they serve in Indian temples. You may use any type of melon instead of the ash gourd. Serve it hot with rice. Enjoy!!
