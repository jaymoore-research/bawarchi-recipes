Carrot Halwa

Ingredients

1 Kg Orange Carrots
1 1/2 litre Milk
500 Gm Sugar
Saffron few flakes
Cardamom powder
20 Almonds (soak in water before 5 hours)
15 Pista
15 Cashewnuts (soak in water before 2 hours)
4 tbsp Fresh Cream

Method

  1. Peel and grate carrots
  2. Put milk and carrots in a heavy utensil. Boil till thick, stirring occasionally. Once it start thickening and stir continuously. Add sugar and cook further till thickens.
  3. Add Fresh cream, cardamom, Almonds, pista, cashewnuts, saffron. Stir on low heat till the mixture collects in a soft ball and set in a tray.
  4. Seve hot and sprinkle the chopped nuts.
