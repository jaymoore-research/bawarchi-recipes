Appam

Ingredients

1) 1 cup- rice flour
2) 1 cup- wheat flour
3) 1/2 cup- jaggery
4) 1 tbsp- Grouted coconut
5) Little- cardamom
6) Oil 250 gms

Method:

  1. Mix both flours well.
  2. Grind jaggery, coconut and cardamom well.
  3. Mix the grinded with the flours which was already mixed in the first step. The dough should not be liquid or thick.
  4. The mixed items should be poured in the oil for deep fry with a chamach (spoon)
