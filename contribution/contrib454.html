Mysore Pak

Ingredients

Channa flour (Besan) : 1 cup
Sugar : 1 1/4 cup
Ghee : 3 cups
Water : 1 1/2 cup

Servings: 12 pieces

Method

  1. Heat ghee and keep aside.
  2. Take Besan and mix it with 2tbsp of ghee so that it is of dropping consistency.
  3. Now put the sugar and the water in a pan and start heating it.
  4. Once it starts boiling then add the mixed Besan in it.
  5. Now put about one tblsp of heated ghee and keep stiring.
  6. Keep stiring(medium flame) and for every 2 mins add ghee.
  7. Continue this until the Besan leaves the sides of the pan.
  8. Once it does so, then pour it out into a greased tray.
  9. After about 2 mins cut it into pieces and allow it to cool.
  10. Once cool it can be eaten.

Note: It can be kept fresh for 10 days in an air-tight container.
