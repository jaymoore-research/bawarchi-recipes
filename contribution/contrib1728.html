Madhur Vade

Ingredients

4 cups of Rice Flour
2 cups of Maida Flour
1 cup Sooji (small)
1/2 stick Butter
3 medium Onions
Coriander leaves (chopped finely)
3-4 Green Chilies (can be added more or less depending on your taste)
Salt (to taste)
1 tsp of red Chilli Powder (optional)
2-3 tbsp Water (put as little as possible)
Oil for frying

Method:

  1. Cut the onions in a very long and thin manner.
  2. Fry sooji in a large pan over medium heat until lightly golden, remove from heat and transfer to a mixing bowl.
  3. Mix the chopped onions with the rice flour, maida, and sooji. Do not add water at this time.
  4. Heat the butter a little and pour it into the bowl and add rest of the ingredients into it.
  5. Mix all ingredients to form a moist soft dough.
  6. Add water only if necessary.
  7. Heat the oil in a kadahi.
  8. Divide the dough into small portions, flatten each portion like a thin flat plate and deep fry in oil until golden and crispy.

Can be served with alone or with Coconut Chutney. This is a very tasty dish and I hope you will enjoy it as much as we do.
