Palak Ki Puri

Ingredients

1cup wheat flour
1/2 cup puree of spinach leaves(grind the spinach leaves into thin paste)
1/2 teaspoon of greenchilly paste
1/2 tea spoon of ginger-garlic paste
salt to taste
1/4tea spoon of garam masala
oil for frying
water to kneed the dough

Method

  1. Kneed the dough by mixing all above mentioned ingredients (except oil).
  2. Roll small puris out of dough.
  3. Deep fry rolled puris in oil. Ā
  4. Serve hot with ketchup or mint chutney(green chutney).
