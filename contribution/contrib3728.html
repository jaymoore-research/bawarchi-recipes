Quick Mushrooms and Peas Curry

Ingredients

Button Mushrooms 1 box [200 gms.] washed & cut into 4 parts of each mushroom.
Peas 1 cup
Onion 1 slitted finely
1/2 Capcicum [green bell pepper] finely chopped
1 Tomato finely chopped
2 Tbsp. Tomato Ketchup
1 Green Chilli finely chopped
1/2 tsp. Jeera [cumin seed]
1 tsp. Kitchen King powder
1/2 tsp. each Garam Masala & Red Chilli powder
2 tbsp. Butter
Salt ot taste

Method:

  1. Take a heavy based pan heat it & melt butter, put chopped mushroom & saute at high flame, sprinkle salt on it, let water dry of mushrooms, when it is sauted take out & keep aside.
  2. In the same pan put jeera, onions,capcicum, peas, salt & saute them also on high heat, when onions are little brown mix tomatoes & let it water dry a little.
  3. Now mix sauted mushrooms, kitchen king powder, garam masala, chilli powder & chopped chilli. Fry for another 2 minutes then mix ketchup, fry for one more minute.
  4. Serve hot with Roti or Paratha.
