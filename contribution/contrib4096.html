Saag

Ingredients

Besan - 3 tbsps. (mix well in 1 cup of water)
Potatoes - 2 medium sized. (cut into small pieces)
Tomato - 1 medium sized (cut into small pieces)
Onion - 2 big (cut into small pieces)
Blackgram Dal - 1 tbsp.
Urad Dal - 1 tsp.
Rai - 1 tsp.
Jeera - 1 tsp.
Red Chilli - 1
Green Chillies - 4 (chopped)
Salt to taste
Chilli Powder - 1/2 tsp.
Tamarind juice - 1/2 cup.
Oil - 2 tbsps.
Coriander Leaves - a few (chopped)
Water - 2 cups

Method:

  1. Keep a pan on the stove and put oil in it. When it is hot put rai, jeera, blackgram dal, urad dal, red chilli, and fry for a minute. When the colour changes, put green chillies and onion and fry for 2 minutes, then add tomato and fry for a minute and then add potato and fry for 2 minutes. Then add water and keep the lid and allow to cook for 10 minutes.
  2. When the potatoes are cooked well add tamarind juice, besan water and mix well. Then put salt , chilli powder and coriander leaves. Cook for 5 minutes till besan is cooked well . This can also be had with rotis.
