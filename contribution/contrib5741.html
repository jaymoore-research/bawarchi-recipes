Bendi

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients

1 cup Rajma
1 coconut shredded ( ready made available in Indian stores)
4 red chillies
1/2 tsp turmeric
Little tamarind

For the Tadka:
8 garlic cloves
4 tsp coconut oil
Salt to taste

Procedure

  1. Pressure cook rajma (Soak it for 3 hours and pressure cook 3 whistles)
  2. Grind coconut, red chillies,tamarind,turmeric together to a fine paste.
  3. Add this paste to the cooked Rajma ,Add salt and water to make it a gravy
  4. LetĀ everything boil well for 2 minutes
  5. Crush the garlic cloves
  6. Take coconut oil in a smallpan and add the crushed garlic cloves to it.Fry it till the cloves become little brown
  7. Add it to the gravy above( Step 3)
  8. And ur Bendi is ready.Serve it with Rice,chappati or plain paratha

Tip: you can make this gravy with Pinto beans,Black eye beans (Bagdo) ,white eye beans (Tingalaro) also.Use little tamarind otherwise the gravy becomes sour.

Preparation time : 40 minutes except soaking time for the Rajma.
