Kesari Bath

Ingredients

1 Cup Rava (Sooji)
1 Cup Sugar
1 Cup Milk
1 1/2 Cups Water
1/2 Cup Ghee (clarified butter)
1/4 Tsp Cardamom powder
1/4 Tsp saffron or orange colour
2 Tbsp finely chopped pistas and badams
10 raisins

Method:

  1. Heat ghee in a pan, add rava, stir and roast on low till a nice aroma is obtained.
  2. Add water, Milk, sugar, orange colour and cardamom powder to a vessel and bring it to a boil.
  3. Add the roasted rava little by little to the water stirring continuously.
  4. Add 1 Tbsp of chopped pistas and badams, cover and simmer till ghee separates.
  5. Fry raisins and 1 Tbsp of pista, badams in 1 Tbsp of ghee and garnish kesari bath.
  6. Place hot kesari bath mix on a greased plate and roll it with rolling pin and cut into squares. Easy to serve kesari bath is ready.
