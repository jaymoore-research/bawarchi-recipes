Spinach Kurma

Ingredients

Moong dhal 1 cup (cooked softly)
Spinach 2cups (chopped finely)
Onion 2 (1 chopped finely)
Tomato 2 (1 chopped finely)
Green Chilli 3 (1 cut lengthwise)
Coconut 1/2 cup (grated)
Ginger 1"
Garlic 4 cloves
Turmeric powder 1/2 tsp
Chilli powder 1 tsp

Ingredients for Paste

Patta, Somfu, Lawang Patta, Ginger, Garlic, 1 Tomato, 1 Onion, 2 Green Chilliesand Coconut

Method

  1. Place a pan on fire. Add 2 tbsp of oil.
  2. Add the onion and fry till golden brown.
  3. Add the green chilli & the tomato. Fry for few minutes.
  4. Add the spinach now, cook for few min, then add little water. Cover & cook until soft. Use a big spoon to mash the spinach.
  5. Add the salt, turmeric powder, chilli powder now. Cook for 2 min.
  6. Now add the cooked dhal and the ground paste. Cook until all mix together and the raw smell goes.
  7. Garnish with coriander.

This goes very well with Plain Rice, Chappati or Poori.
