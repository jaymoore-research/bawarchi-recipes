Masala Bread

Ingredients

6 slices of bread
6 tbsp grated coconut
2 tbsp puffed channa dal (otherwise called pottu kadalai)
1 1/2 coriander seeds
1 tbsp poppy seeds
5 red chillies
Marble sized tamarind
2 tbsp coriander leaves (chopped)
4 small onions
3 flakes garlic
salt to taste
ghee for frying

Method:

  1. Grind all the masala ingredients to a fine paste.
  2. Smear on sliced bread (both sides) and shallow fry in a little ghee or oil till brown.
