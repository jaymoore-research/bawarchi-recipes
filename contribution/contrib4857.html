Carrot Kheer

Ingredients

Carrots: 4 Large Carrots
Sugar: 1 - 2 Cups
Cardamom powder: 1 tbsp
Whole Milk: 2 Cups Can be substituted with Fat-free milk
Saffron : 2 to 3 strands
Ghee: 2 tbsp
Garnish: Pistachois and Almonds Optional

Method:

  1. Boil carrots until they are soft.
  2. Grind the boiled carrots along with cardamom powder to a smooth paste.
  3. Head 2 tbsp of ghee in a vessel. Add the carrot paste and cook the paste until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Next add sugar and cook the carrots for 2 to 3 minutes.
  5. Finally, add milk and saffron and allow it to boil for 15 to 20 minutes.
  6. Garnish with pistachios and almonds.
  7. Serve it hot or cold.
