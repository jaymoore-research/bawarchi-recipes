Kulfi

Ingredients

One 8oz coolwhip
One 8oz half and half ( whipping cream)
One 8oz Evaporated milk
One 8oz sweetened condensed milk
One teaspoonful cardomon powder (elaichi powder)
Four spoons of pista pieces

Method

  1. Mix coolwhip, evaporated milk, condensed milk, whipping cream together for 5-10 minutes or grind them for few seconds.
  2. Add elaichi powder, and pista pieces to it and keep it in freezer for 8-10 hours and serve it for the dessert .
