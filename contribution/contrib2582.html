Majjiga Pulusu (South Indian Kadi)

Ingredients

-Sour butter milk/ curds. (tip: Leave the curd/yogurt/buttermilk outside overnight) - 2 cups
Salt
Turmeric

Seasoning: (Urad Dal, Musturd, Jeera - 1/2 tbl spoon each), Hing , Curry Leaves
Any combination of the vegetables: (2 Tomatoes must)
Potato, Tomato, Eggplant, Bendi, Drumstick

Grind the following into paste:

Chana dal - 4-5 tbl spoons - soaked for 2-3 hours
Dhania - 1 tbsp
Fresh Coconut - 1 medium piece
5-6 Green Chilli
Ginger - small piece
1 Garlic pod
2 cups of Water

Method

  1. Boil vegetables. Add salt and turmeric.
  2. Heat oil in kadai. Add seasoning. When musturd pops, add the paste and boiled vegetables.
  3. Add more water if required. (should be sambar consistency).
  4. Get it to boil in medium flame. Remove from heat.

Goes very well with Hot Rice.
