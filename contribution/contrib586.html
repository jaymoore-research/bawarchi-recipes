Carrot Kheer

Ingredients

carrots - 3
cashewnuts - 15
ghee - 1 tbsp
milk - 5 glasses
sugar - 3/4 glass
cardomom - 6
water - 1 glass

Method:

  1. Grate the carrots.
  2. Fry them in 1 tbsp ghee till the raw smell goes and they soften.
  3. When it gets cooled, grind it with the cashewnuts and 1/2 glass milk to make it into a paste.
  4. Boil the remaining milk in a kadai and reduce it to 3 1/2 glasses.
  5. Add the ground mixture and boil for 5 mins. Then add the sugar and stir for a while.
  6. When it starts becoming a little thick add the cardomom powder.
  7. To avoid burning of the kheer at the bottom of the kadai keep a stainless steel plate at the bottom of your kadai.

Note:

After making the kheer refrigerate it and serve it cold. For best results prepare it in a thick kadai.
