This fat free (except for the oil) vegetarian dish -Kozhu is very tasty and I got it from an aunt who is well known for her culinary skills.

Kozhu

Ingredients

1. 1 Large Eggplant/Brinjal (less seeds variety)
2. Red onions - 1 large, chopped,
3. Some Dry Red chilies - bake them in the oven till slightly dark & crush
4. Tamcon 1- 2 teaspoons (or)
thick Tamarind juice - 1/4 cup
5. Curry leaves - a few, chopped
6. Cilantro/coriander - 2 or 3 tablespoons, chopped
7. Salt & oil - as needed

Method:

  1. Bake eggplant in the oven (400-450) for about an hour till tender.
  2. Make sure you make a slit with a knife before you put it in. Otherwise it may burst.
  3. After it is cooked, remove the skin & mash it.
  4. Mix the red onions, tamarind, crushed red chilies, curry leaves, salt together.
  5. Mix very well and then add the mashed eggplant.
  6. Mix well, add cilantro & some oil.
  7. Mix everything very well.
  8. For presentation, you can put this mixture in a mould (jelly mould) and keep it in refrigerator for 3-5 hours.
  9. When you take it out, put it upside down on a plate and slowly remove from the mould. Lo! it is ready to be eaten with rice or chappatis.
