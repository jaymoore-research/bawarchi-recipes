Easy Cabbage

Ingredients

1/2 Kg Cabbage
6-8 baby Potatoes, peeled and halved
1 Carrot, diced
1 cup Peas
1 big Tomato chopped
2 Bay Leaves
4 Green Chillies, split
2 tbsp Oil
1 tsp Cumin seeds
2 tsp Coriander powder
1 tsp Cumin powder
1 tsp Turmeric powder
1 tsp Chilli powder
1 1/2 tsp Garam Masala powder
2 tsp Sugar
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Shred the cabbage thinly.
  2. Put the pressure cooker on the stove, put the oil in it and fry the potatoes until golden.
  3. Add the chillies and cumin seeds and allow to splutter.
  4. Add the bay leaves first and then the cabbage and stir for a few minutes. Add the other vegetables and fry for a few more minutes.
  5. Add all the masala, sugar and salt and stir thoroughly. Then add 1 1/2 cup water , stir and close the cooker. Allow 2 whistles and then open the cooker. Keep the cooker on stove and allow the water to evaporate. Keep stirring so that it does not catch in the bottom. When the cabbage is dry and thick, it's ready to take the cooker off the stove.
  6. Add tsp of ghee if you want. You can have this with Rice or Chapattis.

Serves: 4
