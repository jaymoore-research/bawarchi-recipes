Chicken Curry

Ingredients for green paste

Spices : Cardamom,Cloves,Cinnamon etc.
Tomatoes : 1-1/2 chopped large.
Onions ; 1-1/2 chopped large
Green Chillies- plenty
Coriander Leaves -plenty
Ginger : 1-1/2 inches, chopped
Peppercorns : 1 tablespoon

Grind together all the above ingredients to a fine paste (without adding water).

Ingredients:

Chicken : 1 large,washed and cleaned.
1/2 onion chopped long
oil for frying
potatoes (optional)

Method:

  1. In a little oil, fry the chopped onions until transparent.
  2. Add the green paste to it and stir for 5 minutes.
  3. Add chicken and potatoes to this masala and leave it to cook.
  4. Enjoy with rice or chapattis.
