Sponge Cake

INGREDIENTS

Maida          1 1/2cup
Egg              4
Oil (any Corn oil or sunflower oil) 2 cup
Sugar            2cup
Yoghurt         1cup
Essence(any) 1 teaspoon
Baking powder 4teaspoon

     
  1. Take a bowl, beat the eggs with a beater.
  2. Beat it till it become double the quantity.
  3. Add sugar, yoghurt, oil & beat it again. Till it mix well. A
  4. Add essence, baking powder & maida & mix it well till all the mixture turns to be thick without any lumps in between.  
  5. Preheat the Gas oven for 15minutes at 190 degree Celsius.  
  6. Take cake pan spread ghee all over the pan evenly & put some maida powder on it & try to spread it evenly all over the pan & throw the extra maida powder.  
  7. Now pour the cake mixture on the pan & keep it in the gas oven for 40minutes.  
  8. Turn off the gas after 40minutes & let it be inside the oven more 10minutes & cool.  
  9. Then take it out & decorate as per ur wish & serve it to your friends & family.  
  10. Really it is very delicious & easy to make.
