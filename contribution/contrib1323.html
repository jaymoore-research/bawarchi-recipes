Anshu's Chicken

Ingredients

1 lb Chicken (Preferrably, winglettes or drumsticks)
4-5 Medium size onions (You can put more/less depending upon your taste), cut into rings
3-4 Tomatoes, cut in a circular fasion
2 Tbl Spoon Tomato Puree
2-3 spoons Ginger-Garlic paste
3-4 Green Chillies paste
1 Lemon (You can also use vinegar if lemon is not available)
2 Eggs (beaten)
Vegetable Oil
Bread Crumb
Salt to taste

Marinade:

First of all we have to marinade the chicken for at least 5-6 hrs.

  1. Wash the chicken
  2. Add Ginger-Garlic Paste, Chilli paste, Salt and Lemon, and mix it well.
  3. If possible Refrigerate

Method:

  1. Take some oil in a deep vessel (Kadai). The quantity of the oil should be such that the chicken peices should not touch the bottom of the vessel while deep frying.
  2. Take the beaten egg in a bowl, and dip the piece in it. Roll it over the bread crumb, then deep fry it till it is brown from outside. One thing should be noted that when you are place the chicken for deep frying the oil should be hot, otherwise the pieces may get spoiled and will consume a lot of oil.
  3. In a pressure cooker, in the bottom lay a layer of (half of the onion rings), then on top of that put a layer of circular tomatoes (half of them). Then put the chicken peices next (all of them).
  4. Add 1 tsp of sugar and 1/2 cup of water to the tomato puree paste. Empty the solution into the pressure cooker re lay the layers of onions and Tomatoes.
  5. Close the lid, put the pressure and pressure cook it for 10-15 mts.
Note:

If you dont want to have a sweet base for this chicken what you can do is, saute the onions with a little bit of salt and do the same procedure. Any comments/Suggestions on this are welcome.
