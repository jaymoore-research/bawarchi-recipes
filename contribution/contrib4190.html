Easy Channa

Ingredients

Grind

Progresso Channa Dal - 1 can
Onion - 1 1/2 cup
Tomato - 1 Cup
Coriander Leaf - a small bunch
Cashew -10
Ginger - 1 tsp
Garlic -1 1/2 tsp
Garam Masala -1 tsp

Other

Oil or Ghee
Coconut - 1 cup

Method:

  1. Fry the Onions till golden brown.
  2. Add all the above said ingredients along with fried onions and nicely grind.
  3. Heat Oil in a pan, add the grinded mixture, channa dal, salt and water as per needed consistency.
  4. Nicely grind Coconut with little water.
  5. After oil got separated from the mixture, add coconut mixture and let it boil for 10 or 15 minutes. That's it! Enjoy!
