Pineapple Kesari Bhath

Ingredients

Rava 1 cup
Sugar 2 cups
Water 2 - 2 1/2 cups
Pineapple cut to very small pieces 1 cup
Ghee for frying rava (more than 1/2 a cup)
Cashews (8-10) for garnishing
Saffron few strands

Method:

  1. First heat the ghee, break the cashews to small pieces & fry them. remove from ghee & set aside. In the same ghee, add the rava & fry till good aroma comes.
  2. Meanwhile add 1/2 cup sugar (from the 2 cups) add very little water, add the pineapple pieces in a pan & cook till soft.
  3. Boil the water in another vessel at the same time.
  4. Add the cooked pineapple to the rava, on med flame & mix with rava, add the boiling water & stir well.
  5. Add 2 cups water first, if required add the remaining water too. Once the rava is cooked add the sugar & saffron strands. Mix well, add more ghee if required or else it'll come very hard.
  6. Stir in the cashews & serve hot or cold.

Note:

U can use fresh pineapple for the best taste but i have tried canned too & it's pretty good.

Variation:

U can try with ripe bananas too.
