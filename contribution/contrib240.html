Set Dosa

Ingredients

1 cup urad dal
2 cups rice flour
2 tbsp pohe
Salt to taste
Oil for making dosas

Method

  1. Soak the urad dal and pohe for 4-5 hours.
  2. Grind it finely to a paste with water
  3. Add rice flour and salt to the paste and mix well. You can add more water to get the required consistency.
  4. Store the dough in a large vessel in a warm place for atleast 8 hours for it to ferment well.
  5. Heat tava. Pour a tablespoon of dough on the tava. Do not make it very thin.
  6. Prepare small round dosas of approximately 10 cm diameter with little oil. Cover it and let it cook for a few minutes.
  7. When the dosa is red, turn the dosa and cook on the other side.
  8. Serve a 'set' of 3 dosas topped with butter or ghee and chutney.

Dosa texture: soft and spongy

Chutney for Set dosa.

Ingredients

1/4 cup roasted gram dal
1/4 cup grated coconut.
3-4 green chillies
A small piece of tamarind
1 tbsp coriander leaves
salt to taste

For seasoning :

1 tbsp oil
1 tsp mustard seeds
1 pinch of asafoetida
1 tsp urad dal

Method:

  1. Grind all the ingredints for chutney with water.
  2. The chutney should be watery.

Seasoning:

  1. Heat oil in a small pan.
  2. Add all the ingredients for seasoning. When the mustard seed splutters, remove the pan from heat and gently pour over chutney.
  3. Mix well and serve.
