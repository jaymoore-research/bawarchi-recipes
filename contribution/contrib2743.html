Rasamalai

Ingredients

1. One litre milk for Paneer
2. Half litre milk for malai
3. One cup sugar
4. 50 gms cardamom
5. 50 gms cashewnuts
6. A pinch of saffron

Preparation:

  1. For preparing Malai grind the cashewnuts to a smooth paste. Mix it with milk and add saffron and cardamom and boil.
  2. For preparing Paneer, heat the milk. When the milk begins to boil squeeze a lime. Strain the curdled milk through a fine muslin cloth. Allow the contents to drain completely. Remove and knead to a smooth dough. Add saffron and cardamom. Form into balls and flatten slightly. Mix sugar with two cups of water and boil it in slow fire. When the syrup begins to boil, add the prepared balls one by one. Cover the pan with a lid having a broad rim. Place a little cold water over it.
  3. Cook on a slow fire till Paneer balls float on top. Remove them drain and add to malai. Allow it for a few minutes over light flame. Afterwards, allow it to cool. Refrigerate it to serve cool.
