Kancheepuram Idli

Ingredients

Yellow Split Moong Dhal - 1 cup
Urad Dhal - 1 cup
Black Pepper - 2 tsp
Cumin Seeds - 2 tsp
Asafoetida - 1/2 tsp
Ghee - 1 tbsp
Curry Leaves - 2 tsp finely chopped
Coriander Leaves - 2 tsp finely chopped
Turmeric Powder - 1 tsp
Green Chillies - 5 to 8 finely chopped
Ginger - 1 inch piece grated
Cashew Nuts - 2 tsp broken into small pieces
Raisins - 2 or 3 tsps for decoration
Salt - To Taste

Method :

  1. Soak the Moong Dhal and Urad Dhal together for 6 hrs.
  2. Drain the daals and grind it into a smooth batter with less amount of water as we do for regular idli batter till it is fluffy. The batter should not be too watery as it will result in the idlis becoming very thin.
  3. Add salt to taste and allow the batter to ferment for 6 to 8 hours.
  4. Heat the ghee in a frying pan, first add black pepper and cumin seeds and fry for a few seconds and take them out of the pan and coarsely powder and add to the idli batter. Then add cashew pieces, turmeric powder, asafoetida, curry leaves, ginger and green chillies, fry for a few seconds and mix it with the batter. Add chopped coriander leaves just before steaming the idlis.
  5. Now place some raisins on the greased idli plates and then pour the batter and steam them for 15-20 minutes just like regular rice idlis.

Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
