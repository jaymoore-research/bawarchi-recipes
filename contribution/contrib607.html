Butter Chicken Fry

Ingredients

Boneless chicken - 2lb
garam masala - 3 tsp
ginger-garlic paste - 2-3 tsp
chilli powder - 3 tsp
turmeric powder - 1 tsp
vinegar - 3-4 tsp
red food colour - 3 tsp
pepper powder - 2 tsp
salt - acc to taste
oil - 4 tsp
cream or malai - 1/2 - cup
cashew nut powder - 1/2 cup

Method:

  1. Marinate chicken with the above ingredients except for cream and cashew powder for atleast a min of 8 hrs or preferably overnight.
  2. Put the pan on fire, add oil.
  3. When the oil reaches its smoking point then add the chicken pieces.
  4. Mix well and cover the pan with a lid.
  5. When the chicken pieces are cooked then add the cream and cashew nut powder.
  6. Fry until the mixture is completely dry.
  7. Serve hot garnishing it with cilantro .
