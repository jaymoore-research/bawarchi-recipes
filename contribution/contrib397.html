This recipe is from Preeti Nair. A young married girl who is trying her mom's recipes. This is one of the easiest recipes.

Adai

Ingredients

Black Urad Dal 1 cup
Channa Dal 1 cup
Tur Dal 1 cup
Rice 1 cup
Red Chilly 4 large
Curry leaves
Salt
Hing powder 1/4 tsp

Method

  1. Soak dal and rice together for 3 - 4 hours.
  2. Grind dal, rice and red chilly coarsely without making it too watery.
  3. Add salt and asafotida powder to the batter and mix will.
  4. Garnish the butter with chopped curry leaves.
  5. Heat a frying pan and add few drops of oil and rub with a piece of paper or cloth.
  6. Pour on big spoon batter on the frying pan and spread it to form a circle.
  7. Pour little oil on the sides of the batter.
  8. Roast the adai both side on a low flame till golden brown.
  9. Serve with chutney, sambar or butter.
