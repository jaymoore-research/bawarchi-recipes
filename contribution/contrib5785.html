Ingredients

Chicken keema - 250gms
Onion paste - 1 cup
Cashewnut paste - 1 cup
Cinnamon powder - 1/2 tsp
Anar syrup - 1 tbsp
Salt - to taste
Ginger garlic paste - 1 tbsp
Red pepper - 2 tsp
Oil - 2 tbsp

Method

  1. Soak cashewnuts for 30 minutes, then blend it in a blender.
  2. Add salt, cinnamon powder & ginger garlic paste to chicken keema.mix well 
  3. Shape it as small balls.
  4. Keep them in refrigerator for 30 minutes.
  5. Heat oil
  6. Add ginger garlic paste to it.
  7. After two, 3 minutes add cashewnut paste & mix well.keep stirring.
  8. Add salt, red pepper & cinnamon powder.
  9. Add little water to it.

For Gravy:

  1. Put oil in a pan & heat it. Add grated onion/onion paste. When it  changes.
  2. Add chicken balls to the gravy.
  3. Add one cup of water, bring it to boil & reduce the heat.cook till the chicken is done.
  4. Add anar syrup to it.
  5. You can garnish it with chopped cashewnut  & fresh coriander.
  6. Serve hot with garam lachedar paratha or butter Nan.
