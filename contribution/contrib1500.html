Eggplant Curd Chutney

Ingredients

1 Egg Plant - big size
1 bunch Coriander
Curd - one cup
Turmeric 1/2 tbl spoon
Salt
1/2 tbl spoon Musturd
12 tbl spoon Methi seeds
1-2 Red Chillies
4 Green Chillies
Hing
Curry Leaves

Method :

  1. Bake eggplant at 400 F for 30 minutes. Allow it to cool, peel the skin off and mash the pulp.
  2. Add salt and turmeric to curd and beat well with spoon.
  3. Heat oil in pan, add all other ingredients and fry.
  4. Grind the fried contents in a blender.
  5. Add eggplant at the end and grind gently. See that eggplant is not made into paste but mixed thoroughly.
  6. Add to curd and mix well.

Easy to prepare and goes very well with Rotis or Rice.
