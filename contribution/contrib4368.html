Sabudana Uppama

Ingredients

2 cups soaked Sabudana (washed and rinse in water) for about three to four hours
Green Chillies to taste
2 Table spoonfull Oil
2 Table spoonfull of Peanuts (roasted)
2 Table spoonfull of dry or Fresh Coconut
1 Teaspoon Cumin
1 Teaspoon Mustard seeds
A bit of fresh Ginger
A pinch of Turmeric
Lemon Juice for taste
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Dry roast groundnuts till light brown and powder (coarse) in a blender.
  2. Heat oil and season with Asafoetida, cumin, Turmeric, mustard seeds. Add cut green chillies & ginger. Then add sabodana that is already soaked and drained in water. Stir well with the other seasoning.
  3. Finally add coarsely ground peanuts, coconut, cilantro and salt. Mix well. This can be served as brunch with chutney.

Serves: 2
