Cheese Stuffed Capsicum

Ingredients

10 pcs Capsicum medium approximately similar sized
300 gms Cheese fine grated
150 gms Tomato
50 gms Ginger
50 gms Garlic
150 gms Onion
100 gms Boiled Peas
50 gms Green Chillies
Deghi Mirch 1 spoon
Salt as per taste
2 tea spoon ful Garam Masala

Method:

  1. Keep frying pan with oils as per taste on fire for making filler cheese mixture on fire.
  2. Put finely chopped pieces of garlic and let them turn brown.
  3. Then put finely crushed mixture of onion, tomato, ginger, green chilli in frying pan and keep for 10 minutes on low fire and put boiled peas in this. Also put garam masala/deghi mirch and salt in the pan.
  4. Finally put grated cheese and fry for 5 minutes. Now the filler mixture for stuffing is ready.
  5. Make all capsicum hollow and fill with the mixutre and fry on low fire for 7 minutes and the dish is ready.
  6. Serve with cucumber and tomato fresh cut pieces on cabbage leaf and enjoy the taste.
