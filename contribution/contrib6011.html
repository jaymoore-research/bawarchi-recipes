Mixed Vegetables

Serves:4

Ingredients peas - 1/2 cup
Carrots cut into small pieces - 1/2 cup
Cauliflower Florets - 1/2 cup
Beans cut into 1" inch pieces - 1/2 cup
Baby corns - 1/2 cup cut into pieces
1 Onion sliced
2 tomatoes sliced
1/2" ginger grated
3 cloves garlic
1/4 tsp turmeric powder (haldi)
1/2 tsp cumin seeds (jeera)
1/2 tspĀ red chilli powder ( u can change according to taste)
1 tsp All Spice ( garam masala)
1/2 tsp Kasuri Methi ( dried fenugreek leaves)
4-5 Tbsp cream
1 tsp sugar
4-5 tbsp oil or butter
salt to taste
chopped coriander for garnishing

Method

  1. Put 1 spoon of oil in a pan & put onions, tomatoes, garlic, ginger, turmeric powder, & chilli powder.
  2. Saute till tomatoes are smooth .
  3. Cool it & then blend it in a mixer& keep aside this puree.
  4. Boil the vegetables Or microwave them for 5-6 min. till they are soft .
  5. Now in a pan put rest of the oil & heat it.
  6. Put cumin seeds,when they crackle put vegetables, puree, garam masala, kasuri methi, sugar, salt & saute for 2 min.covered & then put cream
  7. Cook for another 2 min.
  8. Serve hot garnished with chopped coriander along with Naans& tandoori roti's.
