Toordal Chutney

Ingredients

Toor dal --- 1 cup
Red Chillies-- 3
Salt -- to taste
Jeera ---- 1 spoon
Udadal -- 1/2 spoon
Curry Leaves -- few
Oil --- 2 spoons

Method

  1. Fry the toordal till golden brown
  2. Fry Red chillies, udad dal and jeera 1/2 spoon till golden brown and add curry leaves at the end.
  3. Grind roasted Toor dal with little water. When half done add fried redchillies and left over jeera, salt and grind smooth paste (you will get good aroma).
  4. Remove and add the remaining part of vide no.2
  5. Fry few pods of garlic and add to the chutney. It tastes great!!
  6. Goes well with rice (chutney should be thick) Idli, Dosa (make little thin by adding water)
