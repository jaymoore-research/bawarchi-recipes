Cabbage Chutney

Ingredients

1 Cabbage
6 to 8 Green Chillies or how much ever you prefer
3 to 4 Dry Red Chillies
1/2 tsp Mustard
1/2 tsp Cumin seeds (Jeera)
1/2 tbsp Urad daal
1/2 tsp Channa daal
1 Pinch of Asafoetida (Hing)
1/4 tsp Turmeric (Haldi)
Salt to taste
Tamarind size of golf ball
3 tbsp Oil

Method:

  1. Coarsely chop the cabbage and wash it.
  2. In a big khadia heat oil and add the mustard, cumin, urad daal and channa daal. Fry for a few minutes or till the mustard splutters.
  3. Add hing, turmeric, dry chillies and green chillies and fry for minute or two.
  4. Add cabbage and fry till it is half cooked.
  5. In the meantime soak tamarind and make a thick paste of it.
  6. In the food processor grind the cabbage along with tamarind paste and salt.
  7. Serve it with hot rice or as a dip for bajjis and enjoy it.
