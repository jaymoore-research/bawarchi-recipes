Baked Corn

Ingredients

3 cups cooked Corn Kernels.
2 tsp Maida/ Cornflour.
1 Onion sliced vertically.
2 Green Chillies slit vertically.
2 tsp. Butter.
1.5 tsp Sugar.
1.5 cup Milk.
Cheese.
Salt and Pepper to taste.

Method:

  1. Heat 2 tsp. butter and add green chillies and onion.
  2. Fry till onion turns soft and then add 2 tsp. Maida. Fry till maida turns golden brown.
  3. Then add Milk, cooked corn kernels, salt, sugar and black pepper powder.
  4. Boil this till the mixture becomes slightly thick and creamy.
  5. Put this in a baking dish and cover the top with grated cheese.
  6. Bake in a pre heated oven at 400 for 40 mins till the top turns golden brown.

Baked Corn makes a great side dish.
