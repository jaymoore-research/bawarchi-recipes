Bread Bonda

Ingredients

Ten slices of bread.
Two big aloo (potato)
Two green chilli
One onion
Half teaspoon mustard
Half teaspoon cumin seeds
One teaspoon urad dal
One teaspoon chanadal
Salt to taste
Three or four cups of oil to fry bonda.(vegetable oil)

Method :

  1. Peel and boil aloo and keep aside. Grate green chillies.
  2. Cut onions into small pieces.
  3. Take teaspoon of oil and fry mustard, cumin seeds, urad dal, chanadal.
  4. Put the grated green chillies and onions in that.
  5. Mash potatoes and mix well with the fried ones.
  6. After three or four minutes take the curry out of stove and let it cool down.
  7. Now take the bread and make each slice into two halves.
  8. Take water in a flat plate and put a slice of bread in the water. Make sure that two sides of bread gets wet. Remove from the water and Take slice into your hands and press with your both hands and see that the water is no more in the bread.
  9. Then take the potato curry and make small round balls with curry.
  10. Take each slice of bread (which is dipped in the water) and put each ball of potato curry in each slice of bread and cover nicely so that the curry will not come out.
  11. Now fry the bondas in the oil until golden brown. The Bread bondas are ready to eat. Serve it will hot sauce.
