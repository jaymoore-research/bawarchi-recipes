Bell Pepper Chutney

Ingredients

Red Bell Peppers : 2
Channa dhal : 1 tsp
Urad dhal : 1 tsp
Dry Red Chilli : 3 or 4
Curry Leaves few
Tamarind little
Salt

Method:

  1. Cut peppers into cubes and saute them in oil till they become soft. Allow them to cool.
  2. Take oil in a kadai and fry urad dhal, channa dhal, dry chilli, curry leaves and tamarind for 5 min. Then put all these dry ingredients (except peppers) into a blender and powder them.
  3. Then add the bell peppers and to it add little water and blend these into a chutney consistency. You need not season it. You may garnish with coriander leaves.
  4. Enjoy this with chapathis or rice with little ghee. You can store this in the refrigerator for 2 weeks.
