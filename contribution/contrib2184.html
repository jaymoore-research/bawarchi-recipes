Chilli Chicken

Ingredients

Boneless Chicken
1 spoon Vinegar
Pepper powder 1 spoon
2 Eggs white part
Corn Flour
Soya Sauce
Chilli Sauce
1 Capcicum
1 Onion
Small piece Ginger
Green Chillies 3

Method

  1. Mix the chicken small pieces with salt, vinegar and pepper powder. Let it marinate for one hour.
  2. Cut the onion, capcicum into long pieces, ginger into small piece, green chillies in its middle and keep them aside.
  3. Take eggs white part in a small bowl mix with corn flour to make batter.
  4. Heat the oil in frying pan. Take out the pieces one by one dip them in batter and fry in oil till golden colour.
  5. Take another vessel with 3 spoon of oil put onion, capcicum, ginger and green chillies. Fry for some time. Then add soya sauce, chilli sauce to it. Mix the fried chicken, keep on the flame for 3 minutes. Add little salt for taste.

Then your mouth watering Chilli chicken is ready to serve.
