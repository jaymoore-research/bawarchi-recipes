Mixed Vegetable

Ingredients

1 cup Peas shelled
1/2 cup French Beans (chopped)
1/2 cup Carrots (chopped)
1 cup Cauliflower (chopped)
2 medium sized Potatoes (chopped)
1/2 cup Paneer cubes
3 tbsp. Butter
1 cup Cream
Salt to taste
1/2 tbsp. Pepper
1 tbsp. Oregano
Some Green Coriander

Method:

  1. Melt butter, add peas, french beans, carrots, cauliflower and potatoes. Cook till vegetables get soft.
  2. Add cream, salt and pepper. Cook for 2-3 minutes. Add paneer cubes. Mix well. Sprinkle oregano.
  3. Garnish with green coriander. Serve hot.

You can buy oregano from any bakery shop. If not available you can do without it. You can use garam masala.
