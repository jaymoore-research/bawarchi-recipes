Matar Pattice

Ingredients

1 Kg. Boiled Potatoes
1/2 Kg. Matar
2 Onions finely chopped
Salt, Chilly, Turmeric Powder to taste
2 tsp. Goda/Kala Masala
1 vati Breadcrumbs powder
Oil for frying

Method:

  1. Smash boild potatoes and make a dough by adding little salt. Keep aside.
  2. Heat oil in pan and add onions fry till little pinkish, then add matar stir that now add turmeric & chilli powder as per taste then add 2 tsp. goda masala (Kala Masala), salt and stim it for 10 mts. After bhaji get ready allow it to cool.
  3. Make cover of pattice by smashed potato dough put filling of bhaji & roll pattice into breacrumbs powder and fry pattice in oil till golden brown.
  4. Serve with Sauce.
