Ingredients

1) French beans-250 gms
2) Turdal-1 cup
3) Red chillies-2-3
4) Hing-1/2 tsp
5) Oil-3 tblspns
6) Mustard seeds-1 tspn
7) Salt to taste

Method :

  1. Chop the French beans finely & boil it with little salt & keep aside.
  2. Soak turdal with the red chillies for about 2 hrs. Then grind to a coarse paste with minimum amount of water. Add hing & little salt.
  3. Pressure cook the dal mixture till 5 whistles.
  4. Allow the mixture to cool & break the lumps & make it to a coarse powder. (one can use the chopper attachment in the food processor for this).
  5. Take oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds & allow it to splutter. Add the daal mix & beans to it & fry well. Add salt if required.

This goes well with Kadhi, Jeera rasam & rice. Similar preparation can be made with banana flowers in which case the florets have to be separated & the black stamen to be removed & the flowers can be cooked in the pressure cooker along with the dal mix.
