Muli Sabzi

Ingredients

Radish with leaves or without leaves 1/4 kg
Tomatoes small 2
Ginger grated 1 tea spoon
Green Chillies 2 chopped
Mustard seeds 1/4 tea spoon
Cumin seeds 1/4 tea spoon
Curry leaves 4
Turmeric 1/4 tea spoon
Asafoetida a pinch
Coriander leaves chopped 2 tablespoonful.
Salt to taste
Oil 1 table spoon.

Method:

  1. Wash and scrape radish. Cut into rounds.
  2. Wash and chop tomatoes.
  3. Heat oil in a kadai and add mustards and cuminseeds. When they start spluttering add cury leaves, turmeric and asafoetida. Now add tomatoes and saute for a minute.
  4. Add radish and salt. Mix well and cover with a thali. Pour some water on thali so that muli will cook fast and it will not stick to kadhi. When it is almost done add chillies and ginger and coriander. Mix well and cook for two minutes.
  5. Serve hot with rice or roti. For rotis you can add garam masala to it.
