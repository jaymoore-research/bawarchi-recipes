Dahi Wada

Ingredients

For Wada

2 cups Urad dal (soaked overnight)
Salt to taste
Oil for deep frying

For Dahi

3 cups of Curds (sour if u prefer)
3-4 Green Chillies
1/2 tsp Mustard Seeds
2 tsp of Oil

. Method:

  1. Grind the soaked dal in a grinder with as little water as possible. Add salt to taste and keep aside.
  2. Take a kadhai and add a little oil. Add the mustard seeds and after they start spluttering add the green chillies and fry for a moment. Take off the heat and let cool. Add the curds and some water. (if the curd is thick). Add salt to taste.
  3. Heat oil for deep frying the wada. Make small wadas and deep fry till they are of a lovely golden brown.
  4. Add them to the curd mixture immediately. (Some people like to dip them in water first). Let each wada soak in the curd mixture for at some time.

Enjoy !!
