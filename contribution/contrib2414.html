Ingredients

Potatoes 1/4 Kg
Onion 2
Green Chillies -3
Bayleaf 3 (optional)
Chili powder 2 tsp
Turmeric powder 1/2 tsp
Cashewnuts 15
Coconutmilk (or) Milk 1/2 cup
Salt
Oil

For grinding masala:

Ginger 1/2' piece
Garlic 4 flakes
Cloves 4
Cardamom 2
Cinnamon 1 stick
Cuminseeds 1 tsp
Coriander seeds 1 tsp

Method:

  1. Boil the potatoes. Peel and cut them into medium sized cubes.
  2. Heat oil and add cashews & bayleaf fry till light brown. Add onions, green chillies and turmeric fry till golden brown.
  3. Now add grinding masala and potatoes and chilli powder & salt mix it very well. Add little water in it.
  4. Add coconut milk or milk, mix and cover the pan and continue to cook till the oil leaves the sides of the pan.
  5. Serve hot with plain Rice and Fried Rice.
