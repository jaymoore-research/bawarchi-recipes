Parappu Togayal

Ingredients

Channa Dhal (Chick Pea)- 1/2 cup;
Dry Red Chillies-4-6;
Asafoetida a big pinch,
Oil 2-3 tbsp;
Coconut Scrapings 3-4 Tbsp
Salt to taste.

For the seasoning:

Oil 2 Tbsp;
Mustard seeds-1/2 tspn;
Curry Leaves few,
2 Dry Chillies,
1/2 tspn Urd Dhal.

Method:-

  1. Broil the Channa Dhal & Dry chillies in the 2-3 tbsps oil till golden brown, add the Coconut Scrapings & continue to fry till the Coconut Scrapings turn brown too - remove from fire & let it cool.
  2. Then put all the broiled items into a blender & adding water little at a time grind to a smooth paste.
  3. Remove from blender. Place into a bowl, add Salt to taste & keep aside.
  4. Take the Oil kept for seasoning place on fire when hot add the Urud Dhal, Curry Leaves, Dry Chillies, Asafoetida & Mustard Seeds. When the Dhal turns brown & the Mustard seeds Crackle remove & pour over the Ground Paste,
  5. Mix nicely & serve with Erasari.
