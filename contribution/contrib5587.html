Garlic-Lime Pickle

Ingredients for Cooking

Garlic ( peeled) 1 Kg
Olive Oil/Mustard/GingerlyĀ Oil 500 ml
Red Wine Vinegar/vinegar 200 mlĀ
Lime fruit juice 100 ml / 20 lemons
Chilli powder 10 Table Spoon to 12 Table Spoon
Salt powder 10 Table Spoon
Turmeric powder 5 Table Spoon
Mustard 5 Table Spoon
Fenugreek 20 gms[2 Table Spoon]
Asafetida 10 gms[1 Table Spoon if powder]

Method:

  1. Roast[no oil] mustard and fenugreek in aĀ pan for 2 to 3 minutes in medium flame.
  2. Add vinegar and grind them in a mixer and keep separately.
  3. Add the oil in a separate pan and heat the oil.
  4. When the oil is heated , add Asafetida and then add all the peeled garlic.
  5. Cook for 4 minutes to 6 minutes.[Don't let the garlic turn brown in which case the nutritious value is lost.]
  6. Add Turmeric powder and chilli powder and salt to the garlic in oil keeping the flame less.
  7. After 1/2 minute switch the heat off. [Don't let the turmeric or chilli turn black by heating it longer ]
  8. Add the grinded mixture[of mustard, fenugreek and vinegar]
  9. Add the lime juice and let the pan to cool
  10. Clean a glass bottle and make sure its dry. Fill the pickle in it store.
  11. Can be eaten the same day.
