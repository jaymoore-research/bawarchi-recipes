Brussel sprout Podi adaicha(stuffed) curry

Ingredients

Brussel sprouts 1 pound
Curry leaves 1 Tbl Spoon
Mustard 1 tspspoon
Oil 5 tblspoons.

Grind to powder:
Channa dal 2 Tblspoon
Coriander seeds 4 Tblspoon
Urad dal 2 Tblspoon
Red chillies 4-5
Little salt to taste

Method

  1. Fry the above ingredients in a teaspoon of oil until golden and powder.
  2. Slit brussel sprouts across and stuff the powder.
  3. heat oil in a pan and season with mustard and curry leaves.
  4. Add the stuffed brussel sprout and fry until crisp and tender.

Serve hot with bise bele bath and Enjoy!
