OldĀRoti Chura

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

ĀIngredients

4 rotiĀĀ
5/6 fresh curry leaves optional
1 chopped small onion
1/4Ātsp sugar
1/4Ātsp mustered seed
Chutki turmeric
Salt to taste
1/4Ātsp. lemon juice
Chopped coriander for garnish
1/2tbsp oil
2 green chilli
Total time 10 min. Preparation time 5min.

Method

  1. Mash roti with two hands like poha[chivda]
  2. Heat oil in kadai
  3. Put mustered seed till they crack
  4. Add Green chilli, onion, curry leaves, till brown;
  5. Add turmeric
  6. Add roti chura
  7. Put salt, sugar and keep for 2 min mix more
  8. Now ready to serve Ā

Serve in a plate put lemon juice and garnish with coriander.
