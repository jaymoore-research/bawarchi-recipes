Tomato Rice.

Ingredients

Tomato 3 med. sized or 4 small
Basmati Rice 1 cup.
Onion 1 med. sized, finely sliced, lengthwise.

To grind :

Grated coconut 1/4 cup
Red chilli (Byadagi) 6 - 7
Coriander seeds 2 tsp.
Cumin seeds 1 tsp.
Fenugreek seeds 1/2 tsp.
Asafoetida 1 small pinch
Black pepper 2 - 3 seeds.
Cloves 7
Cardamom 3 - 4 pods
Nutmeg For flavor

For paste :

Mint leaves 2 springs.
Garlic flakes 6 - 7
Ginger 1"
Green chillis 2
To Garnish :

Raisins 3 tsp.
Cashew nuts 3 tsp.
Carrots, beans 1 Tbsp. chopped into small pieces.
Ghee. Cinnamon Stick 1", crushed
Corriander leaves 1 small bunch.
Bay leaf 1

Method :

  1. Cook basmati rice, till just done.
  2. Make smooth paste of the ingradients listed above (For paste).
  3. Heat 1 tsp. ghee in a wok, add the ingredients listed above to grind (take only half the amount of clove and cardamom listed above), fry slighly then grind smooth in a blender.
  4. Heat about 2 tsp. ghee in the wok, add raisins and cashewnuts and fry carefully until cashew nuts are slightly brown, remove from wok and keep aside.
  5. To the same wok add carrot + beans pieces, if too dry add 1 tsp. ghee and fry till just soft.
  6. Take about 2 Tbsp. ghee in a non - stick vessel, add cinnamon, remaining clove, cardamom, bay leaf and fry slighly. Then add onion slices and fry till brown.
  7. Add garlic-ginger-mint paste, fry till slightly brown and dry.
  8. Add ground coconut and spices and stir till dry.
  9. Add 1 tsp. sugar and salt to taste. Then add cooked rice, mix well.
  10. Add veg. chunks, raisin, cashew nuts, chopped corriander and mix well.

Serving suggestions :

Serve hot. If not served immedietly, then pour into a oven safe tray, sprinkle little water, cover with aluminium foil, keep the tray in the oven and maintain oven temp. at 200 F.

If you find it too hot and spicy, then you can serve it with curds or raitha otherwise tastes good without them.
