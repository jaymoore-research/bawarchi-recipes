VegetableĀKorma

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredient

2Ā carrots, peeled and cut into small pieces
12Ā French beans , cut into 2" pieces
3/4 cup peas, shelled
2 potatoes, peel and cut into small pieces
1 cup plain yoghurt, sour
1/2Ātsp Coconut, grated
Salt to taste
4-6 Green chillies
1 tsp Cumin seeds Ā

ĀFor tempering:
Ā 3 tsp Oil
1/2 tsp mustard seeds.

Ā Method

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds.once seeds begins to splutter add one cup water in a pan and let boil the water.
  2. Add all vegetables alongwith salt,cook for 10 minutes over medium heat. Ā
  3. Grind together, the coconut,green chillies and cumin seeds to a paste. Ā
  4. Whisk the yoghurt well,mix-in coconut paste.
  5. Add to the vegetables and bring to a boil.
  6. Cover the pan and leave to simmer until vegetables become tender. Ā
  7. Serve hot. Ā
