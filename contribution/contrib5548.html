Ingredients for Cooking

Chopped Methi leaves - 1 Cup
Toor dal - 1/2 Cup
channa Dal - 1/2 Cup
Fresh Grated coconuts - 1/3 Cup
Red CHillies- 6-8 Nos or as per taste
Salt- As per taste
Cumin seeds- 1tsp
Asafoetida - 1 Pinch
Oil - For Frying 1/4 cup

Ā Method: Ā

  1. Soak the dals for about an hour.
  2. Grind them with the rest of the ingredients taking care to add as less water ( 1/4 cup).
  3. Ground mix should be of Idli batter consistency.
  4. Cook the contents inĀa pressure cooker.
  5. Fry them in oil on low flame for about 20-30 min till the contents are dry to touch. Ā
  6. You can try it as a side dish with rice/sambar orĀ with rice and ghee. Ā
