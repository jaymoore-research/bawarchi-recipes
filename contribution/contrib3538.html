Khasta Kachori

Ingredients

4 cup Maida flour
1 cup wheat flour
1 cup Rawa four
1 cup Besan flour
Salt to taste...
1 TSP Baking soda
1/2 cup Whole dhania
1/4 cup black pepper
1 cup Variyari
1/2 cup Jira
2 TSP Red Chilli
1 TSP garlic paste
1 TSP ginger paste
1/4 cup vegetable oil

For Topings:

2 cup thick sweet tumric paste with little water
1 cup Yogurt

Method:

    Frying oil in pan.
  1. Mix all ingredients like whole Dhania, black pepper, variyari, jeera, red chilli and salt to taste and fry for few minutes in oil.
  2. Grind all mix in grinder to make powder.
  3. Mix grinded powder and remaining ginger, garlic paste with all flour.
  4. Add 1/4 cup oil and add baking soda with little water at a time to make dough.
  5. Keep dough for 15 miutes and then make small round balls to make thick puri like Kachori and fry in pan with oil on stove.
  6. Fry thick kachori - just like Puries....
  7. When kachori is eatable cool, add turmic paste and yogurt on top and sprinkle red chilli powder and serve.
