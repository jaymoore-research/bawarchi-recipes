Vegetable Medley

Ingredients

Mixed Vegetable - 1 lb (Beans, Carrot, Cauliflower, Corn, Lima Beans, Green Peas)
Potato - 2 Medium
Onion - 1 (small)
Tomatoes - 7 (Medium)
Butter or Oil - 4 tbsp
Green Chillies -4
garlic - 2 pieces
Clove - 1
Cardamom - 1
Turmeric powder
Cilantro for garnishing
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Cook potatoes separately and peel the skin and cut into small pieces.
  2. Cut the mixed vegetables in small pieces.
  3. Heat Butter/oil in a pan. Add the vegetables to oil / butter and cook for ten minutes in medium fire. Do not add water.
  4. Grind Onion, tomatoes, Green Chillies, Garlic, clove, cardamom. The amount of onion should be half compared to amount of tomatoes. Add one glass of water.
  5. Remove the cooked vegetables into a vessel.
  6. Heat 1 Tbsp oil/Butter. Add a pinch of turmeric powder. Add the Onion /Tomato gravy and cook for 10-15 minutes till there is no onion smell. To this add the cooked vegetables and potato pieces. Add salt to taste. Cook for ten minutes .
  7. Garnish with Cilantro and serve as side dish for Fried rice, Roti,Puri.
