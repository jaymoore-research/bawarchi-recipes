Dal Spinach

Ingredients

Spinach - 3 cups chopped
Moong Dal - 4 tbsp
Onion - 1 chopped
Tomato - 1 chopped
Green Chilli - 1-2 chopped
Salt to taste
Sugar - 1 tbsp
Oil - 2 tbsp

Method:

  1. Wash the Spinach and dal and put it in a vessel to boil with half a cup of water.
  2. After 10-15 mins when both the ingredients are cooked, remove and wash them with cold water.
  3. Squeeze out all the excess water and grind to a fine paste.
  4. Heat oil in a pan and fry the onion, chilli and tomatoes.
  5. Put in the salt and sugar. Stir.
  6. Add the spinach paste and mix well.
  7. Cover and simmer for 5 minutes.
  8. Serve hot with chapatis or rice.
  9. High in nutrition, low in fat.
