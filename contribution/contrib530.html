Rawa Dosa

Ingredients

2 cups Rawa (Sooji or Semolina)
2 cups rice powder
3 tea spoon flour ( Maida)
4 green chillies finely chopped
1 small piece ( 3 cm cube) of ginger finely chopped.
Curry leaves
Finely chopped coriander
2 oniona finely chopped ( optional)
Coconut ( finely cut) 2 teaspoon full.
Jeera 1 1/2 teaspoon full.
Black pepper corns - 1/2 teaspoon full.
Mustard seeds - 1 teaspoon
Salt to taste.
Ghee 1 table spoon
Oil
Water 9 cups

Method:

  1. Mix all the ingredients (except Onions, mustard, jeera, pepper corns, ghee and oil) with water.
  2. Heat the ghee and add mustard, jeera and pepper corns.
  3. Once they start crackling, add it to the already prepared batter.
  4. Keep it aside for about 15 to 20 minutes.
  5. Place the non-stick tawa on the stove.
  6. Sprinkle finely chopped onions.(optional)
  7. Pour the batter evenly in a round shape from a height of about 6 inches.
  8. Pour 2 teaspoon ful of oil over the dosa..
  9. Allow to cook for 2 minutes.
  10. Turn over and cook for a minute.

Serve hot with coconut chutney..
