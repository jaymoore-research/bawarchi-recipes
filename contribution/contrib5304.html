Toor Dall Rasam

Ingredients

1/2 cup toor dall
1/2 onion, cut
1 tomato, cut
1tsp rasam powder
1/2 tsp chilli powder
2 tsp sugar
1/4 cup lemon juice
salt to taste
little turmeric powder
4 tsp coconut powder
coriander leaves
for seasoning- 1 tsp oil, 1 tsp urad dall,
1/2 tsp mustard & cumin seeds

Method:

  1. Pressure cook toor dall with little water & turmeric powder.
  2. Take a vessel, add oil.
  3. When it boils, add urad dall.
  4. When dall turns golden brown, add cumin & mustard seeds.
  5. When they pop up, add onion, tomato, rasam powder, chilli powder, sugar, lemon juice, salt, turmeric powder, coconut powder, and the cooked toor dall.
  6. Finally add 4 cups of water. Mix them well. Bring it to boil, then add coriander leaves.
  7. Serve with rice.
