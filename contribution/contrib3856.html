Easy Poli

Ingredients

Channa Dhal - 3/4 cup ;
Powdered Jaggery - Little over 1 cup ;
Maida - 2 1/2 cup ;
Turmeric Powder - a pinch ;
Ghee - 3 tsp ;
Cardamom - 2 ;
Grated Coconut - 3 tsp ;
Cooking Oil - 2 tbsp.

Method:

  1. Soak the Channa Dhal in boiling water for about 1 hour. Decant the excess water and grind into an uniform paste along with the coconut, without adding too much water. Mix the dhal paste with the jaggery and cardamom.
  2. Heat up a heavy bottomed pan and add this mixture to it and stir over a medium flame for about 5 minutes. When it no longer sticks to the sides of the pan, add the ghee and mix well. Set aside to cool for sometime.
  3. Make a soft dough of the maida, adding the turmeric powder and cooking oil. Add water little by little and mix them uniformly to form a soft pliable dough. Set aside for some time.
  4. Divide the sweet filling and the covering (maida dough) into equal number of balls. Over a well dusted rolling board, flatten out small ball sized quantity of the maida dough. Place a smaller sized ball of the sweet filling and cover it completely using the flattened maida dough. Dust well on either sides and flatten it out once again uniformly and into a thin "poli". Continue the process to prepare the rest of the polis.
  5. Heat up a tawa and when it is very hot place the polis one at a time and apply oil / ghee all around and cook. Turn and cook on either sides until pink spots appear on the outer covering. Serve hot with ghee.

Note:

Soak the channa dhal first. Then prepare the filling and the maida dough. If you set these aside for atleast 1/2 hour, it will be easier to prepare.
