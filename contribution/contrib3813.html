Hot And Spicy Porridge

Ingredients

Porridge 1 cup,
Salt to taste,
Red chilli powder 1tsp
Red chilli 1 finely chopped
Turmeric powder 1/4 tsp.
Green chilli 1 finely chopped
Coriander leaves 1/4 cup
Coconut freshly grated 1/2 cup
Curry leaves 7-8
Onion 1 big finely chopped
Ginger 1" piece julianated
Garam masala 1/2 tsp.
Dry fruits chopped 1/2 cup
Mixed Vegetables 1 cup
Tomatoes 2 big finely chopped
Oil 1/2 cup
Water 2 cups.

Method:

  1. Dry fry the porridge till little brown. Keep aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add curry leaves, onions, ginger and tomatoes. Fry for a minute.
  3. Add mixed vegetables, half coconut, half coriander leaves, salt, turmeric powder, red chilli powder and cook for 2 mints.
  4. Add 2 cups of water and porridge. Stir well and cover it. Cook it for 10-12 minutes on simmer or till it is done.
  5. Now mix all the left over things except garam masala. Sprinkle garam masala on porridge in the end.
  6. Serve hot with spicy Mint Chutney or Chilli Sauce.
