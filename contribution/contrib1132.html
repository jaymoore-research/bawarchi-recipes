Saboodana Idly

Ingredients

Idly rava 2 cups
Saboodana 1 cup
Thick curds 4 cups
Onion 2 big
Green chillies 15
Soda bicarbonate 1/2 tsp
Coriander leaves 1/2 bunch

Method:

  1. Soak the idly rava and saboodana in curds in the night itself.
  2. Next morning before making the idly chop the onions, green chillies, coriander leaves and mix them to the mix soaked overnight.
  3. Now add little water and little curds to make this mixture into and idly mixture.
  4. Before cooking the idly's add the soda bicarbonate into the mixture.

This can be served for 4 people. Taste this......
