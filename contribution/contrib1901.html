Rice in Microwave

Ingredients

Use Basmathi or Jasmine rice
Use Plastic vessel

Method

  1. First wash the rice.
  2. Add water 1:2 ratio if using 2-4 cups of rice. If you use more rice then lessen the water a little bit ie. 14 cups of water for 8 cups of Basmathi rice or use only 13 cups water for Jasmine rice. You'll know this from experience. Jasmine needs less water than Basmathi as Basmathi is Aged rice as Jasmine is not. Also, use less water for Pulihora/Lemom/Fried Rice.
  3. Let it stay for 10mnts if possible. DO NOT forget to use Plastic vessel. The rice cooked in this will be extremely good.
  4. Put it in microwave and time it depending on the quantity. Rice will be done completely when the rice pops up a little bit on the top/surface. After the microwave stops, check the rice immediately and if it's not done then time it again.
  5. Approximate timings. Varies depending on the microwavve power.
    1 cup rice - 2 cups water - 15 to 18 mnts.
    2 cups rice - 4 cups water - about 20 mnts.
    3 cups rice - 6 cups water - 22 to 23 mnts
    4 cups rice - 7 cups water - about 25 mnts
    5 cups rice - 9 cups water - about 26 mnts
    6 cups rice - 11 cups water - 27 - 28 mnts

    For Rice and Vegetables Pulav, simply add mixed vegetables to the rice and water mixture and put some Ghee/butter/Oil along with a pinch of safron.
