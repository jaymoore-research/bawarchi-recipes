Yummy Egg Do Pyaza

Ingredients

1) 2 Onions (US size)/3 big Indian size onions
2) 4-5 Eggs
3) Coriander leaves
4) Dry Coconut -4 Tea spoons grated.
5) 2 Cloves
6) 1/4 tbsp Ginger paste.
7) Oil
8) Chilli powder
9) Salt
10) 1 clove Garlic
11) Turmeric
13) Methi seeds 2-3 soaked overnight

Method:

  1. Grate Dried coconut and roast it till it becomes brown.
  2. Make paste of the above, 1/4 small spoon shahjira, 2 cloves, 1 pepper ball, 1/4 ginger paste, 1 cardamom, about Quarter bunch of coriander, 1 clove of garlic , half cup of water and keep it aside
  3. Heat some oil and fry the onions till they are brown.
  4. Add salt, turmeric 1/4 spoon garam masala, chilly powder,2-3 methi seeds soaked overnite and simmer it for 5 more minutes.
  5. Add the paste you have made and simmer it for 2-3 minutes more till the oil separates.
  6. Gently break the eggs into the masala. Do not scramble.
  7. Cover it with lid and simmer for 5-6 minutes..... there you go garama garam spicy kharat...tayyar...mmmm...!! Keep watching for many more spicy dishes to come..let me know how you like it.
