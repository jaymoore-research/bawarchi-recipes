Cucumber Koottu

Ingredients

Cucumber -- 2 or 3 (finely chopped)
Onion -- 1 (finely chopped) (it's always good to add a lot of onion in this dish)
Garlic -- 2 to 3 flakes (finely chopped
mustard seeds -- 1/4 tsp
jeera -- 1/4 tsp
red chilli powder -- according to taste (depends on how hot u want)
tomatoes -- 2 or 3 (cut into cubes)
haldi powder -- 1/4 tsp
hing -- a pinch
goda masala -- 1/4 or 1/2 tsp
salt -- to taste
coriander leaves -- for garnishing (finely chopped)
oil -- 2 to 3 tbsp

Method :

  1. Heat oil in a pan, put mustard seeds. after it splutters, add jeera. when it becomes dark brown in colour, add garlic.
  2. When it starts turning a little brown in colour, add the onions, fry till they turn golden brown, then add tomatoes & fry till the raw smell goes.
  3. Now add the finely chopped cucumber with some haldi, hing & red chilli powder.
  4. After it gets cooked well, add the goda masala, salt & garnish it with coriander leaves.
  5. Serve hot with rice & daal or with roti.

Note : This type of dish can be made with doodhi or soraikka (in Tamil).
