Bread Dahivada

Ingredients

Dough
15 bread slices
wheywater or buttttermilk if needed
1.25 cup thick curds
salt to taste

Seasonings

1/2 sp oil
1/2 sp mustard
1/2 sp cuminseeds
1 finely cut greenchillies
a pinch asafoetida
few curry leaves

Garnish

finely cut coriander
chilli powder
pepper powder

Method

  1. Knead the bread slices into a soft dough sprinkling buttermilk only if necessary.
  2. Shape them like vadas(round♭ with a dent at the center) season mustard, cumin, greenchilli, asafoetida&curry; leaves.
  3. Add the seasonings to the curds, add salt, beat the mixture well.
  4. Place the vadas on aserving plate, pour the curds, garnish with coriander & sprinkle chilli &pepper; powder & serve.
