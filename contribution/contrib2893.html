Vegetable Spring Roll

Ingredients

1 cup All Purpose Flour
1/4 cup Corn Flour
1 tsp Butter
1/2 cup thinly sliced Onions
1/2 cup grated Carrot
1/2 cup grated Cabbage
1/2 cup thinly cut (lengthwise) Beans
1/2 cup thinly cut (lengthwise) Bell Pepper
2 tsp. Soya Sauce
Oil for deep-frying
Salt as required

Method:

    Take both the flours in a bowl along with butter & required quantity of salt & mix it well.
  1. Make stiff dough with a help of water & let it set for about an hour & a half.
  2. To make the filling, take a frypan & add about 3-4tsp. (Or enough to stir-fry) oil & add all the vegetables.
  3. Stir-fry the vegetables till it is done well.
  4. Add Soya sauce & salt at the end & fry it for 3-4 minutes on the stove.
  5. Make lemon size balls out of the dough.
  6. With the help of a rolling pin roll the dough as big as a chapathi.
  7. Take 2-3 spoons of the filling & spread it lengthwise.
  8. Now roll the dough till it reaches the other end & seal it with the help of water. You can seal the edges (side edges) too with the help of water. & Deep-fry till it turns golden brown.
  9. Dry it on a paper towel.
  10. After 10 minutes, using a sharp knife you can make 2-3 pieces out of a single roll. Repeat the same with the remaining dough.
  11. Enjoy with Tomato Sauce!
