Appam

Ingredients

Raw Rice (plain rice (if basmathi well and good)) 1 cup
Rava 1 tbspn
Grated Coconut 1 cup
Yeast 1/4th tspn
Sugar 2 tspn
Salt to taste

Method

  1. Soak rice for 1 1/2 hrs.
  2. Cook rava on low flame in 1/2 cup water stir till it forms a thick paste. Leave it to cool.
  3. Soak yeast in little water for 5 minutes.
  4. Now grind soaked rice, rava, grated coconut, sugar. After the whole mixture is ground to a fine paste add the yeast, and once again grind the whole thing for a second. Then remove the mixture to a vessel, and keep it for 10 hrs to ferment.
  5. Add salt after 5 minutes start make appam in a nonstick kaddai. When the non stick is hot pour a big spoon of the mixture and quickly take the kaddai in both the hands and just twist it once so that you get a thin layer in the side and a thick portion in the middle and on low flame leave it to get cooked till the edges become light golden colour. Appam goes well with Veg.Stew.
