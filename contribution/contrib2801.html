Lemon Rice

Ingredients

Cooked Rice - the rice pearl should not clogg; rice will not stick up, by cooking with CORRECT amount of water
Cooking Oil - 2 Tablespoons
Urad Dhal - 1 Tablespoon
Channa Dhal - 1 Tablespoon
Red Chillies - 3 or 4 pieces
Salt - To taste
Haldi (Yellow ) powder - 1/4 teaspoon
Lemon Juice - As required
Asafoetida - 1 or 2 pinches

Method:

  1. Place a Fry pan in oven and pour the cooking oil. When the oil is hot, put the mustard seeds. When the seeds splutter, place Urad Dhal and Channa Dhal and fry till golden brown in colour. Place Red chillies and salt & Haldi powder.
  2. Spread the cooked rice in a large plate & pour the ingredients from the pan and serve the Lemon Rice with the addition of Lemon juice that is required.

This is the best snack/lunch/dinner to serve any number of persons.
