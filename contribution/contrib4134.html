Easy Sandwich

Ingredients

Tomatoes (chopped finely) - 4
Onion (Chopped finely) - 2
Red Chilli powder - As per taste
Salt - As per taste
Oil - for shallow frying
Bread packet - 1

Method:

  1. Mix chopped tomatoes, onion, chilli powder and salt.
  2. Take one piece of bread and spread this mixture evenly. Cover this bread piece with another bread pice. Press the two bread pices. If you have sandwich toaster, toast in that toaster. Otherwise, heat a pan and shallow fry the bread pieces on two sides just for one minute with little oil.
  3. Cut this in to two triangles. Your tasty sandwich is ready. Enjoy with Tomato Ketchup.
