Pepper Chicken

Serves 4

Ingredients

Chicken leg pieces - 8
Butter - 2 tbsp
Zeera - 1/2 tsp
CInammon - 1
Bay leaves - 2
Cloves -- 3-4
Whole black pepper -- 8-10
Powder black pepper (according to taste)
Salt (according to taste)

Method

  1.     Wash the chicken pieces and keep aside.
  2.    Heat butter in a pressure cooker and pour in all the whole spices in it.   
  3. When they begin to sparkle pour black pepper (powder) and salt.   
  4. Pour chicken pieces in and stir and cook for 3-4 minutes.
  5. Pour little    water and close the pressure cooker.
  6. Dont put too much water as it is    not a dish with a lot of gravy.   
  7. After 1 1/2 whistle, open the cooker and stir the chicken and cook until    it takes a good brown colour and the gravy become somewhat thicker.
  8. Try  not to cook too much so that the gravy finishes.
  9. There should be some to   pour on each piece a bit.
  10.    Serve hot either with salad or as snacks with cocktails.
