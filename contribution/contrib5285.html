Ingredients

Thick curd - 2 litres
Green chillies - 1 kilo
Salt to taste
Method:

  1. Split green chillies into two vertically, till about three fourth of the length.
  2. Now, immerse and soak them in a vesselful of curd with salt.
  3. Cover it and keep aside for a day.
  4. Next day, take the chillies out of the solution using a spoon and dry under sun.
  5. In the evening, take the chillies and put back into curd.
  6. Repeat this process for 4-5 days, till the curd is completely absorbed by the chillies.
  7. Now, dry the chillies for one more day and store in tins.

These can be deep fried in cooking oil and used with rice or dosa or idly. : Likewise, you can make kondattams out of many vegetables like bitter gourd (cut into rings), ladies finger, chundanga (chundakkai) and even from raw vegetable parts like pumpkin skin.
