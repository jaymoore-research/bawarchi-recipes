Tomato and Red Chilli Curry

Ingredients

Tomato ripe - 4 (Chopped)
Onions - 2 (Chopped)
Red Chillies - 6 (Whole) or according to taste
Tamarind - a lime sized ball
Salt - to taste

Seasoning:

Oil - 2 tsp
Jeera - 1/2 tsp
Mustard - 1/2 tsp
Curry leaves - 1 stem
Ghee - 1 tsp

Method

  1. Pressure cook the above ingredients for about 2 whistles.
  2. Allow it to cool, then blend it together.
  3. Season the blended mixture with mustard, jeera, curry leaves and add aĀ teaspoon of ghee to it
  4. Garnish with Cilantro leaves.
  5. Serve hot with rice or Chapathis

Variations

To the same ingredients, toor dhal can also be added and the same method is to be followed.
