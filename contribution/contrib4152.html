Hot Garlic Fried Rice

Ingredients

2 cups Rice
1 tbsp. Garlic paste
1 tbsp. finely chopped Garlic
2 dry Red Chillies
1 cup thinly sliced Spring Onion
Salt to taste
1/4 cup finely chopped Carrot
1 tbsp. Garlic Chilli Sauce
3 tbsp. Soya Sauce
1/4 cup finely chopped Capsicum
Medium sized pieces of fried Walnuts, to garnish

Method:

  1. Wash rice. Soak for 10 minutes.
  2. Boil water. Add rice and salt. Drain.
  3. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed vessel. Add garlic paste, chopped garlic and red chillies. Fry well.
  4. Add spring onions, carrots, capsicum and salt. Stir well.
  5. Add rice, soya sauce and garlic chilli sauce. Stir and cook on slow heat till done.
  6. Serve hot.
