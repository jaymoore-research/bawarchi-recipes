Spring Onion Chatti

Ingredients

2 cups Rice (plain)
2 tbsp Coriander Seeds
4 to 5 Red Chillies
1 bunch Spring Onions (8-10 spring onions) (finely chopped along with the leaves)
1 tbsp Salt or as per taste
Oil for frying

Method:

  1. Soak the rice along with red chilli and coriander seeds for 2-3 hours and grind adding a little water. The batter should be of idli consistency and a little coarse.
  2. Mix the finely chopped spring onions well in the batter.
  3. Heat a tava spread a little oil on it and then put about 3 tbsp of batter on the tava and spread it like a dosa but not thin. It should be a little thick.
  4. Pour one tsp oil around the dosa and let it cook till golden brown and cool. Now turn and cook the other side too the same way.

You will get about 8-10 chatti's with this batter. Serve hot with thick Curds. Instead of spring onions you may use any Leafy Vegetable like Methi, Palak etc. Or even Tomatoes.
