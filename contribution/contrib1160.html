Potato Spinach

Ingredients

1 bunch spinach
1 medium size boil potato
2 tomatoes
1 small piece of ginger
2 green chillies
Salt to taste
1 table spoon oil

Method :

  1. Wash the spinach and put in a pan. Add 1 table spoon of water and cover the pan. Put the pan on low flame till the spinach becomes soft.
  2. Grind the tomatoes, green chillies and ginger in mixie.
  3. Remove the skin of boil potato and cut it into small pieces.
  4. Now grind the spinach in mixie.
  5. Heat the oil in pan and add the tomato puree prepared in step 2. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Now add the spinach, potato and salt.
  7. Boil it for 2-3 minutes.

Enjoy it.

You can have this with chapati, rice and Makai ki roti.

Preparing time : 15-20 minutes. For 2 persons.
