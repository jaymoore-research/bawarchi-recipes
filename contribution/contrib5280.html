Masala Bhindi

Ingredients

250 grams of lady finger (take lady finger small one preferably)
150 grams of potato
1 bunch of fresh cilantro
1 cup of grated fresh coconut
1/2 tsp of haldi or turmeric
1 tsp of dhania powder
1 tsp of mustard seeds
salt,red chillie powder and garam masala as per taste
2 spoons of oil

Method

  1. Wash lady fingers,dry and slit the lady finger from the middle cut the top and bottom a little before slitting
  2. Cut the potatoes also length wise like done in french fries
  3. Cut the cilantro very fine
  4. Take a baking plate and arrange the lady finger on the plate see that it is evenly arranged don't keep one top the other
  5. If u have spraying oil then spray it on the ladyfinger or u can take a little oil and drizzle it on the lady finger with the spoon
  6. Place it in the microwave on high for 6 to 7 mins after that check if done then remove it from the oven otherwise u can continue till they are done
  7. Take a kadhai put oil in it heat it after that add the mustard seeds once it is done add the potatoes in it add little salt
  8. Once the potatoes are done add the lady finger which we cooked in the microwave then add all the masala that is turmeric powder,chillie powder,garam masala,dhania powder,salt and mix it properly
  9. Now add cilantro and the coconut mix well and cook for another five mins.
  10. Granish with little cilantro and coconut and serve hot with puri,roti.

Tip:
If u don't have microwave u can cook it on the gas but first u have to add potato and when they are half done u add bhindi and for that u have to take a little bit more oil to cook both in this oven dish u require very little oil compared to one prepared on gas.
