Spring Onions Kootu

Ingredients

Spring Onions 1 bunch
Big Onion 1 but use only half of it
Tomato 1
Thoor dal 1/2 cup
Kulambu poddi (Sambar Powder) 1/2 tsp
Salt 1 tsp
Sugar 1 pinch
Kadugu (mustard) 1/2 tsp
Urud dal 1/2 tsp
Red Chilli 1

Method

  1. Boil thur dal with 2 cups of water in cooker (2 whistle sounds)
  2. After steam goes open the cooker and add chopped spring onions, chopped onion, sliced tomato, salt, sugar, kulambu podi and close lid and boil for 1 sound
  3. Open the cooker after steam goes and now pour 3tsp oil in a kadai and add kadugu, urud dal and red chilli.
  4. Add this to the spring onion kootu and mix. If it is too thick add some water.
  5. Eat hot, mixed with rice.
