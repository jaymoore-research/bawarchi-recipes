Stuffed Capsicum

Ingredients

2-3 Green Capsicum or Bell Pepper (Since we get very big here this is enough. If it is small 1/2kg you can take)
2-3 medium sized Tomatoes.
2 medium sized Onions.
3-4 flakes of Garlic.
4 Green Chillies.
2 tbsp Chutney Dhal or Fried Gram.
1" length Cinnamon stick.
2-3 Cloves.
1 tbsp Saunf or Aniseeds.
1 tbsp Dhaniya/ Coriander seeds
2 tbsp Coconut

Method:

  1. Grind everything except capsicum.
  2. First fry finely cut onions and then add masala. Fry for sometime and finally add cut capsicums and fry for some more time. Since the capsicum we get here is very big we can't stuff it. If it is small we can stuff first and after frying onions and masala we can add stuffed capsicum and fry for sometime.
  3. You must fry the capsicums first separately and then add to the masala. It will be very tasty.
  4. Serve hot with rice.

By the same way you can prepare Bittergourd also. First cook bittergourd with little turmeric powder and tamarind. After stuffing the masala follow the same procedure as above. When frying the masala and bittergourd add a little of the tamarind water which you used for cooking the vegetable.
