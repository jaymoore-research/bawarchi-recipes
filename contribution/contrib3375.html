Saucy Chicken

Ingredients

For marination
Chicken - 1 kg
Chilly powder - 2 tablespoon
salt to taste
soya sauce - 1 tablespoon

Onions - 2 med size
Ginger - 1 med piece
Garlic - 5 flakes
Chilly powder - 2 teaspoon
soya sauce - 2 tablespoon
Tomato sauce - 2 tablespoon
chilly sauce - 3 teaspoon
oyster sauce - 1 teaspoon
cornflour - for thickening

Method

  1. Marinate chicken for an hour.
  2. Fry chicken in oil. Remove from oil
  3. In the same oil saute onions, ginger and garlic till soft.
  4. Add chilly powder and all the sauces. Fry for a few minutes till the oil comes on top.
  5. Add the fried chicken and let cook till chicken catches the flavour of the sauces.
  6. Add cornflour and thicken.
