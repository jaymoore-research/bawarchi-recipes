Easy Curd Rice

Method:

  1. Take about 100 to 150 gms of thin variety of beaten rice (Poha) per person. Before you leave the hotel in the morning wash well with hot water and remove the water. Carry it in a plastic container.
  2. Add salt and pickle or dry chutney powder. Also carry plain or "Natural" yogurt.
  3. Just before eating mix the poha which is well soaked and soft by now, with yogurt and serve. This is the best replacement for the good old curd rice!
