Crunchy Breakfast

Ingredients

100 gms Fresh Curds made of skimmed milk (refrigerated)
1- 2 tbsp of Corn Flakes
1 tsp sugar
1/2 Banana (round sliced)
1 tbsp chopped Apples
A few chopped Pineapple pieces

Method

Beat the fresh curd well to remove any lumps. Add sugar and mix. Now add the rest of the ingredients one by one. Mix well and the crunchy cereal bowl is ready!

This recipe can be adapted to one's personal tastes and a variety of fruits can be added. The idea of replacing milk with curds is that one can add even citrus fruits like grapes and pineapple to the dish. Moreover, well-whipped curd serves as a cream base which is wonderful to the palate! Besides, the flakes remain absolutely crunchy.
