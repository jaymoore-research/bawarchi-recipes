Banarasi Backlava

Ingredients

1. Maida flour (2 cups)
2. Butter (4 tsp.)
3. Almonds (few)
4. Cashewnuts (few)
5. Shredded coconut (200g)
6. Cardamom (few)
7. Powdered sugar (1/2 cup)
8. Milk Maid (1/2 cup) (condensed milk)
9. Raisins (few)
10. Pisthachio nuts (few)
11. Hot sugar syrup
12. Cloves (few)
13. Cooking oil

Method

  1. To maida, add butter and mix them well. Sprinkle water to obtain hard dough. Keep aside for 1-2 hour.
  2. Mix ingredients #3-10 together (stuffing).
  3. Prepare hot sugar syrup by dissolving sugar in water, heating to get one string consistency.
  4. Flatten dough (to approx. 5-6 cm in dia.) and place stuffing (obtained from 2) on it. Fold the dough in such a way that the stuffing is securely held in place inside. One way to secure this filling is by piercing the clove through the folds.
  5. Fry this stuffed dough in oil and place these in the hot sugar syrup for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Backlava is ready for serving.
