Rice Pakora

Ingredients

1 cup Rice
2 cup Water
1/2 cup Wheat flour
1 tbs.Fenugeek seed (methi)
1 tbs.Ajawain
1 tbs.Sesame seed
1 tsp.Jeera
5-6 Green Chillies
1/4 cup Curry leaves
1/4 cup Coriander leaves
Salt to taste
Oil for deep fry

Method:

  1. Add the methi in rice. Wash and cook the rice in double quantity of water.
  2. When done, mash it untill it becomes soft.
  3. Add some amount of water to flour and knead it, till smooth and elastic.
  4. Mix it to the rice and add all the above ingredients.
  5. Make a little ball and deep fry in oil untill it becomes golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with pickle or tomato ketchup.
