Kovakai Masala

Ingredients

Kovakai(a.k.a. Tindora, Dhondakai) - 1/2 kg

Potatoes: 1
Garlic -5 pods
Ginger-1/2 inch
Chilli Powder: 1.5 tsp
Salt: 1.5 tsp
Fennel seeds/cumin : 1/4 tsp
Haldi:1/2 tsp
Mustard seeds: 1/2 tsp
Oil: 1-2 cups as desired.

Method:

  1. Heat oil in a kadai and add mustard seeds, fennel seeds to it. when mustard sputters, add mashed garlic and ginger to it. Then add haldi powder and chilli powder to it.
  2. Slit the tindora vertically and the potato into cubes.
  3. First put the potato in the oil and after it's nicely fried, add the tindora. Add salt as necessary. Sprinkle water and put a lid on the vessel to cook for 6 minutes. Remove lid and add 2 tsp. of oil and mix well and leave to fry for 4-5 minutes. Total procedure takes about 20-25 minutes.
