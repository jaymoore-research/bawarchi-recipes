ZatPat Walnut Barfi

Ingredients

Walnut powder: 1 Cup
Vanilla Wafers or Marie biscuits powder: 1 Cup
(Note: You can crush them finely or coarsly according to your taste)
Condensed Milk: 1/2 Cup approximately.
Nutmeg and Cardamom Powder according to your taste.

Method:

  1. Mix together all the ingredients.
  2. It should be like a dough.
  3. Spread it on to a plate or a tray.
  4. Refrigerate for 10 mins and cut into squares or any shape you want.
