Butter chicken

IngredientsĀ

Chicken=1/4kg(boneless)
Onion(finely chopped)=1cup
Tomato(finely chopped)=3/4cup
Ginger-garlic paste=3tsp
Garammasalapowder=a pinch
Turmeric powder=1 tsp
Red chill ipowder=2 tsp
Lemon=1/2
Kesari powder=a pinch
Tomato ketchup=1tsp
Oil= 2tsp
Ghee or Butter=1tsp
Coriander leaves=3tbs
Salt to taste
Method

  1. Cut the chicken into very small pieces.
  2. Add the chicken, curd, kesari powder and lemon juice in a bowl and marinate for an hour.
  3. In a heated pan add ginger-garlic paste, onion, tomato and fry it for a minute.
  4. Then add red chill ipowder, turmeric powder, salt and the marinated chicken and stir it well.
  5. Cook till chicken is tender.
  6. Add coriander leaves and ketchup and heat it for few more minutes
