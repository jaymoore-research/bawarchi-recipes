US Halwa

Ingredients

One small Zucchini
3 Cups Sugar
Cardomom Powder - 1/2 tea spoon
Cashewnuts - 10
Raisins - 10
Ghee - 4 table spoons

Method

  1. Wash the Zucchini and peel off the skin.
  2. Grate it finely.
  3. Pressure cook the Zucchini and drain the water.
  4. Prepare sugar syrup by adding 1 cup of water with three cups of sugar. Boil it for four minutes till it slightly thickens.
  5. Add the Zucchini, cardomom and ghee to the sugar syrup.
  6. Stir the mixture till it doesn't stick to the vessel.
  7. Decorate it with fried cashewnuts and raisins.
