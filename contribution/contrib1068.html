Bisi-Bela Baath

Ingredients

Rice - 1 cup
Toor Dal - 1/2 cup
Veg (potato, carrot, capsicum, brinjal) - 1 1/2 cup
Onion -2
Tamarind - small lemon size (take the juice out of it and keep)
Coriander leaves

To make the Masala:-

1. Coconut - 1/2 cup
2. Dhania - 2tsp
3. Cinnamon - 1 stick
4. Cloves - 3 to 4
5. Saunf - 1 tsp
6. Red chillies - 4
7. Black pepper (kaali mirch) - 4 to 5
8. Green chillies - 2
9. Onion - 1 ( cut into small pieces)
10. Poppy seeds - 1tsp
11. Elaichi - 1 or 2
12. Ginger - 1 inch
13. Garlic - 3 or 4

Method :

  1. Take little oil in the Kadai, add all the ingredients except coconut and onion. Roast them and at the end add onion and coconut and saute them too.
  2. Grind with sufficient water and make a paste.
  3. Keep the past aside.
  4. Half cook the toor daal in the pressure cooker and keep aside.
  5. Take the cooker vessel add 1 cups of water and the tamarind juice. (Measure how many cups is the tamarind juice. It should not be more than one cup) and boil. when it comes to boil, add the rice and vegetables. Altogether only three cups of water should be there. This includes the water in the toor daal too. If the tamarind juice is more than one cup, just add only little water.
  6. Add the cooked toordaal and the masala, turmeric powder and salt to taste.
  7. Put the cooker vessel in the Pressure cooker and cook it for 3 to 4 whistles.

To garnish:-

  1. Cut the onion into fine pieces
  2. Cut coriander leaves
  3. Roast the cashew, raisins and elaichi in little ghee.
  4. Add all the ingredients to the cooked Bisi bela baath and mix.

Serve with Raitha and Potato Chips.
