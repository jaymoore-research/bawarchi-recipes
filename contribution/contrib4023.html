Cabbage Pakoda

Ingredients

1/4th part of Cabbage
1 Potato
1 Onion
1/2 tsp Ginger paste or 1/2 tsp small pieces of ginger
Salt to taste
1/4 tsp Red Chilli Powder (according to taste)
1 cup Besan flour
1/4 cup Rice Flour
3 cups of Cooking Oil

Method:

  1. Cut the cabbage leaves, onions, potatoes into small pieces.
  2. Mix these vegetables and chilli powder, salt & ginger with besan and rice flours. Add very little quantity of water while mixing. The mixture should not be too thick like atta or too thin.
  3. Heat oil in the frying pan.
  4. Make small balls and flatten them with the fingers. Deep fry these pakodas till they are golden brown.
  5. Serve hot with Tomato Sauce or as a side dish accomplished with a main meal.
