Healthy Tomato Kichadi

This is a very simple and healthy recipe.

Ingredients

4 medium size tomatoes
fresh scrapped coconut 1/2 cup
Thick curd - 2 cups
green chilles - 5 - 6 (depending on how spicy you want it to be)
salt as reqd
2- 3 tspoons of coriander

For tempering:
2 tsp of oil
mustard seeds

Method:

  1. Cut tomato into small bite sized pieces and set aside.
  2. Grind coconut and green chilles with 2 t spoons of curd to a nice paste.
  3. Mix the ground paste with the curd and add the cut tomatoes and salt.
  4. Temper it with mustard and garnish with coriander leaves.
  5. The tomato kichadi is ready to eat.

Goes well with idli, dosa and plain rice.
