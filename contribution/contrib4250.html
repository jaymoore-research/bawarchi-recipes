Chou Chou Mor Kootu

Ingredients

Chou chou or chayote squash - 2 (cut into small pieces)
Cooking Oil - 1 Tb.sp
Pepper (Molagu or poppy seeds )- 2 Tb.sp
Dried red chillies (Molaga vathal) - 2
Cumin seeds (jeeragam)- 1 tsp
Mustard (kadugu) - 1 tsp
Urad dhal (ulutham paruppu) - 1 tsp
Asafoetida powder (Perungayam)- 1/4 tsp
Buttermilk (mor) - 1 glass(240 ml)
Curry leaves (karuvepilai) - one stalk
Salt (uppu) - as desired

Method:

  1. Heat cooking oil in a pan and add mustard, cumin seeds, urad dhal, dried red chillies, asafoetida and when mustard splutters, add curry leaves.
  2. To this, add cut chou chou, with half a glass of water and allow it to boil for a couple of minutes.
  3. Dry grind the pepper and add this to the boiling chou chou and also add salt.
  4. After chou chou becomes soft, turn the gas off and allow this to cool.
  5. Add buttermilk (mor) to the chou chou. (You can also first boil the chou chou with water, ground pepper, salt and asafoetida and then garnish that with the remaining ingredients, cool and then add buttermilk.)
  6. Your Chou Chou mor kootu is ready to be served with Rice. Hope it was delicious!
