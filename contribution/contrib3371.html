Methi Paratha

Ingredients

1 cup Atta
1/4 cup Besan
2 tblsp Rice Flour
Oil
Salt
4-5 Cloves
Garlic
5-6 Green Chillies
Turmeric powder
A bunch of Methi Leaves

Method:

  1. Take atta, besan, rice flour, a pinch of turmeric powder, salt & 2 tblsps. oil. Mix well. Add paste of garlic & green chillies & finely chopped methi leaves. Mix well together. With little water prepare dough. Keep aside for 15 to 20 minutes.
  2. Heat tawa. Take a small ball of dough & roll it into a paratha. Bake the paratha on hot tawa with little oil on both sides.
  3. Serve hot with Butter or Chutney.
