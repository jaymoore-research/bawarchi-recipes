Green Mango Panha

Ingredients

1 cup Mango pulp.
3 cups Sugar
4 Cardamoms
Salt to taste.

Method:

  1. Pressure cook green mango in pressure cooker. Put it off after 4 whistles. Or Boil Green mango until it cooks. Take out pulp from green mango.
  2. Add sugar and cardamoms. Grind it in a mixer. Keep it refrigerated.
  3. While serving take grinded pulp in a container and add water as per good taste.Add salt. It is used as a good refresher in summer. You can store pulp of Panha for 15 days.

Serving size 8 bowls.
