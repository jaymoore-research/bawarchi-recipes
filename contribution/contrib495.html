Chicken Liver Fry

Ingredients

1 lb chicken livers
1 medium sized onion chopped
1 tsp ginger paste
1 tsp garlic paste
pinch of turmeric
salt to taste
1 tsp coriander powder
1/2 tsp cumin powder
1/2 tsp garam masala powder
1 medium sized tomato(can be substituted with ketchup)
fresh coriander leaves(optional)
oil about 5 tbsp
red chili powder as desired

Method

  1. Clean the chicken livers and pressure cook it along with some salt & water. Four whistles on high heat should be enough.
  2. Take the livers out from the cooker but retain the water separately.
  3. Now in a frying pan heat the oil and fry the onion and garlic paste till golden brown.
  4. Then add the ginger paste and fry for another 2 minutes.
  5. Add the garam masala powder, chili powder, tomato, cumin powder, coriander powder, salt and turmeric powder to the onion paste and keep frying till you get the aroma of the fried spices.
  6. Now add the cooked livers to the pan and fry for another 2 to 3 minutes.If you find it too dry add a little water that you had retained in the beginning.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves.
  8. Serve hot with parathas or rotis.
Serves : 2
Cooking Time : 20 minutes approx.
