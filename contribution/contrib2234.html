Masala Fish

Ingredients

2 medium Trout or any other fish
1 tbsp Tandoori Masala
1 tsp Curry Powder
Ginger 1 inch piece
Garlic 2 cloves
3-4 tbsp Lemon Juice
Salt and Chilli powder to taste
Coriander to garnish

Method

  1. Clean the fish and remove the skin. Cut into big pieces (steaks).
  2. Make a paste of ginger and garlic.
  3. Mix all the ingredients together. Rub the marinade generously on and inside the fish. Leave aside for 30 min or so.
  4. Brush oil in a non stick pan. Put fish with all the marinade in it. Cook each side for 2-3 min at medium high heat. Lower the heat and cook for 7-8 min with the lid on. Remove from heat when juices dry up and fish is flaky.
  5. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.
