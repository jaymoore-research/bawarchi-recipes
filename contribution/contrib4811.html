Green Pepper Bath

Ingredients

General:
Green Pepper(Bell Peppers or Kodamolagai) - 2 medium sized washed and cut into small pieces.
Mustard - 1 tsp
Cumin seeds-1 tsp
Channa dhall- 1 tsp
Bay leaves - 1 to 2
Cinnamon sticks- 2
Ghee - 1 T.Sp
Basmati Rice - 2 cups -washed and soaked for 15 minutes

To Grind:
Tomatoes- 2 medium-sized
Ginger paste - 1 tsp
Cumeen seeds - 2 tsps
Red Chillies - 6
Salt - 2 tsps or as desired
Handful of Cilantro - washed and chopped

Method:

  1. Cook rice and keep aside.
  2. Wet grind tomatoes, ginger paste, cumeen, red chillies, cilantro, salt and keep aside.
  3. Take a wok,heat ghee.
  4. Splutter mustard, cumeen seeds, channa dhal, bay leaves and cinnamon. Add curry leaves ,then bell peppers.
  5. Saute until the green bell peppers become soft.
  6. Now add the ground masala to the green peppers, and saute until flavour rises.
  7. Put the gas off and add cooked rice to the above.
  8. Mix and serve hot with Carrot salad or any raitha.
