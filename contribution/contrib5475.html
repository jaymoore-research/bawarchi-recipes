Buns

Ingredients

2 cups maida
1 tbsp bengal gram flour
1 small banana
1 tsp baking soda
1 tbsp sugar
1 cup butter milk
1 tsp table salt
oil for frying

Method

  1. Sieve maida and bengal gram flour separately and keep aside.
  2. Peel banana and mash it with curds, sugar, salt and baking soda.
  3. First, add bengal gram flour and then enough maida the curds can take.
  4. Knead well and make a lump of the dough. Apply 2 tsps of oil to it and keep under cover for 4-5 hours.
  5. Then shape portions of the dough into balls of big lime size, flatten them into small thick rounds.
  6. Deep fry them in hot oil till golden brown.

Serve hot with chilli sauce or ketchup.
