Bavakkai Oorukai (Pickle)

Ingredients

Pavakkai {karela} Big variety 1 no.
Or small 1/4 kg.
chilli powder 2 table spoon
Tamarind lemon size
1 tsp turmeric powder,
mustard seed,
1/4 tsp sugar
asafotita
salt
oil

Method:

  1. Cut the bavakkai into small pieces and Fry it in hot oil till brown.
  2. Soak the tamarind and take thick juice
  3. Put the kadai in fire and add two tea spoon of oil put mustard seeds when it crack pore the tamarind bulb, turmeric power, asafotita, salt when the raw smell of the tamarind disappeared add chilli powder and fried karela.
  4. Remove from the fire. You may add one teaspoon sugar or jaggery with the oorukai or achar.
