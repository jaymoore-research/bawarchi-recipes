Sagoo

Ingredients

Green Chillies-5-6
Coriander leaves-half a bunch
Coconut grated - half
Cinnamon sticks-3-4
Cloves-2-3
Cummin seeds- 1 tsp
Hurgadle-1 tsp (chutney dal)
Poppy seeds-1 1/2 tsp
Onions - 4 (cut in pieces)
Tomato-2-3 (cut in pieces)

Vegetables: Potatoes, Peas, Cauliflower, Carrot, French Beans

Method

  1. Chop the vegetables into cubes, wash them well and in a vessel cook them until tender.
  2. Grind all the above mentioned ingredients into paste without adding much water. Add this paste to the boiled vegetables and keep on stirring so that it does not stick to the bottom of the vessel. Add water if required. The preparation should be neither very watery nor very thick.
  3. Add required amount of salt and stir till it all gets mixed well.
  4. Remove from fire and add garnishing of mustard seeds, asafoetida and bengal gram.
  5. Serve hot with Chapatis.
