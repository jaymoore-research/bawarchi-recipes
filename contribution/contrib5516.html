Mokali Dal

Ingredients for Cooking

Chana Dal - 100g
2 small Green chilies chopped
oil - 1 tbspn
mustard - 1/2 spoon
turmeric - 1/2 spoon
asafetida - 1 pinch
coriander small 1 bunch chopped
salt as per taste
Lemon juice - 1 spoon
tender coconut - grated- 1 spoon

Method:

  1. Soak chana dal in water for 1 hrs.
  2. Once dal is soaked, remove all water and dry grind dal in food processor.
  3. Heat oil and add mustard.
  4. When mustard seeds pop up add green chilly, asafetida, and turmeric.
  5. Add grind dal , salt , and lemon juice.
  6. Mix and Cook for 3/5 min.
  7. Garnish it with coriander and coconut.

Note: This is served as Prasad during Ganapati in Vidharbha Area in India. But it is delicious for snack time also.
