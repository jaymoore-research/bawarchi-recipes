Aval Ladoo

Ingredients for Cooking

1 cup white puffed rice
1 /2cup coconut grated
3/4 cup granulated sugar
5 spoonfull milk
a bit cardamom
1/4 cup ghee

Method:

  1. Take the puffed rice and heat it in a fry pan.
  2. Stir well. Put off the gas and cool it for 5 minutes.
  3. Grind aval, coconut, sugar & cardamom in a mixy.
  4. Take a kadai (big one) heat it and add ghee to it.
  5. When the ghee is hot, add the ground ingredients and stir well.
  6. Stir for 3minutes.add milk to this &stir well for another 2 minutes. (u can add almonds or cashews if u like it.)
  7. Turn off the gasĀ after 5 minutes, make it in lemon sized ball shape using your hand.
  8. It may be a little hot to your hand, but u have to do so to get it in nice shape.
