Cilantro (Corriander) Chutney

Ingredients

2 bunches of cilantro
6-8 green chilies
2 tamatos
11/2 tablespoon salt

Seasoning:

2-3 tablespoons oil
1/2tablespoon jeera
1/2tablespoon cumin
4-5 curry leaves

Method: Take a Frying pan add oil to it chillies, tamatos, then cilantro to it fry for 10-15 minutes in medium heat and grind them when its cool add salt and add the seasoning. serve this with medu vada, dosai or plain rice.
