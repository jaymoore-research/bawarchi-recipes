Chicken Makhani

Ingredients

1-1 1/2 Small packets Whipping Cream
2-3 Fresh Tomatoes - Chopped or
1-1 1/2 Cans Tomato Paste (Hunt's preferably)
1 1/2-3 Tsp Cooking Oil (Canola prefferably) - (You can add how much ever oil U desire to)!!
2-3 Thinly Sliced Onions
1-2 Fresh Red Chillies (U can substitue this with Red Chilly Powder if U want to)
1-2 Packets Chicken Thighs/Breasts or either type (even Drumsticks will be good for this dish)!
1/2 Tsp Salt to taste
2-3 pods Fresh Garlic - chopped into thin pieces

Serves about 2-4

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a cooking pan(Dakechi) on medium heat and when oil is considerably hot, put all the sliced Onions in and fry a bit golden brown at the sides but not overfried!!
  2. Add in the Garlic and fry a bit more on low heat this time. Add in the Red Chillies and Tomatoes/(Tomato Paste) and cook further maintaining low to medium heat.
  3. Once U get a reddish and sort of oily gravy developing, add the Chicken and salt and cook on medium heat until all the Chicken is cooked well but not overcooked.
  4. Slowly start adding the Whipping Cream until U begin to see an orangish gravy forming. Do not add anymore cream after U see a thick orange colored gravy forming.
  5. Let the whole dish cook a little bit more and take off stove when gravy is thickened a bit and the oil separates from the gravy a little bit. The Chicken Makhani is ready to eat!!! Garnish with fresh Coriander leaves if desired.

Enjoy!! with a delicious Raita, Roti or Rice.
