Murmura Upma

Ingredients

Murmura
Onion:1
Green chilli:4
Curry leaves
Coriander leaves (optional)
Seasoning items.

Method:

  1. Soak murmura in water for about 7 min. The dry amount looks a lot but when sqeezed it is less.
  2. Chop onions & chillies keep aside.
  3. Take oil in kadai & when it is hot enough put 1 tsp channa dhal, urad dhal, jeera & mustard seeds. After they splutter add curry leaves. Now add chilli next onion saute them.
  4. Sqeeze murmura & add to the kadai. Add salt, turmeric & chilli power. Mix it well & keep on stove for 5 min. Garnish with coriander. You can also powder roasted peanuts & then sprinkle that on the upma.
