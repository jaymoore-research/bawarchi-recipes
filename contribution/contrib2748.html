Curry Patha Chicken

Ingredients

For 4 person as starter.
Chicken breast - 1 kg
Chilly power - 2 tea spoon
Gharam masala - 1/2 teaspoon
Lemon juice - 2 table spoon
Ginger, garlic paste -1tea spoon
Salt - to taste
Oil - 1 table spoon
Curry leaves - Hand full
Red onion - 2
Crushed black pepper - 2 teaspoon

Method:

  1. Clean the chicken, dice it in to cubes. Marinate with all the ingredients except pepper, onion and curry leaves. Leave it for 6 to 7 hours.
  2. Now pre heat the oven at 175 degrees for ten min.
  3. On a baking tray put asilicon paper and arrange the pieces cook it for half an hour turning them every now and then.
  4. After it becomes crisp remove from oven.
  5. In a pan heat 2 spoon oil when it is very warm put onion rings, pepper powder (not very fine), curry leaves all together in one go.
  6. Immediately add cooked chicken stir for aminute. Remove from heat. This can be served as starter or dry curry with rice or rotis.
