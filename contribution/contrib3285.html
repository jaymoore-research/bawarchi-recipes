Bindi Masala

Ingredients

1) Lady fingers - 1/2 kg
2) Onions - 1 or 2
3) Oil - 2 spoons
3) Jeera powder - 1 spoon
4) Besan - 1 spoon
5) Dhaniya powder 1 spoon
6) Coriander powder -1/2 spoon (this is an optional ingredient)
7) Chilly powder
8) Salt

Method:

  1. Cut onions and fry them in a kadi with 2 spoons of oil till they turn brown.
  2. Slit bindi (lady finger) into to long pieces and add them into the kadi and fry them till they are 1/2 cooked.
  3. Add salt and keep the lid and let it cook for 2 minutes.
  4. When it is cooked add chilly powder and stir it.(Be careful or else the vegetable will become over cooked)
  5. Dry roast all the jeera,dhainaya,besan powders and add them into the cooked curry and stir it and leave it for some time.
  6. The excess water will be absorbed by these powders.
  7. Garnish it with coriander leaves and serve it hot.

You can have this dish either with rice or roti. Happy cooking!!!
