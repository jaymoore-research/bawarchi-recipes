Ingredients

Sugar - according to taste
Evaporated Milk 2 can (385 ml each)
Sour Cream 285 gm
Fresh plain Yogurt 2 tbs spoon

Method

  1. Mix sour cream, fresh yogurt, sugar.
  2. Pour the evaprated milk slowly into mixture and whisk it.
  3. Pre heat oven 250.
  4. When you see the froth on top of the mixture then then put it into the pre heated oven.
  5. Bake it for 15 mins .
  6. Change the temperature to 200, bake it for another 2/3 hours.

You can serve it as a dessert after any spicy dishes like Pulau or Biriyani.
