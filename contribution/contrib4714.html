Ingredients

Liquid Milk - 1 1/2 litre
Egg - 1
Milk Powder -1/2 Kg
Ghee - 1 Table spoon
Baking Powder- 1/4 tsp
Elaichi -10
Sugar - 250 gm

Method:

  1. Pour liguid milk into the big bowl. Add elachi powder. Add sugar as required.
  2. Heat up low flame, need not to boil.
  3. Mix 1/4 spoon baking poder with egg in small bowl.
  4. Add gradually milk powder until the mix become thick enough to make small balls.
  5. Add 1 spoon ghee may required hand mixing process at the end.
  6. Make small balls and directly put into milk (preparation1).
  7. Leave for 5 to 10 minutes and turn the balls other side. Total time 10 to 15 minutes.
  8. Remove from the stove leave for cooling. Dressing if required.
  9. Put into the fridge for cooling.
