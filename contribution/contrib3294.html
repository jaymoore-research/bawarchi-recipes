Arvi Ki Sabji

Ingredients

Arvi, small 8 no
Mustard seeds 1/2 tsp
Jeera 1/2 tsp
Ajawain 1/2 tsp
Green chillies 1 no
Red chilli powder 1/2 tsp
Coriander powder 1 tsp
Turmeric powder 1/4 tsp
Curry leaves a few
Lemon (squeezed) 1/2 tbsp
Hing A pinch
Salt as per taste
Oil 1 tbsp

Method:

  1. Boil Arvi Till cooked (not very soft).
  2. Cool it and peel out the skin and cut it.
  3. Heat oil, add Hing, Mustard seeds, Jeera. Ajawain, curry leaves, Greenchillies, Red Chillies, Coriander Powder, turmeric powder, Salt and then Arvi, Fry it for some time.
  4. When done add lemon Juice.
  5. Serve hot with Puri or Chapati
