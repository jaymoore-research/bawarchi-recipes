Rawa Idli

Ingredients

Fine rawa-2 cups
Thick curd-2 cups [it should be sour]
Oil and ghee-5tbsps
Green chiilies-10-chopped finely
Cooking soda-1/2 tsp
Cut dhania and curry leaves-few
Seasoning
Mustard seeds -1 tsp
Urad dal,chana dal-1 tsp

Method:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and fry the mustard seeds and other seasonings.
  2. Fry the onions till soft and add chillies and dhania and curry leaves.
  3. To this add the rawa and fry it lightly. Add the cooking soda.
  4. Switch off the heat and then mix the curds. If using electric burner, transfer the kadai to another stove and then add the curds.
  5. Steam the above mixture immediately in idli plates for about 10-12 minutes.

Serve it hot with chutney and sambar. You can dice carrots into small pieces and add it to the mixture after frying the onions. The colour of the carrots enhances the look of the idlis. Enjoy it.
