Monaco Cheese Rolls

Ingredients

1/2 cup Paneer
1 cup crushed Monaco Biscuits
1/2 cup Milk
1 tbsp Flour
1 chopped Onion
6-8 Chashewnuts
1 tbsp Butter
Salt, Pepper & Chilli Powder to taste
Extra Monac Crumbs.

Method :

  1. Fry flour, onion & chopped nuts. Add milk & stir till thick. Remove from fire & blend in paneer, salt, pepper chilli powder & monaco crumbs.
  2. Mix well & keep for 1 hr in fridge.
  3. Shape 10-12 rolls.
  4. Roll in extra crumbs & fry till done.
