Fried Chicken Tandoori

Ingredients

10 drumsticks / chicken legs
2 tablespoon of yougurt
1teaspoon each of dhaniya powder(optional),red chili powder.
1 pinch of garam masala powder(optional)
1/2 lemon .
1 teaspoon of tandoori masala or any coloring agent ( powder form is preferred)
1 onion
2 tablespoon -garlic and ginger paste
salt to taste

Method

Ā

ĀFor Garnishing:-
Ācilantro /coriander leaves Ālemon edges Āonion rings

  1. Wash the chicken legs nicely and with the helpĀof Āfork make pierce so thatĀ the masala and the marinade gets into the chicken legsĀevenly .
  2. In a blender or mortar make a paste of the onion , (if required to make a nice & even paste one can put the garlic and ginger paste along with the onion paste)Ā
  3. Now when it is done, rub the chicken legs with the paste of onion, garlic and ginger.
  4. Then put all the ingredients one by one.
  5. Keep the marinated chicken in the refrigerator for 5/6 hrs (overnight is the best way for the marinade to get into the chicken)Ā.
  6. Then bake the chicken in the oven at 450Ā degree for about 25-30 mins till they are nicely cooked.
  7. If one doesnot have an oven ,the chicken legs can be individually fried in any oil but vegetable oil Āgives the best results & that Āalso tastes good.
  8. Garnish it and serve it hot with naan or even rice.

Bon Appetite!
