Coconut Pudhina Thohaiyal

Ingredients

1/2 Coconut [cut into tiny pieces],
One handful Coriander leaves,
One handful Pudhina leaves,
One handful Curry leaves,
Red Chillies - 6,
Ginger - a marble size,
Tamarind a marble size,
Sambar Onions-8,
Salt to taste

Method:

Grind all these ingredients coarsely. Seasoning is not needed. It will taste better if this is ground in a `ammi'.
