Bhendi Chutney

Ingredients

Bhendi/Okra: 1/2 lb
Green Chillies: 10 to 15(varies on the amount of spicyness)
Yogurt or Buttermilk: 1/2 cup
Corriander: some sprigs
Haldi: 1/2 tsp
Jeera: 1 tsp
Hing for taste
Methi powder: 1 tsp
Peanut Powder, Sesame Seeds powder: 2 tbsp each (if you don't have roast both together and add while grinding)
Salt

Method:

  1. Fry the green chillies and Bhendi separately till golden brown.
  2. Once they become warm put in a grinder take green chillies add the above mentioned ingredients except Yogurt and grind coarsely.
  3. Now add Bhendi and grind till they're done but not very smoothly as bhendi becomes mushy very quickly.
  4. Now take the chutney in a bowl, add Yogurt or Buttermilk and mix properly. If you like you can add seasoning also.

This Chutney tastes very tasty with Rice and it's a substitute for curry and sambhar. Goes well with Chapatis also. Try it and let me know your taste.
