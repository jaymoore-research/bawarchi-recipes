Aval Puttu

Ingredients

Aval( Thin Poha)Ā- 1 cup
JaggeryĀ- 1/2 cup
ElaichiĀ2 or 3
Cashew nutsĀfew pieces
Ghee five to six tea spoons
Haldi two pinches

Method

  1. Dry fry the poha or aval in a tawa so that it becomes crispy.
  2. Then grind the fried poha in mixi just for two min till it becomes fine powder.
  3. Now transfer the ground poha to another bigger vessel and add two pinches of haldi powder and mix it with hot water to the consistency that if you hold poha tight in fist it should become like a ball and if u leave it should fall back into crumbs.
  4. Now keep this aside and prepare jaggery syrup in the tawa.
  5. The syrup should not be too thick , it is enough if it is allowed to boil for about 5 min.
  6. Now mix this poha in the syrup and switch off the stove.
  7. Fried cashew and elaichi powder can be added now and mixed well with.
  8. It should be kept in a closed vessel for about an hour.
  9. When consumed after one hour it is very soft and tasty.
