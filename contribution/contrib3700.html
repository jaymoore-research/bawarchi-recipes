Macroni-Pineapple-Mint Salad

Ingredients

Macroni - 1 cup
Pineapple - 1 cup (cut into cubes)
Tomato - 3/4 cup (cut into small pieces)
Mint leaves - 2 table spoon (chopped)
Lettuce - 3 table spoon (chopped into big pieces)
Lemon juice - 1 tea spoon or according to the taste
Salt - to taste

Method:

  1. Boil macaroni with little salt, strain, keep aside.
  2. Mix pineapple, tomato, mint leaves, lettuce, lemon juice well in a bowl.
  3. Add boiled macaroni and salt. Mix gently.
  4. Chill for 15 minutes and serve.
