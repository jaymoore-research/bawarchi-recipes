Banana Sweet

Ingredients

Maida (all purpose flour) 3/4 cup
Sugar 1/2 cup
Butter 3 tbsp.
Fresh Ripened Banana 1 no.
Oil for deep frying
Water

Method:

  1. Take butter and knead well in All purpose flour. Now add sugar in it. Mix well. Add water and make dough.(consistency of the dough should not be tough like for chapatis or very loose. It should be medium)
  2. Take banana, peel the skin and cut the fruit into thin slices.
  3. Now take oil in a pan heat it under a low flame. Side by side, make small balls (small as key lime)out of the dough.
  4. Take each ball and flatten it on your left hand palm and fill one slice of banana and close it properly.
  5. Deep fry in oil until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot.

Note:

  1. While making dough, do not add too much of water for the required consistency because, as sugar and water is added to the flour, sugar dissolves in water. Chances of flour becoming very loose is more.
  2. Take Canola oil or vegetable oil for frying.
  3. You can prepare and refrigerate the sweet. (Not more than 2 days because, chances of filled banana becoming black is more.) Heat it in Microwave for few seconds just before serving.
