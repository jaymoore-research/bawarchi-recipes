Swiss Circles

Ingredients

4 slices of soft fresh bread
100 gm. paneer grated
1 bell pepper finely chopped
1 tbsp butter
2 green chillies deseeded & finely chopped
1tbsp tomato ketchup
1 pinch garam masala
1/2 tbsp salt, 1/4 tbsp chilli powder
2 tsp cornflour or maida with 2 tsp water to make a paste

Method

  1. Grate paneer and add all other ingredients to make a paste.
  2. Cut the sides of a buttered slice, keep it flat on a rolling board.
  3. Press, applying pressure with a rolling so that the holes of the bread close.
  4. Apply a layer of the filling and roll the slice carefully.
  5. Seal the edges with cornflour paste.
  6. Keep the roll, rolled up in a cloth for at least 10-15 min.
  7. Deep fry each roll in hot oil till golden . cut into 4 pieces and serve hot with mint chutney.
