Split Pea Vadas

Ingredients

Split peas- 1 cup
Onion -1 small
Spring onion-4
Garam masala-1 1/2 tsp
Chilli powder -1 tsp
Ginger-garlic paste-1 tbsp
Green chillies-1
Salt
Oil to fry

Method :

  1. Soak peas for 3-4 hours.
  2. Grind peas,onion,into a very coarse paste with less water.
  3. To this add finely chopped spring onion, garam masala, cut chillies, ginger-garlic paste and salt. If you find it hot, you can reduce the chilli powder.
  4. The above mixture should be rolled into small balls and flattened with the palm. You should make a hole in the centre.
  5. Fry it in hot oil and enjoy them as a snack or as an accompaniment for a meal.
