Instant Vadas

Ingredients

1 1/2 cup Water
1 cup Mealie Meal [coarse grinded corn]
1 cup Suji
1 cup Chana Flour
Little Grated Ginger
1 Green Chilli
Salt to taste 2 tbsp. Seasame seeds
1 1/2tsp. Eno Fruit Salt
1/2 cup Yoghurt
1 tbsp.Lemon Juice
Oil for frying

Method:

  1. Boil 1 1/2 cup water.
  2. Add mealie meal and cool.
  3. Add suji, chana flour, ginger, chilli, salt and seasame seeds.
  4. Make a dough with yoghurt and add lemon juice.
  5. Soak for 1 hour.
  6. Add eno and make vadas and deep fry till golden brown.
  7. Serve hot with Coconut Chutney.
