Cauliflower Rice

Ingredients

Cauliflower - 1 medium size
Onion - 1
Lime - 1
Basmati rice - 1.5 cups
Water - 3 cups
Salt to taste
Turmeric powder - 1 tsp.
Ghee - 2 tbs
Cashewnuts (roasted in ghee)

Fry in a teaspoon of Oil and Grind together:

Red chillies - 3
Coriander seeds - 2 tsp
Bengal gram dhal -2tsps
Urad dal - 1 tsp.
Pepper - 1/4 tsp.
Jeera - 1/2 tsp.
Garam Masala Powder - 1 tsp.

Method:

  1. Wash the rice 2 or 3 times and cook it with 3 cups of water.
  2. Cut cauliflower into small flowerets and keep immersed in salted water for 5 minutes. Wash well and boil it with little water for 5 minutes in microwave oven. Drain water after cauliflower is boiled.
  3. Add 1 tbs oil in a pan. Fry 1 onion (chopped) until golden brown then add the boiled cauliflower with it. Add turmeric powder and salt as required.
  4. Add the ground mixture mix thoroughly and cook for 5 - 10 min.
  5. Squeeze one lime and add the juice to the above cooked vegetable and mix well.
  6. Add ghee and mix the vegetable with the cooked rice.
  7. Garnish with cashewnuts.
