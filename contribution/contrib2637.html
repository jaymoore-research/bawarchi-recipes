Kathal Ki Sabzi

Ingredients

Kathal (jackfruit) (unripe) -2 lbs
Onions red - 2 medium
Tomatoes -2 medium
Oil for deep frying - mustard or any
Chilli powder 1 1/2 tsp
Turmeric powder 1/2 tsp
Coriander powder 1 tsp
Garam Masala 1/2 tsp
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Before proceeding, spread a little oil on your palms to avoid the jackfruit from sticking to your hands..
  2. Peel the kathal and chop into 1 - 1 1/2 inch pieces
  3. The seeds in the shape of almonds can be peeled and kept aside with kathal..
  4. Heat oil in kadai and deep fry the kathal and seeds. Drain excess oil.
  5. Take about 2-3 tbsps of oil and to it add sliced onions and fry till golden brown. To this add chopped tomatoes and fry for another 5-7 minutes.
  6. Add coriander powder, chilli powder, haldi powder and fry for about a minute to this add the fried kathal and mix well and cook covered for 15-20 minutes turning in between.
  7. Finally add garam masala and serve hot with chapati.
