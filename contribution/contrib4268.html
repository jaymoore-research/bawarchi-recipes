Lemonade

Ingredients

Juice of one Lemon
6 tsp Sugar
1/4 tsp Salt
1/4 tsp powder of Black Pepper
1/4 tsp juice of Ginger

Method:

  1. Take a glass and mix all these ingredients with a little water.
  2. Stir well till the sugar dissolve properly.
  3. Now add soda or chilled water in it.
  4. Serve it chilled and decorate with mint leaves and lemon.
