Spicy Green Rice

Ingredients

Onions-1 large, chopped in long thin slices.
Ginger garlic paste-1 tsp.
Cloves-5 pieces
Cardamom-3 split open.
Green chilies-6-7 split longitudinally

Mixed Fresh vegetables-
Cauliflower, green beans-cut French style, green peas, carrot-again cut in to about 1.5" long thin slices,mushrooms 3-4(button mushrooms)sliced longitudinally, u can also add red bell peppers, they add more color in to the dish.

Lemon juice-of a 1/2 medium lemon.
Coconut -2tbsp, grated.
Coriander leaves-a bunch
Mint leaves-a few.
Cumin seeds-1 tsp.
Fenel seeds-1tsp (saunf)
Oil-to fry all the above (appx 3 tbsp)
Salt-to taste.

Procedure

  1. Grind the coriander leaves and mint leaves with 2 green chilies, with some water (about 3 tbsp) till it becomes a fine paste. Keep this aside.
  2. You can use coriander chutney off the shelves of many Indian stores here.
  3. Cook the rice with cardamom and cloves in it.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan; add the following one after the other in to it-
  5. Chopped onions, fry them till they are golden brown then add ginger garlic paste, fry a little more, then add the cumin and fenel seeds, split chilis, then add the fresh vegetables, stir fry till they are done ,add the green paste in.
  6. Add a little bit of water again, u don't want it to be too dry, mix it thoroughly. Then add grated coconut, salt and lemon juice .take off the flame.
  7. Mix this with steaming hot aromatic rice (rice smells awesome with all the spicy goodies),

Garnish with a few leaves of coriander and a split green chili.
Serve with raita.
Serves 2 people. Again, easy to make with not too many complicated ingredients.
