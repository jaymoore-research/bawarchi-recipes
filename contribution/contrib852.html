Choco Roll

Ingredients

Condensed milk 1 tin
Grated coconut- 1.5 cups
Crushed marie biscuits 1 cup
Cocoa powder 1/2 cup
Crushed nuts 3/4 cups

Method

  1. Mix all ingredients and form into roll (use hands!).
  2. Wrap with cling film and put in fridge for 1 hr before cutting into slices.
  3. Can be served as dessert with good helping of cream!
