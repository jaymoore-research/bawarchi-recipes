Zucchini and Tomato chutney

Ingredients

Zucchini - 1 chopped with/without the skin
Tomato - 2 chopped
Green chillies - 4/5 cut into pieces
Garlic - 2/3 flakes
Curry leaves - few
Mint leaves - few
Salt according to taste
Coriander leaves - a handful
Oil - 2 tblsp to fry the vegetables and 1 tsp for thadka
Mustard seeds - 1/2 tsp for thadka

Method:

  1. Heat 2 tblsp of oil and fry green chillies, garlic and zucchini for a few minutes or until the zucchini is a little soft.
  2. Add tomato, curry leaves and mint and fry for a couple more minutes.
  3. Sprinkle salt according to your taste and mix it.
  4. After the fried mixture cools down, put it in a blender with fresh coriander leaves and grind it finely. Please do not add water as both the vegetables shed water.
  5. Heat 1 tsp oil for thadka and put mustard seeds. When the seeds splutter, add it to the chutney.

This chutney can be eaten with Idli, Dosai, Vadai, bajji etc. Enjoy!!
