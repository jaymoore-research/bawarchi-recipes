Palak paneer

Ingredients

Palak Leaves 2 bunches. wash in cold water and chop.
Green Chillies 2 nos.
Ginger 1" piece
Tomato 1 medium
Onion 1 large
Garlic , 1\2 chopped
Kastori Methi 2 tbsp.
Paneer 250 grms.
Oil 2 tbsp.
Fresh Cream 2 tsp optional.

Method:

  1. Wash palak nicely in water and chop, add ginger and chillies to it, cook on low flame till all the moisture is evaporated. Cool, and grind in to a fine paste, keep aside.
  2. Now grind tomato and onion together.
  3. Take a pan put oil, add garlic chopped and then onions paste, fry nicely, till the raw smell disappears, then add the palak and stir add salt to taste and some kastori methi also. Cook for a while.
  4. Lastly cut paneer in diamond shape and fry in oil, just before serving mix paneer in the palak gravy.
