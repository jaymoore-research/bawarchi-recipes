Cabbage pakoras

Ingredients

1 small cabbage finely chopped
4 green chillies chopped
2 cup gram flour
1 cup rice flour
1 small bunch coriander leaves finely chopped
1 teaspoon cumin seeds
6 teaspoon salt (adjust the amount according to taste)
1 teaspoon curry powder
1 teaspoon chilli powder
1/4 teaspoon asafoetida

Method:

  1. Mix the above ingredients with enough water to a thick batter. Heat oil.
  2. With a small rounded spoon drop the batter in the hot oil and deep fry under medium heat until golden brown.

Serving: about 50 pakoras.
