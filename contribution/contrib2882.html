Vegetable Kurma

Ingredients

Onion - 1 large, chopped
Mixed vegetables - 3 cups (Beans, Carrots, Peas, Cauliflower, Potato, Sweet corn)
Turmeric powder - pinch
Tomato - 1 large, chopped
Cilantro - finely chopped, to garnish
Salt
Oil

For the paste:

Coconut shredded - 2 to 3 tablespoons
Green chillies - 10 to 12
Cashew nuts - 20
Fennel seeds - 2 to 3 teaspoons

Method:

  1. Soak the cashew nuts in warm water for 20 minutes.
  2. Heat a little oil in a heavy vessel and fry the chopped onion.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables (except tomato), turmeric, salt and a little water. Cover the vessel and cook well till they become soft.
  4. In a blender, put together the soaked cashew nuts, green chillies, coconut flakes and the fennel seeds and make a smooth paste adding little water as required. (You don't have to waste the water in which you soaked the cashew nuts, you can add it to the vegetables)
  5. When the vegetables are well cooked, add the above paste and cook again till it mixes well with the vegetables. Add water as required. When almost done, add the chopped tomato and cook for a few minutes. Add more salt if required.
  6. Garnish with finely chopped cilantro and serve with chappatis or rice.

Variation:

Instead of the mixed vegetables, you could use only potato or only cauliflower or any other vegetable, according to your choice.
