Kalakand

Ingredients

1 Cup Sweetened Condensed Milk
1 1/2 cup dry Milk Powder
Half of quarter sized Butter
Dried Nuts (Almonds, Pistachio, Kaju) as per taste

Method:

  1. Put butter in a microwavable bowl with all the nuts and heat it on high till the butter melts.
  2. Now add the condensed milk and the dry milk powder in the same bowl, mix it well. Heat the mixture for 3 minutes.
  3. Remove the bowl mix the mixture again and put it again for 4 minutes.
  4. The mixture swells so take care not to spill it. After 4 minutes remove the bowl let it cool and then you can shape them in balls or in diamond shapes. Your Kalakand is ready to eat.

Note: If you want more brown color then you can keep it for loger time.
