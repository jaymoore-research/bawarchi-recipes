Instant Sweet Dosas

Ingredients

1 cup wheat flour
Sugar (1/4 to 1/2 cup)
Cardamom powder 1 pinch (optional)
2 tbsp coconut(optional)
Ghee
Water

Method :

  1. Mix flour, sugar, coconut and cardamom.
  2. Add enough water to make batter of thick dosa consistency.
  3. On a Flat griddle or tava spread small dosas over a tsp. of ghee.

Enjoy hot. They taste nice even without coconut and cardamom.
