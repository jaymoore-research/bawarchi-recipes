Ajwain Arbi

Ingredients

Arbi - 500g
Ajwain -Ā1/2 tsp
Chilli powder, turmeric, salt according to taste
Oil - 1 tbsp

Method

  1. Cook the arbi in a cooker
  2. Peel and chop them
  3. Heat oil in a kadai
  4. Add ajwain turmeric and chilli powder
  5. Add the chopped arbi Ā
  6. If you like it to be crisp you can later spread it on a tawa and shallow fry. Goes well with daal chawal Ā
