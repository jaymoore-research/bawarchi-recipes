Bottlegourd Halwa

Ingredients

mediumsized bottlegourd 1
sugar - 1 1/2 cup
ghee - 2 tbsp
kaju pieces 2 tbsp
kismis - 8
elaichi powder
condensed milk - 1 tin

Method:

  1. Remove the skin and seeds of the bottlegourd and grate it.
  2. Take 2 tbsp of ghee and fry the grated bottlegourd. for 10 minutes.
  3. Now add sugar to the grated bottlegourd and stir it for 15 to 20 minutes.
  4. Now add condensed milk to the above mix and stir it till the mix becomes thick.
  5. Now fry kaju,kismis in little ghee.
  6. Add elaichi, fried kaju,kismis to the above mix.
  7. Serve hot or cold.
