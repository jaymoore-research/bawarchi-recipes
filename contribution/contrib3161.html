Vegetable Fried Rice

Ingredients

1 cup Cooked rice
3/4 cup of Vegetables (cabbage, carrot, beans, onions, peas, green bell pepper, broccoli)
2 spoons Spring onions
2 spoons Soya sauce
Salt as required. (Make sure not to add too much salt as you are also adding in the Soya sauce)
1 Green chillies
1 spoon Cumin
5 spoons Oil

Method:

  1. In a fry pan take some oil (preferably olive oil) & add finely chopped green chillies.
  2. Add all the thinly sliced or diced vegetables in the pan along with some cumin & stir-fry. Do not fry too much because the idea here is to keep the vegetables crisp & tender.
  3. Add cooked rice & mix it well along with the vegetables.
  4. Add salt & Soya sauce & mix again.
  5. Garnish it with some finely chopped spring onions=20
  6. Serve it with tomato ketchup.
