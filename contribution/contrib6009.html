Bread Idli

Serves:Ā 2
Time to Cook:Ā 20 mins

Ingredients

Ā4 slices of white bread.
2 boiled potatoes.
1 cup curd
1 tbsp ginger-chilli paste.
1 medium onion grated.
2 tsp hing/asfoetida
Salt to taste
2 tbsp oil.
1 Tsp mustard seeds.
1 Tsp cumin seeds/Jeera
4-5 Curry leaves
1 Tsp Urad Dal

Method

  1. Cut the bread into circular pieces.
  2. Mash the potatoes.
  3. Add salt,grated onions, 1 tsp hing/asfoetida,ginger-chilli paste.Ā
  4. Beat the curd into a thick paste.
  5. Take a small kadai/pan.
  6. Heat the oil.Ā
  7. Add jeera/cumin seeds,mustard seeds, 1 tsp hing/asfoetida,urad dal and curry leaves.
  8. After 1 min switch of the gas.
  9. Pour the contents of the kadai into the curd.Ā
  10. Take the circular piece of bread and apply potato paste on it.
  11. Apply slight oil on the potato paste
  12. Put the Tawa on a medium-low flame.
  13. The piece of bread with potato paste has to be put on the tawa with the side containing the potato paste facing the tawa.Ā
  14. On the top of the bread spread the dahi.
  15. Cover the tawa with a big round utensil.
  16. And let the bread be on the tawa for 15-20 mins on medium-low flame.
  17. After 15-20 mins uncover the tawa and see if the bread indli is ready by inserting a knife in the bread. The knife should come out clean. Ā
  18. Garnishing & ServingĀ If you want you can garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  19. Enjoy this dish but importantly share it with someone u love!!!!Ā Ā
