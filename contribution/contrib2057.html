Aloo Palak

Ingredients

Spinach - 1 big bunch
Potatoes - 3
Onions - 2
Green Chillies - 7 to 8
Garlic - 5
Red Chilli Powder, Turmeric, Garam Masala Powder, Tamarind Powder.
Coriander leaves

Method

  1. Heat oil in pan, add chopped onions and garlic and green chillies. Add finely chopped spinach leaves.
  2. Cut potatoes to medium sized cubes. Add it. Sprinkle 1/4 cup of water and close the pan. Stir occasionally.
  3. After 10-15 minutes, when the spinach and potatoes are cooked, add red chillie powder, turmeric powder, garam masala powder, tamarind powder and salt.
  4. Add 1/4 more cup of water and stir in high flame. Remove from flame.
  5. Add chopped coriander and butter for garnishing.

Serve hot. Goes excellent with Rotis/Paraths/Plain Rice.
