Chilli Chicken

Ingredients

Half kg chicken
3-table spoons Worcester sauce
1 tsp Soya sauce
Half tsp pepper powder
Half tsp salt
[Mix all the above ingredients and put in the
Refrigerator previous night or 2 to 3 hours before cooking.]
Onions 2 big medium size
Green chilies 10-12
Capsicum 1 large size
Coriander leaves 2 bunches
Pepper and salt for taste
Oil as required
Salt to taste.

Method:

Put 3 table spoons of oil in a kadai, put onions and fry till golden brown, put 10 or 12 green chilies Pieces, one capsicum cut into pieces fry them and add the marinated chicken pieces, and let it boil. Add two bunches of coriander leaves, little china salt and fry on low flame. Add pepper powder if necessary.
