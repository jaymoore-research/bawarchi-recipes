Lime Rasam

Ingredients

1.Tuvar dal (boiled with 1cup of water)- 1/2 cup
2.Fresh lime-1 no.(medium size)
3.Water-1 cup
4.Turmeric-1/2 tsp
5.Coriander seeds-1tsp (ligthly toasted)
6.Jeera seeds-1tsp (lightly toasted)
7.Black pepper-1/2tsp (ligthly toasted)
8.Garlic cloves-2-3nos (slightly mashed)
9.Green chillies-2-3nos(slit)
10.Curry leaves-8-9leaves noswith stem
11.Coriander stems-3-4 nos
12.Oil-2tsp
13.Mustard seeds-1 tsp
14.Asafoetida-a pinch
15.Salt-as per taste

Method:

  1. Take a saucepan add boiled tuvar dal with water, 1 cup water, turmeric powder.
  2. Make a powder of all toasted seeds (jeera, coriander, pepper)add half of powder to the tuvar dal mixture, add garlic, coriander stems & curry leaves to it.
  3. Let this mixture boil on medium flame ,it should not be over boiled. Once it it boiled remove it, now add the remaining powder, juice of lime & salt toit and stir well
  4. Heat oil in a pan to it add mustard seeds, green chillies & asafoetida to it add this tadka or tampering to the lime rasam. Check the salt.
  5. Serve this lime rasam hot with steaming rice. Happy cooking!!!
