Carrot Rice

Ingredients

1 cup grated Carrot
1/2 tsp grated Ginger
1/2 tsp Khus Khus (Poppy seeds)
3-4 Hot Pepper (chopped)
2 tbsp Olive or any Veg. Oil
1 cup Basmati Rice
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Rinse and soak rice with measured amount of water for 1/2 an hour. Then cook till it is done.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and saute the first four ingredients for 2-3 min. Add salt and blend it good. Mix this with rice with a fork. Serve hot.
