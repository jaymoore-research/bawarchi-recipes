Kesar Kalakhand

Ingredients

1 1/2 litres fresh whole milk
2 tablespoons desi ghee
3 tablespoons curd (curd should NOT be sour)
1 tin/200 gms Milkmaid /Condensed milk
10 raw almonds sliced into slivers
3-4 strands of saffron
3 big cardamoms
2 teaspoons sugar

Non-stick kadhai/wok to make the dish. If one is not available, you might have to use more ghee (4 tablespoons) to prevent the Kalakhand from sticking to the utensil.

Method

  1. Soak saffron in 2 teaspoons milk, set aside.
  2. Boil milk till it reduces to 1/2 the quantity.
  3. Add curd and keep stirring till the curdling stops (around 10 minutes).
  4. Add condensed milk and keep stirring. Add 1 tablespoon of ghee and all the remaning ingredients (including dissolved saffron), except almonds.
  5. Stir for about 20 minutes. Add remaining ghee, mix and pour the mass into a greased plate. Garnish with almond slivers immediately.
  6. Cut into pieces when cool.
