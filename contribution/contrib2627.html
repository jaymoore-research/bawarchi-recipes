Bharwa Bhindi

Ingredients

Bhindi (Ladies finger) 250 gms
Jeera 1 tbsp
Garam Masala 1 tbsp
Oil 2-3 tbsp

For Filling:

Turmeric powder 1/2 tbsp
Coriander powder 1 tbsp
Red chilli powder 1/2 tbsp
Saunf 1 tbsp
Salt 1 tbsp

Method:

  1. Wash Bhindis and make them dry.
  2. Remove the caps and make slit in all Bhindis lengthwise from top to bottom.
  3. Mix all the ingredients of filling. Fill this fillng in Bhindis very carefully so that it does not come out from Bhindis.
  4. Now put 2-3 table spoon of oil in the pan.
  5. Put jeera, when it splits, put the rest of the filling.
  6. Now put stuffed Bhindis and cover it. After 5-10 min when it get cooked put Garam masala on top of it.
  7. Serve hot with Chapatis.
