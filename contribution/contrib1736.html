Cole Salugh Salad

Ingredients

Cabbage : 100 gms
Carrot : 2 No's
Mayonnaise : 2 Tablespoons
Yoghurt (Thick) : 1 to 2 Tablespoons.
Sugar : 1 teaspoon.
Salt : little to taste

Method :

  1. Grate the cabbage and carrot.
  2. Take the mayonnaise, yoghurt, salt and sugar and beat it nicely till soft with a churner. To this beaten mixture add the grated vegetables raw. Mix it well and refrigerate it.
  3. Once it is chill, take out & serve to have a wonderful tasty salad.
