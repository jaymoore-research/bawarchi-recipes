Badam Burfi

Ingredients

1 cup Badam
1 and half cup sugar
Half cup Ghee

Method

  1. Soak badam for two hours in hot water and peel the skin.
  2. Grind it into a thick paste with very little water.
  3. Make sugar syrup of single thread consistency and put the ground badam and keep stirring.
  4. Pour ghee if the mixture startes sticking to the sides.
  5. Keep stirring until it does not stick to the sides and forms as a ball and then put in a greased plate.
  6. Cut into diamond shape and decorate with silver foil.
