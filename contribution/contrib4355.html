Red Chilli Chutney

Ingredients

10-12 Bedgi Dry Red Chillies
12 flakes Garlic
Some Tamarind to get that tangy taste
Salt to taste

If the chillies are not real hot, add some chilli powder.

Method:

  1. Soak the chillies in warm water for 10 minutes.
  2. Drain the water from the chillies and keep aside.
  3. Add some water to the tamarind ball (size of a small lemon) and crush it.
  4. Put the chillies after they have been soaked, into the grinder.
  5. Add the garlic flakes, the tamarind paste, salt to taste and grind well.
  6. Add the drained water into the mixie to make a fine paste.

This chutney tastes real hot and looks RED.. and can be eaten with upma, parathas, bread almost anything.
