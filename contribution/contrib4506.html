Onion Pancakes

Ingredients

For Filling

Chop fine 100 gms Spring Onionsy 4 Chillies
Grate 100 gms Cheedar Cheese.
Add to chopped onions. Season with 1/2 tsp ajinomoto, salt, pepper to taste.

For Dough

1 cup Maida
1/2 tsp Salt and Water

Method:

  1. Divide dough into 20 portions. Roll out each into a small thin puri. Put the stuffing in the middle shape as momos or wantons and deep fry in oil. Serve with a sweet dipping sauce.
  2. SWEET DIPPING SAUCE-FOR 16: Combine 1 cup white vinegar and 1 cup castor sugar in a small saucepan. Stir over heat without boiling until sugar has dissolved Bring to boil. Uncovered without stirring for about 3 mins or until thickened slightly. Cool for 5 minutes. Pour over combined 1 small green cucumber chopped , 1 small carrot grated, 1 tbsp chopped chives and chill in a small bowl cool. Sprinkle wth paprika before serving.

Makes: 20 pieces
