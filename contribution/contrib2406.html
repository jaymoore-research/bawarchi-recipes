Shavge Payasam

Ingredients

Shavage or Vermecille 1 pocket
Ghee
Milk 1 litre
Sugar 5 tsp
Elaichi 2
Kaju or Cashew 10 grams
Raisins 10 grams
Badami or Almond 10 grams.

Method:

  1. Break the vermecille to small bits and fry them in ghee until it is dark brown, or it starts to give that aroma.
  2. After it cools, take a little bit of cool milk and add the vermecille into it. See to that it does not become clusters.
  3. Then heat it in a low flame, slowly add the rest of the milk stirring it occassionally and bring it to a boil.
  4. After you bring it down from the stove add Sugar and stir it.
  5. Add powdered elaichi.
  6. Saute or fry Almonds, raisins and cashews in Ghee and add them to the payasam.

Optional:

You can grind the Almonds and elaichi into a fine powder and add them to the Payasam in the end.
