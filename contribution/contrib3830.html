Veg. Seekh Kababs

Ingredients

1 cup Black Chanas (soaked overnight boiled & mashed)
2 Potatoes (medium) (boiled &mashed)
3-4 Green Chillies (finely chopped)
1 tsp Ginger (finely chopped)
1 tsp Chilli powder
1/2 tsp Amchur powder
Salt to taste
1 tsp Coriander leaves

For binding

2 tsp Rice Flour
2-3 Bread slices
2 tsp Bread Crumbs

Method:

    Mix together all the ingredients and skewer them on knitting needles. Remove them gently from the needles and deep fry.
  1. Serve garnished with onion rings and lemon wedges.

Note: After boiling chanas remove excess water and dry them completely by heating them then use them in the recipe.
