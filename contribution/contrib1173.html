Ingredients

I cup rajama boiled
2 tbs. Coriander leaves
1/4 tsp. Dry ginger powder.
1/2 cup Tomato sauce
1/4 cup Kala Namak
1/4 cup chopped Onion
1/2 tsp Salt
1/4 tsp Pepper
1/4 tsp Red Chilli powder
1 tsp Sugar
1/2 tsp powdred Jeera

Method:

Mix all ingredients and decorate with few pieces of cut tomato.
