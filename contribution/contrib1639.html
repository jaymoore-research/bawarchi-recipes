Potato Tambude

Ingredients

5 peeled Potatoes
2 Tomatoes
2 tbsp. Oil
1/2 tsp. Urid dal
1/2 tsp. Coriander seeds
5 Methi seeds
1/4 tsp. Turmeric Powder
1/4 tsp. Asafoetida
1/4 Mustard seeds
1 tbsp. grated Coconut
3 Red Chillies
Few Coriander Leaves
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Cut potatoes into small square pieces, mix some salt & keep aside for 10 min. Deep fry it in a pan.
  2. In a pan fry grated coconut (without oil) till it is golden brown. Then remove.
  3. Pour some oil in the pan & fry red chillies, urid dal, coriander seeds, methi seeds & mustard seeds.
  4. Grind coconut and other fried ingredients (coarsely).
  5. In a pan pour oil, add mustard seeds, turmeric powder, asafoetida powder , chopped tomatoes, curry leaves. Fry for a while, add the grinded ingredients , add 1/2 cup of water, when it boils add fried potatoes and coriander leaves & add salt.
  6. Serve with Chapathi or Rice. Enjoy this delicious Konkani dish.
