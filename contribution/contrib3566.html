Pesarattu Rolls

Ingredients

a) Moong Dal 1 Cup (Soak overnight)
b) Red Chilies 5 nos (if you want it to be spicy)
c) Ginger 1/4 (as per your taste)
d) Salt To taste
e) Jeera 1/2 tsp

Ingredients for the fillings:

a) Chopped Onions 1 cup
b) Cilantro
c) Cheese singles
d) Parmesan Cheese (grated)

Method:

  1. Blend the soaked Moong Dal and the rest of the ingredients to a thick batter. Allow it to settle for 1/2 hr .
  2. On a non-stick tava/ iron griddle spread the batter to a fine dosa (make them not too small or big).
  3. Now take the pesarattu, place the cheese single or spread any cheese of your choice, top it with chopped onions, cilantro, parmesan cheese and roll it.
  4. Cut (french cut) the rolls to smaller size and microwave it for 10 seconds (for the cheese to melt).
  5. on the serving plate place the rolls and prick tooth picks to hold them and garnish with cilantro. Have some hot chutney to go with it....

Note: can have tooth picks to hold the rolls on the serving plate
