Cab - Chee Rice

Ingredients

2 cups rice
1 cup finely chopped cabbage
1/2 cup grated cheese
1/2 tsp. chopped garlic
1 small onion (chopped)
1 tbsp. chopped capsicum/green bell pepper
salt & pepper to taste

Method:

  1. Cook rice and cool.
  2. Fry the chopped garlic and onion till it turns golden brown. Once done, add the capsicum/green bell pepper and cabbage and stir-fry for 1-2 minutes on low heat.
  3. Add the rice and stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes. While frying, add salt and pepper as required.
  4. Transfer to a serving dish and serve immediately with grated cheese on top.
  5. Serves 4 people with normal appetites!

* For non-veggies, you can add minced meat or small boneless chicken/mutton pieces while frying.
