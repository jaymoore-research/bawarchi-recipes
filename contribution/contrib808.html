Golden Egg Delight

Ingredients

6 Eggs
6 Bread slices
Oil (for frying)

Method :

  1. Boil the eggs for 20 minutes in water. Let it cool and remove the shells.
  2. Take some water in a bowl. Dip a bread slice in the water and place it between your hands and press such that all the water drips out, then place a boiled egg in the now wet bread slice and wrap it round the egg.
  3. Repeat the same for the rest of the eggs. Fry the eggs in hot oil until golden brown serve with ketchup or with any sauce of your choice.

Tip:

Fat Free Papad in seconds

Place lijjat papad in a microwavable plate and microwave for 35-40 seconds. (1000watts Microwave)
Time may vary depending on the microwave. You may spray Pam on the Papad and microwave for the same amount of time for those of you who like it with little oil.
