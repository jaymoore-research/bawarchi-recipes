Pongal

Ingredients

1 cup white rice
1/2 cup masoor daal
1/4 tsp fresh ground pepper
1/4 tsp whole peppercorns
1 tsp salt
4 green chillies
A few curry leaves
A pinch of asafoetida
1 tbl spoon ghee
1 tbl spoon oil
1 tbl green coriander

Method

  1. Wash rice and daal and drain water completely.
  2. In a large sauce pan, heat ghee and oil on medium heat. Slit green chillies length wise. Fry the green chillies, curry leaves, pepper powder and peppercorns.
  3. Add the rice, daal and asafoetida to this and fry for 3 minutes.
  4. Add 4 1/2 cups of water, salt and cover the sauce pan.
  5. Cook it on medium heat till rice and daal become very soft and keep stirring in between to avoid burning. Add more water if necessary.
  6. Garnish with coriander and serve hot with coconut chutney.
