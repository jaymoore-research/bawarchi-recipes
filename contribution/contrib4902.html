Noodle Bhaji

Ingredients

1 packet of noodles(top ramen or maggie,any brand).
onions-2
green chillies-4
ginger-2
garlic -2 to 4 pods
gram flour- 2 cups.
1 tbsp oil,
1/4 tsp mustard seeds,
3-4 curry leaves,
salt,
1/4 tsp sugar,
1/2 tsp chilly powder,
1/4 tsp turmeric powder,
1/2 tsp cumin seeds,
1/4 tsp fenugreek seeds
and a tsp of Eno.

Method:

  1. Heat 1tbsp oil in a kadai, put in mustard seeds,curry leaves,cumin seeds,fenugreek seeds,onions ,ginger and garlic(cut into small pieces).
  2. When brown,add the noodles along with its masala,fry a bit,add water,check for salt and cook till done.
  3. After it is done,keep it aside.
  4. In a bowl,take the gram flour,add water,green chillies,salt, a pinch of sugar and mix to form a nice thin batter.
  5. Add some cumin seed to this mixture too.
  6. Check for salt.
  7. Heat oil in a kadai,enough to fry the bhajis.
  8. Mix the noodles with the gram flour batter,just before frying add a spoon of Eno to the mix.
  9. Spoon out the mix with a tsp into the already hot oil and fry till it is nice and brown.
  10. After soaking out excess oil with tissues,place bhajis in a plate,sprinkle some chat powder on the hot bhajis and serve with tomato sauce,green chutneyand tamarind chutney!
