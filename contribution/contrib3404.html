Quickie-Cake

Ingredients

1 cup Cream
1 cup Maida
1/4 cup Rava
1 cup Sugar
2 Eggs
1/2 tsp Baking Powder
Nuts, Raisins and Dates

Method:

  1. Beat the eggs, cream and sugar in a mixer-grinder.
  2. Pour it in a vessel, add maida slowly, while stirring continuously to avoid lumps. Add baking powder and the dry fruits. Keep aside for 1 hour.
  3. Pour the cake mix into a greased baking dish and bake for 40 minutes.
