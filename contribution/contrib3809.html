Avial

Ingredients

Onion-3 (big)
Tomato- 3
Potato- 1 (big)
Coconut- half
Cashewnut-6
China dhal- 1(teaspoon)(not necessary)
Soombu-1(teaspoon)
Kasakasaa- half (teaspoon)
Dry Chillies- 10 to 13
Salt

Method:

  1. Scrape the coconut, clean and chop the onion and tomato, cut the potato in diamond shape.
  2. Put little oil in the pan, then put mustard, dhal, onion and tomato. Fry that well. After 5 minutes add potato and salt, fry for 4 to 5 minutes, after that add 5 tumblers water.
  3. Grind Coconut, cashew nut, soombu, kasakasaa, dry chillies and china dhal to a fine paste.
  4. When potato is cooked well. Mix this paste with that. Sim the gas stove. After 10 to 15 minutes the avail is ready. This gravy is nice combination for Idli and Dosai.
