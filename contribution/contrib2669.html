Tomato Curry

Ingredients

Tomatoes 6 big
Onion 1
Green Peas 1/2 cup frozen
Red Chilli powder 1 1/2 tsp
Turmeric Powder 1/2 tsp
Garam Masala Powder 3/4 tsp
Salt as per taste
Oil 3-4 tbsp
Mustud and Jeera seeds 1 tsp each

Method:-

  1. Heat oil in a pan and put the musturd and jeera seeds in it once they start to split then add onions to it and boil them until golden brown.
  2. Then add tomatoes to it and heat it for 4 min by closing the pan with the lid
  3. Add green peas to it and heat it for 1 min.
  4. Now add all the above masalas to it and mix it throughly and heat it for 2 min and remove it. (you can even add the boiled eggs to it so that it tastes even better but its optional)

Now the hot and garam tomato curry is ready you can serve it with Rice or Chapathis.

Hope you enjoy it.
