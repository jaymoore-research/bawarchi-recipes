Chintapandu Pullihara

Ingredients

1 cup rice
5-6 spoons thick tamarind juice
4 spoons of oil
4 red chillies
1 spoon channadal
1/2 spoon uddahadal
1/4 spoon mustard seeds
1/2 spoon jeera
1/4 spoon asofoetida
1/4 spoon haldi
few curry leaves
salt to taste

Method:

  1. Cook the rice in such a way that each grain is separated. Soak channadal in little water and keep aside.
  2. Add oil in a pan and heat it.
  3. After heating the oil add red chillies, uddahadal,mustard,jeera,asofoetida and keep heating till uddahadal becomes brown.
  4. Then add curry leaves,channadal, tamarind juice and some salt. Cook this for 3 minutes.
  5. Add this tamarind juice and others into the cooked rice. Mix well all the ingredients and serve with/without lemon pickle.
