Thavala Dosa

Ingredients

Raw Rice - 6 small cups
Toor dal + Moong dal + Urid dal - 1 cup
Pepper, Salt - for taste

Method:

  1. Break the raw rice in the mixie just like rava. Add hot water in it upto it gets wet.
  2. Soak toor dal, moong dal, urid dal in a same bowl for 3-4 hours. Grind it in the mixie. While grinding add salt and pepper. Remove it from the mixie and mix it with the rice rava. Put it for the night. Next day, it will be a thick paste.
  3. It should be poured in a shallow thick bottommed bowl and should be closed. Put it in sim position in the gas. Pour oil just like dosa. After 10 minutes it will be ready. It should be fried one side only.
