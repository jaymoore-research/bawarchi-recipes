Mixed Vegetable Saagu

Ingredients

Vegetables:
1 med size Potato
2 carrots
1 capsicum
Green Peas
Beans
1 Tomato
1 Onion
(You can also add other vegetables like cauliflower, a bunch of spinach etc)

1 Onion
4-5 cloves
2-3 cinnamon sticks
4-5 black pepper corns
3-4 green chillies
1/2 bunch coriander
5-6 mint leaves (pudina)
1/2 cup grated coconut
1 tsp dalia ( or chutney dal, called hurikadale in kannada)
(In case you dont have this u can substitute with chana dal. But before grinding chanadal, fry it till its light brown )
Oil - for frying
and salt, of course.

For Seasoning:
1 tsp mustard
1 tsp urad dhal
5-6 Curry leaves

Method:

  1. Cut the vegetables into cubes or long stripes. Wash and boil them (except the tomatoes) in water. You can retain this water for later use. Add the tomatoes when the other veggies are almost done.
  2. Chop the onion, coriander. In a pan take a little oil and fry the onions till their raw smell goes.
  3. In the same pan heat 1/2 tsp oil. Fry the cloves, pepper, cinnamon, and mint leaves.
  4. Grind the ingerdients in step 2 and 3 along with the dal, coconut, green chillies and corianer leaves.
  5. Once the veggies are almost done, add the tomatoes and the ground masala salt to taste and boil for 7 - 10 mins. Add 1 cup water before boiling as per the consistancy you like.
  6. In a pan, prepare seasoning. Heat oil, put urad dhal, mustard and the curry leaves... and when the mustard splutters add it to the well boiled saagu.

Enjoy this while hot with hot chapathis or Puris! :)
