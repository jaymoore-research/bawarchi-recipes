Cabbage Rice

Ingredients

1 cup Basmati Rice
1 cup Cabbage chopped
1 Onion chopped
quarter cup Peas

Grinding Masala:

Few Coconut grated, 2 Cardamoms, 4 Cloves, 6 Green Chillies, few Ginger.

Method:

  1. Soak the rice for 10 minutes
  2. Take a pan pour oil fry onion, cabbage and peas.
  3. Add the grinding masala for 5 minutes and than add rice fry for few minutes.
  4. Put all this into rice cooker or pressure cooker add 2 cups of water and salt. After 15 minutes now the cabbage rice ready. Note for pressure cooker keep 2 whistle.

Serve with Pappds or Pickle and Onion Raitha.
