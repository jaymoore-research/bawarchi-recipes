Sam's Chicken Fry

Ingredients

1. Chicken Pieces 1 Kg
2. Red Chilly Powder 1 tsp
3. Turmeric Powder 1 tsp
4. Garam Masala Powder 1/2 tsp
5. Garlic Paste 1 tsp
6. Ginger Paste 1 tsp
7. Green Chilly Paste 1 tsp
8. Salt to Taste
9. Sugar 3/4 tsp
10. Water 2 cups

Ingredients for Covering:

11. Maida 5 tsp
12. Cornflour 5 tsp
13. Egg White 1
14. Salt to Taste
15. Chicken Cube 1/2 Cube
16. Ajinomotto 1/2 tsp
17. Sugar 1/4 tsp
18. Cooking Oil to Fry

Method:

  1. Mix Ingredients no:1 to 10 and pressure cook for 15 Min.
  2. Pour out 1/2 cup of gravy from the cooker and mix it with ingredients no:11 to 17 to get a moderately thick paste.
  3. Dip cooked chicken pieces in the paste to get a thick covering .
  4. Fry the pieces fully immersed in oil till the covering is light brown.
