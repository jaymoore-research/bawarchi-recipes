Beet Root Pattice

Ingredients

Tur dal - one cup
Beet Root - 3
Onion - one (finely chopped)
Jeera - one teaspoon
Red Chilly - 5-6
Curry Leaves & coriander Leaves -finely chopped
Vegetable Oil - for deep frying
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Soak the dal in water for an hour. Drain the water & grind it to paste with red chillies & jeera
  2. Peel the beet root & grate it finely.
  3. Add the grated beet root, salt to taste, chopped onions, curry leaves & coriander leaves to the dal paste & mix well. Make sure that the consistency of the dough is just right to make Tikkies.
  4. Roll them into nice tikkies & deep fry.
  5. Serve it hot with Tomato Sauce & coriander chutney.
