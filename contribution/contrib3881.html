Mango Icecream

Ingredients

1 can Mango pulp
1 can sweetened Condensed Milk
1 container "Cool Whip" available in the super markets frozen dairy section.
1/2 cup chopped Pistachio nuts.
2 tsp Rose Water.

Method:

Mix mango pulp, condensed milk, cool whip with electric mixer for 4-5 mts. Add rose water. Pour it in airtight container (rubbermaid, tupperware etc), sprinkle with chopped nuts. Freeze it overnight in your freezer. Makes about 20 servings.
