Rava Idli

Ingredients

500gms Rava (upma Rava)
3 cups Plain Yogurt
1 cup grated Carrot
1 cup peas
1/2 cup cashew halves

for Seasoning
1tsp Mustard seeds
1tsp chana dal
1tsp urad dal
3 or 4 red chillies
2 green chillies
a pinch of hing
A few curry leaves
Salt to taste
Oil for frying

Method

  1. Heat oil in a pan (add little more oil). Add Mustard seeds, Chanadal, Uraddal, hing, red chillies, green chillies, curry leaves & Cashews. When it splutters add rava and fry till it becomes little brown.
  2. When it cools down, add yogurt to the rava mix. Stir well, so all rava mix mixes with yogurt. Then add grated carrot & peas and salt. Keep aside for 4hrs.
  3. If the rava batter is too thick you can add little water. Pour the rava batter into idli moulds and steam for 15-20 min and you"ll have hot ravaidlis to be enjoyed with GINGER CHUTNEY

GINGER CHUTNEY

Ingredients
50gms ginger
3 green chilles
2tbsp grated coconut
1cup coriander chopped
Small lemon sized soaked tamarind
2tbsp jaggery
1 small onion
1tsp turmeric
Salt to taste

Method

grind all the above ingerdients. Season it with mustard seeds. Enjoy Rava Idlis with this mouth watering chutney.
