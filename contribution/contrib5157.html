Easy pakoda

Ingredients

Besan - 1 cup
Oil - for mixing and for frying
Salt - to taste
Garam Masala powder - 1 tsp
Onion - finely chopped
Coriander leaves - a little
Red chilli powder - 1tsp

Method :

  1. Mix all the dry ingredients first.
  2. Mix them dry.
  3. Add a little oil to it.
  4. Then add water to it accordingly and make a half water constituent.
  5. Add onion and coriander leaves.
  6. Heat oil in tawa.
  7. Then take small scoops of the paste with hand and fry.
  8. Serve with tomato sauce.

N:B - You can add vegetables if needed.
