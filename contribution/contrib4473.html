Jackfruit Seed Fry

Ingredients

1 Kg Jackfruit Seeds, boiled soft
1 tsp Ginger paste
1 tsp Garlic paste
2 tsp Red Chilli powder
1 tsp Jeera powder
1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
Salt as per taste
1 tbsp Lemon Juice
Oil for frying

Method:

  1. Marinate the seeds with ginger and garlic paste, red chilli powder, jeera powder, turmeric powder, salt and little lime juice for 10 minutes.
  2. Then shallow fry them till red and crispy.
  3. Garnish with fried onions.
