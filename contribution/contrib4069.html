Patishapta

Ingredients

For Batter

Rice powder - 2 cup
Flour - 1/2 cup
Cold Milk or Water - 1-1/2 cup
Salt - 1/2 tsp
Sugar - 2 tsp

For coconut Pur:

Fresh grated Coconut - 500 gms
Jagerry - 500 gm
Oil to make it - 1 cup.

Method:

  1. Make a batter of all ingredients and keep aside for 10 minutes.
  2. Heat a kadai. Add jaggery, and after melting it add coconut and cook untill it makes a sticky dough like mixture. Cool it.
  3. Heat a pan. Add 1 tsp of oil and spread it. Put some batter in the pan and spread it lightly. It makes like a pan cake. Stuff the coconut pur and rolled it. Serve hot.
