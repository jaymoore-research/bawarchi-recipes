Methi Leaves Adai

Ingredients

A bunch of Fenugreek Leaves
Parboiled Rice 1 Cup
Bengal Gram dal 1 Spoon
Green Gram dal 1 Spoon
Urad Dal 1 Spoon
Tuvar dal 1 Spoon
Moong Dal 1 Spoon (Optional)
Asafoetida Powder 1tbsp
Green Chillies 3
Grated Coconut (Optional)
Chopped Onion (Optional)
Salt (according to taste)
Coriander Leaves For Garnish
Oil (Shallow Fry)

Method:

  1. Wash rice. Soak it for min 3 to max 5 hours.
  2. Similarly, wash all the dals. Soak them all together for 3 to 5 hours.
  3. Grind the ingredients with salt, green chillies and asafoetida powder.
  4. Rinse and drain the fenugreek leaves from dirt and chop the leaves in fine manner.
  5. Take the batter and mix the chopped fenugreek leaves (Methi Leaves).
  6. Add the grated coconut, chopped onion and the coriander leaves.
  7. Heat tawa on medium high and spread the batter on the top (like dosa) and shallow fry with edible oil.
  8. Serve adai with avial (Adai should be taken when it is hot).

It tastes good and fenugreek leaves (Methi leaves) are good for health.
