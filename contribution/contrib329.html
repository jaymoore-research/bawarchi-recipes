Sweet and Sour Karela

Ingredients

1/2 kg Karels ( bitter gourd)
4 tbps chilli powder
1 tbsp tumeric powder
a lemon sized ball of tamarind
lemon sized ball of jaggery
salt to taste

For seasoning:

2tbsps. of oil
1 tsp. mustard seeds
1/2 tsp fenugreek ( methi seeds)
1/4 tsp asafoetida(hing)
8-10 green chillis
1 sprig curry leaves

Method:

  1. Cut the karela into thin slices, apply salt and keep it aside for 3-4 hrs. (You could do it overnight too).
  2. Squeeze out the awter and deep fry till crisp.
  3. Drain and keep aside. Soak tamaring in hot water and extract juice and keep aside.
  4. Fry mustard in 2 tbsp of oil. When the seeds splutter add fenugreek, asafoetida, curry leaves and small slices of green chillis, chilli and tumeric powders.
  5. Add jaggery and tamarind juice. When the gravy thickens add the fried karela slices and remove from fire.
  6. Serve hot with chappatis, rotis or nans.
