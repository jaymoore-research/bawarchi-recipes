Green Mutton

Ingredients

1 kg Mutton.
1 Bunch coriander leaves.
5-6 Green chillies.
1/4 Bunch mint leaves.
5-6 Pods of garlic.
One long stick of ginger.
5 gm Peppercorns.
5 gm Cloves.
5 gm Cinnamon.
5 gm Cummin seeds.
1 Big onion.
1 Tomato.
1 tps Red chilly pwd.
Salt to taste.
2 tbs Oil for frying.

Method :

  1. Clean, cut and wash the mutton and pressure cook it for 10-12 mins.
  2. Grind the ingredients in the mixie.
  3. Dice one onion and fry it in oil then add chopped tomato and fry the ground masala for 5-8 mins.
  4. To this add the mutton and cook for 10-14 mins on slow fire.

It goes well with hot rice or rotis.

Hope you'll enjoy this dish. Happy eating.
