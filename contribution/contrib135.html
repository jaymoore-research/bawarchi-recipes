Fried Ice cream

Ingredients

Ice cream
Sponge cake
Oil to deep fry.

For batter
Self raising flour (Maida)
Egg & Milk

Garnish
Mango slices(With mango ice cream)
Other fruits depending upon the ice cream used

Method

  1. Make balls of Ice cream (make sure that Ice cream is hard frozen
  2. Wrap each ball in thick sponge cake tightly & freeze these balls.
  3. Deep fry hard frozen balls just until the batter is solid
  4. Serve immediately on a platter of fruit slices.

This recipe comes from China the story is like this A cook was asked to serve fish(single) whose half side was deep fried and the other half was just steamed. To solve this problem the cook used a melon like fruit to wrap one half of the fish and dipped the other half in batter and deep fried this sandwich in hot oil.
