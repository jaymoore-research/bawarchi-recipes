Email: CHUPPANATI@prodigy.net

Chakrapongali

Ingredients

1 cup basmati rice
1/2 cup moong daal
1/4 cup chenna daal
6 cardomoms
10 full cashews
A few sliced almonds
A few raisins
2 cups sugar
1 cup pure ghee
1 liter milk

Method:

  1. Fry the moong and chenna daal in 2 table spoons of ghee.
  2. Wash the basmati rice and drain it. Fry the rice lightly in 1 table spoon of ghee for a minute or two.
  3. Boil the rice, moong daal and the chenna daal together in the milk. After the rice and the daals are well cooked, add sugar and ghee.
  4. Leave everything cooking for 10 more minutes on low flame.
  5. Fry the cashews, almonds, and raisins in a table spoon of ghee, and add them to the mix. Peel off the cardomoms, and powder them, and add the power to the mix.
  6. Be careful that the rice mix does't burn at the bottom.

This is a good dessert and is enough for about 4 adults.
