Mexican Sandwich

Ingredients

Bread Slices 12 nos.
Amul Butter 3 tablespoon.
Onion 1 small chopped.
Tomato 1 small chopped.
Boiled Potato 1 small chopped.
Green Chillies 1-2 chopped finely.
Boiled corn 1 cup.
Grated cheese 1/2 cup.
Salt to taste.
Pepper to taste.
Tomato sauce 2 tablespoon.
Chilli sauce 2 tablespoon.
White sauce 1 cup.

Ingredients for White Sauce.

Milk 1 cup.
Amul butter 1 tablespoon.

Method :

  1. Prepare white sauce by heating 1 tablespoon butter in a pan. Add maida. Fry lightly. Add milk. Stir till it becomes like sauce.
  2. Add chopped onion, tomato, potatoes, green chillies, boiled corn, half cheese, salt and pepper.
  3. Lightly toast the bread slices. Apply butter on one side only.
  4. Spread the white sauce and vegetable mixture on the buttered side. Put the remaining grated cheese on top.
  5. Sprinkle tomato and chilli sauce and bake at 350 degree F or 160 degree C for ten minutes or till cheese melts.
  6. Serve hot.
