Boneless Chicken

Ingredients

2 lb. boneless thigh chicken meat, washed & cut in small pieces.
2 tbsp.Yogurt.
1 tbsp.Tomato puree.
2 tsp. Priya nonveg. curry paste or Kashmiri masala paste.
1 tsp. Garlic paste.
1 tsp. Ginger paste.
1/2 tsp. Chilli powder
1/2 tsp. Garam-Masala powder
1/4 tsp. Turmeric powder
Salt to taste.
1 cup Water
2 tbsp. Olive Oil or any cooking oil.
2-3 Green Chillies finely chopped.
1 tbsp. finely chopped green Coriander.
1/2 cup Whipping Cream.

Method:

  1. In a bowl mix yogurt, tomato puree, masla paste, garlic paste, ginger paste, chilli powder, garam masala powder, turmeric powder & salt.
  2. Heat oil in a heavy based karahi or dutch oven & add this mixture to it. Fry for two minutes, then add cleaned chicken, saute it for two-three minutes, till it's colour will change, then slower the heat, cover it for 20 minutes or till it's done.
  3. Now add water,green chillies, coriender & let it boil for another five minutes, lastly add whipping cream & boil for another two minutes.
  4. Serve hot with Roti or Paratha.
