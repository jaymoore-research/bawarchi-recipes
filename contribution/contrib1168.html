Microwave Popcorn

Ingredients

10 ml of butter
45 ml poping corn

Method:

  1. Place all ingredients in a loosely twisted oven bag.
  2. Place oven bag on an inverted micorwave -safe plate.
  3. Cook on High for 2 to 3 minutes.
  4. Remove from bag and serve sprinkled with salt.
