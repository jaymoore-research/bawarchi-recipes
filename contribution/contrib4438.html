Curd Semia

Ingredients

Semia (Sooji) 250 gms
Onion 2 (finely chopped)
Green Chillies 4 (finely chopped)
Grated Coconut 1 cup
Curd 1 cup
Water 2 cups
Salt to taste
Mustard Seeds 1 tsp
Ghee 1 tbsp

Method:

  1. Fry the semia (sooji) in ghee and keep it aside. Take 2 cups of water and salt and boil. Then add the fried sooji, stir well and switch off the stove. Keep it closed and allow it to get cooked.
  2. Take a little oil in a kadai, add mustard seeds. After it splutters, fry the chopped onions and the green chillies and then add the grated coconut.
  3. To that, add the curd and the cooked sooji and mix well.
  4. The delicious `curd semia' is ready to eat!! You'll definitely like this dish.
