Cauliflower Kofta

Ingredients For Kofta

1 large Cauliflower
3/4 cup Besan (Chick Pea Flour)
1/2 cup bread crumbs
1 tspn Cumin powder
1 tspn dried coriander seeds
1 tspn turmeric
1/4 tspn ground ginger
11/2 tspn garam masala
11/2 tspn ground cayenne pepper(can use red chilli powder)
4-5  tbspn fresh coriander( finely chopped)
Salt according to taste
Ghee /oil for cooking

Method

  1. Grate cauliflower finely.
  2. Add the besan chick pea flour add all the spices, add the bread crumbs and  season to taste mix well.
  3. Make  small or big balls as desired, and fry them in ghee/oil until firm and dark.
  4. Remove drain and arrange in serving plates.

Ingredients for Tomato Sauce:
12 large ripe cooking tomatoes peeled and pureed (easy way use 1 can of canned tomatoes)
1 onion
1 tbspn brown sugar(donot use if you want it spicy)
1 bay leaf
1tbspn paprika
30gm butter
1 tbsn dried basil
1 tbspn garlic
1/2 tspn ginger
1or more Green chilies depending on the spice needed

Method

  1. Grind all the ingredients for the tomato sauce, heat a little ghee and pour  it in a frying pan
  2. Add salt to taste and once it is hot and bubbling pour it over the koftas previously arranged in the serving tray
  3. Garnish with lot of coriander.
  4. Serves a 6-8 people, can  have hot with any kind of Indian bread or fried rice.
