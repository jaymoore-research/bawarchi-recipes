Sweet-Hayagreeva

Ingredients

Bengal Gram -1/4 kg
Jaggery 150 gms
Poppyseeds - 2 spoons
Dry Coconut Powder - 50 gms
Cardamom - 4
Ghee 4 spoons
Fried Cashews, Almonds & Raisins for garnishing

Method:

  1. Pressure cook bengal gram with 250 ml. water.
  2. Add little water to jaggery. Let it boil for 8 minutes. To this add poppy seeds (fried for 2 minutes), dry coconut & cardamom powder & ghee. Add baked bengal gram. Stir well for 5 minutes on low flame.
  3. Garnish with nuts & raisins.
