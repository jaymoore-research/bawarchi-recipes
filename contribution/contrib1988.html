Dhaniya Rice

Ingredients

Long Grained Rice (Basmati) 2 cups
Cilantro 1 bunch
Onion 1 sliced in long pieces
Tamarind Paste 1/2 tsp
Red chillies 3-4 nos.
Urad Dal - 1 tsp
Mustard - 1 tsp
Oil - 3 Tbsps
Garam Masala - 2 tbsps
Cinnamon sticks- 2-3 nos. to be finely ground
Bay leaf - 1 no.
Cloves - 4-5 nos.
Ghee - 1 tbsp
Asafoetida (hing) a dash
Salt to taste

Method :

  1. Soak rice in warm water for about 30 minutes.
  2. Take 1 tbsp oil in a pan & saute mustard. Once the mustard sputters add Urad dal, Red chillies and hing. Fry some more till the dal is slightly brown and keep aside.
  3. Cook the rice with 2 cups of water. The fastest way is to cook the rice in the microwave oven. This will take only 10 minutes.
  4. Chop the cilantro so that they can be easily added to the mixer. Blend the cilantro, Tamarind paste and the fried ingredients. Add salt and grind with little water. This paste can be used as Dhaniya chutney and can be stored in the refrigerator for future use.
  5. Fry the sliced Onions, cloves, powdered cinnamon, bay leaf in the remaining oil till Onion is slightly brown.
  6. Add rice in parts while mixing continuously so that rice does not form lumps. Add 2 tbsp of the chutney and mix, alternate till all the rice is mixed and the Dhaniya paste is evenly coated on the rice. Use your discretion while adding the chutney.
  7. Add Garam Masala/ pulav masala, Salt and mix for few minutes. Add the Ghee and mix well before serving.

Hope you enjoy this Dish. Dhaniya rice goes well with any of the Raitas. The chutney can be stored in the refrigerator and can be used for Sandwiches too.
