Condensed Milk Ice-cream

Ingredients

1 can of sweetened Condensed Milk
2 Eggs
1 1/2 can of Thick Rich Milk
1 heaped tbsp Cocoa powder
2 5-Star bars
2 tbsp. Walnuts chopped coarsely
1 tsp Vanilla Essence

Method:

  1. Mix the milks together till they are well blended.
  2. Beat in the two eggs with an egg beater. Add the vanilla essence. Put into a double boiler and cook till the milk is heated, then add the cocoa powder [this is because the cocoa powder mixes well in hot milk] -- and the 2 chopped up 5-Star bars. Cook gently stirring continuously till the mixture coats the back of your spoon. Don't overcook or it will become custardy. Remove from the fire, beat in the walnuts.
  3. Pour into your ice-cream container. Cool in a pan of water.
  4. Keep in the freezer for a day - it sets beautifully and is most delicious.
