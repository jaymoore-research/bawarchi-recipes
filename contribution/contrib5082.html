Quick Spinach Snack

Ingredients

1 lb [453g] cut spinach
1/4 tablespoon jeera
salt to taste
paprika or chilli powder as per taste
a pinch of turmeric powder
1 cup besan powder

Method

  1. Take a big bowl and to it add all the ingredients.
  2. Take a small cup of water and slowly add a few drops of water ,thoroughly mixing the ingredients into a batter.
  3. Fill the frying pan with oil and let the oil get moderately hot.
  4. One can use a spoon to take the batter from the bowl and slowly put it in the oil.
  5. The spinach batter balls deep fry to a beautiful golden brown color with a green shade of spinach.Remove them from oil and drain on paper towels to remove the excess oil.

These snacks can be served hot or cold, with hot tea or tomato sauce.
