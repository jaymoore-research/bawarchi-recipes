Malai Kofta

Ingredients

250 gram Peas
1 Carrot
2 Boiled Potatoes
1/2 cup Cream
1/2 cup Milk
1 Onion
1 can 16 Oz Tomato sauce
1 cup Corn Starch or flour
4 cup Vegetable Oil
2 Green Peppers
2 TSP Ginger
1 TSP Coriander
1/2 TSP Baking Soda for soft koftas (optional)
Salt to taste

Method:

For Koftas:

  1. Crush (do not grind to paste) green peas, boiled potatoes, carrot, chillies, ginger.
  2. In bowl, mix crushed vegetables from item-1, corn starch or corn flour and add 1/2 tsp of baking soda. After well mix, make round small koftas.
  3. In deep pan, boil oil and fry koftas and keep aside.

For Gravy:

  1. Grind 1 pealed cut onion , 2 TSP fresh ginger paste, 1 TSP coriander, 2 green peppers in grinder with 1 can tomato sauce in grinder for few minutes to make thick gravy.
  2. Heat small pan on stove and put 1 TSP oil and add whole black peppers (Mari) and 2 - 3 Lavang for vaghar.
  3. Pour, tomato paste from item-4 in heated pan with vaghar and add milk and cream. Your Gravy for kofta is ready.

Put Koftas in grvy before eating only as they are soft due to baking soda. Garnish Kofta curry with fresh cut corianders.

Serves: 4
