Kala Channa

Ingredients

Kala channa 250 gms

To grind :
Onion 1 whole
Garlic 4-5 flakes
Ginger 1/2"
Cinnamon 2-3 sticks
Cloves 3-4
Star Anise 2
Brown Cardamom 2
Green/White Cardamom 1

To season :
Turmeric powder 1/4 tsp.
Red chilly powder 1 tsp.
Salt to taste

Method :

  1. Soak the kala channa overnight (optional).
  2. Boil it either in a cooker or a microwave cooker.
  3. Grind the onion, ginger and the garlic flakes coarsely, donot make it a paste, keep it aside.
  4. Next take all the other spices and grind them together into a fine powder.
  5. Heat oil in a pan or a kadai and add the ground spices and fry it for a few minutes, add the turmeric and the chilly powder too.
  6. Now add the onion, ginger and garlic mix and keep frying until you get the pleasant aroma.
  7. Finally add the boiled kala channa and cover it with a lid.
  8. Allow it to boil for a few minutes.

It is now ready to serve!
