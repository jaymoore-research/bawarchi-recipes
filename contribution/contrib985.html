Ingredients

Split urad dal- 2 cups
Sugar - 1 cup
Ghee - 1 cup

Method :

  1. Roast urad dal till light brown in a pan and keep it aside.
  2. Powder the sugar and urad dal separately.
  3. Mix these two and make lemon shaped laddus using sufficient amount of ghee.
  4. This is also a healthy dish.
