Ragada-Patties

Ingredients

For the Patties

500 gms Potatoes
3 slices of Bread
2 Teaspoonfull of Lime Juice
Salt to Taste
Oil for frying

For Ragada

250 gm White Peas
200gm Onions
2 medium sized Tomatoes
4 Green Chillies
1 Teaspoonful Red Chilli Powder
6 to 8 tablespoons Oil
6 Cloves
1 teaspoon Coriander seeds (Dhania)
1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Badishaep or sounf)
15 Pepper seeds
4 inch Cinnamon pieces
3 Cardamoms
6 Garlic petals
1/2 inch piece of Ginger
Handful of Fresh green Cilantro (Coriander) leaves chopped finely.
Two pinches Asafoetida (Hing)
One-fourth teaspoon Turmeric
One teaspoon Jeera and Rai

Method

For Ragada

  1. Soak the white peas overnight.
  2. Cook them in the Pressure cooker.
  3. Chop the onions into fine square pieces.
  4. Cut green chillies into small pieces.
  5. Cut the ginger into small pieces
  6. Fry the onions, green chillies, Dhania seeds, cloves, cinnamon, fennel seeds, cardamoms, garlic petals, and the ginger in oil.
  7. Allow the above mixture to cool, and then grind it to a smooth paste for the gravy. Add little water.
  8. Heat a little oil in the pan, and add Jeera, and rai.
  9. Add asafoetida, and turmeric powder after the rai and jeera stop fluttering.
  10. Add red chilli powder, and tomatoes.
  11. Stir-Fry till the tomatoes become soft.
  12. Add the gravy paste, and the cooked white peas. Add little water. Add salt to taste. Your ragada is ready. Now make the patties.

For Patties

  1. Boil the potatoes, peel them, and mash them
  2. Crumble the bread pieces by using the mixer. Add these to the mashed potatoes.
  3. Add lime juice, and salt for taste.
  4. Knead the mixture to form a dough-like ball.
  5. Make 12 to 14 equal portions (balls) of the above mixture, and flatten them by hand to form the pattties.
  6. Fry these Patties in Oil.

While serving, Put the patties in the bowl, and then put ragada on it. You may add Tamarind Chutney or Mint (pudina) Chutney to it for making it more tasty.
