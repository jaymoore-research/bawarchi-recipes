Potato Curry

Ingredients

Potatoes 4
Onion 1
Tomato 1
Somb 1/4 tsp

Method:

  1. Peel potatoes and cut them into cubes.
  2. Take oil in a kadai & add channa dhal, urad dhal, jeera, mustard seeds, somb and curry leaves. Then add sliced onions and saute them.
  3. Then add diced tomaotes to it. Add salt & chilli powder to it. After 2 minutes add potato to it and pour 1 cup water and allow it to cook. Stir in between and pour water if you need. After the potatoes are done add little garam masala and garnish with cilatro.
  4. You can enjoy this with hot chapathis, puris or rice.
