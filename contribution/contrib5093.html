Pudina Puris

Ingredients

2 cups plain flour (maida)
1/2 cup mint leaves (phudina)
1 teaspoon cumin seeds (jeera)
2 green chillies, chopped
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon sugar
salt to taste
oil for deep frying

Method:

  1. Pound the mint leaves, cumin seeds, green chillies, lemon juice, sugar and salt together.
  2. Combine this mixture with the flour, add enough water and knead into a firm dough. Divide into small portions
  3. Roll out on a lightly greased surface into thin circles
  4. Deep fry in hot oil till golden brown and drain on tissue paper
  5. Serve hot.
