Falafel

Ingredients

For curry:

1 1/2 cups mixed Vegetables
1 small Potato, diced finely
1 tsp crushed Garlic
2 tsp Salt
2 tsp Chilli powder
2 tbsp Oil
2 tsp Mustard seeds

For chutney:

2-3 cups Coriander leaves
2 cloves Garlic
1 1/2 tsp Salt
3-4 Chillies
1/2 tsp Lemon Juice or Citric Acid
Yoghurt - about 1 cup

To serve: 1 pita bread per person

Method for Curry:

  1. Heat the oil in a pan, add the mustard seeds and let pop.
  2. Add the potatoes, just cook for a few minutes and add the mixed vegetables, salt, chilli powder and garlic.
  3. Cook on medium heat till the veges are cooked.

Method for Chutney:

Grind all the ingredients (except yoghurt) together. Add enough yoghurt to make the chutney a drizzling consistency. (If using frozen chutney, just let thaw for about 10 min before using).

To serve: Cut each pita bread in half and split. Warm in the microwave for about 40 seconds per pita bread. Fill each half with some curry and drizzle with some chutney. Curry and chutney quantities given are enough for about 2 pita breads. Change the quantities to suit how much you like in your pita breads.
