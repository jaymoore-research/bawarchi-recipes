Paruppu Usili Recipe

Ingredients

1 Vazhaipoo(Banana Flower;you can also use Cluster beans, beans, raw banana)
2 Teaspoon Chana Dal(Kadalai Paruppu)
2 Teaspoon Toor Dal(Tuvaram Paruppu)
1/4 Teaspoon Mustard Seeds(Kadugu)
1/4 Teaspoon Urad Dal(Ulutham Paruppu)
3 Red Chillies
1 Pinch Asafoetida
Salt to Taste
Oil

Method:

  1. Soak Chana Dal, Toor Dal in Hot water for about 1 hr.
  2. Cut Vazhaipoo Petals into small pieces. Boil in Water for 5 mts and drain.
  3. Grind the Dals with Red Chillies, Salt and Asafoetida in a Blender, with no water.
  4. Heat a frying pan.Add oil. Add Mustard seeds and Urad dal.
  5. Add the Ground Dal paste when the seeds splutter. Stir until the mixture crumbles.
  6. Add the Vazhaipoo(or any other vegetable). Stir for five minutes and take off fire.
Bon Appetite!
