Wheat Dosa

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients

2 cups Leftover Rasam
1/2 cup Wheat flour
3 spoons Rava
1 spoon Jeera
Salt ( to taste)

Method

Ā

  1. Strain the rasam so that only the fine juice is extracted. Ā
  2. Take 2 cups rasam(juice) in a vessel and mix the wheat flour slowly and remove any lumps that are formed, by hand.
  3. Add 3 spoons of rava, jeera and salt(rasam already contains salt) if required, and mix it to a waterish consistency. Ā
  4. Spread out the batter onto a hot tava.
  5. The batter cannot b spread like a regular dosa, so just make circular motions with the ladle and let the batter take its own course. Ā
  6. Fry the dosa till it is crisp. Ā
  7. Serve hot with coconut chutney. Ā
