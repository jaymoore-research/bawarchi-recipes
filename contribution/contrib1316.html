Gulab Jamuns

  1. Take 175 gm of Vanilla or Honey or Banana cream
  2. Take any Milk powder, 5 round tablespoon full
  3. Mix them above like you prepare it for puri dough
  4. Knead well till a good consistency of dough is obtained
  5. Make balls of the above as per your desired size
  6. Then deep fry them till brown
  7. Make sugar syrup, 1 glass sugar to 1 glass water
  8. Put the small balls into the syrup and allow it stand for sometime.
  9. Yummy it has the taste of same old gulab jamuns which my granny prepares for me.

Serves: 10
Time requirement: 35 mins.
