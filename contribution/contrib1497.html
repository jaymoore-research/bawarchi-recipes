Molagutal

Ingredients

1 White Bhopla
1 Potato
1 Carrot
1/4 cup Peas
Or you can only use Cabbage or only Palak (simple isn't it)
1.5 cups of grated Coconut
1 or 2 Red Chillies depends how hot you want
1/2 tsp Jeera
1 tbsp Wheat Flour
Curry Leaves
1 cup Tuvardal you can take 1/2 cup too
1/2 tsp Mustard seed
1/2 tsp Udit daal (white daal)
1 tsp Oil

Method:

  1. Cut vegetable in to square cubes and cook them with haldi and salt in cooker.
  2. Grind together the coconut, jeera, red chillies.
  3. Boil the tuvardal too.
  4. Now take a vessel and add vegetables and use the boiled water because it is got all the proteins and you need to use it.
  5. Add the mixture and the daal and cook for 5 minutes and then add the salt.
  6. Taste and see first use it as per required and once's all this is done put the wheat flour wetting it little bit with water. And mix it with the molagutal this will thicken the mixture and now remove the vessel from the gas and keep a pan instead put oil and put mustart seed, curry leaves, udit daal after they splutter put it on the molagutal and you can also use the coriander leaves if you want.

Now your molagutal is ready this simple dish kids love and is also good for their health and ours too and less hassle to prepare it too.
