Green Chilli Rice

Ingredients for Cooking

Fenugreek seeds – 1/4 teaspoon
Sesame seeds – 1/4 teaspoon
Jeera – 1/2 teaspoon
Green chilli – 4-5 or as per taste
Tamarind paste- 1 teaspoon
Salt – as per taste
Jaggery- 1teaspoon
Basmati Rice- 1 cup
Freshly Grated coconut – 2 tablespoon
Coriander leaves

Seasoning ingredients:
Oil – 3 tablespoon
Mustard – 1teaspoon
Asafoetida – 1 pinch
Turmeric – 1 pinch
Groundnut – 1tablespoon
Bengal gram – 1 teaspoon
Urad dal – 1 teaspoon
Curry leaves

  Method:

  1. Dry Roast Fenugreek, sesame, jeera and green chilli and make a paste. Keep it aside.
  2. Boil tamarind paste, turmeric, salt and jaggery in 1 cup water till it becomes a thick paste.
  3. Heat oil and add mustard seeds.
  4. Add curry leaves and asafoetida and rest of the seasoning ingredients subsequently.
  5. Mix cooked basmati rice with the above pastes and seasoning uniformly.
  6. Add grated coconut and coriander for garnishing.
