Potato Sang

Ingredients

Onions -2.
Potatoes-2.
Mustard seeds-few.
Chilli powder-2 tsps.
Salt to taste.
Tamarind - 1 lime size.
Oil-10 tbsps.

Method :

  1. Cut the onions lenghtwise.
  2. Cut the potatoes into cubes.
  3. Heat the oil in a pan. Add the mustard seeds. Once it starts to splatter fry the onions to a golden brown colour. Then add the potato cubes. Then add salt & chilli powder.
  4. Mix the tamarind only once, thickly by adding water & add it to the above mixture & cook till potatoes are well done.
Note:This dish goes well with chapathi/rice.
