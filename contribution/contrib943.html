Cabbage Vada

Ingredients

Urad Dal- 2 cups
Cabbage- half cup (very finely chopped)
Onion - medium sized (very finely chopped)
Green Chillies - 2 in number (finely chopped)
Salt - to taste
Oil for frying.

Method :

  1. Soak the Urad daal for 2 - 3 hours.
  2. Chop finely cabbage, onions & green chillies.
  3. Take the soaked Urad dal & make a fine paste using very little water.
  4. Now add the chopped cabbage, chopped onion, chopped green chillies & salt to the urad paste.
  5. Heat oil in a pan, wet your hand & take small quantities of the dough & make a hole in between (just like the plain Vada) & gently slide it into the oil.
  6. If u cannot do it with your hand you can do the same procedure using a plastic cover & then slide it into the oil.

Note:Always wet your hand before taking the dough in your hand. Wet the plastic cover, if you are using one. Do not add excess of water while grinding the daal.
