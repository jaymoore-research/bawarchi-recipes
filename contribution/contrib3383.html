Begum kee Mirch

Ingredients

4 Capsicum - Red, Orange, Yellow, Green - 1 each
1 Large onion
2 cups cashews
5-6 cloves
5-6 black pepper
1 cinnamon stick
2 tablespoon ghee
1 teaspoon cumin seeds
5-6 green chilly
2 cups yogurt
1/4 inch ginger
1/2 teaspoon saffron
coriander leaver for garnish

Method :

  1. Grind cashews, cloves, black pepper, cinnamon stick, green chilly and ginger well together
  2. Add yogurt to the above mixture and blend it into a fine paste.
  3. Cut Capsicum and onions into thin slices of around 3 inch long.
  4. Heat ghee in a kadhai(preferably earthern-ware).
  5. Add cumin seeds to the ghee.
  6. After the seeds start breaking, add onions to it.
  7. Shallow fry it for 2-3 minutes or until it becomes golden brown.
  8. Add Capsicum to it and stir fry it for 5 mins till it becomes tender.
  9. Add the spiced paste, saffron and a cup of water and let it cook for 5-7 mins over a low flame.
  10. Add salt to taste.
  11. Garnish it with coriander leaves and serve it hot with pulav or any indian breads.
