Pea-Corn-Noodle Deli

Ingredients

(1) 1/2 Onion sliced into small pieces, 1/4 Tomato sliced
(2) Olive oil (any oil will do) 3 table spoons.
(3) 1 tbsp Salt, pinch of Red Pepper (chilli) powder, pinch of
Turmeric powder.
(4) 1/2 cup Peas, 1/2 cup Corn, 1/4 cup Noodle, 2 tbsp Sunflower seeds
(5) Lemon Juice - 2 tbsp

Method:

  1. Boil noodle in water for 10 minutes. Drain the water. Leave it aside.
  2. In a pan pour the olive oil...bring it to heat. Fry onions to red colour(not black).
  3. Add sliced tomato the frying pan (do not fully fry), add salt and red pepper powder, pinch turmeric powder, green chillies (1/4 piece), add a pinch of dhaniya powder or garam masala or noodle masala powder and fry for three minutes.
  4. Add peas, corn to the above mixture in the frying pan. Fry for a while and then add noodles to it.
  5. Fry for some time and switch off the stove.
  6. After 4 minutesWhen it is getting cold. Add lemon juice to it and Cilantro(Dhaniya leaves) to it. It is ready to eat.
