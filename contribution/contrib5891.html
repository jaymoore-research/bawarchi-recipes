Aloo Posto

Ingredients

Poppy seeds - 4 teaspoon
Onion sliceĀ- 1
Potatoes-2/3 medium size
Kalonji-1/2 teaspoon
salt & sugar to taste. Oil for frying and water

Method

Ā

  1. First of all heat the oil in a pan and put the kalonji seeds , and when they startĀto pop Āout put the onion slices in that.
  2. When the color of onion changes to transparent ,put the potatoes pieceĀ, cook for sometime.
  3. Fry the potatoes for 5 minutes or so.
  4. Then put salt and sugar ( according to individual taste).
  5. After stirring for another 4/5 minutes put the poppy seed paste in it.
  6. ĀAgain stir it and cover the pan with a lid ,if required one can put some water .
  7. Serve the dish with rice only . Ā
