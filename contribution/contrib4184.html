Bean Sprout Paruppu Usili

Ingredients

Bean Sprouts 2 to 3 cups
Toor dal 1 to 2 cups
Red Chillies 2 to 3
Turmeric
Hing
Salt

For Seasoning

Mustard
Urud dal
Curry Leaves

Method:

  1. Cut bean sprouts and cook them with little water in the microwave for 7 to 10 mins.
  2. Soak toor dal for an hour or so and coarsely grind it with red chillies, turmeric, salt and hing.
  3. Pressure cook the ground mixture in a cooker. Cool and grind again to get the powder form.
  4. Take oil in a kadhai, when hot add urud dal, mustard and curry leaves. Once they splutter, add the ground mixture and keep turning till everything gets evenly mixed and then add the cooked bean sprouts and keep turning till everything is mixed. Let it sit for a while till the bottom starts turning light brown to get a crunchy, crispy paruppu usuli.
