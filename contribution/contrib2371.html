Fish Cutlets

Ingredients

Potatoes - 5 (boiled and mashed)
Canned Tuna - 2 cans (drain water or oil off)
Mustard seeds - half teaspoon
Garlic - 5 cloves (cut into tiny dices)
Onion - 2 big or 5 small (cut into thin slices)
Chilli - 3 (crushed)
Chilli powder - 1 teaspoon
Tumeric powder- 1 teaspoon
Eggs - 2
Breadcrumbs

Method

  1. Fry garlic, onion, ginger and chilli until aroma fills kitchen;
  2. Add mustard seeds;
  3. Add potatoes when mustard seeds start to 'pop';
  4. Add turmeric and chilli powder;
  5. Salt to taste;
  6. Add tuna after 7 minutes. Continue to fry for 5 minutes. Remove from stove and allow to cool.
  7. Beat egg in separate bowl and spread breadcrumbs on a dry plate;
  8. When potato and tuna have cooled, roll into small balls;
  9. Dip each ball in egg mixture and roll on breadcrumbs;
  10. Deep fry until golden brown.
  11. Cool and serve with rice or have it as a snack.
