Rava Adai

Ingredients

Semolina (rava) 1 cup
Finely chopped onions 2
Finely chopped green chillies (small) 2
Grated ginger
Chopped tomato 1 (optional)
Yoghurt 1/2 cup
Cilantro (as required)
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Fry the rava till slightly brown.
  2. Mix the rava, onions, chillies, ginger, cilantro, salt and tomato.
  3. To this mixture add the yoghurt and set it aside for 15-20 minutes.
  4. After add just enough water to be able to spread on the tava or non-stick pan like dosas.
  5. Can be served with coconut chutney or cilantro chutney..
