Okra Masala Kuzhambu

Ingredients

okra 6-8
onions 1 cup
tomatoes 1 cup
urad dhal 1/2 tsp
mustard 1 tsp
curry leaves a few
tamarind thick pulp
oil a little bit
salt to taste

To make paste
coconut 1 cup
red chillies 4-5
chana dhal 1 tsp
coriander seeds 2 tsp
Heat all the above in oil and saute till it brown and grind it to make a paste.[Add a little amt of water]

Method

  1. Heat the oil and add mustard and uraddhal till it splutters.
  2. Then add onion and saute till it turns golden brown.
  3. Add curry leaves and tomatoes
  4. Fry till it extracts the juice.
  5. Then add okra and fry it. [For the better taste you can also fry the okra separately in the oil and add to the above mix]
  6. Add the tamarind pulp when the okra is half- cooked.
  7. Allow it to boil and add the paste.
  8. Remove from the heat when it thickens.

This dish goes well with rice, chappatis, dosas and idli.
Enjoy your meal.
