Beans Paruppu Usili

Ingredients

Beans -200 gm or 2 cups of cut beans.
Channa dhal (kadalai paruppu) -1cup.
Coconut grated - 1/2 cup.
Red Chillies -3
Salt
Asafoetida.
Oil -3tsp.

Method:

  1. Soak channa dhal for 1 hour & grind along with coconut, red chillies & asafoetida.
  2. Boil the beans.
  3. In a kadai, add 3tsp of oil & the ground paste. Cook it well with little salt for 7-10 minutes. Now add the boiled beans to it & cook it for 5 minutes. Serve hot.
