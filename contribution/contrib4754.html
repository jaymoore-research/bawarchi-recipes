Pudina Rice

Ingredients

Pudina (Mint leaves) - 1 cup
Onion -1
Ginger - little cut into pieces
Green Chilli - 2
Pepper powder - 1/2 teaspoon
Cashewnut - 4
Rice basmati optional - 1 cup
salt - as required
oil - 1 tabl.spn

Method:

  1. Cook rice separately.
  2. In a pan put little oil and fry chilli, onion, ginger and finely cut mint leaves. Now add pepper powder, salt and fry it.
  3. Fry the cashewnuts in little ghee and mix the cooked rice to the pudina mixture.
  4. Garnish with coriander leaves.
  5. Serve hot with any Raita.

Serves: 3-4
