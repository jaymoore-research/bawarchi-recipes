Vinegar Chillies and Mayonnaise

Ingredients

1 cup Brown or White Vinegar
Half cup finely chopped Green Chillies
Half tsp Salt
Half tsp Sugar

Method:

Put the vinegar in a bowl, add the green chillies, salt and sugar. Stir well with a spoon. Cover and set aside for about 4 hours.

Note: Vinegar chillies are usually prepared a few hours before use in order that the chillies may lose their bite. Serve with all chinese dishes.

Mayonnaise (eggless):

Ingredients:

3 cups fresh Yogurt
2 tsp chopped Onions
2 tsp fresh Cream
2 tsp chopped Capsicum
1 chopped Green Chilli
Salt to taste

Method:

Tie curds in a thin cloth and hang for 2 hours to allow the water to drain out. Blend the thickened curds with salt in a blender add the rest of the ingredients. Store in a refrigerator and serve cold.
