Green Chilli Chicken Masala

Ingredients

Serves 6

Chicken -- 21/2 pounds clean and cut into small pieces
Green chillis -- 10-20 small ones sliced into two
Onions(green onions or ordinary) -- 1 or 2 sliced
Corriander -- 1 handful
Mint -- 1 handful
Cloves -- 6
Black Jeera -- pinch
Ilaichi -- 1
Cinnamon -- 3 small pieces
Oil -- 3 tablespoons
Salt -- to taste.

Method:

  1. Fry onions (3/4th part) and green chillis till golden brown. Take out in a plate to let it cool.
  2. Make a paste of the above, corriander and, mint leaves.
  3. Take oil in a big pan and fry cloves, cinnamon, illaichi, black jeera, onions and, the paste all in one, one after the other.
  4. Put the chicken and mix well. Let it cook for 1/2 an hr till tender.

Serve hot.

Note:

  1. The paste and the other ingredients give enough gravy.
  2. You can wash chicken with turmeric after its clean for bacterial purpose. I did not use it 'cause I want it green in color.
  3. You can add green color if it's handy.
