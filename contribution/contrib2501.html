Onion Pakoras

Ingredients

One big Onion (medium length chopped)
All Purpose Flour (Maida) 1/2 cup
Ajwain a pinch
Salt as per requirement
Green Chilli one tbsp
Ginger paste two pinches
Jeera Powder one tbsp
Water to mix
Oil to fry

Method :

  1. First of all mix all of the ingredients given above to make a batter of slightly liquid consistency. Keep for 2-3 minutes. Mix into it, the chopped onion.
  2. Now fry in hot oil till brown.
  3. Take them from pan into paper towels so that the oil is all removed.
  4. Serve hot with ketchup.
