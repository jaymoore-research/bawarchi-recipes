Traditional Pesari Pappu (Plain Moong Dal)

Ingredients

1 cup Moong Dal
2 Tblsp Ghee or butter or oil
4 Green Chillies
4 cups water
1 pinch Turmeric powder
1/4 Tsp Mustard seeds (Optional)
1 Tsp Salt
6 Curry leaves
Green Coriander Onion leaves

Method :

  1. Take a medium size bowl and heat up the Ghee. Note that the Ghee gives better taste than oil and the more the Ghee the tastier the dish.
  2. Put cut green Chillies and Mustard seeds.
  3. Fry Moong Dal in Ghee till it turns brown along with Curry leaves. DO NOT fry till the Dal becomes too dark as you loose the taste.
  4. Put Turmeric powder and pour water and let it cook.
  5. After it's half way cooked then put cut green onion stems.
  6. After it's completely cooked (should be soft when you touch), put cut green onion leaves and coriander leaves along with the salt.

Adjust the consistency to your desire either by cooking more so that it thickens or adding more water and boiling for some more time. To vary the taste, add finely chopped/grinded Ginger while frying Dal. This goes well with Chapati/Roti or Rice.
