Sukku Malli Coffee

Ingredients

Dry Ginger (100 Gms)
Dried Coriander (100 Gms)

Method :

  1. Beat Dry ginger to a flat form so that it is lose and easy to grind.
  2. Grind together the beaten Dry Ginger and Coriander in a grinder.
  3. Put one or two tea spoon full of the powdered ingredient in a pan.
  4. Add one glass of milk. Boil the milk .
  5. Add sugar to taste.
  6. Serve hot or cool!
