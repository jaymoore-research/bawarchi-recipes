Quick Vermicelli Upma (for kids)

Ingredients

1 pack vermicelli or semya
1 small onion (finely chopped)
1 small tomato (finely chopped)
1/ cup mixed vegs (fresh or frozen)
mustard seeds
sambhar powder
jeera
oil
coriander leaves for garnishing

Method:

  1. Cook the mixed vegs and keep aside.
  2. In a pan or kadai, take a tsp of oil and fry the semya till it turns golden brown in colour. Keep aside.
  3. In the same kadai,take some more oil,put mustard seeds and jeera and let it splutter.
  4. Add the onion pieces and sauti well
  5. Next add the tomatoes and fry for sometime.Now add sambhar powder and salt and cook till it gets soft.
  6. To this add the semya along with a little water and cook till tender.
  7. Finally add the mixed vegs and give it a good stir.
  8. Adjust the salt seasoning as required.
  9. Garnish with coriander leaves or you can omit it if your kids don't prefer it.

Hot vermicelli upma is ready to serve. Isn't it quick ?
