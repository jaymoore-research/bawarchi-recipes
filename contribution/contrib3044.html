Lemon Chicken with Green Chillies

Ingredients

Chicken 1 kg
Oil 5 tablespoons
Green Chillies 15 (whole)
Onion 1 (big)
Ginger 1 inch
Garlic 4 cloves
Salt to taste
Lemon juice 2 tablespoons
Water 2 cups

Method:

  1. Wash & cut chicken pieces into medium portions/sizes.
  2. Rub some salt onto the chicken pieces. Keep aside for atleast 1 hr.
  3. Meanwhile, grind ginger & garlic together into paste.
  4. Chop onion.
  5. Heat oil in pan and fry the chopped onion till golden brown. Then add ginger & garlic paste & fry for some more time.
  6. Add the chicken pieces and fry for atleast 15-20 minutes. Add water and cover pan. Simmer till chicken is done.
  7. Finally, add the whole green chillies and lemon juice. Boil for another 1 min. (max).
  8. Warm and serve this dish after 8-10 hrs. after it has been cooked.

Note:

  • The green chillies should not be broken or slit or damaged.
  • This dish should be kept aside for a minimum of 8-10 hrs. after cooking. This helps in the gravy absorbing the flavour of the green chillies without becoming hot.
  • Avoid breaking the chillies when serving.

Enjoy!
