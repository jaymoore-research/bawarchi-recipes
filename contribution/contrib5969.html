Chicken Capsicum

Ingredients

chicken - 500gms
capsicum - 250gms
green chili - 7-8(paste)
chili sauce - 1tsp
curd - 250 gmr
ajinomota - 1 tsp
ginger-garlic - 2tsp
maida - 2 tsp
cornflour - 2tsp
egg - 1
blackpepper - 1-2tsp(powder)
coriander - 1 bunch
curry leaves - 2-3 sticks
salt - to taste
oil - for deep fry
haldi - 1/2 tsp

Method

  1. Marinate chicken pieces in 1 tsp of cornflour, maida, chili sauce, salt, blackpepper, egg, 1/2 tsp ajinomoto,1tsp of ginger garlic paste for 1 (one)hour.
  2. After one hour deep fry and keep aside
  3. Know take pan put 1-2 tsp of oil and add chopped capsicum fry for while after that add 1 tsp of ginger garlic paste and 1/2 tsp of turmeric
  4. Fry till capsicum is soft
  5. Meanwhile take a bowl and mix curd with 1tsp of cornflour,1tsp of ajinomoto,green chili paste andĀ1/2 tsp of salt
  6. Add this mixture to the capsicum and again fry for 2-3 minutes
  7. Add fried chicken pieces,curry leaves ,little of coriander leaves
  8. Cook well till gravy is thick
  9. Garnish with coriander leaves
  10. Serve hot with chapattis or rice Ā
