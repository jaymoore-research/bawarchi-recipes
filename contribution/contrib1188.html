Brinjal Chutney

Ingredients

One large purple Brinjal
4-5 Green Chillis
Salt to taste
Tamarind- a small lime size
Coriander leaves, chopped-1/2 cup
Hing - a pinch

For tempering:

2 tsp-Oil
1/2 tsp. Mustard seeds
2-3 Red Chillis
a pinch of Hing (optional)

Method:

  1. Cut brinjal into small pieces and cook with sufficient water till soft.
  2. Cool, and grind to a paste with coriander leaves, salt, tamarind, hing and green chillis to a smooth paste.
  3. Temper with mustard, red chillis and hing.
