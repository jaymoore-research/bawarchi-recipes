Caramel Fudge

Ingredients

50 gm ground Almonds
2 tbsp Ghee or Butter
1 cup (250 ml) Cream
1/2 cup (100 gm) full Cream Powder
1/3 cup (80 gm) Sugar

Method :

  1. In a medium- sized saucepan, fry the ground almonds in the ghee. Stir constantly for about 3 mins, or until the mixture turns a deep golden colour.
  2. Pour in the cream ; mix well. Add the milk powder; continue to mix well. Reduce the heat to low. Stirring frequently, untill the mixture begins to thicken.
  3. Meanwhile, in another saucepan, combine the sugar and 1/4 cup water; bring to the boil. Reduce heat to medium and simmer until the syrup begins to darken. (You are aiming at achieving a soft toffee at " drop stage").
  4. Pour the toffee mixture onto the cream and almond mixture. Stir in thoroughly; continue to cook for about 8 mins until the mixture is heavy thick and leaves the sides of the pan.
  5. Lightly grease an 8-18 cm plastic container or heat proof glass dish, spoon in the fudge and smooth the surface. Chill until firm, about 1 1/2 hours, then cut into bite-sized squares.
