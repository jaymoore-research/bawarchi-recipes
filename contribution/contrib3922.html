Charu

Ingredients

1.
Tamarind juice -2 tspns
Turmeric powder - 1 tspn
Salt - 2 tspn
Water-3 cups
Tomatoes - 2
Cooked toor dal - 1 serving spoon

2.
Charu podi or rasam powder

3.
Ghee - 1/2 tspn
Fenugreek seeds - 1/2 tspn
Urad dal - 1/4 tspn
Mustard seeds - 1/4 tspn
Cumin seeds - 1/4 tspn
Dry red chili - 1
Asoefaetida - 1/2 tspn

4.
Cilantro

Method :

  1. Bring 1 to a boil.
  2. Add 2.
  3. Put 3 in a separate vessel and let them splutter.
  4. After spluttering , add them to the boiled charu.
  5. Garnish with Cilanro.
  6. Can be used as soup [appetiser] and goes very well with rice.
  7. Proportions of charu podi and salt can be changed according to one's taste
