Vegetable Cutlet

Ingredients

Boiled Potatoes (mashed) 250 gms
Onion chopped finely 2 big ones
Ginger shredded 1 small piece
Green chillies chopped 2 or according to spiciness
Red chillies powder 2 tsp.
Garam Masala powder 2 tbsp
(Dalchini, laung, jeera, Badi elaichi)
Brinjal (Aubergine) chopped 1 medium sized
Cauliflower chopped 1 small one
Dill Leaves 2 sprigs
Besan (Gramflour) for binding
Bread Crumbs 1/2 cup
Marie Biscuits powdered 4-5
Salt According to taste
Oil for deep frying

Method

  1. Take the mashed potatoes and add into it red chilly powder and 1 tsp. garam masala powder and a little salt. Mix well.
  2. In a pan heat some oil. Add onions and green chillies and saute till onions turn pink. Add the chopped vegetables (Cauliflower, brinjal, dill), rest of the garam masala powder and salt. Cover and cook till vegetables are done.
  3. Let the mixture cool slightly. Mash roughly. Add Besan, enough that it binds well to form balls.
  4. Make equal portions of the potato and vegetable mixtures.
  5. Shape the potato mixture like a cutlet and fill it with a vegetable portion. Shape into a cutlet and keep inside. Do the rest in the same manner.
  6. Roll all the chops into breadcrumbs.
  7. Heat oil in a pan and deep fry the chops to golden brown
  8. Drain and then finally before serving roll each chop into the biscuit mixture.
  9. Serve hot with Tomato Ketchup or Mint Chutney.
