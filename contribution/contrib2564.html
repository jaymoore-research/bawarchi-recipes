Vellarikkai (Cucumber) Koott

Ingredients

Cucumber - 2 or 3(medium size)
Onion - 1
Channa Dal - 1/4 cup
Turmeric Powder 1/4 spoon
Chilly Powder 1 spoon (as desired
Black Gram 1 spoon
Mustard seeds 1 spoon
Curry Leaves
Coriander
Salt
Oil for seasoning

Method:

  1. Soak channa dal in water for 1 hour. Then clean & cook in a pressure cooker. (upto 1 whistle)
  2. Cut cucumber finely.
  3. Chop onions finely & keep aside.
  4. Heat oil in a pan and add black gram & mustard seeds. When mustards splutter add curry leaves, chopped onion & fry until the onions are slightly brown.
  5. Add the cucumber & fry for 3-4 minutes. Now add the turmeric powder, chilly powder & salt.
  6. Pour a cup of water and add the boiled channa dal. Cook in medium flame for 15 minutes or until the cucumber is tender. Add more water if required.
  7. Garnish with coriander & serve with Rice or Chapathis.
