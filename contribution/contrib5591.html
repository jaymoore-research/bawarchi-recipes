Tuvar dal Nuchinunde

Ingredients for Cooking

3 cups tuvar dal
5 green chillies
5 red chillies
coriander leaves
dried coconut (kobari)
hing
salt
dil (optional)

Method:

  1. Soak tuvar dal in water for an hour.
  2. Strain the water completely and grind coarsely without adding water.
  3. Also add green and red chillies and dried coconut powder, salt, hing while grinding.
  4. Cut coriander leaves and dil finely, and add to the above mixture.
  5. Make small balls and steam in cooker like idlis.
  6. Serve hot with coconut chutney and ghee.
