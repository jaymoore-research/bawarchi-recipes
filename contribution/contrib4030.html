Bellpepper Curry

Ingredients

Bell peppers - 3 medium sized.
Roasted Chana flour- 5-6 spoons.
Grated Dry Coconut- 3 spoons.
Red Chilli powder- 1/2 spoon.
A pinch Turmeric
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Cut bellpeppers in one inch squares. Put heat to high for just a minute or so. Then mix all with out crushing bell pepper. Switch off the stove and close the lid.
  2. Garnish with coriander.
  3. Serve hot with Chapathi or rice.

Note:This recipe can be applied to Broccoli too. It will be excellent.
