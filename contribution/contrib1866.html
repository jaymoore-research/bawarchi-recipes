Kheer

Ingredients

Milk - Half a Litre
Milkmaid- One Can
Rice-Basmati Preferred-Small Cup
Badam-Pista-Cashews-100gms All Put Together
Sugar- 125 Gms

Any flavoring agent or color (avoid if you are satisfied with the natural color of milk/milkmaid)

Method:

  1. First cook rice in a mixture of milk and milkmaid and check for consistency and completion.
  2. Then grind half the quantity of the badam-pista-cashew and add it to the above mixture while continuing to cook on a low fire.
  3. Fry the remaining cashew-badam-pista in a small quantity of ghee and add them along with sugar to the simmering mixture.
  4. Slowly churn the mixture till the sugar gets dissolved and the entire mixture gains servable consistency.
  5. If need be, flavoring agents like vannila, etc., can be added (a drop or two).
  6. Serve hot. Or keep them in the fridge and serve cold. Suitable for any occassion and more when unexpected guests drop in. Try it out.
