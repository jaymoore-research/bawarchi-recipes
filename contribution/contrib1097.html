Coconut Chutney

Ingredients

Daalia 1 cup
Long chilies 8 PC.
Frozen coconut 2 cups.
Salt to taste.
Mustard seeds, curry leaves and whole red chilies crushed.

Method

  1. In a small kadhai heat oil and put the daalias and keep stirring for about 4-5 min.
  2. Take it off from the heat and add chilies and coconut.
  3. Put them in mixie and grind it well.
  4. Once grinded heat oil in kadhai and sputter the mustard seeds, curry leaves and crushed chilies and add it to the chutney.
  5. Add salt to taste. Serve it with idlis, dhosa or medu vada.
