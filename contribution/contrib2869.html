Garlic Boneless Chicken

Ingredients

Boneless Chicken - 1 Kg
Onion finelly (chopped) - 3
Garlic paste - 1 1/2 tbsp.
Green Chillies (chopped) - 2 to 3
Coriander Leaves (chopped) - 1 cup
Salt to taste
Oil -2 tbsp.

Method

  1. Wash the chicken and cut into 2.5 cm cubes.
  2. Heat oil in a saucepan fry onions till light brown. Now add chicken cubes green chillies and salt to taste. Cover and cook for 10 minutes or when the chicken is cooked.
  3. Before serving add chopped coriander.
