Texas Chicken

Ingredients

2 -- Boneless chicken breasts --cut into 1 inch pieces
1 1/2 tsp-- Ginger paste
1/2 cup ----Yoghurt
2 tsps------Soya sauce

for the gravy:
1 tsp-------finely chopped garlic
1/2 cup-----Tomato Puree
2 nos-------Slit green chillies
2 tsps------Soya Sauce
1-cut into small pieces--Capsicum
1 tbsp-chopped------------Spring onions
1 tsp-----Cornstartch
1/2 tsp --- white pepper pwdr
1/2 tsp --- sugar
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Marinate the chicken pieces in the yoghurt, soya, ginger and a little salt. Leave aside for a minimum of 1/2 an hour or upto 2 hrs.
  2. Deep fry these chicken pieces till light brown in colour. Keep aside. In the same oil fry the capsicum and spring onions. Keep aside.
  3. In a seperate kadai take a little oil --add garlic, green chillies and then add the fried chicken, salt, sugar, soya sauce and cook for 5 mins.
  4. Add 1/4 cup water and add tomato puree and at the end add cornstarch paste and pepper pwdr and mix and let cook for 5 -- 10 mins.

Serve hot with chinese fried rice.
