Bottle Guard Snacks

Ingredients

Bottle guard 1 kg
Rice flour 4-5 cups
Onions 5
Green chilli 3
Coriander leaves
Salt to taste
Oil to fry

Method:

  1. Peel off the bottle guard and grate it thoroughtly
  2. Grind the chopped onions lightly
  3. Grind the green chilli very well
  4. Add grinded green chilli and onions to grated bottle guard, and add sufficient rice flour.
  5. Add salt and coriander leaves.
  6. Take a wet cloth, make vada and fry it in heated oil.
  7. Eat with any pickle.
  8. You can make pakodi also.

Making time : 1 hr
