Cucumber Puppu

Ingredients

Red gram dhal 250gms
Cucumber (medium size) 2
Tamarind small lime size

For seasoning:

Red chillies 2
Green chillies 2
Onion
Cumin seeds
Mustard
Asaefoetida
Curry leaves

Method:

  1. Remove the skin from the cucumber and cut into small pieces.
  2. Steam the cucumber and red gram dhal separately in pressure cooker.
  3. Soak tamarind for 15 to 20 minutes in minimum water and strain the juice.
  4. Blend the steamed cucumber and dhal throughly. Make certain that there is no lumps of cucumber is seen.
  5. Place a kadai on the stove, pour required amount of oil and do the seasoning with mustard, cumin seeds, onion, curry leaves, red, green chillies and asefoetida.
  6. To the seasoning add tamarind extract and cucumber dhal blend.
  7. Add salt and leave it to boil for 10 minutes.
  8. Chopped corriander leaves can be added for best aroma.

Cucumber Puppu will go well with Rice and Chappathi.
