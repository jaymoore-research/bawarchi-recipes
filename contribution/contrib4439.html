Potato Stew

Ingredients

3 medium sized Potatoes (cut in cubes)
1 medium sized Onion (diced-keep them long)
1" inch piece of Ginger (juliened)
3 to 4 Chillies (depending on how hot you like)
1 stem of Curry Leaves
1 cup thick Coconut Milk (may use tinned coconut milk)
2 cups Thin Coconut Milk (just dilute with water)
1 teaspoon Oil
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. In a pressure cooker put 1 teaspoon oil and add onions, chillies, ginger and curry leaves and fry on low heat until the onions gets transparent. Make sure not to let onions change colour, they should be transparent only or you spoil the taste and look of Stew.
  2. Now add the potatoes and 2 cups of thin coconut milk. Add salt to taste and pressure cook for 2 whistles. After the steam escapes from cooker on cooling, transfer stew to a serving bowl and mash some of the potatoes to make the gravy thick.
  3. Finally add thick coconut milk. No heating once the thick coconut milk is added. Your Potato Stew is ready to eat. Enjoy this with either Appam or even rice and bread go very well with it.
Serves 2
