Winter Vegetable Salad

Ingredients

Peas boiled - 1/2 cup
Beans chopped and boiled - 1/2 cup
Potatoes chopped and boiled - 1/2 cup
Carrots chopped and boiled - 1/2 cup
Turnip chopped and boiled - 1/2 cup
Mayonnaise - 3 tbspn
Arcort - 6 pieces
Onion chopped - 2
Tomato chopped - 2
Curds - 4 tbspn
Pepper and Salt - 1/2 tspn
Coriander - 1/4 cup
Egg - 2

Method

  1. Take a bowl. Add all the boiled vegetables and also chopped onions, tomato and arcort.
  2. Add 4tbspn of curds and 3 tbspn of Mayonnaise cheese
  3. Add pepper and salt.
  4. On top add coriander leaves for garnishing.The salad is ready.

Garnish:

     
  1. Take a plate arrange the sliced tomato,lemon onion and boiled egg.
  2. Take the salad in a bowl and keep it in the centre of the plate.
  3. Add coriander on top. Now winter vegetable salad is ready.
