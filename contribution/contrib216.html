Bean Burrito

Ingredients

Haricotbeans: 1 cup (soak them in water for atleast 4 hrs)
1 onion
4 cloves garlic
1/2cup pasta sauce
1 onion cut finely
Grated cheddar cheese 100gms
Tabasco Sauce(optional)
6 plain torillas(maida rotis)

Method

  1. Pressure cook the haricot beans with the onion and garlic.
  2. Mash well and add salt.
  3. On a tortilla spread the bean paste on one side.
  4. Top with a spoonful of pasta sauce, sprinkle onions and cheddar cheese.
  5. Roll it up and heat in microvave for a minute.
  6. Serve hot.
  7. Can sprinkle tabasco sauce while eating for those who like it spicy.
