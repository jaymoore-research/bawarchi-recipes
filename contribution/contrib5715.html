Tandoori Chicken

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients

1 Kg Chicken
6 cloves garlic
5cm x 2 inches Ginger
25ml Yoghurt
Juice of 2 Lemons
1 Table Spoon sweet paprika
2 Tablespoon ground corainder
1 tablespoon CuminĀ
1/2 tablespoon turmeric
2-teaspoon chilli powder
3 large cardamoms
Salt to taste
A hand full of chopped coriander leaves
100 gms butter

METHOD

  1. Wash the chicken pieces and pat them dry.
  2. Slash the chicken pieces all over with a knife.
  3. Grind all the ingredients together (without adding water) to make a paste.
  4. Marinate chicken overnight with this mixture.
  5. Sprinkle coriander leaves on top, baste with butter and grill.
  6. Serve with lemon wedges.
