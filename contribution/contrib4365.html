Murungaikerai Porial

Ingredients

Murungaikerai (Drumstick leaves) 1 bundle
Dhal 100 grams
Onion (big) 100 grams
Garlic 20 flakes
Red Chillies 3
Mustard 1/2 teaspoon
Black Gram 1 teaspoon
Cooking Oil 3 teaspoon
Grated Coconut 7 teaspoon
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Separate the green tender leaves of the drumstick, wash and drain the water.
  2. Boil one cup of water in a vessel, add washed dhal and 4 drops of cooking oil, and cook for 15 to 20 minutes. Cooked dhal should not become a paste; use it as separate grains. Drain the water thoroughly.
  3. In a kadai, heat oil and add pieces of red chillies and mustard seeds. When mustard splutters, add black gram and fry until it becomes light brown.
  4. Then add onion and garlic and fry for 5 minutes.
  5. Add drumstick leaves, mix with onion. Add half a cup of water and close with a lid. Keep on stirring for 5 minutes. When the leaves are completely cooked, add salt, dhal and grated coconut and mix. Serve hot.

SERVINGS: 4
