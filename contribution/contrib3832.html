Fish Fingers

Ingredients

1/2 kg. Rawas
1 tsp Garlic paste
1/2 tsp Ginger paste
Salt to taste
Juice of 1 Lemon
1 tsp Red Chilli powder
Rice Flour

Method:

  1. Cut fish into long fingers.
  2. Mix together ginger garlic pastes, salt, chilli powder and lemon juice and apply to the fish. Leave aside for 2-3 hrs.
  3. Mix together rice flour, salt. Coat fish with this flour and deep fry till crisp. Sprinkle with Amchur and Coriander leaves.
