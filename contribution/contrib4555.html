Capsicum Rice

Ingredients

1 Green Bell pepper
1 Red Bell pepper
1 Yellow Bell pepper
1 Orange Bell pepper
(or you can use same color but different colors look more attractive and colorful. Any way Its optional)
Ginger/Garlic Paste 1 tsp.
Garam Masala powder 1 tsp.
Butter/Olive oil or any other oil.
Salt 1 tsp.

Method:

  1. Cut the Capsicum into fine thin and long slices.
  2. Take a big pan, Pour sufficient Butter or oil in it.
  3. Add the colorful Bell pepper in to the butter and add little bit of salt and 1 tsp of ginger/ garlic paste to it. Close the lid, and let it cook for a while.
  4. When the slices are well cooked add little bit of Garam masala powder to it.
  5. Mix this curry gently in the cooked Basmati Rice. Add salt according to your taste.
  6. Finally Squeeze lime and garnish with coriander leaves. Serve hot and Enjoy!
