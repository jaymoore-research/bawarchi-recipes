Suseums

Ingredients

sweet potatoes - 3nos
coconut - grated 1cup
sugar required amount
rice flour
elaichi powder
salt

Method:

  1. Boil the sweet potatoes and when cooled smash it fine.
  2. Add grated coconut and sugar and mix well.
  3. Add the elaichi powder as well.
  4. Keep the mixture aside.
  5. Mix the rice flour with water and a bit salt.
  6. This rice mixture is for the coating.
  7. Make small balls of the potato mixture and dip in the rice mixture and fry it.
  8. It gives a brown color and it tastes crispy.

Depending how small or big you make the balls for 3 small potatoes you will approximately have 10 suseums. It is a very good snack.

Time for preparation - max 30 min
