Appachi

Ingredients

1/2 cup all purpose flour
1/2 cup fine rava (sooji)
1/2 cup rice flour
3 onions finely cut
2-3 tsp red chilli powder
Salt to taste
2 tsp heated oil
Oil to fry

Method:

  1. Mix the 3 flours together.
  2. To the above mixture add 2 tsp heated oil, chilli powder, salt and cut onions. Mix well.
  3. take aside a little of mixture each time into another bowl and mix well by adding little by little water
  4. Take little by little of the above mix and pat it on your palm to make it flat and round and considerably thick.
  5. Deep fry them in oil. This can be done before hand and stored for a fortnight.

Note: Make sure when u flatten the mixture it should be quiet thick. If u fry a very thin flattened mixture then u'll get crusty appachis.
