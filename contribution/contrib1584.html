Moong Dal Chutney

Ingredients

Moong dal - 1 cup
Red Chilies - 7 to 10 (as per your taste)
Garlic - 2 cloves
Cumin seeds - 1 tsp
Salt - As per taste
Lime Juice - 2 Tbsp

For Seasoning:

Urad dal - 1 tsp
Chana dal - 1 tsp
Hing - a pinch
Mustard Seeds- 1 tsp
Curry leaves - 2 to 3
Oil - 2 Tbsp

Method:

  1. Soak moong dal for nearly 30 mins or until done.
  2. Then grind soaked moong dal, red chilies, cumin seeds, salt and garlic into a thick fine paste.
  3. Then take oil in the seasoning pan, first put mustard seeds. When they start spluttering add urad dal, chana dal and hing. When the contents become golden brown, add curry leaves and add the seasoning mix to the ground paste. Then add the lime juice.

Serve with Hot Rice and Ghee. It goes with Bread also. Enjoy the recipe.
