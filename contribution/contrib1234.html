Pineapple Souffle

Ingredients

Sweet Pineapple - 1 small tin (cut into very small peices)
Cream - 250 gms
Gelatin - 1 tb.sp
Sugar - 6 tb.sp
Water - 1/4 cup

Serves for 8 people

Method:

  1. In 1/4 cup of warm water mix gelatin and stir till the gelatin is dissolved.
  2. Heat half cup of pineapple syrup and sugar, when sugar is dissolved mix gelatin to it and take it off from the stove immideatly.
  3. After it cooles keep this in the freezer.
  4. When it is half set, put it in the mixy and grind it well, and now pour it in a serving bowl and add cream and pineapple tit bits, mix well and keep in the freezer.
  5. After it has set, keep it in the fridge.

This souffle should be served cool.
