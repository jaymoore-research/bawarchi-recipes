Apple Pie

Ingredients

Apple 2 nos
Sugar 1 cup
Cinamon Powder 1 tbs

For Pastry:
White Flour 200 gms
Baking Powder 1 tbs
Salt to Taste
Oil / Ghee for deep frying

Method:

  1. De seed Apples & chop them into small pieces.
  2. Heat a pan add the chopped apples & sugar, cook till the sugar dissolves & apples become tender.
  3. Check if more sugar is need because the apples might be sour.
  4. Add cinnamon powder & mix, take out & keep aside.
  5. Add a little ghee/oil to the flour, baking powder & salt.
  6. Kneed it to soft pliable dough adding water.
  7. Roll the dough into small squares , add spoonful of the apple mix in the center, seal the edges with little water.
  8. U can also shape in the gujiya mould.
  9. In heavy bottomed pan heat oil , fry on a low fire till crispy& done.
  10. I hope you have a great time enjoying this wonderful pie.
