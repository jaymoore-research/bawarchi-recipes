Kamli's Chutney

Ingredients

10-12 flakes of Garlic
1 small Tomato
a small bunch of fresh green Coriander
8-10 Green Chillies
1 tsp. of Jeera
A pinch of Asafoetida
Pinch of Sugar
Pinch of Turmeric
and salt to taste

Method:

  1. Cut the tomato into half. Roughly chop the coriander.
  2. Cut the chillies into smaller pieces.
  3. Put the above into a grinder and add the rest of the ingredients and grind to fine paste.
  4. Add a little water if the chutney's too thick!!

Tastes great for a change!! A Chutney without coconut!!
