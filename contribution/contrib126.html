Uttapam

Ingredients

Rice flour -3cups
Coconut milk-1can
Dry yeast-1tsp
Sugar -1tbs
Water-3cups

Method

  1. Warm 2tbs of water, mix in 1tbs sugar & 1tsp yeast,keep aside.
  2. In a mixing bowl, mix the flour & coconut milk.
  3. Make it into a smooth paste making sure there are no lumps.
  4. Add water gradually & mixing thoroghly till a thin batter is made (like that of dosa).
  5. Add the dissolved yeast into the batter, mix & let it ferment for 6-8hrs.
  6. When the batter has rised up significantly, it's ready to be used.
  7. Heat a Tawa (in medium flame), pour 1 ladel full of batter into the tawa, rottate the tawa once so that the batter spreads around a little.
  8. Cover & let cook for a minute.Using a spatula remove the appam when ready.
  9. Serve it hot with a spicy side dish(korma)
