Choco-Coconut Balls

Preparation Time: 20 mins.
Cooking Time: None
Serves / Makes: 60 balls

Ingredients

200 gms – Desiccated coconut (coconut powder)
200 gms – Drinking Chocolate powder
2 tsp – Salted Butter
ľ tin condensed milk

Method

  1. First leave aside 3 tbsp of desiccated coconut for decorating the balls.
  2. In a big vessel, mix the desiccated coconut powder and drinking chocolate powder thoroughly.
  3. Put in the butter and mix it lightly.
  4. Lastly add the condensed milk and mix it properly to form a slightly sticky mixture.
  5. Grease the palm of your hand with butter and start forming balls.
  6. Make a slight depression in the balls to give shape to the balls.
  7. Add few granules of the desiccated coconut powder in the depression on the balls to decorate.

Tips

The balls look pretty due to the color combination of white coconut powder over the brown chocolate balls as well as it tastes yummy!
Serve it immediately or can be preserved in the fridge for a long time.
