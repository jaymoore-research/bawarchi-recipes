Gobi Manchurian

Ingredients

Cauliflower/Gobi - 1
Onion - 2 medium sized
Spring Onion - bunch (optional)
Garlic
Green chillies - 5
Ginger paste - 1 tsp
Garlic paste - 1 tsp
(if not paste can grate some ginger and garlic)
Corn flour
Rice flour
All purpose flour (maida flour)
Gram flour
Oil
Salt
Red chille powder
Soya Sauce
Tomato Sauce(I prefer maggi hot and sweet)
Coriander / cilantro(optional)

Method

  1. Chop onion, garlic, chilli and spring onion into very fine pieces andĀ keep it aside.
  2. Cut the Gobi onto medium sized pieces and steam it/Microwave until it's half cooked.
  3. MixĀ gram flour, corn flour, all purpose flour and rice flourĀ in this ration(1:1/2:1/4:1/4)cups respectively. Can also use either rice flour onlyĀ or all purpose flour only if u don't have bothĀ in this ratio (1:1/2:1/2)
  4. Add salt, redchilli powder, ginger and garlic paste, 6 spoons of Soya sauce and water make it thick consistency.
  5. Here's batter ready. (Add salt and chilli powder according to ur taste)
  6. Dip the cooked Gobi into this batter and deep fry all of them.
  7. MeanwhileĀ parallely put some oil in a pan and fry the above finely chopped vegetables
  8. Once its friedĀ put in a very low flame and then add all the deep fried gobi's
  9. Sprinkle some salt and soya sauce and tomato sauce on them mix it well
  10. Warm it and garnish with coriander/cilantro.
  11. Warm itĀ before serving. yummy!
