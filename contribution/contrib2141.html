Payathamparuppu Masial (Moongdal Masial)

Ingredients

Payathamparuppu (Moongdal) -1 cup
Green Chillies -6, slit sideways
Turmeric Powder -1/2 teaspoon
Tamarind - Lemon sized/1 spoon (if it's in the paste form)
Small Bunch of Coriander leaves, chopped fine
Sambhar Powder -1/2 teaspoon
Tomatoes -2 medium sized/ 1 large sized, chopped into quadrants
Salt to taste

For Tempering

2 Teaspoons Oil
1 Teaspoon Mustard Seeds
1/2 Teaspoon Fenugreek seeds
1 Red Chilli, halved
1/2 Teaspoon Asafoetida Powder
A few Curry leaves

Method

  1. Pressure cook the dal & set it aside.
  2. Soak the tamarind in 1 cup of water & extract the juice. Set aside.
  3. Heat 2 teaspoons oil, add all the ingredients for tempering. When the mustard seeds splutter, add the fenugreek seeds, after they turn slight brown, add the tomatoes & green chillies and saute for 2 mins. Then add the tamarind juice, turmeric powder, sambhar powder and salt. Simmer till the raw smell of the tamarind disappears.
  4. Add the cooked dal. Simmer till everything blends well.
  5. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  6. Serve hot with Rice or Chapathis.

Preparation Time: 10 mins.
Cooking Time : 30 mins.
Serves: 4.
