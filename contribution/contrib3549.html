Vegetable Masala Curry

Ingredients

Green Beans - 1 cup
Carrots chopped into small pieces - 1/2 cup
Peas - 1/4 cup
Potatoes cut into cubes - 2
Onions thinly sliced - 2
Tomatoes cut into pieces - 2
Finely chopped Cilantro or Coriander leaves - 3 Tbsp
Butter - 3 Tbsp
Shahi Jeera - 1/4 tsp
Garam Masala - 1/2 tsp
Red Chilli Powder - 1 tsp(optional)
Channa Masala - 1 tsp
Salt - according to taste
Water - 1/4 cup (optional)

For the Masala

Cashews - 5 or 6 pieces
Red Chillies - 4 or 5
Garlic - 4 or 5 flakes

Method:

  1. Cook all the vegetables except onion and tomatoes keep it aside.
  2. Heat 1 tbsp of butter in a pan on medium flame and fry the cashews, garlic and red chillies till cashews turn golden brown, cool it and blend it together.
  3. Heat the remaining butter in a sauce pan, add the shahi jeera allow it to splutter, add the onions fry till it turns pink. Then add the wet masala, fry till the oil separates. Add the tomatoes cook it for a few minutes till the tomatoes are soft. Then add the veggies, salt and the dry masalas.
  4. Mix it well, add water if necessary since the curry should be in gravy consistency. Lower the flame and allow it to cook for a few minutes.
  5. Garnish it with Cilantro and serve it with Rotis or Rice.
