Lemon Juice Pickle

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Iingredients

Lemons 
Redchilli powder 1/4 cup
Mustard seeds   1/4 cup
Methi seeds   1 tsp
Salt     1/4 cup
Oil

Method

   

  1. Fry mustard seeds and grind it to fine powder.
  2.  Fry methi seed and make powder
  3. Extract juice from lemons
  4. Add chili powder,  mustard powder, methi powder and salt to it and blend it        heat oil and add mustard seeds,jeera, red chilies and curry leaves and pour the pickle in it
  5. Heat it for some time.your lemon juice pickle is ready.it is good with idli and dosa.
