Mushrooms on Toast

Ingredients

1 Cup Sliced Can - Mushrooms
Four Slices of toast
White Sauce

Method

  1. In a frying pan heat 1 Tblsp of margarine or butter and semi fry the mushrooms.
  2. FOR WHITE SAUCE : In two mugs of milk add 2 tsp full white flour, salt and lots of black pepper powder. Mix well.
  3. In the same frying pan of mushrooms add this mixture and keep on stirring till thick and then pour over the toast. serve immediately.
