Papad Vada

Ingredients

1 packet of papad
2 cups of gram flour
1/2 tsp turmeric
1 tsp red chili powder
salt as per taste
Oil to deep fry

Method

  1. Make a thin batterĀusing gram flour and water.
  2. Add red chili powder,turmeric and salt to the batter and mix well.
  3. Dip the papad one by one in the batter and deep fry in oil. Ā

Useful tips:

  1. For crispy microwave papad, spread little bit of oil over a papad, and heat it in a microwave.
  2. If you have any broken pieces of papad, then add that papad while making kadi(yogurt curry)Āand boil for 2-3 minutes.The kadiĀwillĀtasteĀgood.
  3. Store the papad along with rice and pulses to avoid their drying and breakage.
