Cabbage Salad

Ingredients

1/2 small Cabbage finely cut
1/4 Tblsp Salt
1 Tblsp Oil
1 Tblsp Mustard seeds
Pinch of Turmeric powder

Method:

  1. Add the salt to the cut cabbage and keep aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and put the mustard seeds. When they start spluttering, add turmeric and the cabbage.
  3. Mix it and remove it immediately. (let it be a little crunchy)

I don't know the calories and all but, I know its cabbage and its kind of raw so, its got to be good for health.
