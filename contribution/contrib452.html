Modhakam (Kolakatte)

Ingredients

1 cup riceflour
1 cup grated fresh coconut,
1 cup jaggery or brown sugar,
1 tsp cardamom powder,
pinch salt.

Method

  1. Mix sugar, coconut and cardamom powder with little water in a pan and keep stirring until it is thick and can be made to balls. Keep hoorna aside.
  2. Then mix riceflour, salt and little water in a pan and keep stirring until it forms to a right consistency. It should not be sticking to the pan, and with your hand u should be able to make balls.
  3. Take some cooked riceflour mix and spread it in the size of a small puri (like the ones used for panipuris), put some hoorna inside and close it.
  4. Make some 10-15 of them at a time, and in a pressure cooker, put water at the bottom, keep a plate and put these on them, cook at high for 10 mins. Then take them out, keep aside, serve with ghee on top.
  5. Repeat the same for rest of them.

Time: 30 mins
Makes: 25-30 kolakatte.
