Watte Koumbo

Ingredients

oil-2 big tbsp
dry red chillie-5-6
rai(mustard seeds)_ 1 tbsp
fenugreek seeds_ 1 tbsp
asafetida (hing)
bengal gram- 2 tbsp
urad dal- 1 tbsp
sambhar powder- 2 tbsp
curry leaves
extracted tamarind water - 2 cups
jaggery a bit
salt to taste

Method

  1. Heat oil in a pan put hing dry red chillie and then slowly add rai( mustard seeds) , fenugreek seed let it splutter add bengal gram dal, urad dal curry leaves fry it till golden brown in medium flame.
  2. Add sambhar powder and without frying quickly add tamarind water, a bit of jaggery and salt to it.
  3. Let it boil till it becomes little thick.
  4. To make it more tasty sprinkle little fenugreek and turmeric powder to it.

Eat it with hot piping rice and roasted potato
