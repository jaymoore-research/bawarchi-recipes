5-Minutes Zucchini Pickle

Ingredients

1 zucchini one medium size
2 Red chilli powder four teaspoons
3 salt according the taste
4 Asafetida
5 one teaspoon lime juice or imli (mango) powder

Method

  1. Remove the skin and cut the zucchini into small pieces.
  2. Add the ingredients and mix it. Now it is ready to serve.
