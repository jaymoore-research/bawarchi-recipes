Mango Kulfi

Ingredients

32 oz Fat-Free Ricotta Cheese
28-35 oz Fat-Free Sweetened Condensed Milk
48 oz Mango Pulp/Pieces/Juice
Sugar to taste

Method:

  1. Let Ricotta Cheese soften by removing it from the fridge for a while.
  2. Mix all but sugar till smooth. You can use a food processor or a hand blender.
  3. Add Sugar to taste. Keep mixing so sweetness is uniform.
  4. Pour either in Kulfi containers or a Bundt cake mold.
  5. Freeze till set (at least 5 hours).
Enjoy!

This serves several comfortably.
