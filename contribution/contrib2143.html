Cashew Chicken Fry

Ingredients

Boneless Chicken - 2 lb (cleaned and cut into cubes)
Onions - 2 (finely chopped)
Green Chillies - 2 (finely chopped)
Ginger-Garlic paste - 2 tbsp
Chilli Powder - 4 tsp
Turmeric - 2 tsp
Cinnamon - 3 sticks (powdered)
Cloves - 3 (powdered)
Star Anise - 1
Cashews powdered - 1/2 cup
Tomatoes - 1 (finely chopped)
Oil - 4 tbsp
Cilantro - 1/2 bunch (finely chopped)
Salt - according to taste

Method :

  1. Take a pan add in the chicken, salt, ginger garlic paste, turmeric, chilli powder, half of the cinnamon + clove powder. Mix well and cook the chicken until completely dry. Take care and add very less of water or otherwise the chicken may become mushy.
  2. Take another pan add oil. When the oil reaches its smoking point then add in the onions, star anise and fry until the onions are golden brown in colour. Then add in the chopped tomato and cook until the tomato is soft.
  3. Add in the cooked chicken and mix well. Now add in the rest of the cinnamon + clove powder.
  4. Fry the chicken for about 4 - 5 min. Then add in the cashew powder and mix well.
  5. Fry the chicken for about 7 - 8 min more until completely dry and golden brown in colour.
  6. Garnish it with chopped cilantro and serve hot either with Chapathis or with Rice and Sambhar or Rasam.

Enjoy !
