Rice Crisps

Ingredients

2 cup rice
1/4 cup urad dal
A pinch of asafoetida
A pinch of soda bicarb
Oil as needed
Salt to taste.

Method:

  1. Soak the rice in water for 2 hours.
  2. Dram and pound it to a fine powder.
  3. Roast urad dal and powder.
  4. Mix both rice flour and dal powder, add sesame seeds, asafotida, soda bicarb and salt.
  5. Add oil and roll into a soft douhg with water.
  6. Press batches of this dough through a murukku mould and deep fry in an oil till crisp.
  7. Drain and store in an airtight tin.
