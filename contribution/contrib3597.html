Potato Jeera Rice

Ingredients

Basmati Rice : 1 1/2 cup
Potatoes : 3 (big)
Onion : 1
Tomatoes : 2 (small)
Garlic : 6 cloves
Ginger : a small piece
Green Chillies : 3
Jeera : 1 tblsp
Tumeric powder : 1 pinch
Chilli powder : 1/2 tblsp
Masala powder : 1/2 tblsp
Cashewnuts : 10
Curry leaves & cilantro : a smal bunch
Salt : to taste
Ghee : 3 tblsp

Method:

  1. In a Pan, add little ghee and fry the rice till it becomes slightly brown and keep aside.
  2. Again in a pan, add 1 tblsp of oil, fry the jeera and keep aside.
  3. Cut the Potatoes in the shape of french fries.
  4. Chop the Onions and Tomatoes.
  5. In a Frying Pan, add 1 tblsp of Ghee, put the onion, when it turns slightly golden brown add tomatoes and let it cook for a while.
  6. Make a paste of garlic, ginger and chillies and add that to the vegetables, finally add the potatoes and let it cook for another 6 to 10 mins. Add, turmeric, chilli powder and salt.
  7. In a Rice Cooker, pour 2tblsp Ghee and the cooked vegetables and the rice with 1 1/2 cups of water add now masala powder (1/2 tsp) and fried Jeera.
  8. When the rice is done, remove from the heat and add fried cashewnuts and chopped curry and cilantro leaves.

Servings:

For 4 people and serve it with green pepper raita. If you want this rice to be simple and make it fast add just the jeera, boiled and cooked potatoes with chilli powder and masala powder to the rice and when it is done add the cilantro leaves and serve it with Tomato or Onion Raita.
