Batati Bhajji

Ingredients

2 medium sized Boiled Potatoes
1 cup of Curd
1 Big size Onion (chopped fine)
2 Green Chillies
2 small piece of Ginger
Salt to taste
Turmeric powder, Mustard seeds
1 tbsp. Oil
1 Tomato
Few leaves of fresh Coriander

Method:

  1. Smash boiled potatoes in a bowl.
  2. To this add chopped onion, tomato, ginger and coriander leaves.
  3. Heat oil in a small frying pan, add mustard seeds, green chillies and turmeric powder. Pour this seasoning on the mixture, add salt according to taste
  4. Serve it with Parathas, Puris, Chapatis etc.
