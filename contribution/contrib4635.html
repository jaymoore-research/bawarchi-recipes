Apple Pickle

Ingredients

Two small Green Apples.
Chilli powder -1/2 tea spoon
Salt to taste
Oil 2 tea spoon
Mustard seeds 1/4 tea spoon
Vinegar 5 drops
Fenugreek seeds 1/2 tea spoon
Asafoetida powder 1 pinch
Turmeric powder 1/4 teaspoon.

Method:

  1. Cut apples into small pieces, add chilli powder and salt.
  2. Take oil in pan. Add mustard seeds, heat till seeds crackle. Immediately close the flame after adding fenugreek seeds, asafoetida and turmeric powder. Allow to cool then pour oil in the apples and you are ready to serve lunch.
  3. Store in fridge in air tight container.

Shelf life:1 weeks.
Preparation time:30 mins.
