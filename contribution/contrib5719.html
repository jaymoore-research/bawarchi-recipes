Basmati Rice Halwa

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients

Basmati Rice - 1 Cup
Milk               - 3 Cups
Water            - 1 Cup
Sugar            - 2 Cups
Cashewnuts   - 6 nos
Raisins          - 8 nos
Cinnamon      -1
Cloves           - 2
Cardamom - 2

SUGAR SYRUP:
Prepare a thick sugar syrup by adding half cup of water with the sugar.In a frying pan heat 2 tablespoons of ghee and fry the cashewnuts, raisins, cardamom, cloves & cinnamon one by one till golden brown and mix it with the sugar syrup.

METHOD  

  1. Soak the rice for 30 mins & drain it. 
  2. In a thick pan boil the milk with water, while boiling starts add the rice and cook it completely by reducing the stove to low heat.
  3. Stir the rice for two minutes once, to avoid the burning in the bottom of the pan.
  4. When rice is completely cooked add the prepared sugar syrup to the rice and stir well to avoid the lumps. 
  5. Keep it for ten minutes in low flame & serve it after 30 mins to enjoy the taste.
