Mango Chutney

Ingredients

1 large Mango
50 ml. (2 fl oz) Apple Vinegar with honey
115 gm. (4 oz) Demerara Sugar
225 ml. (8 fl oz) Water
1/2 teaspoon shelled Cardamom seeds
1/2 teaspoon ground Ginger
1/2 teaspoon ground Cloves
150 gr. (5 oz) Sultanas
2 Cinnamon sticks

Method:

  1. Peel the mango, remove the stone and cut the flesh into large chunks.
  2. Place the mango chunks in a saucepan and add all the remaining ingredients. Stir well.
  3. Place over high heat and bring to a boil, then reduce heat and bring to a simmer. Simmer for 15 minutes. Let cool.
