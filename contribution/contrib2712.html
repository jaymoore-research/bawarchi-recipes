Bread Upma

Ingredients

Bread : 6 slices trimmed at the edges (prefferable) and cut into 4-6 pieces
Oil 1 tbsp
Mustard , 1/2 tsp
Channa dhal 1/2 tsp
Kadi patha 8 leaves
Urad dhal 1/2 tsp
1 medium sized onion
Cut vegetables 1 cup (carrot , beans , peas) pre boiled
Asoefetida (LG) foa dash
Salt ,
Chillies (2 red and 3 green)

Method:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and addmustard asoefetida, channa dhal, urad dhal, chillies, kadi patha and let it fry till the dhal becomes brown.
  2. Now add the pieces of Bread and sprinkle some water and fry for some time till the breead turns brown.
  3. You can add salt and add sugar to it if preffered. Best in combination with mint chutney.
