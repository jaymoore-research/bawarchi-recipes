Dhanya Chicken

Ingredients

Chicken Legs : 6 pieces
Bay Leaves : 3-4
Onion (cut into thin stripes) : 2 medium size
Ginger : 1 teaspoon (paste)
Garlic : 1 teaspoon (paste)
Dhanya (coriander Leaves) : 1 bunch
Green Chilies : 4-5
Curd : 3 tablespoon
Turmeric Powder : 1 /2 tablespoon
Ghee : 1-2 tablespoon
Cooking oil : 4-6 tablespoon
Sugar : to taste
Salt : to taste

Garam Masala:

Cardamoms :5-6, (Coarsely Crushed)
Cloves : 2-3 (Coarsely Crushed)
Cinnamon Stick : 1-2 (Coarsely Crushed)

Method:

  1. Take the chicken legs and marinate it with curd, a pinch of salt and turmeric powder for 30 min.
  2. Take the coriander (dhanya) leaves and the green chillies and grind it well in a grinder for 1 min and make a paste.
  3. In a cooking pan add cooking oil, ghee and heat it. Then add sugar, crushed garam masala, bay leaf stir a little then to it add the onion pieces, ginger and garlic paste and fry till light brown.
  4. Then to it add the marinated chicken mix it well with the spices in the pan in medium heat. Then cook it covered for sometime until the chicken becomes tender and the gravy thicken.
  5. Now add the dhanya paste and salt according to your taste to the chicken. Cook for sometime (5-10 min) in low flame so the paste enters into the juicy chicken. Serve hot with rice or chapatties. You can also decorate the dish with coriander leaf and tomatoes.
