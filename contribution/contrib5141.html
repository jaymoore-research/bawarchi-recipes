Manpasand Gobi

Ingredients

One whole cauliflower
One or two potatoes(optional)
Green peas(optional)
Jeera(1 tsp)
Vegetable oil (1 tsp)- Minimum oil is required if non stick pan is used.
Salt- (To taste)
Coriander to garnish

For the Marinade

Minced Ginger and garlic - 1 tsp approx or depending on your taste
Turmeric Powder - 1/4 tsp approx
Red Chilli Powder -1/2 tsp
Coriander Powder-1 tsp
Tomato ketchup-3-4 Tsp(approx)
Tomato paste or plain puree-1-2 Tsp(approx)

Preparation:

  1. Separate the base of the cauliflower and cut it into big size florets by splitting action of the knife so that the pieces look like open book
  2. Cut cubes of the potato
  3. Mix the ingredients mentioned for the marinade well in a big bowl,put the cauliflower potato and peas in the bowl and coat well with the marinade. Close the bowl and allow to rest for a maximum of 2 hrs.
  4. Heat given oil in the non stick pan well, splutter jeera, put the marinated gobi and potato, give a good stir so that the vegetables get turned well and simmer.
  5. Close the lid to the pan with a little vent and let the vegetables cook in the marinade juices slowly. Occasionally remove lid and give gentle stir without breaking florets.
  6. After well cooked(avoid over cooking) add salt, mix well , taste and take out on serving dish.
  7. Garnish with coriander.

The recipe is low in oil.It tastes wonderful and all the 4-5 times I have made it, the taste has been consistent. Guests have loved it saying quote" it the first time we have eaten gobi so succulent and juicy and the best of all gobi florets remaining whole". I have also tried variation in marinades by substituting tomato ketchup and puree by yoghurt, but the tomato variation is better according to me. Please try the yoghurt variations and give your experience.
