Mango chutney

Ingredients

4 large half ripe green mangoes (cut into small pieces)
1 onion chopped
3 cloves of garlic
3 red chillies
1 tablespoon sugar
2 teaspoon jeera
salt to taste

Method:

  • Blend all the ingredients in a mixer and grind to smooth paste.

Enjoy!!!
