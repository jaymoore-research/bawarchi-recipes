Ingredients for Cooking

Beaten Rice ( Poha ) – 1 Cup
Tamarind paste – 2 teaspoon
Jaggery- 2 teaspoon
Salt- as per taste.
Water- one and half cup
Chilli powder – 1 teaspoon
Grated coconut – 2 tablespoon
Coriander leaves for garnishing.

Seasoning ingredients:
Oil – 2 tablespoon
Mustard seeds – 1/2 teaspoon
Asafoetida – 1 pinch
Turmeric – 1 pinch
Bengal gram dal – 1/4 teaspoon
Urad Dal – 1/4 teaspoon
Groundnut – 1/2 teaspoon
Curry leaves

  Method:  

  1. Coarsely grind poha for a few seconds in a blender to break it to coarse bits. Make sure it doesn’t become powdery.
  2. Soak tamarind, jaggery, salt, chilli powder and salt to water. Mix well and add the coarse poha.
  3. Heat oil and add mustard. Allow it to pop
  4. Add turmeric, asafetida, curry leaves and rest of the seasoning ingredients.
  5. Add the soaked poha to the seasoning
