Capsicum-Potato Fry

Ingredients

1. 4 capsicums (washed and cut into small pieces)
2. 2 potatoes (peeled and cubed)
3. 3 tsp Jeera
4. Pepper to taste
5. Salt to taste
6. Oil - 2 tbsp.
7. Butter (optional!)

Method of preparation:

  1. Heat oil in a kadai. Add the jeera seeds to it and fry lightly.
  2. Add the potatoes to it and fry till the potatoes turn golden brown.
  3. Add the capsicum and mix well with potatoes. Cover the pan with a lid and turn the vegetables over from time to time.
  4. When the capsicum is cooked, add pepper to taste (this is the only source of spice, so add accordingly) and stir. Add salt to taste.
  5. If you dont mind a few extra calories, add butter also and stir well. Simple, yet great tasting!
