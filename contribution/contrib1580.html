Tomato Thugayal

Ingredients

Tomato -1/2 kg
2 tbs Tamarind extract (Juice)
1tbs Mustard seeds
1/2 tbs Fenugreek seeds
1tbs Bengalgram Dhal
A pinch of Hing powder
Red Chillies - 4 (break it into two pieces)
Chilli powder - 1 tbs
Oil as required
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. First cut tomato into small pieces.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and when it splutter add fenugreek seeds, bengalgram dhal and red chillies. Fry till it become light brown.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes, mash it well and let it cook for 10 mnts.
  4. Add tamarind juice, salt, chilli powder, hing powder and mix it well.
  5. Let it boil well and become thick, so that the oil will be separated from the gravy.
  6. Preserve it in clean dry airtight jar.

This Fabulous Thugayal goes very well with Idlis, Dosas, chappathi, Puri, Uppma and Rice. Enjoy! Happy Cooking!
