Tomato Thokku

Ingredients for Cooking

Tomatoes - 1 kg
Tamarind - lemon size ball
green chillies - 4-5
salt- to taste
oil - 6 tbsps
red chilli powder - 3-4 tbsps
hing - 1 tsp
mustard seeds - 1 tsp

Method:

  1. Cut the tomatoes into half and then again into 4 cubes.(one tomato will be cut into 8 cubes)
  2. Put these pieces in a big vessel, in between these pieces put the tamarind thus covering the tamarind with the tomato pieces and keep like this aside for 3-4 hours.
  3. Make a puree of the tomatoes, tamarind and green chillies till smooth.
  4. In a kadhai, put oil add hing and splutter mustard seeds.
  5. Put the tomato mixture, chilli powder and salt to taste and keep stirring till it becomes thick.
