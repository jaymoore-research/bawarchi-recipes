Rice kheer

Ingredients

Rice - 1/2 Cup
Milk 4 cups
Condensed Milk 1 cup
Sugar - 2 cups
Cashews, Elachi powder and Saffron

Method :

  1. Pressure cook 1/2 cup rice with 1 cup water and 1/4 cup milk.
  2. Meanwhile, boil milk, in reduced flame, add condensed milk.
  3. Soak one spoon of raw-rice and 10-15 cashews in milk for 10 minutes. Grind it to a coarse paste. Add to the boiling milk. Add 2 cups of sugar, stir till it dissolves.
  4. Then add the cooked rice, and boil for a few more minutes. Finally add Saffron, Elachi powder and cashews and almonds (fried in butter/ghee)

Can make this kheer in any consistency you want. Can be consumed either hot or can refridgerate for a couple of hours and serve cool. Excellent dessert for mini parties at home.

Serves : 5-6 people
