Chicken Roast

Ingredients

chicken - 1kg (cut into big pieces)
cinnamon - 2-3 sticks
cardamom - 4
cloves - 6-7
ginger garlic paste - 2 tbs
whole red chilli -10
onion - 2 big cut in to thin long pieces
tomato - 2 big diced
salt to taste
oil for frying

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a pan after two minutes put cinnamon stick, cardamom, cloves and red chillis
  2. When it is frying, add onion and fry till golden brown.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste fry for a while.
  4. Add diced tomatoes and fry for another two minutes.
  5. Finally add the chicken pieces and fry well for a minute or more.
  6. Add half a cup of water and salt to it.
  7. Close the pan and allow it for boiling.
  8. Stir occasionally otherwise the chicken pieces will break.
  9. Fry till the gravy turn thick paste.
  10. When the gravy and chicken looks dark brown colour put off the stove.
  11. Your delicious roast is ready.
  12. Serve hot with chappathi or rice.
  13. You can keep the leftover for more than three days without refrigerate.
