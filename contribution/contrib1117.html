Veggi Cutlets

Ingredients

3 Potatoes
2 Carrots
Few green beans
1/2 cup peas
1 onion
5-6 Green chilies paste (per taste)
Garlic paste
Oil to shallow fry
Rava
Salt
Coriander (optional)

Method :

  1. Boil potatoes, remove the skin, mash and keep aside
  2. Finely chop onion, beans and shred carrots
  3. Then in a Kadai, put 2 tea spoons of oil, keep on medium flame and add onions
  4. Take care that the onions don't burn.
  5. After 3-4 minutes, add beans, peas and carrots
  6. Close the lid and keep for a while on medium-low flame, till you see that beans are done
  7. Add this mixture of vegetables to the mashed potatoes.
  8. Add garlic and chilies paste with salt to taste
  9. One can add finely chopped coriander (optional).
  10. Now thoroughly mix it.
  11. Make patties and roll them in rava.
  12. Heat a frying pan, place these patties and add very little oil and shallow fry (you can use fat free sprays too)
