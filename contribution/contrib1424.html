Corn Kheer

Ingredients

Frozen Corn - 1 1/4 cups
Milk - 2 litres
Sugar - 1 1/4 cups
Elachi powder -1/2 tsp
Pistas and Badam for garnishing

Method :

  1. Pressure cook corn with enough milk. Then grind it to a nice paste. If it is not a smooth paste you can filter it. Otherwise leave it like that.
  2. In a heavy-bottomed pan, put the paste to a boil. Add little more milk if necessary. Boil it till the raw smell of corn goes.
  3. Add 1 litre of milk and let it thicken to less than half. Always remeber to keep it at low heat.
  4. When reduced, add the sugar and after 5 minutes add the rest of the milk.
  5. Allow this to thicken for another 20-25 minutes.
  6. Chill it and before serving add chopped pistachios and badam so that it floats on top.
