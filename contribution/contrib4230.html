Seyal Kadai Gosht

Ingredients

1.. 750 grams Meat Boneless or bones as you prefer [Tender Lamb Meat]
2.. 100 grams Butter
3.. 5 medium sized Onions
4.. 2 medium size Tomatoes
5.. 1 inch Ginger
6.. 1/2 bunch Coriander leaves
7.. few sprigs of fresh Mint Leaves or pudina
8.. 2-3 Green Chillies
9.. 1 cup fresh Curd

Dry Masalas

1.. 6 Green Cardamoms
2.. 1 teaspoon Jeera
3.. 2 Black Cardamoms [use only seeds]
4.. 2 Bay Leaves
5.. 3 Cloves
6.. 1 inch Cinnamon
7.. 1tea spoons of Kasuri Methi [heat it on take for a minute and powder it with rolling pin or hands

Powder Masalas

1.. 1 table spoon Coriander powder
2.. 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder
3.. 1 tea spoon Garam Masala
4.. 1 tea spoon Red Chilli Kashmir powder
5.. Salt to taste [as the butter is salted add less salt]

Method:

  1. Take 100 grams butter heat it in a pressure cooker add all the spices from 1-6 as the stop spluttering add onions and ginger.
  2. As it turns golden brown add the mutton, tomatoes fresh coriander and the powder masalas and 1/4 cup of hot water and allow it to simmer over slow flame for 25-30 minutes. keep stirring so that it does not stick to the bottom.
  3. As the butter separates from the mutton and 1-1/4 glass of water and pressure cook it for 20 minutes or till the mutton is soft tender and juicy.
  4. Take out mutton and gravy in a kadai or frying pan and keep it on a slow flame.
  5. Take 1 cup of fresh curds and beat the curds till fine add this to the mutton add the powder kasuri methi and simmer it for 5 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh mint leaves.
  7. Serve hot with Roti or lachha parathas and onion rings with mint chutney.
