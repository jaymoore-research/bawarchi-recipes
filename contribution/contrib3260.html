Potato-Onion Stew

Ingredients

Potato 2
Onion 1 (big)
Green Chillies 3
Coconut milk 1/2 tin
Salt

Method:

  1. Cut the onions in cubes and slit the green chillies and cook it with little water.
  2. Cut the cooked potatoes in cubes and once the onions are cooked add the potatoes and let them cook together for few min.
  3. Now add coconut milk and keep on med flame for 5 min.
  4. Don't keep on the stove for too long after adding coconut milk.
  5. This is a simple dish which you can try.

Have fun !
