Milk Powder Jamun

Ingredients

Milk Powder -- 3 cups
All purpose flour -- 1/12 cups
Baking Soda -- 1/2 tsp
Sugar -- 3 cups
Water -- 2 cups
Cardamom Powder -- pinch
Saffron -- pinch
Crisco shortening -- optional
Ghee -- 6-tsp
Milk -- 1 cup

Milk Powder (Nido milk powder You can buy Indian grocery store)

Method

  1. Mix Milk Powder, All purpose flour, Baking Soda & Ghee in the bowl.
  2. Make the dough by adding with milk.
  3. Make the dough in to small balls.
  4. Heat Crisco shortening and fry the balls
  5. Take one vessel put sugar and water boil until it becomes a light syrup
  6. Add saffron and cardamom.
  7. Let it cool for an hour and then serve with syrup.
  8. I hope you like it.
