Spicy Groundnuts

Ingredients

Groundnuts (roasted) 1 cup
Besan 1 tbsp.
Chilli powder 3 tsp.
Aamchur 2 tsp.
Garam masala 1/2 tsp
Baking powder a pinch
Salt to taste
Oil for frying

Method:

  1. Make a paste out of all the ingredients, except besan and groundnuts.
  2. Pour a cup of groundnuts over this paste.
  3. Sprinkle besan over it.
  4. Mix well to see all the groundnuts are coated with the mixture.
  5. Fry in little oil on medium heat for 5 minutes.
  6. Cool it and enjoy with tea.
