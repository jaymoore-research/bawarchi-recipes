Spinach & Moong Dal Special

Ingredients

1 fresh bunch of Spinach thoroughly washed and cut
1 cup Moong Dal
1 medium sized tomato chopped
2 pods of garlic, crushed
1 piece of ginger, crushed
1 tsp cumin seeds
1 tsp aajwain seeds
2 tsps red chilli powder or as desired
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
2 tsps corriander powder
Juice if one lemon
2 tsps sugar
2 tblsps Oil
Salt to taste

Method

  1. Wash the Moong Dal well and pressure cook (three whistles)
  2. Take Oil in a deep nonstick pan/pot
  3. Add cumin seeds, aajwain seeds and let them splutter
  4. Add garlic and ginger
  5. Add tomatoes and saut for a couple of minutes till they are soft
  6. Add pressure cooked Moong Dal alongwith the water which is used to pressure cook the dal.
  7. If the gravy is too thick,add some more water
  8. Add salt, sugar, red chilli powder, turmeric and corriander powder and let the gravy simmer till everything is mixed well
  9. Now add the chopped Spinach and mix well
  10. Add juice of lemon
  11. Cover the pan/pot and let the vegetable simmer for few minutes till the spinach is tender, stir occasionally
  12. And your Spinach & Moong Dal Special is ready to be served!

Serve hot with phulkas,rice and pickel!

Have fun eating!
