Modak

Ingredients

1 cup maida (fine flour)
1 tsp Ghee
1 lb Khoya
100 gm mixed nuts unsalted
100 gm Coconut powder (optional)
1 lb sugar
Ghee to fry

Method:-

Filling:

  1. Fry dry khoya in a thick bottom pan on medium heat till it starts leaving some ghee on the sides.
  2. Mix in sugar, nuts and coconut powder.
  3. Stir vigorously. Reduce heat and cook till the mixture leaves sides.
  4. Take away from heat and cool.

Covering::

  1. Knead together maida and ghee and form into a soft dough with help of some warm water.
  2. Make as many small (1/2 inch diam ) balls as possible.
  3. Roll out one into a small round (3 inch diam).
  4. Put 2 tsp of filling in between and gather all the sides at once at the top and press all sides together to seal it. Thus prepare all modaks. Keep aside.
  5. Heat ghee.
  6. Fry in medium heat in ghee till it has little touches of red on the sides.

Serve hot or cold. Can store in airtight container for a few days.

An important tip;

When preparing raw modaks they have a tendency of drying up before frying. So this can cause them to open or burst (in rare cases) in the frying ghee thus making a great mess.

To avoid this, wet a muslin cloth or any thin cloth. Squeeze out as much water as possible, and as the raw modaks are made, cover them with this. That'all help.
