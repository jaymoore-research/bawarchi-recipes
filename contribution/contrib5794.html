Low-fat Mango Icecream

Ingredients

1 large mango
1 tub (1/2 litre) store bought yogurt
3Ātablespoon powdered sugar, add more to taste

Ā Method

  1. Cut the mango flesh into small cubes and freeze solid.
  2. Hang the yogurt in a muslin cloth until the whey is removed, preferably overnight.Ā I normally place the muslin cloth with the yogurt on a strainer placed over a container in the fridge.Ā
  3. ĀThe hung yogurt should be very cold but not frozen.
  4. Place the mixing bowl of your food processor in the freezer around 15 minutes before you begin making the ice-cream, so that the ingredients remain cold in a warm kitchen.
  5. Whisk the mango cubes in the food processor until it become slushy - don't overdo this.
  6. Add the powdered sugar and hung yogurt and whisk till smooth.Ā
  7. Check the sugar, add a dash of lemon if you like the tart taste.
  8. Quickly remove the ice-cream from the food processor into a plastic tub and place in the freezer.Ā Ā

Its ready to eat almost immediately and its delicious, low in fat and free of eggs.Ā I sometimes add a little store-bought vanilla or strawberry ice-cream to enhance the flavours.Ā Its great even without that!
