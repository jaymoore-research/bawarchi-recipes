Easy Mysropak

Ingredients

1 cup Besan
1 cup Water
1 and half cups Melted Ghee
1 and a half cups Sugar

Method:

  1. Mix 1cup besan with 1cup of ghee, stirring well, in a bowl.
  2. In a heavy bottom pan (or kadhai) take water, sugar and the mixed besan with ghee.
    Note: Turn on the heat only when all the contents are ready in the kadhai.
  3. On low flame keep stirring until a net is formed.
  4. Add the remaining melted ghee.
  5. Stir till pale brown or nice golden colour with the netted formation, like in a mysorepak.
  6. Empty the contents on a greased plate.
  7. Do not flatten with spoon.
  8. Cut into squares when slightly cool. Cut one piece at a time, so as to not crumble the contents.
