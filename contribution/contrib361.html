Karnataka style Kootu

Ingredients

for Masala :
1) 4 tbs Dhaniya ( Gota / whole)
2) 8-9 red chillies ( increase the chillies for more hot taste)
3) 2 tbs Jeera
4) 7-8 pepper corns
5) 1 small piece of cinnamon
5) 1 tbs Chana dal
6) 1&1/2 to 2 spoons Urad dal
7) 2 tbs dry/fresh coconut powder
Soak required amount of tamarind in water.

Method

Veggies & Dal :

  1. Mixed vegetables (potatoes,carrots,cauliflower,cabbage,knol- khol,greenpeas) and ground nuts.
  2. Take 2 cups of Arhar dal ( Take more than usual ).
  3. Cut the veggies in slightly bigger sizes as they melt away if cut in very smallsizes.Mix them with dal to boil in a cooker for upto 2-3 whistles.

Procedure:

  1. Fry the masala taken above and grind them to a smooth paste,add this to the boiled vegetables and dal.Add tamarind water, salt, chilli powder , turmeric and boil for 5-7 minutes or more until it gives a good aroma.
  2. The kootu is ready.

For final touches :

  1. take 4-5 tbs oil in a small wok or frying pan and heat it (take more oil if required ),
  2. 3 tbs jeera.
  3. add 3-4 pinches of hing
  4. immediately add small peices of red chillies and roast for a moment.
  5. add 1&1/2 or 2 tbs rai ( mustard seeds) and let it splutter immediately remove from heat and put this in the prepared kootu and its ready to serve !

I hope you all enjoy the taste of Kootu prepared in Karnataka style .

Serves : 4
Time: 30 min. (excluding boiling time in cooker)
