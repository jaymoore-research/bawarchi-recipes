Adai

Ingredients

Chana dal : 100 gms
Pesar dal : 100 gms
Rice (raw) :100 gms
Urad dal :100 gms
Black Pepper one tea spoon
Red Chillies (5) five;
Salt to taste
Hing powder; Turmeric powder 1/2 tsp.
Raw Grated Coconut a small cup (optional)

Method:

  1. Soak all the dals & rice together in water (of course after cleaning them for stones etc.) for about four hours; preferably overnight.
  2. Wash thoroughly & grind to a coarse paste in a mixie adding very little water and with red chillies and black pepper. Consistency should be that of vadai (paruppu vadai). If ground very nice the adais will not come out nice. Mix hing powder & turmeric powder and salt. Leave it for about half an hour. Do not allow to ferment.
  3. Heat a thava /griddle (thick one) and apply little oil evenly. Spread the batter (adai mavu) evenly and make a few holes with a knife (one in the center & one each on all four directions). Add little oil into these holes and cook the adai. Turn around and cook till golden brown and crisp.
  4. Serve hot with home made Butter & Jaggery / or Morkuzhambu/ or Avial/ or Sambahr/ or Curd.

Serves for eight to ten "adais".

Husbands if your wives request(?) you to follow my example do not blame me.

P.S. You can make the adais more tastier by adding cabbage / grated coconut / finely chopped onions / and even grated carrots. Happy eating.
