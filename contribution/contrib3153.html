Cauliflower Parathas

Ingredients

For filling:

  1. Wash one medium size cauliflower and finely chop it. Keep in a bowl of water and keep aside.
  2. Take oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds to it. When they start to change colour, add a pinch of asafoetida powder, 2 pinches of turmeric powder. Drain the cauliflower and add it to this. Cook covered by stirring occasionally till cauliflower is soft.
  3. Add (3-4 tspn) dhana powder and 2 tspn cumin powder, some red chilli powder and 2-3 tbspn gram flour (besan) to this cooked cauliflower. Stir fry till the cauliflower is coated well and dry. Than add a little salt and 1 tspn sugar.
  4. Remove from gas.

Making Parathas:

  1. Make paratha dough and keep aside.
  2. After filling is cooled, make balls of dough and fill them with the mixture.
  3. Roll into 5-6 inch round parathas.
  4. Shallow fry with oil or ghee.
  5. Serve hot with Chutney or Curd.

Tasty Cauliflower Parathas are ready!!!
