Quick Spiced Rice

Ingredients

Rice 1 cup
Chilly powder 1 tsp.
Turmeric powder 1/2 tsp
Sugar 1 tsp.
Karipatha (curry leaves) - a few leaves
Salt to taste
Ground nut 2 Tblsp.
Mustard seeds 1/2 tsp.
Urd dhal 1/2 tsp.
Oil 2 Table spoon

Method

  1. Add a tsp oil and salt to rice and cook with less water so, when rice is done the grains are separate.Keep aside.
  2. Heat remaining oil in a pressure pan.
  3. Crackle mustard seeds and fry urd dhal.
  4. When half done, add groundnuts and fry till groundnut crackles. Turn off heat.
  5. Add turmeric, powder, chilly powder and karipatha (curry leaves).
  6. Add rice and sugar and mix well and serve.
