Chinese Veg Delight

Ingredients

1. Shredded cabbage 1 cup
2. Sliced beans 3/4 cup
3. Spring onions 2
4. Diced carrot 1 cup
5. Cauliflower florets 1/2 cup
6. Green chillies 5
7. All purpose flour 3 tbsp
8. Corn flour 5 tbsp
9. Ajinomoto 1 tsp
10. Chilli sauce 1 tbsp
11. Salt to taste
12. Oil to deep fry

For garnishing :

1. Finely cut spring onions
2. Finely chopped chillies and garlic
3. Ginger julienes
4. Soya sauce
5. Finely chopped curry leaves
6. Chilli sauce

Method :

  1. Steam the vegetables(1..6). Do not cook fully.
  2. Blend the half cooked vegetables coarsely.
  3. Add salt, chilli sauce, ajinomoto, flour and cornflour and mix well.
  4. Make small dumplings of the above mixture and deep fry until brown.
  5. Dish them onto a paper napkin to drain excess oil.
  6. Take a shallow pan, put 2 tsps oil and add the spring onions, garlic, ginger julienes and curryleaves.
  7. Put the fried dumplings into the pan and add soya and chilli sauce.
  8. Add about 1/4 cup or less of water so as to soften the dumplings.
  9. Stir fry on high flame for 2 to 3 minutes.
  10. Sprinkle ajinomoto if desired.
  11. Serve hot with tomato or chilli sauce.
