Oothappam and Cilantro Chutney

Ingredients

(For Oothappam)
Rava (Sooji) 2 cups
Rice flour 1 cup
Urad flour (Ready made flour is fine) 1/2 cup
Green Chillies 5 (finely chopped)
Onions 1 (big, cut into very small pieces)
Cilantro 1 small cup
Curd (preferably sour) 3 cups
Salt to taste

(For Cilantro Chutney)
Cilantro, chopped 2 cups
Channa dal 2 tsp.
Red Chillies (or green, but former is better) 5
Salt to taste

Method:

(for chutney)

  1. Fry the channa dal and red chillies in two tsp. of oil.
  2. Chop the cilantro, add the tamarind and roasted channa dal, salt to taste and grind fine.

(For Oothappam)

  1. Make a smooth flowing batter of Rava, Rice and Urad flour, by adding Curd alone. Use more curd to dilute the consistency.
  2. Add to it the chopped chillies, onions.
  3. Add salt as required.
  4. Spread the not too thin batter on a heated tawa and add oil on the sides. Roll it over after sometime, add some more oil. Once the Oothappam is cooked to the desired level, serve it away with the hot cilantro chutney!

Making time: 15-30 min
Makes 10 Oothappam
Shelf life: Batter can be stored in refrigerator for as much as 2 days time!
