Hot and Sour Soup

Ingredients

1. Vegetable Broth.(You can also make this at home by boiling vegetables) (1 Can)
2. Fresh vegetable (total of 2 cups (qt can vary))
Note: (cabbage,green Onion(scaley onion), cauliflower,borocali,carrot, green chili)
3. spices: black pepper, salt.
4. ketchups: soya, tomato,green chili(optional).
Note: add the amount of ketchups according to your taste.
5.vinger(optional)
6. Corn starch (2spoon)
7. Oil (1tbsp)

Method:

  1. Wash all the vegetables and grate them with the grater.
  2. Take a sauce pan. Put the pan on the stove and add oil in it. Warm the oil until fumes.
  3. Now add all the grated vegetables and stir for half minute.(note: vegetable should be crisp)
  4. Add the can of vegetable broth.
  5. Now fill the can with the water and add 2tsp of corn starch in it and add this to the pan.
  6. Now add salt, black pepper, vinger and all the ketchups in the pan.

Enjoy the soup!

Note: You can also serve soup with Garlic bread.
