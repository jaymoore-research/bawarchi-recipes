Stuffed Buns

Ingredients

5-6 Buns (not the sweet ones)
1 small Cucumber chopped
1 Onion chopped,
3/4th cup shredded Cabbage,
5-6 boiled Potatoes,
1 cup White Sauce,
Tomato & Chilli Sauce.

Method :

  1. Scoop the buns. Apply butter. Place potato slice.
  2. Spread 1 tablespoon white sauce. Make a layer of cabbage and cucumber. Sprinkle salt & pepper. Cover with white sauce & grated cheese.
  3. Bake in pre-heated oven at 100 degree for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Serve with both sauces.

White Sauce:

Heat 1 tablespoon butter. Fry 1 tablespoon maida. Fry till light brown. Add 1 cup milk. Keep stirring constantly till thick. Add salt & pepper to taste.

Tip: goes well with Wafers/ French Fries.
