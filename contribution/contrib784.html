Pudina Poories

Ingredients

Wheat flour - 2 cups
Fresh pudina (Mint) - 1 small bunch
Chilli powder - 1 - 2 tsps
Salt - as per taste
Oil for the dough - 1 tbsp
Oil for frying

Method :

  1. Separate the pudina leaves from the stem and clean them.
  2. Grind the leaves to a fine paste adding water.
  3. Take the wheat flour add 1 tbsp of oil, chilli powder, salt and the pudina paste and knead to soft dough. You can add water if required.
  4. Set aside for 15-20 minutes. Make small balls out of the dough and roll them exactly like you would do for poories and deep fry them in oil.

Serve hot with pickle or tomato ketchup.
