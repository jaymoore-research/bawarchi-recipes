Ammini Kozhakkattai

Ingredients

1 cup Raw Rice
Half tsp Mustard seeds
Half tsp Urad dal
1/2 tsp Chilly Powder
3 tbsp grated Coconut
Hing (Asafoetida)
2tbsp Til / Refined Oil
Curry Leaves
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Soak rice in water for half an hour and grind to a smooth dough.
  2. Add water to make a loose consistency. Add salt and mix well.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and pour the dough and keep stirring in medium heat, till it becomes thick.
  4. Remove from oven and allow it to cool.
  5. Prepare small balls (10 mm) from this and steam cook in idli pan.
  6. Heat oil in a non stick pan. Add mustard seeds, urad dal chilli powder, hing and curry leaves
  7. Now add the balls and coconut. Stir well.
  8. Now remove from gas and serve.

Cooking Time : 30 min
Serves: 2
Shelf Life : Best fresh
