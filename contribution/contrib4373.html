Bhadam Kathli

Ingredients

Almonds - 1 cup (Level)
Sugar - 1/2 cup (with the same cup one measured the almonds)
Yellow Food Colour - little
Almond Essence - few drops

Method:

  1. Boil 4 cups of water in a vessel and remove from fire. Add the almonds and close the vessel. After 15 minutes drain the water.
  2. Wash the almonds in cold water and then remove outer skin.
  3. Wipe the skinned almonds with a paper towel or a clean cloth to remove excess moisture.
  4. Dry roast them in a hot pan under very reduced flame without discoloring itfor a few minutes and remove from fire but keep the almonds in the pan as they will become more crispy. If you have a microwave oven keep the almonds in a microwavable plate in high for 2 minutes. Check now and then to see it does not get overdried and looses the pale white colour.
  5. Powder nicely in a mixie after the roasted almonds gets cooled. Transfer it into a large basin and add a few drops of almond essence. Mix well.
  6. Add minimum amount of water and half a pinch of yellow food color to the sugar and prepare sugar syrup to a one string consistency. If the syrup gets very thick then the texture of the final product is completly lost.
  7. Pour the hot syrup slowly into the badam powder (not vice versa), mixing well to form a thick dough.
  8. Knead the mixture well while it is still warm.
  9. Keep the dough in between two greased (Ghee) plastic paper.
  10. Flatten the dough with hand or with a roller like chappathi (Thin). Slowly remove the top plastic paper.
  11. Cut into diamond shapes when it is still warm. If you want you can decorate it with silver paper.
