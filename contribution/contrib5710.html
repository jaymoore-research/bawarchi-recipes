Easy Potato Curry

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients

Ā 4 medium potatoes
I large onion
2 pods garlic
Small piece of ginger
3 green chillies
Oil as needed
Some mustard seeds
Curry leaves
1 red chilli dry
3 tomatoes
1 tsp chilli powder
2 tsp coriander powder
1 tsp cummin powder
1 tsp garam masala
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
Salt
Pinch of sugar

Method

Ā

  1. Boil potatoes till done and mash roughly and keep. Do not make it too smooth. Should be chunky.
  2. Heat oil and fry mustard, red chilli and curry leaves.
  3. Now add onion sliced long and garlic and ginger all chopped.
  4. When golden add tomatoes and chilies. Fry till pulpy.Ā
  5. Add all the powders and the potatoes.
  6. Fry a couple of minutes then add salt and enough water to cover the lot.
  7. Bring to boil and simmer till thick. Add sugar to taste. Lemon juice is optional.
  8. This curry can be done inĀ no time and goes well with chappathis and rice.
