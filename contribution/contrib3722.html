Peanut Chutney

Ingredients

Peanuts (Paleelu) 300 gms
Red Chillies 3-4 nos.(according to your taste)
Garlic Cloves 2 nos.
Oil 2 tspn
Cumin seeds 1/4 tspn
Mustard seeds 1/4 tspn
Coriander leaves 3-4
Turmeric powder 1/4 tspn
Parsily 1/2 cup
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. In a pan fry peanuts and after it cools remove skin. Add these peanuts into mixer alongwith red chillies and garlic cloves, and parsily grind it like paste. After it is done, add some salt according to your taste.
  2. Take a kadai, add oil, cumin seeds, mustard seed, turmeric and coriander leaves and pour into this chutney, that's it. It is quick and easy to prepare. Serve with Dosa/Idli.

Making time: 10 Minutes
