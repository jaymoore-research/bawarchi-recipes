Leftover Stir-fry Veg Rice Parathas

Ingredients

1 Cup Leftover Stir fry veg. Ārice
1 cup Wheat Flour
as per taste red chili powder, haldi powder, salt
3 tabelspoon oil for kneading
water to knead

MethodĀ

  1. Mix rice , flour, powder masala , salt, oil and water.
  2. Knead the dough for paratha.
  3. Make 6 balls out of it. Use dry plainflour to help.
  4. Place on a heavy iron or earthen griddle and roast till crisp.Ā
  5. Apply oil to both sides until it's little red in color.
  6. Serve hot with any curry or with yoghurt .
