Potato Podimas

Ingredients

4 to 5 potatos
3 green chillies
2 teaspoon oil
1/2 teaspoon turmeric
10 gms ginger
1/2 teaspoon musturd
1/2 teaspoon urud dhal
2 teaspoon lemon juice
salt to taste
coriander leaves for garnishing

Method:

  1. Boil potatos till it becomes soft and peel the skin off.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add musturd and urud dhal and wait till the musturd splatters
  3. Add greenchillies and ginger and lower the potatos and add turmeric and salt
  4. Gently turn the potatos now and then for 3 min.
  5. Now turn off the stove and add the lemon juice and garnish with coriander leaves.

This is very easy to make with very little ingredients.This goes very well with rice and will be a very good side dish for chapatis and rotis too.. serves about 3 people.. Cooking time is 10 mins.
