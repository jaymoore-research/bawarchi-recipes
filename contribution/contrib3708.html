Vegetable Masala Vadai

Ingredients

Moong dal-1 cup
Channa dal-1/2 cup
Mixed Vegetable-1/2 cup (finely chopped)[Carrots,Beans,Beets, Capsicum, Cabbage, Peas]
Onions-1 medium (finely chopped)
Green Chillies-3-4 (finely chopped)(you can add more or less according to your taste)
Ginger paste (fresh)-1 tspn
Curry leaves-7-8no.
Coriander leaves-1 tbspn (roughly chopped)
Turmeric powder-1/2 tspn
Red chilli powder-1 tspn
Coriander powder-1 tspn
Salt
Oil- as to deep fry.

Method:

  1. Wash and soak the two dals for 4-5 hrs. Grind them coarsely. Add onions, chillies, vegetables, powder masala, salt & green leaves to the batter mix them.
  2. Heat oil in the kadai and make small round balls flatten with your fingers into vadai shape, deep fry them on medium flame until golden brown.
  3. Serve hot with Tomato-coriander chutney.

Tomato-Coriander Chutney:

Ingredients:

Red Tomatoes (fresh)-3-4 nos medium size (diced)
Coriander leaves (fresh)-1/2 bunch (washed throughly,roughly chopped)
Sesame seeds-1 tspn (toasted without oil)
Cummin seeds-1 tspn (toasted without oil)
Tamarind -1 small lemon size
Jaggery-1 small piece
Red chillies dry-2-3 nos (toasted without oil)
Oil-2 tspn
Mustard seeds- 1tspn
Urad dal-1 tspn
Salt-as per taste

Method:

  1. Heat oil in a pan add tomatoes fry for 3 min on medium flame until raw smell goes, add coriander leaves allow it to cool.
  2. Heat oil in a pan add urad dal after it turn light brown add mutard seeds once it splutters allow it cool. Grind sesame, Cummin, red chillies into a fine powder then add tomatoes-coriander, tamarind, jaggery, salt grind the to a fine paste then add half of the urad dal& mustard mixture grind coarsely (urad should coarsely ground). Then adjust the seasoning add remaining tampered mixture, mix it and serve. (BEST FRESH)
