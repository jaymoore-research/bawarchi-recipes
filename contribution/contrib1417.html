Kobi Bhath

Ingredients

To be fried and ground into a paste

2 tsp Corriander seeds
1 tsp Jeera
1/2 tsp Shahijeera
4-5 Cloves
1" Cinnamon stick
5 Peppercorns
Piece of Nutmeg
3 Red chilles
1/2 cup Coconut (fried)

Other ingrediants

1/2 kg Cabbage (chopped)
1 Potato (diced)
1/4 cup Peas
2 Red Chilles
1 sprig of Curry leaves
2 cups Rice
Marble sized Jaggery
Marble sized Tamarind
1/2 tsp Mustard seeds
Salt to taste

Method :

  1. Grind the fried thing along with jagggery and tamarind into a fine paste.
  2. Heat oil in a kadai, add mustard, red chilles and curry leaves. When it splutters, add the diced potatoes and fry for 2 mins.
  3. Add cabbage and peas and fry for sometime.
  4. Finally add masala rice and salt. Fry for 2 mins. Add hot water and cook till done.
  5. Serve hot with onion raita.

Onion Raita

Ingredients:

2 medium sized Onions
2 green Chilles chopped
1 cup thick Curds
Salt

Method

Mix all the things and season with mustard and curry leaves.
