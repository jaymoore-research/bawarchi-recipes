Sweet & Spicy Chicken (Malaysian Dish)

Ingredients

5kg chicken (cut into small pieces)
2 tbsp shallot paste (shallot)
1 tsp garlic paste
1 tsp lemon grass paste
1 tbsp coriander paste
1 tsp ginger paste
1 tbsp chilli paste
2 tbsp tomato paste
2 tbsp sugar
250ml thick coconut milk
150ml water
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Mix chicken pieces and season it with some salt and turmeric powder. Let it marinade and leave it aside for 1 hour.
  2. Mix all the spices with coconut milk and water.
  3. Brown chicken with just a little oil, then add the spice mixture and leave to simmer to the consistency you want. Season with salt to taste and serve with fragrant rice.

Enjoy!!
