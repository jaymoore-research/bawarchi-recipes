Peanut Chutney

Ingredients

Tomato paste 1/2 cup measure
Dessicated coconut 2 tbsp (tablespoons)
Peanut butter 4 tbsp
Green chillies mashed 2 tbsp (may vary to taste)
Garlic mashed- 1 clove
Ginger- grated 1/4 tsp
Mustard seeds 2 tsp (teaspoons)
Turmeric 1/4 tsp
Onion paste 2 tbsp
Salt to taste
Oil for frying 2 tsp

Method:

  1. Heat the oil and put in the mustard seeds. After they sputter, add the garlic, ginger,onion paste and fry for few seconds.
  2. Add the coconut and green chillies paste, salt, turmeric and tomato paste. When the paste leaves the side of the pan, add the peanut butter and switch off the stove.

Chutney is ready to be served and will hold in the fridge for days.
