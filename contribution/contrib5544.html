Coconut Milk Rice

Ingredients for Cooking

Basmathi Rice - 1cupĀ (soaked for 5 min)
Onion - 1 Medium Size (sliced)
Garlic - 8 pearls
Coconut Milk - 2 cups (from fresh grated coconut)
Pattai - 1 piece
Lavangam
Oil - 2 tbspĀĀ
Ghee - 3tbsp
Salt (As per taste)
Fried Cashewnuts (optional)

Method:

  1. Heat the oil and ghee in a pressure cooker.
  2. Add pattai and lavangam.
  3. Add chopped onions and garlic pearls and fry it until it becomes golden colour.
  4. Add the coconut milk, reduce the flame to medium when the milk starts boiling add the soak rice.
  5. Pressure cook until 2 whistles
  6. Add fried cashewnuts.
  7. Serve hot with vegetable kurma or any kurma
