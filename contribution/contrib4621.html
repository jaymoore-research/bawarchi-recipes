Pacholey (Steamed Corn)

Ingredients

Maize Soft Corn 2 nos.
Green Corriander 25gms
Green Chilly 2 no.
Lemon Juice 1 no.
Salt According to taste
Oil 5 ml
Water (For Poaching) 1 litre

Method:

  1. Grind corn to rough paste.
  2. Chop green coriander and green chilly finely and add to the corn paste.
  3. Add lemon juice, salt and mix well. Grease two maize skin/peels.
  4. Put the mixture in the greased peels. Wrap in aluminium foil and seal with thread.
  5. Poach in boiling water till done. Take out from water.
  6. Unpeel and cut into pieces and serve hot with Desi Ghee, Anardana Chutney or Mint Curd.

Note: To make the dish rich serve with Desi Ghee otherwise with mint curd or anardana chutney.
