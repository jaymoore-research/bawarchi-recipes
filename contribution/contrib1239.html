Groundnut Chutney

Ingredients

1 cup ground nuts (peanuts)
6 red (dry) chillies
2 tea spoons urad daal (black gram)
1/2 tea spoon few methi/fenugreek (menthulu in Telugu)
1/2 tea spoon few mustard seeds
1 pinch of hing (asafotaeda)
1/2 table spoon canola or other vegetable oil
1 tea spoon tamarind paste
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Fry the ground nuts (not in oil; just dry fry. Be careful they don't get burned).
  2. Wait for them to cool down, and remove the skins by rubbing hands over the peanuts in a plate.
  3. Bring the cooking oil to a boil; add the urad daal, and fry for a 30 sec. The urad daal is not fully done yet; at this time, add the mirchi (chillies).
  4. Then, gradually add, methi, mustard and finally hing in that order, until they all are done. Put the peanuts, the fried mix, tamarind paste, and salt in a grinder, add a small amount of water, and grind to a paste.

This goes very well with idlis, and dosas.
