Potato Cake

Serves: 2-3 people.

Ingredients

4-5 big potatoes boiled and mashed,
1 grated carrot,
1/4th cup groundnuts powdered,
1 cup boiled corns or peas,
1/4th cup bread crumbs,
1 teaspoon chilli-ginger paste,
Salt to taste,
Sugar to taste,
Juice of 1/2 lemon or citric acid,
1/3rd cup sabudana soaked in water,
1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder,
2-3 tablespoon green chutney.

For the Chowk:
2 tablespoon oil,
1 tablespoon urad dal,
8-10 currypattas,
3-4 green chilliies.

Method:

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon oil.
  2. Add 1 cup boiled corns or peas, 1 teaspoon chilli-ginger paste, salt and sugar to taste, juice of 1/2 lemon or citric acid. Mix well and cook.
  3. Mix salt sabudana and groundnuts.
  4. Grease a 8 inch ring mould. Spread 1 grated carrot, sprinkle bread crumbs.
  5. Give chowk in the mashed potatoes.
  6. Spread half of the potato mixture.
  7. Make a layer of green chutney. Place corns/peas on it.
  8. Spread tomato sauce and cover with remaining potato mixture.
  9. Bake in pre-heated oven at 200 degree centigradefor 20-25 minutesor till golden brown. Cool.

Unmould it on a plate and serve.
Goes well with soft drinks.
