Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients

60 gm Sugar
2 Eggs, separated
300 ml Milk
10 gm Gelatin, dissolved in lukewarm water
5 ml Chocolate Essence
50 gm Chocolate, melted

For the topping:

4 Cherries
1 tbsp Jam
1 tbsp Brandy
1/2 tbsp Honey
2 tbsp Water
A few sprigs of Mint
1 Sweet Lime, cut into four wedges

Method:

  1. Beat the egg yolks with sugar. Add lukewarm milk and cook on low heat till the mixture coats the back of a spoon.
  2. Add the dissolved gelatin, melted chocolate and chocolate essence. Allow the mixture to cool, and then place in the refrigerator.
  3. Beat the egg whites till stiff, and stir into the semi-set mousse. Place in the refrigerator again and allow to set completely.
  4. Place the jam and water in a bowl and mix well, till the jam dissolves and forms a smooth sauce. Add the brandy and honey and whisk well together.
