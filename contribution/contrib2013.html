Angry Pasta - Italian Style

Ingredients

Pasta- 1 cup (Uncooked)
Pasta Sauce- (recommended ones without cheese)
Garlic- 2 cloves
Red chili powder- 1 tsp
Olive oil- 3 tbs
Basil- 1 tsp
Parsley- 1 tsp
Salt to taste
Cherry tomatoes- 6-7
F.Peas- 1/2 cup

Method:

  1. Cook pasta with little bit oil and salt. Drain it and keep it aside.
  2. Pout some olive oil in a pan and add the Gairlic, F.Peas., Fresh Tomatoes. Let it cook for a few min.
  3. Now add the sauce and let it boil. Now season it with Red chili powder, Basil and Parsley leaves.
  4. Once this is cooked, arrange the pasta in a plate and pour this sauce over the cooked paste and serve it hot.
