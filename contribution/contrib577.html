Spicy Dosa

Ingredients

Ordinary Rice 1 cup
Toor Dal 1/2 cup
coconut(grated) 1/2
Red chillies 6-8
Hing 1/2 tsp
Salt(as per taste)
Oil

Method:

  1. Soak the rice and dal in water for a couple of hours.
  2. Grind the mixture to a fine paste adding coconut, red chillies, hing, and salt.
  3. Add water if required(it should be of pouring consistency)
  4. Heat a tawa and smear a little oil over it.
  5. Pour a ladleful of the batter and spread it like a dosa.
  6. Pour a tsp of oil around the dosa.
  7. When cooked, turn the other side and cook till golden brown.
