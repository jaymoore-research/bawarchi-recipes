Beetroot Salad

Ingredients

Beetroots 3 nos
Potatoes 2 nos
Roasted Peanut powder 4 tbs
Chopped Green Chillies 2 nos
Oil, Mustard, Asafoetida (Hing), Turmeric, Cumin (all in small quantity)
Salt to taste
Chopped fresh Cilantra (coriander) & grated Coconut to garnish

Method:

  1. Peal & Chop Beetroot and Potatoes into cubes and cook them in pressure cooker for two whistles.
  2. Drain remaining water after cooking & add salt, as per taste, peanut powder.
  3. Heat oil in a small pan, add mustard, asafoetida, turmeric, cumin & chopped green chillies.
  4. Mix all well and garnish with fresh chopped coriander & grated coconut.
  5. Enjoy it with chapaties (pita bread ), tortillas, rice or just by itself!!
