Aloo Nirali

Ingredients

Potatoes - 500 gms
Onions - 4 medium sized
Garam Masala, Chilli Powder, Geera Powder, Salt to taste
Bread - 10 slices
Cooking Oil for deep frying
Finely chopped coriander leaves

Method

  1. Cook peel and mash the potatoes.
  2. Finely chop the onions and saute them till transparent.
  3. Mix the mashed potatoes, sauted onions and the spice powders finely.
  4. You can also add to this finely chopped coriander leaves to enhance taste and flavour.
  5. Make oval shaped balls of this and keep aside.
  6. Heat sufficient quantity of cooking oil.
  7. Now, soak one bread slice in water and hold it in between your palms and squeeze the water out of it.
  8. Place one of the oval potato balls on the slice of bread, and using your palms, gently see to it that the entire ball of potato is covered by the bread. If you have squeezed too much water out of the bread slice, it may be difficult to bind the bread and potato together. You can judge the amount of water to be retained so that perfect binding can take place.
  9. Similarly do so with the other bread slices of bread as well.
  10. Now, deep fry these rolls till golden brown.
  11. Serve hot with tomato ketchup or chilli sauce.
