Mixed Nuts Katli

Ingredients

1.Cashewnuts-25 gms
2.Almonds-25 gms
3.Pistachio-25 gms
4.Pecans-25 gms
5.Condensed Milk-1 can
6.Cornflour-1 tspn
7.Rose Essence-1 tspn

Method:

  1. Soak all nuts separately in warm water for 3-4 hrs. Drain and grind them together into a fine paste using very less water if required.
  2. Grease a marble or wooden surface with ghee(1tspn)
  3. Mix together the condensed milk and nuts paste. Cook in a pan on a medium flame. When mixture thickens, reduce the flame and keep stirring till the mixture leaves the sides (make sure there are no lumps) of the pan. Sprinkle cornflour, stir well and remove from fire immediately.
  4. Mix in the rose water and quickly turn it onto the greased marble or wooden surface. Roll thinly with a greased roller, spread the silver sheet over if you like and cut it into diamond shaped KATLIS.
