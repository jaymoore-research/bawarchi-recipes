Pandan Chicken

Ingredients

200 gm Chicken pieces
1/2 cup grated Coconut
3 Red Chillies
8 Shallots
2 Lemon Grass
1 tbsp Oyster Sauce
1 tbsp Plum Sauce or Honey
1 Egg
60 gm Corn Flour
60 ml Light Soya Sauce
Salt and Sugar to taste
3 tbsp Yogurt
Pandan leaves
Cooking Oil for deep frying

Method:

  1. Dice the chicken into required size. Coarsely pound the red chillies, shallots and lemon grass.
  2. Mix in grated coconut (the white part only), yogurt, oyster sauce, beaten egg, corn flour, salt, sugar to taste.
  3. Marinate for 2 hours.
  4. Wrap one chicken piece at a time in pandan leaves and deep fry chicken until cooked.
