Mango Ice Cream

Ingredients(To serve 4)

4 ripe mangoes,
1 litre (4 1/2 cups) whole milk,
1 teaspoon lime juice,
4 to 5 tablespoons ground sugar or as desired,
115 ml (4 fluid oz) whipped cream.

Method:

  1. Peel, stone and cut mangoes into pieces and puree in a blender.
  2. Boil milk until it thickens to half its volume.
  3. Cool, mix in mango puree and sugar.
  4. Pour into the ice tray and cover with aluminum foil or waxed paper and freeze partially.
  5. Then take it out, beat well and mix in cream and 1 teaspoon lime juice.
  6. Freeze (covered) until set.

Enjoy Mango ice cream.
