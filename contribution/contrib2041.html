Tiranga Delight

Ingredients

Carrots - 2 medium sized
Babycorn - 4 cods
Potatoes - 1 large
Capsicum (Green pepper) - 1
Mustard - 1 tsp
Cumin seeds - 1 tsp
Turmeric powder - a pinch
Chilli powder - 2 tsp
Garam masala powder - 0.5 tsp
Coriander powder - 1 tsp
Salt - to taste
Powdered mint (optional) - 1 tsp
Cooking oil - 3 tbsp
Tomato sauce - 3 tbsp

Method:-

  1. Peel the carrots. Deseed the capsicum. Chop all the vegetables into small equal sized pieces.
  2. Heat oil in a frying pan. Add the mustard and cumin seeds and allow them to splutter.
  3. Add a pinch of turmeric powder. Add the chopped vegetables and fry.
  4. Add the spices and mix well.
  5. Sprinkle water now and then to ensure that the vegetables are properly cooked.
  6. After the vegetables are cooked, reduce the flame and add tomato sauce and stir for just a few minutes more.

This subzi is an ideal accompaniment for chappatis.

Cooking time: 10 mins
Serves: 4
