Ingredients

Toor dal:1 cup
Potato:1
Green peas :1/2 cup
Carrots:2
Drumsticks (optional):2
French beans:10
For masala:
Coriander seeds:1/2 cup
Jeera:2tsps
peppercorn:2tsps
Raw rice:2tsps
Coconut grated:1/2cup
Red chillies:3
Ghee:1tbps

Method:

  1. Roast toor dal till golden brown and boil it till it is well done.
  2. Cut potatoes and carrots into cubes. Cut drumsticks into 3" long pieces and cut beans into 1" long pieces.
  3. Wash the dal and vegetables and keep them separately.
  4. For masala add half the ghee in a pan and add coriander seeds, jeera, peppercorns, red chillies.
  5. Wash rice and add that also to the pan and fry them together till the rice and coriander turn golden brown.
  6. Again add the remaining ghee and fry the grated coconut till golden brown.
  7. Grind the masala together with the fried coconut into a smooth paste.
  8. Add the masala to the dal and add vegetables and boil them together with 3 cups of water in a pressure cooker till vegetables are done.
  9. Do not forget to add salt before boiling.
  10. Serve hot with rice or roti or if you prefer eat it just like that!
