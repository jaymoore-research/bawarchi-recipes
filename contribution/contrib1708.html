Potato Curry

Ingredients

3 medium potatoes, chopped into big pieces
1 big onion, chopped
1 cup fresh grated coconut
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp black pepper corns
1 tbsp coriander powder
1 tsp turmeric powder
1/2 tsp chilli powder
4 big flakes garlic
1 stalk curry leaves
2 tbsp ghee
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Heat 2 tsp of ghee in a pan, add garlic flakes and fry till slightly brown. Add cumin, pepper corns and fry for a few seconds. Add the coconut and fry for 5 mins. or till slightly golden. Then add the turmeric, coriander and chilli powders and fry for a few more seconds. Cool and grind to a fine paste adding enough water.
  2. Heat the rest of the ghee and saute the onions till translucent.
  3. Add the potatoes and the ground paste. Pour enough water to immerse the potatoes, add salt and curry leaves and mix well. Cover and cook till the potatoes are tender and gravy is medium thick.
  4. Serve hot with plain rice, chapattis or any pulaos.
