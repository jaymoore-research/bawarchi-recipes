Tomato-Pudina Soup

Ingredients

3 Cloves
1 small piece of Cinnamon.
3 small pieces of Garlic (peeled)
2 green Chillies
1 teaspoon finely cut Ginger.
1 bunch of Pudina (mint) leaves
6 Tomatoes.(sliced in half)
Salt to taste
2 tablespoons Oil

Method :

  1. Heat Oil in a pan
  2. Add the cloves and the cinnamon stick, chillies, ginger and garlic and fry till light brown
  3. Add the pudina leaves and fry till they are cooked (they change color)
  4. Add cut tomatoes and fry on low flame till cooked
  5. Cool the mixture and grind in a mixie and strain.
  6. You can now add water/milk to this mixture and Voila ! that's tomato pudina soup !

Note :

1. When you add milk, make sure that the mixture and the milk are both cool - otherwise the milk tends to break.
2.To give it that professional touch, you can add a litle butter and a corainder sprig into the hot soup just before serving the soup
