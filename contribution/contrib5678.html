Onion Coconut Chutney

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

INGREDIENTS

2 cups grated coconut
3/4Ācups water
8 dried chillies
3 tbsps oil
1 cm piece ginger
1 tsp mustard seeds
1/2Ātsp tamarind paste
1tsp urad dall
Enough salt
3 onions chopped coarsely

METHOD

  1. Grind grated coconut, dried chillies, ginger, tamarind paste, salt and chopped onions in a blender with water until smooth.
  2. Heat oil , add mustard seeds, urad dall. fry till fragrant.add curry leaves,onions.
  3. When onions turn golden brown in color add in the blended mixture. when chutney comes to boiling pointtransfer it in to a bowl
  4. This chutney tastes good with idly or dosa.
