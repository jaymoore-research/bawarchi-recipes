Grilled Veggie Sandwiches

Ingredients

1/4 cup unsalted butter
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
12 slices white bread
1 (8 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese (we like mozzrella cheese, you can use any type of cheese)
1 teaspoon dried oregano

For the Filling

2 small Potatoes (boiled and mashed)
1 Onion (finely chopped)
1 Green Chilli (finely chopped)
1 tablespoon grated Ginger (or ginger paste)
2 tablespoons coriander/cilantro (finely chopped)
1 teaspoon cumin seedsv 2 tablespoons oil
salt (to taste)
Red Chilli powder (to taste. Remember: Green chillies are also there. So add red chilli powder accordingly)

For making the filling The filling is optional. If very urgent (no time to make the filling!!!), the sandwiches can be served simply as "Grilled Cheese Sandwiches". That also tastes very good.

  1. In a Frying Pan / Kadai add the oil. Place it on a stove and heat it.
  2. To this add the cumin seeds.
  3. When the cumin seeds start splattering, add the chopped green chillies, onion, grated ginger/or ginger paste. Stir. Let it fry.
  4. When the onion turns golden brown, add the boiled and mashed potatoes to it, and stir. Mix everything together. Let it fry for some 5-10 minutes. Garnish with chopped cilantro/coriander.
  5. Now, switch off the stove. Remove the contents from the pan/or kadai into a bowl/or container and let it cool for sometime.

The Filling is Ready !!!

For making the Sandwiches

  1. Preheat the oven's broiler.
  2. Place 6 slices of bread onto a baking sheet. Spread a small handful of the mozzarella cheese (or any other shredded cheese) over each slice. Add the filling (optional). Top with the remaining 6 slices of bread.
  3. Mix together the butter and garlic powder. Brush some over the top of the sandwiches (or spread with the back of a tablespoon). Sprinkle with the dried oregano.
  4. Place the baking sheet under the broiler for 2 to 3 minutes, or until golden brown.
  5. Remove the baking sheet from the broiler, flip the sandwiches, and brush the other side with butter-garlic powder mix, and sprinkle with oregano. Return to the broiler, and cook until golden brown (i.e. about 2-3 minutes).
  6. Remove the sandwiches from the broiler. Place them on the serving plate. Cut the sandwiches diagonally, and serve them hot with tomato sauce / ketchup/ or any chutney for dipping.

MAKES 6 SERVINGS.
