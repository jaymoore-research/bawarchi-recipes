Masala Poori

Ingredients

Flour-2 -3 Cups
Salt 1/2 tsp
Red Chilli Powder a pinch
Turmeric
Coriander and Cumin Powder 2 tsp
Oil or Butter 2 tbsp
Water 3/4 cup
Oil for frying

Method

  1. Mix the flour with salt, chilli, turmeric, coriander & cumin. Add oil/butter in it and by adding water knead into a medium soft dough.
  2. Leave it for an hour and knead again briefly and shape them into small balls.
  3. Place the balls on a flat surface and roll them
  4. Heat oil in a wok. Carefully slip them one after another into hot oil. Fry until it puffs up and is golden brown on both sides remove and drain it on a paper towels.
  5. Serve hot with a Masala Curry.
