Gujarati Khaman

Ingredients

a) 4 to 6 cup Channa Dal
b) 1 - 2 cup Yogurt
c) 2 Green Peppers
d) Small piece of Ginger
e) Salt for taste
f) 1 TSP Sugar (Optional)
g) Yeast or Eno
h) 2 cup Vegetable Oil
i) 1/2 cup Mustard seeds (Rai)
j) 1 bunch Dhania (Coriander)

Method:

  1. Keep channa dal in soacking water for 12 hours or overnight.
  2. In the morning grind dal with yogurt and water.
  3. Add to grinded dal, fresh shredded green pepper/ ginger, sugar and salt to taste.
  4. Keep the mixure for 6 - 8 hours at room temp.
  5. In thali, spray some oil and pour item d) with 1/4 TSP ENO
  6. Put 2 or thali in cooker cook upto 2 whitsles. After cooker cool down, get thalies out, cut khaman to the size you need.
  7. Make Vagar: 1 cup oil with mustard seeds and pour on khaman.
  8. Garnish with cut corianders.
