Rock Toast

Ingredients

Bread slices
one onion
2 tomatoes
1/4 cup chopped coriander
1 cup rava
2-3 green chilies
salt as per taste
sugar as per taste

Method:

  1. Take a bowl mix rava,cut all rest ingredients into small pieces and mix with rava with help of water.
  2. Cut the bread into four small square or you can keep in one slices if you like.
  3. Then take the square and dip into batter and shallow fry in the griddle,like you do for paratha. Hope you like it
