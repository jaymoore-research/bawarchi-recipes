Jam Biscuits

Ingredients

50 gms Butter
50 gms Sugar
100 gms Maida

Method:

  1. Cream Butter and sugar together (i,e mix together in one direction only)
  2. Add in the maida.
  3. Mix in 1/2 tsp strawberry essence.
  4. Bind it well into a dough.
  5. Make a chapati like and cut into any shape with the help of a biscuit cutter.
  6. Mash mixed fruit jam throughly in a bowl .
  7. Put mashed jam in the middle of the biscuit & then to the whole biscuit ie spread it.
  8. Cut biscuits & then cut in the centre (a small circle) and place this on the top of the jam applied biscuit.
  9. Fill in some jam in the centre.
  10. Bake these for 20mins at 160oC sprinkle sugar on the jam in the centre after they are baked.
