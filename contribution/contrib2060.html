Masala Vadai

Ingredients

1 cup Urad Dal
1 cup Bengal Gram Dal
7-8 Red Chillies
3-4 Onions
5-6 Green Chillies
Ginger, Coriander

Method

  1. Soak the dals and Red chillies for 1 hour.
  2. Drain all the water. Grind them to granules, don't grind them to smooth paste.
  3. Finely chop the rest of the ingredients - onions, green chillies, ginger and coriander.
  4. Mix all these with the ground dals, add salt to taste.
  5. Make Vadas, (for convenience grease palm with oil or butter). Deep fry Vadas in hot oil.

Makes 10-15 Vadas. Serve with Chutney or Tomato Ketchup.
