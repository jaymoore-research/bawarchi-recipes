Palak Rice

Ingredients

Palak - 1 Bunch
Rice - 1 1/2 cups
Onion - 1 nos.
Ginger-Garlic-Cilantro-Chillies paste - 1 tsp
Coriander powder - 1/2 tsp
Cumin powder - 1/4 tsp
Garam masala powder - 1/2 tsp
Asafoetida - 1/4 tsp
Oil - 1 tsp
Salt to taste
Cheese (optional)
Butter (optional)

Method:

  1. Soak rice for 30 mins. Cook and keep aside.
  2. Clean, wash the Spinach and boil it for 5 min. Strain and puree it.
  3. Heat oil in a pan. Add asafoetida and chopped onion. Saute till the onion turns light brown. Add the ginger-garlic... paste. Saute for 2 min. Add the powdered masalas and stir for another 5 min on medium flame.
  4. Add the palak paste and some water if required. When it boils add the cooked rice and salt and mix well.
  5. Top it with butter and grated cheese (optional) and keep on dum for 10-15 minutes or in preheated oven at 325o F for 10-15 min.
  6. Serve with Onion-Cucumber-Tomato Raita and Papad
