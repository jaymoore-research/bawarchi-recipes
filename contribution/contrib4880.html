Ingredients

4 litres full cream milk
200 grams rice
100 ml ghee
90 grams kismis
25 grams phool makahana
50 grams almond flakes
300 grams apples grated
700 grams palm sugar
25 grams cardamom
25 grams cinnamon
4 lafs gold leaves
25 ml rose water
2 grams saffron

Method

  1. Heat ghee in a hevy bottom pan and shallow fry makahana and nuts
  2. Drain the ghee of the nuts
  3. Heat ghee again and add in rice bhuno and then add in milk and cinnamon
  4. Lower heat and let rice cook in milk. Keep stirring regularly
  5. When rice is 90% cooked add in cardamom powder and cinnamon powder and grated palm sugar, add in saffron and cook further.
  6. Remove half the cooked kheer and blend roughly.
  7. Mix back into the balance half
  8. Add in dry fruits, cool the kheer.
  9. Add in the grated apples and rose syrup.
  10. Chill it for 6 hrs, garnish with dry fruits and gold leaves.
