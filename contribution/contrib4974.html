Chicken And Tomato Melts

Ingredients

8 slices buttered wholegrain bread toast
8 slices chicken fillet cooked
8 slices large tomatoes
125 gm grated cheese
2tbs Worcestershire Sauce

Serving : Line up a tray with either Chinese Cabbage leaves or a long banana leaf.

Garnishing : Can be done with a few well chopped paisley leaves.

Method:

  1. Arrange toast in a well greased shallow baking tray.
  2. Place a slice of chicken on each toast and top it with a slice of tomato.
  3. Salt and pepper could be added according to your taste buds.
  4. Mix cheese with the sauce and spread over the toasts thickly covering a good layer.
  5. Grill it well until the cheese has melted evenly.

Serve it hot garnished with herbs or salad greens for a nourishing snack.
