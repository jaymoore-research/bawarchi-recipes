Burffi

Ingredients

1. Ricotta cheese 16 oz (1 pack)
2. Dry milk powder 16 oz
3. Sugar 1 cup
4. Ghee 1 tbspoon
5. chopped pistachios 2 tbspoon
6. 1 greased thali

Method

  1. Heat the ghee in a kadai on medium heat.
  2. Add the ricotta cheese and keep stirring.
  3. Add the milk powder (use ricotta cheese empty box for measure dry milk powder).
  4. When mixture starts to get thicken add sugar and stir until mixture start separating from kadai.
  5. When done pour it in a greased thali.
  6. Sprinkle pistachios on mixture, let it cool.
  7. Cut it into small pieces, and taste it.
