Chicken Dish

For Marination:

Chicken: 1 kg
Chilly powder: 2 teaspoon
Soya sauce: 1 tablespoon
Salt to taste

Ingredients:

Onions-2
Ginger-2" piece
Garlic-5 flakes
Chilly powder-1 teaspoon
Soya Sauce-1 teaspoon
Tomato Sauce-2 tablespoon
Chilly Sauce-3 teaspoon
Oyster Sauce-1 teaspoon
Cornflour-for thickening
Oil-for frying

Method:

  1. Marinate chicken for 2 hours
  2. Fry the chicken in oil and reserve.
  3. In the same oil saute onions, ginger and garlic. When light brown add all the sauces and salt.
  4. Add the fried chicken and cover cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Add cornflour in a little water and thicken the gravy.
