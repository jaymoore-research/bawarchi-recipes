Vangi Bhath

Ingredients

Basmati or any normal rice - 2 cups cooked and allowed to cool in a large vessel 1hr before u mix or refrigerate it for 10 min .Make sure each grain is seperate.

Eggplants - 3
Capsicum - 1
Green peas - 1/2 cup or as required
Curry leaves - 1 Strand
Vegetable oil - 4 Tbsp
Chana dal - 1/2 Tsp
Urad dal - 1/2 Tsp
Mustard - 1/2 Tsp
Jeera - 1/2 Tsp
A pinch of Turmeric
Tamarind juice - 3 Tbsp (u could use lemon juice instead)
Salt accordingly
A pinch of Sugar

To make vangibhath powder :
Chana dal - 3 Tbsp
Urad dal - 1 Tbsp
Coriander seeds - 3/4 Tbsp
Red chillies - 6 or 7 depending on the spicyness u prefer
A pinch of Asafoetida
Dry coconut grated - 3 Tbsp
Except coconut, roast all the other ingredients in a kadai and make a powder of them along with the coconut.

Method :

  1. Heat oil in a kadai. Add mustard seeds and jeera seeds and allow to splutter. Add chana dal and urad dal ..allow till they turn golden brown.
  2. Add curry leaves and a pinch of turmeric and add both the chopped eggplants and the capsicum vegetables ... Allow them in oil for a minute or two and then add salt accordingly.
  3. You could add a pinch of sugar if u want to. When the veggies are half done, add the tamarind juice or paste and allow the veggies to be done completely.
  4. When all the moisture of the tamarind juice is done,add the vangibhath powder and mix properly. Add the green peas now.
  5. Add the Rice now and do the mixing properly. If u prepare it ahead of schedule, maybe an hour or more than that, heat a little oil in a pan and pour it on the mixed rice and mix it once again.
