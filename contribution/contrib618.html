Cucumber Bhakri

Ingredients

1 cucumber[med.size]
1 1/2 cup rice flour
1/2 cup whole wheat flour
6 green chillies[chopped finely]
3 tbsp grated coconut
4 tsp sugar
1" ginger piece [chopped finely]
1 sprig curry leaves[chopped]
salt to taste

Method:

  1. Peel the cucumber skin, remove the rind & grate it.
  2. To this, add the above said ingredients and mix well.
  3. Add water if reqd.
  4. It shouldn't be watery.
  5. Spread this on the tava & fry on both sides with ghee and enjoy hot hot with any hot drink.
