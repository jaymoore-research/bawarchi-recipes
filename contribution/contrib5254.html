Spaghatti Vegetable Soup

Ingredients

Spaghatti --- 100 gms
Tomatoes --- 4 (medium size)
Carrot --- 1
Potato --- 1
Grated cheese --- 1 tbsp
Bay leaf --- 1
Cornflour --- 1 teaspoon
Butter --- 1 tbsp
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Break the spaghatti into pieces & place in a container with water . In another container place tomatoes with one cup of water . Place both containers in a pressure cooker and cook for 10 mins.
  2. Extract puree of tomatoes . Drain excess water from spaghatti . chop vegetables into thin and small cubes.
  3. Heat butter in a saucepan add the vegetables and bay leaf . Fry until veggies are tender . Add tomato puree , boiled spaghatti,salt to taste and water, Allow to simmer on low fire ,
  4. Add grated cheese just before serving
