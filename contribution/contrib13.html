Almond     Burfi

Ingredients

1 cup almonds 1 cup sugar
Milk enough to blend
Saffron a pinch
Ghee - 2 tblsp
Silver warq for decoration

Method

1. Soak almonds in boiling water for 5 minutes.
2. Peel out the skin of the almonds.
3. Blend the almonds and sugar in very little milk (just enough to blend).
4. Cook the mixture in a non stick cooking pan stirring continously.
5. Add the ghee and saffron and cook till it forms a ball and comes clean.
6. Spread the mixture on a greased cookie sheet and put a cling wrap on top of it and
roll it will a rolling pin to spread.
7. Decorate with silver warq.
8. Cut to desired shapes.
