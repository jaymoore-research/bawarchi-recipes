Almond and Pistachio Dessert

Ingredients

1. 4 tbsp Unsalted Butter.
2. 1 cup ground Almonds.
3. 2/3 cup single (light) Cream.
4. 1 cup Sugar.
5. 8 Almonds, chopped.
6. 10 Pistachio Nuts, chopped.

Method:

  1. Place the butter in a medium-sized saucepan, preferably non-stick. Melt the butter, stirring well.
  2. Gradually add the ground almonds, cream & sugar to the melted butter, stirring to combine. Reduce the heat & stir the mixture constantly for 10-12 minutes, scraping the bottom of the pan.
  3. Increase the heat until the mixture turns a little darker in colour.
  4. Transfer the almond mixture to a shallow serving dish & smooth the top of it.
  5. Decorate the top of the dessert with chopped almonds & pistachios.
  6. Leave the dessert to set for about 1 hour, then cut into diamond shapes or with variety of shaped pastry cutters of your choice & serve cold.

Tip: This dessert can be made in advance & stored in an air-tight container in the refrigerator for several days. Enjoy.
