Mackeral in Coconut Gravy

Ingredients

1/2 kg Mackeral
1cup coconut grated
small piece of ginger sliced thinly
4 flakes of garlic sliced thinly
2 green chillies
a lot of curry leaves
1tbls red chillie powder
1/2 tbl turmeric
1/2 tbl cummin powder
juice of tamarind -lemon sized

For grinding
Grind the above 1 cup of coconut grated with 1/2tbls of turmeric nicely and fine.

Seasoning
Cut onions into small, mustard. and oil.

Method.

  1. Clean the fish and cut each fish to two pieces.
  2. Now add 3 cups of water to the coconut ground and add the ginger, garlic, green chillies, red chillie pd, cummin pd and tamarind juice to it and finally the curry leaves. Let this mixture boil nicely. Once boiled add the fish pieces and cover till the fish is done.
  3. When the fish is done, now is the seasoning.
  4. Heat oil and add mustard seeds, when it splutters add onions and brown it and add this mixture over the fish.

Excellent with hot basmati rice.
