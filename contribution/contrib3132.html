Beetroot Halwa

Ingredients

Beetroot 1
Sugar 1/2 cup
Ghee 2 tbs.
Cardamom Powder
Nutmeg Powder
Garnish
Pista (chopped)
Cashew nuts (chopped)

Method

  1. Peel the beetroot and grate it.
  2. Now in a kadai pour the ghee, and add the grated beetroot.
  3. Stir fry it in ghee, till the beetroot gets soft and cooked, and it absorbs the ghee well.
  4. Then add the sugar, keep stirring till the sugar dissolves.
  5. Add the cardamom powder and nutmeg powder.
  6. Then switch off the gas and place the beetroot halwa on a serving dish.
  7. Garnish with Chopped pista and cashew roasted in ghee and serve delicious beetroot halwa.

Its so colourful to look at, I am sure even your kids will love it !
