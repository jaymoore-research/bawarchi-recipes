Quick Potato

Ingredients

Potatoes (cubed) 4
Turmeric powder 1/2 tsp
Chilli powder as needed
Green Chillies (split) 4
Tomato (chopped finely) 1/2 of a medium sized
Cloves 3
Cinnamon 1/2 a stick
Garlic 2 cloves
Ginger 1/2 inch piece
Chicken Masala 2 tsp.
Onion (chopped) 1 medium sized
Coriander leaves handful

Method:

  1. Boil the cubed potatoes with salt, turmeric powder, chilli powder, and 1 tsp. of chicken masala till its soft but without breaking.
  2. Crush the cloves, cinnamon, garlic and ginger together and fry it in oil till it sputters.
  3. Fry the chopped onions along with the above till its golden brown and add coriander leaves and chillies.
  4. Put a little more chilli powder if needed, 1 tsp. of chicken masala and fry it with the chopped tomatoes. Fry till the oil separates.
  5. Add the potatoes to it and fry it till it turn golden brown. That's it! Just 30 minutes. Now it is ready to be served with rice or with Kerala Porotta.
