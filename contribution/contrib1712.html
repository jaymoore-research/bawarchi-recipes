Chicken Kadhi

Ingredients

Chicken (preferrably boneless) : 2 pds
Ginger-Garlic paste : 1 1/2 tbsp
Mustard seeds : 1 tbsp
Urad daal : 1 tbsp
Whole Chillies : 5
Curry Leaves : 1 stalk
Ginger : 1/2 tbsp
Onions : 1 big (or 2 small)
Crushed Chilli : 1/4 tbsp
Turmeric Pwd : 1/4 tbsp
Salt : as per taste
Shredded Coconut : 4 tbsp
Cashewnuts : 2 tbsp
Beaten Curd : 1 cup
Flour : 1/4 cup
Oil : 1/4 cup
Coriander for garnishing

Method:

  1. Make a paste of shredded coconut and cashews.
  2. Mix curds and flour to a smooth mix
  3. Wash chicken well. Add ginger garlic paste, salt and water enough to cover the chicken.
  4. Put it to cook and boil. Once well done, discard the broth (or use it for any other dish).
  5. Take out the chicken pieces and fry them lightly in oil. Keep aside.
  6. Put mustard seeds and urad daal in hot oil so that it splutters.
  7. Add whole chillies, curry leaves, cut ginger and stir.
  8. Add finely cut onions and fry till light brown.
  9. Add crushed chilli, turmeric pwd, coconut-cashew paste and saute till oil separates.
  10. Add curd- flour mix and 3/4 cup water to the sauteed mixture and stir till boil is reached. Check for salt, add some more if you feel so.
  11. Add fried chicken pieces and simmer for a few minutes.
  12. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve piping hot with rice, roti or parathas.

Enjoy the new try!
