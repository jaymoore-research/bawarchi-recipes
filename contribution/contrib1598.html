Aloo Beans

Ingredients

1. Potato 1
2. Beans (frozen green beans)
3. Chana Daal (2 tbsp)
4. Turmeric Powder (1/2 tsp)
5. Mustard Seeds (1 tsp)
6. Jeera (1/2 tsp)
7. Curry leaves
8. Red Chilli (2)
9. Onion (1) (cut into small pieces)
10. Chilli Powder 1/2 tsp
11. Cooking oil 2 tbsp

Method:

  1. Boil potato and chana daal
  2. Boil beans in microwave for 7 min
  3. Take 2 tbsp oil in a frying pan, add mustard seeds, jeera, curry leaves, red chilli.
  4. Add onions, fry till transperant
  5. Add beans, chana daal, potato, Chilli powder, turmeric powder and salt.
  6. Fry for 5 min and serve hot.
