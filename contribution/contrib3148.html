Capsicum Cabbage

Ingredients

Cabbage 1 lb
Bell Pepper (green, red) 1 small each
Dry Kastori Methi 1/2 Katori
Paneer grated 3/4 cup
Oil 1tbsp
Mustard seeds 1 tsp
Black Pepper 1/4 tsp
Salt to taste
Chopped Coriander leaves 1tsp

Method:

  1. Shred cabbage finely
  2. Chop bell pepper into fine pieces
  3. Heat oil in a pan and add splutter mustard seeds
  4. Now saute cabbage and capsicum for 2-3 mins and then add rest of the ingredients and cover for 1-2 mins
  5. Occasionally stir and remove from heat when capsicum becomes tender.
  6. Decorate with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with parathas.
