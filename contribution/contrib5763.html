Yellow Pumpkin Soup

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients

Yellow Pumpkin - 200 gms piece
Red chilli powder - 1/2 tsp
Salt to taste
Butter (optional)

Method

  1. Deseed and clean the pumpkin.
  2. Sprinkle some water on the pumpkin piece, microwave on high for 7Ā minutes. (Alternatively, you can also steam it in the pressure cooker for 1-2 whistles).
  3. After it cooks, place it in cold water for 2 minutes
  4. Scoop out the pulp, carefully avoiding the skin.
  5. Puree in a blender until smooth.
  6. Add salt, red chilli powder, water and microwave in a deep bowl for 4 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with a dollop of butter.

This soup serves 2 persons.
