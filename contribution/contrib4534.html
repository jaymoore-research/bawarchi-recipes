Roti Upma

Ingredients

Left Over Rotis.
Onion-1 small(for every 5 rotis),carrot

Method:

  1. Make (cut/tear) small pieces of rotis.
  2. Cut onions in to small pieces. Grate 1/4 th of carrot.
  3. In a pan put 1 tbsp. oil, add mustard seeds (1 tsp), 1/2 tsp urad dal, 1/2 tsp. jeera, when ulad dal turn red, add grated carrots, chopped onions stir for couple of minutes, then add 1 tsp. turmeric powder, red chilli powder or 2 chopped green chilli.
  4. Then add the roti pieces, salt to taste, sprinkle some water to soften the Roti, mix well, cover it with a lid for 10 seconds, then again mix well & turn off the gas.
  5. Sprinkle chopped cilantro (coriander leaves), serve hot.
