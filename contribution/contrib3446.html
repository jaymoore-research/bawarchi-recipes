Methi Pulao

Ingredients

1 tbp spoon kasuri methi (dry methi)
1 tsp jeera
A few Green Chillies according to taste
2 cups of rices
Red chilli powder to taste
Whole Garam Masalas for seasoning
Bay leaves 1 no, turmeric powder 1/4 tsp, garam
masala powder 1/2 teaspoon
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. In a cooker put a little oil and add bay leaves, jeera and the whole garam masalas.
  2. When it is slightly brown add red chilli, salt and turmeric powder then add kasuri methi and add the rice.
  3. Cook as you cook normal rice.
  4. Serve with any of your favourite Raitas.
