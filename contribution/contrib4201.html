Kalan

Ingredients

Raw Banana 100 gms
Yam 100 gms
Green Chilli 100 gms
Turmeric Powder 1/4 tsp
Pepper Powder 10 gms
Salt To taste
Ghee 1 tsp
Yogurt 1 litre
Grated Coconut Half of a coconut
Cumminseed 1/4 tsp
Fenugreek powder 1/2 tsp

For Seasoning:

Coconut or Refined Oil 1 tbp
Mustard Seeds 1 tsp
Dried Red Chilli 2
Curry Leaves

Method:

  1. Cut the raw banana and yam into small pieces after removing the skin.
  2. Split the end of the green chillies.
  3. Add splited chillies, turmeric powder, pepper powder, salt into the pieces of banana and yam and pour enough water and cook nicely. Cook till the water absorbs completely. Add ghee and stir nicely. After stirring add the lump free yogurt in it.
  4. Grind the grated coconut and the cumminseed nicely. Let the yogurt boil for a while and when it become thick add the grated coconut into it and take off from fire.
  5. Season with all the ingredients above.
  6. Atlast add the fenugreek powder and stir well.
