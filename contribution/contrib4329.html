Sweet Dal (Hayagreeva)

Ingredients

Bengal Gram 1 cup
Water 1 cup
Jaggery (powdered) 1 cup
Poppy seeds 2 tea spoons
Dry Coconut (grated) 1/4 cup
Almonds, Cashews, Raisins For garnishing
Ghee 5 tea spoons

Method:

  1. Cook bengalgram by adding water in pressure cooker. After removing it from the cooker cook it directly for 5 more minutes.
  2. Fry the poppy seeds for 2 minutes. Melt jaggery in little water & let it boil. To this Add poppy seeds, Dry coconut, Ghee & let everything boil.
  3. Now add cooked bengal gram & keep it on low flame for 5 mins.
  4. Finally garnish with fried cashewnuts, raisins & almonds.
