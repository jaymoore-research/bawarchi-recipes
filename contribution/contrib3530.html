Milk Kozhakattai

Ingredients

Rice Flour - 1 cup
Jaggery - 2 1/2 cups
Coconut - 3 cups

Make Coconut Milk:

Grind coconut with little water and cardamom. Use a strainer to get milk out of the ground coconut. Keep that milk aside (Coconut milk A).

Grind the coconut again with little water and take milk out of it. Keep that aside (Coconut milk B).

Make Rice Pellets:

Mix 1 cup jaggery with 1 tumbler of water and boil it with 1 tsp ghee. Add rice flour and stir it till it becomes thick enough to make pellets (small balls) out of it.

Method:

  1. Make pellets out of the flour and heat it along with the coconut milk obtained the second time (Coconut milk B).
  2. Add the remaining 1 1/2 cups of jaggery into it. Simmer until it melts. Add the coconut milk obtained first time (Coconut milk A). When the milk gets thicker, remove it from the stove and add cashews and almonds to the milk kozhakattai.

Milk Kozhakattai will be ready to serve hot! Hope you like this recipe and share it with everyone.
