Passerette

Ingredients

You can either grind the ingredients or if the flours are available then mix them in a vesssel and keep for about half hour and can make dosa. You wont even need a non-stick:

Mong dal flour : three measures:
Rice flour : one measure
Jeera powder: one teaspoon
Pepper powder one spoon (as per taste, you can increase)
Hing powder two pinches
Roasted white Til one spoon
Ginger Paste one spoon
Salt to taste

Method :

  1. If you are grinding the moong dal and rice, keep them soaked for about half hour and then grind.
  2. For taste you may add two, three pieces of tomato and coriander leaves while you are grinding.

This goes with Coconut Chatney or Onion Chatney

Fry Chana dal, Urud dal, Jeera seeds, Hing, and Red Chilli with a little oil and when the flames are removed add Coconut Powder or grated Coconuts and add Salt to taste and grind them. Add roasted sarasu seeds for taste.
