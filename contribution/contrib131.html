Dabeli (Double Roti) Kutch recipe

Ingredients

Aloo 1kg
Onions 4 Medium Sized
Double roti masala or garam masala can be used
Peanuts 1 cup
Salt 2 tspn
Red chilly powder 2 tspn
Emlee and gur chutney 4 tblspn
Lasoon chutney 2 tblspn

Method

  1. Boil the aloos and after pealing mash them well and keep aside.
  2. In a dekchi heat 1/2 cup oil for frying the peanuts, deep fry and add salt about 1 tspn, 1tspn sugar, red chilly powder 1/2 tspn.
  3. In the remaining oil add lason chutney, allow to sizzle,add garam masala, redchilly powder, emlee chutney, to this add the mashed aloos.
  4. Serve hot as filling in bun with fried peanuts & chopped onions.
