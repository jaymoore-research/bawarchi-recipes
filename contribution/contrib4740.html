Vegetable Halwa

Ingredients

Potato : 1/4 kg.
Carrot : 1/4 kg
Sugar : 1/2 kg
Ghee : 50 gms
Cardamom : 4 to 5 nos.

Method:

  1. Boil the potato & carrot and make it into a past in the mixie.
  2. Mix this paste with 1/2 kg sugar zeera, keep in the flame till it becomes thick.
  3. Add cardamom for flavour.
