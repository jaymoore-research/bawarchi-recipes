Kesar Bhath

Ingredients

1 Cup raw rice (Basmati)
1 Cup sugar
1/4 Cup ghee
10-12 Cashewnuts (Broken)
1 tsp Raisins 1/2 tsp nutmeg pd
1 tsp cardamom pd
1 tbsp strands of saffron
1/2 tsp edible yellow food colour (optional)

Method:

  1. Wash rice and cook it till just done.
  2. It should not be well cooked. Add 1/4 cup water to sugar and heat till one string syrup is formed.
  3. Add the rice and cardamom pd, saffron strands and yellow colour.
  4. Mix well.
  5. Simultaneously in another kadai, heat ghee add cloves, cinnamon sticks and cashew bits.
  6. When cashew bits turn light brown, add raisins and nutmeg pd.
  7. Pour immediately over the rice and mix well.
  8. Turn over constantly till ghee appears on the sides of the vessel.
  9. Remove from flame. Serve hot.
