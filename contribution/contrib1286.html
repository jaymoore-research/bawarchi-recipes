Corn Vada

Ingredients

Corn Kernels (3 cups)
Besan or Gram flour (3/4 cup)
Coconut powder (1 cup)
Green chillies (no. to your taste)
Jeera (1 tps.)
Salt to taste

Method :

  1. Coarsely grate the corn kernel in the mixer.
  2. Finely chopped the chillies.
  3. Mix every ingredient together and deep fry in medium hot oil untill brown.
  4. Serve hot with Imli chutni. It taste hot & sweet and crunchy.
