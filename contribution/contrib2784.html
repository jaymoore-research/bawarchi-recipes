Vegetable Omlette

Ingredients

Besan Flour 1/2 cup
Milk 1/4 cup
Onions 1 finely chopped
Green Chillies 1 finely chopped
Tomatoes 1 finely chopped
Ginger-Garlic paste 1/2 tsp
Coriander leaves 1 tsp finely chopped
Turmeric powder 1/4 tsp
Red chilli powder 1/2 tsp
Garam Masala powder 1/2 tsp
Cumin powder 1/ 2 tsp
Salt to taste
Oil 2 tsp

Method:

  1. Heat oil in a kadai. Saute ginger-garlic & chillies for 1 minute. Then add the Onions & saute it until onions become transparent and soft. Then add the tomatoes & cook until soft. Add turmeric powder, red chilli powder, garam masala & cumin powder.
  2. Check the salt & keep the mixture aside once cooked.
  3. In a bowl, Beat Besan & Milk until soft & Fluffy. Add a pinch of salt.
  4. Heat a Pan. Add a little oil & let the pan become hot. Reduce the flame & then pour the mixture of Besan & milk into the pan.
  5. Add the above prepared mixture of onions & tomatoes on top. Granish with coriander leaves & then allow it to cook on low flame.
  6. Once the down portion is done flip it over & cook the other part.
  7. Serve Hot with toasted Bread & Butter. It tastes good.
