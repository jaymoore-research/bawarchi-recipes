Coconut Burfi

Ingredients

Grated Coconut 2 cups.
Sugar 1 cup.
Ghee 1 tblsp

Method:

  1. Mix grated coconut, sugar and ghee in a bowl and microwave it for 8 minutes in 'high' (for 800 watts oven).
  2. Transfer it immediately to a greased tray.
  3. Cut pieces when it is lightly hot. You can add food colours also.
