Masala Vada

Ingredients

Soak:

Equal amount of Toor dal, and Chana Dal (1:1 ratio).
100 gms of Moong dal.
50 gms of dried peanuts.
50 gms of Badaam (optional).
50 gms of Urad dal.

Soak all dal for about 1 hour.

Grind :

Grind all the above soaked stuff, with little bit of water alongwith 3 or 4 red chillies, asafoetida, curry leaves and salt in a mixie or food processor. The dal should be coarse and don't make it into a smooth pulp. You should see bits of dal here and there like you find pebbles in the sand. Grind them with little water sprinkled now and then.

Mix :

Mix the ground stuff with finely chopped onions, green peas (50 gms) (optional), finely scraped ginger, fried cashews (optional).

Climax :

  1. Make small flat rounds on your palm with the above mixture and drop it in a hot oil slowly ....
  2. Fry them, as you turn them over a couple of times, adjusting the heat, and when it turns brownish yellow, take those vadas out and wrap them in a paper towel to remove excess oil ...
  3. Taste one vada as you make, and adjust the heat, frying time accordingly.
