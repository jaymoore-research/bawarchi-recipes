Kaju Katli

Ingredients

500 gms cashewnuts
500 gms sugar
1 tsp. coarsely powdered cardamoms seeds
1 tsp. essence of rose
Silver wrap

Method :

  1. Grind cashewnuts to a paste.
  2. Mix together cashewnuts, sugar and cardamoms.
  3. Keep over a slow fire and cook stirring frequently till the mixture turns thick and leaves the sides of the vessel. Put in a lightly greased thali.
  4. Level the surface, cover with silver wrap.
  5. Cut into pieces when cold.

Sabzi Handi

Ingredients :

100 gms French Beans, chopped
100 gms Carrots, chopped
100 gms Potatoes, peeled and chopped
100 gms Lotus Stems, chopped
100 gms white Radish, scraped and chopped
Salt to taste
200 gms Cashewnuts
50 gms Red Chilli powder
50 gms Garlic, chopped
8 tsps Cuminseeds
fresh Coriander leaves
Garam masala

Method :

  1. Boil the vegetables separately.
  2. Grind the cashewnuts to a paste.
  3. Saute the cuminseeds and garlic.
  4. Add the chilli powder and cook.
  5. Add the cashewnut paste and stir.
  6. Add the vegetables, salt, garam masala and cook for a few mintues. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
