Ragi Rotti

Ingredients

2 cups Ragi (red millet) Flour
Salt to taste
Water
Cummin seeds 1 tsp
Fennel Seeds 1 tsp
Peanut / Vegetable Oil 2 tsp
Ginger pieces cut finely
1 Green Chilli cut finely
1 Onion cut into small pieces (you can use green/red bell pepper instead of Onion)
Coriander leaves chopped finely

Method:

  1. Take 2 cups of Ragi (Red millet) flour in a vessel. Use water as required.
  2. Add all the ingredients to the flour.
  3. Knead into a soft dough add water as required. Use your palm to do the above or with a rolling pin.
  4. Take a ping pong sized ball sized dough. Pat it into a flat pancake/ chapati like on a thin sheet of greased plastic.
  5. Place a warm gridle (Bandley), apply water with hand on the upper side and slide the flat pancake on the gridle. Cover it with a lid.
  6. Invert it and roast it on the other side. When it is cooked remove from heat.
  7. Serve with Coconut chutney or Saagu or vegetable gravy.

Red millet is a good source of protein. Can be given to kids and adults.
