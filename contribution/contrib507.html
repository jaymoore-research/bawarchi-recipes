Chocolate Chip Muffins

Ingredients

75gms Bitter Chocolate
250gms Butter
250gms Sugar
5nos Eggs
250gms Flour
1/4tsp Baking powder

Method

  1. Grate the chocolate.
  2. Sieve flour and baking powder together. Mix the grated chocolate with the flour and keep aside.
  3. Cream together butter and sugar.
  4. Now add 1 egg at a time to the butter sugar mixture and keep beating till all eggs are used up.
  5. Now add the flour mixture to the above mixture and fold in with a wooden spoon or spatula. (Donot use a beater or a mixer here as the chocolate in the flour will melt due to excessive beating).
  6. Fill in this cake mixture in paper bags and pipe into greased muffin trays.
  7. Bake at 180C in a preheated oven for 15-20 minutes.
  8. To check if the muffin is cooked insert a toothpick through the centre of the muffin and if it comes out clean, the muffins are ready. Also the cake will give a very nice aroma and will look nice and brown on top.

Note:- In case of non availability of muffin tray it can baked in small katories or baked in a huge cake tray and cut into pieces and served.

Bitter Chocolate can be substituted by ordinary milk chocolate.
