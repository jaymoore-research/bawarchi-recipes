Spicy Pepper Chicken

Ingredients

1/2 kg Boneless chicken
3/4 spoon of Oil

For the Paste
3/4 cup Grated coconut
2 spoon Jeera powder
4/5 Red Chillie
6 Garlic Flakes
3 Spoon pepper powder (to suit your taste)
Few karipatha leaves

Method

  1. Make a paste of all the above said ingredients except the chicken and oil.
  2. Heat oil in a pan , add the paste to it and add salt to taste.
  3. Mix it well and add the Chicken pieces and one cup water , keep the pan covered at medium heat till the chicken is cooked.
  4. Serve hot with Rice
