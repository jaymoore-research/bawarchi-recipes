Spicy Indian Eggplant in Cilentro

Ingredients

4 Indian eggplants (Brinjals)
1 bunch of Cilentro (coriander)
7 to 8 hot green chillies
1 Tsp mustard seeds
3 Tbsp Vegetable oil
Salt to taste.
Pinch of Turmeric powder

Method:

  1. Grind cilentro and green chillies to a paste.
  2. Cut eggplants vertically.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds.
  4. Now as the mustard seeds pop add the eggplant pieces to it and fry for 2 mins.
  5. Add the ground paste to this.
  6. Add salt and turmeric powder and mix well.
  7. Cover the pan and cook it on a low flame until the brinjal is cooked and no water residue is left.

Serve it with hot rice.
Hope you enjoy the dish.
