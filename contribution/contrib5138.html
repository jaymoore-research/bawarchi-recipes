Capsicum Rice

Ingredients

rice 1 cup
capsicum 4 medium size chopped
onion 2 medium size chopped

To make powder
coriander 4 table spoon
channa dal 3 table spoon
urd dal 3 table spoon
jeera 1/2 teaspoon
pepper 1/2 teaspoon
coconut little
tamarind little
oil required

Fry all this till golden colour and make powder

Method:

  1. Soak the rice for 5 minutes and fry for few minutes
  2. Add salt and cook with pressure cooker or electric rice cooker.
  3. See the rice must not over cooked.
  4. Take a pan add little oil make the bagar with little musterd, urd dal , peanuts and little kadi patha.
  5. After that add onion, fry till light brown, little haldi powder and capsicum.
  6. Cook for 10 minutes with slow fire (do not add water to cook the vegetable )
  7. Ad salt and powder (it is given above) little to the vegetable and remaining powder to the rice.
  8. Mix well serve with papad, raitha like boondi, cucumber, onion and tomato.
