Gola Masala

Ingredients For Gola:
Besan - 1 cup
Turmeric powder - 1 tsp
oil/ghee - 1 tbsp
salt

garlic - 4 cloves
ginger - 1/2" piece
green chillies - 4
cilantro - 1/2 cup chopped

Method:

  1. Grind the last 4 ingredients and mix with besan, oil/ghee, turmeric and salt and make a dough.
  2. Roll it into a (1" diameter) long roll and steam cook for 5 mins.
  3. Take out, slice into 1/2" thick pieces.
  4. Deep fry these slices and set aside. (Steaming avoids absorption of too much oil.)

Ingredients For gravy:
Onions - 3, chopped
Tomatoes - 3, chopped
garam masala - 1 tsp
Optional chilli powder - 1/4 tsp
salt - to taste
oil - 2 tbsp
cilantro - to garnish

Method:

  1. Fry onions and tomatoes very well, add garam masala, (chilli powder, if you like it hot) salt and water.
  2. When it comes to a boil, add the golas, warm it through and remove from stove.
  3. Do not let it boil.
  4. Garnish with cilantro and serve with Chapati, puri or pulao.

Note: Fried Golas can also be served as a snack with coffee/tea.
