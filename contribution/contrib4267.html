Mirapakaya Bajjilu

Ingredients

Thick and long Green Chillies (about 10-15 cm) 5
Ajwain 3 tsp roasted and powdered
Besan 2oo gm
Salt to taste
Turmeric powder a pinch
Red Chilli powder/Ajwain 1 tsp
Soda-Bi-Carbonate 1/2 tsp
Curds 2tsp full
Onions chopped 2
Lemon Juice of one lemon
Coriander leaves chopped 2 tbsp
Tomato chopped 1
Chat Masala 1 tsp (optional)
Carrot or Beetroot grated 1 for garnishing
Rice Flour 50 gm

Method:

  1. Take Besan in a bowl. Add turmeric, salt, curds, soda and ajwain with it and mix well adding water. Make a thick batter. Keep it aside for 10 minutes.
  2. Now discard seeds from chillies making a slit lengthwise. Mix salt with ajwain powder and rub this powder into the slitted area of chillies. Dip these chillies in the batter and coat well.
  3. Slowly drop this dipped chillies in the oil like bhajias. Fry in medium flame till golden brown in colour. Take and keep aside.
  4. Now take a bowl and mix chopped onions, tomatoes, coriander leaves, salt, lemon juice and chat masala. Mix all this ingredients before you serve.
  5. Make a slit lengthwise in bajjis and stuff this masala carefully. Place them in a plate and Garnish with grated carrots or beetroots and sprinkle chatmasala again over them.
  6. Serve them hot with sauce and cucumber, onion and tomato salad. Its nice to eat in rainy day with tea.
