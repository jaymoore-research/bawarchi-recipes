Kaju Paneer

Ingredients

Paneer 1/2 lb. Deep fry after making cubes.
2 medium onions (finely chopped ),
2-3 tomatoes puree (depending on the gravy U need)
2 TBSP of ginger garlic paste (remember TBSP)
1 green chilli (grind with ginger-garlic paste above)
1 tsp kasuri methi
1 spoon poppy seeds (khuskhus)
15 whole kaju
1/2 to 1 tsp chilli powder
1/2 tsp garam masala
1/4 tsp turmeric powder
cilantro
1 elcha (U get it in Indian stores, Big brownish elaichi)
Oil

Method:

  1. Soak kaju & khuskhus for 1/2 hr. or more. Grind into a paste (add water if needed).
  2. Heat 1 tbsp of oil. Put slightly crushed elcha.
  3. Add onions & fry till slightly Brown
  4. Add tomato puree & ginger-garlic-green chilli paste. Fry nicely till oil comes up.
  5. Add Chilli powder, turmeric powder & fry.
  6. Add Kasuri methi & fry only for 10 SECONDS.
  7. Add ground kaju-khuskhus paste, salt & water (acc. to consistency needed).
  8. Let it boil nicely.
  9. Add fried paneer cubes & bring one more boil. Add garam masala.
  10. Garnish with cilantro.
