Muruku

Ingredients

1 k.g. maida
3 teaspoons salt
two spoons of til
and sufficient oil to fry.

Method:

  1. First of all take the maida and add salt and then put it in a dry vessel and put it in the pressure cooker and cook without weight and allow it for 15 to 20 minutes.
  2. After wards when it cools add the til seeds and sufficient water and prepare murukus and fry till little brown. It is very easy and very tasty. Thanks.
