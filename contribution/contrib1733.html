Gulabjamun

Ingredients

For the Balls

Nonfat dry Milk : 2 cups.
All Purpose Flour : 1/2 cup.
Heavy whipping Cream : according to the need to make the dough.
Baking Powder : 1/2 teaspoon.
Oil for frying.

For the Syrup

2 cups White Sugar
1 cup Water

Method :

  1. Mix all the dry ingredients.
  2. Make the dough with heavy whipping cream.
  3. Make medium size balls & deep fry them in oil.
  4. Boil the water & sugar till little thick.
  5. Soak the balls in the syrup.
Gulabjamuns are ready & enjoy them.
