Sundal

Ingredients

Channa 1 cup
Green Chillies 2 or 3
Red Chilly 1
Ginger 1 cm
Lime 1/2
Mustard seeds 1/2 tsp
Urad dhal 1/2 tsp
Turmeric powder 1/4 tsp
Salt 3/4 tsp
Grated Coconut 2 tbsp
Raw mango 1/4 cup (cut into small pieces)
Asafoteda
Curry leaves a little
Oil

Method

  1. Soak channa overnight in plenty of water. Next day wash well, add one cup of water & pressure cook for 8 minutes. After removing from the cooker, drain off excess water.
  2. Chop green chillies & ginger finely.
  3. Heat oil in a thick pan. Add mustard seeds and when it starts spluttering, add urad dhal, red chilly, asofoteda & curry leaves.
  4. Fry a bit. Add the cooked channa, salt, turmeric & mango pieces.
  5. Fry well till the mixture is dry. Add grated coconut & lime juice.
  6. Toss abit & reomove from fire.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves.
  8. Serve hot or cold as evening snack.
