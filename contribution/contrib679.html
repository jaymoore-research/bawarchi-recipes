Sevai Rasu

Ingredients

For the Sevai
1 Cup of rice flour
2 Cups of water
a pinch of Salt
1 tsp oil

For the Rasu
2 tbsp coconut milk (canned from the Market is good enough)
2 cups of milk (quantity can be changed as per desired)
1/4 cup jaggery powdered (Depending on the sweetness desired).
7-8 Ellaichi

Method :

Sevai

  • On a medium flame, in a container, Mix the Rice Flour and the Water. Once the mixture is hot keep stirring it till the rice flour cooks. the color changes from White to a a little pinkish and the paste which started out with now turns almost into a ball. It would take about 15-17 mins. Add the oil.

  • When the Mixture is still hot. Apply a little oil into your palm and then make big balls of the Mixture. Steam these in a cooker or container for about 10 mins. The test for that balls having cooked is that if you poke into it with a clean knife, the knife comes out clean.

  • Now , just like one makes chaklis , put these balls through the Chakli stainers. The Seive (sacha) to be of the ones used for thick sev.

The Vermicellis are ready.

For the Rasu just mix all the Ingredients in a mixer to a fine juice.
Chill the Rasu.
Pour the Rsasu over Sevai and its ready to eat!
Have a great time.
