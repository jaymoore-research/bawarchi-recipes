Indian Pizza (Rava)

Ingredients for Cooking

1 cup rava
1/2 cup curd
1 tsp baking powder
1 cup chopped vegetables (carrots, capsicum, beans, peas, cauliflower)
1 small onion, chopped
1cup grated cheese
salt to taste
oil for greasing

Ā Method:

  1. In a bowl mix rava , curd, salt & baking powder into a paste for 15 minutes
  2. In the mean time heat oil in a pan, fry onions, add raw vegetable & salt to taste
  3. Fry till vegetables are done.
  4. Take out & keep aside in a plate.
  5. Take a tava , greeze it with oil , & put 2 to 3 tbsp of the rava mix
  6. Flatten it with a spatula
  7. Add some cooked vegetable & top it with cheese, press it.
  8. Cover it with lid.
  9. Turn it upside dowm carefully, add some oil , fry it till crispy.

This is a nutritious quick breakfast & kids will enjoy it
U can also add sweet corns &unsalted groundnuts for taste.
