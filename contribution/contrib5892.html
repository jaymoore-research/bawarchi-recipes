Rawplantain Channa Usili

Ingredients

raw plantain - 1
onion
scrapped coconut - 3 tsp
channa dhal (kabuli channa) - 1/2 cup
curry leaves - few
mustard seeds - 1/2 tsp
urad dhal - 1/2 tsp
chilli powder - 1/2 tsp
turmeric - 1/2 tsp
salt - 1 tsp
oil - 4 tsp

Method

  1. Soak channa dhal overnight and pressure cook it.
  2. Drain and allow it to cool completely.
  3. Cut onion into small pieces finely.
  4. Cut raw plantain in small round slices and again cut it into four pieces each round slices.
  5. Heat a pan, pour oil and season it with mustard andĀ urad dhal.
  6. Put the onion and fry it till transparent.
  7. PutĀ red chilli powder, turmeric and salt.
  8. Then add the raw plantain and fry it till tender.
  9. Grind the cooled channa in a chopper .
  10. It will be a dry crumbled powder.
  11. Add it to the curry.
  12. Mix it well to blend.
  13. Now put the scrapped coconut and curry leaves.
  14. Continue to fry for 2 more min.
  15. Mix well and serve hot with rice and sambar.
