Musti Dosa

Ingredients

2 cups-raw rice
half cup-uradh dal
1 tbsp-methi seeds
1 cup-poha
half cup -grated coconut(fresh)
salt-as per taste

Method:

  1. Soak rice+ urad dal+methi seeds for 2hrs in water
  2. Grind the above with the grated coconut.When half done add washed(soaked) poha & grind to a fine thick batter.
  3. Add salt & mix well.Keep aside for 6-8hrs to ferment.
  4. Preheat a non-stick tawa & spread out a laddle full of batter into it. The dosa should be of medium thickness.
  5. Heat it on one side only and serve it hot with onion coconut chutney.
