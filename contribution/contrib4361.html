Crispy Karelas

Ingredients

1. Karelas -10 nos. medium
2. Onions-2 medium thickly sliced
3. Raw Mango-1/2 cup finely sliced
4. Amchoor Powder-1/2 tsp
5. Sugar -1/2 tsp
6. Salt -to taste
7. Chilli powder-1/2 tbsp
8. Oil for deep frying..
9. Fennel seeds-1/2 tsp
10. Jeera-1/2 tsp
11. Pickle Oil-optional

Method:

  1. Wash and peel karelas lightly and rub some salt and keep aside for a few hours.
  2. Heat oil in a wok and deep fry karelas till deep brown but not black.
  3. Spread on paper towel for it to soak all oil.
  4. Heat oil in a wok or if you have some mango pickle oil its better. Put about 1 tbsp of pickle oil or plain oil and add to that fennel seeds and jeera. Now add onions and saute on high flame stirring constantly. Add the raw mango and stir fry.
  5. Add the remaining spices and cook for about 2 min. Make sure the onions dont get mushy. They should remain crisp and half cooked.
  6. Take a serving bowl and spread the fried karelas in that and spread this mixture on top.
  7. Serve hot with Chapati and some Dal.
