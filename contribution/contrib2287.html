Val Whole Usli

Ingredients

1 Pound of Vall whole
Mustard seeds 1 tsp
Ginger 2 tsp
Jeera 1 tsp
Garlic 2 tsp
Grated Coconut 1 Cup
Green Chillies 5 to 6 (Depending on How spicy/ Hot you want)
Butter 4 tsp
Handful of Coriander Leaves
Onions 3
Lime Juice onefull size (or 3 to 5tsp)
Usli powder 3 to 4 tsp
2 cups of Water
Oil for seasoning

How to make Usli Powder?

Heat the saut'e Pot add 4tsp of jeera and 4 tsp of Dhania and Dry fry it for 5 mins then grind it to very fine dry powder. Don't add water.

Vall whole is called Avare kaalu in Kannada, it resembles black eyed small beans which is light yellowish in colour which is available in all Indian Stores.

Method:

  1. Soak Vall whole in water overnight say for 6 to 8 hrs.
  2. Dice the onions in medium size so do green chillies.
  3. Heat the oil in a kadai add mustard seeds, jeera, ginger garlic paste and diced onions and saute for 3 min.
  4. Add soaked Vall whole to the above mixture and add usli powder which is prepared ahead and grated coconut.
  5. Add limejuice salt to taste, coriander leaves chopped and add butter, saute for 5 to 6 min then at last add two cups of water stir the ingredients for 3 min then cover the lid for 20 min.

Can't wait to taste. Serve hot with Poori/Roti/Chapathi.
