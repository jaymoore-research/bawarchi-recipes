Chicken Green Masala

Ingredients

1 Kg Chicken
1 large Onion( sliced)
Handful of Coriander Leaves
1" Ginger
10 flakes of Garlic
8 Green Chillies
1/2 tsp Geera
1 tsp Pepper seeds
Pinch of Mustard seeds
1/2 tsp Poppy seeds
1/4 tsp Turmeric powder
6 Cloves
1 Cinnamon Stick
3 Tomatoes (chopped)
Grated fresh/dry Coconut (1/4 cup)
3 tsp of Ghee
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Clean chicken & cut into medium pieces ,keep aside.
  2. Grind together ginger, garlic, chillies, pepper, jeera, mustard, poppy, cloves, cinnamon, grated coconut, coriander leaves. Add turmeric powder & grind to fine paste with adding little water (if needed only)
  3. Heat ghee in a Kadai. Add 1 sliced onion and fry for sometime, then add the ground paste and fry nicely till it becomes slightly dark brown.
  4. Add tomatoes chopped and fry for 2 mins.
  5. Add chicken pieces, salt to taste and cover and cook till done. (add little amount of cleaned masala water if needed)
  6. Serve hot with ghee/normal rice.
