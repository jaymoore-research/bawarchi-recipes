Chane Ke Kebab

Ingredients

Kala chana 1 cup (soaked 6-7 hrs)
Chana Dal 1/4 cup (soaked)
Onions 2 ( medium sized and sliced)
Ginger A small Piece (chopped)
Garlic 8-10 flakes
Red whole chillies 2-3 (broken into pieces)
Whole garam masala (Bay leaf, cloves, cardamom (big and small dalchini, pepper)
coriander powder 2 1/2 tsps
Salt to taste
Lime juice (according to taste)
Green chillies 1-2 (chopped finely - optional)
Onions 1 ( Chopped finely)
coriander leaves A Bunch chopped finely
Oil

Method:

  1. In a pressure cooker, take oil and add sliced onions and till light pink.
  2. Add chopped ginger, garlic and whole garam masalas, dry whole red chillies. Fry a little more.
  3. Now add the Kala chana and chana dal.
  4. Add coriander powder and fry on medium heat till the oil separates out.
  5. Add salt and little water (just enough for cooking) and pressure cook for about 10 minutes. The chana should be boiled well.
  6. Now grind the chana mixture in a grinder to a coarse paste. In case the Chana mixture is too watery, you can evaporate the excess water till it dries up.
  7. In the ground paste add the chopped onion, lime juice, chopped coriander leaves and green chillies.
  8. Make round kebabas of the mixture and shallow fry on a non-stick pan.

Serve hot with green chutney. This tastes good with parathas too.
