Chilli Cucumber Bhartha

Ingredients

2 Pcs Medium Sized Cucumbers
4 Pcs Medium Tomatoes
4 Medium Sized Onion
2 Teaspoonful Garam Masal
9 Pcs Grren Chilli(Medium)
2 Teaspoonful Salt Or As Per Taste
3 Teaspoonful Cooking Oil
2 Teaspoonful Deghi Mirch

Method:

  1. Peel cucumbers and boil them in open container
  2. Duration of boil 10 minutes on high and 1o minutes on low
  3. Alternatively give one whistle in cooker
  4. Mash this cucumber after boiling and draining
  5. Cut onion,tomato and green chilli in fine pcs
  6. Put pan with cooking oil on heat
  7. Now first put onion on pan fry for 4 minutes
  8. Now put tomato and green chilli
  9. Then put salt,deghi mirch and garam masala fry for five minutes
  10. Now put mashed cucumber in this and keep frying for 8 minutes till the time recipe gives away oil meand all water is gone.
  11. Please ensure that cucumber when mashed don't have much water for tasty preparation
