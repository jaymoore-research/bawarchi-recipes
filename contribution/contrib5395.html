Keerai Kootu

Ingredients

Moong Daal -- 1/2 cup
Baby spinach (or normal spinach)--- 0.75 Oz (340 gm)
Cumin seeds (jeera) -- 3 teaspoons
Grated coconut -- 6 teaspoons
Pepper -- 1 teaspoon
Green chillis -- 4 (or less if u want it less spicy)

For Seasoning :
Red chillis -- 2 ,
Mustard -- 1 teaspoon ,
and cooking oil -- 1 teaspoon
and a teaspoon of salt

Method :

  1. Boil Moong Dal and spinach in the cooker and allow 2 whistles. (can be put in the same vessel)
  2. Grind the green chillis, pepper, coconut gratings, cumin seeds.
  3. In a wide mouthed vessel (kadai) pour 2 t-spoons of cooking oil and season with mustard and red chillis.
  4. Now add the cooked Dal-spinach mixture and put salt and the above paste.
  5. Let it cook for 5 mins....stirring in regular intervals.
  6. Now add Asafoetida.

Your Spinach kootu is ready !!!!

Enjoy !!!!
