Pineapple Pudding

Ingredients

Pineapple (chopped ) 2 cup
Milk 2 cup
Condensed Milk 1 tin
Chinagrass 50 gm
Sugar 2 tbsp

Method

  1. Boil the milk & condensed milk together.
  2. Soak the china grass in 2tbsp water. Then add this china grass to the boiling milk & stir it till the china grass dissolves. Boil the milk for 10mts.
  3. Take the chopped pineapple & mix sugar.
  4. Cook the pineapple for 10 min. or till the pineapple becomes soft. Cook it in slow fire. When the pineapple & the milk becomes cold, take a pudding dish & spread the pieapple first then slowly pour the milk in the dish.
  5. Keep the pudding in the fridge till the pudding sets.
  6. You can garnish the pudding with pineapple slices & cherries.
