Karanji

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients

For filling:
1 cup grated dry coconut
1 cup powdered sugar
1 pinch nutmeg powder
1 pinch dry ginger powder
4 ilaichi
2 tea spoon maida
2 tea spoon poppy seeds

For dough:
1 cup maida
1 tea spoon ghee
Salt to taste
Warm milk to prepare dough

Method

  1. Roast Dry coconut.
  2. Roast maida in ghee
  3. Roast poppy seeds
  4. Mix all the above ingredients & gind them.

For dough:

  1. Mix all the ingrediants & prepare dough(a little hard).

For karanji:

  1. Make small oval shaped puris from dough.
  2. Fill the stuffing inside.
  3. Apply some water or milk so that it will seal properly (don't apply too much of water as it will not seal properly).
  4. Deep fry it till it becomes brownish colour.
  5. Serve it hot or cold.
  6. It can stay for weeks.
