Spinach Gravy

Ingredients

1. Spinach - 1 1/2 cup
2. Toor dhal - 1/2 cup
3. Cumin seeds - 1 1/2 tsp.
4. Dry red chillies - 10 nos.
5. Grated Coconut - 3 tsps.
6. Garlic - 4 flakes
7. Tamarind - a lemon size
8. Tomato puree - 4 tsps.
9. salt to taste

Method:

  1. Soak toor dhal in for 10 minutes.
  2. Soak tamarind in water for 10 minutes.
  3. In the meantime, cook spinach in a minimum of boiled water so that you don't lose its vitamins. Don't overcook. 3/4 th cooked spinach is also fine.
  4. Add cumin seeds, red chillies & coconut with the toor dhal and grind it to a smooth paste.
  5. While the spinach is on fire, add the above ground paste, tamarind extract, lightly smashed garlic, tomato puree & salt. Bring to a boil.
  6. Heat oil, add mustard, gram dhal, curry leaves & pour it in the keerai kulambu.

Good for idli & dosa. You will surely like it. It's an easy & tasty recipe. Try it.
