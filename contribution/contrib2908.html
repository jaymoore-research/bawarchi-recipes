Coconut Kozhambu

Ingredients

Coconut grated - 1 cup
Tamarind - lemon size
Methi seeds - 1 teaspoon
Urad dal - 1 tsp
Red Chillies - 8 - 10
Salt to taste
Oil for baghar
Mustard seeds - 1 tsp.

Method

  1. Soak tamarind in 2 cups water for half an hour
  2. Roast methi seeds, urad dal and red chillies in a tsp of oil
  3. Finely grind the above roasted ingredients with coconut to form smooth paste.
  4. Squeeze tamarind, remove it, and boil tamarind water for 10 mins in medium flame.
  5. Add salt to the boiling tamarind water.
  6. Add ground masala and cook for 5 mins. Now coconut kozhambu is ready.
  7. Separately heat 1 tsp of oil and add mustard seeds. When they splutter add to kozhambu.
  8. Serve with Hot Rice and Papad.

Cabbage Poricha Kozhambu

Ingredients

Cabbage - 1 cup finely cut
Tuvar Dal - half a cup
Coconut - half a cup grated
Jeera - 1 tsp
Red Chillies - 6 - 8
Haldi - 1/2 tsp
Oil - 1 tsp
Mustard seeds- 1 tsp
Urad Dal - 1 tsp
Curry Leaves
Salt to taste

Method

  1. Cook tuvar dal separately in pressure cooker.
  2. Boil cabbage with 1 cup water, salt and haldi.
  3. Grind coconut, jeera, and red chillies to fine paste.
  4. Add cooked dal, and ground coconut mixture and curry leaves to cooked cabbage and cook for 10 mins on low flame.
  5. Heat 1 tsp of oil, add mustard seeds and urad dal. When urad dal browns add baghar to cabbage poricha kozhambhu.
  6. Serve hot with Rice and Vadams.
