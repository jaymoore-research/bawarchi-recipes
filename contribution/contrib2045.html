Tomato Dosa

Ingredients

1 Cup Rice
1 Cup ripened and diced Tomatoes
100 gm fresh Coconut
8-10 Red Chillies (for mild taste)
Pinch of Asafoetida (Hing)
Salt to taste
A few Coriander leaves

Method:

  1. Soak rice for two hours.
  2. Grind the rice to form a very fine paste.
  3. Grind rest of the ingredients with a little water and add to rice batter.
  4. Mix everything together and ferment for 6 to 8 hours.
  5. Chop coriander leaves and add to the batter.
  6. Heat the hotplate (tava). Spread very little oil.
  7. Spread a thin layer of batter (preferably start from outside and spread towards the center).

Serve hot.

Making time: 20 minutes (excluding soaking and fermenting)
