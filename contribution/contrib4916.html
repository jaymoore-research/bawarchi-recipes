Broccoli Paruppu Usili

Ingredients

For Usili
1)Toor dal-1/2cup
2)chana dal-1 tbsp.
3)red chilies-3
4)ing-1 pinch
5)salt-required amount.
6)curry leaves-1 stick

For curry
Broccoli- 1 bunch - cooked with salt and turmeric powder

Seasoning:
oil
Mustard-1 tsp
Urad dal-1 tsp
Green chillies-1
Curry leaves-1 stick

Method:

  1. Grind the ingredients for usili into a coarse paste[like for paruppu vadai].
  2. Steam cook paste for 10 minutes.
  3. Heat oil in a pan, add the seasonings.
  4. When golden brown add the steam cooled usili and fry nicely under low fire till it becomes small crisp particles.
  5. When golden brown, add to this the cooked broccoli and fry for 5-10 minutes.

This combines well with Vathal Kuzhambu rice or morkuzhambu.

This usili can also be made with beans or banana flower or a miture of beans, green peas and broccoli.
