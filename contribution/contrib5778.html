Microwave Kopra Pak

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients

1 pound /16 ounces ceyloni kopra (coconut powder)
8 ounces Carnation milk powder (or any other milk powder will do)
8 ounces ricotta cheese (Polly- O / Sorrento)
10 ounces sugar
Few strands of saffron
Few strands of pistachios and almonds
3 tbsp ghee

Method

  1. Take a microwable glass bowl with a lid (preferably corning ware) take 1 tbsp ghee in it.
  2. Add coconut powder, milk powder, Ricotta cheese and Sugar.
  3. Dissolve saffron in 2/3 drops of warm milk and add to the above mixture.
  4. Mix all above. Close the lid and put the bowl in microwave for 5 minutes
  5. Remove from microwave and stir.Add 1 tbsp ghee and stir.
  6. Close lid and put it again for 5 minutes.
  7. Remove and add 1 tbsp ghee to it and stir.
  8. Close lid and again keep it back for 2 minutes only.
  9. Remove and set it on a greased baking tray (non-stick).
  10. Leave it for sometime then cut into square shape with a throw away (plastic) knife.
  11. Keep the whole tray overnight in the fridge.
  12. You can eat it the next day.
