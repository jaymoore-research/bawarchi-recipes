Sweetpotato Jamun

Ingredients

3 cups Sugar
3 cups Water
1/2 kg Sweetpotatoes
Oil
2 tsp Elaichi powder

Method:

  1. Boil sweetpotatoes and mash them.
  2. Make small balls.
  3. Heat oil and deep fry the balls till they are brown. Keep aside these balls.
  4. Make sugar syrup and put elaichi in that and then put these balls in the hot syrup.
