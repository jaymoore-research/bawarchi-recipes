Peas Bade

Ingredients

Split peas - 1 cup
Green cabbage - 1/2 cup chopped
Green chilies - 1 chopped
Onions - 1/4 cup chopped
Red chili powder - 1 teaspoon
Coriander powder - 1 teaspoon
Garam masala - 1/2 teaspoon
Jeera 1/2 - teaspoon
Besan/ Maida - 1-2 teaspoon
Salt to taste

  1. Take split peas and soak it for 5-6 hrs.
  2. If u take 1 cup of peas and soak it will appx. become 2 cups after 5-6 hrs. Grind it into a thick paste.
  3. In that add 1/2 cup of chopped green cabbage, 1/4 cup chopped onions and 1-2 green chilies chopped into small pieces.
  4. Add 1 teaspoon of red powder, 1 teaspoon of coriander powder, and 1/2 teaspoon of garam masala and jeera and salt to taste.
  5. Add little Besan or Maida to it.
  6. Mix all the ingredients nicely...can also add coriander (cilantro) leaves. Take oil in a frying bowl and deep fry it.

Serve it with chutney or sauce.
