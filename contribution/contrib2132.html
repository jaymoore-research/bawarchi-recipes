Rava Pancake

Ingredients

Rava 1 cup
Peas 1/2 cup
Tomatoes 1 finely chopped
Onion 1 finely chopped
Potato 1 grated
Some florets of Cauliflower finely chopped
Water 1 cup
Some Mustard Seeds
Salt as per taste

Method:

  1. Pour water in rava and make a paste if its thick add more water to make it a bit runny and leave it for 1/2 hr
  2. Now add all the veggies in the rava paste. Add salt and mix it well.
  3. Heat oil in heavy bottom pan. Sprinkle some mustard seeds let it crackle.
  4. Now add the rava paste and flatten it with the ladle till it covers the whole bottom of the pan. Adjust the heat accordingly better if its low. Cover the pan and let it cook.
  5. Check the down part it should turn brown. Turn the side as you would do to a omellette and cook it till done covered. The pancake will come out easily from the pan when its done properly and you can turn it easily.
  6. Eat it with Coconut Chutney or Sauce its tasty
