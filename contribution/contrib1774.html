Masala Dosas

Ingredients

For the batter for Dosa :

Rice - 2 cups
Urad Dal - 3/4 cup
Chana Dal - 1/2 cup
Methi seeds - 4 - 5 seeds

Soak these together in water overnight and grind together. Do not add extra water when you are grinding. Cover the vessel with the batter and place some heavy weight on top of the plate covered for the vessel. This is for natural fermentation. Some people place the batter in the oven at warm.

For the Aloo curry :

Potatoes - 2 to 3 boiled, peeled and mashed
Onions - 2 medium sized
Green chillies - accordingly
Coriander - finely chopped
Green peas - 1/2 cup
Salt to taste
Oil - 2 tsp
Mustard seeds - 1/2 tsp
chana and urad dal together - 1 tsp

Method :

  1. Heat oil in a kadai, add mustard seeds, allow to splutter, now add chana dal and urad dal, wait till they turn golden brown, add turmeric and onions, chillies and chopped coriander.
  2. Wait till the onions become translucent and add salt now and then add the mashed potatoes. Do the mixing properly on a medium flame and remove the kadai off the flame.

For the Red Chutney (inside the Dosa)

Ingredients (Red Chutney)

Chickpeas - 2 Tbsp
Peanuts - 1Tbsp
Ginger - a very small piece (some use garlic - according to your preference)
Red Chillies - 2 to 3
Tamarind juice extract - 1/2 tsp
Fresh grated coconut - 2 Tsp
Asafoetida - accordingly
Salt and Sugar to taste

Method :

Grind all of them together to a fine paste. Do not add too much of water to this chutney.

Note : For the Green chutney, repeat the same procedure with Green chillies and Chopped coriander instead of Red chillies and make this chutney a little watery.

Method for Masala Dosa :

  1. Heat a pan on a medium flame. Preferably, the pan should be nonstick.
  2. Take a big spoon full of batter of the dosa and spread it on the pan evenly making the edges a little thicker than the inner edge.
  3. Spread a spoon of ghee over the dosa.
  4. Now smear the red chutney on the dosa top and add a spoon full of the potato curry over it and remove the dosa from the pan without inverting it and by folding it on the other half.

Serve this Masala Dosa with the Green Chutney as the side. This tastes yummy! Good Luck!
