Chutney Murghi

Serves - 6

Ingredients

1:Chicken Breast (Boneless) 1Kg
2:Garlic 1-2Tbsp
3:Ginger 1-2Tbsp
4:Fresh Curds 250gms
5:Fresh Cream 250gms
6:Green chutney 2-3Tbsp
7:MSG 1-2Tsp
8:Salt to taste.

Procedure:

  1. Clean chicken thoroughly. Cut the chicken into 1inch cubes.
  2. Marinate chicken with, ginger garlic paste, fresh curd and cream.
  3. Apply green chutney, MSG, and add salt to taste to the chicken. Leave the marinade, for 4 hrs, in the Fridge.
  4. Grease skewers with butter.
  5. Push the pieces of chicken on the skewers, put in oven, which has been preheated for 10 Minutes at 150 degrees. Place a greased perforated tray, with bread slices under, for collection of the moisture drip.
  6. Reduce temperature of oven to, 120 degrees and increase timer to 15 minutes.
  7. Check chicken pieces, for medium browning and remove.
  8. SERVE HOT WITH LIME.

FOR MAKING CHUTNEY FOR APPLYING TO THE CHICKEN.

  1. Cut coriander and mint into shreds.
  2. Put into mixer along with green chillies.
  3. Spin to a paste and add salt to taste.
