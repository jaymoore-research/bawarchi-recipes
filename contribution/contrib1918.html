Choclate Truffles

Ingredients

Ground Almonds 75 grammes (3 oz)
Icing sugar 100 grammes (4 oz)
Choclate powder 1 tablespoon
1 egg-yolk
Evaporated milk
Coffee or vanilla essence

Method:

  1. These sweets are made without cooking at all, so you don't need to waste your time in putting on your oven and baking your recipe.
  2. Put into a basin the ground almonds, icing sugar and choclate powder.
  3. Put the egg yolk into a cup, add a few drops of evaporated milk, beat toghther, then add a few drops of vanilla essence or 1\2 teaspoon of coffee essence.
  4. Put this into the basin of mixture and, using your hands , mix it into a soft dough. Leave this to get very cold - you could out this into the fridge for half an hour.
  5. Sprinkle a large sheet of greaseproof paper with a little more choclate powder, or vanilla vermicelli is better to use if you have any; Shape the mixture into small balls and roll them in the powder or vermicelli. If you have any paper cases , put the truffles into them.
