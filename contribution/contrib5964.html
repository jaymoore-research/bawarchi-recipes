Tomato-Coconut Curry

Ingredients

Grated coconut - 1 cup
Tomatoes - 2-3 numbers(cut into small pieces)
Red Chillies - 5-6 (depending on how hot it is)
Onion - 1(This needs to be chopped finely)
Green Chillies - 2(This needs to be chopped finely)
Ginger - a small piece.(This is to be grated or finely chopped).
Coriander Powder - 1 tablespoon
Curry leaves
Salt to taste

Method

  1. Heat some oil in a kadai.
  2. Put the red chillies and stir .
  3. Next add the coriander powder and continue to stir.
  4. Next add the grated coconut to it followed by the sliced tomato pieces.
  5. Continue to stir the mixture until it turns golden brown in colour.
  6. Keep the same aside to cool. Once it is cooled grind the same.
  7. Next heat some oil in a kadai.
  8. To it add some mustard seeds.
  9. Once it begins to splutter add the curry leaves followed by the finely chopped onion, green chillies and ginger.
  10. Fry the mixture for some time.
  11. Next add the ground paste to the same add boil for 10 minutes.
  12. Serve hot with chappathis or rice.
