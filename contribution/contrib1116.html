Lotus Stem Koftas

Ingredients

Lotus Stem (Bhee) : 500gms (Fresh, Peeled, Grated, Squeezed with hands)
Besan : 3 tsp
Salt to Taste
Red Chilli Pwdr: 1 tsp

Ingredients for Curry :

Onion : 1 medium (Cut in any shape & size)
Tomato : 1 big (Cut in any shape & size)
Ginger & Garlic Paste : 1 tsp each
Green Chillies : 4 (cut into 1/2)
Water : 2 cups
Fresh Cream : 1tbsp
Corriender leaves : Few sprigs
Garam Masala powder : 1tsp

Method (For Koftas)

  1. Mix all the ingredients together in a bowl with your clean hand & make sure there is no water at all.
  2. Take a little mixture in your hand & make medium sized balls with light hands (don't press too much)
  3. Heat oil in karahi for deep frying & fry these balls till little dark brown (make sure they don't stick to each other).
  4. Keep aside on a blotting paper to soak oil.

Method (For Curry)

  1. Heat 3 tbsp of oil in karahi & put onion, Fry till light brown (do not burn).
  2. Add tomato & green chilies to these onions & cook till tender on Mid-High. Take out, cool & grind this mixture to paste.
  3. Now return this mixture back to the karahi & add ginger garlic paste to it, fry for 5 min.
  4. Add 2 cups of water to the mixture & bring to boil for about 10-12 min.
  5. Just before serving add the Koftas in the curry & garnish with little cream, coriander leaves & garam masala powder.

Serve steaming hot with Rice / Chappaties.
