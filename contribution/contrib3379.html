Homemade Mini Pizza

Ingredients

For the Pizza
225gm flour
1/4 tsp salt
15gm yeast mix with 4-5tbsp hot water
1 tbsp sugar
150ml evaporated milk
2 tbsp margerine

For the filling
1/2 cup tomato sauce
1 pkt grated cheese
2 large onions (cut into rings)
1 cup chicken pieces
1 egg (beaten)
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper powder
2 tbsp margerine (melted)

For the pizza:

  1. Mix yeast in hot water and leave it to be frosty.
  2. In a bowl put in the flour, salt, sugar and mix with milk and yeast.
  3. Add butter. Knead until dough become soft and pliable. Leave the dough to rise double in size.

For the filling:

  1. Mix chicken in the beaten egg along with salt and pepper powder.Leave aside.
  2. Punch into the dough with your hands and knead again and make into equal balls shape (as big as you want) and flatten the dough with a rolling pin.
  3. Sprinkle with flour if the dough is sticky.
  4. Put the mini round dough in a greased flat baking pan.
  5. Brush the mini pizza with tomato sauce.
  6. Add in a bit of chicken pieces in the centre, top with onion rings and grated cheese.
  7. Sprinkle a bit of melted margerine to get a crunchy taste.
  8. Do the same to all the other pizza.
  9. Pre heat oven for 180C and bake for 15-20 minutes. You can serve with chilli sauce.

Tips: You can also add mushroom and capsicum in your filling
