Prawn Pizza

Ingredients for the dough
Maida - 1 cup
yeast - 1/2 tsp.
Sugar - 1/2 tsp.
Salt - 1/4 tsp.
Warm water - little
oil - 1 tbsp

Method:

  1. Dissolve yeast & sugar in little warm water and keep it aside.
  2. In a bowl, mix maida & salt.
  3. When the yeast mixture comes up, pour this to maida and knead.
  4. Use little warm water also.
  5. Add 1 tbsp. of oil to this and make a soft dough.
  6. Cover this bowl with a wet cloth and keep it for sometime.
  7. When the dough comes up, press the dough and keep it as before.

Ingredients for prawn masala
Prawns - 10 large
onion - 1
ginger - 1/2" piece
garlic - 2
Tomato - 1 small
coriander powder - 1/2 tsp.
Chilli powder - 1/2 tsp
Turmeric powder - 1/4 tsp
prawn masala/chicken masala - 2 tsp
capsicum-1 small

Method:

  1. Heat little oil in a pan.
  2. Add crushed ginger and garlic and saute.
  3. Then add diced onions and saute.
  4. After that add chopped tomatoes and saute.
  5. Add coriander powder, red chilli powder, turmeric powder and saute.
  6. Add the chopped prawns, salt and masala.
  7. Cover and cook for some time.
  8. When it is almost done, add diced capsicum and saute for a while.

Ingredients for the pizza mix
Garlic - 1 tbsp
onion- 1
peeled tomato- 1 can (225gms);
tomato paste - 1 small tin
Organo leaves grinded (dry) - 1/2 tsp
chicken stock - 1 cube sugar - a pinch

Method:

  1. Heat little oil in a frying pan.
  2. Add finely chopped garlic and saute.
  3. Then add finely chopped onion and saute.
  4. After that add grinded organo leaves and chicken stock. Saute.
  5. Add the mixture of peeled tomato and tomato paste (blend in a mixer).
  6. Cover and lower the flame.
  7. Cook for about 1 hour in a very low flame.
  8. Add a pinch of sugar at last and cook for a while.
  9. When it becomes cool, store in a bottle and keep it in fridge.

Method

Pre heat the oven at 200 degree C. Flatten the dough in a baking tray and pork with fork all over the dough. Bake at 10 mins. Take it out and spread the pizza mix. Then spread the prawn masala and sprinkle grated mozerella cheese on the top. Bake the top for about 8 mins till the cheese melts.
