Ingredients

Sooji (rava) 1/2 cup
Milk 2 cups
Sugar 7 tsp.
Oil/ghee 1/2 tsp.
Mango pulp 2 cups

Method :-

  1. Heat oil/ghee in a pan and roast sooji in it. Till it becomes a little brownish.
  2. Add milk and sugar to sooji and stir continuously, so that no lumps are formed.
  3. Within minutes, the above mixture would turn into a paste.
  4. Take it off the pan and pour it in a bowl. Refrigerate.
  5. Pour mango pulp over it at the time of serving. Voila! you are all set for praises.
