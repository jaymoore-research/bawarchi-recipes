Coconut Rava Cake

Ingredients

3 Coconuts
500 grams Rava (Suji)
500 grams Sugar (Fine)
12 Eggs
200 grams Butter
Vanilla Assassins or Cardamoms optional.
Backing Pan.

Method:

  1. Scrape the coconut with the scraper. It should be scraped very fine.
  2. Take a bowl put the butter, the sugar and the twelve eggs. Beat the mixture till everything gets mixed and the sugar gets melted.
  3. After that put the scraped coconut and the Rawa (Suji) in the mixture. Beat it till it is well mixed with the butter sugar and the eggs.
  4. Then put the vanilla assassins or cardamoms in it and mix well.
  5. Grease the baking pan with ghee, put the mixture in it and let it bake till it turns golden brown.
  6. Let it cool.
