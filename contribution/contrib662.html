Kema

Ingredients

Minced meat (300-400 gms)
3 medium onions cut length wise and thin
One tomato cut coarse
2 large potatoes peeled and cut triangular not too small
250 gms Mushrooms, cut
Green peas (100 gms)
Garam Masala Sabut (whole spices: cinnamon, coriander, jeera, cloves) 1/2 teaspoon total, levelled
Garam Masala powder, Jeera Powder, Dhaniya powder, Meat Masala:- all 3/4 teaspoon, levelled
Haldi Powder 1/2 teaspoon, levelled
Bay Leaves (1-2)
Mirchi powder (1/2 teaspoon) or fresh green chillies(3) or according to taste.
Coconut powder (if you wish), dhaniya leaves (if available)

Method:

  1. Heat oil.
  2. Add Bay leaves (1-2) + Garam Masala Sabut (1/2) and one green chilly, chopped, if you have. Heat a while (30 sec).
  3. Add chopped onions + Garam Masala powder (3/4)
  4. Fry till onions light-pink.
  5. Add equal amounts of Jeera Powder and Dhaniya powder (about 3/4 teaspoon, more if you wish more spicy), 1/2 teaspoon of Haldi Powder, and chilly according to desire (1/2 teaspoon moderate, more if you wish it more chilly), and Meat Masala.
  6. Fry till the spices are cooked. You can see this when the oil absorbs the spices and floats.
  7. Add Tomatoes and fry a bit ( 30 sec).
  8. Add the meat, and make it really fine. Add Potatoes and mushroom. Add Coconut Powder (4-5 spoons, if one wishes)
  9. Fry somewhat till meat is brown, smells good and done, you also smell the spices integrated in the meat.
  10. Add Water to taste, typically, it should just cover the meat, less if you like it dry and more if wet. Add green peas.
  11. Put lid, lower fire and cook for about 15-20 min. Add dhaniya leaves in the end.
