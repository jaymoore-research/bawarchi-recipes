Fish Puttu

Ingredients

Tin Fish (Tuna Fish) 2 tins
Onions 2 finely chopped
Green Chillies finely chopped as desired
Coriender leaves finely chopped one bunch
Curry Leaves chopped a few
Ginger finely chopped one small piece
Salt
Turmeric Powder 1/2 small tea spoon
Oil 3 table spoon
Grated Coconut 5 table spoon

Method

  1. Heat oil in a frying pan and fry onions till transparent.
  2. Add green chillies, ginger, coriander leaves, curry leaves and turmeric fry for a few minutes.
  3. Add the tuna fish after draining the liquid add salt and keep frying till it is dry.
  4. Add grated coconut and serve with Rice or Chappathi.
