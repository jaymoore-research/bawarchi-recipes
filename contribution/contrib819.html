Mango Rice

Ingredients

Raw mango - 1 (medium size peeled and grated )
Cooked rice - 3 cups
Green chillies - 2 (cut into long slices )
Urad daal - 2 tsp
Mustard seeds - 1 tsp
Chana daal - 2 tsp
Dry red chillies - 4
Ground nuts (roasted ) - 2 tsp
Cashew nuts ( roasted ) - 2 tsp
Curry leaves - 5 - 6
Turmeric - 1 tsp
Oil - 5 tsp
Sesame seeds ( roasted and powdered ) - 5 tsp
Salt - acc to taste

Method :

  1. Cool the cooked rice.
  2. Now mix in salt, turmeric, grated mango to the rice.
  3. Add half a spoon of oil so the rice does not stick together.
  4. Take a pan add oil. When it reaches its heating point add in all the ingredients for seasoning along with the green chillies.
  5. When the seeds start to splutter then add in the sesame seeds powder.
  6. Mix well and take off the stove.
  7. Cool it and then add this seasoning to the rice.
  8. Mix well adjust the salt and then enjoy .
