Dhalia

Ingredients

1 Cup Wheat Medium Lapsi
1 Cup Moong Dal
1 1/2 Table Spoon Salt
Pinch of Hing
Half Spoon Turmeric Powder
One Table Spoon Pure Ghee
Half Spoon Jeera
2 cup Water

Method:

  1. First wash the Moong dal half an hour before cooking.
  2. Then wash the lapsi & mix with Moong dal.
  3. Add 2 cup of water put 1 1/2-tablespoon salt, Pinch of Hing & Half spoon Turmeric powder.
  4. Put one tablespoon of ghee with half spoon jeera & duly heated pour in to Dhalia & mix with all this things with spoon and put in a Pressure cooker.
  5. After 2 whistle put the gas in slow flame for 10 minutes. After half an hour open the cooker & ready the Dhalia for serve. You can also put coriander leaves on it.
