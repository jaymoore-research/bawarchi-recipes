Wheat Laddu

Ingredients

Wheat flour - 1 cup
Oil - to deep fry
Powdered sugar - 11/2 cup
Finely chopped cashew nuts - 1 tbl.sp
Ghee - 1/4 cup

Method:

  1. Mix wheat flour with just enough water and knead like chappathi dough.
  2. Prepare thin chappathis (appalam) and deep fry in hot oil till crisp.
  3. After frying all the appalam till crisp and golden, allow to cool.
  4. Crush and powder it coarsely. Add powdered sugar and pound them together. Add fried cashew nuts and prepare lemon sized balls.

For variation :
Fry wheat flour with ghee til it gives out pleasant smell. Add powdered sugar and mix well. Add cahew, cardomom powder. Mix more melted ghee if necessary and form into balls. To decorate the same you can cover the balls in fresh grated coconut powder.
