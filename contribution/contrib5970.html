Spicy Kidney Fry

Ingredients

Goat kidney’s - 500 gms
onion - 1 large
chili powder - 11/2 tsp
turmeric - 1/2 tsp
coriander powder - 2 tsp
coconut milk - 1cup
tomato - 1 large
ginger-garlic paste - 2-3 tsp
green chili - 2-3 no
black pepper - 1 tsp(powder)
salt - to taste
oil - 4-5 tsp
lemon juice - 1 tsp
water - 3-4 cups
coriander - small bunch

Method

  1. First clean the kidney’s thoroughly with water .
  2. Drain the water and keep aside.
  3. Chop onion and tomato and keep aside.
  4. Know take a pressure pan and keep it in stove and put oil .
  5. When the oil gets heated put chopped onions and it till golden brown.
  6. When the onions are golden brown put ginger –garlic paste and fry 2-3 min.know add kidneys and fry it well .
  7. After frying the kidneys add chilipowder,turmeric,dhania powder,greenchili,tomato,salt and mix it properly.
  8. Know add coconut milk and mix thoroughly .
  9. After mixing it know add required water to cook kidney’s and pressure cook it till done(5-6 whistles)
  10. When the kidney’s are well cooked adjust the gravy according to your taste.
  11. To this add black pepper and lemon.
  12. Garnish with coriander.
  13. Serve hot rotis or rice.
