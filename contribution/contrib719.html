Butter Chicken Fry

Ingredients

Boneless chicken - 2lb
Ginger garlic paste - 3 tsp
Chilli powder - 3 or 4 tsp
Turmeric - 1 tsp
Garam masala powder - 2tsp
Pepper powder - 2tsp
Red food colour - 1tsp
Vinegar - 3 tsp
Salt - according to taste
Malai or cream - 1/2 cup
Cashew nuts powder - 1/2 cup
Coriander - to garnish
Oil - 4 tsp.

Method :

  1. Marinate the chicken with the above ingredients except butter and cashew powder preferably over night or atleast for 8 hrs.
  2. Add oil to the pan.
  3. When it is heated then add the marinated chicken and mix well. Cover the pan.
  4. Approximately after 15 min or when the chicken is cooked add malai and cashew nut powder and fry the whole until completely dry.
  5. Garnish it with coriander and serve hot with Lemon pieces
