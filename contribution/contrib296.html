Aloo Wadi (Patra)

Ingredients

2-3 leaves of patra (aloo)
besan 1 cup
turmeric powder 0.5 tbsp
chilli powder 1 tbsp
tamarind paste 1/4 tbsp
jaggery
garam masala
salt to taste
oil to fry
grated coconut, sesame seeds and coriander leaves to garnish.

Method

  1. Clean the patra leaves and then dry it.
  2. Mix the above mentioned ingredients to the besan and make a thick paste of it by adding proper proporrtion of water.
  3. Spread this mixture on the patra leaves .
  4. Then roll the leaves thoroughly and closing the sides by the above paste.
  5. Cook the leaves in a pressure cooker .
  6. Let them cool and then cut it into small pieces(vadis) .
  7. Heat some oil in a pan. Shallow or deep fry these aloo vadis in it.
  8. Finally, garnish it with grated coconut, coriander leaves and sesame seeds.
  9. Serve it hot or cold its delicious!!!
