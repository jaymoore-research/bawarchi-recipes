Marellapudi.Subbalakshmi@am.sony.com

Patholi

Ingredients

Channa (any variety) - 2 cups soak them over night
Green Chillies - 10
Curry Leaves - few
Ginger - 2 inches
Salt to taste
Oil - 4 table spoons
Mustard and Urad Daal just for seasoning

Method:

  1. In a blender, mix channa, green chillies, ginger and salt. Do not make it too smooth a paste. Put very little water while grinding to paste.
  2. In a kadai (non stick preferably) put seasoning with mustard and urad daal. When Mustard splutters add curry leaves and the ground paste. Keep it on a slow flame and keep stirring every 5 min. It will take like 30 min for this paste to get roasted (it turns pinkish).
  3. Add coriander at the end. Do not cover it any time while cooking.
Happy cooking and happy eating.
