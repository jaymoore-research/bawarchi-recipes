Italian Squash Curry

Ingredients

Italian SquashĀ- 3/4
Usual ingredients required to cookĀ [oil, mustard seeds, jeera, turmeric,salt, cumin powder, dhana powder]
Sugar to taste
Corriander to garnish

Ā Method:

  1. Cut squash into big pieces removing inner white part containing seeds.
  2. In one vessel, put 2/3 spoons of oil.
  3. Once oil is heated add mustard seeds and let them cracker.
  4. Add jeera, turmeric powder, chili powder, cumin powder, dhana powder,salt all accordingĀĀĀ to taste.
  5. Immediately add squash pieces.
  6. Let it be there for a while and then add sugar to taste.
  7. Add little bit of water such that squash is completely immersed in water.
  8. Put lid on the vessel and keep gas on medium heat.
  9. After a while the squash will change its color and that is when your curry is ready to serve.
  10. Garnish with coriander

Makes For: 3/4 people
Preparation time: less than 10 minutes
Caution: When you buy squash, buy them firm. Before cooking taste each squash and see if itĀis not bitter.
