Rava Upma

Ingredients

Upma rava-1 cup
water -2cups
Salt to taste
Green chillies-4-5 (chopped)
Ginger-1 inch (finely chopped)

Seasoning: little amount of all these -->oil, urad dal, mustard seeds, chana dal, curry leaves.

Method:

  1. Heat oil in a pan or kadai and all the above seasoning, chillies and ginger. When they turn little brown add Rava and fry a little.
  2. In a small bowl add 2 cups of water and salt and heat it to boil.
  3. Then pour this water into the kadai.
  4. Mix well and close it with a lid and stir it occasionally. (If not rawa remains as thick balls). When rawa is completely cooked remove from fire.
