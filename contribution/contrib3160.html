Potato Raitha

Ingredients

4 potatoes, boiled,peeled and diced into small chunks
a small bunch of spring onions, chopped very fine
a small piece of ginger grated
2 tsp of jeera
3 tsp of finely chopped coriander
2 chilles, finely chopped
2 cups of yoghurt, beaten well
2 tsp of oil

Method

  1. Heat the oil in the pan and add jeera. After a 30 to 40 secs add ginger and chillies and fry well.
  2. Add spring onions and fry for a couple of minutes.
  3. Once they are done add salt and boiled potatoes and mix well.
  4. Take it off the heat and add yoghurt and garninsh it with coriander leaves. This is very tasty with rotis or any rice preparations.
