Masala Potato Fry

Ingredients

2 medium sized Potatoes
1 tsp turmeric Powder
1 tsp Red Chilli Powder
1 tsp Coriander-Cumin Powder
Salt to taste
1 small bowl of Rice Flour
1 small bowl of Oil for shallow frying.

Method:

  1. Peel and Cut the potatoes into round circles-horizontally
  2. Wash them well and drain the water completely.
  3. Apply the chilli powder, turmeric powder, coriander-cumin powder and salt so that the pieces are covered completely.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan and add half the oil-about 2-3 tsp
  5. Dip the potato pieces in rice flour and see that they are covered completely and then put them in the pan one by one and let them cook on medium flame. Use half the pieces at a time
  6. Turn them over after they are done on one side. They should be light brown on both sides.
  7. When they are done, remove them and put the rest of the pieces in the pan for frying.
  8. Serve hot with Rice and Dal as a side dish
