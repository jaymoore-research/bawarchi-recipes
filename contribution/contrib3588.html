Party Punch

Ingredients

1 big (1 liter) bottle of Lime/Pineple/Orange Soda
1/2 litre Vanila Icecream
1/2 litre Vanila Sorbet (in frozen Section of Super store)
1 litre Ginger Soda
1 can (small) Red Cherries
1 can (small) Delmont mix Fruit
1 can Sprite

Method:

    Mix in a bowl all above ingradients, 10 minute before serving and keep in bowl with 2 cup crushed (very small pieces) ice. If you serve, occationaly stir to avoid fruit left at bottom. Enjoy the party and be Happy...
