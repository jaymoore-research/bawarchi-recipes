Mix Dal

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients

Urad Dal, Masoor Dal (which is orange), Moong Dal, Chana Dal, Tur Dal....in equal quantities.1/4 cup each
Tomato- 1 big
Turmeric powder, Hing,salt, mustard seeds, Cumin seeds
Dried red chillies 3-4Āor red chilli powder
Chopped garlic - 2 teaspoons
Coconut powder - 3-5 tablespoons
Oil - 3-4 teaspoons
Lemon juice
Cilantro

Method

  1. Soak all dals in water for few minutes. Pressure cook soaked dals with turmeric powder, hing, tomato. Keep it aside.
  2. Put oil in kadai, add mustard seeds, jeera,hing,Āred chillies or powder.
  3. Add coconut powder and fry it till brown.Ā
  4. Add chopped garlic and fry for a minute.
  5. Add water and the cooked dal.
  6. Add salt to taste.
  7. Garnish with lemon juice and cilantro.
  8. Enjoy with rotis!
  9. It takes only 15-20 minutes for this dal. If the dal is cooked in excess quantity, refrigerate excess dal and use it later!
