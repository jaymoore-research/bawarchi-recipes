Potato Masala Rice

Ingredients

Pototoes 3-4 (Boiled)
Sanuf

Wet Grind

Pudhina (Mint) leaves 1 bunch
Cumin seeds 1 tspn
Turmeric powder 1 tspn
Green Chillies 4 to 5 nos
Ginger 1 big size
Garlic 2 pieces
Coriander leaves

Cooked rice

Oil/Ghee
Salt As per one's taste.
Coriander leaves to garnish

Tip : punch some tiny holes to the boiled potatoes before adding to the gravy/Masala paste.

Method

  1. Chop the boiled potatoes
  2. In a pan, add some oil/Ghee. When the oil/Ghee gets heated, season the oil/Ghee with cumin seeds , sanuf and to it add the wet Grind Masala paste and turmeric powder. Fry till the raw smell vanishes.
  3. Add the boiled potatoes and a little bit of water for the potatoes to absorb the masala.
  4. Close the pan with a lid and Simmer it for some time.
  5. To the cooked Potato masala add the cooked rice ,mix well ,simmer the flame and close it with lid for 1/2 a Minute.
  6. Finally garnish with Coriander leaves
