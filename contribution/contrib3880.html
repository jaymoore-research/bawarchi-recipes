Choco Milk

Ingredients

1/2 Litre Milk
1 tbsp Chocolate powder
1 tsp Cocoa powder
1/4 tsp Nescafe Sunrise Powder
2 tbsp Sugar
Any Choclate Pieces

Method:

Blend all the ingredients together in a blender and top it with grated chocolate pieces and serve chilled or warm.
