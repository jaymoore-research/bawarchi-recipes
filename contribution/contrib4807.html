Banana Muffins

Ingredients

1 cup maida (all purpose flour)
1 big ripe banana
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup refined oil
1/2 teaspoon cooking soda

Method:

  1. Mix well cooking soda with maida and keep aside
  2. In another bowl smash banana with sugar. Then add oil and maida mix very well.
  3. Then put that mixture in to bake cups (paper) or muffin pan and bake it in the oven at 250 or 300 F (temperature) for 30 to 35 minutes.
