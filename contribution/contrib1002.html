Breadtoms

Ingredients

Bread slices -4 (each cut into four square pieces)
Tomatoes-3 (pureed)
Garlic pods-4 (crushed)
Ginger paste -1 1/2 tsp
Green chillies -4 (cut fine)
Gram flour to make a paste
Salt to taste
Coriander leaves - 2 tbsp chopped fine
Oil for frying

Method :

  1. Cut each bread slice into four squares.
  2. In a bowl mix the ginger garlic, tomato puree, salt, green chillies, coriander leaves and gram flour (besan) to make a paste. (Add water if necessary).
  3. Heat oil in a pan.
  4. Dip each bread square in the paste and deep fry till crisp and done.
  5. For weight watchers - dip each bread square in the paste and either shallow fry in a tsp oil or bake at 180oC for 5-10 mins or as required.
  6. Enjoy with your tea!
