I am Mrs. Della Alphie. I am a Keralite, permanently resident in Bolivia, South America, working as Co-ordinator at an American school here. I like to experiment with South American cuisine, whenever I have the time & this is one of my favorites. Salpicon is the local favorite too. Doesn't take much to make it. Tasty too.

Salpicon

Ingredients

Chicken 1 - 2 kg
Green Peas 75 grams
Carrots 2 medium
Potatoes 4 large
Eggs 4
Lettuce 1 leaf per person
Mayonnaise 1 cup
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Cut chicken into large pieces, add salt & put to boil in a large pan of water. When cooked well, remove pieces from pan, shred and place in a large bowl.
  2. Boil the potatoes whole, with their peels on, till soft. Dice into tiny cubes, upturn onto shredded chicken, sprinkle a pinch of salt and leave.
  3. Cook the green peas in 1 glass of water.
  4. Chop carrots into small cubes & when green peas are almost cooked, add the carrots to the pan, adding a pinch or two of salt. When cooked, add to chicken and potato.
  5. Boil the eggs, chop and add to the chicken, etc. Pour mayonnaise onto everything.
  6. Place a leaf of lettuce on a plate & serve chicken mixture. Can be served hot or cold.
