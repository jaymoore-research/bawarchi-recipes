Bread Upama

Ingredients

10 slices of Bread
1 big Onion
A small piece of Ginger chopped
Two Egg
Little Oil to fry
Pinch of Red Pepper
Turmeric and Salt.

Method:

  1. Make bread into really small pieces
  2. Put little oil in a pan, and slightly golden brown onions chopped, chopped ginger and pinch of red pepper, turmeric and salt into it.
  3. Break two eggs into it scramble and add bread pieces and sprinkle couple tsp water into it and mix everything and serve hot with tea for breakfast, instead of egg can use mixed vegs also if vegatarian. Thanks.
