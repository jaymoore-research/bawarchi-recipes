Chettinad Vellai Appam

Ingredients

Raw rice-2 cups
Urid dhal : 1/2 cup
Salt-1-1/2 tsps
milk - 1/4 cup
Cooking soda-2 pinches
Oil- enough to deepfry

Makes: 40 Appams

Method:

  1. Soak rice and dhal together for 2 hours.
  2. Grind to smooth paste.
  3. Add salt and cooking soda.{no need to ferment}
  4. Dilute to soda batter consistency with enough water.
  5. Pour oil in small deep curved pan and heat as for that of gulab jamuns. Keep in medium flame and pour with a small laddle like appam.
  6. Turn over once, do not fry till crisp.
  7. Drian excess oil by keeping on absorbent paper. Repeat the process till all the batter is used. This can be served hot with any chutney.
