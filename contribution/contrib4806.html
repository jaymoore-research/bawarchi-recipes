Nan Cuts

Ingredients

Maida - 500 grms
Sugar - 500 grms
Dalda or Ghee - 500 grms
Baking powder - 15
Salt - 1 pich

Method

  1. Mix Sugar powder & Ghee then add Maida, elachi powder, baking powder, salt then mix it well.
  2. Make it small balls and press buy hand. (Nan Cuts shape) Microwave this balls for 20 minitues on high.
