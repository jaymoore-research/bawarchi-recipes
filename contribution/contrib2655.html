Prawn Pakodas

Ingredients

Prawns- 10 nos.
Flour - 1/4 cup
Gram Flour (Besan)- 1/2 cup
Onion - 1 large or 2 small ones
Green Chillies - 4 (depends on individual preference)
Turmeric powder- a pinch
Salt - as required
Tomato Sauce - 1 tsp
Soya Sauce - 1 tsp
Baking Powder -1/4 tsp
Oil - for deep frying.

Method

  1. Shell the prawns and de-vein them.
  2. Cook in a little salted water till its slightly hard or till you feel it is properly done.
  3. Remove and drain the excess water and chop into small pieces.
  4. Similarly chop the onions and green chillies into tiny pieces. Mix all ingredients, add enough water to make a thick batter of dropping consistency.
  5. Heat oil in a kadai and drop in spoonfuls of the batter to make pakodas.
  6. Remove when the pakodas turn golden brown.
  7. Serve hot with Tomato Sauce and Mint Chutney.
