Kadamba Saadam

Ingredients

Tuvar dal: 1 cup
Rice : 1 cup(soaked in water for 1 hour)
Dry chillies: 7 or 8
Fenugreek seeds: 1 tablespoon
Asafoetida: 1 tablespoon
Channa dal: 5 tablespoons
Cinnamon:2 sticks
Clove: 3 or 4
Dry coconut: 1/2 cup
Coriander seeds: 2 tablespoons
veggies :including potatoes, carrots, bellpeppers, beans, peas, carrots, brinjals etc.
Green chillies: 4 to 5 (as required)
poppy seeds: 1 tablespoon
peanuts: a fistful
Oil: for preparation
Tamarind: 2 lemon sizes
Mustard : 1 tablespoon

Method:

  1. Pressure cook the tuvar dal as usual.
  2. Fry in 1 tablespoon of oil fenugreek, channadal, drychillies, asafoetida, cinnamon, clove, coriander seeds, dry coconut and poppy seeds in the order and grind the whole mixture into a smooth paste.
  3. In a large pan add the cooked dal, all the veggies, a little turmeric, green chillies and half a cup of water and let the veggies cook with the dal for 10 minutes.
  4. Once the veggies are cooked, add the above paste, the tamarind extract and salt and let the whole mixture boil for a while.
  5. Once the sambar is ready, put the presoaked rice into it and allow it to cook it in the sambar itself, so that it absorbs all the flavors and come into a nice smooth mixture.
  6. Heat ghee in a pan, add mustard, one dry chilli, asafoetida and peanuts and fry them well and add it to the above preparation.
  7. Serve hot with chopped coriander leaves.

Tip:You can go on adding water and take care to see that the rice is cooked well.

Variation:Once you prepare the sambar, you can also add the cooked rice and allow it to boil with sambar for while, till the rice absorbs all the moisture in the sambar. Happy cooking!!
