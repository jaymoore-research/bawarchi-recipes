Lango Lata

Ingredients

flour (maida)---250gms
sugar---------1cup
water---------1/2cup
khoya--------100gms
pistachios-----3tbsps(chopped)
almonds-------4-5tbsps(chopped)
raisins---------3tbsps(chopped)
cashewnuts----3tbsps(chopped)
cloves---------15-20
nutmeg &cardamom powder--1/2tsp
oil-------------3tbsps
ghee----------4tbsps
ghee----------to fry

Method:

  1. Rub ghee (4 tbsps) and oil into the flour well. Then add water to form a  stiff dough.
  2. Fry khoya in little ghee and then add all the nuts & raisins & nutmeg & cardmom powder.
  3. Make sugar syrup of one string & keep aside.
  4. Make small balls of dough & roll into puris. Put little mixture of khoyain each puri  &  fold each into a square & seal each with a clove.
  5. Deep fry them in ghee on slow flame & put them in sugar syrup. Delicios labanga lata's are ready.
