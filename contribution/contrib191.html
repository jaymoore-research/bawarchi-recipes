Raitha With An Attitude

Ingredients

2 tbsp. grated fresh coconut
3-4 dry red chillis
1/2 tsp. methi (fenugreek) seeds
curry leaves
1 tsp oil to fry this
3 cups buttermilk or curds (yogurt)
salt to taste
1 tsp coconut oil

Method:

  1. Fry methi and red chillies in oil and grind it to a paste w/ the grated coconut.
  2. Mix this masala in the buttermilk adding salt to taste.
  3. Keep it on the stove and keep stirring.
  4. Don't let this boil.
  5. Fry mustard seeds in coconut oil and season the raitha w/ this. add curry leaves.
  6. You can add sauted eggplant, okra, etc. to the raitha.
