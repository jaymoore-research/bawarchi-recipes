Easy Spagatti : Italian

Ingredients

Spagatti - 1 pack (Pasta Barilla)
Olive Oil - 6 Tsp
Fresh Garlic Cloves - 8 nos crush a little
Black Pepper freshly grounded - 1 tsp
Dry Red Chilly - 4 nos (If Non-spicy reduce to 2 nos)

Method :

  1. Cook Pasta with salt in Boiling water as recommended time.
  2. In the mean time in a separate pan add 4 tsp of olive oil.
  3. When it gets hot Add Garlic cloves and Red chilly. Simmer till the garlic gets roasted.
  4. By the time Pasta will be cooked, drain and add 2 Tsp of olive oil.
  5. Put this cooked pasta in the rosted garlic and olive oil, pan sprinkle some pepper. That's it ready to serve hot.

Optional:
Can be added: Raw chopped Tomato & basil & cheese.
