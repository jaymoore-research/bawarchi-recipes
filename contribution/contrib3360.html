Rice Noodles

Ingredients

Rice noodles - 1 packet
Lime - 2(big)
Green Chillies
Curry Leaves

For Seasoning:

Peanuts - 1 tbsp
Chana daal - 1 tbsp
Urad daal - 1/2 tbsp
Mustard - 1/2 tbsp
Turmeric - a pinch
Oil - 1 1/2 tbsp
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Boil water in a saucepan. Add salt to the water. When it is rolling, add noodle and leave for 2 min. Now the noodles are cooked. Remove water and keep it aside.
  2. Put seasoning with all the above ingredients and once mustard splits add green chilli and curry leaves. Add this to cooked noodles.
  3. Squeeze lime and add the juice to the noodles.

Tasty noodles are ready (if you like, break the cooked noodles into small strings by a sharp laddle)
