Inji Curry (Ginger Curry)

Ingredients

Ginger cut into small thin slices - 1/4 kg
Tamarind - 50 gm
Water- 2 cups
Red Chilli powder-1 tsp
Coriander powder- 1.5 tsp
Asafoetida-1/2 tsp
Salt
Sugar - 1 tsp
Green Chillies sliced into thin rounds -1/2 tsp
Shallots sliced into thin rounds - 1/2 tsp
Coconut Oil - 100 gm
Mustard seed - 1/2 tsp
Dry Red Chilli - 3
Curry Leaves

Method:

  1. Heat coconut oil & fry the ginger slices in it till brown in colour. Then powder it nicely.
  2. Soak tamarind in 2 cups of water & strain the juice. To this add all the powdered masalas & ginger. Boil the mixture & remove it from the fire.
  3. Heat oil. Fry green chillies & shallots. Add mustard seeds & curry leaves. Add this to ginger curry. Then add sugar.
