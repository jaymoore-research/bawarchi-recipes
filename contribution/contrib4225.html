Easy Crisp Dosas

Ingredients

Rava - 1 cup,
Maida - 1 cup,
Cumin seeds (1 tsp),
1/2 tsp ground Pepper,
Onion (1) (optional)
Salt to taste.

Method:

  1. This proportion of rava and maida will make about 20 dosas. Take 1 cup of rava. Add 1 cup of maida to this and add 3 cups of water. Do not worry about adding excess water. This dough will be very thin and watery. Add salt. Add finely chopped onion to this.
  2. Now in a small kadhai, fry the cumin seeds and pepper in little oil and add to the dough. Thats it. Your dough is ready. You can start making dosas immediately.
  3. To make Dosas: In the Dosa tava, put a little oil, pour the batter roughly on the tava. Dont try to do it in a circular motion like the regular dosa. Once one side is cooked, flip and cook the other side. Each dosa will come out thin, cripsy in 2 mintes. It goes best with Coconut Chutney.
