Porridge (Kanji)

Ingredients

1. Raw Wheat 1/2 kg
2. Boiled Rice 1 tumbler
3. Moong dal 1 tumbler
4. Pottu Kadalai 1 tumbler
5. Groundnut 1/2 tumbler
6. Almonds 1/2 tumbler
7. Barley 1/2 tumbler
8. Sago 1/2 tumbler

Method:

  1. First fry the above ingredients and then keep it aside
  2. Grind these ingredients into powder and whenever you want to use it you can boil the milk and mix this powder with some sugar and make it thick consistency and the drink is made which has full nutritonal value.

Enjoy the drink!!
