Ingredients

Cauliflower 1 medium
Green Chillies 15
Ginger 1"
Garlic 10 flakes
Corn Starch or Corn flour 1 cup
Oil
Soy Sauce 3 Tbsp
Tomato Ketchup/Sauce 3 Tbsp
Water
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Clean and wash cauliflower.
  2. Cut cauliflower into tiny pieces and put it in a vessel. Apply salt to the cut pieces and mix.
  3. Cut half of the green chillies into small pieces and half of them lenghtwise. Add to small pieces to cut cauliflower.
  4. Cut ginger and garlic to small pieces. Add half the quantity to above mixture.
  5. Add Corn starch/Corn flour to above mixture. Mix well and try to form samll dumpings out of it. If the mixture is not sticky add some more corn starch or add some water. Keep in mind that the mixture shouldnot have lot of flour in it.
  6. Take some oil for frying. After the oil is hot, add these small dumpings and fry till light brown or crunchy.
  7. After you finish frying, take about 2 Tbsp. of oil in a wok/frying pan. You can use the same oil and the pan used previously for frying.
  8. Add the lengthwise cut green chillies and remaining half of the ginger-garlic. Fry for some time. Then add the fries dumplings to it.
  9. Add all the sauces and water. If you want gravy add around 2 cups of water or if you want it dry just add 1/2 cup of water. Add salt as per the taste.
  10. Serve hot with Fried Rice.
