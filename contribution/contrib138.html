Potato Stew

Ingredients

2 large potatoes.
Curry leaves.
a big piece of ginger.
5-6 hot green chillies.
1-2 cups of grated coconut.
mustard seeds.
salt to taste.

Punch line:

Coconut and ginger are the main ingredients.
Feel free to be generous on that front.

Method

  1. Boil the potatoes, remove the skin and mash them into relatively big pieces.
  2. Grind grated coconut, green chillies and ginger to a paste.
  3. Heat oil in a pan, toss in the mustard seeds and after seasoning add in the paste, pour a cup of water and boil the mixture for about 5 minutes.
  4. Now add the mashed potatoes into the mixture and allow it to boil for another 5 minutes or until the mixture becomes thick. Feel free to add little water to meet your required consistency.
  5. Finally add curry leaves to the stew.
  6. Serve with tomato rice, sambar rice, dosai, chapati and what not.
