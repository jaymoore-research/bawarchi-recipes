Avial

Ingredients

Vegetables pealed and chopped lengthwise to 1 1/2 inch pieces 3/4 in each variety. Potatoes, Rawbanana, Beans, Carrot, Malabar Yam, Red Pumpkin, Peas, Drumsticks, Chow-Chow etc.

Other Ingredients:

Coconut Oil -3 tbsp
Curry leaves-3 sprigs
Fresh thick curd - 1/2 cup
Plantain leaf to cover

Grind together

Greated Coconut -- 1 1/2 cup
Cumin seeds- 1 1/2 tsp
Green Chillies- 15
Raw Rice- 1 1/2 tbsp (soked in water)
Salt to taste

Method

  1. Pressure cook all the vegetables in just enough water till 2 whistles.
  2. Gently mix the ground paste with vegetables and heat in medium flame till it boils.
  3. Add whipped curd and mix well.
  4. Reduce flame completely.
  5. Put curry leaves on the top of avial just before removing from fire.
  6. Heat coconut oil separetely in a frying pan till it becomes luke warm.
  7. Pour over avial mix well transfer into servving vessel.
  8. Cover with a plantain leaf and then with a proper lid on top. (This helps to retain its flavour)
