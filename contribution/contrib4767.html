Aloo Mirchi

Ingredients

Capsicums : 4 Nos
Potatoes : 500 Grms
Tomatoes : 3 Nos
Onions : 3
Chillies Green : 4 Nos
Jeera Powder : 1/2 tsp
Peanuts raw chopped : 2 tbsp
Salt : to taste
Ghee : 1 tbsp
Garam masala : 1/2 tsp

Method:

  1. Halve the capsicums and remove pith and seeds.
  2. Boil the potatoes, peel and mash.
  3. Chop tomotoes, slice the onions.
  4. Heat the ghee in a pan and fry onions till translucent. Add tomatoes and peanuts and fry for a minute. Now green chillies, cumind seed powder and salt to taste and cook for a couple of minutes.
  5. Add the potatoes and fry for a minutes. Now add garam masala. Mix well.
  6. Cool and stuff into the cacpsicums.
  7. Put in grill for 15 minutes, or bak in the hot over.
