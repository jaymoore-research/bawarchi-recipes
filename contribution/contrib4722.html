Rice Rotis

Ingredients

Rice Flour- 1 1/2 cups
Methi Leaves- chopped -1/2 cup.
Curry Leaves and Coriander Leaves- 1/4 cup-chopped
Onion- 1 med sized, chopped finely.
Carrot-1 -grated.
Green Chillies-2 chopped finely.
Salt to taste.

Method:

  1. Mix all the ingredients in a bowl and add water and mix to get a dough which is slightly softer than that for chappatis. Even if the water is slightly more its okay.
  2. Now take a pan with handle, smear oil on it and take a round of the dough on the pan. Pat it into a thin round shape with your palms using some water if necessary. Now keep it on medium flame and cook, pour a tsp oil on the sides and cover with a plate. After few minutes, remove the plate and cook uncovered after turning the side.
  3. Sevre with coconut chutney.

This type of roti is not only very tasty but also very nutrituous. Note that you should cool the pan under a tap before uyo pat the next roti on it. Also, any other green leaves can be used in place of methi leaves.
