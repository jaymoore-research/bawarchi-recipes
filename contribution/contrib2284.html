Egg Delight

Ingredients

6 Eggs
2 big Tomatoes
3 big Onions
1 big Bell Pepper {Capsicum}
1 slice of Cheese {sandwich single}
1/2 a tsp Turmeric
Red Chilli Powder - according to your taste {I use 4 tsp for hot and spicy}
Salt
Chopped Coriander
Oil
Pinch of Hing.

Method:-

  1. Slice onions, tomatoes and capsicum
  2. Heat oil in a pan {I also add butter for extra flavour}
  3. When oil is hot enough add hing and onions & fry onions nice and brown
  4. Add tomatoes & fry 3 to 4 minutes
  5. Add capsicum and fry for another 2 to 3 minutes
  6. Now add all the spices and cheese and keep frying so the cheese is melted
  7. Lower the heat and add a cup of water
  8. Stir the sauce for some more time. Sauce should not be watery, just thick enough to hold the eggs.
  9. Now very gently break the eggs side by side, do not distrub yolk
  10. Sprinkle the coriander leaves and cover the pan and let it cook on very low heat for about 10/15 minutes till eggs are set.
  11. Serve with Chapatti or Bread.
