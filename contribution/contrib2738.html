Butter Beans-Potato Kuttu

Ingredients

Butter Beans-1 cup
Boiled Potato-2
thick Coconut Milk-1 cup
Garlic- 1 tsp
Green Chillies-3 slit
Mustard seeds-1 tsp
Curry leaves-2 tsp
Red Chillies-2
Salt to taste
Oil-2 tbsp
Dhaniya powder-1 tsp

Method:

  1. Boil butter beans.
  2. Heat oil. When hot add mustard seeds, red chillies, curry leaves. Fry.
  3. Add chopped boiled potatoes. Add 2 whole peppercorns. Fry on low heat.
  4. Add dhaniya powder and green chillies. Add the boiled butter beans. Mix well. Add salt and 1/2 cup water. Simmer.
  5. When done add the coconut milk and after two mins off the gas. Serve hot.
