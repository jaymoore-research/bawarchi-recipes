Omlete Curry

Ingredients

For the Omlete:

6 eggs
2 small onioins cut into small peices
1 green chilli (cut into small peices)
Green peas
Cheese (grated)
Salt (to taste)
Chilli powder (1/2 tsp)
Amchur masala (1/2 tsp)

For Making the Gravy:

1 big onion (cut into thin long peices)
tomato puree (3 tbsp)
Jeera powder 1tbsp
Coriander powder 1 tbsp
Chilli powder (according to taste)
Turmeric powder
Garam masala
Salt (to taste)
Oil
Soya sauce (1 tbsp)
Ghee (1 tsp)
Ginger Garlic paste

Method:

  1. Take a bowl and beat the eggs.
  2. Add the onions, green peas, chilli peices, chilli powder and coriander powder and beat it again.
  3. Heat oil in a pan. When it becomes hot put the egg-mixture and fry as a single layer. When done, spread the grated cheese on the single layer of the omlete.
  4. Now fold the omlete from one side till you get about three layers.
  5. Cut the omlete into small square peices and keep aside.

To cook the Gravy:

  1. Heat oil in a pan. When it becomes hot add the onions to it and fry untill golden brown. Add the ginger garlic paste to this and fry again for a few minutes.
  2. Add the jeera powder, coriander powder, chilli powder, and turmeric powder and fry for some time.
  3. Add the tomato puree and cook till oil separates.
  4. Add soya sauce and cook for a minute.
  5. Add the peices of omlete to it. Cook for 1 minute.
  6. Add water (around 2 cups).
  7. Add salt.
  8. Cover and let it cook till you get a thick gravy.
  9. Before serving add ghee and garam masala.
