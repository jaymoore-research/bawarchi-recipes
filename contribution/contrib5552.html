Jhatpat Sandwich

Ingredients for Cooking

1.Ā 6-7 bread slices
2Ā 5 tsp butter
3. 4 tsp mix fruit jam
4. 100 gm cottage cheese (grated)
5.Ā a pinch of salt
6.Ā a pinch of black pepper
7.1 CAPSICUM (DICED)

ĀĀ Ā METHOD

  1. In a bowl mix butter and fruit jam thoroughly till paster resembles light pink in colour
  2. Cut the bread slices in roundels
  3. Apply the paste of butter-jam on bread
  4. Place the tomato roundels in center of bread
  5. Spread the grated cottage cheese on the sides of tomato( where there is empty space)
  6. Put some diced capsicum in the center of tomato as garnishing
  7. Sprinkle salt and pepper
  8. Serve and enjoy
