Bhindi Masala

Ingredients

250 gms Bhindi (ladies finger/okra)
2-3 medium sized potatoes (optional)
3-4 green chillies
8-10 flakes garlic
1/2 inch ginger
i/2 bunch coriander leaves(cilantro)
1 tsp coriander powder
1/2 tsp haldi
1 tbsp oil
Salt as per taste
2-3 medium sized tomatoes or 1/2 tsp Amchoor powder(mango powder)

Method:

  1. Take garlic, ginger, chillies, coriander leavesand half grind it do not grind it to paste it would taste good if grinded slightly thick.
  2. Add salt, little oil, coriander powder and haldi powder in to the mixture and keep it aside. If u are using amchoor mit in the mixture too.
  3. Slit the bhindis from the center and cut potatoes in round shapes and mak some gashes on them.
  4. Now fill bhindis with all the prepared mixture and on the potatoes.
  5. Heat oil in a wide pan slide bhindis and potatoes gently in the pan.
  6. Stir it gently so that the masala does not ooze out. Keep it on slow fire and cover it with lid, let it cook till the bhindis have become dark green in color and almost cooked.
  7. Now add finely chopped tomatoes. Again cover it with the lid and cook it on slow fire till the tomatoes almost disappear.
  8. Serve with hot chappatis/phulkas.

Note: you can also add small onions (shallots) and brinjals in the above receipe.
