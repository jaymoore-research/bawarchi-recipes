Garlic Sauce

Ingredients

Three and a half cups of water,
One cup Samovita (Suji),
5-6 Cloves (bunches) of Garlic,
Juice of three lemons,
Half cup of normal cooking oil,
Salt to taste.

Method

  1. Mix properly two cups of water with one cup of Samovita (suji).
  2. Put on the fire cook it for 5-10 mins.
  3. Till it forms into a shape of a hard ball. Cool it.
  4. Meanwhile, grind garlic with one a half cup of water in a blender/grinder.
  5. Add Samovita mixture lime juice oil, salt.
  6. Grind it till it becomes a smooth paste.

Hope you enjoy this.
