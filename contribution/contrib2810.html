Now when it is the season of Mangoes I am sending a recipe of raw mangoes called Kairicha Kaayras. It is a side dish and is sweet & sour in taste. It is a good substitute for chutney. It is easy to make and requires less time. Try this out.

Kairicha Kaayras

Ingredients
Raw Mango peeled and cut into small pieces --- 2 cups
Gud ( jaggery) -- 1 -- 1&1/2 cup
Chilly powder -- 1 tsp
Water -- 1 cup
Salt to taste

For tempering:
Oil --2 tbsp
Mustard seeds
Methi seeds (fenugreek) 1/2 tsp
Hing (Asafoetida) -- a pinch
Turmeric powder 1/2 tsp.

Method:

  1. Wash, peel and cut the Mangoes into 1/2 inch square pieces.
  2. Heat oil in a kadhai and add mustard seeds, hing, turmeric powder, and methi seeds. When methi seeds turn dark brown add mango pieces.
  3. Add a cup of water, put a lid and cook mango pieces for 5 mins. Remove the lid and add chilly powder, salt and jaggery and cook for another 15 mins on medium low flame till all the jaggery melts and forms syrup and the mango pieces are tender and water evaporates. This dish should not become too watery.

Serves: 4
This dish goes well with Parathas, Roti, Bread or toast or even rice.
Enjoy !!!
