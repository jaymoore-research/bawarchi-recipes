Roasted Sandwiches

Ingredients

bread
rava 1 cup
tomatoes 2
onions 1
green chillies 4
coriander leaves(cilyantro) 1/4 of the bunch
and salt to taste.

Method:

  1. Cut tomatoes, onions, green chillies, coriander leaves into fine pieces and mix it with rava and salt.
  2. Keep it aside for 5 minutes.
  3. Then take bread slice and spread this paste on it.
  4. Heat the pan put little butter on it and fry this bread slice on it, let the paste become golden brown then turn the other side of the bread.
