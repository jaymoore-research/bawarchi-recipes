Eggless chocolate Cake

Ingredients

1 1/3 cups Maida
1 cup powdered Sugar
3/4 cup Butter Milk
1/2 cup Refined Oil
3 tbsp. Cocoa Powder
1 tps. Baking Powder
1/4 tps. Baking Soda
1 tsp. Vanilla Essence
Dry fruits if necessary

Method:

  1. Sieve Maida, cocoa & baking powder atleast thrice.
  2. Mix baking soda in buttermilk & keep aside.
  3. Dissolve the powdered sugar in the oil.
  4. Now mix the sieved mixture & buttermilk alternatively to the oil & sugar mixture. Beat well.
  5. Add vanilla essence & mix again.
  6. Transfer this mixture into a pre-greased cake pan & bake it in pre-heated oven at 350oC for about 35 to 40 minutes.

Enjoy!
