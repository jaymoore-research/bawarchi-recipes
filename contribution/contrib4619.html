Cheesy Broccoli Potato Topper

Ingredients

1 can [10-3/4 ounces] CAMPBELL'S Condensed Cheddar Cheese Soup
4 Large hot baked Potatoes, split
1 cup Cooked Broccoli Flowerets

Method:

  1. Stir soup in can until soup is smooth.
  2. Place hot baked potatoes on microwave-safe plate. Carefully fluff up potatoes with fork.
  3. Top each potato with broccoli. Spoon Soup over potatoes. Microwave on HIGH 4 minutes or until hot.
Serves: 4
Preperation Time: 5 Minutes
Cooking Time: 5 Minutes
