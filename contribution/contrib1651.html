Khus-Khus Kheer

Ingredients

1 tblsp Rice
1/4 kg Jaggery
1/2 cup Milk
4 cups Water
1/2 Coconut grated
1 tblsp Poppy seeds
Powder of 4 Cardamoms

Method:

  1. Soak rice for half an hour. Grind it.
  2. Grind poppy seeds, coconut and cardamom.
  3. Melt jaggery in 4 cups of water on low fire. When melted, increase heat and bring to a boil. Add ground mixture and rice.
  4. Cook for 10 minutes stirring all the time.
  5. Cool and add milk. Decorate with nuts.
