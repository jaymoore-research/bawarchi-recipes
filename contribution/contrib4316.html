Carrot/Cabbage Bhakarwadi

Ingredients

For Cover:

250 gm plain flour (wheat)
3 tsp gram flour (besan)
1 1/2 tbsp. Oil
Salt to taste

For Filling:

2 Carrots(grated)
250 gm Cabbage (grated)
1 tsp. Sesame seeds
1 tsp. Cumin seeds
1/2 tsp. Ginger paste
1/2 tsp. Green Chilli paste
1 tsp. Red Chilli powder
1 tsp. Turmeric powder
1 tsp. Garam Masala
3 tsp. dried Coconut powder
1 tsp. Lemon Juice
2 tsp. Groundnut powder
Curry Leaves(5)
Salt to taste
1/2 cup fine `Sev'(used in bhel)
Oil to deep fry

Method:

For Cover:

Mix flours, salt, oil, mix with fingers. When crumbly add little water, and knead into a smooth dough. Cover with a moist cloth.

For Filling:

Heat oil in a kadai. Add curry leaves and cumin seeds. cook until cumin seeds starts to change color, add grated carrot and cabbage. Mix and cook on low heat for 5 minutes. Add remaining ingredients and mix well.

  1. Take a chappati size lump, roll into thin chappati.
  2. Use dry flour to help rolling.
  3. Spread filling thinly all over chappati. Make a tight roll press down edges.
  4. Cut into 1" pieces and deepfry in hot oil. Fry till cover is crisp and golden brown.

Making time: 25 minutes
Makes: 20-25 pieces
