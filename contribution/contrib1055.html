Mutton Rezala

Ingredients

Mutton 1 kg
Ghee 3 tbsp
Onion 3
Garlic 5-6 pods
Ginger paste 2 tsp
Curd 500gm
Whole red chilli 10-12
Black pepper 15-20
Salt to taste
Bay leaves 3

Method :

  1. Marinade mutton with curd onion, ginger, garlic paste, salt for 1 hour.
  2. Heat oil, add black pepper, red chilli, bay leaves & fry for 2 min.
  3. Put the marinade into it, stir for 5 min & pressure cook till meat is tender.
  4. Serve with Naan
