Wheat Dosa

Ingredients

1 cup wheat flour
1/2 cup water
1/2 medium sized onion
1/2 tspn red chilli powder
Salt to taste
1/4 bunch cilantro

Method :

  1. Cut onion and cilantro into very small pieces.
  2. Take wheat flour into a bowl, put onions, cilantro, red chilli powder, and salt in it.
  3. Add water to this mixture slowly and mix it well till it gets consistency of dosa batter.
  4. Put tava on stove. When it becomes hot pour a spoon of this batter and spread it.
  5. Put oil around the dosa. When it turns golden brown on the bottom, turn it the other side and remove it in 5 seconds.

That's it! Easy-to-make wheat dosa is ready and serve it hot. There's no need of any chutney or pickle in this since it already contains red chilli powder.
