Rasam

Ingredients

1 cup tuvar dhal
3 tomatoes
1 tsp sugar
coriander leaves (little)
1/2 tsp haldi
5 flakes garlic
2 tsp jeera
1 tsp black whole pepper

Seasoning:
2 tsp oil
1 tsp mustard
half tsp jeera
hing (pinch)
curry leaves(kadi patta)
2 or 3 red whole chillies

Method:

  1. Boil tuvar dhal and 2 tomatoes with enough water in pressure cooker (whistles). Let it cool.
  2. Churn it and add 1 cut tomato cut into small pieces, haldi(turmeric) half tsp, salt 11/2 tsp, sugar, cilantro and keep it boil.
  3. Mash garlic, jeera and pepper coarsely without adding any water.
  4. Heat oil, add mustard, jeera, hing, mashed (garlic, jeera, pepper), curry leaves and red whole chillies ans just fry till it turns brown and to the boiling rasam.
  5. Your rasam is ready. It is a little bit spicy, but goes very well with white rice and ghee.
  6. Serving for 4 to 6 people.
