Masala Bhendi Fry

Ingredients

1. Bhendi - 1/2 kg
2. Coconut piece - 1 small, dry one would do
3. Onions - 2 small ones
4. Sesame seeds - 4 spoons
5. Dhaniya powder - 2 spoons
6. Garlic - 4 pieces
7. Tamarind or Vinegar - 1 small piece
8. Red chilly powder, Turmeric, Oil to fry
9. Salt - as per taste
10. Coriander leaves.

Method:

  1. Wash the bhendi, clean it with a dry cloth till its totally dry. Give strokes(don't cut) lengthwise and keep aside.
  2. Fry ingredients #2 to #6 in a pan in a 1/2 spoon of oil.
  3. Grind everything except bhendi....ofcourse. (Masala's ready!)
  4. Stuff the masala carefully in the bhendi, make sure they don't split into two pieces.
  5. Fry them in a pan with a spoon of oil(not much of it). Simmer the gas. Put the lid and cook for 5 minutes till bhendi is tender. Take off the lid and fry till brown and crispy.
  6. Garnish with coriander leaves.
