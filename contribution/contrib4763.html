Savoury Malpuas With Rabri

Ingredients

For the puas:

Maida - 1 cup.
Milk - 1 Cup approx.
Raisins. - 1 tbsp (optional)
Cashews nuts - 1 tbsp chopped. (optional)
1 Ripe Banana - mashed.
Oil for deep frying

For the syrup:

Sugar 1 cup
Water 1.5 cups

For Rabri:

Whole Milk - 1 litre
Sugar 3/4 cup
Pistachio - chopped 1 tbsp (optional)
Kesar (optional)

Method:

  1. Make the syrup by combining sugar and water and bringing to a boil. Simmer for 3-4 minutes. Cool.
  2. For Rabri - boil milk on low heat until nearly reduced to 1/3rd. Dissolve kesar (if using) in cold milk and add. Add sugar and simmer for some more time until milk thickens (adjust quantity of sugar to taste). Take it off the heat and add pistachios. Cool and refrigerate.
  3. For puas make a batter with the maida and milk. Make sure no lumps are formed. The consistency should be like the besan batter used for pakoras. Now add mashed banana and whip up batter. Add raisins and cashews mix well.
  4. Heat oil in a pan. Add a ladle full of the batter in the hot oil and let it take it's own shape. Deep fry until golden brown on both sides. Remove from oil and soak in sugar syrup for a couple of minuted. Remove to serving dish.
  5. To Serve: Place pua in a serving dish and coat with rabri. Sprinkle dry fruits if desired.
