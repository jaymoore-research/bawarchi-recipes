Cauliflower Rice

Ingredients cauliflower - 1/2
jeera - 1 spoon
cloves - 2
salt
coriander -1/2 bunch
green chillies - 2
tomato ketchup - 2 spoons
basmathi riceĀ-1 1/4 cup

Method

  1. Fry cloves,jeera ,green chillies in oil.
  2. Add cauliflower and fry till it turns brown. Ā
  3. Add salt and tomato ketchup.add coriander leaves. Ā
  4. Cook basmathi rice separately and mix the rice with the cauliflower.

Enjoy!
