Ingredients

Ginger (chopped)
Green Chillies (chopped- optional)
Onions (chopped)
1 Cup Rava (Suji)
Salt to taste
5 teaspoon cooking oil
1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds
About 1 serving spoon Chana Dal
2 cups Water
4 teaspoon Lemon Juice

Method:

  1. Soak the Chana Dal in water for 5 - 10 minutes.
  2. Heat the Rava in a pan until very light brown.
  3. In a pan heat the cooking oil and add the mustard seeds until they crack.
  4. Add the Dal and fry until brown.
  5. Add Chopped Green Chillies, Onions and Ginger and fry until light brown.
  6. Add the salt and water. Boil it.
  7. Very slowly add the rava and stir until thick.

Serve hot. (Optional - Garnish it with coriander leaves, Cashew, Raisins and green Chillies). Hope you Enjoy it!
