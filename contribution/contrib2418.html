Bharali Vaangi (Stuffed Brinjal)

Ingredients

10/12 small round Brinjals (Available in Indian groceries)
1 cup Coconut
3/4 cup Peanut Powder or Peanuts (roasted)
1/4 cup Sesame seeds
Coriander-Cumin seeds (1 tbsp each)
Maharashtrian Goda Masala (4 tbsp)
Tamarind (a pinch)
Jaggery
Chilli powder
Salt
Ginger Garlic paste (if you like)
Asafoetida
Turmeric powder

Method:

  1. Remove stems & give four slits (not fully) to the brinjals. Wash them in a salt water & keep aside.
  2. Roast coconut, sesame seeds, coriander-cumin seeds together. Grind this mixture with tamarind, jaggery, peanut powder, ginger-garlic paste, salt, chilli powder & goda masala.
  3. Fill all the brinjals with this paste.
  4. In a pan heat oil & add mustard seeds. When starts spluttering add asafoetida, turmeric powder.
  5. Add the brinjals. Pour in some water just to cover the top of brinjals.
  6. Cover & heat on a medium flame for about 15 min.
  7. Remove the lid & stir. Garnish with coriander leaves.
Serve hot with Chapati or Rice.
