Dum Aloo

Ingredients

small round potatoes 500 gms
peanuts 1 tbsp
sesame seeds 1 tbsp
chironji 1 tbsp
garam masala 2tbsp
amchoor powder 1 tbsp
dhaniya patha 1 tbsp

Method:

Put all the potatoes in a polythene bag and keep them in microwave for 5 min take them out and peel them Prick them with fork fry peanuts chironji and sesame separately till light brown Make a paste by adding a little curd Add salt to taste garam masala and amchoor powder and marinate for 1 hour then in a closed container microwave it in power 50 for five min serve hot decorated with dhaniya patha

Healthy Eating

Tip: To store lemons apply a layer of cooking oil on it and then refrigerate it
