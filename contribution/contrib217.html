Panneer Paratha

Ingredients

Maida 1 cup
Flour 1 cup
Ghee 3 Table spoon
Peas 300 grams
Paneer 200 grams
Butter Milk 1/2 cup Ginger
Green Chilli 10pc.
Corriender
Garam Masala 1 Table spoon
Salt
Onion 2pc.
garlic

Method

  1. First mix flour & maida & mix ghee in it.
  2. Add Butter milk and make a tough dough.
  3. Add salt and green chilli.
  4. Grind Ginger,onion,garlic and corriender and make fine paste.
  5. Roast peas in a pan with 2 table spoon of ghee.
  6. After frying add paste in it.
  7. Add Garam masala in it and fry little more.
  8. Grind peas finely.
  9. Cut corriender finely and add Paneer &salt.;
  10. Add peas paste to it.
  11. Make small dough of flour & make Roti as usual &apply; paste on full Roti,
  12. Make another Roti & put it on the paste
  13. Bake Roti on tawa.
