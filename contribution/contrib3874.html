Ankurit Moth Salad

Ingredients

1 cup moth
1 small Cucumber finely chopped.
1 large Tomato finely chopped.
1 small Onion finely chopped.
1 Green Chilli finely chopped
2 tsp. Chat Masala
1/4 tsp. Red Chilli powder
1/4 tsp. Black Pepper powder
2 tbsp. Lemon Juice
1 pinch Turmeric
1 tbsp. Vegetable or Olive Oil
1/2 tsp. Cumin seeds
Salt to taste

For Garnish:

2 green Onion
1 tbsp. chopped Coriander leaves

Method:

  1. Soak moth for 5-6 hours, then put it in a sieve overnight in a warm place.Ankurit moth are ready.
  2. Now take a heavy based pan, heat oil in it 7 put cumin seeds, when it starts splutter put turmeric, moth & salt. Pore 1/4 cup water in it, cover it & let it simmer for 4-5 minutes. Open it, dry it & let it cool. Now mix all ingredients with soft hands.
  3. Garnish it with green onions & coriender.
  4. Serve as a side dish or as a snack in the evening [DON'T MIX ONION IF USING AS A SNACK].
