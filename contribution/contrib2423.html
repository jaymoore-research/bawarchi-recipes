Cheese Balls

Ingredients

1 Bread(Plain)
10-12 cloves of Garlic (chopped or crushed)
3-4 Green Chillies
2 cups(approx.) of Mozarella Cheese (Grated)
Salt to taste
Black Pepper

Method

  1. Soak bread in water and then remove immediately.
  2. With your hands remove water from bread and the bread part in a vessel.
  3. Then add garlic, green chillies, salt and black pepper.
  4. At the end add all the cheese and mix everything.
  5. Make small pakodas with hand and deep fry them.

So, your cheese balls are ready. They go best as starters with ketchup or salsa. The above serves 5 people as a starter.
