Thenga Kozhambu

Ingredients

1cup of grated coconut
1 tspoon of vendhayam(fenugreek)
5or6 red chillies (depending upon how much hot u want)
lemon size tamarind
salt to taste and asafoetida

For this you don't need dhal

Method

  1. Take the tamarind and squeeze it in 500 ml of water.
  2. Add salt asafoetida and any veg (mostly okra,chowchow or drumstick) and boil.
  3. Take pan and fry vendhayam and red chillies in one spoon of oil.
  4. Grind this with coconut (u need not fry the coconut) in mixie using water.
  5. Add this paste to the boiling mixture.
  6. Cook till the veg is done.
  7. If the kozhambu is too watery take a spoon of rice flour and make a paste using little water and add this to kozhambu and boil for 5 mts.
  8. Season it with mustard seeds.
