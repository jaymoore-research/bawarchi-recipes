Tangy Methi Rice

Ingredients

Rice 1 cup
Methi leaves, finaly chopped 1 cup
Coconut, grated 1/2 cup
Spring Onion, finaly chopped 1/3 cup
Tamarind pieces 5-6
Red dry chillies 7
Methi seeds 1/2 teaspoon
Jeera 1 teaspoon
Urad dal 1 teaspoon
Gram dal 1 teaspoon
Asafoetida 1 pinch
Mustard seeds 1 teaspoon
Oil 1 tablespoon
Salt according to the taste

Method:

  1. Boil the rice so that it is cooked and the grains are separate.
  2. Dry roast red chillies, jeera, grated coconut and methi seeds and powder them.
  3. Heat oil, add mustard seeds. When mustard seeds splutter, add asafoetida, urad dal, gram dal, chopped spring onion and methi leaves. Fry for 2 minutes.
  4. Add powdered masala and mix well.
  5. Add salt and cooked rice and mix well.
  6. Serve hot with your choice of raita.
