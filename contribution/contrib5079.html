Cauliflower Aava Pettina Koora

Ingredients

Cauliflower Grated- 2cups
Mustard-1 tbsp+1/2 tsp
Tamarind Pulp- 2tbsp
Sugar-1 tsp. (optional)
Green Chilli-6
Red Chilli-6
Curry Leaves-1 sprig
Chana Dal-1tbsp.
Urad Dal- 1 tbsp.
Vegetable oil- 2 tsp.
Turmeric- pinch
Salt- to taste

Method:

  1. Microwave cauliflower for 4 mins. or boil in plenty of water and drain. It should not be mushy.
  2. Grind 1 tbsp. mustard to a fine paste by adding enough water
  3. Heat oil in a kadai and add chana dal, urad dal,1 tsp. mustard and red chillies.
  4. Allow mustard to splutter and add curry leaves, green chillies, sugar and tamarind pulp. boil for a couple of minutes. Add turmeric.
  5. Add cauliflower and salt and mix for a couple of minutes until dry.
  6. Add mustard paste, remove from flame and mix well.

This is a spicy recipe and for people who like hot food. Serve it with plain rice.
