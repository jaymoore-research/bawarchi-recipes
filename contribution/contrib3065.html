Kala Jamoon

Ingredients

Milk Kowa (Mawa) - 1/4 kg
Maida - 1 table spoon
Diamond Sugar 5 gms
Paneer - 50 gms
Sugar - 2 cups
Water - 1 cup
A big pinch of Safforn
Coconut powder - 50 gms
Ghee for frying.

Method :

  1. Mix kowa, maida and paneer with little milk and mash gently to make a batter of smooth and soft.
  2. Make small balls of size ping-pong ball size.
  3. Place 3-4 diamond sugar in each ball
  4. Dissolve 2 cups of sugar in 1 cup of water and boil it till it becomes a syrup. add saffron soaked in warm water or warm milk.
  5. Heat ghee in a frying pan. the heated ghee should be at least 3 inches deep in the pan.
  6. Deep fry balls till blakish. When the balls are well fried, remove them from the pan & slip them into the syrup.
  7. After keeping them sunken in the syrup for about 10 minutes, take them out and drain.
  8. Roll each ball on the coconut powder and keep it for dry.
  9. Chill and serve. It can last for 15 days.
