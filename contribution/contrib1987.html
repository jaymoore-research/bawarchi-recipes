Chicken Curry

Ingredients

For Marinating :

Chicken 1 Kg
Curd 2 tbsp
Ginger-Garlic paste 1 tsp
Turmeric Powder 1/2 tsp

For the gravy :

Onions 2 (sliced)
Tomatoes 2 (chopped)
Green Chillies 2 (chopped)
Ginger-Garlic paste 1 tsp
Whole Garam Masala 1 tsp (Clove, Cinnamaon, Elaichi, Poppy Seeds, Bayleaves)
Red Chilli Powder 1 1/2 tsp
Turmeric Powder 1/2 tsp
Coriander Powder 1 tsp
Garam Masala Powder 1 tsp
Cumin Seeds 1/2 tsp
Methi seeds 1/2 tsp
Cumin Powder 1/2 tsp
Coriander Leaves 1 tsp (chopped)
Lime Juice 1/2 tsp
Oil 2 tbsp
Salt to taste

Method :

  1. Clean the chicken & marinate with curd, turmeric powder, ginger-garlic paste & salt. Keep it aside for 1 hour.
  2. Heat oil in a pressure cooker, saute cumin seeds, methi seeds, whole garam masala. Once done add ginger-garlic paste & chillies. Saute for 1 minute or so then add the onions & fry them until golden brown.
  3. Once the onions turn brown, add red chilli powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, garam masala & cumin powder. Saute till the oil separates from the masala.
  4. Then add the tomatoes & mix all together & let it cook.
  5. Once a thick gravy is formed, add the chicken & mix all together. Season it with salt & check the spice taste. If you need more spice add it accordingly. Then add one glass of water to the chicken. Mix it well
  6. Close the lid & allow it to cook. Once the steam comes, put the whistle. Let 2 whistles come & then low the gas flame for about 5-6 minutes. Then switch off.
  7. Just before serving, open the lid, garnish with coriander leaves & lemon juice.

Serve hot with Chapathis, Rotis etc.
