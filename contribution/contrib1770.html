Chocolate Cookies

Ingredients

1 and 3/4 cups -- All Purpose Flour (Maida)
1/4 cup -- unsweetened cocoa powder
1 cup -- finely powdered sugar (or 1/4 cup more if you like it sweeter)
1 cup -- butter/ margerine
1/4 tsp -- vanilla
1/2 tsp baking soda

Method :

  1. Sieve Flour, Cocoa and baking soda together and keep aside.
  2. Beat Butter / margerine and vanilla essence with sugar till fluffy. Add in the flour - cocoa and soda mix and mix well.
  3. Shape into an inch sized flattened balls and place on greased baking trays and bake at 150 degree centigrade for 20 mins or till done !
  4. Cool and store in air tight containers.
