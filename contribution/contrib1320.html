Stuffed Brinjals

Ingredients

8 - 20 small brinjals (Eggplant)
4 tbl spoons fried Peanut powder
1 tsp red Chilli powder
1/2 tsp Jeera powder
1/2 tsp Dhania powder
1/2 tsp Garam masala
4 -5 tbl spoons Oil
Salt to taste
Coriander leaves for garnish

Method :

  1. Cut the brinjals lengthwise in 4 parts from top but do not cut till the base.
  2. Mix all the above ingredients with 1 tblsp oil and stuff in the brinjals.
  3. Fry the stuffed brinjals in low flame and keep the vessel covered.
  4. When done garnish with coriander leaves.

Hope you enjoy this.
