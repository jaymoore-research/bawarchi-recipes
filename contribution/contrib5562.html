Inchi Curry

Ingredients for Cooking

Ginger -1 pound cleaned and cut thin and round
Tamarind juice -1 lemon size tamarind's juice
Jaggery/sugar -1/4 cup
Chilli powder -4 Tbsp
turmeric powder -1tsp
coriander powder -3tbsp
methi powder -1.5 tsp
salt to taste
1 small bunch curry leaves
1 tsp mustard seeds
2-3 dry red chilli
oil for frying ginger

Method:

  1. Pour oil in tawa till it is half of it.
  2. Boil the oil for frying ginger.Ā
  3. Fry it till golden brown and till crispy.Ā
  4. Transfer the fried ginger to a kitchen paper towel to absorb excess oil.
  5. Fry all the ginger and let it cool.Ā
  6. In another dry tawa warm the powders together with salt till it leaves the fragrance.
  7. Grind ginger with these powders till very fine.
  8. Boil 2-3 tbsp of oil used for frying ginger and splutter mustard, red chillies and curry leaves in that.
  9. Pour the tamarind juice and allow it to boil.Ā
  10. When it boils add the ginger -chilli powders to it and let it thicken.Ā
  11. When it starts to thicken add the jaggery.Ā Adjust seasoning to taste.Ā
  12. Add more tamarind juice if required and allow it to boil.Ā
  13. Transfer it to jar after cooling.Ā
  14. Use it like pickle and enjoy!
