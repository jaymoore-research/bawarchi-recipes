Spinach Rice

Ingredients

Basmati rice - 2 cups
Spinach - 2 cups (finely chopped)
Red chillies - 5
Coriander seeds- 2 tbs
Coconut -1/2 cup
Garlic - 4 flakes
Mustard seeds - 1 tsp
Urad dal - 2 tsp
Onion - 2 large (1 chopped lengthwise)
Water - 3 cups
Salt to taste
Ghee - 2 tbs
Coriander leaves
Cashewnuts (roasted in ghee)

Method

  1. Wash the Rice 2 or 3 times and soak it 15 min in 3 cups of water.
  2. Cook the Rice with little salt.
  3. Grind Coconut, Coriander seeds, Redchilles, Garlic, 1 Onion & Coriander leaves to a paste.
  4. Add 1 tbs oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, urad dal, fry 1 onion until golden brown then add the spinach with it. Close and cook for 5 min.
  5. Once cooked add the ground paste & salt. Mix throughly.
  6. Add this to the cooked rice. Add ghee & mix well.
  7. Garnish with cashewnuts.
