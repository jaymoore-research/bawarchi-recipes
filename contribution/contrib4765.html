Crispy Chicken Pakoda

Ingredients

Chicken Boneless : 500 gm
Curd : 2 tablespoon
Ginger paste : 1 tablespoon
Garlic paste : 1 tablespoon
Lemon : 1
Green Chillies : 2
Onion paste : 1 tsp
Turmeric powder : 1 tsp
Red Chilli powder : 1 tsp
Salt to taste
Oil for frying : 500 gm

Method:

  1. Wash chicken thoroughly & cut it into small pieces.
  2. Mix all the ingredients mentioned above to the chicken pieces and marinate it for 1-2 hrs.
  3. Deep-fry it & yummy crispy chicken pakodas are ready.
  4. Serve hot with spicy green chutney.
