Tender Coconut Delight.

Ingredients

2 Tender coconuts with thin layers of cream
1 tin condensed milk
1 tin fresh milk
1-2 tsp gelatine
1 tsp vanilla essence(optional)

Method

  1. In a vessel take the tender coconuts water.
  2. Add 1 tin of condensed milk.
  3. Then with the same tin measure 1 tin of fresh milk. Add this milk to the above. Stir this well.
  4. In a small cup dissolve gelatine,add this to the coconut water-milk mixture.
  5. Now remove the coconut cream from inside the tender coconuts and add to the mixture.
  6. Keep this mixture in the freezer to set. After it has set you can just spoon it out in serving bowls.
  7. No cooking is required for this recipe.
