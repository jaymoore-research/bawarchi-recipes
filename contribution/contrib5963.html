Arisium Paruppu

Ingredients

rice - 1 cup
toor dal - 1 cup
tomatoes(big) - 1 no (diced)
Onions - 1 no (diced)
garlic - 5 big pieces (diced)
carrot & potato(optional) - 1 each (diced)
dried red chilly - 2 nos
red chilly powder - to taste
mustard seeds - 1/4 tsp
jeera -1 tsp
turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp
salt - to taste
curry leaves
Oil - 1 tsp

Method

  1. Heat oil in a cooker or pan.
  2. Add mustard,jeera, curryĀleaves,Ā red chillies, onion, garlic& tomatoes.
  3. Fry for aĀĀ minute. (Don't fry much)
  4. Add rice, dhal, vegetables, turmeric & chilly powder,ĀĀ salt & 5 1/2 cups of water.
  5. Adjust salt & chilly powder.
  6. Pressure cook for 4 whistles.
  7. Serve with papads or pickles.
  8. Tastes very good withĀcurd.
