Tomato Kadhi

Ingredients

5 to 6 medium size Tomatoes which have been blanched and pureed or you can substitute with 12 to 14 oz. can of tomato puree.
6 to 8 Ladies Fingers (bhindi or vendakkai) - cut into three inch pieces if too long
Few Cluster Beans (guvar or kothavarikkai) - string them and cut off the two ends
1 to 2 Drumstick - cut into three inch pieces.
1 medium size Potato - cut into long pieces like for French fries.
Water to dilute the tomato puree (if necessary).
2 tbsps. Gramflour (besan)
2 tbsps. Wheat Flour (atta)
Salt to taste
Turmeric
Red Chilly Powder to taste
Oil to fry the attas and for seasoning -- approx. 2 to 3 tbsps.
Seasoning -- Mustard seeds, Methi seeds (fenugreek) and a pinch of Asafoetida (hing).

Method:

  1. Heat the oil in a pressure cooker vessel. Season with the mustard, methi and hing. When the mustard starts to sputter, add the besan and wheat attas. Fry till the raw smell disappears and it turns a golden brown. Stir constantly so that it does not blacken or form lumps.
  2. Add the cut vegetables (excluding the tomato purree). When it comes to a gentle boil, add salt, haldi and chilli powder. Add the tomato puree and water if needed to dilute the puree. Cover and cook till you hear two to three whistles.
  3. Serve hot with Chapathis or Rice. It tastes yummy.
