Rajma Pilaf

Ingredients

2 cup Basmati Rice
1 cup Red Kidney Beans
1 cup Coconut Milk
1 small Onion sliced
1 large Tomato diced
3 Green Chillies sliced
1 tsp Ginger Garlic Paste
2 Bay Leaves
1 tsp Shaajeera
2 Elaichi
5 Cloves
1 small Cinnamon stick
10 roasted Cashews (optional)
1/2 cup Mint Leaves
1/2 cup chopped Coriander
3 tsp Butter or Ghee
1 tsp Oil
Salt to taste

For masala : roasted and powdered

1 tsp Urad Dal
1 tsp Chana Dal
2 tsp Toor Dal
1 tsp Jeera
1 tsp Dhaniya
3 dried Red Chillies

Method :

  1. First Rajma is soaked overnight and pressure cooked with little salt in it.
  2. Drain all the excess water and set aside.
  3. In a large pan heat oil and ghee. Add bay leaves, cloves, shajeera, cinnamon and elaichi to it. Now add onions, chillies and ginger garlic paste and fry.
  4. Now add the diced tomato, cooked beans and mix well.
  5. Now add the freshly grounded powder and pre soaked rice to it. Fry them for 5 min. Add salt and mint leaves.
  6. Add the coconut milk and 2 1/2 cups of water to it and let it boil.
  7. Stir once again and cook in med.low and cover it. Now simmer it and check for every 5 min. till the rice is done. Add chopped coriander to it.
  8. Serve hot with roasted cashews on top.
