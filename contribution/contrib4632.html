Tomato Dosa

Ingredients

1 cup Maida Flour (All Purpose Flour)
1 cup Rice Flour
1 cup small Soji
1 Kg. Fresh Tomato.
100 grams of Onion
1" piece of fresh Ginger
1 bunch of fresh Coriander
1 tablespoon of Red Hot Chilli Powder
2 Green Chilies for seasoning.
A pinch of Hing (Asafoetida)
Salt to taste
One OZ fresh Lemon Juice
A pinch of Methi (Fenugreek Seeds)
Olive Oil for seasoning.
A spoon of Mustard seeds.

Method:

  1. Grind tomato along with half portion of ginger to fine paste adding sufficient water.
  2. Mix all the flours in a big bowl and add the tomato juice by mixing thoroughly till you get the dosa consistency. Add lemon juice and salt to taste.
  3. Chop onion very small and add the onion to the batter. Also cut one large tomato size 38C into very small pieces and add it to the batter.
  4. Mix the batter thoroughly to make certain that there are no unmixed flour. Also cut the remaining ginger piece into very tiny pieces and add it to the batter. Chop coriander leaves into tiny pieces and add it to the batter. Mix thoroughly into smooth consistency.
  5. Seasoning: In a small vessel, add one OZ of olive oil and when the oil is hot, add mustard seeds and a pinch of methi and after they are popped add hing and add that to the batter. Mix thoroughly. Let it set for two hours at the room temperature.
  6. Use a large thava and add a spoon of olive oil and when the thava is hot pour two ladles of the finely mixed batter smear it and cover the thava. After a few minutes when the dosa turns brown, turn it and let it cook on a medium flame.

Now the dosa is finished and put it on a plate and smear with ghee and the baby is ready to eat. It can be eaten alone or use coriander chutney to go with it. If you want more spicy, add more red chilly powder to appease your taste buds.
