Idli Upma

Ingredients

leftover idlis
onion - 2 or 3 medium size
curry leaves - few leaves
haldi pdr - 1/4th spoon
chilli pdr - one tspoon
salt - as per taste
coriander leaves - few

Method

  1. Powder the idlis with ur hands don't squash it make it bit like pieces.
  2. Then in a kadai heat oil, add mustard seeds after it splutters add urad dal ,curry leaves after that add onion after it fried for few mts.
  3. Then after that put the idlis and mix it well if u have idli chutney pdr.(moligai pudi) it is a south indian podi. If it is not there u can put chilli pdr as per ur taste.
  4. Then just sprinkle few drops of water and close the lid with some plate and after some time garnish with coriander leaves and eat it hot.
