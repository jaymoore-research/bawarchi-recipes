Prasadam

Ingredients

1 cup of RavaĀ [Semolina ]
1 cup of Sugar
1.5 cup of Milk
1 cups of Water
1 gm of crushed Cardamom powder
Dry fruits, if required (Almonds, Cashews -Raisins]
A pinch of Saffron
4-5 table spoons of pure ghee to fry the rava

Method

  1. Take the mentioned amount of rava in a deep thick vessel.
  2. add the mentioned amount of pure ghee without burning it.
  3. Roast it continuously in the vesselĀ till you smell
  4. Roast it forĀ (3-4 minutes) on a medium flame without burning the rava.
  5. Now add sugar and mix
  6. Now add the milk and waterĀ and keep stirringĀ [ to avoid lumps
  7. Add the cardamom powder, dry fruits, saffron & all on low flame
  8. Cover the vessel with a lid, so that the rava gets steam cooked & the liquid evaporates to form a solid mass and leaves the sides of the vessel..
  9. It is used as Prasadam in --[ Satyanarayana puja ] orĀ as a sweet dish
