Chakri

Ingredients

1 cup Sour Cream
3 cup Rice flour
Ginger, Green Chili (to taste), Salt (to taste), Sesame seeds, Ajwain seeds (optional).

Note: If you noticed, no oil or butter is used.

Method :

  1. Mix all together to make the soft dough (in case the dough comes out too soft, add more flour).
  2. Fill the dough in the sev machine using the chakri plate (which is the plate with one hole looks like the star).
  3. Press the machine to make chakri starting from the middle and circle around about 3 times to make a round shape.
  4. Slide them in the hot oil one at a time to fry till changes colour.

Note: You can make them on the plastic paper first instead of pressing them direct in the hot oil. Then lift one at a time from the paper and fry.
