Rawa Banana Delight

Ingredients

Rawa 1 cup
Sugar 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Ghee 1/2 cup (weight watchers can reduce it to 1/4 cup)
Ripe Banana (Green variety) cut into small bits 2 cups
Cashew nuts, cardamom powder

Method

  1. Mash the banana with sugar in a separate bowl, add the milk and whisk it nicely till it is thick
  2. Put the ghee and cashewnuts in a glass bowl and microwave (high) for 30 to 40 mts.
  3. Add the Rawa, mix well and microwave for another 1 minute or till the mixture bubbles.
  4. Add the banana, milk, sugar mixture, add cardamom and cover.
  5. Microwave for 3 mts, stopping in between to stir.

Cooking time depends on the heat of microwave.

Happy cooking!
