Brocolli

Ingredients

1 cup Moong Daal
1 small size Brocolli flower
2 table spoons oil
1 teaspoon mustard
1 teaspoon Jira
2 dried red chillies
1 stem of Curry Leaves
2 green Mirchis
3 to 4 Garlic cloves
Salt to taste

Method

  1. Pressure cook Daal along with brocolli cut in to pieces, green Mirchi and Garlic cloves.
  2. After two to three whistles, remove the cooker. Now for seasoning, in a skillet add oil and sputter mustard seeds and jira, also add dry chillies and curry leaves and the seasoning along with salt to the cooked Daal.
  3. Serve hot with chapathis or Rice.
