Mexican Rice Casserole

Ingredients

Cooked Rice - 1 cup
Rotel Chopped Tomatoes & Green chillies - 1 can
Salsa - 2 tbsp
Black Beans - 1 can [Optional]
Cumin Seeds - 1tsp
Cilantro Chopped fine - 1/4 cup
Grated Cheese - 1/2 cup
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 deg F
  2. Greese an ovenproof casserole.
  3. Mix all the ingredients except cheese and pour into the casserole
  4. Make 2 portions of the grated cheese and add one portion to the casserole.
  5. Bake for 15 mins until the casserole is hot and bubbly.
  6. Top with the remaining grated cheese and let the cheese melt.
  7. Serve hot with some beans, Corn chips and salsa on the side.
