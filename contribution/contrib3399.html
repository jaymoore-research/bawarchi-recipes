Poori Payasam

Ingredients

1) Rava 1 1/2 glass
2) Milk 1 litre
3) Sugar 1 1/2 cup
4) Cardamom 8 nos
5) Kesar powder 1/2 tsp
6) Oil for frying
7) Salt 1 pinch

Method:

  1. Add water and salt to rava and make dough.
  2. Spread the batter like poori and deep fry in oil.
  3. In another vessel, add milk and boil well. Add sugar, kesar powder and cardamom powder to the boiled milk and stir it. Remove from fire.
  4. Then break the poori into pieces and to the boiled milk.
  5. Now Poori Payasam is ready and everybody will enjoy.
