Chatapate Aloo

Ingredients

4 large Potatoes
1 Green Chili chopped
1 tsp Garlic finely chopped
1/2 tsp Mint leaves finely chopped (optional)
3 tsp Coriander leaves finely chopped
1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
1 tsp Chilli powder
1/2 tsp Coriander powder
1/2 tsp Cumin powder
1-1/2 tsp Lemon Juice
Salt to taste
4 tbsp Oil

Method:

  1. Peel the potatoes with the peeler & chop them into thin pieces. Soak them in the water as soon as you chop them.
  2. Heat the oil in a skillet or a heavy saucepan. Add chopped garlic & chilli, stirfry for � minute.
  3. Add potatoes & salt. Stir well & cook for 5 minutes. Stir often so that potatoes are cooked evenly.
  4. Add turmeric, chilli powder, coriander & cumin powder, lemon juice & stir well.
  5. Add mint & coriander leaves stir gently. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes or till oil separated.
  6. Garnish with freshly grated coconut.
  7. Serve hot with Puris, Rotis, Bread or Curd Rice.

Making Time - 20 min.
Make - 5-6 servings
Shelflife - 2 days (good for picnics)
