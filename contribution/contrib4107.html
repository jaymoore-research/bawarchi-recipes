Red Pumpkin Race

Ingredients

1. Red Pumpkin one large piece (about 250 -300 gms)
2. Tamrind : one large lemon size (say 100 gms)
3. Sambhar powder 2 table spoons
4. Thuvar Dal 1 tbl.spoon
5. Chana dal 1 tbl.spoon
6. Urad dal : 1 tea spoon
7. Methi seeds 1/2 tea spoon
8. Rai (kadugu)/ mustard seeds : 1/2 tea spoon
9. Turmeric powder :1/2 tea spoon
10. Hing- asafoetida : 1/5tea spoon
11. Fresh Curry Leaves
12. Salt to taste.

Most important Gingly Oil (til ka thel) two /three table spoons. If not available any cooking oil. But not ghee!

Optional Chinna Vengayam (Sambahr Onions 100 grams. Or small onions about 15 (the regular ones pick smallest from your vegetable vendor)

Method:

  1. Cut pumpkin into 1/2 inch pieces (do remove the skin)
  2. Peel onions and remove the crowns and tails.
  3. Soak tamarind in hot water (1 & 1/2 big glasses) with a little bit salt so that it soaks fast and more juice could be extracted. Extract the juice (as thick as possible).
  4. In a thick bottomed vessel or kadai heat til oil and add mustard seeds and when it splutters add thuvar dal, chana dal, urad dal and methi seeds. And allow them to turn golden brown. Add in a portion of the curry leaves, onions and saute for a few minutes, when the onions are done add the pumpkin pieces and cover the vessel for a few minutes.
  5. Add hing powder and sambhar powder when the pumpkin is cooked (fried) and fry the powder. If required add littile oil (one or two tea spoons). When you get the aroma of the sambhar powder add the tamarind juice and turmeric powder and add little (1/2 cup) water and allow the whole mixture to boil for about ten to fifteen minutes. add little more water (if required) and salt to taste. Ensure that the pumpkin pieces are very soft before you add the salt. When the "race" is boiling add the remaining curry leaves and simmer until the "race" comes to an even consistency.
  6. Serve with steaming plain rice.

Note: If you are using regular onions if they are very large you can slice them into four pieces. In which case you should use only two big onions, if the onions are very large cut them into small pieces.

Serves six to eight adults. Preparation time: 30 -40 minutes. Happy eating.
