Corn Halwa

Ingredients

One corn ear
2-3 green chilli
1 punch of coriander
1 large onion
2 Tomato
1 cup semolina(Rava)
1 cup hot water
salt red chilli powder
1/2 tsp sugar
fieid peas
mustard seed
grount nuts 30-40 quntity
oil

Method:

  1. Cut the corn butta on the khisani which we use to cut the coconut into vertical form
  2. Add oil into fry pan
  3. Add the above ingredients fry for 5-10 mintues then add rava salt sugar fry sill it will brown then add hot water
  4. Put in dish, add the coriander
  5. Serve with tomato sauce
