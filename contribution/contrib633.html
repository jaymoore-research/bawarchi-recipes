Chatpatey Aloo

Ingredients

500 gms Potato
2 Onions
Greeen Chillies (I leave it to you)
Redchillies crushed 3
Chat Masala 2tsp
Saunf 1 1/2 tsp
Jeera 1 tsp
Juice of half a lemon
Oil
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Heat oil add jeera, red chillies and saunf.
  2. Then add chopped onions with the green chillies.
  3. When the onions turn golden add potatoes cut in big chunks.
  4. Add very little water, salt, mix well, cover and cook till potatoes are almost done.
  5. Remove the lid and add more oil and roast the potatoes.
  6. When roasted well add chat masala and lemon juice, mix well and remove from the gas stove.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves.

PS: Tastes just great as a snack also.
