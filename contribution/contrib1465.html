Chana Masala

Ingredients

1 cup of Kabuli Chana,
1/2 cup chopped Onions,
2 Tomatoes,
3 Potatoes,
Little Ghee
Salt to taste,
3 - 4 tbsp. Coriander (dhania) powder,
3 tbsp. of Dry mango (amchur),
Salt to taste,
3-4 Red chillies,
2 tbsp. Jeera (cumin seeds)
1 tbsp. Methi seeds,
Little Dalchini (cinnamon bark)
1 tbsp. Black Pepper,
A pinch of Dry Ginger
1/2 tbsp. of Elaichi (cardamom)
1/2 tbsp. Cloves powder
1/2 tbsp. of Mustard seeds
Little Caraway

Method :

  1. First grind all the ingredients except vegetables into powder form.
  2. Soak Chanas overnight or at least for 7 hours.
  3. Add 1/2 tbsp. cooking soda and pressure cook till done.
  4. Heat ghee in vessel and fry cut potatoes till soft.
  5. In the same ghee add onions and cook for some time.
  6. Add pieces of Tomatoes and cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Add the ground powder of masala into it and cook for 2 minutes.
  8. After that add precooked chanas and cook for 5-10 minutes stirring continuously.
  9. Decorate with sliced tomatoes and ring onions or sliced ones and whole green chillies, cut coriander leaves and slices of lemon.
