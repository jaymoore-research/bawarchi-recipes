Sweet Potato and Capcicum rice

Ingredients

2 cups Basmathi Rice
1 Sweet Potato (medium) cut into cubes
1 Green Bell Pepper
1 Onion sliced
2 tbsp Groundnuts

Powder

2 Tbsp Channa Dal
1 Tbsp Urad Dal
2 Tbsp Dhania
2 Tbsp Dry Coconut
2 Red Chilly

Method :

  1. Fry the above ingredients in little oil and powder them.
  2. Cook rice
  3. Heat 2 Tbsp of oil in a pan and season with mustard, channa dal, urad dal, curry leaves & Hing.
  4. Add groundnuts and fry until golden.
  5. Add onions and fry until golden.
  6. Cook sweet potatoes seperately.
  7. Add the cut bell pepper and cook until soft.
  8. Add the cooked sweet potatoes & the ground masala powder.
  9. Add 2 Tbsp of salt & a pinch of sugar.
  10. Mix with the cooked rice & garnish with coriander leaves.
  11. Serve hot with Onion Raitha & Papad.
