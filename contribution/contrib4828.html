Farzana Ka Taj

Ingredients

1 cup curd beaten
1 cup milk
1 tin (400 gms) condensed milk
5 almonds sliced
10 pistachio nuts sliced
5 raisins
2 cups water

Method

  1. Beat together curd, milk and condensed milk till well blended.
  2. Add almonds, pistachio and raisins. Mix well.
  3. Pour the mixture into a 1 litre pudding mold. Cover mold tightly with aluminium foil securely tied.
  4. Pour water into a pressure cooker. Put grind in cooker. Place mold on grind.
  5. Close cooker.Bring to full pressure on high heat. Reduce heat and cook for 20 minutes.
  6. Remove cooker from heat. Allow to cool naturally.
  7. Open cooker. Take out and uncover mold. Allow to cool to room temperature. Drain off water if any. Refrigerate untill well chilled (approximately 5 hours)
  8. Place a beautiful serving dish on top of mold and invert. Shake gently to release.Remove mold. Serve cold.

Recipe secret: You can add little cardamon powder in step 1 to add flavour to this desert.

Serves 6
