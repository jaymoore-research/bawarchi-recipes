Baked Fish

Ingredients

Any fleshy Fish - 1 lb
Yogurt - 1 cup (hung to drain out the excess fluid)
Salt - according to taste
Ginger- Garlic paste - 1 tbsp
Chilli powder - 1/2 tsp
Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp
Crushed Black Pepper - 1/2 tsp
Lemon Juice - 2 tbsp

Method:

  1. In a bowl, combine all the items except the fish.
  2. Blend well and coat the fish pieces with this mixture. Keep it marinated for an hour atleast.
  3. Preheat the oven to 350 Centigrade. Bake fishes for 45 minutes turning once in between.
  4. Serve hot with lemon juice and coriander leaves.

This recipe has very low fat because it is a baked dish with no oil or butter.
