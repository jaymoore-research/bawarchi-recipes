Orange Kheer

Ingredients

Milk 1 litre
Sugar 150 gms
Oranges 2 medium size
Kesar 1/2 tsp

Method:

  1. Peel the oranges. Also remove the transparent skin from the orange slices. Keep aside.
  2. Put the milk in a kadai keep boiling and stirring till the milk becomes thick, add the sugar. Keep boiling till the color of the milk changes. Once done remove the milk from the flame. Cool for 5 mins.
  3. Now add the orange and kesar.
  4. Cool for at least 2 hrs before serving.
