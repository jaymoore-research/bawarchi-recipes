Tomato-Dates Chutney

Ingredients

1/2 kg Tomatoes,
3/4 cup Dates (Khajur)
1/2 cup Kismis,
1 tsp. (Cinnamon, Clove & Black pepper powder)
1/2 cup Vinegar,
3/4 cup Sugar

Method:

  1. Put tomatoes in boild water for 2 - 3 minutes, then remove the skin of tomatoes and make a paste with hand.
  2. Mix sugar in vinegar and keep it on slow flame till sugar gets dissolved, then put masala powder and kismis into it.
  3. Remove the seeds of dates and make small pieces of it and add it to the above mixer alongwith tomato paste, stir continuously. Keep it on slow flame till mixer gets thick.
  4. Enjoy this chutney with Paratha or Chapati.
