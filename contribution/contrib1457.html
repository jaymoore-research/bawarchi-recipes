Mixed Vegetables Sag

Ingredients

Onions - 2
Chow - Chow - 1/2,
Carrots - 2
Beans - 100 gms.
Potatoes - 2
Peas little,
Cauliflower - 1/2
Salt to taste,
Oil - 3 tbsp.

Grind together :

Coconut - 1/2 scraped
Green chillies - 10
Coriander leaves - 1 big bunch
Ginger - 1 pinch
Roasted Gram - 1 tbsp

Method :

  1. Cut onions finely.
  2. Dice all the vegetables, and add enough water and cook in pressure cooker.
  3. Heat oil in a pan, season with mustard seeds, black gram dhal, bengal gram dhal and curry leaves.
  4. Add onions and fry till translucent.
  5. Add the ground mixture fry for a minute, mix all the vegetables with enough water and salt.
  6. Cook for 10 - 15 minutes or till the gravy thickens.
  7. Serve hot with puris or idlis.
