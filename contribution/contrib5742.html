Hot Chili Garlic Sauce

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients:

10 dry red chilies
2Āchopped red tomatoes
5 cloves of garlic
1 big piece of ginger
1/2 celery stick
1/2 cup chopped onions
1 tbsp soy sauce
1 tbsp oil
1 tbsp corn starch(mixĀlittle water)
Salt as per taste

Ā Method:

  1. Mix all the above ingredients in a pan add some water and cook inĀthe medium flame until cooked.
  2. Grind it and make a smooth puree.
  3. Heat the pan, pour this puree and add the corn starch and simmer until sauce thickens.Ā

Useful Tips:

  1. Use plastic gloves while working with chilies to protect your hands.
  2. Always chop onions from the top, not the root end, or else you will cry.
  3. To cure baldness, rub head with the onion.
  4. If anyone suffers from motion sickness, and while traveling (air, land, sea),Āchew fresh ginger before and while traveling.
  5. Do not allow garlic to brown or burn,burned garlic has an acrid flavor that permeates an entire dish of food.
