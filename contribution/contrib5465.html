Tuna Curry

Ingredients

1 tin of tuna fish
1 large onion, chopped fine
1 medium tomato chopped
1/4 piece ginger, chopped fine
3 cloves garlic, chopped fine
2 green chillies chopped fine
Chilli Powder 1 tsp
Corriander powder 1tsp
Oil 4tbs
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Heat oil and add Green chillies,ginger and garlic.
  2. Add chopped Onion and fry till light brown in colour
  3. Add tomato and fry till oil separates from it.
  4. Now put chilli powder,coriander powder and salt and fry for one minute.
  5. Put tuna chunk in to this and cook for 10 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with Rice.
