Tomato Coconut Curry

Ingredients

4 tomatoes (medium)
1 cup fresh grated coconut
4 green chilies
1 onion
1/2 tsp cumin seeds
2 red chilies
2 tsp coriander powder
3 pinches turmeric powder
4 - 5 curry leaves

Method:

  1. Grind the coconut, sliced onion, red chili powder, 1 pinch turmeric powder & cumin seeds together well.
  2. Chop the tomatoes & the green chilies into small pieces (long pieces). Add the paste to the cut tomatoes, add 2 cups of water to it, the coriander powder, curry leaves & 2 pinches of turmeric powder.
  3. Cook till the tomatoes are totally cooked & smashed. (If wanted add more chillies for spicy curry).

Serve with steaming plain rice.
