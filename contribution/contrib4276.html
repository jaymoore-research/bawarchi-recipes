Papaya Pickle

Ingredients

200 gm Young Yellow Papaya, skinned & sliced thinly
100 gm Tomatoes, skinned, seeded & diced
20 gm Red Onion, diced
300 ml White Wine Vinegar
30 gm Sugar
150 ml Water

Method:

Put all the ingredients together in a bowl. Mix well. Allow to pickle by leaving the bowl in the fridge for atleast one day before eating.
