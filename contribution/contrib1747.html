Coconut Burfi

Ingredients

Coconut 2 cups (grated)
Sugar 2 cups
Milk 2 tbsp
Ghee 2 tsp
Elaichi 3-4 crushed
Raisins/Nuts few

Method:

  1. Heat ghee in a kadai & then fry the coconut till it becomes light brown in colour.
  2. Then add the sugar & keep stirring until the whole mixture becomes soft. this will take 20-30 minutes.
  3. Then add the milk & again stir for another 15 minutes.
  4. Add the crushed elaichi & raisins/nuts. Mix very well.
  5. In the mean time, grease a tray & then pour the the mixture once it is done, nicely pat it.
  6. Cut it in any shape u want & coconut Barfi is done.
