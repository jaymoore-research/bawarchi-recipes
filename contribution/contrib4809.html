Malai Kofta

Ingredients

Kofta:
3 medium potatoes.50gm
paneer,
2-3 green chillies,
20gm cashewnuts,
1 tsp chopped corriander leaves,
3tbsp ghee,
3tbsp gramflour,
salt

Gravy:
2 flakes garlic,
2 tsp ginger,
1/2 cup coconut,
1 onion,
1red chilli,
1" cinnamom,
6 cloves,
6 cardamoms,
1 tsp poppy seeds,
150ml milk,
salt,
ghee,
oil for fry

Method:

Kofta:

  1. Boil the potatoes, peel and smash them.
  2. Grind the cashewnut in the mixergrinder.
  3. Mix smash potatoes,cashewnut paste, gratted paneer, chopped greenchilly, corrianderleaves,salt.
  4. Heat ghee in a frying pan in a low flame cook the mixer until it become almost dry.
  5. Now shape them like balls.
  6. Make a paste of gramflour,salt and water.
  7. Dip the balls in the paste and fry them deep till golden brown.
  8. Keep this koftas in casserole.

Gravy:

  1. Fry: chopped- garlic, ginger, onion, coconut, cinnamom, cloves, cardamoms, red chilly one by one .
  2. Now mix all these and poppy seeds in a mixer grinder.
  3. Heat ghee fry this mixed ingredient for 10-15 min. If it sticks add milk.

Note:- Pour hot gravy on the koftas, just before serve.
