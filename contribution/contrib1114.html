Baked Beet Root with Yogurt Walnut Dressing

Ingredients

4-5 medium size beet roots washed.
1 tbs. Butter
2 Cups beaten Yogurt
3/4 cup Walnuts roasted and chopped
1 -2 green chilies finely chopped
2 tbs. Coriander leaves finely chopped
Salt to taste

Method :

Step 1

  1. Take the washed beet roots and pierce holes with fork or tooth pick.
  2. Take foil and grease with butter
  3. Fold the beet root and bake in the pre heated oven for 30 minutes or till done. Let it cool open the foil and peel the beet root. Cut into 4 parts make sure the beet root is in tact and not separated into 4 pieces.

Step 2 ( Dressing)

In a bowl mix the yogurt, add salt, finely chopped green chilies and 1/2 the coriander leaves and walnuts and chill for 1/2 hour.

Step 3

Before serving place the Beet Roots in a broad serving dish. Spoon the dressing and garnish with coriander and red chilies.

Serves: 4.
