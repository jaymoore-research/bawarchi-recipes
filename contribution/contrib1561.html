Dry Aaloo Baingain Sabji

Ingredients

1/2 kg Baingan (Brinjal)
1/4 kg Potato
2 tbs Coriander Seeds (dhaniya)
2 tbs Channa dal
Red Chilli
Pinch of Amchur
Pinch of Haldi
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Dry Roast the coriander, channa dal and chilli till the channa is a little brown. Grind in a mixer and keep aside.
  2. Cut the baingan and peeled potato into small cubes and keep aside separately.
  3. Put little oil in a frying pan and put the potato in it. Add little water. Just enough to cook.
  4. Once the potato is half cooked add the baingan, salt and haldi to it. Add water if necessary.
  5. After the potato is cooked well and the water is reduced add the ground dry masala alongwith amchur and mix well.

Serve with Chapati or Paratha.
