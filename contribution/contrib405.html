Doi Maachh (Curd Fish)

Ingredients

Rohu Fish or Salmon Fish -1kg
Doi or Yoghurt -500 gm
Onion paste -150 gm
Ginger paste-2 tbsps
Salt -2 tsps
Raisin -50 gm
Turmeric powder -2 tsps
Chilli powder -1 tsp
Cinnamon stick & Cardamom -10
Sugar -1 tbsp
Oil -100 ml

Method

  1. Clean & cut the fish into pieces according to your choice and marinate with salt & turmeric powder for half an hour.
  2. Heat the oil in a pan and shallow fry the fish pieces upside down and keep aside.
  3. In the meantime, beat the Yoghurt with 1/4 tsp salt and 1 tbsp sugar smoothly. Again heat the oil and put the whole spices, Cinnamon & Cardamoms, and add Onion, ginger paste, turmeric & chilli powder.
  4. Fry for sometime until the oil separates from the paste. Add beaten yoghurt and mix properly.
  5. Add the fish pieces & raisins.
  6. Cover the pan for sometime and cook until the curry thickens.
  7. Serve with plain Basmati rice.
