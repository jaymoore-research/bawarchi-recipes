Badam Halwa

Ingredients

Almond paste - 2 cups
Sugar - 2 cups
Ghee - 1 cup
Saffron strands - a pinch
Milk - as required for the paste
Green Cardamom - 10

Making almond paste:

Boil the almonds for 5 minutes and blanch them. Add saffron and blend to a smooth paste adding as little milk as possible.

Method:

  1. Put almond paste and sugar in a thick bottomed pan, stir frequently and cook till the mixture thickens and leaves the sides of the pan. Add powdered cardamom and remove from fire.
  2. Now add the ghee little by little till all the ghee is absorbed.
  3. Cool to room temperature and serve. You can store this in the fridge for a few days.
