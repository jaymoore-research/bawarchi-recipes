Ridge Gourd Chutney

Ingredients

Ridge Gourd - 1
Mustard Seeds (Rai)
Chana Dal - 2 tbsp
Urad Dal - 2 tbsp
Green Chillies - 4 to 5
Ginger
Coriander leaves
Curry leaves few
Grated fresh coconut - 1 tbsp
A small piece of tamarind ( paste or powder )
Salt
Oil - 2 tbsp

Method :

  1. Remove the extreme ridges of the ridge gourd and cut it into 1" pieces.
  2. Heat oil, add mustard seeds and let it splutter.
  3. Add Chana Dal and Urad Dal and let them brown a little.
  4. Add slit green chillies, ginger, the leaves and the cut pieces of the gourd and let it cook till the vegetable softens.
  5. Cool the mixture, add coconut, salt, tamarind and grind to a paste.

This chutney goes well with Rice, Chapati, Dosas, Idlis and Pakoras too.
