Vegetable Cutlet

Ingredients

Boiled and mashed potatoes : 1/4 KG
Finely cut carrots, beans, cauliflower, peas & capsicum: 100G each
Coriander leaves, garlic & ginger paste: to your taste(optional)
Chilli powder or Green chilli & salt: to your taste
Garam Masala: optional
Bread crumbs
White Flour (Maida) mix with :100ml water
Oil: 50ml
Chopped Onion: 1

Method:

  1. Fry the chopped onion in the pan with little oil.
  2. Add cut vegetables and fry for 5 minutes in sim
  3. Add mashed potatoes, salt, chill, masala powders & coriander leaves, garlic &ginger; paste and make it like a dough.
  4. Make the dough into small balls like making balls for chapptis.
  5. Dip each ball in Maida-Water mix and then roll all balls in bread crumbs.
  6. After that roll all balls in a little oil if you want.
  7. Put all balls in roasting sheet and put in the oven in 400F,for 45minutes or until golden brown.
  8. It can also be made by deep fry in oil,if you like.You can also turn balls into different shapes before heat, if you like.

It can be served with Tomato sauce or Puthina cutney.
