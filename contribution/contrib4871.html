Chicken Roast

Ingredients

1.Chicken breast
or the whole chicken
(cut into pieces) ----- 300 gms .
2.Red chilly Powder ------ 3 tsp.
3.Turmeric Powder ------ 2 pinch.
4.Garam Masala ------ 2 tsp.
5.Soya sauce(Optional) ------ 2 tsp.
6.Ginger & Garlic paste ------ 3 tsp.
7.Salt for taste ------- As required.
8.Chicken stock(optional) ------- Knorr chicken stock. 1 cube mixed in 2 glasses of water.
9.Oil

Method:

  1. Wash the chicken very well and marinate the chicken with Ginger and Garlic paste,Red chilly powder,Turmeric powder,Garam masala powder,Salt for nearly 1 hour.
  2. Pour 3 tsp of oil in a deep kadhai,after the oil gets heated up,put the chicken which was marinated into the kadhai, keep frying well for about 5 min.
  3. Then add the soya sauce to it and keep frying for about 10 min.
  4. Let the chicken be fried until it turns to brown in colour.
  5. Now add the chicken stock(which gives an additional taste) or water(2 glasses).
  6. Let the chicken stock or the water gets dried and fry again.
  7. Now your chicken roast is ready to without any gravy left,serve hot,can season it with the coriander leaves.

Chicken Roast ,goes well with fried rice,Biryani,Pulav and simply delicious with white rice and sambhar.

For those,who did sambhar and feel like to have a quick dry tastoer non-veg side-dish,Chicken Roast is the good suit for it,ENJOYYYYYYYYYY.

Cooking Time : 15 min.
