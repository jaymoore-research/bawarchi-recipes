Bread Kofta

Ingredients

Bread slice 8
Milk
Onion 1 (chopped)
Potatos 2 (boiled)
Green Chillies 2-3
Garam Masala powder - 1/2 tsp
Coriander Leaves (finely chopped)
Salt
Oil for frying

Method:

  1. Dip the bread slices in the milk and smash the slices very well.
  2. Add the smashed potatoes, onions, Green chillies, coriander leaves and garam masala to the bread and mix it well.
  3. Make small balls of the mixture.
  4. Heat the oil in a kadai and fry the balls in the oil.
  5. Serve hot with Coconut Chutney or Tomato Kethcup.

Preparation Time: 5 minutes (except boiling)
Cooking Time: 10 minutes
