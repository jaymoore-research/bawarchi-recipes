Mircha Nu Shak

Ingredients

3 bell peppers (I use 2 green and 1 red for some color) - chopped
3/4 cup peanuts
1/2 cup chana (chick pea) flour
1 tbsp. shredded unsweetened coconut
1 tsp. lal mirchu
1 1/2 tsp. garam masala
1/2 tsp. urdhur (haldi)
1 tbsp. salt
2 dashes hing (asofoetida)
5 tbsp. Oil
1 tsp. sugar

Method:

  1. Chop peanuts in a blender until you have a combination of powder and some small chunks
  2. In a large bowl, mix chopped peanuts with flour, coconut, lal mirchu, garam masa, urdhur, salt and sugar.
  3. Add chopped bell peppers to the bowl and coat thoroughly with the peanut mixture.
  4. Heat 3 tbsp. oil and hing in a microwave safe bowl until you hear hing sizzle.
  5. Transfer coated bell peppers and remaining mixture to oil and hing.
  6. Pour 1 tbsp. Oil over the bell peppers.
  7. Cover bowl with plastic seran wrap and microwave on high for 4 minutes.
  8. Take out, stir, add 1 tsp. more oil on top of bell peppers and microwave for another 3 minutes.

Ready to serve (will generally serve 4)
This usually goes well served with roti or puri.
Enjoy!
