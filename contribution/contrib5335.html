Sweet Bread Balls

Ingredients

1. Bread(any size)- one pack (Remove the sides)
2. Sugar - 2tbsp (more if u want it sugary)
3. Nuts (cashew) - 1/2 cup
4. Elachi(if u wish) - a pinch of powder
5. Water

Method:

  1. Make the bread into very small pieces.
  2. Add all the above ingredients into it except water.
  3. Add a little water just to make it a dough.
  4. Keep for 15-20 min.
  5. Heat oil in a vessel.
  6. Make small balls out of this dough & put in the vessel.
  7. Fry till it gets brownish. That's it.
  8. Here is a tasty dish. I bet none will guess it's bread.
  9. Try this out & let me know if u like it.
