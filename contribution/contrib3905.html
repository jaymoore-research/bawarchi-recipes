Birthday Biscuit Malai

Ingredients

Marie Biscuit - 1 ( packet)
Chocolate - 10
Polo - 1 (packet)
Round Porri - 1 packet.
Stick - 1

Method:

  1. First cover the stick with design colour paper.
  2. Then take equal size of three pieces thread and tied with that design stick.
  3. Tie three needles in 3 pieces of thread.
  4. Start to do malai.
  5. First porri, next biscuit and chocolate. Then atlast polo, so one side is finished. Start other sides like this.
