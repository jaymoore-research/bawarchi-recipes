Bread Recipes

1) Spicy Bread Chutney :

Ingredients

2-3 Slices of Bread.
1 Onion
1 Tomato
A little Tamarind
Salt to taste
4 Green chillies, Coconut Powder or scrapings
Small bunch of Cilantro (Coriander Leaves)
Ghee or Oil for frying
Half a spoon Mustard Seeds.

Method:

  1. Make small pieces of bread slices. Fry these in ghee or oil. Keep aside these pieces until they cool.
  2. Heat mustard seeds in oil until the seeds crackle. Add a pinch of asfoetida, tomato pieces and also the onion slices, followed by cilantro.
  3. Fry all these with the coconut scrapings. Keep aside for a little while.
  4. Add the fried pieces of bread to the above mixture.
  5. Grind the whole mixture for 2 or 3 minutes (till becomes a smooth paste).
  6. Add salt to taste and a little tamarind.

Now the 'spicy bread chutney' is ready to be served with Hot Puris, Chapatis, Dosas, Idlis or also with Rice. This can also be done with the leftover bread in the kitchen.

2) Yummy Halwa:

Ingredients :

One Loaf Bread.
10 tsps Pure Ghee
10 tsp Sugar
1/2 litre Milk
1/4 kg Khoya
50 gm Raisins
50 gm Cashewnuts
25 gms chopped Walnuts
2 tsp Rose Water
1/4 tsp Saffron

Method

  1. Break bread slices into approx 1" squares.
  2. Heat ghee in a large frying pan.
  3. Fry raisins and cashewnuts, remove and keep aside.
  4. To the same ghee, add bread squares and fry till golden brown.
  5. Mix khoya and milk together to a smooth consistency and add to the fried bread, stir well and cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Add sugar, raisins, cashenuts and saffron ground in rose water. Blend well and cook for 2 minute. Remove from fire, cool and garnish with chopped walnuts.

Enjoy your halwa!!

Serves 10
Preparation time : 20 minutes
