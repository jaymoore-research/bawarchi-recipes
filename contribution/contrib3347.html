Ghee Rice

Ingredients

Basmati Rice 1 cup
Onion 2 sliced
Tomato 1 sliced
Garlic 5 flakes sliced
Green Chillies 3 sliced
Coconut milk powder 6 table spoon
Cinnamon 1 inch
Cloves 1
Water 2 cup or less.
Curry leaves small amt.
Ghee 2 teaspoon.
Oil 2 teaspoon

Method:

  1. Soak the rice in normal water for 15 minutes.
  2. Prepare coconut milk with the 2 cups of water. Set it aside.
  3. Take a frying pan, pour 2 teaspoon of ghee & slightly fry the soaked rice.
  4. Take pressure cooker, pour oil fry cinnamon, cloves, then add onion & garlic and fry it.
  5. Add green chillies, tomatoes, curry leaves & fry it.
  6. Add the coconut milk, add salt to taste & allow it to boil.
  7. Now add the fried rice & cook for 1 whistle.
  8. Serve it with Chips & Raita.
