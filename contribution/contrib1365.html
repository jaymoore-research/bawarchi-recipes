Chalna

Ingredients

Shredded coconut - 1 cup
Green chilli - 2
Ginger and garlic paste - 1 teaspoon
Onion - 1 (cut into small pieces)
Jeera - 1 teaspoon
Garam masala - 1 teaspoon
Turmeric powder - 1/4 teaspoon
Oil
Salt

Method:

  1. Grind shredded coconut, green chillies and ginger garlic paste into a fine paste.
  2. Add oil in a pan and add jeera to split. Then add the onion and fry them until it becomes golden brown.
  3. Then add the grinded paste, garam masala, salt, turmeric and add a little water.
  4. Allow it to boil till the oil separates.

Your chalna is ready. You can serve this with any dosa.
