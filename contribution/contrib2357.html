Potato Pizza

Ingredients

2 Potatoes
2-3 chopped Green Chilies (as per your taste)
1 tsp Chili Powder
1 tsp Cumin seeds
1 finely chopped Green Pepper (capsicum i.e simla mirchi)
1 finely chopped Tomato
Grated Cheese
Coconut (optional)
Salt to taste.

Method:

  1. Wash & grate the potatoes. (Do not wash them again after grating)
  2. Add salt, cumin seeds & chilli powder to it.
  3. Take a flat frying pan (Tava). Rub some oil on it.
  4. Spread grated potatoes on the pan. Press it with the flat surface of bowl (Vaati) so that it makes a firm base.
  5. Now mix rest of the ingredients listed above & spread this mixture on this base.
  6. Cover for 3-4 minutes.
  7. Now remove the lid. Fold the pizza (like a Karanji) & serve with tomato ketchup.

This is a very quick snack. Substitute your morning breakfast or a tea time snack with this.
