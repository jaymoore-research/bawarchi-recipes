Tomato Dosa

Ingredients

Ponni rice (pacharisi) - 1 measure
Moong dal (pasiparuppu) - 1/2 measure
Tomatoes - 3 to 4 big
Green chillies - 2
Ginger - a small piece
Hing/perungayam - a small chunk
Salt to taste
Cilantro and curry leaves for garnishing.

Method:

  1. Soak the ponni rice and moong dal for at least 5-6 hours.
  2. In the blender first grind tomatoes, chillies, hing, and ginger.
  3. Keep it aside.
  4. Then grind the rice and dal mixture until fine.
  5. Make sure you don't add too much water while grinding.
  6. To this, add the pureed tomato mixture and blend well.
  7. Add the salt to it at this stage.
  8. Keep the batter in the fridge for a good one hour, and then pour it over the tava like any dosa.
  9. This tastes good with chilly powder or a green chutney.

Enjoy the dosa!!!!
