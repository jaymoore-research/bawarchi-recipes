Spinach Pockets

Ingredients

1. Whole spinach leaves (washed thoroughly)
2. Finely grated Ginger
3. Small pieces of Tofu lightly fried.
4. Grated Copra - Not fresh coconut - (lightly roasted)
5. Roasted Peanuts
6. Finely chopped Onions
7. Very small pieces of Lemon
8. Tamarind juice (you could use the hot and sweet chutney that you get in Indian stores instead)

Method

  1. Take a spinach leaf, fold it to make a pocket (just like you would do for samosas).
  2. Now put a little of all the other ingredients into this pocket.
  3. Well, there's only one step left now. You got to pop the pocket into your mouth and relish it.

You could try a few variations of your own if you like. I think Spinach Pockets would make a healthy party food. What say you?
