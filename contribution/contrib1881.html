Rice Kurmuras

Ingredients

Cooked Rice (leftover perhaps a dinner party) 10 cups
Red chillies 10 -15
Pepper 2-3 tsp
Baking soda 1 tsp
Salt

Method:

  1. Grind all the ingredients together will very less water.
  2. Once the paste is ready, put it into the chakli maker. Take a thin long cloth or polythin sheet. Slowly make the chaklis on the cloth, but very small circles.
  3. Let it dry in the sun for a week. Once totally dried store it in air tight containers. It can remain for many months. When ever required fry in hot oil & serve as snacks along with tea or even with rice like papad.
