Panzeri

Ingredients

100 gm Dry Goondh
200 gm Phulmakane
Badam
Desi Ghee or Butter
You can vary amount of dry fruits

Method:

  1. Firstly grind goondh, badam and phulmakane separetly.
  2. Take one spoon ghee in pan and saute goondh first, remeber do it at very low flame and stir it.
  3. Then take out goondh in plate and put two spoon of ghee and put phulmakhane and saute then add goondh and badam in it and stir it continuosly.
  4. When it starts giving good fragrence and it turns little brown then switch off flame and add sugar according to your taste. So panzeri is ready for eating.

You can store it as long as you want it has good medicinal properties. So try it write me feedback.
