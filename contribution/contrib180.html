Rainbow Sandwich

Ingredients

1 cup gram flour
4 slice bread
green chutney
tomato ketchup
2tbs whipping cream
half tsp cane sugar
salt as per taste
2 pinch turmeric
oil for deep frying

Method

  1. Add water in gram flour and make batter of dosa consistency. add salt and turmeric in the batter.
  2. Add sugar in cream, mix well and keep aside.
  3. Take one slice of bread and spread chutney over it and cover it with the second slice of bread.
  4. Spread cream on top of the second slice and cover it with the third slice.
  5. Spread tomato ketchup on top of third slice and cover it with the fourth slice.
  6. Dip these slices in the batter of gram flour and deep fry in oil.
  7. Cut the sandwich diagonally and serve.
