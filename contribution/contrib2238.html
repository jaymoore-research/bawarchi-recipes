Leftover Rice

Ingredients

2 cups Leftover Rice
1 tbspn peeled Garlic pods
2 green chillies slit lengthwise 1/2 a cup Coconut gratings
A sprig of Curry Patta leaves
2 tbsp Ghee (clarified butter)
A tsp of Black Gram dal ; a tsp of Mustard seeds.

Method :

  1. Heat ghee in a thick bottomed pan, add blackgram dal, when slightly brown, add mustard seeds.
  2. When mustard begins to splutter, add curry leaves and slit green chillies, and garlic pods and fry till it is brown.
  3. Add the leftover rice, 3/4th tsp salt and stir over a slow flame for about 5 minutes.
  4. Keep down from the fire and add the coconut gratings, and mix well.
  5. Serve hot.
