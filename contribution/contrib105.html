Thakkali Kozhambu (Tomato Dish)

Ingredients

5 medium sized tomatoes.
1 large onion.
4-5 dry red chillies.
5-6 spoons of grated coconut.
Grated ginger.
Garlic (optional).
Cumin seeds, mustard seeds , fenugreek seeds and coriander seeds for seasoning.
a piece of asafoetida.
curry and corainder leaves.
Salt to taste.

Method

  1. Cut Onions.
  2. In a vessel, heat oil and toss in mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, cumin seeds and coriander seeds.
  3. After seasoning, add cut onions and fry them till the onions appear light brown.
  4. Add tomatoes and red chillies, pour 4-5 cups of water and allow the mixture to boil for 10 - 15 minutes.
  5. Add ginger, asafoetida and grated coconut into the mixture and leave it on low flame for about 5 minutes.
  6. After removing the pan from the flame, add curry and coriander leaves.

Goes well with rice but there is no hard and fast rule. Cooking is interesting when we are creative. Feel free to eat it with Dosai, Idli or any other suitable dish of your choice.
