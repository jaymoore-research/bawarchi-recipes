Sabudhana Bonda

Ingredients

1 Cup of Sabudhana (they call it as Sabakki, Javrasi etc.,)
1 Cup Rice Flour
3/4 Rava (suji)

Method :

  1. Soak the above ingredients in the curd or yougurt in the morning if you are going to do it in evening.
  2. While doing bonda cut green chillies into small pieces, coariander leaves, curryleaves and hing and salt. Mix this with the soaked one and make it into small balls.
  3. Keep the oil in tava and boil after the oil is boiled put the balls in the oil and deep fry it.

You can make 30 bondas. If you want you can serve it with chutney also.
