Hi, I am Krithika Rajgopal and I am a housewife, living in California. Thohayal is a basic south Indian recipe usually made out of grated coconuts or vegetables like onion, brinjal, chow-chow. The one I am going to share with you is with beetroot. This is a dry dish to be mixed with rice and accompanied by a kadi. It can be kept in the refrigerator for 2-3 days.

Thohayal

Ingredients

Peeled, washed and grated beets - 2 cups
Tamarind - size of a betel nut
Salt according to taste
Seasoning
Urad dal - 4 tsps
Red chillies - 1-2 medium size according to the hotness required
pinch of asafoetida (hing) powder

Method

  1. Take little oil in a kadai and fry the dal till golden brown in colour.
  2. In the end, add the chillies and hing powder.
  3. Keep it aside.
  4. Steam cook the beets in little oil till the raw smell goes and it becomes tender.
  5. First grind the seasonings with salt and tamarind to crumble it.
  6. Then add the cooked beets and grind it further till it all mixes well.
  7. Do not add water.
  8. Make sure that it doesn't become pasty
