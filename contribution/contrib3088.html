Quick Vegetable Curry

Ingredients

Potato 2
Carrots 2
Capsicum 1
Onion (big) 1
Tomatoes 2 (chopped)
Coriander leaves
Garam Masala 2 tblsp
Chilli Powder 1 tblsp
Oil 2 tblsp
Water
Salt

Method:

  1. Chop the onions nicely and saute them in the oil.
  2. Add the chopped tomatoes and saute.
  3. Cut all the other vegetables and saute them for a few minutes.
  4. Add garam masala, chilli powder and sault, saute for 1 minute and add water.
  5. Pressure cook the whole thing to 3 whistles.
  6. Garnish with coriander and serve it with Chapathi.

Preparation Time: 20 min.
