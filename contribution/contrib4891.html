Chicken Kabab

Ingredients

6 pieces of chicken
1tb spoon ginger garlic paste
1 tea spoon green chilli paste
1 tb sp. ghee
1 tea sp maida (all perpose flour)
1/2 cup milk
1/2 egg
1 tea sp. aji-no-moto pow.(chinese salt)
1/2 tea sp black pepper pow.
salt to taste
Bread crumbs and eggs for frying

Method:

  1. Wash the chicken.
  2. Add ginger garlic and chilli paste and boil till tender
  3. When it tenders, remove all the bones and make small pieces of chichen. keep it aside.
  4. Now heat oil and put maida in it.
  5. When its became pink add milk and cook for 2-3 min, or when paste became very thick.
  6. When it little cool add pepper salt aji-no-moto pow.
  7. Egg and chicken, mix well and make round shapes.
  8. Deep in bread crumbs and then deep in eggs and then deep fry in oil,
  9. Now chicken kababs are ready to serve.
