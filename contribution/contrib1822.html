Vegetable Kurma

Ingredients

Oil - little
1 big Onion
Salt - little
1 Garlic piece
Two Carrots, 1 Capsicum, 1 Potato, small Ginger piece
7 to 10 Beans, 3 to 4 Cauliflower pieces
2 to 3 Cloves, little Elaichi
1/2 spoon Cinnamon
10 to 15 Cashew pieces
1 spoon of Khus Khus
1/2 cup Coconut

Method :

  1. Cut the onions into small pieces and one fourth of the onions into big pieces.
  2. Take a Kadai with little oil and add cloves, cinnamon, elaichi and cashews and then fry a little add ginger, garlic and big pieces of onions and then keep it to cool a little after everything is fried.
  3. Then grind the whole mixture with coconut and khus khus and keep it aside.
  4. Now take a little oil and add sombu, and then onions, fry it nicely.
  5. Take all the vegetables cut into small pieces and boil it in the microwave. After everything is cooked nicely add it to the onions and mix it.
  6. Add salt in the ground mixture. Now after everything is mixed well add water if it is very thick.

Instead of coconut add little coconut and you can use coconut milk. Coconut milk is available in Indian stores.
