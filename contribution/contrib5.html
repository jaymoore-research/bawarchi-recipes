BEEF       BULGOMIA

Ingredients:

Beef
Dark Soy Souce
Method

1. Cut Beef into 3 inch X 2inch X 1/2 inch pieces. (The size can vary. You decide the count !)
2. Marinate in Dark Soy Souce for 13 minutes.
3. Broil in the oven for about 10 minutes.
4. Turnover the pieces and broil for another 7 minutes.
5. Enjoy your Beef Bulgomia with cool Beer.
