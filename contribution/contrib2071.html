Mutton Dish (Phaal)

Ingredients

1. Mutton pieces or Chicken 1/2 kg.
2. Bunch of Coriander/Cilantro. More than a handful. Well chopped.
3. 4-5 Red Chillies
4. 1 tbsp Pepper Powder.
5. Ginger-Garlic paste- 1 tbsp
6. 2 Onions
7. 1 Tomato
8. 3-4 Potatoes
9. Oil - 2 tbsp
10. Salt to taste

Method

  1. Fry the onions for a while. Then add ginger-garlic paste and fry a little longer.
  2. Make a paste out of Coriander, red chillies, pepper powder, tomato and fried onions (from step 1 ).
  3. Take a wok and put in the paste and heat well. Keep stirring until paste thickens and the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add some water, mutton or chicken pieces along with potatoes and cook for 20 minutes.
  5. If it is mutton you will have to pressure cook for 3-4 whistles. Stir once in a while and add water if you require a little extra gravy.

Happy cooking.
