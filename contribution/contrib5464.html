Spinach Rice

Ingredients

1 cup Spinach - blanched
2 cubes Maggie vegetable stock
2 medium sized onions - sliced
2 cloves garlic finely chopped (optional)
2 1/2 cups rice boiled and separated
2 tsp Oil for seasoning
Salt as per taste
Chopped coriander for garnish

Method:

  1. In a wok put garlic in oil and sauti until light brown.
  2. Add the sliced onions and sauti until golden brown.
  3. Crush the Maggie cubes and add to the onions and let it mix thoroughly.
  4. Add the blanched Spinach and add salt as per taste (Maggie cubes are quite salty). Stir for a while.
  5. Add the cooked rice and mix it well with the spinach.
  6. Garnish with coriander.
  7. Put ghee on top while serving the kids for extra calories!
