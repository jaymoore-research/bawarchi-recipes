Shankar Pole

Ingredients

Allpurpose flour or Maida 1 cup
Water
Sugar 3/4 cup
Salt a pinch
Oil for frying

Method:

  1. Add about 4 tbsps of hot oil to maida. Mix well. Knead the dough by adding water to it. While kneading add powdered sugar and salt. Keep it covered so that it will not dry.
  2. Roll it on a flat surface and cut into small diamond shapes.
  3. Deep fry till golden brown and crispy. It can be stored for one month.
