Bread Gulabjamun

Ingredients

Bread- 4 slices
Ghee - 1 big spoon
Sugar- 3/4 of a tumbler
Water- 2 tablespoons

Method

  1. Pour the sugar in a pan and add 2 tablespoon of water to it, keep it for sometime until the syrup is formed that is it should a bit sticky (pagu)
  2. Cut the bread slices into small pieces and fry it with ghee and add them to the sugar syrup
  3. Allow it to stay for five minutes until it gets soaked in the syrup and u can garnish with nuts if u want.

That's all folks hope u all enjoy the taste after trying the sweet at home.
