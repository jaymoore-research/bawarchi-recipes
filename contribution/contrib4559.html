Jhutpat Chutney

Ingredients

Groundnut powder 3 tsp
Coconut 2 tsp
Garlic 4 pods
Onion 1
Tomato 1
Tamarind a small lime size
Red Chillies 3
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. In a kadai add 1 tsp of oil. To it add the garlic, chillies, tomato, onion and stir for 2 minutes.
  2. Take a mixie to this add groundnut powder, coconut, garlic, chillies, tomato, onion, salt and tamarind. Grind this and make into a paste without adding water mix and serve.
