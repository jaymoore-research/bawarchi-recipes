Steamed rice cakes

Ingredients

Plain Rice - 1 cup
Jaggery - 1 cup
Kidney beans - 1/2 cup (soaked for an hour)
Coconut - 1/2cup(cut into small pieces)

Preparation:

  1. Wash the rice in cold water and dry it in a muslin cloth for half an hour.
  2. Make a fine powder in a mixie often sieving out the coarse ingredients.
  3. In a kadai fry the soaked kidney beans for 2 minutes.
  4. Add two cups of water and let it boil.
  5. Now add the powdered jaggery and let the jaggery dissolve thoroughly.
  6. When the water starts boiling add the powdered rice and mix well.
  7. Keep on mixing till all the water is absorbed and now add the coconut pieces.
  8. Switch off the gas and keep covered.
  9. After 10 minutes mix again and make handful of balls.
  10. Flatten them slightly and make a hole at the centre.
  11. Repeat with rest of the mixture.
  12. Steam the cakes for 10 minutes in a pressure cooker.
  13. Serve with fresh butter.
