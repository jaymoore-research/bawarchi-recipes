Mango Pulisheri

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients

Mango ( ripe)ааааа : 1 count
Gingerаааааааааааааааа :а 2 tsp
Onionааааааааааааааааа : 3 tbsp
Turmeric powder : 1/2 tsp
Curdаааааааааааааааааа :а 4 tbsp
Coconut (grated) :аа 1 cup
Dry chilliesааааааааа :а 5 count
Curry leavesааааааа :а 4 -5
Mustardааааааааааааа :а 2 tsp
Oilаааааааааааааааааааааа : 3 tsp

Method

  1. Cut mangoes into medium size pieces.
  2. Put the cut onions, ginger, turmeric powder and enoughа salt to the mangoes and boil it until the mangoes are fully cooked.
  3. Then grind the grated coconut along with the curd.
  4. Add this to the cooked mangoes.
  5. Heat oil in some pan and add mustard, dry chillies and curry leaves.
  6. Then add the mixture to the pan and simmer for 30 sec.
  7. Ur mango pulisheri is ready to be served.
