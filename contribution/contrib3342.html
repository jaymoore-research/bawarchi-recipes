Spinach-Carrot Rice

Ingredients

A. For rice layer:
1-1/2 Tea cups uncooked rice
1/2-tsp. cumin seeds
2 tbsp. butter or vegetable oil
Salt to taste

B. For spinach layer:

3 tea cups chopped spinach, (frozen)
1 tea cup green peas, (frozen)
3 chopped green chillies,
1 chopped onion,
1 tsp butter or vegetable oil,
Salt to taste.

C. For carrot layer:
2 tea cups grated carrot,
1/2 tsp cumin seeds,
1/2 tsp curry powder,
1 tbsp butter or oil,
salt to taste.

D. For baking:
1 cube grated cheese

Method:

For rice layer:

  1. Cook the rice. Each grain of the cooked rice should be separate.
  2. Heat the butter or vegetable oil in a vessel & fry the cumin seeds for a little time. Add the cooked rice, salt to taste & mix well.

For spinach layer:

  1. Heat butter or oil in vessel, add chopped green chillies & onions and fry for two minutes.
  2. Add green peas and cook for 3 minutes. Then add spinach, salt to taste mix well & cook for two minutes.

For carrot layer:

  1. Heat butter or oil, add grated carrot and cook for 2 minutes.
  2. Add curry powder and salt to taste, mix well, cover it & cook for 2 minutes.

HOW TO PROCEED further...

  1. Grease baking dish & spread half of the cooked rice. Next spread the spinach mixture over rice.
  2. Then spread the balance rice, cover it with the carrot layer and then sprinkle the cheese on top.
  3. Cover the baking dish with lid and then bake in a hot oven at 230 degrees centigrade (about 450 F) for 20 minutes.

Serve it hot. Add more green chillies if you need for spiciness (hot).
