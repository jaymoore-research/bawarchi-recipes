Potato Pepper Fry

Ingredients

Baby Potatoes - 500 gms
Garlic - 6-8 cloves
Maa Ingi / Galangal* - 1-2 inches
Pepper - 15 pepper corns
Oil - 1 tablespoon
Salt to taste

* Maa Ingi is the Tamil name for galangal. This is a milder, juicier and non-fibrous variation of ginger; easily available at your local grocery.

Method:

  1. Boil potatoes in pressure cooker for 1-2 whistles till well cooked. Peel and keep aside.
  2. In a wok warm the oil (do not allow to smoke). Add minutely cubed garlic and galangal - saute on low heat for 2 minutes.
  3. Add cooked potatoes and salt - mix - take care not to break the potatoes. When potatoes have become slightly brown, add ground pepper and saute on medium heat for 2 minutes.
  4. Serve hot as accompaniment to rotis / rice or as a starter.

Note: The quantity of garlic and pepper can be varied depending upon taste.
