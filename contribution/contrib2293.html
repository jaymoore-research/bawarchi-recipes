5 Cup Sweet

Ingredients

Besan- 1Cup
Sugar- 3 Cups
Refined Oil- 1 Cup
Grated Coconut- 1Cup
Milk- 1 Cup
Cardamom- 5-6 pieces (pod removed and powered)

Method:

  1. Roast besan in a teaspoon of ghee till the raw smell goes. Turn off the stove and add all the other ingredients one by one.
  2. Turn on the stove and place the pan on it. Keep on stirring with the flame low. As the mixture starts leaving the sides of the pan add few strands of saffron and powdered cardamom.
  3. Keep a well greased plate and a greased rollong pin ready. When the mixture does not stick to the pan any more, pour it onto the plate and while hot, roll over it lightly to bring it into shape.
  4. With a greased knife make markings for the desired shape and size you want. When cool, store in a air tight container.

Ingredients

Rice- 1 Cup
Toor Dal- 2 Cup
Tamarind- Size of a lemon

For the Masala

Channa Dal- 1 tablespoon
Urad Dal- 1/2 tablespoon
Dhania- 1 tablespoon
Red chillies- 7-9
Lavang-4pieces
Dalchini- 5 pieces
Khaskhas- 1 teaspoon
Jaikai- 1/2 of 1 piece
Marathmug- 5-6 pieces
Cardamom pod- 1
Grated Coconut- 150 gms
Vegetables; Carrrot, Beans, Peas, Cauliflower, Potato, Tomato

For the Garnishing:

Mustard seeds-2 teaspoons
Aesofotida - A pinch
Turmeric powder- little
Channa dal- little
Groundnut seeds- 10-12 (optional)
Cashews- 8-10 (optional)
Shredded Onion- 6 onions (optional)

Method:

  1. Wash the dal and put it in a pressure cooker (12 litres) and cook it without the weight.
  2. Add a spoon of turmeric and oil. After it is half cooked add the sliced vegetables and allow it to cook.
  3. Add salt a little and after the vegetables are half cooked add the washed rice and keep on stirring. After the rice is almost cooked add the ground masala, pices of tomato, tamarind pulp, required amount of salt, a little jaggery to taste, coriander leaves. Let all the ingredeients mix well while you keep stirring.
  4. Remove the cooker and in a pan put 2-3 tablespoon of oil and add turmeric, asafoetida and mustard seeds. When the seeds pop add channa dal, groundnut seeds, cashewnut pieces and onions. Fry all these and add this to the prepared dish.
