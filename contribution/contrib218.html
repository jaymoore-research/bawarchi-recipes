Chicken 65

Ingredients

Chicken (boneless & skinless) - 2lb Cutted into 1-2 inch pieces
Self Raising Flour (Maida) -2tbsp
Corn Meal or Corn Starch or Corn Flour -3tbsp
Eggs - 2no's OR Yoghurt - 2tbsp(any one)
Red Chilli Powder - 1tsp
Salt - 1/2 tsp or According To Taste
Haldi or Turmeric powder -1/4 tsp
Ginger Garlic Paste - 1tbsp
Dhaniya Powder - 1tsp
MSG(Mono Sodium Glutamate or Agino Moto)
Tomata Sause -1tbsp
Chilli Sause - 1tsp
Garam Masala - 1/2 tsp
Oil For Deep Frying

Method

  1. Take all the above ingredients except oil, mix them nicely and keep it aside for marinading for two to three hrs.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and fry the chicken pieces with the mixture untill brown.
  3. f you want to decorate, keep them a side and follow as below.
Decorating

Oil - 2 tsp
Green Chillies - 4or5 (cut into two parts)
yoghurt or curd - 1tbsp
Red Orange Colour - Pinch
Coriander Leaves - bunch

  1. Heat oil in a pan and put the green chillies into it and fry them for a minute.
  2. Then pour curd into it and coock it for a minute or two, then put the colour in it.
  3. Now take the fried chicken pieces put them into the above curd mixture and cook it again untill all the curds evaporate.
  4. At last garnish it with corainder.
