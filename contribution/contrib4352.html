Wheat Halwa

Ingredients

Wheat Flour - 1 cup
Jaggery - 3/4 cup
Ghee - 1/2 cup
Water - 1 1/4 cup

Method:

  1. Roast wheat flour in 1/4 cup ghee till it become pinkish brown and you start getting a roasted flavour. Keep aside.
  2. In the same kadai, take water, add jaggery and let it melt. After it melts, add the remaining ghee and add roasted wheat flour little by little. When it starts leaving the sides, remove in a bowl.

Its a very easy recipe with no garnishing and is supposed to be eaten plain for the authentic wheat flavour. Please note that wheat flour should be roasted well and it can be had along with Puris.
