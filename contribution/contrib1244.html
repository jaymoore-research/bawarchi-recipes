Cucumber Carrot Raita

Ingredients

1 cucumber grated
1 carrot grated
2 tbsp. small pieces of onion
2 green chillies
2 cups of yogurt/curds (diluted with water)
Salt

For Seasoning :

1/2 tsp. urad dal
1/4 tsp. mustard
Less than 1/4th tsp of jeera
1 dry red chilli
1 tbsp oil
A pinch of hing
Curry leaves

Method :

  1. Take the curds in a bowl and mix the (Cucumber, Carrots, Onions and Salt) season it with green chillies and other ingredients.

    That's it !!!
    U are ready with raita.
