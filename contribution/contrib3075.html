Moongdal Puttu

Ingredients

Moong dal 1/2 kg
Jaggery 1/2 kg
Ghee 2 tbsp
Elaichi 4

Method:

  1. Soak the moong dal for 4 to 5 hours.
  2. Drain the water and grind in a mixer without water to a smooth paste.
  3. Make small balls and steam cook in the pressure cooker.
  4. Let it cool. After it became cool make small balls into mixer and run at medium speed. It became a nice flour.
  5. Heat heavy bottom vessel put jaggery, little water. Keep stirring. Take a drop at your hand if comes like a thread then add the powdered moongdal. Add powdeered elaichi.
  6. Heat ghee, add to the puttu.
  7. Serve hot or cool.
