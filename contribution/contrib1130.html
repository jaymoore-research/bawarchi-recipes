Asparagus-Paruppu Ushali

Method

  1. Take a bunch of Asparagus.
  2. Lightly peel the skin off and cut into small pieces.
  3. In a separate pan, fry some Sesame seeds, Urad Dal, Hing powder and Curry leaves in some Vegetable oil.
  4. Add the asparagus to it with salt, and Red chili powder to taste.
  5. You can add a pinch of turmeric powder to it for light flavoring and color.
  6. Cook it for about 15-20 minutes with lid.

Paruppu Ushali

Specialty of the South, healthy and easy to make.

  1. Soak a cup of Tuvar Dal for 1-2 hours, and coarse grind it with 1 red chili, pinch of hing and some curry leaves.
  2. Steam cook this mixture for 30 minutes or so.
  3. Now add the two ingredients and cook it on low heat (so that the dal does not get caught on the pan surface and burn), with lid till it cooks.
  4. Serve hot.

You can cook and keep it in the fridge, use it as filler for sandwiches too. Very healthy, and "D" licious!!! Enjoy!
