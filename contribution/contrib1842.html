Avalakki Dosa

Ingredients

Rice 3 cups
Beaten Rice (Avalakki) 1 cup
Buttermilk 4 to 5 cups
Salt to taste

Method :

  1. Wash the rice and beaten rice thoroughly.
  2. Soak this mixture in buttermilk for atleast 5 hours.
  3. Grid this mixture to a fine paste adding water if needed(the batter should have dosa batter consistency)
  4. Mix the salt with the batter.
  5. Heat a tawa and spread a tea spoon of oil.
  6. Pour in a spoonful of batter on the tawa and spread it.
  7. Add oil to the edges if necessary.
  8. Turn the dosa and cook on the other side.
  9. Serve hot with coconut chutney
