Tuna-Coconut Delight

Ingredients

Set (1)

One small piece (1 inch) of ginger - finely chopped
Green chillis - 3 no.s (cut in two, longitudinally)
One medium-sized potato (chopped in half-inch cubes)
Turmeric - a pinch

Set (2)

Tuna (2 small cans (8oz))
One medium-sized tomato( finely chopped )
Coconut milk - 0.75 cups

Set (3)

Mustard seeds (one pinch)
Curry leaves (a few)
Dry red chilli (whole) - 2 no.s

Method

  1. Boil the items in Set (1) in a cup of water for 7 minutes.
  2. Add the items in Set(2), reduce the flame and let it cook for another 7 minutes.
  3. Add the right amount of salt.
  4. Heat oil in a pan, add items in Set(3) , let it sputter and add to main dish. (avoid this step if you do not want to have any oil at allĀ in your diet)
  5. Serve hot with rice or roti.
