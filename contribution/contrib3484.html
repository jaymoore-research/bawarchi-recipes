Tomato Omlett Curry

Ingredients

Tomatoes : 4
Onion : 1 1/2
Chilli : 4
Coriander powder : 1/2 tsp
Garam masala : 3/4 tsp
Cilantro : little
Eggs for omlett : 2 or 3

Method:

  1. Cut onions, chilli & tomatoes, keep them aside.
  2. Take a pan pour little oil & season it. Then add onion & chilli saute for 3 min, then add turmeric and then tomatoes. Add salt & chilli powder to it, simmer on low flame. If needed pour little water.
  3. After 7 to 10 min the tomato curry will almost be done, then add coriander powder, garam masala & cilantro & then add curd 3 tsp (optional).
  4. Then beat the eggs to fluffy, add salt to it & then pour it on a hot tava to make a omlett.
  5. After the omlett is done cut it into equal size & then drop them into the tomato curry. Let it soak for 10 min.
  6. You can enjoy this curry with Rice, Fried Rice or Chapathis.
