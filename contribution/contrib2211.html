Petha

Ingredients

1 tbsp Chuna (Indian store OR Paan shop)
Water
Small piece - White Pumpkin (Chinese/Indian store)
Sugar
Saffron/Kesar
Ice-cream essence (Indian store) - optional

Method:

1st Day:Make CHUNA ke Pani - Add 1 tbsp of chuna to water (approximate 3/4 full, big container like bucket or stock pot). Add it well and let the water sit for 3-4 hrs and then drain the water with cloth in another container. Cut the pumpkin pieces (peel the skin, app 1 inch x 1 inch) and add to the drained water. Every hour keep moving the water with a big spoon so that all pieces get covered by water.

2nd Day:Boil some fresh water. Wash the pieces with fresh cold water few times and then add the pieces to boiling water until they are kind of transparent. Make chasni of 2 tar (if the pcs are appox 1 kg then u need app 800 gms or little less sugar). After the pieces become transparent add them to chasni and keep moving the pieces every hour or so with a big spoon so that all pieces get covered by chasni.

3rd Day: Take out the pieces and boil the chasni and again make the chasni of 2tar (do not add extra water or sugar). Once chasni is done add the pieces back and repeat the process of day 2.

You might have to do it one more day. Then add ice-cream essence and kesar to chasni.

Note:

  • Petha will stay good in refrigerator for few months.
  • Do not over boil the pieces on the 1st day.
  • Weigh the pieces before you put them in water on the 1st day.
