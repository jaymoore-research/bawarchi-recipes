Baingan ma prawns

Ingredients

2 big onions
one small piece ginger
garlic-2 to 3 pods
oil,
turmeric powder,
chilly powder,
corriander powder,
garam masala,
curry leaves,
mustard seeds,
cumin seeds,
fenugreek seeds
2 cups of small dried prawns or fresh prawns as desired and based on availability.
4 small bringals

Method:

  1. Heat oil in a kadai, fry the mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, cumin seeds, curry leaves.
  2. Add chopped onions, ginger and garlic and let it fry a bit more.
  3. Add turmeric, chilly powder, corriander powder, garam masala and salt.
  4. Now add the prawns and keep cooking on a low flame till the prawns are well cooked.
  5. Now add the finely cut bringals, a little water and keep stirring till the bringals are also cooked. Check for salt.

This dish is very delicious to eat with chappathis or bread. Enjoy.
