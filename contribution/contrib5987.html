Bread Pizza

Ingredients

onions-1
chillies-2
tomatoes-2
coriander-1 bunch
carrot-1 grated
capsicum-1
semolina/rawa-1 cup
milk-1/4 cup
salt
bread-4 slices
tomato chilli sauce
tomato sauce
cheese
oil

Method

  1. Mix all the ingredients and make a paste.
  2. Apply chilli sauce on each bread slice.
  3. Put 2 spoons of mixed paste on bread slice & grate cheese on top.
  4. Heat non stick tawa.put 1 spoon of oil.
  5. Keep the bread slice.fry it and turn the other side ofbread.
  6. Fry it until cheese melts.
