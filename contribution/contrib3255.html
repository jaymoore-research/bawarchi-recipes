Pop Corn

Ingredients

Dry corn seeds - 1/4 cup
Powdered salt to your taste
powdered kala namak - about 1/2tsp
black pepper powder - 1/4tsp
garam masala powder - 1/4tsp
oil - 2 Tbl sps
And a pinch of turmeric

Method:

  1. Heat a kadai with oil
  2. When it smokes reduce flame and put all the powders and corn in the oil.
  3. Now close the kadai with a proper big lid so that when corn pops with splutters, it will not spill over.
  4. When the spluttering stops take the lid out and switch off the stove.
  5. Serve immediately or store in air tight container.

Note:

Do not put more corn at one time. A handful or 1/4 cup makes a kadai full of pop corn..
