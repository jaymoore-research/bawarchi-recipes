Potato Veg

Ingredients

Medium sized Red Potatoes 4
Ginger paste 1 tsp
Garlic paste 1 tsp
redchilli powder 1/2 tsp
Paprika 1 tsp
Tamarind 1/4 tsp
Turmeric 1/2 tsp
Cumin powder 1/2 tsp
Salt to taste
Curry leaves 5
Mustard seeds 1 tsp
Urad dal 1/2 tsp
Oil 3 tbsp

Method:

  1. Cut the potato into small cubes and mix with the ingredient 2 to 9 and set aside for 15 mts.
  2. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a non stick stir pan and add the mustard seeds. Once it pops add curry leaves urad dal and fry sligtly.
  3. Now add the prepared potatoes and stir it so that the oil spreads. Now close the lid and cooking medium for 5-7 mts. Once the potatoes are half done remove the lid and cook it well adding oil in between till the potatoes have turned dark brown and are crispy stirring softly occassionaly.

Tip:

The taste is in the cooking. The potatoes wont be crispy if they are over cooked.)
