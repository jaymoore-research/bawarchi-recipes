Pohe Ka Dhokla

Ingredients for Cooking

2 cups thick poha (the one used for kanda poha)
1 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
3 teaspoon chopped green coriander
1 green chilly (chopped fine)
2 teaspoon freshly squeezed lemon juice
1 teaspoon sugar
2 teaspoon grated coconut
Salt to taste

TADKA INGREDIENTS:
1 Pinch asafoetida (hing)
1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds (jeera)
1 spoon oil for frying

Method:

  1. Wash poha and drain it completely of water and keep it aside.
  2. Heat milk in a frying pan until the milk is boiled.
  3. Add sugar and mix it with the milk.
  4. Now mix the poha with the milk in the frying pan.
  5. Add turmeric, salt,chopped green chilly to the pan.
  6. Keep stirring all the ingredients in the pan until it becomes like thick dough.
  7. Now shut the heat off and add the lemon juice to the dough and blend it well.
  8. Take a wide plate, apply ghee (or butter/oil) to the plate lightly.
  9. Pour the dough onto the plate and spread it such that it forms a thick layer on the plate.
  10. Take a frying pan for tadka. Add oil and other TADKA ingredients and heat for 1/2 a minute.
  11. Take the TADKA and pour it over the dough on the plate.
  12. Garnish with grated coconut and green coriander.
  13. When it becomes cool,cut the dough into square pieces and serve plain or with chutney/sauce!
  14. This serves 4 persons.
