Poha Masala Rice

Ingredients

2 cups extra thick poha
2 cups of mixed vegetables(carrots, french beans, potatoes, peas, raw banana, brinjal, sweet yam - you may add vegetables of your choice)
1 small onion
1/2tsp mustard seeds, jeera
2-3 strings of curry leaves (kadi patta)
3-4 red chilles
2 tbsp oil for tadka
little turmeric and hing
Butter for taste
salt for taste

For Masala

1 small piece of cinnamon
4 tsp coriander seeds
4-5 red chilles
1tsp fenugreek seeds
1tsp poppy seeds
1tsp chana dal and urad dal
1/2 cut onions
3 medium sized tomatoes
1-2tbp grated fresh coconut
1/2tsp tamarind concentrate or string of tamarind
(roast all the dry masalas and grind all the above masalas together with some water and keep aside)

Method

  1. Clean poha and keep aside.
  2. Now place the cooker in the stove and heat oil.splitter mustard seeds ,jeera, curry leaves, turmeric, hing and red chilles.
  3. Now add onions for fry till they turn slightly brown.
  4. Now add all the vegetables and poha .
  5. Stir every thing and add salt according to your taste.
  6. Stir it for some time.
  7. Then add the above prepared masala into the cooker.
  8. All the stuffs should be well soaked inside the masala , if the water is less add some more.place the cooker lid without whistle for 10-15 minutes.
  9. Keep the contents stirring for sometime. Then put the whistle and allow it to blow 3 times.
  10. Now serve with butter ,coriander leaves and coconut garish.
