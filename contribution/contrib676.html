Sambar

Ingredients

1/2 Cup Arhar Dal (Tuvar Dal)
1 Onion- chopped lengthwise
1 Large potato- chopped into big cubes
Pressure cook the above ingredients together. Mash the potatoes into the dal.

Masala
1 tsp Jeera
3/4 tsp Dhaniya seeds
1/2 tsp Methi seeds
5-6 Black peppers
1/2 tsp Hing powder
1/2 tsp Haldi powder
2 medium Tomatoes- chopped into cubes.
1/2 tsp Tamarind paste

Method

  1. Heat very little oil in a pan and roast all the ingredients except tomatoes till they are golden brown.
  2. For roasting first add dhaniya seeds and black pepper, then ass jeera,hing and haldi.
  3. Grind the roasted masala with the tomatoes to a fine paste.

Seasoning 1/2 tsp Mustard seeds
8-10 Curry leaves
1/2 tsp Hing
1 Onion- chopped into long thin pieces
1/2 tsp ready made sambar powder (opt)

Method

  1. Heat oil. Add mustard. Once it splutters add curry leaves and hing powder. Immediately add onions and fry till golden brown.
  2. Add the tomato masala paste and fry till a little oil appears.
  3. Then add the cooked dal, salt and 1/2 tsp sugar. Add sambar powder. Bring to a boil. Garnish with coriander leaves.
  4. Sambhar is ready.
