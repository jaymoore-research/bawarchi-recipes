Spicy Curd Rice

Ingredients

Rice 1 cup
Onion 1 big
Cucumber 1/2 peeled and grated
Fresh curds 3 cups
Salt According to your taste.

For Seasoning:

Ginger 1"
Curry leaves 4-6
Mustard seeds 1 tsp
Chanadal 1 tsp
Green Chillies 3-4

Method:

  1. Prepare Rice with enough water.
  2. Chop the onions finely, mix the grated Cucumber, to the cooked Rice add Salt and keep it aside.
  3. Heat little oil in a pan add the above ingredients turn off the heat and transfer the contents of the pan into the rice.
  4. Mix well with enough curd.

Variation:

  1. Cucumber can be chopped along with onion.
  2. Red chilli can also be used for seasoning.

Note:

Do not use buttermilk or diluted curd as it will spoil the whole dish. Happy cooking !!
