Keralastyle Kuzhambu

Ingredients

Fenugreek seeds - 2 tablespoons
Urud dhal - 2 tablespoons
Red Chilli - 3 or 4 pieces
Grated Coconut - 5 to 6 tablespoons
Curd - 2 cups
Vegetable needed : Either Bringal or Lady's finger
Salt according to taste

For Seasoning:

Mustard seeds - 1 tablespoon
Curry leaves if available

Method:

  1. First fry all the ingredients except curd in little oil and make a powder.
  2. Fry the vegetable in little oil until it becomes soft and add curd and salt and allow it to boil for few seconds and add the ground ingredients immediately and allow it to boil well.
  3. Add the seasoning to the kuzhambu. It goes very well with Rice and Pappad.
