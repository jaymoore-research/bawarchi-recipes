Masala Puri

Ingredients

1. Chapathi Atta - 200 gms
2. Jeera - 1 tsp
3. Chilli powder - 1 tsp
4. Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp
5. Yoghurt - 3-4 tbsp
6. Saunf powder - 1 tsp
7. Cooking Oil - 1/2 kg
8. Salt - 1/2 tsp

Method:

  1. In one bowl take 200 gm chapathi atta. Add jeera, chilli powder, turmeric powder, saunf powder and salt. Mix it well.
  2. Add 3-4 tbsp yoghurt and 2 tbsp cooking oil. Add very little water. Mix it well to make a soft dough.
  3. Keep it for about 15 to 20 minutes.
  4. Take a small ball from dough and roll it. Not very thick, not very thin.
  5. In kadai take 1/2 kg cooking oil and heat it on boiling temp. Slow the fire and insert a rolled puri into the oil slowly. With in notime the puri puffs up.
  6. Turn it and fry for a while.
  7. Take out the puri and place on tissue paper to drain excess oil.
  8. Serve hot puri with Pickles.

Please note that this puri can be preserved at least for two days. So, it is very best recipe for people who travels long distance.
