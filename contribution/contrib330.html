Chura Matar

Ingredients

1 1/2 cups of thin chura (obtained from Indian grocery store)
2 cups of green peas
1 finely chopped potato
1 big red tomato
2 finely chopped green chilies
1 Tea sp. chopped fresh ginger
1/2 cup finely chopped coriander leaves
1 Table sp. fresh lemon juice
1/2 Tea sp. garam masala
1/2 Tea sp. salt
4 Tea sp. sugar
1 Tea sp. amchur powder (dried mango powder)
3-4 black cardamom seeds
5-6 cloves
5-6 whole black pepper seeds
1/2 cup chopped cashew nuts
1/2 cup of raisins

Method:

  1. Saut in a little oil over a medium heat, the green chilies, ginger, cloves, black peppers, and cardamoms for a few minutes, add the potatoes and peas and spices and saut till the peas are tender.
  2. Grind tomato in the grinder to form the liquid juice. Mix this tomato juice in the chura with your fingers. Do not mash it.
  3. If chura is still looks dry then add little water as needed.
  4. Remember this should be done just before you are ready to add chura in the mixture.
  5. Add the salt, sugar, cashews and raisins along with the prepared chura to the mixture and cook for a further 5 minutes. Turn off the heat.
  6. Add amchur powder, garam masala and mix them well.
  7. Before serving dot with a few Tea sp. of butter and sprinkle with the lemon juice. Add coriander leaves.
  8. This dish can be used as appetizer, snack, and side dish.

Serves: 4
Making Time: 10-15 minutes
