Onion Chutney

Ingredients

Onion 2
Dry Red Chillies 4 (according to your taste)
Coconut powder 1 tbl spoon
Salt
little Oil

For seasoning(optional)

Mustard seeds
Curry leaves

Method:

  1. Cut oinion into pieces.
  2. In a frying pan put little oil and saute the onion for 2,3 minutes and remove from pan and keep aside.
  3. Same pan fry the red chillies.
  4. Grind all together -onion,redchillies, coconut powder and salt. adding little water to a paste. Remove that from and keep it in a bowl.
  5. If you want you can make seasoning to the chutney - in little oil fry mustard seeds and curry leaves and add that to the chutney.
