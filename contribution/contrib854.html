Microwave Baigan Bhurtha

Ingredients

Round Baigan 1 (200gms)
Frozen Peas 100 Gms
Onions (Coarsely chopped) Big 2
Garlic Cloves 4
Tomato Puree 2 Tbs
Salt 1 Tsp or to taste
Red chilli powder 1/2 Tsp.
Coriander seeds semi crushed 1 Tbs.
Oil 1 Tbs.

Method

  1. Roast the baigan in microwave after pricking at four, five places for three minutes on high temp. Remove the skin with water and crush it nicely. Keep aside.
  2. Put oil in the microwave pan and add coriander seeds, onions, salt, chili powder and tomato puree.
  3. Heat it on high temp for five minutes. Add baigan and peas. Again put it back in the oven for three minutes.

Serve hot with chappaties.

Note :- Microwave heat will vary depending on the model.
