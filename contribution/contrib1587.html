Pal Payasam

Ingredients

Rice - half a cup
Milk- 4 litres
Sugar- 2 and half cups
Cardamom- (optional)

Method :

  1. Take the rice and grind it in the mixie so that it becomes small granules. Use just a handful of rice.
  2. Take a vessel add the milk and rice and cook it using a pressure cooker. After 4 to 5 whistles keep it in medium for about 40 min. After it cools add sugar and caramom if necessary.

Note:If u pressure cook it u get a good color and flavour. Hope u like it.
