Paruppu Usili

Ingredients

ToorDhal 3 cups
Red Chillies 4
Asafoetida
Cabbage/Cauliflower/Beans/Cluster Beans - Finely chopped
Turmeric Powder 1/2 tsp
Mustard Seed 1/2 tsp
Urad Dal 1/2 tsp
Oil 4 Tbsp
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Soak the Dhal along with Red Chillies for 1 hour.
  2. Wash and drain the dhal and grind it coarsely with chillies. (without adding water).
  3. 3. Put a little asafoetida powder and required salt in the ground dhal.
  4. Spread oil evenly in one separator Vessel of the pressure cooker.
  5. Spread the ground dhal evenly in the vessel.
  6. Put the finely chopped vegetables mixed with a little turmeric and salt in another separator vessel of the cooker and pressure cook till done. (atleast 4 whistles).
  7. After removing from the cooker, cool the dhal well and grind it in mixie without adding water. (Run in speed 1 for 2 seconds)
  8. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds and when it splutters, add urad dal.
  9. Add the ground dhal and fry till golden brown
  10. Put the boiled vegetable in dhal and stir well and keep in a low flame for 2-3 minutes and remove from fire.

Hope you enjoy this dish. Making Time: 30 Minutes Serves : 4
