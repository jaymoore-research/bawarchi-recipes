Quick Ladoos

Ingredients

Royal Tea biscuits (or mari biscuits) 2 full rows
Sweetened Condensed Milk 1 tin
Dessicated Coconut 1 cup
Hot Chocolate Powder 6 tsps
Cocoa 4 tsps
Unsalted Butter 2 tbsps

Method:

  1. Dry grind the biscuits into fine powder.
  2. Mix in all the dry ingredients (keep aside little dessicated coconut for garnishing) with butter and condensed milk.
  3. Make ladoos and roll on dessicated coconut to give a coating.
  4. Eat and enjoy.
