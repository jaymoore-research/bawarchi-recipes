Modak

Ingredients

For Stuffing:

250 gm Coconut (grated)
250 gm Jaggery
1 1/2 tbps Sesame
1 tbps Ghee

For Modak Base:

Maida or All purpose Flour 400 gm
3 tbps Oil
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Make a soft dough of the flour by adding oil and salt.
  2. Heat ghee in a vessel, add jaggery, coconut and sesame seeds. Keep stirring continuously on medium flame. The jaggery dissolves and a sticky solution is formed. Leave it to cool for 30 minute.
  3. Make small balls of dough and roll it, let the diameter be 3-4 inches approximately.
  4. Stuff inside the above stuffing and pack it by taking all the corners up giving it a shape like a cone on top. This is a modak.
  5. Take oil in a kadai and deep fry the Modaks till brown.
  6. Serve hot or cold, both ways it tastes good. You can make approx. 15 Modaks.
