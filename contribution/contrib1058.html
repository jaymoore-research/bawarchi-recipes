Thenka Pal Kozhukattai

Ingredients

1 cup coconut milk (taken from half the coconut)
1 cup rice flour
1 cup water
2 tsp sugar
1 tsp salt

Method :

  1. Mix flour, sugar and some water in a bowl and bring it to a soft dough.
  2. Make small balls out of it and keep aside.
  3. In a pan mix water, salt and coconut milk allow to boil.
  4. Into the boiling mixture slowly place these flour balls.
  5. Allow the contents to be boiled for about 5-10 minutes so that the ball is cooked well.
  6. Stir the contents now and then when only the mixture remains in a gravy state .
