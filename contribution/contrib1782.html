Aloo Poha

Ingredients

Poha 250 gms
Onions 4
Potato 4
Oil 2 tbsp
Jeera 1/4 tbsp
Rai 1/4 tbsp
Corainder (to garnish)
Green Chilli 2

Method :

  1. Boil potatoes and mash them.
  2. Chop onions finely.
  3. Cut green chillies into fine pieces.
  4. Take a pan put oil, rai, jeera and green chillies.
  5. Next put onions and fry till they become light brown in colour.
  6. Next put the mashed potatoes into the pan and stir well.
  7. Soak poha in water for about 2-3 min.
  8. Strain water from poha and put into the pan and stir well. Cover with lid and keep stirring once every 3 min.
  9. Add salt to taste.
  10. Empty contents of pan into serving bowl and garnish with finely chopped coriander.

Cooking time : 10 min
Servings : 2
