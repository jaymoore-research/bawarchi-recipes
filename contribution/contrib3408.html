Instant Kalakhand

Ingredients

Sweetened Milk (Milkmaid) - 1 tin.
Milk powder - 6 Table spoon
Curd - 2 Table spoon
Ghee - 2 Table spoon
Food Essence - If needed

Method:

  1. Mix all the ingredients in a glass bowl. Stir the same till it becomes a fine paste. Leave it for a minute.
  2. Keep the bowl in Microwave for 3 minutes on High.
  3. Take the bowl out and stir the contents once. Keep the bowl in Microwave again for 3 minutes on High. You would get a good smell of the Milk sweet.
  4. Serve it Hot or Cold.
