Tomato Bhath

Ingredients

Rice - 1-1/2 cup
Tomatoes - 2 medium sized chopped
Onions - 1 Big chopped
Peas (optional)
Green chillies (optional) slit 20
Rasam powder - 1 1/2 Tsp
Chana dal - 1/2 Tsp
Urad dal - 1/2 Tsp
Mustard and Jeera seeds - 1 Tsp
Oil - 2 Tsp

Method :

  1. Cook the rice and allow to cool for 1/2 an hour or keep the rice refrigerated for 10 min so that each grain is seperate. You could cook the rice the previous night and put it in the fridge for convenience (for people who've to run to work in the morning ).
  2. Heat oil in a kadai. Add Mustard and Jeera seeds and allow to splutter. Now add chana dal and urad dal and add a pinch of turmeric and then add curry leaves. Add chopped onions, tomatoes and green chillies. After 2 min of light frying them in oil add required amount of salt and a pinch of sugar.
  3. Now when the onions and tomatoes are done, add the rasam powder and mix properly and leave it on a low flame till the raw smell of the rasam powder is gone. Now, add the cooled rice and mix properly and leave it on the cooktop on a low flame. Your Tomato Rice is done now!
