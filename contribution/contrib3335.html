Sesame Supreme

Ingredients

Bread slices : 4 nos.
Boiled Potato : 1
Spring Onions : 3 chopped fine
Capsicum : 1 small chopped fine
, Green Chillies : 2 or 3 chopped thin
Carrot (grated) : 1
Cabbage (grated): 1/3 cup.
Soya Sauce : 2 tsp
Sesame seeds : 4 tsp
Flour : 1/2 cup
Salt : as per individual requirements
Water for mixing
Oil for deep frying.

Method:

  1. Cut bread slices into halves diagonally.
  2. In a wok heat about 2 tbsps of oil. Add the cabbage and carrot, capsicum and spring onions and sautee until soft.
  3. Add the green chillies and also the mashed boiled potato. Add salt and soya sauce and remove and let it cool.
  4. Mix the water and flour to make a thick paste. Take a piece of bread, spread the veg mixture on top. Now coat it with the flour paste binding all the sides. Sprinkle a few sesame seeds on top.
  5. Heat the oil in a frying pan. Fry the slices one by one. Be careful, it cooks very fast and can be removed in about a minute or when golden brown. Remove to absorbent paper to drain excess oil.
  6. Serve with Mint Chutney and Tomato Ketchup.
