Vegetable Fried Rice

Ingredients

2 cups - Basmati rice (boiled)
1/2 cup - Beans (finely chopped)
1/2 cup - Carrot (grated)
1 tsp - Black Jeera (Shah Jeera)
1 tsp - Jeera
2 tbsp - Oil
1/2 tsp - Green Chilli Paste
1/3 tsp - Pepper Powder
Salt - according to taste

Method :

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add jeera & shah jeera.
  2. Add beans to it & saute for 1 minute (till beans are half fried).
  3. Add grated carrot to it & saute it until the raw smell goes.
  4. Add green chilli paste, pepper powder & salt.
  5. Lastly add the boiled rice to the vegetables & mix well.
  6. Serve this item with vegetable kurma & tomato sauce.

Ingredients (for kurma) :

1 1/2 cup - Carrot, Beans & Potatoes (chopped in small cubes)
2 medium - Tomatoes (chopped)
12 no's - Cashew Nuts (kaju)
12 no's - Raisins (kismiss)
1 tbsp - Groundnut (fry without oil seperately)
1 tbsp - Sesame seeds (thil) & make fine powder
1 tbsp - Poppy Seeds (kas kas) together
1/2 tsp - Green Chilli Paste
1/2 tsp - Red Chilli Powder
1/2 tsp - Ginger- Garlic paste
1/2 tsp - Garam Masala Powder
1/2 cup - Milk & Water
2 tbsp - Oil
Seasoning, Corriander & Salt to taste

Method :

  1. Heat oil in a pressure pan & add seasoning to it
  2. Add kaju, raisins & vegetables (except tomatoes)
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, green chilli paste, garam masala powder, red chilli powder, salt & saute it.
  4. Then add tomatoes & mix it.
  5. Add the powdered ingredients to the top of the vegetables without mixing it (if you mix the powder with vegetables while pressure cooking it sticks to the pan)
  6. Finally pour milk & water, put some coriander on top of it & presuure cook for 2 whistles.

Note :

  1. Without pressure cooking we can also do this kurma in a pan.
  2. We can mix the powder along with the vegetables & cook it until the powder is well cooked.

Servings : 4 - 5.
