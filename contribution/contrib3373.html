Puffed Rice Uppuma

Ingredients

Puffed Rice-3 cups
Peanuts-2 tbsp
Onion-1
Green Chillies-2
Lime Juice-2 tsp
Oil-1 tsp
Gram Dhal-1 tsp
Udhadh dhal-1 tsp
Mustard seeds
Salt to taste
Turmeric powder- 1 pinch
Coriander and Curry leaves.

Method:

  1. Pour oil in a pan and season it with mustard seeds, gram and udadh dhal. Saute finely chopped onions and add green chillies and turmeric powder. After sometime, add the puffed rice and mix well.
  2. Add powedered peanuts, salt and a dash of lime juice.
  3. Finally add finely chopped coriander and curry leaves.
