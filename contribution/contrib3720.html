Gobhi Ki Puri

Ingredients

1 cup Wheat Flour
1/2 cup greated Cabbage
1/2 tea spoon Ginger Garlic paste
1 Green Chilly finely chooped
1/4 tea spoon Cumin seeds
Salt to taste
Oil to fry

Method:

  1. Take all the above ingredients add 2 table spoon oil and knith a dough.
  2. Roll puries out of that dough and deep fry it in hot oil.
  3. Serve it with Green Chutney or with Tomato Ketchup.
