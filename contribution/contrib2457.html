Ingredients

Black Sweet Rice - 1 cup. (available in vietnames / oriental stores in USA, Karaikudi in India)
Sugar - 2 cups.
Ghee - 4 tablespoon.

Method

  1. Soak the rice in water for 1 full night. Then cook it by adding 3 cups of water for 1 cup of rice in cooker for 5 whistles. Then if you feel there is more water just drain it.
  2. When rice is cooked just add sugar, ghee and just mix well. If you need still sweet add little of Sugar.
  3. You can serve this as sweet for morning breakfast for your guest.

Tips:

If you like to add fresh coconut powder of 1 cup, you can just mix that too, along with sugar and ghee and then serve.
