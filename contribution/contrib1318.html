Fried Iddlis

Ingredients

Left over Iddlis - 10 (kept in the fridge and made hard)
Onion - 2 medium sized cut fine
Tomato - 2 medium sized cut fine
Cumin powder - 1/2 teaspoon
Coriander powder - 1/2 teaspoon
Garam masala powder - 1/4 teaspoon
Chilli powder - to taste
Salt - to add to taste only for the gravy
Oil for frying
Coriander leaves - to garnish

Method :

  1. Cut each Iddly into 4 pieces.
  2. Deep fry in oil. Drain and keep aside.
  3. Heat one tablespoon oil and add the cut onion. Fry for sometime and add the cut tomatoes.
  4. Add all the powders and add a glass of water. Let the gravy thicken. Remember to add salt - only for the gravy - iddly has salt in it.
  5. Add the fried iddlies to the gravy just before serving. The iddlis would be slightly crisp and would absorb the gravy also.
  6. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve.

When you fry the cold iddlis, they should be really crisp. You can serve the crisp iddlis as such also (without the gravy) as cocktail snacks. You can also cut iddlis in the shape of fingers. Believe me it tastes much better than the original soft iddlis!!
