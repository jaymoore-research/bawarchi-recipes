Prawn Malai Curry

Ingredients

1. Medium sized Prawns - 10-12 nos.
2. Bay Leaves - 2
3. Onion Paste - 2 tbsp
4. Ginger Paste - 1.5 tsp.
5. Garlic Paste - 1 tsp
6. Tomato Puree - 2 tbsp
7. Coconut milk - Half cup
8. Ghee - 1 tbsp
9. Turmeric Powder - Half tsp
10. Chilli powder - 1 tsp
11. Salt to taste
12. Sugar - Half tsp
13. Garam masala Powder (Cardamom, Cloves & Cinnamon) - Half tsp
14. Coriander Leaves
15. Cooking Oil - 3 tbsp (Mustard Oil preferable)

Method :

  1. Wash & pat dry the prawns. Sprinkle salt & turmeric powder on them. Keep aside.
  2. Heat cooking oil in a frying pan.
  3. Fry the prawns till light golden. Leave them aside.
  4. Add two Bay Leaves in the remaining oil (while it's still hot from frying).
  5. Add onion, ginger & garlic paste carefully into the oil and stir for a minute.
  6. Add the Tomato Puree, Turmeric Powder, Chilli Powder, Sugar & salt and stir till oil gets separated from the mixture.
  7. Add the prawns now & mix with them well.
  8. Slowly add the coconut milk and then add half a cup of water & bring it to boil in low heat.
  9. When the curry thickens, spread the ghee & Garam Masala Powder evenly all around the pan.
  10. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with rice / nan.
