Cabbage

Ingredients

1 Kg Cabbage
1/2 tsp Chilli Powder
1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
1/2 tsp Salt
1/2 tsp Garam Masala
2 Onions
4 slices of Garlic
10 Gm grated Coconut
50 Gm Vinegar
Mustard seeds

Method:

  1. Wash the cabbage thoroughly and then grate it.
  2. Put some oil in a pan on the gas, when it is heated add mustard seeds.
  3. When they start popping add the onions, which should be cut earlier, also add the garlic. Fry them till golden.
  4. Add chilli, turmeric, salt to this mixture, add the grated cabbage. Cover your pan with lid, sim your gas. Let it cook like this for 5 minutes, taking care it does not burn from the bottom.
  5. Add vinegar, coconut and the garam masala. Cook for another 2 minutes. Now it is ready to serve. Serve it hot.

Preparation Time : 7 mins
Serves : 5 persons
