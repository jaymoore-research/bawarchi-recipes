Humble Potato - Microed

Ingredients

Potato medium size 4 to 6
Onions medium size 3 to 4
Tomato Ripe Red medium size 2
Gram Masala tablespoon 1
Salt to taste
Refined oil tablespoons 4

Method

  1. Peal the Potato and cut them length wise like in case of potato finger chips.
  2. Cut the onions into medium size - four to six pieces per onion.
  3. Cut the tomato also into length wise slices.
  4. Put the oil in a ovenproof dish and warm it in the micro for 2 mts.
  5. Mix the cut vegtables in this warm oil. Add the Gram Masala and salt to taste.
  6. Add about half cup water and mix well.
  7. Keep the dish with the ingredients in the MICRO and micro it for 15 to 20 min. till tender.

Total time taken 30 min.
Enough for four searvings.

Eat with Chapaties or Paranthos. If in a hurry can be eaten with bread slice.
