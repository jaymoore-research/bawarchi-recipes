Prawn-Mango Curry

Ingredients

Prawns : 1/2 kg
Mango : 1
Coconut : 1/2 cup
Jeera : 1/4 tsp
Methi : 1/4 tsp
Mustard seed : 1/4 tsp
Chilli powder : 1/2 tsp
Coriander powder : 1 tsp
Turmeric powder : 1/2 tsp
Shallots : 2
Salt to taste.

Method:

  1. Peel and cook Prawns with turmeric and salt.
  2. Add the mangoes cut into medium pieces, along with coriander and chilli powder. After the Mangoes are cooked add coconut grinded with jeera boil for 5 minutes.
  3. Heat 1 tsp of oil, add mustard and methi seeds, with crushed shallots pour this in the cooked curry.
  4. Serve the dish with rice and any fried vegetable or fish.

This curry tastes good when mangoes are sour. Ripe or green mangoes can be used.
