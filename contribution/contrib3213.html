Spicy Tamarind Paste

Ingredients

Tamarind -2 lime sized.
Chanadal-5 tbsp
Urad dal-3 tbsp
Peanuts or groundnut-5 tbsp
Mustard-3 to 4 tbsp
Asafoetida- 1 tsp
Red Chillies-20 in nos
Tumeric powder-1 tbsp
Sesame Oil-150 ml
Salt to taste.

Seasoning for the Paste:

For Dry Grinding:

Dhania or Coriander seeds-2 tbsp

Fenugreek-1 tbsp
Sesame seeds-1 tbsp

Method:

  1. Soak tamarind and salt in 2 cups of water for half-an-hour.
  2. Heat a thick bottomed kadai. Fry all the ingredients for seasoning separately without oil. Allow them to cool separately.
  3. Then pour the Sesame oil in the kadai. When the oil is hot put asafoetida and Mustard in it. When mustard splutters put the chana dal, urad dal and red chillies and the groundnut. Keep this in a medium flame. When this dals turns golden colour pour the tamarind juice in it. Keep stirring occasionally. This will be done in Half-an-hour. When the oil separates out the paste is done.
  4. Now grind the ingredients for seasoning. And put dhania, sesame seed and fenugreek one by one for seasoning. Allow this to cool. Put this paste in a air tight container. This will be good with Rice.
