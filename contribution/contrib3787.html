Potatoes With Groundnut (Peanut) Powder

Ingredients

3-4 Medium size Potatoes peeled and cut into small pieces.
4 tsps roasted Groundnut (peanut) coarsely ground powder.
1 Medium Onion finely chopped.
1 Medium Tomato finely chopped
2 Green Chilies chopped
1 tsp Turmeric Powder
2 tsps Chilly powder
1 tsp Dhania powder (Coriander powder)
Half tsp Jeera (Cumin) seeds
Salt to taste
Few Curry leaves (Karyapak)
Few Fresh Coriander leaves
1 tsp Lemon juice

Method:

  1. Heat pan with oil and add Jeera. Fry for some time till the seeds pop and then add Onion with green chillies. Fry till onions are golden brown. Add tomato and stir for few minutes.
  2. Add Turmeric, Chilly and Dhania powders alongwith salt to taste.
  3. Add cut potatoes and cover the pan with lid and cook for 7-8 minutes on medium flame stirring occassionaly till the potatoes are cooked.
  4. Add the roasted groundnut (peanut) powder, curry leaves, coriander and stir for a minute. Add 1 teaspoon of lemon juice and remove from the flame.
  5. Serve hot with Phulka, Chappatis or even with Rice.
