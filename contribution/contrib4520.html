Batturas

Ingredients

Maida - 1/2 Kg
Eggs - 2
Sugar - 2 Tbsp
Milk - 1/2 cup
Curd - 1/2 cup
Salt - pinch
Baking powder - 1 pinch

Method:

  1. In a bowl, put the flour then add egg, sugar, milk, curd, salt and baking powder. Then mix it well until you have a smooth dough.
  2. Cover with wet cloth and leave to rest for 2 hours.
  3. Divide the dough into small ball.
  4. Take one ball roll out on a light floured surface into a circle.
  5. Heat the oil in a frying pan and fry it both side.
  6. Serve with chicken curry or any vegetable curry.
