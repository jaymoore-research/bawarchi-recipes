Easy Noodles

Ingredients

Maggi 2Minute noodles - 1 packet
1/2 Tomato (can used canned tomatoes)
Beans, Peas (as much as desired, can use frozen again)
Few pieces of Cauliflower (cut into small pieces)
1/2 Carrot (cut into very tiny pieces)
1/2 Onion (cut into 1/2 rings)
1/2 Lime
Pinch of a few masalas (like garam, pavbhaji, chana etc - optional)

Method:

  1. Boil the water and add the vegetables except the cauliflower, carrots and onion. Add the noodles and the sachet (and the other masalas). Let this boil for a few minutes. When almost done, throw in the carrots and cauliflower and let it simmer for a minute (no more, the uncooked carrot and cauliflower give the noodles a crunchy feel).
  2. Empty into a bowl and sqeeze in the lemon and scatter the onions on top (no need to add chillies as th onions provide for that). The end result is a very nice and crunchy tasting noodles - fast! Enjoy.
