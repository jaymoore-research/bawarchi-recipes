Cauliflower Manchurian

Ingredients

Cauliflower - 1
Onions - 3
Garlic - 8 flakes
Ginger - small piece
Tomatoes - 4
Jeera - 1 tsp.
Chilli powder - 2 tsp (according to your spicy)
Dhania powder - 1/2 tsp
Turmeric powder - 1/4 tsp.
Oil
Salt

For Cauliflower :

Maida (or) All purpose flour - 1 cup
Garam Masala powder - 1/2 tsp
Red Chilli powder - 1 tsp
Salt
Oil for deep fry

Garnishing :

Grated Cheese - 2 tsp.
Corainder leaves - 1 Tbl.Sp

Method:

  1. Wash and cut the cauliflower into small floweretes.
  2. Cook for 2 mts.
  3. Mix Maida, Garam masala, Red Chilli powder, salt and water and mix well without any lumps. The batter should be little thick.
  4. Dip the cauliflower in that batter and deep fry in the oil till light brown and keep it aside.
  5. Heat oil in another pan fry onions, ginger and garlic. Allow to cool.
  6. Grind Onions, ginger and garlic to smooth paste.
  7. Blanch tomatoes in hot water and remove the skin and make it into paste.
  8. Heat oil season with Jeera, then add Onions, ginger, garlic paste and fry until raw smell goes.
  9. Add Tomato paste and fry for 5 mts.
  10. Add Red Chilli powder, Corainder powder, Turmeric powder, Salt to that paste and allow it to boil.
  11. While starts boiling add fried Cauliflower into that and allow to boil until the gravy becomes thick.
  12. Sprinkle some grated Cheese and garnish with Corainder leaves.
