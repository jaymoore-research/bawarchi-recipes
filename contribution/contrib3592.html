Methi Chutney

Ingredients

Fresh Fenugreek leaves 3 bunches approx 100gms
Groundnut 20 gms
Green Chillies 6-8
Tamarind small rasagolla size
Jaggery small lime size
2 tsp Oil (for seasoning)

Method:

  1. Wash and chop finely the fenugreek leaves.
  2. Take oil in a frying pan and to this add little mustard seed, cumin seeds and when it splits add chopped green chillies, pinch of turmeric powder and saute. When the chillies turn to light colour add groundnuts and fry it the groundnuts are done and add the chopped fenugreek leaves, jaggery, salt to taste and cook for a while and add tamarind after removing from the fire.
  3. Cool it and grind to fine paste. Goes well with Chapati or hot Rice or even with plain Rawa Dosa.
