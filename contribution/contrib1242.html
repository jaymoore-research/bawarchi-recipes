Fish Podimas

Ingredients

Shark fish - 300 gms
Onion big - 2
Green chillies - 3
Urad dal - 2 tsp
Mustard- 1 tsp
Tumeric powder - 1 tsp
Grated coconut - 1/2 cup(optional)
Curry leaves - little
Oil - 2 table spoon
Salt to taste

Method

  1. Cut the onion and green chillies into small pieces.
  2. Cut the fish into medium size pieces and cook with little water
  3. When it is cooked drain the water and smash it well
  4. In a pan heat the oil and allow the mustard seed to splutter followed by adding urad dal and curry leaves and onion pieces.
  5. Fry the onion till it becomes transparent and add the green chillies and fry for 2-3 mts
  6. Then add the smashed fish pieces along with the tumeric powder and salt
  7. After frying the above for 10 more mts add the coconut and fry till light brown colour appears
