Mutton Do Piyaza

Ingredients

1/2 kg Mutton
1 tbsp Ginger paste
4-5 dried Red Chilli
1 tbsp Garlic Paste (5 cloves)
500 gms coarsely chopped Onions
1" Cinnamon
350 gms thick Curd, beaten
8-10 nos. Black Pepper
4-5 Cardamoms, big
Salt to taste
2 tbsp Oil
2-3 Bay leaves
2 tbsp Ghee
A handful of Coriander leaves, choped

Method:

  1. Clean mutton. Marinate with ginger, garlic paste for 1 hr.
  2. Heat oil in a pressure cooker. Take about quarter of the chopped onions and fry it till it is well browned.
  3. Add all the remaining ingredients except ghee and bay leaves. Pressure cook till mutton is done. Do not over cook.
  4. In a kadai heat ghee, add bay leaves. Pour out the mutton from the pressure cooker and cook till the water has dried away and the gravy is thick.
  5. Serve garnished with coriander leaves.

NOTE:

1. No water is used, as their will be enough water from the onions and the curd and the mutton itself.
2. Since mutton is pressure cooked small quantity of oil is enough.
3. The garam masala can be tied in a muslin cloth and removed before serving. Ground spices are not used in this recipe.
