Dhal Chur Curry

Ingredients

Musoor dhal - 1 cup
Onion sliced - one
Lime - 1/2
Garlic and Ginger paste - 1 teaspoon
Red Chillies ground paste - 1 teaspoon
Salt to taste
Oil to fry

Method:

  1. Wash and soak dhal.
  2. Heat oil. Saute onions. Add ground ingredients and saute.
  3. Add dhal and fry for a while. Cover with sufficient water to cover dhal.
  4. Add salt, simmer till dhal is cooked. And dry. Remove from fire.
  5. Squeeze the juice of lime.
  6. Garnish with crispy fried onions and chopped coriander leaves.

We can use it as side dish for chappaties.
