Tomato Omlete

Ingredients

2 cups rice
1/2 cup channa dal
1/4 cup urad dal
3 medium size tomatoes
4 green chillies
4 tbsp fresh cocont
2 tsp jeera powder
3 tsp coriander powder
2 finely chopped onions
1/2 cup chopped coriander leaves
Few curry leaves (optional)
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Soak rice, channa dal, urad dal for 7-hrs.
  2. Grind it to a smooth paste like dosa batter.
  3. Leave it to ferment over night.
  4. Grind tomatoes, coconut, green chillies to a smooth paste & add it to the fermented batter.
  5. Now add jeera & coriander powder, chopped onions, coriander leaves & curry leaves & salt according to taste.
  6. Mix all the ingredients well.
  7. Now heat griddle, pour the batter & spread it lightly (not to thin like dosa).
  8. Put 2 tsps of oil.
  9. Once it is heated for two minutes turn the omelet to the other side using a flat spatula and heat it upto two more minutes.

Now tomato omelet is ready to eat. It goes well with coconut chutney.
