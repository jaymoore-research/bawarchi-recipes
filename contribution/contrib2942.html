Garlic Rice

Ingredients

500gm cooked rice.
3 tsp ghee.
6-8 cloves of garlic, finely cut into pcs.
2 big green chillies, finely cut
10-15 cashewnuts.
8-10 groundnuts.
Salt and Pepper as per taste.

Method:

  1. Heat 3tsp ghee in a yok.
  2. Add Green chillies and fry.
  3. After about a minute, add garlic pcs and fry till becomes golden brown.
  4. Now add cashews and groundnuts and fry again.
  5. Add the cooked rice and mix well with salt and pepper according to your taste.
  6. Serve this when it is hot.

Note: This dish has got a v.good madicinal value,and has got v.good digestive power because of garlic.
