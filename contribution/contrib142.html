Pohe Chivda

Ingredients

4 servings

Thin Pohe (3 to 4 cups)
Churmura (1 to 2 cups)
Oil (5 tablespoons)
Peanuts (1/2 cup)
Corriander seeds (Dhaniya seeds)
Kurry leaves (Kadipatta 4 to 5)
Garam masala ... 1 pinch
Turmeric,hing ,mustard seeds for seasoning.
Green chilies ( 1 or 2 )
Salt and sugar as required.

Preparation: (Time required approx. 30 minutes).

Method

  1. First heat very little oil in a vessel and then heat the poha on low seam till it becomes crispy.
  2. Set aside and do the same with the churmuras.
  3. Set both aside and heat the oil in a vessel and add the seasoning to it.
  4. Then add peanuts, corriander seeds, kurry leaves garam-masala and green chillies and stir for a while.
  5. Then add the pohe and churmuras to it and mix well.
  6. Add salt and sugar to taste.
  7. Store the chivda in a container only after it cools down completely.
