Chick Pea Curry

Ingredients

400 gm tin Chick Peas
1 medium size Onion (finely chopped)
1/2 teaspoon grated Ginger
1/4 teaspoon mashed Garlic
1-2 Green Chillies seeded and finely chopped
1/4 teaspoon Turmeric
2 tablespoons Ghee or Vegetable Oil
2 large ripened Tomatoes (chopped)
1 teaspoon ground Coriander
1 1/2 teaspoons Garam Masala
1 1/2 tablespoons Lemon Juice
2 tablespoons chopped Coriander

Method:

  1. Drain chick peas, reserving the liquid.
  2. Cook onions, ginger, garlic, chillies and turmeric in the ghee or oil until golden and softened.
  3. Add the tomatoes and cook to soften, then add the coriander and chick peas. Cook for 10 minutes, add 1 cup of the reserved liquid and cook a further 10 minutes.
  4. Add garam masala, lemon juice and stir in the coriander. Cook for 2-3 minutes adding more liquid, if needed, to make a sauce.
  5. Serve with chapaties.
