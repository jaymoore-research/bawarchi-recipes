Chilli-Garlic Rice

Ingredients

Basmathi rice - 1 cup
Cabbage cut in to strips - 1 cup
Beans cut in to strips - 1/4 cup
Capsicum cut in to strips - 1/4 cup
Onions - 2
Spring onions - 1 small bunch
Bren sprouts - 1/4 cup
Carrot grated - 1'4 cup
Oil - 3 1/2 Tbl.sps
Salt - as required.

Grind together
Red Chillies - 7
Garlic - 5 flakes

Method

  1. Wash and soak rice for 10 minutes.
  2. Cook the rice in boiling water and when it is half cooked ,drain water and allow it to cool.
  3. Separate the grains using a fork. To this rice, mix the ground mixture of chillies and garlic.
  4. Heat oil in a broad frying pan and fry the prepared vegetables one by one as follows.
  5. Onions,beans,carrots,bean sprouts,capsicum,cabbage and spring onions.
  6. When the vegetables are tender ,add salt and cooked rice.
  7. Continue frying in a high flame till rice becomes very hot.
