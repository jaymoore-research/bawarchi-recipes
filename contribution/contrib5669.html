Ingredients

1 cup gram flour
3 cup water
1tbsp oil
1/4Ātsp mustard seed
1 cup yogurt[card]
2tbsp imli [tamarind] chatni
1tsp roast cumin seed powder
1 tsp red chili powder
Salt to taste

Total time: 30 min.
Preparation time: 7/8min.

Method

  1. Mix gram flour in water properly
  2. Heat oil in pan, putĀ mustard seed gram flour mix in same direction when it boil then put salt
  3. Sim flame cover it keep 10 min. for cook.
  4. Now ready to spread.
  5. Put some oil in kitchen platform or big plate.
  6. Here spread gramflour mixture and spread finely.
  7. Keep aside for cooling.
  8. After cooling cut square or diamond shape with the help of knife.

Serving suggestion-

  1. Put 6/7 piece in serving plate
  2. Spread mash liquid yogurt,Ā imli chatni, cumin seed powder, red chili powder, salt.

Option: 1tbsp oilĀ 1/4tsp mustard seed 10/12 fresh curry leaves 1/4Ātsp sesame seed[tilli] 1 chopped green chili Ā1/4tsp dry coconut powder

  1. Heat the oil put all things now tarka is ready
  2. After spreading the mixture put this tarka all over the place then cut it in long strips and roll it like spring roll and serve it.
