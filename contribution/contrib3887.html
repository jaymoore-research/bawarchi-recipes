Cucumber Bajji

Ingredients

1 big Cucumber
4 small Tomatoes
1 Onion
5 to 6 Green Chillies
Thick Curd.

For Seasoning

Pinch of Asafoetida
Musterd seeds
1 tsp of Oil

Method:

  1. Cut the cucumber, tomotoes and onion into small pieces.
  2. Cut the green chillies and keep it aside.
  3. Now take the pan and add oil and heat it. put the musterd seeds.
  4. Now mix cutted cucumber, tomatoes, onion and green chillies. Add curds and asafoetida to it. Mix it well. Now it is ready to serve with chapathi, poori etc.
