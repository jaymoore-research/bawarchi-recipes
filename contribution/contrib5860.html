Aloo-Gosht

Ingredients

1 kilo lamb/goat/beef meat
500 gm. small potatoes
1big onion thinly sliced
1 big onion paste
75 gm. natural yoghurt
2 tea spoon coriander powder
1 tea spoon cumin
1 1/2 teaspoon chilli powder
150 gm. ghee or oil
2 teaspoon ginger paste
2 teaspoon garlic paste
1 bay leaf
1 teaspoon turmeric
garam masala made of 3 black cardamom, 1 inch Cinnamon, 4 cloves, 5 green cardamom, 12 black paper.
salt 1 or 2 teaspoon

Method

Ā

  1. If potatoes are small, peel themĀ and prick them with fork. If the potatoes are big, peel them and cut in halves length wise.
  2. Mix all the spices and onion, ginger-garlic pastes with yoghurt.
  3. Heat oil and fry the potatoes till light golden in colour.
  4. Take them out of oil/ghee and fry sliced onion till transparent.Ā
  5. Now put yoghurt mixture in fried onions pot and stir fry for few minutes.
  6. Add meat piecesĀand stir for some time.
  7. Cover and cook on very low heat. Stir occasionally.
  8. When meat is tender put the potatoes and 4 table spoon of hot waterĀ and cook.
  9. When potatoes are tender, take off the cooker.
  10. Sprinkle garam masala and serve hot with chapati or rice. Ā
