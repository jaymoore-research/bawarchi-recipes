Chilli Eggs

Ingredients

4 Eggs hardboiled
1 tsp Redc Chilli Paste
1 tsp Green Chilli Paste
1" Ginger finely chopped
2 cloves Garlic finely chopped
2 tbsp Tamarind juice
Salt to taste
1 medium Onion finely chopped
A pinch of Ajinomoto (Mono Sodium glutamate)
1 tsp Soya Sauce (optional)
Oil for frying

Method :

  1. Peel the harboiled eggs and deep fry in oil till the cover is crisp & brown.
  2. Cut them into half lengthwise & keep aside
  3. Heat a little oil in a pan
  4. Fry the chopped ginger & garlic pieces
  5. Add chopped onions & fry well.
  6. Add the red chilli paste & green chilli paste & fry for 1 minute.
  7. Add soya sauce, tamarind juice, salt and let it boil for a while.
  8. Turn off heat. Add the eggs and mix well till they are coated with the sauce completely.

Tastes good with Chinese fried rice or even with roti/ paratha. Non egg eaters can use potatoes instead of eggs.
