Rice Flakes Upma

Ingredients

Rice flakes 200gms
Green chillies 6-7
Urad dal 1ts
Turmeric 1/2ts
Ginger garlic paste 1ts
Salt to taste
Onion 1(big) finely chopped
Putnalla powder 3-4ts
Oil as required

Method:

  1. In a pan put oil, urad dal, onions, turmeric powder and then ginger garlic paste. Fry till onions are golden brown.
  2. Take the rice falkes and put them in water for a little while. Take them and squeeze water from it and add this rice flakes to the above pan mixture.
  3. Add salt and putunalla powder to it. Mix the mixture well and then place the pan on the stove for abt 3 minutes and take it out.
  4. Garnish with coriander.
  5. Serve this with any pickle.
