Quick 4N Easy Tomato Rice

Ingredients

a.. 1 Cup Basmati Rice
b.. 1 Large Sliced Onion
c.. 3 Large Tomatoes
d.. 2 Tablespoons Coconut (Grated). You can use the frozen grated coconut available in Indian stores.
e.. 1 Handful Cashews or Groundnuts
f.. 5-6 Curry Leaves
g.. 4-5 Green Chilies
h.. 1 Teaspoon Chilly Powder
i.. 1/2 Teaspoon Turmeric Powder
j.. 1 Teaspoon Split Black Gram (Urad Dhal)
k.. Handful Chopped Cilantro (Coriander Leaves/Hara Dania/Kotmeeri)
l.. 1 Teaspoon Mustard Seeds
m.. 1 Teaspoon Cumin Seeds (Jeera)
n.. 1/4 Teaspoon Tamarind Concentrate
o.. Salt according to taste

Method:

  1. Cook rice and set aside in a big plate to cool so that rice grains are separated.
  2. Add 1 Tablespoon of oil and fry the Cashews or Groundnuts a little, remove from oil and keep them aside.
  3. Pour 2 additional Tablespoons of oil to the same pan.
  4. Add Mustard seeds and Cumin seeds.
  5. Once mustard seeds starts to crackle add Urad Dhal, Curry Leaves and chopped green chilies.
  6. Next add sliced onions and fry till golden brown and then add tomatoes and continue to fry.
  7. Add tamarind and chilly powder, turmeric powder, salt, grated coconut and cook till the oil separates from the pan.
  8. Mix the already cooked rice to this and stir it well for a minute or two.
  9. Garnish with roasted cashews/groundnuts and chopped cilantro.

Here is your yummy tomato rice to enjoy. Hope you like it. Happy cooking!!!

Serving Suggestion: Serve hot with cucumber and onion raita.
