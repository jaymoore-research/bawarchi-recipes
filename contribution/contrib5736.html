Simple Methi Dosa

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients

Raw rice - 3 cups
Urad dal - 1 cup
Methi seeds -Ā1/4 cup
Salt - to taste

Method

  1. Soak the rice, dal and methi seed overnight.
  2. Grind the rice, dal and the methi seeds together in the mixer till a smooth batter is formed.
  3. Add salt and mix well.
  4. This dosa need not be fermented like the normal dosa. Do not add much water. Make the dosas.
  5. This dosa will be very soft at the same time crisp with a very different taste.
  6. Try this recipe and give me your feedback.
