Bread Balls

Ingredients

12 slices bread
1 cup capsicum, finely chopped
1/2 cup button mushroom, finely chopped
1 cup cottage cheese grated
1 tbsp soya sauce
a pinch of ajinamoto
1 tsp pepper powder
oil for frying
salt to taste

Method

  1. Mix all the ingredients except oil and bread in a bowl.
  2. Keep it aside for 15 min.
  3. Now cut the bread along its edges so that the hard brown part is removed.
  4. Taking a slice of bread dip it in water just for a second and remove.
  5. Squeeze out the excess water from the bread slice.
  6. Take a small quantity of the mixture from the bowl and keep it on this bread.
  7. Now cover the mixture with the bread making it into a shape of ball.
  8. Repeat similarily for the remaining bread slices.
  9. Heat oil in wok for deep frying.
  10. Put 3 to 4 balls into the wok.
  11. Deep fry on low flame till the balls turn into light brown in color.
  12. Serve hot with tomato sauce.
