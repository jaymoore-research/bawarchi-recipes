Brussels Sprouts

Ingredients

1) Brussels Sprouts - 1/2 Lb
2) Onions cut in the form of rings - 1/2 Cup
3) Chilli powder - 1/2 Tsp
4) Garam Masala powder - 1/2 Tsp
5) Salt - 1 Tsp
6) Oil - To fry onion rings and to roast brussels sprouts
7) Jeera - 1 Tsp

Method:

  1. Wash and dry the sprouts.
  2. Slit open them from the top.
  3. Mix salt, Chilli powder, garam masala powder and fill inside each sprout.
  4. Apply the remaining powder all over the outside of each sprout.
  5. Microwave the sprouts for 10 mts till they are cooked well.
  6. Heat some oil in a fry pan.
  7. Fry the onion rings till they become crisp and golden brown and keep them aside.
  8. In the remaining oil, add jeera and the cooked sprouts and cook for 2 mts.
  9. Granish the cooked sprouts with the onion rings before serving.

Note: Frozen Onion rings can also be used. These Onion rings can be prepared in the oven.

Serve hot with rice and chapathis
