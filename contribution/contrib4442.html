Chapati Ka Chiwda

Ingredients

1. Leftover Chapattis - four (not more than one day old)
2. Onion - one big size
3. Tomato - one big size
4. Green Chillies - 5 or 6
5. Yoghurt (Dahi) - 100 ml.
6. Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp
7. Chilli powder - 1/2 tsp
8. Jeera - 1/2 tsp
9. Mustard seeds - 1/2 tsp
10. Curry leaves - one stalk
11. Coriander leaves
12. Salt to taste
13. Cooking Oil - 2 - 3 tbsp

Method:

  1. Cut chapati into small pieces and grind them into mixer for one minute. (Please do not grind fine)
  2. Sliced onion and chopped tomato into small pieces.
  3. Cut green chilli vertically.
  4. In pan or kadai, heat 2 - 3 tbsp cooking oil. Add mustard seeds, jeera and curry leaves. When mustard seeds starts splitting then add sliced onion and green chillies. Fry until onion turns golden brown.
  5. Then add chopped tomatoes. Stir for a while.
  6. Add turmeric powder, chilli powder and salt. Stir it for a while. Add 100 ml yoghurt and mix it well. Add grinded chapati and mix it well.
  7. Keep it on slow fire for about five minutes. Later stir it well for a while.
  8. Garnish with coriander leaves. Delicious Chapatti ka Chiwda is ready to serve.
