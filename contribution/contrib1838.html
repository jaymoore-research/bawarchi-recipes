Kalia (Lamb chops)

Ingredients

Lamb chops : 2 lbs
Turmeric : 1 1/2 tsp.
Ginger pwd : 1 /2 tsp.
Fennel pwd : 1 Tbsp
Cinnamon : 1 med stick
Cardamoms : 3 - 4
Bay leaves : 2
Yogurt : 1/2 cup.
Salt to taste.
Oil : 3 - 4 tbsp
Asafetida or Hing : 1/4 tsp
Cloves : 2 - 3
Black cardamoms : 2-3
Cumin Pwd. : 1 tsp.
Garam masala : 1 tsp.
Water : 4 - 5 cups

Method:

  1. Wash the lamb chops and keep aside to drain.
  2. Heat oil in a pressure cooker or a pot (whichever you prefer, although p.cooker saves time.)
  3. Add hing, bay leaves, cloves, cinnamon stick, both the cardamoms.
  4. Add all the spices, stirring it a little. Be careful not to burn the spices. Keep the flame medium.
  5. Add the yogurt to the spices and keep stirring till you see all the spices separating from the oil.
  6. Now add the salt and the lamb chops.
  7. Cook it a little until it is no longer pink.
  8. Add 4 cups of water (more, if you cook it in a pot).
  9. Pressure cook it till it gives out 3 - 4 whistles.
  10. Let it cool. Check the meat if it is tender. Cook it accordingly.
  11. When ready, sprinkle the garam masala on it and always serve it hot with rice.
