Vege Kola

Ingredients

Chenna dal - 1 cup
Carrot - 1 grated
Beans finally chopped - 1 cup
Cabbage grated - 1 cup
Potato smashed - 2 nos

To Grind:

Garlic - 10 pods
Ginger little
Masala powder - half tsp
Pudina leaves little
Corriander
Mango powder little [optional]
Cumin powder - half tsp
Red chilli powder - 2 tsp
Salt
Lemon juice little
Maida - 2 tsp
Oil to fry

Method:

  1. Cook chenna dal and potato in a cooker.
  2. Strain the water and mash.
  3. Cut the vegetable into very small pieces.
  4. Add all the ingredients expect oil. Add 2 tsp ghee. No need to add water.
  5. Give a shape as you wish or like kola.
  6. Fry it in a hot oil.

Note: If you wish you can add grated cauliflower and any spinach. (finely chopped)
