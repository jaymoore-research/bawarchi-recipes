Lauki (Green Gourd) Kofta Curry

Ingredients

For Kofta:

Green Gourd about 1/2 Kg
Besan 1+1/2 Cup
Salt to taste
Chopped Cilantro
Oil for Deep Frying.
For Curry:

1/2 tsp Tumeric
1/2 tsp Chilli Powder
1/2 Paprika
1 Tsp Ground Zeera
1 Tbsp Ground Coriander
8 Black Pepper Corns
1/2 tsp Fenugreek (Meethi Seeds)
1 Black Cardamoms
4 Cloves
2 Tsp Garlic Paste (Fresh)
1+ 1/2 Tsp Ginger Paste
Water as needed.

Method For Curry:

  1. Grind Black Cardamom, Cloves and Black Pepper.
  2. In a bowl add the ground masala and all the other ingredients, except Methi, grind in a grinder to make a smooth paste, set aside.
  3. Remove skin from Lauki and grate it, (Kaddukash)
  4. Add Besan, Cilantro and salt to grated Lauki. Add little bit of water and make into a batter. Make sure batter is more on the thick side to be able to handle it for making Pakora. Stir for about 15 minutes, by hand, to make it light for frying.
  5. In a skillet add enough oil to deep fry. Drop batter in hot oil with a spoon to make small size pakoras. Fry until brown, set aside on a paper towel.
  6. In a pot heat 2 tbsp oil, add Methi and brown. Add Masala paste and 1 tbsp of water, stir until water evaporates and masala starts to turn brown. Add about 1+1/2 cup of water (or as needed). Cover and cook for about 15 minutes on medium heat.
  7. Add Pakoras and cover to cook for another 20 minutes. Continue to check if there is enough water, add water if needed. Lower heat to minimum. Wait for oil to float on top. Pakoras are to immersed in curry. Turn heat off. Koftas are ready to be garnished with chopped cilantro.
  8. Serve hot with Rice or Bread. When cold liquid will be saoked up by Pakoras, still taste great.
