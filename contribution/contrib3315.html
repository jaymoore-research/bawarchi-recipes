Milk-Almond Sweet

Ingredients

Milk --- 1 lt
Almond --- 10 nos
Sugar --- 3 cups
Rawa --- 1 tbsp
Ghee --- 1 cup

Method:

  1. Boil milk till it reduces into quarter litre.
  2. Soak almond for about an hour, peel off and grind them into fine paste.
  3. Add sugar, rawa and almond paste into milk and cook till the mixture thickens.
  4. Now add ghee and switch of the stove.
  5. Stir continuously till the mixture leaves the sides rolls into a ball.
  6. Pat this mixture in a plate cut it into desired shapes.
