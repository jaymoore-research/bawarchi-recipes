Senaga Vadaa

Ingredients

1-1/2 cups Chana (small variety)
5-6 (mild hot) Green Chillies
1&1/2inch Ginger
3-4 Cloves Garlic
1 medium sized onion (optional)
salt to taste.

Method

  1. Soak the chana overnight the previous day.
  2. Grind the soaked chana with the chillies, ginger, garlic & salt.
  3. Don't make a complete thin paste of it.
  4. Grinding should be thick & little of chana pieces must be seen here & there. (Hope I'm clear)
  5. Then after taking it out in a bowl, dice the onions into small pieces & add to the vadaa batter.
  6. Heat oil in a kadai, make small flat cakes of the batter either on your palm (moistened with water) or on an oiled / moistened plastic sheet.
  7. Then on medium heat fry the vadaas nicely till they appear reddish brown.
  8. Serve them with Ketchup or with any chutney.
  9. But they can be had just like that too. You can add green chillies according to the degree of hotness you prefer.

Hope you will like it, I love it! Good Luck!!!
