Pizzaco

Ingredients

3 large Tortillas
3 tbsp Kidney Bean Paste (cooked and seasoned)
1/4 cup Parmesan Cheese (grated)
1/4 cup Cheddar Cheese (grated)
1 tsp Pizza Seasoning
1 tbsp Tomato Ketchup
1/2 cup sliced Onions
1/4 cup sliced Mushrooms
1 Tomato chopped
1/2 tsp Oil
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Saute onions, mushrooms and tomatoes in the oil and season with salt and pepper and set aside.
  2. Spread little parmesan cheese between two tortillas to form a base.
  3. Now spread the bean paste on the tortilla evenly and cover it with the remaining tortilla.
  4. Take the ketchup and apply evenly on the top of the tortilla. Now spread the sauted veg like toppings and season with salt and the pizza seasoning.
  5. Cover the veg. with the cheeses evenly.
  6. Bake it at 350oF for 10 min or until the cheese melts.
  7. Cut into triangles and serve with Chilli Sauce.
