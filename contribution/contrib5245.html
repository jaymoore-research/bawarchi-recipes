Khade Masala Chicken

Ingredients

Chicken: 1 kg cut into small pieces
Whole Red Chillies: 12 to 15
Onions: 3 medium chopped
Salt: 1 tsp, heaped
Water: 2 cup
Green Chillies: 3 to 4 chopped
Oil: 1/2 cup
Cinnamon: 2 sticks
Ginger: 1 tbs chopped
Garlic: 1 tsp chopped
Tomatoes: 3 chopped
Black Pepper: 1/2 tsp (grounded)
Fresh Coriander Leaves: few chopped
Cloves: 6
Black Pepper: 8
Method:

  1. Put chicken with chopped onion; whole spices and chopped ginger/ garlic;chopped red chillies and water
  2. Cook on slow fire
  3. When water dries, add in oil chopped tomatoes and green chillies
  4. Bhuno the mixture very well when chicken is tender
  5. Add chopped fresh coriander leaves,and sprinkle black pepper on it,
  6. Put on dum for 15 minutes till oil floats on top.
  7. Serve hot with naan, salad and lemon juice.
