Gobbi Koora

Ingredients

1/2 kg White Pumpkin
100 gms Red Pumpkin
1 Sweet Potato
1 Potato
100 gms Yam
1/4 cup Chick Peas (soaked overnight)
100 gms French Beans
1/4 fresh Coconut (grated)
1 tsp Sesame seeds
2 tsps Coriander seeds
1 tsp Chana dal
1/4 tsp Udad dal
5 dry Red Chillies
Salt to taste
Turmeric powder
1 tsp Cooking Oil
1 cup Tamarind extract

Method:

  1. Pressure cook the vegetables.
  2. In a hard bottomed pan, add oil, add coriander seeds, sesame seeds, chana dal and udad dal and fry till the lentils start to change colour. Add the coconut and fry for some time.
  3. Grind the above lentils and coconut smoothly.
  4. Add the tamarind extract, salt and turmeric powder to the boiled vegetables. Boil for some time. Add the above ground mixture to the vegetables and boil for five minutes.
