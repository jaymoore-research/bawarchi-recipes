Sai Bhaji

Ingredients

Fresh Spinach leaves : 2 cups washed and chopped
Eggplant/Brinjal : 5 small , washed and cut
Gram/Chana dal : 1/2 cup , washed and soaked in water for half an hour Tomatoes : 2 chopped (you may also use 3 tsp. tomato paste)
Vegetable Oil : 2 tsp.
Salt : According to taste
Coriander/Dhania powder : 1/2 tsp.
Chilli/Lal Mirch Powder : 1/2 tsp.

Method :-

  1. Heat oil in a pressure cooker, add coriander and chilli powder to it. Stir for a minute.
  2. Add tomatoes , stir for a while and then add rest of the ingredients .Close the cooker.
  3. Allow one whistle at high flame. Take off the stove and wait for 10-12 minutes for steam to die.
  4. Your Sai Bhaji is all set to be eaten with puri, chapati or rice.
