Spicy   Poories

Ingredients

1 cup Wheat flour
1 cup All purpose flour (Maida)
1 tsp Chilli Powder
Salt to taste
Pinch of asefotida
1/4 tsp cumin powder
4 tsp oil

Method

Mix the above ingredients into a dough by adding water and 4 teaspoons of oil.
Keep it aside for about 15-20 minutes.
Make poories and deep fry in oil.
Serve hot with pickle or chutney.
