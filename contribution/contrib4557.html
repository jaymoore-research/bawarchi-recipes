Spicy Clams Masala

Ingredients

Clams (washed & shelled) 1 cup
Onion chopped (medium) 1
Tomato chopped (medium) 1
Green Chillies slit 2-3
Coriander leaves 1/2 cup
Ginger Garlic Paste 1 tbsp
Turmeric Powder 1 tsp
Coriander Powder 1 tsp
Red Chilli Powder 1/2 tbsp (more if you like it spicy)
Fish Masala 1/2 tbsp
Basil leaves 2-3 (Dry basil 1 tsp)
Oil 2-3 tbsp
Water 1 cup
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Heat oil in a kadai & Fry the green chillies and ginger- garlic paste for 1 min. Add the onion and fry till soft. Stir in the tomato and cook for 4-5 mins.
  2. Add clams and cook for 2-3 minutes. Now add all the powders, herbs and salt. Mix thoroughly as to coat all the clams. Add water and cook till the clams are done. Add more water if needed.
  3. Serve hot with chapati.

This recipe can also be used for other shellfish like shrimp and mussels.
