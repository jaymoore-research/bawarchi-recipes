Mint Lime Juice

Ingredients

7 teaspoon Sugar
400 ml Water
4 leaves of fresh Mint
Juice of one medium sized Lemon
1/5 portion of an Apple peeled and sliced in small pieces.

Method

  1. Dilute the sugar in the water.
  2. Add everything else and put it in the mixer for a minute. The drink is ready.
  3. If you like you can add a bit of orange peel or lemon skin in the mixer. Optional - sieve it. ( for 2 persons) You may try out with some other fruits (instead of apple) as well.
