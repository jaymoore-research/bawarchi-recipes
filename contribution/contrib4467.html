Bread Upma

Ingredients

5 Dry Bread slices (each cut into nine squares)
1 big Onion (finely chopped)
1/2 tsp Dhaniya-Jeera powder
A pinch of Red Chilli powder
1/2 tsp Mustard seeds
1 tsp Udad dal
3 tsps of Oil
Salt to taste
A pinch of Turmeric powder

Method:

  1. In a hard bottomed pan, add oil. Add mustard seeds and after it starts spluttering, add udad dal.
  2. Add the onions and fry till it changes colour. Add the bread pieces and sprinkle a little water to soften the bread.
  3. Add salt, turmeric powder, dhaniya-jeera powder and chilli powder. Mix well in a low flame.

Notes:

This requires very little salt as the bread already contains salt.
