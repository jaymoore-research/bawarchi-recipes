Bread Upma

Ingredients

10 Bread Slices( make it into small pieces)
1/2 tbsp. Mustard
1/2 tbsp. urad dal
4-5 Green chillies (chopped)
small piece of ginger(scraped)
asafoetida
1 or 1 and 1/2 onion finely cut
small bunch of cilantro finely chopped

Method:

  1. Season mustard seed, urad dal, chilli, ginger and asafoetida with little oil and fry the onion till light brown.
  2. Then put very little salt and stir well.( you can also cook without salt ) then put the bread pieces and stir for a while till it becomes light brown.

Serve it with chutney or pickle.
