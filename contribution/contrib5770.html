Kashmiri Biryani

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients

1/2 Kg basmati rice.
1Kg meat cut into large pieces.
200gm yogurt. 1/2 tsp chilli powder.
1tsp lemon juice.
Salt to taste.
100gm butter or ghee.
1 medium onion, finely sliced.
4 cloves. 4 green cardamamoms.
1 tsp cumin seeds. Ā

For the Yakhni:
1 tsp chopped ginger., 2.5 cm piece cinnamon.
1/4 tsp grated nutmeg or grounded mace.
1 chopped green chilli.
12 whole peppercorns.
Ā

For garnishing:
2 tbsp slivered almonds.
1 medium,onion finely sliced and fried till golden.
2tbsp raisins.lightly fried. 2 hard boiled eggs, quartered.

Method

  1. Pick and wash rice .soak in cold water for half an hour .
  2. Drain. Wash meat well.Add to it all the ingredients for the yakhni along with 1 litre water.
  3. Pressure cook for 10 minutes after the cooker reaches maximum pressure.
  4. Take out the meat with a slotted spoon, strain the liquid and discard the contents of the strainer. Keep the stock aside.
  5. Meanwhile, combine the yogurt, chilli powder lemon juice and 1 tsp salt.Marinate the meat in it for 2 hours.
  6. Melt half the butter or ghee in a frying pan. fry the onion till golden.
  7. Bruise the whole garam masala and add along with the cumin. stir fry.
  8. Add the rice and fry for 7-8 minutes, stirring frequently, or until the rice turns opaque.
  9. Heat the stock and add to the rice along with 1tsp salt. Cover the pan.
  10. When the liquid comes to a boil, lower heat. Cook till the water is absorbed and the rice is fluffy. Lightly stir with a fork.
  11. In a frying pan, melt the remaining butter or ghee.
  12. Add the mutton and the yogurt.Fry for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
  13. Reduce heat and fry for a further 10 minutes.
  14. In a large pan layer the rice and meatĀ beginning and ending with rice.
  15. Cover the pan tightly.
  16. CookĀ on a low flame for 15 minutes. you may put a tawaĀunderneath for the last 10 minutes.
  17. Alternatively, it can also be finished an oven preheat to 180oCĀ bake for 20 minutes.
  18. Garnish with the fried onions. raisins almonds and quartered eggs. Serves 6.
