Capsicum Chutney

Ingredients

1 Green squash (Medium size)
1 Red capsicum (Big One)
3-4 Green chillies
1 tsp Cumin seeds
3-4 Garlic Cloves
3-4 tsp of Oil
Small ball of Tamarind
Coriander leaves
Salt to Taste

For seasoning:

1 tsp Mustard seeds
1 tsp Urad dal
2-3 whole Red chillies
5 Curry leaves
2 tsp Oil

Method:

  1. Cut squash and Red Capsicum into small pieces.
  2. Heat a Pan with oil and add Cumin Seeds, Garlic and Green Chillies. Fry them till they turn Brown.
  3. Add the Squash and Red Pepper pieces and keep on cooking until they are tender.
  4. Add the Coriander leaves and turn off the heat.
  5. Soak the Tamarind in water and make a thinck paste.
  6. Grind all these into a coarse paste by adding salt.

Seasoning:

Heat oil and add Mustard, urad dal, red chillies, curry leaves and add this to the Chutney.

If you like it more hot add 2-3 tsp of Red Chilli Pickle to this chutney. It really tastes Great!
