Egg Masala

Ingredients

6 Eggs
1 1/2 Onion
4 Green chillies
1 Tomato
4 table spoons grated Coconut
1 table spoon grated Dhania Powder
1/2 table spoon grated Jeera Powder
1 table spoon grated Ginger Garlic paste
1/2 table spoon grated Garam Masala
A pinch of Methi Powder
Little Tamarind (as your wish)

Method:

  1. Soak the tamarind in little water.
  2. Cut the green chillies and one full onion and grind them. Add the coconut powder also while grinding.
  3. Now cut the 1/2 onion into small pieces and also cut the tomato into small pieces.
  4. Boil the eggs and peel them.
  5. Take a cooking bowl and do the phodni. Then add the ginger garlic paste and sliced onions.
  6. When the onions turn into golden brown colour add sliced tomato to it.
  7. When the tomato also get cooked add the grinded paste of onions etc to it. Add required salt and cover the bowl with a lid. After ten minutes add the juice of the soaked tamarind to the curry.
  8. Make some holes into the eggs using a fork or a knife and add them also into the curry. Cover it and leave it for 10 minutes.
  9. Then add dhania powder, jeera powder, methi powder and garam masala.
  10. You can decorate it with some corriander leaves.
  11. Allow the curry to be cooked for some more time so that the eggs also get some masala into them.
