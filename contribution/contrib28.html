Mix   Flour   &   Cabbage   Uttappam

Ingredients :

1 cup wheat flour
1 cup rice flour
1 cup gram flour
1 cup corn flour
1 cup cabbage finely chopped
2 cups curds well beaten with water added to make buttermilk
1 medium onion finely cut into long strips
6 to 7 green chillis finely chopped
2 pcs. of garlic crushed
1/2 tsp. turmeric
Oil
1 tsp. sugar
Salt to taste

Method

  1. Mix all flours together
  2. Add cabbage
  3. Add curds (buttermilk) to make the batter.
    (The batter should not be too thick or too thin) Add more buttermilk if needed according to the consistency of batter.

  4. Mix well and keep it aside for about 7 to 8 hours in a warm place
Before preparing the Uttappam

In the batter:

  • Add onion, green chillis, garlic, turmeric, sugar and salt
  • Mix it well
  • Heat a non-stick pan and grease it with little oil
  • When the temperature is right, pour a large spoonful of the prepared batter.
  • Spread it to a little thick round shape
  • Put oil on all sides of the Uttappam
  • Flip once and let the other side cook well
  • Repeat for all of the remaining

Serve hot with green chutney , tomato ketchup or pickels.

Hope you all will enjoy this recipe! Have a good time eating!!
