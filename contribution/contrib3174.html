Aloo Gobi(Potato- Cauliflower)

Ingredients

1. Aloo - 1 large
2. Cauliflower- 1/2
3. Onion paste - 1
4. Tomatoes- 2
5. Red Chili powder- 1 tbsp
6. Coriander and Cumin powder- 1 tea spoon
7. Turmeric powder- 1 teaspoon
8. Salt as per taste
9. Garlic paste

Method

  1. Boil potato, turmeric powder and cauliflower with salt in it for some time (nearly 2 min)
  2. Remove the boiled water from it.
  3. Take frying pan and pour oil, garlic and oinion paste. Stir it, till the oninon colour changes to brown
  4. Add all dry powder to it.
  5. Add cauliflower and potato. Mix and add one cup water
  6. Add chop tomatoes and salt
  7. Cover the pan and heat it on low heat till it get cooked.
  8. Serve with Rice or Chapati.
