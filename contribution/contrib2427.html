Corn Flakes Mixture

Ingredients

Corn Flakes - 1/2 pound
Green Chillies - 6
Salt - 1 tea spoon
Sugar - 1 tea spoon
Chilli Powder - 1 tea spoon
Ground Nuts - 1/4 cup
Curry Leaves - 20
Oil to fry

Method

  1. Deep Fry the Corn flakes and keep them aside
  2. Deep Fry the Curry leaves separately
  3. Cut the Green Chillies in 1 inch pieces and deep fry them separately
  4. Deep fry the Ground nuts.
  5. Mix the Corn flakes, Curry leaves, Green chillies, Ground nuts, salt sugar and chilli powder.
