Vegetable Korma

Ingredients

1 Cup Beans
half cup Carrot
1 Potato

half cup Peas
half cup Ground coconut
small piece Ginger
3 flakes Garlic
1 Onion
half tbs Cumin seeds
1 tbs Corriander powder
few Corriander leaves
half tbs Chillie powder
half Lime
half tbs Turmeric powder

  1. Take oil in a pan. Add the sliced onions.
  2. After that add ginger, garlic, corriander powder, chillie powder, salt & fresh corriander leaves .
  3. Fry it nicely.
  4. Then add the vegetables & pressure cook till done.
  5. When the vegetables are cooked add the ground coconut and little water to it & let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Squeeze lime to it & the veg korma is ready.
  7. Serve hot with chapatis.
