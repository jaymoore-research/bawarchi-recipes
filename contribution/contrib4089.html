Chatpat Aam Chutney

Ingredients

Raw Mango - 500 gms
Sugar - 500 gms
Raisin- 100 gm.

Dry Masala:

Mustard seed - 1 tsp
Sanuf - 2 tsp
Methi-1 tsp
Jeera - 1 tsp
(all fried and then powderred)

Method:

  1. Grate the mango and remove its water.
  2. Add the sugar with mango properlly.
  3. Heat a pan and add the mango and sugar mixture.
  4. Cook it with a medium flame carefully.
  5. When its turns soft and sticky, then add the raisin and the powdered masala. Mix it well and cool it.
  6. Store it in a glass container for long days. You can enjoy it with anything.
