Sagu

Ingredients

Vegetables:

1 packet of frozen Spinach or 3 bunches of fresh Baby Dill.
2 cups of french cut Beans.
3 Carrots cut into thin, 1 inch long pieces.
3 peeled and chopped Potatoes.

Ingredients for gravy:

2 cups of fresh Coconut pieces.
2 Jalapenos.
5 -6 Mint leaves (optional if you are using baby dill instead of spinach with other vegetables)
2 medium sized Onions chopped.
3 spoons of Chana dal.
4 Pepper corns.
3 Cloves
1 inch Cinnamon
1 inch of Ginger.

Method :

  1. Fry the onions till brown.
  2. Fry Jalapenos, mint leaves, pepper corns, cloves cinnamon and ginger.
  3. Fry chana dal till brown
  4. Grind all the above with coconut very finely in a blender.
  5. Boil all the vegetables with a little salt added to them.
  6. Mix the finely ground mixture with the vegetables and add salt to taste. Boil this mixture for 15 minutes
  7. Serve hot with puris or chapathi.

Bon Appetit!
