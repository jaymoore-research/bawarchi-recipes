Mango Kuzhambu

Ingredients

Mango - 1 big
Mustard seeds - 1/4 spoon
Oil - 2 spoonful
Fenugreek powder - 1/4 spoon
Hing - 1/4 spoon
Green Chillies - 4
Red Chillies - 4
Tamarind (lime size)
Chilli Powder - 1/2 spoon
Jaggery/Sugar - 2 spoons
Curry Leaves
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Cut mango into small pieces. Dont have to peel the skin.
  2. Heat oil in a kadai, add mustard seeds, fenugreek powder, hing.
  3. When mustard splutters, add red chillies and fry for few seconds.
  4. Add cut mangoes and cut green chillies and fry till mango gets tender.
  5. Add tamarind water to it.
  6. Add chilli powder, salt, jaggery/sugar and allow it to boil for 5 inutes.
  7. Add curry leaves.

Cooking time: 10 minutes
