Egg and Veg Moliee

Ingredients

Eggs - 3
Potatoes- 200 gm
Cauliflower - 100 gm
Carrots- 100 gm
Tomatoes - 150 gm
Onions - 200 gm
Green Chillies -2-3
Coconut-1/2
Lemon-1
Ginger-small piece
Turmeric powder-2 tsp
Oil - 15 ml
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Hard boli the eggs, peel the potatoes & quater them.
  2. Wash the cauliflower & break it into small florets.
  3. Peel carrots & cut it into pieces.
  4. Slice onions & ginger & slit the green chillies.
  5. Grate coconut & prepare 3 extractions.
  6. Extract lime juice.
  7. Heat oil & fry onions, green chillies & ginger.
  8. Add turmeric powder & potatoes. Add third extraction of coconut milk and cook till potatoes are 3/4 -th done.
  9. Then add remaining vegetables (ie) carrots & cauliflower & cook them. After well-cooked, add remaining of the extraction of the coconut milk & lime juice & simmer.
  10. Finally add eggs & cook till done.
