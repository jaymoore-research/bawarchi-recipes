Sesame Curry

Ingredients

Peas - 1 lb packet

For Seasoning:

10-12 Cashews, 1/2 tsp mustard seeds, 1/2 tsp Cumin, Curry leaves, 1/4 th tsp turmeric, 2 tsp cooking oil.

Grind to fine Paste:

1/2 cup Sesame, 4-5 Red chillie pods, 1 tsp Cumin, 1 tsp rice flour (or 2 tsps soaked rice) and 1/2 cup water.

Method:

  1. In a pan heat 2 tsp cooking oil.
  2. Add the seasonings.
  3. When the seeds pop add the peas and fry for a few minutes.
  4. Now, add 2 cups of water and bring to boil.
  5. Once the peas are tendor add the ground paste. Add salt to taste. Bring to boil.
  6. Top with 1 tsp Ghee.

(Other variations: You can use Whole corn, Beans, Tindora, Spinach, Ridge Gourd instead of Peas or in combination of Peas)

TOFU Scramble - Desi Style (Served at least once a week at my home!) 1 Packet - Hard Tofu
6 green chillies
1/2 TSP Cumin seeds
1/2 TSP Red Chillie powder
1/4 tsp Turmeric
3-4 tsp cooking oil.
2 onions cut into small pieces

  1. Wash the Tofu in running water for 4-5 minutes.
  2. Squeeze excess water off the TOFU block.
  3. Scramble the tofu into a non-stick pan.
  4. Without any oil jus fry the tofu on the nonstick pan till the smell in TOFU disappears. Set aside.
  5. Heat 3-4 TSP cooking oil in the pan. Add cumin. Once the cumin is sizzling add the onions, and green chillies. Fry for 5 minutes.
  6. Now add the scrambled TOFU and fry for 4-5 mts.
  7. Add red chillie powder and salt to taste.

(Works good with Bread in the sandwich maker, or with rice or chapatis. The tofu scramble stays good in the refrigerator without any change of taste like scrambled eggs would. And above all TOFU is better for health).
