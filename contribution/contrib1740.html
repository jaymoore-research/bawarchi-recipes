Mixed Vegetable Curry

Ingredients

1 cup cut cooked Carrot
1 cup cut cooked Beans
1 cup cut Green Pepper
2 Potatoes cooked
1 big Tomato cut
1 big cut Onion
2 tsp Oil
1 Green Chilli
1 tsp Dhaniya Powder
1 tsp Chilli Powder
Salt
Lemon Juice
1 tsp Turmeric

Method:

  1. Cook a few of veggies before hand or side by side (as mentioned).
  2. Heat 2 tsp oil and add cut onion and 1 minced green chilli.
  3. Add turmeric. Fry for a minute on high flame.
  4. After it turns transparent add dhaniya powder and chilli powder
  5. Add cut green pepper and fry. Add little salt, sprinkle some water, cover and let cook
  6. Just before green pepper is cooked add cut tomatoes and mix well.
  7. After tomatoes are done add rest of cooked veggies. Mix well. U can add some more masala if desired.
  8. Garnish with lemon juice.
