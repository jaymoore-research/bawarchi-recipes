Ridge Guard (peerkangai) with Prawns

Ingredients

2 Medium sized Ridge Guard.
1 Medium sized Onion
1 lb Prawns
1/2 Tsp Red Chilli powder
1/2 Tsp Dhaniya powder
1/4 Tsp Turmeric powder
4-5 Tsp Coconut paste/milk
1-2 Tsp of Oil
Salt to taste.

For Seasoning:

1 Tsp Mustard
1 Tsp Urad dhal
Curry Leaves

Method:

  1. Peel off the skin from the ridge guard and cut it into small pieces. Pressure cook it for about 1 whistle with 1/2 cup of water. (keep in mind that the vegetable itself will have sufficient water in itself). You can also microwave it if you have a microwave oven.
  2. Wash the prawns thoroughly, add some turmeric and salt to it and keep it aside.
  3. Heat the oil in a pan, add mustard seeds and urad dhal, after the mustards splutter add the onions to it and fry it till becomes soft and translucent.
  4. Now wash the prawns once again with water and sqeeze it well to drain all the water and then add it to the onions and fry well till it shrinks.
  5. Now add the cooked ridge guard, chilli powder, dhaniya powder, salt, 1/2 cup water stir well and close it with a lid.
  6. Open the lid after 3 mins and add the coconut milk to it. Stir it well for about 5 mins and then put off the heat.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve it with hot rice or chapatis.
