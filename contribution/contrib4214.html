Easy Taco Salad

Ingredients

15 oz. can of Kidney Beans
1 cup Salsa (hot or mild salsa depending on your palate)
1 8 oz. package of grated Mild Cheddar Cheese
1/2 Onion chopped finely
1/2 Green Bell Pepper chopped finely
(For colour - you can add bit of Red or Yellow Bell Peppers if you choose - otherwise omit)
5 or 6 Lettuce Leaves cut into small pieces
1 Tomato chopped
4 or 5 Taco Shells - crumbled (can substitute corn tortilla chips here also)

Method:

  1. Drain and wash the Kidney Beans. Mash them with a spatula to make a bean paste. To the bean paste add the salsa to create a pinkish looking mixture.
  2. Place half of the bean mixture on a microwave proof serving plate.
  3. Sprinkle half of the onion, peppers, and cheese.
  4. Microwave on high for 3 minutes or until the cheese melts.
  5. Garnish with lettuce, tomatoes, and the crushed taco shells (or tortilla chips). Repeat the same process in a second plate! Takes less than 15 minutes and Makes two plate fulls. Enjoy for lunch or dinner!!
