Thirshman Kuttan

Ingredients

1. Onion 1 small
2. Potato 1 small
3. Tomatoes 2
4. Any vegetable you like can use
5. Coriander leaves
6. Ginger
7. Salt to taste if wanted
8. Mustard seeds
9. Jeeram (cummin seeds)

Method:

  1. Take the leftover of sambhar and rasam and mix them and boil them for few minutes.
  2. Take a pan and put mustard seeds cummin seeds and ginger when it splutters put onion, tomatoes, potatoes and any vegtable fo your choices and fry a little and pour it in the leftovers and boil it for few minutes.
  3. When ready garnish with corriander leaves and serve hot with Chappati, Roti or Rice. Enjoy the dish!!!
