Eggplant Rice

Ingredients

1 Eggplant cut into small cubes
1 Onion finely chopped
1 1/2 cups of Basmati rice

For Masala
1 - tsp of Channa Dal
1 - tsp of Urad dal
1 - tsp of Coriander powder
Couple of red chili according to taste

This masala can be made and kept ready with more quantity and can be added when ever eggplant curry is made.

Method

  1. In oil fry all the masala and grind it into fine powder.
  2. In oil add musturd seeds, once they crack add channa dal and urad dall little and fry until golden brown.
  3. Add the finely chopped onions until golden brown
  4. Add the eggplant and add salt to it and cover it until it is cooked.
  5. Once the eggplant is cooked add the finely ground masala to it and add rice and twice amount of water and cook.
  6. Once ready garnish it with coriander leaves and serve with raita.

Enjoy it.
