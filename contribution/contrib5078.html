Aanapakaya Payasam

Ingredients

1. 1 cup aanapakaya/lauki(grated)
2. 1 1/2 cups sugar
3. 2 cups milk
4. 2-3 spoons ghee
5. 10-12 raisins
6. 10-12 cashewnuts
7. 1/4 tsp cardamom powder

Method

  1. Peel the aanapakaya/lauki and cut it.
  2. Remove the center part so that you can avoid the seeds.
  3. Grate aanapakaya/lauki and measure 1 cup of it.
  4. Place a pan on the stove and add half of the ghee.
  5. Fry the grated aanapakaya/lauki for about a minute or so.
  6. Then add milk to it.
  7. Let it cook till it becomes soft.
  8. After that add sugar and cook till you get a nice texture of payasam/kheer.
  9. Add fried cashewnuts, raisins and cardamom powder on top.

You can always change the measurements of the ingredients as per your taste!!
