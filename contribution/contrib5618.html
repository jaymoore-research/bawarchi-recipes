Kaima Idli

Ingredients

4 or 5 left over idli's

For Seasoning:
Mustard seeds
Urid dhal
Curry leaves

For Dipping :
Maida - 4 or 5 Tbl
Bread Crumbs

For Grinding:

Small Onions- 3 or 4
Tomatoes - 2 medium
Garlic -3 or 4 flakes
Ginger-small amount
Dhania Powder-2 Tsp
Chilli powder
Salt

Ā Method:

  1. Add little bit of chilli powder & salt to the maida & make it into a paste by adding water.
  2. Cut the idli's into small squares .
  3. Dip them first in maida & then in bread crumbs.
  4. Deep fry them in oil till golden brown.
  5. Heat oil in a pan & add mustard seeds .
  6. After it splitters add urid dhal & curry leaves.
  7. Add the ground paste & allow it to boil till the oil comes out from the paste.
  8. Then add the fried idli pieces & mix it gently in low flame for about 5 to 10 minutes.
  9. Kaima Idli is ready to eat.
  10. Garnish it with lemon & coriander leaves.
  11. This is a good recipe for left over idli's .
