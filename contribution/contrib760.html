Ingredients

1/2 -3/4 cup gram flour
2 onions cut
5- 6 green chillies
Oil
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard, jeera and green chillies and fry
  2. Add cut onions, some water and cook the onions
  3. Meanwhile mix gram flour in water and consistency should be that of buttermilk.
  4. Add the above mix to the cooked onions, add salt.
  5. Let boil for approximately 10 mins till desired consistency is reached and till raw taste of gram flour vanishes.
  6. Serve hot with rotis/puris

    Note: If desired consistency is not reached then add more gram flour and let boil. Hasi Raitha

    Ingredients

    1/2 cup each of cooked beans, cucumber, carrots, radish etc..
    3 cups yogurt
    Handfull of desicated coconut
    5 green chillies
    1 tsp mustard
    A pinch of asafoetida
    Salt to taste

    Method:

    1. Grind together coconut soaked in water with green chillies and asafoetida. Keep it ready.
    2. After the cooked veggies cool down add yogurt to them and mix well. Add more yogurt if you please.
    3. Add the grinded mixture into the above cooked veggis and mix well.
    4. Serve cold.

    Preparation time: 10 mins (excluding cooking of veggies)
