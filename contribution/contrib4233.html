Butter Chicken

Ingredients

1/4 pint Sour Cream
1 tsp. Ginger (crushed)
1/4 tsp. Food Color (Red)
3 lbs. Chicken (skinless, cut into pieces)
2 ozs. Butter
1 piece Cinnamon (1 inch)
6 nos. Cloves
6 nos. Cardamom
1 leaf Bay leaves
1/4 pint Yoghurt
1/4 tsp. Saffron (crushed)
1/4 pint Raw Cream
2 tsps. Almonds (ground)
1/4 tsp. Corn Flour
Vegetable Oil for garnishing
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Mix yogurt, ginger, salt and red colouring and rub into chicken. Let it marinate overnight.
  2. Place in an ovenproof dish and brush with oil.
  3. Bake in a 375 degree oven for 40-50 minutes. Save the liquid, if any.
  4. In a saucepan, melt butter and fry cinnamon, cloves, cardamoms and bay leaf for 1 minute.
  5. Add sour cream and chicken liquid.
  6. Add crushed saffron, and cream.
  7. Cover and simmer gently for 5-6 minutes.
  8. Add chicken pieces and adjust seasoning. Add ground almonds.
  9. Dissolve cornstarch in water and add to the chicken. Let it thicken. Cover and simmer for 3-4 minutes. Remove from heat.
