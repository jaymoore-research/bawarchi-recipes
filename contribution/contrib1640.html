Pineapple Custard

Ingredients

Pine Apple 1 big cut into cubes
Custard powder 2 tbsp
Milk 1/2 litre
Condensed Milk 1 can
Milk 1/4 cup
Sugar 2 tbsp
Elaichi powder 1 tsp
Vanilla Icecream 2 tbsp

Method:

  1. Boil the milk in low flame until it is reduced. Keep stirring continously so that the milk does not stick to the pan
  2. Then take 1/4 cup of milk & just warm it up in the microwave for a minute or so. Then add the custard powder in the milk & mix it very well. See to it that no lumps are formed.
  3. Once the first milk is boiled, then add the condensed milk to it & keep stirring continously. Once that is done then add the custard & milk paste. Mix well together & allow it to boil for 2-3 minutes. Then add the elaichi powder & mix it well
  4. Allow it to cool & then refrigerate it (not in the freezer)
  5. Cut the pineapple & add the sugar. Mix it well & keep it in the fridge.
  6. Just before serving, beat vanilla ice cream & pour it into the custard, also add the pineapple pieces into the custard & Serve cold.
