Back-Eye Peas Vada

Ingredients

Black eye peas (dried) 1 lb (makes about 30 pieces)
1 Medium Onion
1-2 Green Chillies
2 inch Ginger
1 bunch Cilantro (coriander leaves)
Salt to taste
Oil for deep frying

Method:

  1. Soak black eye peas for about 3-5 hours
  2. Wash the soaked peas thoroughly to get rid off the seed coats, and grind 3/4 of the peas in the food processor with green chillies and ginger (Not very fine, but slightly coarse, sprinkle few drops of water if needed)
  3. Chop onion and cilantro (Chinese parsley or regular parsley) very fine and add to the black eye peas mixture
  4. Add salt, and a pinch of cinnamon and cloves.
  5. Heat oil in skillet. Take small amounts of the mixture, make 2 inch round patties of about less than1/4 inch thick, and fry in hot oil till golden brown
  6. Serve hot eat as is or with any Chutney, tomato sauce, hot sauce or ketchup.
