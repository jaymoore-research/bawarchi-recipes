Kaara Chutney

Ingredients

Red chillies which are used to make bhajjis.(not dry) 10 chillies
2 Tomatoes
1 Onion (big)
2 Garlics
1 spoon Sugar
2 drops of Tamarind water
Salt to taste.

Method:

  1. Now grind the chillies in mixie until it becomes paste like.
  2. Cut the onions tomatoes and garlic into very small pieces.
  3. Pour 2 spoon oil in a kadai and put the grinded chillies in it. Put the onion, garlic and tomatoes in it. Stir it until it boils. Put salt and sugar and add the tamarind water. After 2 minutes take it away and put the chutney in a container and pour 1 spoon gingelly oil and stir with spoon.

This can be taken with idly dosa. The name of this is SAMBAL CHUTNEY.
