Ingredients for Cooking

Cauliflower                           1 big flower
  Onions chopped                   2 nos.
Curd/Yogurt                         Ẅ cup
Almond paste                      Ẅ cup
Red chili powder                  1 tsp.
Coriander powder                 1 tsp.
Juice of a lime 
Turmeric powder                   Ẅ tsp.
Cumin seed powder              1 tsp.
Ginger paste                        1 tsp.
Garlic paste                         1 tsp. 
Garam masala powder         1/2 tsp.
Coriander chopped               1 tbsp.
Saffron                           A few strands 
 Oil                                2 tbsp.+ to fry
Salt                               As per taste
Cashew(for paste with almonds) 1 tsp 

   Method

     
  1. Separate the cauliflower florets.
  2. Add salt, turmeric powder and lemon juice. Mix Well.
  3. Sprinkle gram flour and chili powder and mix well again.
  4. Deep fry. Once half fried, strain the cauliflower florets and keep aside. Sprinkle Garam Masala Powder over it.

For Gravy:

  1. Chop onions finely.
  2.  In a pan heat oil. Add the chopped onions and fry till light brown. Sprinkle garam masala and mix well.
  3. Add ginger paste, garlic paste. Mix well, stirring continuously.
  4. Put turmeric, coriander powder, cumin seeds powder, red chili powder. Fry the masala well.
  5. Add almond and cashew paste.
  6. Cook for 2 min and add curd/yogurt. Mix again thoroughly.
  7. Mix  fried cauliflower, salt, saffron, green coriander, sprinkle water, cover and cook on low flame.
  8. Serve hot with chappathis, parotas, puris.
