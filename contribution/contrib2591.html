Lagan Seekh

Ingredients

Spread half beaten egg on the pan
250 gm Mince Meat
1 tsp. Butter or 1 tsp Oil
1 big Onion
1 tbsp. Garlic paste
1 tbsp. finely chopped Green Chillies
1 tbsp finely chopped Coriander Leaves
1 tbsp. finely chopped Mint Leaves
1 Egg
Salt to taste and Peeper Powder

Method

  1. Firstly, put garlic paste, coriander leaves, mint leaves, salt to taste in to mince meat. Mix it well. Marinate for more than one hour.
  2. Finely cut onion and deeply fry in oil. As soon as it turns golden colour, remove from oil and keep it aside. It is known as birasta.
  3. Beat egg with pepper powder and salt to taste
  4. Take flat pan and grease it with butter/ oil. Then spread about half quantity of a beaten egg on the pan. Mix half birasta and marinated mince meat dough. Press lightly to spread through out the pan. Spread the remaining part of beaten egg on the dough.
  5. Cover the pan with foil and bake in the oven or on the gas for 25 min.
  6. Garnish with Birasta, Capsicum, Tomato, Carrot or whatever you like.
