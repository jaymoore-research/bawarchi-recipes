Five Star Chutney

Ingredients

1. Tomatoes--3 medium sized
2. Garlic-5 pods( optional)
3. Onion-1 medium size
4. Coconut -1/4 Moodi (1/4 slice from one half of the coconut) Grate it or cut into small pieces.
5. Pudina - 1 small bunch

For seasoning:
1. Mustard seeds -1 tsp
2. Jeera seeds -1/2 tsp
3. Bengal gram dhal-1 tbsp
4. Urad dhal -1/1/2 tbsp
5. Green chillies or red chillies - 4-5 (depending on what colour you need the chutney to be)
6. Curry leaves - a few
7. Asafoetida - (Hing) - a pinch

Process:

  1. Cut the onion into square pieces and leave aside.
  2. Clean pudina and slice tomatoes and garlic. Set aside.
  3. In 2 tbsps of oil season mustard and jeera seeds and the dhals.
  4. Once done, add the chillies, curry leaves and hing
  5. After a minute add onions and fry them till they are half done.
  6. Add the pudina, tomatoes and garlic.
  7. Cook well and when done, add the grated / sliced coconut.
  8. Cook for 2 minutes and allow it to cool.
  9. Take a small ball of tamarind - and warm up with water for the syrup.
  10. Grind all the cooked ingredients with tamarind water.
  11. Add salt as required.

This is excellent for idlis / dosas / bread sandwitches / chapati / riceand puri too.
