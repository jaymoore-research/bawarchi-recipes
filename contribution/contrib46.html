Mango-Coconut Burfi.

Ingredients

1 cup mango pulp(tinned will do)
1 cup grated coconut.
1/2 cup milk powder.
1 1/2 cups granulated sugar.
3 tbsps.ghee.
1/2 cup powdered sugar.
1/2 tsp.cardamom powder.

Method:

  1. Cook mango pulp over a low flame stirring continuously, preferably in a non-stick pan, till it solidifies. Cool.
  2. Add coconut, milk powder and the granulated suger and keep aside for 15 minutes.
  3. Then cook over a low flame, stirring all the time.
  4. When it boils add the ghee and keep cooking till it thickens into a lump and froths slightly.
  5. Switch off gas and stir another 3 minutes.
  6. Add the powdered sugar and the cardamom powder and mix well, pour into a greased plate and pat with the back of a spoon to spread it evenly.
  7. Decorate with varq (edible silver foil). This is optional.
  8. When slightly cooled,cut into pieces.
  9. Makes about 30 pieces.

Happy eating!!!
