French Toast

Ingredients

6-7 slices of white bread.
2 cups milk
2 eggs
3 tbsps sugar
Elaichi powder ( cardamom) -optional
1 spoon Custard powder mixed in 1/2cup warm milk (optional)-
Oil - 2tbsp

Method

  1. Take 2 cups milk in a bowl.Add eggs,sugar,elaichi and custard milk to it.Make a mixture.
  2. Place a pan on the gas and a add 1/2 spoon cooking oil.
  3. Dip the bread slices in the mixture one by one and roast them in the pan till brown.
  4. French toast is ready.
  5. Serve with Tomato / Chilli sauce/ketchup.
