Microwave Mixed Vegetable

Ingredients

Onion - 1 finely chopped
Mixed vegetables - 1 1/2 cups (Beans, carrots, potatoes, peas and cauliflower)
Milk - 1/2 cup
Chilli Powder - 1/4 teaspoon
Tomato Ketchup - 2 Teaspoons
Maida - 1 table spoon
Oil - 1 tablespoon
Salt to taste

Method

  1. Wash all the vegetables and cut them into uniform small pieces (except peas).
  2. Put the vegetables in a mico safe glass bowl and pour 25 to 30 ml water and micro high for 7 to 9 minutes.(see that the peas is at the bottom ).
  3. In another microwave safe bowl ,Ātake the oil and put the onion and micro high for 3 minutes.
  4. Add the cooked vegetables, chilli powder and micro high for 1 minute.
  5. In a small katori ,Āmix the milk, maida and tomato ketchup and salt and mix to the vegetables and micro high for 3 minutes.
  6. Let it stand for 4 minutes.
  7. Microwave Mixed Veggie is ready
