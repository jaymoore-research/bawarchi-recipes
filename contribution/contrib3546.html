Spicy Indian Noodles

Ingredients

100 gms Noodles (medium size)
2 medium Onions
2 medium Tomatoes
1 small cup Green Peas
2 small Carrots
1/2 tsp Pavbhaji Masala
1/2 tsp Curry Powder
1/2 tsp Paprika powder/ or pepper
1 Egg
1 tbsp Oil
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Boil the noodles with salt and oil for 8 minutes. Strain the noodles and keep aside.
  2. Boil the green peas, chopped carrots.
  3. Fry the onions transluscent, add chopped tomatoes and fry till the water dries out.
  4. Add the dry powders, boiled vegetables and stir fry.
  5. Add the beaten egg
  6. Add the strained noodles and mix well. Add salt if necessary. The yummy noodles is ready to eat! You can enjoy the noodles with Tomato Sauce.
