Stuffed Chillies

Ingredients

Big Chillies : 6
Garlic : 15 pods
Besan
Salt

Method:

  1. Grind garlic pods with little salt.Mix the garlic paste with little besan. Now this becomes a paste like.
  2. Now slit chillies & remove seeds & then stuff with the garlic & besan mixture. Stuff all chillies in the same way. Now if you have excess of paste don't throw this away.
  3. Take oil in a kadai & when it is hot. Put all the chillies in the kadai & simmer the flame & cover with a lid. Add the garlic besan mix to the chillies. Allow all this to cook.Add salt if you want.
  4. Garnish with coriander. This is a fry.You should not put lot of oil. Just use 2 or 3 tsp of oil. Enjoy this with ghee rice.
