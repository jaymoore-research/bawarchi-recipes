Yam Chutney

Ingredients

Fresh Yam 200 gms
Red Chilies 5
Urud Dhal 6 tbsp
Channa Dhal 3 tbsp
Asafoetida a pinch
Oil 2tbsp
Tamarind Pulp small lemon size
salt 2 tsp
grated coconut optional [4tbsp]

Method

  1. First heat the oil in a pan. Fry the asafoetida remove from the pan and keep aside. Now add the chilies, urud and channa dhals and roasted till its golden brown. If need be coconut is added and roasted too.
  2. The Yam is cut into small pieces and kept aside.
  3. The fried items, tamarind and salt to taste is ground to a fine powder in a mixie.
  4. Now add the cut pieces of yam and grind it to a nice paste using a little water. The chutney is now ready for serving.
