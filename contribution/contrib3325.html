Ghee Roast Pepper Toast & Sugar Toast

Ingredients For Pepper Toast:

1] White Bread - 4 slices
2] Ghee - 4 tspn
3] Pepper (freshly crushed) - 4 tspn (you can add more or less pepper according to your taste)
4] Salt as per taste

Method:

Apply ghee on both the sides of the bread slices place it the toaster oven grill sprinkle salt and pepper on one side of the bread slices toast it to crispy brown. Remove it, your ghee roast pepper toast is ready to serve.

Ingredients For Ghee Roast Sugar Toast:

1] White Bread - 4 slices
2] Ghee - 4 tspn
3] Sugar - 4tspn (you can add more sugar if you like more sweet)

Method:

Apply ghee on both the sides of bread slices, Sprinkle sugar on one side of the bread slice and toast it until nice and brown colour. Serve this yummy breakfast, I'm sure your children will like them.
