Manglorean Chicken Curry

Ingredients

1 Kg Chicken cut into medium size pieces
5 Dry Red Chillies
1 tbsp. Coriander seeds
1 tsp. Jeera
1/4 tsp. Methi seeds
1/2 tsp Pepper corns
1/4 tsp. Turmeric.
1 tsp Tamarind paste.
2 Onions.
4 large Garlic cloves
Coconut (Grated) 1 cup
Coriander leaves
Oil
Salt to taste

Method:

Red masala

Dry roast separately chillies, coriander seeds, jeera, methi seeds, pepper corns. Add a little oil in a pan and fry 1 onion (diced) till light brown. Grind all these ingredients adding turmeric, tamarind and little water.

Coconut masala

Fry together coconut, jeera, garlic in little oil, Grind it and keep aside.

  1. Add 2 tbsp oil in a sauce pan.
  2. Add 1 chopped onion and saute till pink.
  3. Add chicken, red masala and salt. Cook till chicken is done.
  4. Add coconut masala and water (if necessary) and cook for 10 min on low flame.
  5. Garnish with coriander leaves.

Consistency of the gravy can be adjusted.
