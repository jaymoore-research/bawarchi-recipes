Pan Cakes (South Indian Oothappam)

Ingredients

Bisquick pan cake mix - 4 Cups
Milk ( or Butter milk ) - 2 cups
tomatoes 4
Onions 4 ( medium size )
Green Chilles ( medium hot ) 3
Coriander ( cillanthro ) 1 bunch
Oil ( small cup )
Salt ( 1/4 tbl. spoon )
Chilli powder ( 1/8 ths of tbl.spoon )

Method :

  1. First cut tomatoes, onions and green chillies into very small pieces. Chop the coriander leaves also.
  2. Mix milk with pancake mix and make it as semi-liquid paste.
  3. Then add salt and chilli powder to the mix.
  4. Add tomatoes, onions, green chilles and coriander leaves to the pancake mix and mix well.
  5. Heat a thick nonstick pan and spread the pancake mix on the pan.
  6. Add oil around the corners of the pancake. Once it is heated for two minutes turn the pan cake to the other side using a flat spatula and heat it upto two more minutes.

Take it off from the pan and now the pan cake is ready to eat. This tastes similar to Oothappam and tastes very good. Instead of milk, you can add buttermilk also to get a different taste.

This is very easy to make it within five minutes.
