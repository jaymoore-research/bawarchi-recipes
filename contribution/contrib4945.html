Onion-Coconut Chutney

Ingredients

Half - medium onion
1 cup -grated coconut
2-3 red chillies
salt for taste

Method:

  1. Grind the above to a fine paste.
  2. Thadka of oil, mustard seeds and curry leaves is optional.
