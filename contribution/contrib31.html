Akhini   (Mutton)

Ingredients :

1 Kgs Mutton
3/4 Kgs Basmati Rice
8 Nos Big Onion
25 Nos Green Chillis
50 Grams Ginger & 1 Pod Garlic grind together
1 teaspoon Big Jeera
1 teaspoon Small Jeera
4 Nos Cardamom
6 Nos Cloves
2 pieces Cinnamon
3 Nos Tomato
1 1/2 cup Curd
2 Nos Lime
2 Nos Coriander Leaves
Oil Salt to taste

Method

Heat oil. Add garam masala and onion sliced and green chillies sliced.
Fry light and add another masalas except lime and coriander leaves.
Add mutton with it and keep it in cooker for 15 miuntes .
Then in another vessel fry rice in oil for 3 to 4 minutes and add all the ingredients
Add 3 to 4 cups of water.
Keep on high pressure for 5 to 7 minutes and low the pressure and it for 10 minutes and serve with chatni.

Method for preparing Chatni

100 Grams Small Onion
3 Nos Green Chillies
1 inch piece Ginger
few Curry leaves
Few Corriander leaves
1 teaspoon Red chilli powder
2 table spoon Sugar
Tamarind thick water
salt to taste

Mix all these together and serve it with Akhini
