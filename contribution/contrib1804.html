Bharela-Karela

Ingredients

Karela 1/4 kgs

For stuffing paste & gravy:

Grated Coconut (wet) 1/2 coconut
Besan 2 tea spoons
Powdered groundnut 2 tablespoons
Red chili powder 2 teaspoons
Coriander seed powder 1/2 teaspoon
Cumin seed powder 1/2 teaspoon
Lime juice 1 full lime
Water 1/2 cup
Chopped coriander leaves
Sugar 2-3 tablespoons
Salt to taste

For seasoning

Mustard seeds 1/2 teaspoon
Asafetida 1/4 teaspoon

For garnishing

Chopped coriander leaves

Method:

  1. Peel the karela. Cut into 3 pieces horizontally. Remove the seeds from inside the karela.
  2. Slit each piece vertically for putting the stuffing paste.
  3. Apply salt to the above pieces and marinate for 10 minutes. Water will start to leech out of the karela pieces.
  4. Squeeze the karela pieces to remove the water. This will also help reduce the bitter taste.
  5. Mix all the ingredients for stuffing paste with 1/2 cup water. Mix it to make slightly thick paste. Keep part of this paste aside for making gravy.
  6. Stuff the karelas with this paste.
  7. For seasoning heat 1 tablespoon oil in a pan. Add Mustard seeds & Asafoetida. Put stuffed karela pieces. Cover the pan & cook for 10 - 15 minutes. Turn the karela pieces while cooking in oil.
  8. Add the paste kept aside for the gravy. Cook in the gravy till the karelas are done.
  9. Garnish with coriander leaves. Serve with Roti or Chapati.
