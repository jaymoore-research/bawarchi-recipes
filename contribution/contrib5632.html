Cream of Chana

Ingredients:

white chick peas - 3 cups
green chillies - 7 medium slit vertically
ginger - 1 inch slice finely chopped
garlic - 6 pieces finely chopped
shah jeera - 1 tsp
fennel seeds - 1/2 tsp
garam masala-3 tsp
turmeric powder-1/2 tsp
salt- 3 tsp or to taste
cashewnuts-10
oilĀ - 3 tablespoons
coconut milk - 10 ounces (1.5 tumblers)
curry leaves - 10
cilantro- 1/2 bunch

Method:

  1. Soak chick peas overnight. Boil it in pressure cooker and keep aside.
  2. Heat kadai with oil, and put cashewnuts, shah jeera, fennel seeds,curry leaves,garlic and ginger.
  3. Add the turmeric powder and the garam masala.
  4. Add the chick peas to the above and mix really well. Mash some chick peas with the ladle.
  5. When the mixture starts to boil, add the coconut milk. Let it boil for 2 minutes and turn off the stove.
  6. Add some cilantro.

Serves: 4-6 people.
Shelf life: 4 days in the refrigerator, 2 months frozen.
