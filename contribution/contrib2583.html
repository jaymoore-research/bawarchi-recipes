Capsicum Fry

Ingredients

1. Green Capsicum - 4 to 5
2. Besan - 3 to 4 table spoons
3. Mustard - 1/2 teaspoon
4. Oil - 3 tablespoons
5. Brown chopped Onion - 1 (I don't use it though)
6. Turmeric - 1/4 teaspoon
7. Chilli powder - depends on individual taste
8. Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Heat the oil and add mustard. After it splutters add the chopped onion and fry till it turns brown.
  2. Drain the onions and keep them aside. Now add the capsicum and fry till these turn brown. Then add the besan over the capsicum and mix them well the capsicum and fry on medium heat (until the mixture turns brown - the usual colour fried besan turns to).
  3. Add the fried chopped onions to this.
  4. Sprinkle the chilli powder over this to add to the taste.
  5. Blend them well one more time and turn off the stove. Ofcourse, how good would a dish be without salt.

This dish can be served with warm Rice and Ghee (sounds wierd? try it) and also with soft rotis (rumali would be the best choice).
