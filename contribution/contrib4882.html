Chicken in Spicy yogurt

Ingredients

6 chicken pieces
juice of 1 lemon
1 teaspoon salt
lemon or lime wedges and lettuce leaves, to garnish

For the Marinade
1 teaspoon coriander leaves
2 teaspoon cumin seeds
6 cloves
1 onion quartered
2 bay leaves
2 garlic cloves
2 inch in piece fresh ginger, chopped
1/2 teaspoon chilly powder
1 teaspoon turmeric
2/3 cup plain low-fat natural yogurt

Method

  1. Skin the chicken joints and make deep slashes in the fleshiest parts with a sharp knife. Sprinkle over the lemon juice and salt and rub in.
  2. Roast the coriander and cumin seeds,cloves and bay leaves in a frying pan until the bay leaves are crispy.
  3. Cool the spices and grind coarsely.
  4. Finely mince the onion,garlic and ginger in a blender. Add the ground spices,chilly, turmeric and yogurts well.
  5. Strain the lemon juice from the chicken pieces.
  6. Arrange the chicken in a single layer in a shallow dish. Pour over the marinade,then cover and chill for about 24-36 hours, turning occasionally the chicken pieces in the marinade.
  7. Cook the chicken pieces on low barbecue for about 30-45 minutes, turning the pieces and brushing with the marinade occasionally.
  8. Garnish with fresh lettuce and lemon wedges and serve.

Recipe secret: Never cook this dish on high heat because then chicken won't be done from inside.

Serves 6
