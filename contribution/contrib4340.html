Microwave Sabudana Khichadi

Ingredients

2 1/2 katori Sabudana
5 tbsp. Ghee.
1 tsp Cumin seeds.
6 /7 Green Chillies finely chopped.
1 katori Roasted Peanut (Find below the method to roast peanuts in microwave)
1 tsp. Sugar.
Salt to taste.
2 tsp. Lime Juice
3 tbsp. each - Cilantro (coriander) cut and grated Coconut.
(optional) 2 small Potatoes boiled and cut into small pieces.

Method:

  1. Soak and drain sabudana and keep covered over night. (It puffs up the next day).
  2. Mix peanut mill, sugar and salt with sabudana.
  3. In a microwave safe bowl mix ghee, cut potatoes, cumin seeds and chillies.
  4. Microwave for 3 min. on high setting.
  5. Put in sabudana mix . Mix well. Microwave for 3 min.
  6. Put lemon juice and mix well.
  7. Microwave for 3 more min.
  8. Remove from microwave garnish with coriander and coconut and serve hot.

Note: If you reheat khichadi, sprinkle with some water and microwave.
If using only a katori of sabudana, eliminate step 7.
Method to roast peanuts in microwave Put 1 katori peanut in microwave safe bowl. Sprinkle some water. Microwave for 2 min. Stir and again microwave for 1 1/2 min. Let it cool down . Rub a little bit and peanut covers come out very easily.
