Oomapodi

Ingredients

Besan - 3 cups
Rice Powder - 1 cup
Chili powder - 1 1/2 tsp (according to the hotness you need)
Asofeotida (hing) - a pinch.
Salt to taste
Oil for frying

Special Vessel you need: Presser used for chiwda etc.

Method:

  1. Mix the besan, ricepowder, chilli powder, salt and Asofoetida with water to make them to a paste (similar to that of a chapathi dough).
  2. Make a ball and put in the presser.
  3. In a kadai, keep oil for frying. When oil is hot enough, press the presser so that the oomapodi falls into the oil like threads.
  4. Fry them till golden brown and take them out.

Serve them hot with tea or coffee. It will be a great snack to much while seeing T.V, reading, talking etc with anything.
