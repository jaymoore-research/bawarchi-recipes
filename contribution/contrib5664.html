Puffed Rice Upma

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients

Puffed rice    1/2 kg
Green chilles 
Roasted bengal gram  1/4 cup
Salt
Curry leaves
Oil  4tsp
Onion 1

Method

 

  1. Soak puffed rice in water after they become tender remove them from water
  2. Grind roasted bengal gram and chillies with salt
  3. Add that powder to puffed rice
  4. Cut onion into small pieces length wise
  5. Heat oil and add mustard seeds and urad dhal
  6. Add curry leaves after they cook add some turmeric
  7. Add onions after they turned into reddish color add puffed rice
  8. Mix it and cover the pan after 5 minutes upma is ready
