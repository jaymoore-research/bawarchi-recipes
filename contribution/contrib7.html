CAULIFLOWER       DELIGHT

Ingredients

1 big Cauliflower well cleaned and cut
3 tbsp of Oil
1/4 tsp asofoetida
1/4 tsp methi seeds
1 tsp sugar
1 tsp coriander leaves
2 green chillies

 Mix together in a blender

1 tsp garlic
1 medium sized onion
1-2 green chillies
1 tsp fresh coriander leaves
1 medium sized tomato
1 tsp of vinegar (white)
Salt as per requirement

Method

Blend in a mixer all the ingredients mentioned and keep aside.
In a heavy bottom pan heat the oil and fry the cobs of cauliflower till they are light brown. Then keep them aside.
In the same pan use the remaining oil, and when hot, add the asofoetida and methi seeds and the sugar and follow by pouring in the blended mixture.
Fry well till oil separates from gravy.
Now add the Cauliflower pieces one by one and cover on low heat for about 10 minutes.
Garnish with with chopped coriander and green chillies.
Cauliflower Delight may be served with rotis or parathas.
