Chicken Dish

Ingredients

Chicken breast or thighs cut into chunky pieces about half a kilo.
Green chillies -10 no. (hot ones)
Turmeric powder -1/2 tsp
Salt to taste
Oil - 2 tbsp

Method:

  1. In two tbsp oil fry chillies for a minute and add chicken pieces, salt and turmeric and fry for a while.
  2. Now add little water and cook till the chicken is done. And fry till it leaves the sides of the pan.
