Navaratna Koorma

Ingredients

Beans - 20
Carrot - 2
Potato - 2
Green Pea - 1 cup
Lima Bean - 1 Cup
Apple - 1
Cashew - 1/2 cup
Raisins - 1/2 Cup
Pineapple chunk - 1/2 Cup (Canned ones are preferred)
1/2 Cup Ghee or Vegetable Oil.
Mustard - 1 tsp
Jeera - 1 tsp

For Masala

Onions - 2
Tomato - 2
Green Chilli - 2
Garam Masala - 2 Spoon
Cashew - 1 cup
Haldi - 1/2 spoon
Ginger - 1/2"
Cardamom - 3
Tomato Puree - 1/2 cup
Put all the above in a mixer and blend it to smooth paste adding 1/2 cup water.

Method

  1. Cut Beans into 1/2" pieces, Dice Carrots, Potato, Apple into small Cubes.
  2. Boil all the vegetables except apple, cashew, raisins and pineapple in a microwave until they are tender.
  3. Drain them and keep it separately. You can save the water to add it to the gravy depending on the consistency you need.
  4. In a Pan put 2 spoons of Ghee and Fry Cashew, Raisin until cashew turns to golden yellow color. Then add Apple and Pine apple (Without Juice) and fry for another 5 minutes. Take it out of stove. Close the lid and let it simmer.
  5. Then in a thick bottom vessel put the remaining ghee and put Mustard and Jeera. After they sputter put the stove to low flames and add the grinded masala. Keep stirring the masala on low flames until they start boiling and raw smell goes away.
  6. Add Salt according to taste.
  7. Once they start boiling add the Cooked vegetables and Fried nuts and Fruits from Step 4.
  8. Add the water to required consistency and boil it for 10 minutes in a medium flame.
  9. After you take it out of stove Garnish with Cilantro and Cherry Fruits if you like.
  10. Serve it hot with Rice, Naan or Paratha.
