Madhur Vadai

Ingredients

Maida-1/2 cup
Rawa-1/2 cup
Rice Flour-1/2 cup
Wheat Flour-1/2 cup
Finely chopped Onions-1cup
Chopped Coriander and Curry leaves-3Tbl sps
Minced Green Chillies- 1 Tbsp
Salt -as required
Oil-to deep fry.

Method:

  1. Mix all the ingredients together in a bowl.
  2. Put 3 tablespoons of hot oil to that. Blend evenly using finger tips to mix the flour.
  3. Divide the flour into many portions to knead it well. Sprinkle water to first portion to make it like a thick dough (consistency of chapati dough).
  4. Make balls and flatten it on aluminium foil to make thin vadai.
  5. Deep fry in hot oil till crisp and brown.
  6. Serve hot with Tomato Sauce, Salsa or Mint Chutney.
