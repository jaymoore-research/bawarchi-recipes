Milk Poha

Ingredients

1/2 cup rice flakes (poha)
1 Cup milk
2 tbspn sugar
10 raisins

Method

  1. Wash the rice flakes with water
  2. Drain the water and keep it for 2 min. ( To make washed Poha soft ).
  3. Add milk ( hot or cold ) , sugar and raisins.
  4. Milk-Poha is ready.
