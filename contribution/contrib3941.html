Majjige Huli

Ingredients

2 Raddish
1 tsp Channa dal (soaked for 1 hour)
2-3 Green Chillies (roasted in a little oil)
1/2 tsp Jeera
2 tsp Mustard seeds
Sprig of Curry Leaves
1/2 cup grated Coconut
1 cup Curd
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Wash and scrape radish and slice it in slightly thick rounds. Cook it along with a sprig of curry leaves and salt.
  2. Grind together coconut, jeera and roasted green chillies in a fine paste. Just before removing add soaked channa dal and grind it coarsely. Add it to the cooked radish. Give one boil.
  3. Season with mustard seeds,curry leaves and 1 red chilly. Just before serving add the beaten curd. Serve it with hot rice and sambar. Other vegetables like ash gourd and cucumber can be used for preparing this huli.
