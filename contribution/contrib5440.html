Italian Country Salad

Ingredients

200 gms boneless chicken cut in 1" square pieces
Salt as per taste
1/4 cup French beans chopped in medium pcs
2 carrots chopped in medium pcs
4 Spring onions chopped in small pcs
1/4 cup Cucumber chopped in small pcs
4 Tomatoes sliced in length 4/6 each
4 tbsp corn kernels (tinned) strained
3 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp thyme dried
1 tsp basil dried
1/2 tsp black pepper (coarse ground)

Method

  1. Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in non stick pan
  2. Add chicken and cook for 7 minutes on high heat till slightly brown
  3. Add french beans, carrots, salt, 1/2 tsp basil, 1/2 tsp thyme
  4. Cook for 5 minutes on medium heat
  5. Remove pan from heat and empty contents in serving bowl
  6. Add spring onion, tomatoes, corn kernel, rest of olive oil, thyme, basil and black pepper
  7. Add salt to taste, toss well and serve
