Tomato Rice

Ingredients

1 cup Basamati Rice
1 Carrot
1 Capsicum
1/2 Cup shredded Cabbage
1 Potato
1/4 cup Green Peas
French Beans
4 Green Chillies
4 cloves of Garlic
A small piece of Ginger
2 cups of Tomato Puree
1/2 tsp. Red Chilly powder
2 tspns. Garam Masala Powder
1 tsp. Mustard seeds
1 tsp. Oil
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Chop all the vegetables finely and put them in the pressure cooker along with the peas.
  2. Grind green chillies, Ginger and Garlic to a paste.
  3. Clean and wash the rice and put in to the cooker.
  4. Now pour the tomato puree into the cooker. Add salt, chilly powder, garam masala powder to the ingredients in the cooker.
  5. Heat oil in a ladle and add mustard seeds. After they splutter add the ginger, garlic and green chilly paste and mix for a second. Add this seasoning into the cooker and mix all the ingredients very well. If required add water enough for cooking the rice and the vegetables. Pressure cook and serve hot.
