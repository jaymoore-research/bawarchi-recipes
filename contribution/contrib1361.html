Palak Rice

Ingredients

3 cups of Basmathi Rice
1 full Spinach (Palak)
6 - 8 Mint leaves
1-2 Patta
1-2 Lavang
2 green Chillies
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Cook 3 cups of basmathi rice with only 3 cups of water and keep it aside to get it cool.
  2. Cut 1 full spinach (palak) into small pieces and boil with little water in the microwave for 3-4 min and grind the palak with 2 green chillies and make it past.
  3. Take a non-stick vessel and add some oil in it. When the oil becomes hot add Patta and Lavang after 2 min add cooked basmathi Rice and the palak past to it and mix it very well. The rice should mix properly with the past.
  4. Simmer the rice 5-7 min add mint leaves and some salt to it.

Palak rice are ready to eat and you can eat it with Raitha /chutney.
