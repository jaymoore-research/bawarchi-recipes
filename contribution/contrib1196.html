Morrappam

Ingredients

Left over Dosai batter: 1 cup
Gram Flour : 1 cup
Chilli powder : 1 tbsp
Salt : 1/2 tbsp
Urad Dhal/Channa dhal : 1/2 tbsp
Mustard Seed : 1/2 tbsp
Refined oil : For frying

Method:

  1. Mix the left over Dosai batter, Gram flour, chilli powder and salt and add water if it is too thick. The consistency should be that you should be able to pour in the Kuzhi (holed vessel used to prepare Ghee appam).
  2. Pour oil in the Kadai and put Mustard seed, urad dhal and channa dhal and after the mustard splutters add this to the batter.
  3. Pour two tbsp of refined oil in all the Kuzhi and once it gets heated pour the batter in all the holes and fry them.

This is a good snack for husbands returning from office in the evening. Happy cooking. You can add onions and green chilli for a varied preparation.
