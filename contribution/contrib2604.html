Bindi Potato Curry

Ingredients

Bindi 1/2 kg
Potato 2 big ones
Onion 2 big ones
Turmeric Powder 1/2 tsp
Oil to saute 3 tsp
Cummin seeds 1/2 tsp
Salt to taste
Curry Powder

Method

  1. Wash/dry bindi.
  2. Cut bindi 1 inch long.
  3. Cut potatoes into thin slices.
  4. Chop onions.
  5. Heat the oil in a frying pan. Put the cummin seeds. When it splutters add the onion. Fry till light brown.
  6. Add the bindis and potato. Add turmeric powder and salt. Keep the flame low. Cover with a lid. Frequently keep mixing the bindis.
