Adai

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients:

Raw rice-1 cup
Thuar dhal-1 cup
Channa dhal- 1/2 cup
Urad dhal-1 tsp
Moong dhal-1 tsp
Groundnut(raw)-1 tsp
Red Chillies-as per taste(10-12)
Small onions-1/4 kilo
Curry leaves-a few
Asafoetida- aa per taste
Method:

  1. Soak rice & the dhals separately.
  2. Grind rice with red chillies, salt & asafoetida.
  3. Grind the dhals to a coarse paste.
  4. Mix it to the ground rice mixture.cut the onions into medium sized bits & mix it to the batter.
  5. Also add cut curry leaves.
  6. The consistency of the batter should be like dosa batter.
  7. Heat a tava & pour the mixture like a dosa with enough oil.
  8. Adai is ready now.

Variations: Methi leaves or drumstick leaves can be added to the batter to make tastier adais.
