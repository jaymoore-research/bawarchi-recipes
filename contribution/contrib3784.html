Matar Kabobs

Ingredients

Green Frozen matar - 2 cups
Onion finely chopped - 1 cup
Ginger finely grated - 1 tsp
Garlic finely chopped - 1 tsp
Coriander leaves finely chopped - 2 tsp
Green Chilies finely chopped - 1 tsp or according to taste
Lime juice - 2 tsp.
Salt to taste
Cornflour - 2 tsp.
2 slice Bread
Bread crumbs
Cooking Oil
Maida or Egg to dip the kabobs before rolling in the breadcrumbs.

Method:

  1. Cook matar in the microwave for 3 to 4 min till it softens. Drain excess water if any.
  2. Mash the cooked matar properly with hand (do not grind).
  3. Mix all the above mentioned ingredients and give it the shape of a kabob. Do not leave mixture for long after adding salt. It is not necessary to add bread slices to the mixture if the consistency of the mixture is good enough to give the shape of a kabob.
  4. Dip each kabob in a thin paste of maida/egg and roll it properly in the bread crumbs.
  5. Deep fry the kabobs till crispy brown and serve hot with Coriander Chutney.
