Mango Kulfi

Ingredients

Mango Pulp 1 can
Evaporated Milk 1 can
Condensed Milk 1 can
Vanilla Ice cream 1 can

Method:

  1. Empty the contents excluding Vanilla ice cream in a bowl & mix it well
  2. Then beat Vanilla Ice Cream into the mixture. Blend it all together very well.
  3. Cover the bowl with Foil & Refrigerate it.
  4. Just before serving keep it out & then Serve.
