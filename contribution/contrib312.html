Dum Aloo Chutney Wale

Ingredients

750 gm potatoes [medium size]
oil for frying
Filling

125gm Paneer [grated]
4 green chillies[deseeded and chopped]
1 1/2 tsp chopped ginger
10 cashewnuts[crushed]
salt to taste

Chutney

150gm hara dhania[coriander]
1 small raw mango or amchoor to taste
75gm poodina [mint]

Gravy

3 tsp ghee or oil
2 large onions[ground to a paste]
4 tsp ginger paste
4 tsp garlic paste
1 1/2tsp jeera[cumin ]seeds
1 tsp red chilli powder
1 1/2 cups yogurt
1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
2 tsp cashewnut[ground to a paste]
2 tsp garam masala
salt to taste

Method

  1. Peel & wash potatoes. Heat oil in a kadhai and deep fry over medium heat until three-fourths cooked and light brown in colour.
  2. Cool potatoes and scoop out the centre leaving 1/4''from the sides.
  3. To prepare the filling, mix all the ingredients of the filling in a bowl, add salt and stuff the scooped potatoes with the filling. Keep aside.
  4. Clean and wash coriander and mint.
  5. Peel and roughly chop the mango. Put coriander and mango in a blender and make a paste. Keep chutney aside.
  6. Beat yogurt in a bowl. add 1 1/2 cups water and mix well.
  7. Heat ghee in kadhai add onion paste, stir over medium heat until transparent.
  8. Add ginger, garlic pastes and stir until the ghee leaves the masala.
  9. Add jeera, stir for a minute, add the chutney and red chilli powder, stir for 1/2 min.
  10. Then add yogurt and water mix, bring to boil, reduce to low heat and simmer until the fat leaves the masala.
  11. Now add the stuffed potatoes, simmer until cooked.
  12. Add tomatoes, simmer for a min.,add cashewnut paste and garam masala, stir and bring to a boil.
  13. Adjust the seasoning. Don't cover the kadhai while cooking. Serve hot.
