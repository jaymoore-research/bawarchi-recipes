Carrot Pickle

Ingredients

2 Big Carrots
2 medium sized lemon
Gingelly (Til) oil
Hing Powder 1 Teaspoon
Methi Powder 1 1/2 teaspoons
Red Chilli powder 2 Tablespoons. (Increase or decrease according to individual needs
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Peel and cut the carrots into small cubes.
  2. Heat about two tablespoons oil
  3. Add mustard when it splutters and the hing and methiPowder.
  4. Add the cut carrots. Switch off the stove. Add salt and chilli powder.
  5. Finally squeeze Lemon juice and mix. Lemon juice can be increased depending on how sour you need.
