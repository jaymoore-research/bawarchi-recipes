Spicy Coconut Wheat Dosa

Ingredients

Wheat flour - 2 cups

Ingredients to make it Spicy

Onions (big) - 1
Green chillies - 6 no. (Can reduce it if you don't want that hot)
Grated Coconut - 1/2 cup
Turmeric Powder -1/2 tsp
Salt to taste
Oil - 4 tbsp
Mustard seeds - 1 tsp
Coriander leaves finely chopped - 2 tsp

Method:

  1. Add sufficient water to the wheat flour and mix it well without forming lumps in the dosa batter consistency. Add salt to taste. Keep this batter aside.
  2. Heat the oil and put mustard seeds in it. When the seeds splutter, put the finely chopped onion and green chillies and fry it in the oil. Add turmeric powder and coconut. When all of these get sauted well add the coriander leaves in the end. Then put this in the wheat flour mix and mix it well again. If you think you need to add some more water at this stage, do so.
  3. Make dosas out of this batter in the dosa pan. You may not need a separate chutney for this since it is already hot!. Enjoy
