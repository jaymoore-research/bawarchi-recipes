Green Chillies Pickles

Ingredients

250 Gms fresh Green Chilies
4 medium sized Lemons
1 tbsp Cumin Seeds
1 tbsp Fenugreek Seeds
1 tbsp. Vinegar
4 tbsp of Oil
Hing (asafoetida)
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Finely cut green chillies.
  2. Add lemon juice + salt to chillies
  3. Dry roast cumin and fenugreek seeds and powder them
  4. Heat oil, add hing, add the powder, vinegar, green chillies and mix it well
  5. Remove from fire
  6. Bottle this and use this after 3 or 4 days
