Fish Delight

Ingredients

Fish: (Rohu) cut into 2" long (0.5" thick) pieces.
Onion: 1 big (paste).
Garlic: paste of about 10-12 cloves.
Ginger: paste of 2" long piece.
Black Pepper: 10-12 full.
Bay Leaves: 2 med. sized.
Chilly: 3-4 green; slitted (or else face the consequences)
Coriander leaves: 1 cup finely chopped.
Vinegar: 2-3 teaspoons.
Salt: 1.5 teaspoon (or to taste)

Method:

  1. Add all the pastes, salt and vinegar and marinade the fish for at least an hour.
  2. Add 2 tablespoon oil (any kind) to a non-stick (preferable) cooking-ware and heat.
  3. Add the pepper, bayleaf and green chilly to the heated oil.
  4. Add the marinaded fish along with the pastes (used for marinading) into the oil. Do not put the pieces of fish one over the other. Take care to lay them separately.
  5. Simmer the fire and cover.
  6. Wait for 5 minutes and turn the fishes. Cover till the fish turns reddish brown and the juices dry.
  7. Garnish with finely chopped coriander and cover for five minutes. Serve with hot Basmati Rice.
