Stuffed Red Chilli Pickle

Ingredients

Fresh Red chillies-15-20 pieces
Salt-10 tbsp
Rai-15 tbsp
Amchoor powder-20 tbsp
Saunf-5 tbsp
Jeera-2 tbsp
Peppercorn-1 tbsp
Cloves-1 tbsp
Black Cardamoms [badi ilaichi] - 20 piece
Mustard Oil-3 cups

Method

  1. Dry chillies with a soft cloth. Remove stem and cut from the top. Hollow from inside removing seeds and pulp with the stem of the spoon. Save the seeds and pulp.
  2. Grind all the spices in a dry grinder. Mix with salt and seeds.
  3. Heat half of the oil and mix masala in hot oil. Cool it.
  4. Stuff chillies with this masala and put them in a jar. Pour rest of the oil on the top.
  5. Keep the pickle in a warm place for 2-3 days. After that it is ready to eat
