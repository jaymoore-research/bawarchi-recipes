Omlett Curry

Ingredients

Eggs : 2
Tomatoes : 4
Onion : 1 1/2
Chilli : 4
Masala Powder
Curry leaves
Cilatro

Method:

  1. Dice tomatoes & onions, slit chillies, beat eggs and keep them all aside.
  2. Now take oil in a pan season it & then add onions to it. Saute them & then add tomaotes add salt & chilli powder to it & pour 1 cup of water. Let it cook for a while.
  3. Add 1 tsp curds just to make it thick & add more water if you like. Now add 1 tsp of masala powder and garnish with cilantro. Well this is a tomato curry.
  4. Take a pan and make an omlett out of the beaten eggs. Cut the omlett into equal pieces and add that to the tomato curry. Let it soak for 10 minutes before you eat.
  5. You can eat this with rice, pulav or with chapathis.
