Date Roll

Ingredients

1. 14 oz pitted dates
2. 7 oz condensed milk
3. Milk powder-3 tsp
4. 1/2 stick butter
5. Coconut powder
6. grated almonds and pista

Method:

  1. Melt butter on slow flame and put Dates in it and keep smashing it until it blends well and turns soft.
  2. Then add Condensed milk, Milk powder, grated almonds and pista.Mix well along with dates in slow flame.
  3. Take the hot date mixture and make rolls by dipping your hands in ice cubes so that your hands become numb while rolling the dates.
  4. After rolling sprinkle coconut powder on the rolls.
  5. Keep it in the freezer section for 1 hour.
  6. Your date roll is ready.
