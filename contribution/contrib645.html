Kaju Curd Delight

Ingredients

1 tbsp. cornflour
1/2 kg. beaten curds
1 tin condensed milk
1/4 tsp. saffron (heated & powdered)
2 tbsp. hot water
1/4 tsp. yellow food colour
50 gm. finely cut or coarsely chopped cashewnuts

Method:

  1. Mix cornflour to a smooth paste in 1/4 cup water.
  2. Mix heated saffron powder & colour in hot water.
  3. Beat curds & mix with condensed milk & cook till it starts boiling.
  4. Remove from the fire & add cornflour & return to heat, stir & cook till thick.
  5. Mix in saffron, pour into a bowl & chill.
  6. Sprinkle cashewnuts on top before serving.
