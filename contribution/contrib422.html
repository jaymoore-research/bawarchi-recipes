Shahi Chicken

Ingredients

2 pounds chicken ( skinless & boneless)
or 6 leg pieces (skinless).
2 tablespoon oil.
1 large onion finely chopped.
2 Tsp tomato paste.
2 Tsp poppy seeds powder.
2 Tsp cashewnut powder.
10-15 strands of saffron.
2 Tsp milk.
1 cup hot water.

Ingredients to marinade :
2 table sp thick curd.
2 Tsp dhania powder.
2 Tsp cumin seed powder.
1 Tsp ginger paste.
1 Tsp garlic paste.
1/2 Tsp turmeric powder.
2 Tsp red chilli powder.
Salt to taste.

Method

  1. Marinate the chicken pieces in marinade for 4-5 hrs.
  2. Heat oil in vessel & add onions. Stir fry until onion become light brown in colour.
  3. After onion fried add tomato paste, cashewnut powder, poppy seed powder & fry for 2-3 minutes on low flame.
  4. After that add chicken & keep on mixing chicken with the masala. When chicken will dry add hot water.
  5. Cook the chicken on low flame for 20-25 minutes or till chicken is well done. Keep stirring occasionally.
  6. Melt the saffron in milk & mix with chicken, keep on low flame for 2 minutes.
  7. For garnishing sprinkle some chopped coriander leaves & some fried onion.
