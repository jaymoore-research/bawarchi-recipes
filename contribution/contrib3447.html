Gobhi Mussallam

Ingredients

Cauliflower 2 no.
Grated onion 1 cup
Garlic paste 1 tsp.
Ginger paste 1 tsp.
Tomato puree 1/2 cup
Red chili powder 1 tsp.
Garam masala powder 1 tsp.
Coriander powder 1 tsp.
Turmeric powder 2 tsp.
Cumin powder 1 tsp.
Oil 2 tsp.
Salt As per taste

  1. Remove stalk from cauliflower. Boil in salted water with 1 teaspoon turmeric powder till half cooked.
  2. Heat oil in a Kadhai. Add grated onions and saut� until golden brown in colour.
  3. Add Ginger Paste, Garlic Paste, cumin powder, coriander powder, remaining turmeric powder, Garam Masala Powder and salt. Stir for half a minute. Add tomato puree and cook till oil leaves the masala.
  4. Add 1/2 boiled cauliflower and cook on steam for 10 minutes.
  5. Garnished with chopped green coriander and serve hot with Chapati.
