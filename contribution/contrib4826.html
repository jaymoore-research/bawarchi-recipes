Sour Stuffed Dahi Vada

Ingredients

Noodles : 1 Pack
Masala Chilli Sauce : 4 tablespoon
Potatoes : 1 cup(boiled and mashed)
Mixed vegetables : 2 cups
bread : 3 slices(crumbs)
Salt as per taste
Cumin seed(roasted) : 1/2 tablespoon
Red chilli powder : 1/2 tablespoon
Garlic : 2 -3 cloves(mashed)
Curd(sour &thick) : 1 KG
Oil for frying

Method

  1. Cook noodles according to the pack instructions given behind the pack.
  2. Mix noodles, chilli sauce, mashed potatoes, vegetables, garlic and bread.
  3. Add salt as per your taste.
  4. Heat some oil, prepare vada from the the above mixture and deep fry till it turn out to be golden brown.
  5. While the vadas are frying, beat the curd to break the lumps and add salt, cumin seeds and red chilli powder to it.
  6. Place the vada in a dish, pour curd over them and keep them in fridge till there are chilled.
  7. Before serving, garnish with pieces of ginger, finely chopped coriander leaves and green chillies.
