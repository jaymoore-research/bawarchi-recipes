Haldi Roast Chicken And Potatoes

Ingredients

1 kg. chicken washed and dried on paper towels.
4 large boiled potatoes
3 tbsp oil + 1 tspsalt + 1 tsp sugar + 3/4 tsp haldi

Method:

  1. Put 3 tbsps oil into a pressure pan and heat.
  2. Add 1 tsp salt + 1 tsp sugar and let it caramelise till quite dark golden brown. Add 2 tbsp hot water to stop caramelisation. Add 3/4 tsp haldi.
  3. Put in the chicken joints and stir them quickly to cover with the caramel/haldi mixture. Continue to fry the chicken on medium heat till the pieces are nicely browned.
  4. Now add about 1/4 cup hot water and let it pressure cook for about 5 mins. Cool the cooker and open. Dry up and let the chicken continue to fry in this combination of chicken fat and oil till dry. Remove to a hot plate.
  5. Put in the halved boiled potatoes and sprinkle with a little salt and fry in this fat till golden brown. Remove to the chicken hot plate.
  6. Into the remaining fat, stir in 1/2 cup water with 1 heaped tsp corn flour and cook till you have a nice gravy.
  7. Serve the chicken roast with garlic bread and coleslaw.
