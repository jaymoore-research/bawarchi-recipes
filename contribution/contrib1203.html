Easy Bitter Gourd (Karela)

Ingredients

Karela (bitter gourd), 250 gms, peeled, deseeded
Onions, 2 medium or 1 large
Split peas (Channa dal) , 75 gms, parboiled
Onion seeds (Kalonji), 1 tbsp
Coriander powder, 2 tsp
Turmeric powder, 1 tsp
Red Chilli powder, 1 tsp (or as desired)
Tomatoes, 2 medium or 1 large, chopped
Salt to taste
Vegetable Oil, 2 tsp

Method :

  1. Slice onions finely
  2. Slit karelas in centre, rub a little salt on the inside and let stand for 30 mins. Rinse well and chop into small pieces
  3. In a saucepan, combine karelas, sliced onions, tomatoes, channa dal, kalonji and all the masalas. Add salt. You dont need to add water at all, but if you like you can add a little (1-2 tbsp). Cover lid tightly and let it steam on low heat till karelas and dal are soft and tomatoes are well blended
  4. Uncover saucepan, add 2 tsp vegetable oil to the pan and cook stirring for a few minutes till oil leaves the masala.
  5. Before serving, heat well and garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
