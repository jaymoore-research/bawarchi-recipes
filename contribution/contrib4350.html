Manga Perukku (Mango Chatni)

Ingredients

1. Raw Mango - 2 nos
2. Coconut (grated) - 1 cup
3. Chilli Powder - 2 tbs
4. Mustard (Kaduku) - 1 tbsp
5. Cumin Seed (Jeerakam) - 1 tsp
6. Curry Leaves - 1 stalk
7. Curd - 1 tbsp
8. Salt - As required
9. Coconut Oil - 1 tbsp

Method:

  1. Peel out the Mango and either grate it or make small pieces.
  2. Grind mango pieces along with grated coconut, chilli powder, mustard, cumin seeds, curry leaves (few) etc.
  3. Then add curd, coconut oil, curry leaves and mix well. Now your manga perukku is ready to increase your appetite. Taste it and send me your feedback. Best of luck!!
