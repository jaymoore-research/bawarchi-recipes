Rawa Kesari

Ingredients

1 cup rawa
ghee/oil
2 cups sugar
A pinch of kesari powder mixed with milk
3 cups of boiled water/milk
cashewnuts, dry grapes
1 banana

Method:

  1. Fry rawa, cashew and the grapes in ghee/oil till golden brown.
  2. Then add the boiled water/milk.
  3. Keep stirring and when it gets cooked add sugar and simultaneously the kesari powder.
  4. Keep adding ghee as and when required.
  5. Finally cut the banana into small pieces and add them to the kesari.
  6. Garnish with cardamom powder. Serve hot.
