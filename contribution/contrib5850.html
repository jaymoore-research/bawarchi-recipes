Royal Idli

Ingredients

Idly - 5
Tomatoes - 3
Onions - 2
Green chillies - 2
Cornflour - 2 tbsp
Chilli powder - 1 tbsp
Turmeric powder - 1 tsp
Tomato Ketchup - 1 tbsp
Salt - 2 tsp
Food colour (red) - a pinch (optional)
Oil
Coriander leaves

Method

  1. Cut the idlies into 1/2" cubes.
  2. Make a thick batter using cornflour, salt & food colour.
  3. Heat oil in a pan, dip idly pieces in batter and deep fry.
  4. Place the fried idly pieces on a tissue paper, till all the excess oil isĀĀĀĀ absorbed.
  5. Finely chop tomatoes, onions and green chillies.
  6. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan.
  7. Add chillies and allow them to splutter.
  8. Add onions and saute till they become transparent.
  9. Add chilli powder, turmeric powder and salt, stir for 1 min.
  10. Add tomatoes and cook till the oil separates.
  11. Add the idly pieces and cook for 5 mins on a low flame.
  12. Add tomato ketchup, stir well and cook for 1min.
  13. Garnish & serve with chopped coriander leaves.

Alternatively you can use mini idlies as it is, instead of dicing regular size idlies. This is a nice tea time snack and can also be a part of a multi-course dinner.
