Here I present the recipe for a very tasteful Rajasthani Dish.

Gattey Ki Bhaji

Ingredients

1 & 1/2 cups Wheat flour
1 tbsp Gram flour
2 tbsp Oil
1/2 cup Butter milk
1 tsp jeera/cumin seeds
2 tsp red chilli powder
2 green chillies,chopped
1 tsp turmeric powder
1 tsp saunf/ani seeds
chopped coriander
salt to taste

Method

  1. Mix wheat flour, gram flour, 1 tbsp oil, jeera, red chilli powder,turmeric & saunf.
  2. Knead a stiff dough with luke warm water.
  3. Shape this dough to a cylinderical shape & then cut it to about 10-11 inch long pieces.
  4. Steam these pieces in cooker for 15-20 min.
  5. Now these steamed dough pieces are called Gattey. Cut them to small pieces approx. 1 inch long.
  6. Now heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan, add jeera & green chillies & fry.
  7. Add butter milk & bring to a boil.
  8. Now add the small pieces of Gattey, heat for 3 min.Add chopped coriander.
  9. Your dish is ready. Serve hot, this tasty Gattey Ki Bhaji with Rotis/Parathas.

Serves : Four

Notes : Gattey is not a vegetable, but is prepared from wheat flour & gram flour.
