Dahi Vada

Ingredients

1) Udad daal - 1 cup
2) Green Chillies - 3 Nos
3) Ginger - 1-2 small pieces
4) Dahi - 4 cups
5) Rai - 1 tsp
6) Green Chilli paste - 2 tsp
7) Oil - 6 table spoon
8) Salt to taste
9) Cilantro (coriander) (Dania patha)- optional

Method:

  1. Soak udad daal with green chillies and ginger for 4-5 hours.
  2. Then drain the water and Put the contents (add salt) in the blender and blend it till it becomes into a course paste (do not blend it into a fine paste).
  3. Put the pan and add oil to it and heat it till it is ready for frying. Make the paste into a small balls and fry it in the oil till it is golden brown.
  4. Immediately put the vada in the Dahi.

Dahi

  1. Beat the curd in a bowl
  2. Add Green chilli paste to the curd
  3. Take another pan, put 2 tsp of oil and add rai to it (wait till it splutters). Put the rai in the curd.
  4. Add coriander leaves for garnishing.
  5. Add salt to taste.

For 1 cup of udad daal you will get 8 - 10 vadas.
