Bread Dahi Wada

Ingredients

One loaf Bread
3 cups Curds
1 1/2 cup Water
1 Cucumber
2 tsp Sugar
2 tsp Salt
1 tsp Jeera powder
1 tsp Amchur powder
1 tsp Red Chilli powder
Green Chillies according to taste
Raisins
Cashewnuts
Little Ghee for frying

Method

  1. Take two slices of bread at a time and cut the edges and cut it into four pieces in whichever shape you like keep them aside.
  2. Mix curds, water, sugar and 1 tsp of salt. Mix them well (put it in a mixie for good results).
  3. Grate the cucumber, cut green chillies into small pieces and then fry raisins and cashew nuts in ghee and add all these and little bit of salt to the filling. Put this filling in between the two slices of bread which u cut in shapes.
  4. Pour some of the curd mixture in a flat container or plate put these slices on the mixture and again pour the rest of the curd mixture on these slices.
  5. You can sprinkle jeera powder, amchur powder and red chilli powder on it.
  6. Put it in fridge an hour before serving and then serve.

I am sure everybody will enjoy it the most. The edges of the bread which u cut need not be wasted either they can be dried and make them into bread crums or fry them and use them in soup.
