Ingredients

2 cups of Spagatti
1 cup of Boiled Corn
2 tbsp of Oil
2 Green Pepper (chopped finely)
Salt
Mustard seeds
Turmeric
Coriander Powder (Dhana Jeera powder)
Red Chilli Powder (if you prefer)

Method:

  1. Cook spagatti in boiled water with some salt and water. Drain and keep aside.
  2. Heat oil in the pan and add mustard seeds, green pepper, turmeric and coriander powder but make sure it doesn't burn.
  3. Add boied corn and add salt. Cook the corn well. When corn is done, just add boiled spagatti and add little bit of salt and red chilli powder and cook till everything is well mixed and spagatti is hot.
  4. You can also add little bit of lemon juice if you prefer in the end.

No certain quantities for masalas are written since you can add them according to your taste.
