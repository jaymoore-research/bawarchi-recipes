Shrimp and Egg Sandwich

Ingredients

Boiled shrimps 1/2 lbs
Green chillies 5- 6 nos.
Cumin powder 1/2 tsp
Garlic flakes 3-4 nos.
Fresh Coriander 5-6 sticks
Sandwich bread sliced
Eggs 3-4 nos.
Turmeric powder 1/2 tsp
Salt to taste
Oil to fry

Method:

  1. Grind boiled shrimp, green chillies, cumin powder, garlic flakes, fresh coriander, & salt without water, paste this mixture on the bread slices.
  2. Cut those bread sandwiches into half & dip those in mixture of egg, turmeric powder, & salt.
  3. Now shallow fry those in oil.
  4. Serve the sandwiches with salad.
