Stuff Bell Pepper

Ingredients

Two fresh bell pepper
Two medium size patato
One big onion
One red fresh tomato
Little green coriender leaves
Half tbls. turmuric
One tbls.coriender powder
Half tbls.chilly powder
Half tbls.garam masala
One tbls. salt
Vegetable oil

Method

  1. Boil and peel the potatos.
  2. Chop the onion,tomato ,coriender leaf and little green chilly also.
  3. Put some oil in a pan some musturd and cumin seed also.
  4. Then put chopped onion and green chilly and fry it till onion becomes in golden colour.
  5. Mash the potato over the pan and put all spices.
  6. Fry it for another 2-3 minute.
  7. Put chopped tomato and coriender leaves,and mix it properly,close the stove.
  8. Wash the bell pepper,cut the upper part in the round shape and take off the inside material.
  9. Put potato masala inside the bell pepper and grease it outside the bell pepper with some oil.
  10. Bake it for 15-20 minute at 400F.

Now your stuffed bell pepper is ready .
Serving time you may cut it into the round shape .

Shelflife: 2-3 days in the refrigerated.
Making time: 45 minute.
