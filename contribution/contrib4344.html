Beetroot Varuval

Ingredients

4 to 5 medium sized Beets
3" piece - Cinnamon
4 to 5 - Cloves
1 tsp - Hot Pepper Powder (hot chilly powder) or your favorite curry powder or garam masala
Salt - to your taste.
4 Tblsp (or more) - Oil

Method:

  1. Slice beets thin into 3 to 4mm thick pieces of about 1 x 1" squares by hand or in food processor. (Of course, you know the beets stain your fingers. Wear gloves if you are touchy about the transient stain.)
  2. Heat oil in a skillet. Add cinnamon and cloves. Cover with lid to avoid being splashed by hot oil.
  3. When the cloves puff up - caution! do not burn the spices- add the sliced beets, stir and roast the beet for about 5 minutes.
  4. Add hot pepper or curry powder, mix and keep stirring and roasting on low heat. It takes a good deal of time on stove top. To cut short the time, one can transfer the skillet contents into a microwave pan or plate and keep roasting 5 minutes at a time on high, stirring the beets in between. When the beets are little chewy and little crispy it is done.

If you are using microwave, be careful towards the end. If you over cook it, you may end up with beet chips. This is a good side dish for rice with sambar, rasam or buttermilk.
