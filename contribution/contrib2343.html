Tomato Chutney

Ingredients

Medium sized Tomatoes - 3
Chilli Powder - 1 tsp
Asafoetida - 1 small piece
Garlic - 5 big flakes
Curry leaves - 2 stalks
Tamarind - marble sized
Salt - as desired
Mustard seeds - 1 tsp
Oil - 4 tsp

Method:

  1. Heat 2 tsp oil and fry the asafoetida till crisp, drain and keep aside.
  2. In the same oil, add chopped tomatoes and saute till it comes to a pulp consistency. Allow to cool a bit.
  3. Add the chilli powder, asafoetida, garlic, tamarind, 1 stalk curry leaves and salt and grind to a smooth paste.
  4. Heat oil, add mustard and allow to splutter, then add the remaining curry leaves and the ground paste. Mix well and cook till oil starts leaving the mixture.
  5. Store when cool.

This chutney stands for 2 days without refrigeration and about 2 weeks in the fridge.
