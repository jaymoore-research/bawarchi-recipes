Coriander Powder for Idli (Idli Podi)

Ingredients

1/2 cup (Red Dried Chillies)
1 Tbsp. Oil
3 Tbsp. whole Coriander seeds
1 Tbsp. Tamarind (seedless)
1 tsp. Salt
5 whole Garlic pods
1 Tbsp. Cumin seeds

Note: tsp. teaspoon, Tbsp. Table spoon

Method:

  1. At medium heat, in a medium size wok, add oil and chillies. Stir the chillies until they are fried (takes about 7 minutes). Then add whole coriander seeds and fry them for about 4 minutes.
  2. Remove seeds from tamarind and make small pieces from this. Mix this tamarind (1 Tbsp) and salt with all the above ingredients and blend them for about 4 minutes changing from high speed to low speed and vice versa as required.
  3. Add small pieces of garlic pods and cumin seeds to the above mixure and blend them for 5-6 seconds.

we can store this coriander powder in a bottle in a refrigerator for longer periods. Very good to enjoy with ghee and idli. Also, in the good olden days in AP, India, the ladies will be served this, rasam with the rice as a meal the first three days after delivery.

Note: Add a small amount of tamarind for sourness as per your taste. If necessary, add a few garlic pods and cumin seeds in the above process as per your taste. Though okay with AP people, If hot for you, reduce garlic pods and a few chillies. Hotness is reduced after keeping one week in a refrigerator.

Qty for serving: 1 cup
