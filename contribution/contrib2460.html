Rava Ladoo

Ingredients

Rava (Sooji) - 2 cups
Sugar (fine granulated) - 2 1/2 cups
Clarified butter/Ghee - 1 cup
Cashewnuts - 10
Raisins (Kismis) - 10
Cardamom (Elaichi) - 3
Cloves (Laung) - 5
Nutmeg powder - 1 pinch (optional)

Method

  1. Fry Rava in a non-stick pan till it turns light brown & gives out an aroma.(Don't over fry it). Let it cool.
  2. Fry Cardamom, Cloves & powder it with Nutmeg.
  3. Heat 2 spoons of ghee in a pan & fry Cashewnuts, add raisins later.
  4. Powder the Rava & Sugar separately in mixie, then mix them.
  5. Mix all the ingredients well & make ladoos by adding the rest of the ghee little at a time.

Yummy Rava Ladoos are ready. It is very easy to make. You can make upto 25 ladoos.
