Mango Pulp Sheera

Ingredients

Soji Rava - 1 cup
Sugar -1 cup(can use 3/4 gives very mild sweetness)
Ghee - 2 tbs
Cashews - 6-8 chopped
Raisins/kismis- 6-8
Water - 3 cups
Mango pulp-1 1/2 tbs(usually u get mango pulp tins at Indian stores else can squeeze the regular raw sweet mango or grind them coarsely)
cardamom/elachi powder -little for flavour(optional)
saffron (kukumpuvu/kesai)4-5(optional)
orange color optional(IĀ prefer either orange or yellow color)

Method

  1. First add 1 spoon of ghee in pan/vessel warm it and fry the cashew and raisin and keepĀ it aside.
  2. Then add the rava to the same panĀ
  3. Fry it until u get the aroma of soji and keep the rava aside in a different vessel let it cool.
  4. Now in a pan add the water close the lid bring it boiling.
  5. Now mix rava and sugar together well
  6. Add them to boiling water.keep stiring to avoid lumps after 2 minutes
  7. Add color ,saffron,raisins,cashew,mango pulp, ghee and little oil cardamom powder and mix it well and close theĀ lid until it gets cooked.
  8. Serve it hot.
