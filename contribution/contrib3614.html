Poritha Kuzhambu

Ingredients

Vegetables - Carrots, Potatoes, Brinjal, Peas, Chou-Chou (chayote), Squash, Wintermelon (also Drumsticks and other Indian vegs can be used).
Sambar powder- 2 tbsp
Toor dal - 1/2 cup

For ginding:

Coconut Powder - 1/2 cup
Jeera - 1 tsp

Method:

  1. First chop the vegetables into small pieces.
  2. Put them in a vessel and add turmeric powder, salt and sambar powder.
  3. Pressure cook these and the toor dal separately in a pressure cooker.
  4. Meanwhile, grind the coconut along with jeera and make a smooth paste.
  5. After the vegetables and the dal are cooked. Put the vegetables in a kadai and boil for 5 mts.
  6. Add the coconut paste and also the mashed toor dal into the kadai. Check seasoning. If you find there is not enough spice, you can add a little bit more Sambar powder into the mixture.
  7. Let it boil for sometime. If its very watery, you can take 1 tsp rice powder, mix some water with it and then pour it over the mixture. This acts as a thickening agent. After the mixture as attained a kootu consistency(semi solid), remove from fire
  8. For garnish, heat some oil in a pan, add mustard and urad dal. After the mustard splutters and the dal turns light brown, pour it over the mixture.
  9. Serve hot with rice. This also makes a nice side dish for Chapathi.
