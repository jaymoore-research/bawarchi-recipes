Corn Malai

Ingredients

mashed boiled corns - i cup( tin corns can be used)
fresh meethi - 3 bunches
garlic cloves crushed - 2 tsp.
red chilli powder - 1/2 tsp.
onions - 1 chopped
fresh cream - 1 cup
garam masala - 1/2 tsp.
oil- 2-3 tsp.
salt to taste.

Method:

In a pan pour oil,add onions sau'te fr a while,now add fresh meethi cook fr good 5 minutes till you discover meethi aroma, then add garlic to it followed by red chiili powder add 1 cup water cook then add corn cook till thick consistency, finally add fresh cream cook on low flame,last add salt amd garam masala,and your corn malai is ready to be served with hot chappatis.

Enjoy.
