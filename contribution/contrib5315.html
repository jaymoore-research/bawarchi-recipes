Chicken Masala Fry

Ingredients

Chicken - 1 lb
Onion - 1 medium
Ginger & Garlic Paste- 1 tbsp
Dry Chillies - 4 no
Coriander seeds - 1 tbsp
Turmeric powder- 1 tsp
Sour Cream - 1/2 cup
Pepper corns- 1/4 tsp
Cooking Oil - 3 tbsp
Cilantro - 1 tbsp

Method:

  1. In a pan fry all the dry masala ingredients till the aroma comes then grind them into powder.
  2. To the chicken add the sour cream, turmeric powder and the grounded masala powder. Let them marinate for atleast half an hour.
  3. The marinated chicken should be added to a kadai containing oil and fried using little amount of water. When the chicken is almost done add thinly sliced onion and let them cook for few min.
  4. Finally the cilantro leaves are added for garnish.
