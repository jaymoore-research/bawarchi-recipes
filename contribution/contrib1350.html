Bittergourd Gojju

Ingredients

1 tbsp. of Dhania (coriander)
2 tbsp. Urad daal,
2 tbsp. Channa daal
2 tbsp. dry Coconut
3-4 Red chillies
1 tbsp. Til
1 tbsp. Methi seeds
2 tbsp. Oil
2 or 3 Bittergourds
1 tbsp. Mustard seeds
1 tbsp. Jeera
2 cups Tamarind juice
3 or 4 cups of Jaggery
Salt to taste

Method :

  1. First we should fry dhania, urad daal, channa daal, coconut, red chillies, til and methi seeds in a kadai with little oil.
  2. After cooling blend all those mixture to powder with turmeric and salt.
  3. Now take some oil in a kadai and season with mustard seeds, jeera, red chillies, urad daal, channa daal.
  4. Cut bittergourd into round thin slices and fry it deeply with the above.
  5. Add tamarind juice to the above and now add the mixed powder sufficiently with more jaggery.
  6. Boil it for 5 minutes. Thats all its done.

It can be served with Rice, Idlis or Pongal.
