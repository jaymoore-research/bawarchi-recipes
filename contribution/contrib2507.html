Ingredients

2-4 Ripe Mangoes, diced
A small peice Ashgourd or Cucumber, diced
Pinch Turmeric Powder
1 cup freshly grated Coconut
2-4 Green Chillies
1 teaspoon Rice
1 teaspoon Cumin Seeds
1 tablespoon Coconut Oil or any oil
1 teaspoon Black Mustard
1-2 dry Red Chillies
1/4 teaspoon Fenugreek Seeds
1 sprig Curry Leaves
Salt to taste

Method

  1. In a thick sauce pan cook mango and ashgourd pieces in 1-2 cup water containing turmeric powder and salt.
  2. Put the grated coconut, cumin seeds, green chillies and rice into a blender and make a fine paste.
  3. Once the vegetables are cooked well (about 15 minutes) add the coconut paste and simmer for 5 minutes more.
  4. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat, add black mustard seeds, broken red chillies and fenugreek seeds- saute till the mustard sputters. Quickly add curry leaves and saute for a few seconds more and then combine with mango-coconut mixture.
  5. Serve hot with rice and stir-fried vegetables (thoran or poriyal).

Note: If you desire add half cup sour buttermilk. If the mangoes are sourish add some jaggery.
