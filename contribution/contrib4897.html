Capsicums Curry

Ingredients

3 Large Capsicums
1/2 tsp chilli powder
1/4 tsp turmeric powder
1/4 tsp cinnamon powder
1/4 tsp mustard seeds
1/2 tsp cumin seeds
1 tbsp dhania powder
2 tbsp besan powder
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Cut the capsicums into small pieces.
  2. Season the mustard seeds, cumin seeds & turmeric powder.
  3. Add the cut capsicums, chilli powder, you can keep it covered it for 5-10 mins depending on the time it takes to cook or cook it open.
  4. Add salt, cinnamon powder, dhania powder and the besan powder when the capsicums are almost done.
  5. Mix all the ingredients thoroughly & leave it on the stove for 5 mins and its all done.
  6. This can be had as a side dish with rice or chapatti.
