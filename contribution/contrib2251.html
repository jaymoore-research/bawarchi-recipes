Buns

Ingredients

2 cups Maida
1 tbsp Bengal Gram Flour
1 Plantain
1 tsp Baking Soda
2 tbsp Sugar
1 cup Curd
1 tsp Salt
Oil for frying.

Method

  1. Seive maida and bengal gram flour.
  2. Mash plantain with curds, salt, sugar and baking soda in a bowl.
  3. Add the flour and little water to make into a soft dough. Apply a little oil to it and keep the dough covered for 6 hours.
  4. Then shape portions of the dough into balls of lime size and flatten them into thick puris.
  5. Deep fry in oil till golden brown.

Buns can be eaten plain or with any gravy dish.

Note: My family likes to eat it sipping hot coffee in between bites.
