Ennai Katrikai

Ingredients for Cooking

Green brinjals - 1/4 kg
TamarindĀpaste - 3 cups
Ginger paste - 1/2 tsp.
Oil - 4 tsp.
Mustard seeds - 1/2 tsp.
Methi seeds - 1/2 tsp.
Curry leaves - 5-6
Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp.

Ingredients for dry masala:-
Dhaniya seeds - 50 gms
Urad dal - 2 tsp.
Chilli powder - 50 gms
Hing (asafoetida) - a pinch
Salt as required.

Method:

  1. Dry roast the dry masala ingredients.
  2. Grind it into smooth powder in a grinder. U can add little water while grinding the masala.
  3. Wash the brinjals & make 4 cuts in the brinjals. Don't cut the stem of the brinjals.
  4. Fill the masala into each of these whole brinjals. Leave the remaining masala for the gravy.
  5. Heat the oil in a kadai, splutter the mustard seeds, methi seeds, gingerĀpaste & turmeric powder. Also add the curry leaves.
  6. Now place the brinjals one by one slowly in the kadai & let it fry for 3 mins.
  7. Now pour the rest of the masala & the tamarind water.
  8. Let this cook covered in a medium flame for about 1/2 hour. Check after every 15 mins.
  9. The indication for it to be completely cooked will be when the oil comes above the gravy.
  10. Serve hot with steamed rice & papadum.
