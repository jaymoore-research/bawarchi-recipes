Suji Laddu

Ingredients

Sooji or Bombay Rava- 2 cups
Sugar - 2 cups (If you like more sugar yon put � more cup)
Ghee - 1/2 cup
Cashewnuts - 10
Raisins (Kismis) - 10
Cardamom (Elaichi) - 3
Coconut powder - 1/2 cup (Optional)
Milk - 1 cup

Method:

  1. Fry Suji in 2 tsp of ghee till it turns light brown, Let it cool.
  2. Fry Cardamom and make a powder.
  3. Heat 2 spoons of ghee in a pan & fry Cashewnuts, later add raisins.
  4. Powder the Rava & Sugar separately in mixer, then mix them. (Optional)
  5. Mix all the ingredients well & make ladoos by adding the little bit milk.

Laddu's will be soft and yummy. If you would like to store laddu's for a longer period then store them in a closed container and when ever you feel like eating microwave it for 10 sec, then also laddu's will be soft and hot.

Yammy tastes good, isn't it!!!

Note: Milk is other way of doing laddu's, if you like ghee use ghee to make laddu's, but I usually avoid ghee.
