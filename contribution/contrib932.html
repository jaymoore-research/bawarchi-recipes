FatFree Clove Chicken

Ingredients

1/2 Lb. skinless chicken (preferably breast pieces)
10 clove leaves
1/2 tsp. ground cardamom
1 sq. Inch ginger
5 garlic buds
1 tsp. Cinnamon powder
Ground black pepper
Salt
3 large tomatoes, onions
Turmeric
Halapeno peppers or mirchis,
Poppy seeds
Cup of water
1 tbsp. olive oil

Utensils: Deep vessel, preferably with a lid, cutting board

Method :

  1. Cut onion pieces 1/4sq.inch, cut tomatoes, make a paste of Garlic and Ginger, chop halapeno pepper, cut the chicken into sizable pieces (small pieces will shrink later)
  2. Heat oil on a very low flame. Add onions, stir till light brown. Add a pinch of salt, add the ginger/garlic paste, add chicken pieces, wait for the sizzle to settle. Add a cup of water, add the cloves, cardamom, add cinnamon, add black pepper and cover the vessel. Cook for 15 minutes on a med-high flame, till chicken turns fibrous.
  3. Open the lid partly, to let steam, moisture out. Cook for 10 minutes, on a low flame.
  4. Taste for sourness, saltiness and also check whether the spices have been absorbed thoroughly, in the broth and the meat
  5. Ready to serve with loose, starch free rice, or wheat biscuits

Note: Add water according to gravy structure. This entry�e can be served pasty or dry. To make gravy more thick, use tomato paste or cooking starch. Add more cloves to enhance the flavor.

Serves: 4 adults

Nutrition: Cholesterol free - olive oil and garlic. Antiseptic - turmeric, Refreshing and Energizing - cloves, cardamom and cinnamon, protienous - chicken.
