Palak Subzi

Ingredients

Potatoes : 1/2 kg
Amaranth leaves or Spianch Leaves : 2 bunches
Oil : 3 tea spoon
Channa Dal : 1 tea spoon
Turmeric powder : 1 tea spoon
Chilli powder : 2 tea spoon
Salt : to taste
Cummin seeds : 1/2 tea spoon
Curry leaves : few

Method:

  1. Pressure cook the potatoes with their skin, peel, mash and set aside.
  2. Wash the spinach or amaranath leaves and chop fine.
  3. Heat 3 teaspoons oil, add the mustard seeds and the curry leaves. When the mustard seeds splutter, add the cummin seeds, channadal and saute for a couple of minutes. Add the chopped spinach and sprinkle water. Cover on a low heat tilll the spinach is tender and dry.
  4. Add to the mashed potatoes, turmeric powder, salt and chilli powder. Mix well. Cook for a minute, till everything blends well.
  5. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  6. Serve hot with puri, phulkas and naans.
