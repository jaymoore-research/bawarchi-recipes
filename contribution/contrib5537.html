Masala Khichdi

Ingredients for Cooking

1. Rawa                                          ...    1 cup
2. Onion -- chopped                         ...    2 nos
3. Tomatoes (country) -- cut             ...    2 or 3 nos
4. Garlic                                         ...    3 pearls
5. Green chilies-- cut                    ...    3 nos
6. Elachi                                        ...    2 nos
7. Clove                                         ...    2 nos   
8. Cinnamon                                  ...    small piece
9. oil                                              ...    to fry
10. Mustard                                     ...    1 tsp
11. Ghee                                         ...    1/2 tsp
12. Turmeric powder                         ...    1 tsp
13. Coriander leaves -- cut                 ...    a little
14. Curry leaves -- cut                       ...    a little
15. Salt                                            ...    to taste

  Method:

  1. Fry rawa in a pan with a little oil or ghee.
  2. Grind garlic, green chillies, elachi,  clove, cinnamon in a mixie  into a paste.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and add the mustard. 
  4. After it splatters, add the cut onion and a pinch of salt and saute in low flame, till the onion becomes transparent.
  5. Add the vegetables and saute. and add the turmeric powder.
  6. Add the tomatoes, increase the flame and saute for 2 more minutes.
  7. Add the ground masala and saute till the raw smell of the garlic goes.
  8. Add 2-1/2 cups of water and allow it to boil.
  9. Add the rawa, mix well, reduce the flame and keep it closed with a lid for 5 minutes.
  10. Take it off from the stove, garnish with a tablespoonful of ghee, coriander leaves, curry leaves 
  11. Serve hot for a better taste.  
  12. As an alternative to 2 1/2 cups of water, you can add 1 cup of  coconut juice (coconut milk) and 1-1/2 cup of water.
