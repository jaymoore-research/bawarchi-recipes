Allu Manchuria

Ingredients

For Deep frying
Potatoes 4 medium sized (Cut into bite sized pieces)
Soya Sauce 1tsp
Chilli Sauce 1tsp
Pepper Powder 1/2tsp (according to taste)
Corn Flour 2tsp
Ajnamoto Pinch
Salt to taste

For Gravy

Curd 2tsp
Coriander
Mint
Green Chillies 2
Ginger Garlic Paste 2tsp
Garam Masala
Red Color Pinch.

Method

  1. Mix the Deep frying ingredients and keep it aside for 2 minutes. Deep fry them and Keep it aside for later use.
  2. Grind all the gravy ingredients into a thick paste. Don't add a lot of water.
  3. Fry the gravy in 4 tsp of oil with curry leaves and garlic pods, till the gravy gets thick and a little oil should stand on top
  4. Add the fried Potatoes into this gravy and fry on simmer for 4min.

Variation

The same curry can be replaced with Cauliflower or Chicken. Tastes great. Serve it with steaming rice with little ghee. Tastes very good and takes very little time to prepare. Hope you like this.
