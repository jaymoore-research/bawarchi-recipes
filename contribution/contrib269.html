Kadabus

Ingredients

2 cups of thoor dhaal
2 cups of jaggery(brown sugar)
2 cardamoms
for the leaves to cover
all purpose flour
oil
water

Method

  1. First prepare the dough for the leaves to the flour add water and 2 tea spoons of cooking oil and kneed well and leave aside for about 3- 4 hrs
  2. Then boil water add the toor dhal when the water is rolling boil
  3. After it is half cooked, then add the 2 cups of powdered jaggery with 2 pods of cardamom, cook well
  4. And grind it well when it is warm,
  5. Prepare the leaves about 2-3 inches and stuff it with dhal and stick the two sides by wetting lightly on one side ,see that they dont open while deep frying, precaution should be taken not use the frying spoon over and over as the kadabu may break open
  6. Serve hot ,
  7. Hope you enjoy ganesh chathurthi with hot delicious kadabus
