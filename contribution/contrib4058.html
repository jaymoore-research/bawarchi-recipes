Bhature

Ingredients

Plain self raising flour (maida) 1/2 kg
Baking powder 1/2 tsp
Soda bi-carbonate 1/2 tsp
Salt 1tsp
Oil
Lukewarm Water

Method:

  1. Add baking powder, soda, salt to self raising flour (maida).
  2. Knead the above to dough with the help of Luke warm water.
  3. Finally add oil to it and knead again for few seconds.
  4. Fry like puree and serve with cholle.

For summer: You can prepare the dough half an hour for fermentation earlier to frying.

For winter: At least two hours for fermentation before frying.
