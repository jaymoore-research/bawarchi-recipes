Masale Bhath

Ingredients

1 Kg Ambe Mohar rice
1/4 kg Green Peas
1/4 tsp Turmeric
1/4 tsp Mustard seeds
Salt
7-8 Curry leaves
Oil
Roast in little oil and dry grind in mixer 1 tblspn Coriander seeds, 1 tblspn Cumin seeds, 1 tbsp Sesame seeds, 5-6 Cloves, 5-6 Cinnamon.

Method:

  1. Heat oil in pressure pan or cooker. Add curry leaves, turmeric powder, mustard seeds and fry well. Add green peas. Then add cleaned and washed rice. Add water in the proportion 1:2. Put the ground powder. Add salt and mix well. Close the cooker or pan and keep for 2 whistles.
  2. Heat ghee in a pan and fry some cashew nuts / plums.
  3. Garnish the rice with cashewnuts/plums, little coconut and coriander leaves. Serve hot.
