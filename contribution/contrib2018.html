Rice and Jaggery Pancakes

Ingredients

Rice 1 cup
Jaggery 1 cup
Cream 1/2 cup
Baking soda a pinch
Elachi pwd

Method:

Soak the rice overnight ,drain it & powder it dry.....Or even ready made rice flour can be taken. Roast the rice flour. Take a pan add the jaggery & water to it & once it melts add elachi pwd to it strain it.Meanwhile take a baking dish & grease it with oil.

Now slowly add the jaggery syrup to the rice flour along with the baking soda& cream,mix it well & make a thick batter.

Pour it into the baking dish & steam it for 15 minutes.

Once it is slightly cool cut it into square pieces & serve it for breakfast or even as a snack.
