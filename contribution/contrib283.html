Ingredients

1/4 kg rice flour
100gm roasted channa dal powder
small cup dry coconut powder
1 tbs red chilli powder
salt as needed
1 small cup peanut

Method

  1. Take the rice flour in a big mixing bowl,
  2. To that add the other ingredients channa dal powder salt, chilli powde,r dry coconut powder and 1 big tbs hot oil
  3. To that add coersely ground peanuts
  4. Mix well by adding little by little water
  5. Take little by little , pat them on a plastic sheet ,or with your hand & deep fry in oil you can keep them for about a month or so this can be prepared before hand too
