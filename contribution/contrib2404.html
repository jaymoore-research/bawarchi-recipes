Veg Boiled Yong Tow Foo

Ingredients

200 Grams Bee Hoon (White narrow chinese noodles, made of rice, fresh If you can get it in your local area).
100 Grams Tow Foo (Chinese Bean Curd)
4 fresh Ladies Fingers
4 straws of green Chinese Kai Lan (Green Veg)
2 pieces Chinese Bitter Gourd.
2 Strings Spring Onions

Method:

  1. Place a 4 Liter boiling deep pan (or a similar utensil) on gas fire, pour 3 liters of water & boil it to steam.
  2. Put the fresh Bee Hoon in a strainer of 6 inches diameter and dip & hold the strainer into the boiling water to let the bee hoon boil just for 2 mins. Remeove, strain & keep the dry been hoon in a bowl.
  3. Place the pre cut Bean curd in small cubes, Sliced the lady fingers, strips of the Kai Lan in small bunches and pre cut spring onions in pieces into the same strainer and lower & dip deep into the boiling water & keep & hold for good 3-4 minutes.
  4. Remove , put all boiled Veggies together into the same bowl on top of the boiled Bee Hoon already inside. Spray a little of dark Soya Sauce (not light) on top and lavishly spray any light soya on top of the dark soya sauce.
  5. Serve the lot in the Bowl, piping hot along with with Chilli sauce and cut Green chillies in vinegar separately in small serving dishes.
Eat with Chop sticks, toss stir the bowl already with Bee Hoon & Veggies well before you eat and use chilli sauce & chillies in vinegar to dip into with each mouthful as you eat. It's really Yummy .....
