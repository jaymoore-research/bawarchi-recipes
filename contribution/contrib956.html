Spicy Chutney Podi

Ingredients

Channa Dal - 1 Glass
Grated Dry Coconut - 1 Glass
Red Chillies - 1/4 Glass
Tamarind - Size of a lime
Salt - To taste
Sugar - A spoon
Mustard - 1 Table Spoon
Coriander Seeds - 1 Table Spoon
White Til - 1 Table Spoon

Method :

  1. Dry fry all the above ingredient each one separately.
  2. First powder the Mustard, White Til and Coriander Seeds and to this add the sugar, salt, tamarind dry coconut and Channa Dal which was fried to a golden colour.
  3. Tasty Spicy Chutney Powder goes very well with Idlis , Dosas and also with Steamed White Rice.
