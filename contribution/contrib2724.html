Mixed Vegetable Bonda

Ingredients

1 cup Mixed Vegetables (Peas, Carrot, Beans, Kidneybeans, Potato, Onion (Optional))
1 1/2 cup Channa Besan Flour
1 Handful Rice Flour
Pinch of Hing and Turmeric
2 tsp of Red Chilli powder or 10 green chillies cut into small pieces
1/2 stick of Butter
Salt to taste
Oil to deep fry.

  1. Mix all ingriedients in a bowl and make into a dough which could be rollable.
  2. Make the dough into balls and deep fry in the oil.
  3. Serve with Coconut chutney
