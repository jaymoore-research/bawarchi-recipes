Tomato Gojji

Ingredients

1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
2 teaspoonfuls of sugar
1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
1 teaspoon blackgram
1 red chilli made into pieces
3-4 curry leaves
2 tablespoons of oil
2 big tomatoes cut into pieces
2 medium sized onions cut into pieces
3 sprigs coriander finely cut
2 tablespoon curd
1 karhai, water & salt to taste.

Method:

  1. Place the karhai on fire and fry the blackgram, mustard & redchilli in oil.
  2. When mustard starts sputtering, add the onions and curry leaves and fry till the onion becomes transparent.
  3. Add the tomatoes & fry briefly.
  4. Now add the rest of the ingredients and fry with the lid closed.
  5. Add a little water just enough to partially cover the vegetables.
  6. Remove from fire after 8 minutes when tomato and onion have got well mixed.
  7. Now add the curds. Add a little more curds & water if it is necessary. You can also add the cut coriander leaves later.

Now the gojji is ready to eat. Enjoy!
