Masala Peppers

Ingredients

Minced Meat or Chicken - 1/2 cup
Mushrooms (finely chopped) - 1/2 cup
Rice (cooked) - 1/2 cup
Onion (chopped) - 1 no.
Tomato (chopped) - 1 no
Ginger garlic paste - 1 tsp
Garam masala - 1 tsp
Chilli powder - 1/2 tsp
Coriander(chopped) - 3 tbsp
Bell peppers - 2 nos
Oil - 2 tbsp
Shredded Cheese - 1/2 cup
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and fry onions and ginger garlic paste.
  2. Add minced meat to it and fry for 5 min in medium heat. Add Tomatoes and chilli powder and cook with little water .
  3. Now add the mushrooms and rice and fry for 5 min and cover it .
  4. Add garam masala and coriander and keep aside.
  5. Take bell peppers and cut horizantaly into two halves (like two cups).
  6. . Remove seeds and the inner material and brush with oil. Stuff them with the cooked mixture and add cheese on top. Grease the peppers with oil outside.
  7. Preheat oven to 375oF and bake for 20 - 25 min. Now the peppers are ready. Enjoy!
