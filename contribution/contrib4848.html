Here is an instant snack item from the leftovers

Chapati Upama

Ingredients

Leftover chapatis
Onion 1 medium
Green chillies finely chopped
Salt to taste
Hing
Mustard Seeds
Urad Daal 1/2 teaspoon
Chana Daal 1/2 teaspoon
Jeera 1/2 teaspoon
Pinch of Sugar
Corriander leaves
Oil for cooking

Method:

  1. Cut the onions and the green chilliesinto fine pieces.
  2. Make small pieces of the chapatis (You can either do it by hand or put in in a grater) It should not be powdered. It should be in pieces.
  3. Heat oil in a pan
  4. Add mustard seeds and let it crack.
  5. Now add the chana dal, urad dal , jeera.
  6. Add the onions and chillies and saute.
  7. When it turns golden brown, add the chapati pieces to it.
  8. Also add sugar and salt for taste.
  9. Cook for 2 minutes & garnish with corriander leaves
  10. Serve hot
Suggestions
a) Add less salt and use very little oil for cooking.
b) No side dish needed.
