Firni

Ingredients

1 liter Milk
1/2 cup of Sugar
1/2 cup of Rice
Essence-Vanilla or Rose
Dry fruits-Pista, Cashews, Badams

Method:

  1. Soak the rice for 2 hrs
  2. Dry the rice completely
  3. Grind the rice to fine powder
  4. Boil the milk, on high flame, see that no foam formation is there by continuous stirring.
  5. Add the sugar, essence and rice powder.
  6. Now on low flame, allow it to cook for 15 minutes till thickening.
  7. Allow it to cool
  8. Garnish with the dry fruits
  9. Chill for 2 hrs before serving.

Serve hot or cold as desired.

Preparation time : 25 minutes.
