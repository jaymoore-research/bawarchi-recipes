Aloo Bhath

Ingredients

Basmati Rice 2 cups
Potato 3 to4
Onion 2 big size
Salt According to taste

For vaghere

Ghee 2 tsp
Cumin Seeds 1 tsp
Turmeric powder 1 tsp

For Masala

Mint Leaves 1 Bunch
Green chilles 4 to 5
Garlic 1-2 cloves
Ginger Small Piece.
Scraped Wet coconut 1/2 cup.

Method

  1. Firstly keep the rice to be cooked.
  2. Cut Potatoes little big and onion very finely.
  3. Take a kadai and in it put ghee. When the ghee is hot a put Cumin seeds in it. Fry it for some time and the add Turmeric powder to it.
  4. Then add to it onion pieces and fry it till it becomes transparent. Then add to it potatoes and salt and let it cook.
  5. Cut green chillies, garlic, ginger and mint leaves. Also add scraped coconut to it and grind it. Then add this paste to the potaoto which is being cooked.
  6. After potato is cooked and rice is also cooked, add the potato gravy to the rice and mix it slowly.
  7. Serve it when it is hot.
