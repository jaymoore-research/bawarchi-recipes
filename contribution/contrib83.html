Nankathai

Ingredients

1 cup shortening
1 cup powdered sugar
2 cups all purpose flour
Pinch of nutmeg powder
1/4 tsp cardamom powder
5-6 tblsp of pista powder (optional)

Method

  1. Mix the shortening and powdered sugar
  2. Gently add the flour, cardamom, nutmeg, and pista powder
  3. Make into small balls
  4. Keep on a baking sheet 2" apart and bake at 300 degrees for about 20-30 minutes or till done.
