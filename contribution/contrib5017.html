One more recipe from Shantha Radhakrishnan, Madurai, South India for you to have a wonderful side dish for Chapathi, Puri or Naan.

Email: eearkay@yahoo.com

Malai Gobi

Ingredients

1. Cauli Flower -- cut into small bits ... 1 cup
2. Tomatoes -- made into a paste ... 2 nos
3. Small onions made into a paste ... 1 cup
4. Salt ... to taste
5. Ginger ... 1/2" piece
6. Garlic ... 3 pearls
7. Green chillies - medium sized ... 3 nos.
8. Milk powder ... 1/2 cup (100 gms)
9. Chilli powder ... 1 tsp
10. Turmeric powder ... 1 tsp
11, Garam masala powder ... 1 tsp.
12 Oil for frying ... 1 TBSP

Procedure:

  1. Make a fine paste of ginger, garlic and green chillies.
  2. Keep oil in a frying pan, and after it becomes hot, add the ginger, garlic - green chilli paste and saute till the raw smell goes off.
  3. Add the onion paste and saute till golden brown
  4. Add the tomatoes paste (roughly 2 tablespoonfuls), 1 tsp chilli powder, 1/4 tsp turmeric powder, 1 tsp garam masala and the required quantity of salt and saute for 2 minutes.
  5. Make a thin paste of the milk powder with a little water and add this to the mixture (above) and saute for 5 minutes.
  6. Add the cut cauliflower, add very little water, close it with a lid and cook for another 5 minutes.
