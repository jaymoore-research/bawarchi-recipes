Fried Chicken in Curry Sauce

Ingredients

1/2 cup flour
1/2 tsp salt and pepper
3 lbs. frying chicken cut up
1/2 cup oil

Method

  1. In plastic bag, combine flour, 1/2 tsp salt and pepper, shake to mix. Add chicken with mixture.
  2. Heat oil in large wok. Brown chicken in hot oil, turning to brown all sides; drain.

Ingredients

For gravy:
2 Tbsp oil
1/2 Cup Onion
4 garlic clove, minced
2 tsp cumin powder or curry powder
3 tsp chilli pepper
1 med bellpepper, chopped
2 Tbsp coriander leaves (cilantro)
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup tomato sauce

Method

  1. Add remaining ingredients. Bring to boil for 30 to 45 minutes or until chicken is tender.
  2. Serve with hot rice and lovely smile.
