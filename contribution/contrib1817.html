Yumyum Noodles

Ingredients

1 pack of Chicken Noodles (cooked as per cover instructions)
Prawns 25-30 pieces
Egg 1
Ajinomoto 1 tsp
Soya Sauce 2 tbsp
Tomato Sauce 2 tbsp
Chilli Sauce 1.5 tbsp
A spring of Curry Leaves
Ginger-Garlic paste 1 tbsp.

Method :

  1. Add salt as required to the beaten egg and make scrambled with some oil. Keep aside.
  2. Take some oil and deep fry prawns. Keep aside.
  3. In the same oil put ginger-garlic paste and curry leaves, stir for a minute.
  4. Add ajinomoto, soya sauce, tomato sauce, chilli sauce and mix well (all this to be done on a low flame)
  5. Now add the scrambled egg, fried prawns and mix well. Keep stirring till everything is evenly coated.
  6. Add the boiled noodles and mix well.
  7. Remove from fire and garnish with coriander leaves.
