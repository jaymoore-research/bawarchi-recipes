Puddi Sagle (Stuffed Brinjal Masala)

Ingredients

10 Brinjals (small variety)
4 Big potatoes
2 Drum-sticks (optional)
1 cup coconut gratings
10 roasted red chillies
2 tsp corriander seeds
1 tsp black gram dal (udad dal)
1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds (methi seeds)
Tamarind lump (marble sized)
Jaggery (marble sized)
4 tbsp oil
1 tsp mustard seeds
1 sprig curry leaves
Salt to taste

Method

  1. Remove stalks of brinjals.Cut 4 slits lengthwise from top.Cut potatoes lengthwise into 4 pieces. Cut drumsticks into 1" pieces.
  2. Wash all vegetables and keep aside. Roast corriander seeds, udad dal and fenugreek seeds in 1/2 tsp oil.
  3. Grind coconut gratings, roasted red chilles, tamarind, jagerry and salt to a thick masala (adding a little water).
  4. Add the roasted seeds and grind for some more time.
  5. The masala should be dry and coarse. Stuff this masala into the brinjals through the slits.
  6. Spread the excess masala over the vegetables. Keep aside the water used for washing the grinder.
  7. In a thick bottom pan, prepare seasoning with mustard and curry leaves in oil.
  8. Drop all the vegetables along with the water kept aside into the seasoning. Turn over. Cook. Open lid 2-3 times and turn over gentely to prevent sticking into the bottom.
  9. When vegetables are done remove from flame. Goes very well with Manglorean dal and rice.
