Tandoori Baby Corn

Ingredients

250 g. baby corn
100g. curd
1tsp.(5g,) ginger-garlic paste
1tsp. Red chilli paste
1/2tsp.(2g.)garam masala powder
a pinch of cumin powder
a pinch of cardamom powder
chilli powder to taste
a pinch of black salt
salt to taste
20ml. Mustard oil

Method:

  1. Tie the curd in a muslin cloth and hang it till all the water drains out.
  2. Put the strained curd in a bowl.
  3. Add red chilli paste, ginger-garlic paste, garam masala powder, mustard oil, black salt, salt, cumin powder, cardamom powder and chilli powder, mix well.
  4. Marinate the baby corn well in this masala paste. Grill the marinate baby corn in a hot tandoor till done.
