Quick Dal Dosa

Ingredients

Green Moong dal 1 cup
Udad dal 1/2 cup
Chana dal 1/2 cup
Yellow Moong dal 1/4 cup
White Flour or Maida [optional] 1 cup
Rice Flour [optional] 1 cup
Salt 1 tsp
Green Chillies [optional] 3
Onion 2 medium
Cilantra [coriander] 1 bunch
Carrots 1

Method:

  1. Soak all the dals for half an hour and then grind it.
  2. After grinding mix the flour and all the remaining ingredients. Your batter is ready to make instant colourful dosa.
  3. Enjoy with chutney or chutney podi or plain yougurt.
