Quick Kalakand

Ingredients

1. 8 oz Ricotta Cheese
2. 1 can of Condensed milk.
3. Chopped nuts (Almonds, Pista + ..)
4. Saffron

Method:

  1. Open Condensed milk and Ricotta Cheese.
  2. Mix them together in a vessel (preferrable non stick) and set on Cooking stove at medium heat.
  3. Stirr it in between.
  4. Add some saffron, chopped nuts. It will all begin to curdle and thicken. Constantly stirr. Once it is pretty thick, take it out on a flat plate and set it.
  5. Garnish with cardamom powder. After it cools down, cut into pieces and serve.
