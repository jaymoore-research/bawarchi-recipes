Watercress Desi Style

Ingredients

Watercress -2 bunches
Medium sized Potato-1
Tomato-2
Onion-1
Garlic Flakes-4
Turmeric powder-2tsp
Garam masal-2tsp
Method

  1. Wash watercress, trim stems, peel and cut potato lengthwise.
  2. Saute onion , tomato and garlic paste.
  3. Add turmeric, salt, red pepper powder, garam masala, watercress leaves, potato.
  4. Add 1/4 cup water and cook in pressure cooker for 10 minutes on medium flame.
  5. Serve hot with roti.
