Cheese Cutlet

Ingredients

Milk -- 3 cups (Fat free to whole milk, Anything is fine)
Cheese -- 1 cup or less
Spinach (Pre cooked) or Coriander Bunch -- 1 cup
Fine Rava (Suji) -- 2 1/2 cups
Salt - as needed
Chilly Power -- 1 1/2 tsp
Greenchillies Chopped -- 3 small
Butter -- 4 tsp to 6tsp
Bread Crumbs - 1/2 cup
Oil needed In case of deep frying

Preparation:

  1. Boil the Milk in a vessel.when it starts to boil, simmer the stove & add Cheese & butter little by little. Start stiring when the cheese starts melting.
  2. After is melts add rava little by little while stiring the Milk.
  3. After adding all simmer the stove and start stiring with out forming any clums in the rava.
  4. When it becomes like a dough. Spread in a tray which is greased with little oil or ghee. Spread evenly and cut in to small square or round as desired with a knife.
  5. Then freeze it in the Refridgerator for 15 to 20 min. Then, take it out split the cubes separately, Dip it in a maida paste (Little maida and water), spread it in bread crumbs, deep fry or Bake.
  6. Nice evening Munch!! or Nice Appetizer for party.
  7. You can store the cubes in freezer for 4 to 5 days and deep fry when ever you want.

Easy to make, Goes with any sause!!
Note: You can add any green vegetables, Here I recomended spinach or coriander. To give a little flavor to spinach add some garlic powder & onion powder for seasoning. Coriander has it's own flavor.
