Mumura (Puri) Upama

Ingredients

Mumura - 200gms
Carrot, Beans, Cabbage, Peas - 1 Cup each
Green Chilli - 8 N0s (you may add more to your taste)
Onion Chopped - 1 Cup
Lemon Juice - 2 spoons
Grated fresh Coconut - 1 Cup
Urad dall - 1/2 spoon
Mustered seeds
Jeera - 1 spoon
Coriander & Curry leaves - Fine Chopped
Salt to taste
Oil - 5 to 6 spoon
Turmeric - 1/2 spoon

Method:

  1. Place the medium size pan on the stove, heat up the oil with Mustered seeds after it splitters add Urad dall, Green chilli, Curry leaves, Jeera, Turmeric powder, Onion wait for 2 minutes, add all cut vegetables, fry for 10 minutes.
  2. Wash Mumura in fresh water, repeat wash once or twice, drain the water and add to the above mixture, add Lemon, Salt, Coriander leaves, mix thoroughly and wait for 5 minutes. Upama is ready.

Serve hot at any time of the day.
