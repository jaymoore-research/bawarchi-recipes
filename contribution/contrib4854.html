Quesadilla

Ingredients

4 Plain tortilla or maida roti
1/2 cup Cheese Mozzarella
Butter
1 Onion
1 Tomato

Method

  1. Spread little bitof butter on pan place a tortilla and when it becomes brown on both side it should be soft and not too crispy.
  2. Than put cheese on half side on tortilla and fold it and when cheese starts melting than turn over folded tortilla.
  3. You can put onion , tomato , beans inside while cooking.
  4. Eat with salsa

Salsa Recp
2 green tomato
1/4 onion
1 flake of garlic
salt
1 green chili
few leaves of corriander
1/4 of avocado
Mix it in mixture
