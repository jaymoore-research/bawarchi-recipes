Palak-Paneer Kofta

Ingredients

Frozen or fresh chopped spinach 10oz
Home made paneer 50 gm or
American Singles cheese 2-3 slices
Besan 5-6 tbs
Salt
Amchur 1 tsp
Chilli Powder 1 tbs
Tomatoes 2 medium size
Turmeric Powder
Corriander Powder
Garam masala Powder
Green chillies & Ginger paste 1tbs
One onion in the form of paste
Sour cream 3 tbs
Green corriander leaves to garnish
Oil to deep fry the Koftas & for making curry

Method

  1. Mix paneer, amchoor, 1/2 tsp chilli powder,salt ,besan & spinach in a big bawl (if spinach is frozen then take it out from the freezer and put it in the fridge overnight, to defrost it).
  2. Heat oil in a karahi (high flame) & put spinach mix in the shape of small balls in it.
  3. Now flame should be medium. Deep fry all the Koftas and keep it aside.
  4. In a separate pan heat 2 tbs of oil and put ginger-garlic paste, onion paste,tomatoes, dry masalas powder & fry for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Then put 3 tbs of sour cream & fry little more. Put some water let it boil.
  6. Now put Koftas in it and after 2 minutes switch off the flame.
  7. Garnish it with corriander leaves.
  8. This is a very easy, delicious, beautiful,and royal dish.
  9. For convenience you can make Koftas and put them in the freezer and use them any time when needed.
