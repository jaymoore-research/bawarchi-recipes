Chicken Hariyali Fry

Ingredients

1 1/2 lbs of chicken, cut into small pieces
1/2 bunch of corainder leaves
1/2 bunch of mint leaves
1 tsp chilli powder
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1 tsp cumin powder
1 large onion, sliced finely

For paste (grind it)

1 whole garlic clove, medium size
1" piece ginger
3-4 green chillies
1 cinnamon stick
2-3 cloves
1-2 cardamom pods
2 tsp lemon juice
Salt to taste
Oil or Ghee for frying

Method

  1. Rub the salt, turmeric, chilli and cumin on the chicken pieces and keep covered for 20 minutes.
  2. Heat the oil for frying and add the onions.When the onions are well browned, add the chicken pieces and fry by alternating between high and medium heat till the chicken is well done.
  3. Add the ground paste and fry till all the water evaporates and the mixture is dark green.
  4. Add the lemon juice and heat for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Adjust the salt, if needed.
  6. Remove from the heat and drain the extra oil.
  7. Serve hot with rice and dal.
You can also add some Garam masala just before adding the lemon juice, to vary the taste. Enjoy your meal!
