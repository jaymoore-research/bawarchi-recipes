Dahi Maans (Mutton)

Ingredients

2 lbs. Mutton cut into pieces
1 tbsp. Ginger-Garlic paste
4 nos. Medium sized potatoes
4 nos. Medium sized onions chopped fine
3 nos. Bay leaves
2 inch Cinnamon stick
8 nos. Cloves
1 cup Plain yoghurt
2 tsp. Red chilli powder
1 tsp. turmeric powder
1 tsp. Garam masala
Salt to taste
Oil for cooking

Method :

  1. Marinate mutton pieces with ginger-garlic paste for 10 minutes.
  2. Pressure cook mutton and set aside.
  3. Remove the skin from potatoes and cut them into long wedges.
  4. Add oil to a vessel and fry potato wedges and set aside.
  5. To oil add bay leaves, cinnamon and cloves.
  6. Add onions and fry till golden brown.
  7. Add red chilli powder, turmeric powder, yoghurt and salt to taste.
  8. Next add cooked mutton pieces, garam masala and mix well.
  9. Then add potato wedges and mix well and cook on slow flame for a while.

Serve hot with chapatis.
