Beetroot Raita

Ingredients

Beetroot grated - 2 cups
Mustard seeds - 1 tsp
Curry leaf - 1 stick
Red chilli whole - 4 nos.(or add according to spice)
Onion chopped - 1 small
Oil - 1 tbsp
Salt to taste
Curd - 2 cups

Method

  1. Heat oil add mustard seeds let it crackle add curry leaf, red chilliĀand onion fry till onion is golden brown.
  2. Add beetroot and salt and cook until 3/4th done.
  3. Let it cool.
  4. Add curd and stir just before serving.

Optional: Can add fried corn flakes (used in mixture) as dressing. Sprinkle on top.
