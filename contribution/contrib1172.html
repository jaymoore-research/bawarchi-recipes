Moolangi Curry

Ingredients

1. 1 White mooli (White radhish or moolangi)
2.1 big onion,
3. 2 small tomatoes,
4.Small bunch of curry leaves,
5.1 cup turdal,
6.1 tbsp jeera,
7.1 small piece of finely choped ginger.
8.1 1/2 tbsp Sambhar powder(I used Ashok sambhar powder),
9.1 tbsp Red chilli powder,
10.1 tbsp tamarind paste soaked in water,
11.1/2 cup of green peas,
12. a pinch of asfoetida
13. Salt to taste.

Method:

  1. Cook toordal in the pressure cooker till it can be mashed (3 whistles).
  2. Heat the oil and put jeera and mustard seeds and asfoetida.
  3. When they start spluttering put the finely cut onions and saute them.
  4. Wash curry leaves cut them into small pieces and put them along with the onions. Then put tomatoes and finely grated mooli and very small pieces of ginger. Mix them with the spoon and keep on the gas for 5 minutes till the mooli becomes soft. Put the peas. Put the sambhar powder, red chilli powder and salt.
  5. Then add the the tamarind water and put the dal and boil it for 15 minutes constantly stirring it .

You Sambhar will be ready!.Serve it hot with Dosa or idlis.
