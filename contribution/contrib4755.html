KhasKhas Payasam

Ingredients

Rice - 1 tbsp
Coconut -1/2 cup scrapped
Khaskhas- 2 tbsp
Milk - 1 cup
Cardamom powdered - little
Coconut cut into small pieces- 1 teaspoon
Jaggery - 3/4 cup powdered
Little Ghee

Method:

  1. Soak rice, khuskhus separately in hot water for 10 minutes.
  2. Gind the above said with coconut scrapped.
  3. In a heavy bottom pan take little water and put the grounded paste and mix well thoroughly without any lumps.
  4. In another pan take the jaggery add little water and keep stirring until it dissolves. Now filter the jaggery to avoid mud and again transfer it to the pan. Once it starts boiling add the khuskhus mixture to it. After everything blends together switch off. Add boiled milk to the above.
  5. Season with cardamom powder and coconut pieces fried in little ghee.
