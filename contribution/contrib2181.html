Raw Banana Thoran

Ingredients

Raw Banana -3
Turmeric powder-1teaspoon
Salt-to taste
Oil-3 teaspoon
Mustard seed-1/2 teaspoon
Curry leaves-5 to 6
Red Chilly powder-1/2 tsp
Cumin seeds-1/2 tsp
Garlic-2 pods

Method

  1. Peel the raw banana and cut it into very small square pieces. Soak it in 3 cups of water with 1/2 tsp turmeric powder for 5 minutes.
  2. Grind the coconut with cumin seeds, garlic and chilly powder coarsely. Keep it aside.
  3. Pour 3 tsp oil in a hot griddle, put mustard seeds and allow it to splutter, put the curryleaves.
  4. Now drain the water from the banana pieces put the banana pieces also in the same griddle and put the remaining turmeric powder and the salt in it, keep on stirring continuously for 5 to 10 min.
  5. Now put the coconut mixture which we grind already with other things in the fried banana, keep on stirring till the water evaporates. Take away from flame.

Now it is ready to serve. It will go along with plain Rice.
