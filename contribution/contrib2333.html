Tomato Chutney

Ingredients

2 big Tomatoes
1 tbsp Channa Daal
1/2 tbsp Urad Daal
1/2 tbsp Mustard
1/2 tea spoon Methi seeds
Lemon size Tamarind
Pinch of Hing
4 Red Chillies
4 Green Chillies
4 tbsp of Oil
Salt to taste

Method :

  1. Fry channa daal, urad daal, mustard, methi seeds, red chillies in 2 tbsp of oil. When the mustard splutters add the tamarind, hing and green chillies. Let it cool.
  2. Cut the tomatoes into small cubes.
  3. In a blender first grind the seasoning mentioned in (1) and then add the cubed tomatoes.
  4. Add salt to taste.
  5. Grind until consistency is smooth.

This can be eaten with Chappatis, Rice or even Dosais and Idlis.
