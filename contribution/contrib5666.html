Uyindu Koyakattai

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients

Urad Dal- 1 Cup ( soaked in water for 1/2 hr)
Rice- 1/2 Cup( Soaked for an Hour)
Oil- To Season
Red Chilli Whole: 1-2
Green Chillies : 2-3
Grated Coconut
Mustard Seeds
Asafoetida

Method

For making the Stuffing:

  1. Grind Dal with green chillies to make a consistence mixture. DO NOT OVERGRIND. It should not be a smooth batter.
  2. Add salt and cook on steam for 10 minutes on idli plate
  3. Heat the kadai, add oil, whole red chillies, asafoetida, grated coconut and mustard seeds.
  4. Allow to splatter and then add the steamed urad mixture, mix well.

For making the Cover:

  1. Grind the soaked rice to form a smoothĀ batter.
  2. AddĀ sufficient water to make it loose and cook it all the water vapurises and we get a dough which can be rolled into balls .
  3. Take a medium sized lump of dough, and spread it by hand into a cup form.
  4. Fill two spoons of stuffings and close the kuyakattai.
  5. When all are stuffed, cook them and steam for 15 minutes.

This is a very delicious recipe and is prepared for Ganesh Chaturthi along with the sweet poornam koyakkattai.
