Katthirikai Thugayal(Baingan Chutney)

Ingredients

Mustard Seed 2 Teaspoon
Urad dal Seed 3 Teaspoon
Red Chillies 4 (According to your taste)
Tamarind Take Lemon size tamarind, boil it in the water and make thick paste.
Salt to taste
Vegetable oil 2 tb spoon
Big Baingan (Egg Plant) 1
Hing powder (Assafoetida powder) 3 pinch
Curry leaves
Coriander leaves to garnish

Method :

  1. Heat oil in kadai and add mustard seed, urad dal. Let it splutter.
  2. Add Red chillies in the above.
  3. Bake the Baingan (Eggplant or Katthirikai) in oven for 45 minutes at 400 degrees.
  4. Turn each side of the baingan while baking.
  5. Let it cool. Keep it in the cool water for 2 minutes.
  6. Remove the outer layer of the baked baingan.
  7. Mash it with hand and grind it in the mixi along with the above mentioned seeds and Tamarind paste.
  8. Add salt to taste.
  9. Add hing powder, Curry leaves.
  10. Garnish it with Coriander leaves.

Happy cooking ! Enjoy !
