Raddish Puttu

Ingredients

1. Chopped Radish .... 1
2. Fried gram (Pottukadalai) powder ... 1 cup
3. Onion - chopped .... 1 cup
4. Curry leaves .... a little
5. Asafoetida .... 1 pinch
6. Chilli powder .... 3 tsp
7. Mustard .... 1 tsp
8. Urad dhal .... 1 tsp
9. Salt ....to taste
10. Oil ....to fry

Method:

  1. Dry fry radish (chopped) and keep it aside.
  2. Add oil in a saucepan and season mustard -- after it splutters, add Urad dhal, after it turns golden brown, add chopped onion and fry then add the fried radish to it. Then add asafoetida, chilli powder, salt to taste.
  3. Add a little water and cook it. After radish is cooked (boiled), add fried gram powder, curry leaves -- cover with a lid and allow it to be cooked for another 2 minutes -- Take and serve hot.
