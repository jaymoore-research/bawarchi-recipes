Muglai Paneer

Ingredients

1tsp khus khus
1tsp watermelon seeds
1 cup beaten curd
1 large onion grated
1/2 tsp garam masala
1/2 tsp red chill pd
salt to taste
1 cup cream
250 gms paneer
Few corainder leaves for decoration

Methord:-

  1. Soak khus-khus and watermelon seeds in water and grind it to a fine paste.
  2. Heat 11/2 tbsp ghee and cook the grated onion till transparent.
  3. Add ground paste cook for 2 mins. Gradually add curd. Cook for 5-7 mins till it turns golden brown and leaves oil. Add all the masala, cream and again cook for 2-3 mins. Add paneer and 1 cup water and let it cook till the paneer softens and the gravy thickens. Add corainder leaves and elaichi powder before serving.

Serve with Parathas.
