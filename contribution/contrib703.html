Tomato Gol Bajji

Ingredients

Sufficient oil to fry bajjis
1 cup besan (Bengalgram flour) powder
4 - 5 small tomatoes (bigger than cherry tomatoes)
1/2 tbsp cumin seeds (optional)
Pinch of baking soda
Salt

Filling Ingredients:
1 onion sliced very thin
1/4 cup roasted peanuts
1 tbsp Dhania (Coriander) Powder
1/2 tbsp Jeera (Cumin) Powder
3 tbsp lime juice
1/4 bunch of sliced coriander leaves
Salt to taste

Filling preparation:

  • Mix all the filling ingredients in a bowl and keep them aside.

Bajji Preparation:

  1. Mix 1 cup of besan powder, very little salt, pinch of baking soda, 1/2 tbsp of cumin seeds (optional) with water.
  2. Add water until the mix becomes smooth paste (suitable for bajji preparation).
  3. Dip the tomatoes into the besan paste and deep fry them till golden brown.
  4. Keep them aside and let them cool for about 5 min.
  5. Cut these tomatoes into halves and scoop out the seeds/pulp (not the skin) carefully from the middle of the fried tomato half.
  6. Add this pulp to the filling we prepared earlier and mix them well.
  7. Now stuff the tomato shell with this filling and serve.

Enjoy !!!! Have fun!!
