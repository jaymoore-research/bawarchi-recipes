Saffron Cracked Wheat Pudding

Ingredients

Cracked Wheat -2 tbsp
Cold Milk-6 cups
Hot Milk- 2 tbspn
Saffron Strands- 1/2tsp
Ghee--2tbsp
Thick Cream--2 tbsp
Sugar-- 6-8-tbsp (if desired can be added more or less)
Almonds--8-10 toasted flakes , Raisins-15-nos
Salt--1/2 tsp
Water--1/2 cup

Method:

  1. Soak the saffron strands in the hot milk.
  2. Take a pan heat ghee and fry the cracked wheat in it for 2 mins, then add water and cook it for 5 mins. Mash it a paste
  3. Start heating the milk on medium heat reduce it to half, now add the wheat paste to it keep on stir until it is thick, then add cream, saffron milk cook for 1 min.
  4. Then turn of the heat add sugar & salt to it and mix well.
  5. Spoon into serving dish, sprinkle with almonds & raisins to it.
