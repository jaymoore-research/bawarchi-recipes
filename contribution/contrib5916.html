Brocolli Sabji

Ingredients

Brocolli 2-3 small bunches
1 medium sized onion
1Āmedium sized tomatoes
1 cup peas
salt to taste
mustard seeds
1 tsp jeera
1 tsp garam masala powder
red chillie powder according to taste
1 tsp coriander powder

Method

  1. Chop onions and tomatoes finely
  2. Chop brocolli finely
  3. In a pan, heat oil and add rai, jeera to it
  4. Now saute onions till pink and add tomatoes to it
  5. Add the masalas and stir till the water is absorbed from the tomatoes
  6. Now add peas and cook it for 2 mins
  7. Now add brocolli and salt and cook it for 10 to 12 mins by adding little water Ā

This is a very tasty dish and can be prepared in 15 mins( excluding the time required for chopping the vegetables)
Can be had with rotis as well as rice!
