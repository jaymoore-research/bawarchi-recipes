Ingredients

Long grain rice - 1 cup
Tender brinjals - 1/4 kilo
Green chillies - 3-4
Small onions -1/2 cup peeled (frozen onions will do)
Tomatoes - 3-4
Oil - 3 tbls sps.
Salt - as reqiured.
Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp
Curry leaves - A few

Grind:
Red chillies - 6
Cloves - 2
Cinnamon - 1/2 inch
Garlic - 8 flakes
Ginger - 1/2 inch

Seasoning:
Mustard seeds - 1 tsp
Black gram dhal - 1 tsp
Bengal gram dhal - 1 tsp

Method:

  1. Cook rice and spread over a broad basin to cool.
  2. Dice brinjals into small pieces and soak in salted water till it is ready to use.
  3. Slit green chillies into two.
  4. Grind the masala together into a fine paste.
  5. Cut tomatoes into fine pieces.
  6. Heat oil, add the seasonings with curry leaves, then fry green chillies and small onions for a few minutes.
  7. Add dices brinjals, tomatoes, ground masal paste, salt, turmeric powder and little water.
  8. Close the pan and cook on medium heat till brinjals become tender and the moisture has absorbed completely.
  9. Stir in between to prevent burning, now add cooked cooled rice and fry for a few minutes.
  10. Serve hot, garnish with finely chopped cilantro.
