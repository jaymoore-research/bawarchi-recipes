Gujarati Muthia

Ingredients

3 cup Wheat Flour
1/4 cup Rava flour
1/4 TSP ENO or 1/4 TSP Soda bi carb
1/2 cup shreaded Duthi (Squash) or Cabbage (Optional)
1/2 TSP shreaded Ginger
1 TSP Shreaded Hot Green Pepper (jalapeno)
1/4 TSP Garlic powder
1/4 TSP Sugar
3/4 glass Water

For Vaghar:

1/2 cup Vegetable oil
3 TSP Sesame seeds
3 TSP Mustard seeds

Method:

  1. Mix all ingredients and make chapti style dough, using water.
  2. From dough make round 6" long dough sticks (look like thin base ball sticks)
  3. In cooker, add water and warm up on stove.
  4. Put round dove sticks, in round container that can fit in cooker. You may need 3 to 4 such cooker containers.
  5. Put these containers in cooker, above 1/2 steel ring so water don't get in to steel contaiers filled with round dough sticks of muthia.
  6. Cover the cooker with lid and wait for 2 cooker whistles.
  7. Once, cool, get muthia sticks out and cutr in 1/2" thick round pieces.
  8. You can pour "Vaghar" over the cut muthias with warming up 1/2 cup oil and adding 3 TSP mustard seeds (Rai) and 3 TSP Sesame seeds (Til) to warm oil.

You are ready to eat as a snack in the morning or we eat it as a Sunday Lunch with Tea, Milk or Mango Pickles.
