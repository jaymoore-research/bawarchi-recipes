Tomato-Onion Curry

Ingredients

Onion 4 (long cut)
Tomato 3 (cubes)
Oil 4-5 table spoon
Salt To taste
Sugar 1 table spoon
Turmeric powder a pinch
Mustard seeds 1/4 table spoon
Corriander leaves To garnish

To roast and powder

Red chillies 4
Corriander seeds 2 table spoon
Black pepper 1/4 table spoon
Urad daal 1 table spoon
Channa daal 1 table spoon
Jeera 1 table spoon

Method:

  1. Heat oil and add mustard seeds.
  2. Add the onion after the mustard seeds splutters.
  3. Fry until the onions are golden brown.
  4. Add tomatoes and fry for 5 minutes.
  5. Add a pinch of turmeric powder, salt (as required), sugar, powdered masala and cook until raw smell is gone.
  6. Arrange on a serving dish and garnish with finely chopped corriander leaves.

This curry goes well with Chapathis, Puris and Naans.
