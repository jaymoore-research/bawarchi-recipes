Ingredients

1 pack TOFU/ optional Paneer (firm or extra firm you get it from American grocery stores)
2 onions (finely chopped)
2 Green Chillies ( chopped or cut lengthwise)
1 big Tomato (chopped finely)
1 Capsicum (chop into small cubes)
1/2 tsp Turmeric
1/2 tsp Red Chilli Powder or Paprika
1/2 tsp garam masala
Salt - to taste
2 tbsp oil
cilantro (finely chopped)

Method:

  1. Heat oil in a kadahi or a nonstick pan (with some depth).
  2. Add finely chopped onions and green chillies and saute them till onion is pink.
  3. Add tomatoes, the spices, salt and cook till the masala is well done.
  4. Add capsicum (green pepper) and cook for 1-2 mins in the masala.
  5. Crumble the TOFU into tiny parts and add to the masala.
  6. Cover and cook for 5 mins. and then add little chopped cilantro mix it and cook for another 5 mins.

Serve it in a serving bowl garnished with chopped cilantro.

Serve it with nans/parathas/rotis or bread.

Note:

You can also add a little of red capsicum along with green capsicum.
