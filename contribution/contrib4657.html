Apple Pickle

Ingredients

Big Sour Apples - 5 nos
Red chilli powder - 200 gms
Salt to taste
Mustard seeds - 1 tsp
Gingilly Oil or any Vegetable oil - 150 gms
Methi seeds - 1/2 tsp
Asafoetida - 1/2 tsp or as per taste
Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp

Method:

  1. Fry methi seeds without oil in a kadai and powder fine.
  2. Peel off apple skin, and grate to fine.
  3. Place kadai on heat, add gingilly oil, let it become hot; add mustard seeds allow to splutter.
  4. Now add the grated apple pulp, and saute in oil let it loose water completely. Add salt, chilli powder and turmeric powder mix well; allow it to form a thick paste, keep heating for 10 - 15 mins.
  5. Add asafoetida mix well for 3- 5 mins. Remove from heat. This can remain fresh for more than a month.
  6. This is a wonderful side dish for curd rice, dosa, idly, chappati etc.

Total time taken: 45 minutes max.
