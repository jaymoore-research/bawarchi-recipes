Bread Bhaji

Ingredients

5 loaves of Bread
Chana powder
Garam Masala Powder
Pav Bhaji Masala Powder
Chilli Powder
Salt
oil

Method

  1. Put the Chana Powder in a basin. Add the rest of the powders with the chana powder.
  2. Add water to it and salt and make a semi-thick paste.
  3. Cut the bread into 4 pieces
  4. Dip the pieces in the masala paste and fry till light brown.
  5. Bread Bhaji is ready to eat.
