Naroo

Ingredients

1 fresh Coconut grated (powered) as for ladoo.
1 cup Sugar
1 small Cardamom

Method:

  1. Heat a pan or kadai, Put all the ingredients together, and keep on frying till sugar and Coconut are mixed well and are light brown in colour and sticking to your finger.
  2. Remove from heat. Let it cool for a minute and make lemon size ball with hand.
  3. Don't cool it too much other wise the NAROO won't be made.

You can keep it for 10-15 days. Happy cooking.
