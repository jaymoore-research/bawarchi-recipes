Kancheepuram Idli

Ingredients

Take fermented idli batter and mix the following into it :

1. Pepper : 2 tbsp powdered
2. Jeera : 1 tbsp
3. Aesofatida : 1/2 tsp
4. Ginger : 1" grated
5. Urud dhal : 2 tsp
6. Bengal gram : 2 tsp
7. G.chillies : 8 (finely chopped)
8. Oil : 100 grams

Method :

  1. Pour water into the idli vessel and let the water boil.
  2. Place the plate without the idli cloth.
  3. Take small steel cups and pour 1 tsp ghee or oil into it and pour the prepared idli batter into the cup. Place the cups on the plate in the idli vessel. Steam the idli's for twenty minutes.

These idli's are best served with Ginger Kulambu.
