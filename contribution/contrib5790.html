Nutri Burrito

Ingredients

Tortilla - 4 in number and the large sized ones
Canned beans - 1 can
Chopped Onion - 1
A cup full mix veggies (Carrots, Beans, Cabbage, Bell pepper in all different colors,Pea, Potatoes, finely chopped cauliflower)
Mexican hot sauce - 1-2 tbsp
Salt and powdered red pepper
Mozzarella or cheddar cheese - 1/2 cup
Chopped lettuce, Tomato, Onion and Cucumber - 1 cup
Sour creme - 1/2 cup
Oil/Butter - 1-2 tbsp

Method

  1. First heat the tortillas on tawa or girdle one by one and keep aside.
  2. In a pan heat oil and fry the onions .
  3. Steam the mixed veggiesĀ so that they become soft.
  4. Add veggies and beans to the onion .
  5. Add salt, red pepper and Mexican hot sauce.
  6. Mix all the ingredients and let the mixture simmer on low heat till itĀ becomes a kind of dry.
  7. Spread sour creme on tortillas and in the center put the prepared mixture.
  8. Top it with cheese and lettuce , onion and tomato mixture.
  9. Fold tortillas carefully with mixture in the center and then seal both the other ends like you do in folding an envelope.
  10. Microwave for a minute or two on high power so that cheese melts.
  11. Serve it hot .
