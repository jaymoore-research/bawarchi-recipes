Ridge Gourd Chutney (Hirekai Chutney)

Ingredients

1 med. size Ridge gourd (The ridges may be removed and the gourd (?) cut into 1 inch pieces)
1 tbsp. Chana dal
1 tbsp.Urad dal 4 -5 green chillies
Ginger
Curry Leaves
Coriander leaves
Salt
Tamarind
Jaggery (Optional)
1 tbsp. of grated coconut
Oil

Method:

  1. Heat Oil add mustard seeds, after it splutters, add Chana dal and Urad Dal.
  2. Once they are done, add slit green chillies, ginger, both the leaves and the cut ridge gourd pieces.
  3. Fry till done and grind in the mixie with salt, tamarind and coconut till the required consistency.
  4. Can be served with rice, Chapatis, Idlis and Dosas.
