Peas Pulao

Ingredients

1 cup Basmathi Rice (wash and keep aside)
1 cup Peas
1 cup of Coriander leaves (finely chopped)
1 cup of Mint leaves (no need to chop)
4 Garlic pods
1 big Onion (cut into small pieces)
2 tsp Rasam powder
1 cup Milk
1 cup Water
1 tsp Jeera
Salt to taste
Coconut gratings (optional)
Lemon juice

Method :

  1. Put some oil in a pressure pan. When heated put some jeera into it.
  2. Then add the coriander, mint leaves and onion. Cook until the onion is half cooked.
  3. Now add the washed rice and heat for 2 minutes.
  4. Add the water and milk into this and finally add the rasam powder.
  5. Cook in pressure for 3 whistles.
  6. Before serving the pulao add the lemon juice on it and serve with some raitha.
