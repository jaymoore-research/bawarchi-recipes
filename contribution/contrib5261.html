Coconut Milk Rice

Ingredients

1 tsp Ginger,Garlic Paste
1 chopped Onion
2-3 Green Chilies (beaten in the mixer once) ie not as a paste
2 Tomatoes
10 Mint Leaves
2 cups Coconut Milk extract
2 tbsp Coriander Leaves
1 cup rice

Method:

  1. Soak rice for 15min. & drain it and fry it with few spoons of ghee.
  2. In the cooker pour oil or Ghee when hot put Cinnamon sticks, Cardamom, Cloves, Bay Leaves then put the ginger garlic paste & sauti it until the green smell goes then put the onion and green chilly mixture and sauti it then add the cut tomatoes then salt then mint leaves and mix for minutes.
  3. Then add the rice then coconut milk extract and keep it in pressure.
  4. For one cup of rice 2 cups of coconut milk.This goes well with fish fry,potato fry,onion salad & chicken fry.
