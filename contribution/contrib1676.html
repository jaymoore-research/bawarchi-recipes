Tortilla Chips Bhel

Ingredients

1. One packet unsalted Tortilla Chips
2. One finely chopped Onion
3. One bunch finely chopped Green Onion
4. Tamarind paste or extract (as per taste)
5. 2-3 chopped Green Chillies
6. 1 tsp. Cumin powder
7. One medium Potato (boiled, chopped)
8. Finely chopped Cilantro (coriander) leaves
9. 1/2 tsp. Dried Ginger Powder
10. 1/2 tsp. Chilli Powder
11. Sugar and Salt as per taste
12. 1/4 tsp. Chat Masala

Method :

  1. Crush tortilla chips into small pieces.
  2. Add together tamarind extract, cumin powder, ginger powder, salt, sugar and chilli powder. Make thin paste. (add water if required.)
  3. Add boiled potato pieces to tortilla chips.
  4. Add tamarind mixture, green chillies, and chopped onions. Mix well.
  5. Top with chopped cliantro and chat masala before serving. Enjoy.

Note:

You can adjust the ingredients as per your taste.
