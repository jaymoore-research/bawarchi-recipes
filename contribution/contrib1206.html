Coconut-Apple Chutney

Ingredients

1 cup fresh grated Coconut
3 medium, green Apples (a little sour)
1/4 cup Mint leaves
Juice of 1 Lemon (optional)
2 Green Chillies
Salt to taste

Method :

  1. Peel the apples and cut into small pieces.
  2. Grind the apples, coconut and the mint leaves into a paste in a mixer.
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients. The quantity of green chillies can be reduced.

You now have a delicious chutney that can be eaten with almost everything!
