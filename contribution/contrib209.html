Masoor & Tuvar Daal With Zuchinni

Ingredients

1 cup masoor daal
1 cup tuvar daal
2 small zuchinni (cut to small cubes)
2 onion (finely sliced)
6-7 garlic (finely sliced)
1 inch long ginger (finely sliced)
3-4 tomatoes (cut into big cubes)
A few curry leaves
5 green chillies (finely sliced)
4-5 tspns ghee
salt to taste

Garnish

1 tsp jeera (cumin seeds)
1 tsp rai (mustard seeds)
a few bunches cilantro (dhaniya leaves)
1/2 tsp chaat masala or a few drops of lemon juice

Method:

  1. Pressure cook the two daals with a little turmeric, salt, green chillies, few drops of ghee, ginger, curry leaves & garlic for about 20 minutes. Let it cool.
  2. In a frying pan add the ghee, add Jerra & Rai, when they start spluttering, add Onion & fry till its light brown in color.
  3. Add zuchinni & fry till it becomes soft.
  4. Add Tomatoes & continue frying.
  5. When you see the masala & the ghee separating, add the cooked daal. Add a little water if needed.
  6. Cook for about 5 minutes. Mix well.
  7. Garnish with cilantro & chaat masala/lemon juice.
  8. Serve hot with Chappati Or Rice.
