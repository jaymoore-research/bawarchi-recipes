Gasagase Payasa

Ingredients

KashKash or Gasagase - 5 tsp
Ghee - 1 tsp
Fresh grated coconut - 4 cups
Jaggery - 1 cup or according to your taste
Milk - 1/2 cup
Cardamom - 1/2 tsp

Method:

  1. Fry the kashkash to light brown color with ghee
  2. Grind fried kashkash, coconut.
  3. Mix jaggery with water and boil the mixture. Filter the boiled mixture if necessary. Take the juice of the ground mixture and mix both mixtures and heat it until it boils.
  4. Add milk and cardamom powder to the boiling mixture and stir it well. Your gasagase payasa is ready to serve or drink.
