Omelette

Ingredients

Small Egg - 1 no
Onion - Small size 1 no. cut to small pieces
Tomato- Diced about 6 to 7 pieces. Preferably a fleshy one
Milk - 1 to 2 tablespoon
Green Chilli - 1 small cut to small pieces
Pepper 2 teaspoon
Salt to taste
Butter 2 table spoon
Mustard 1 teaspoon
Asafoetida 2 pinches
coriander leaves. 5 sprigs cut to small pieces

Method

  1. Heat 1 table spoon butter and add the Mustard. Then add the Chilli and quickly add the Onion. Fry till the onion just changes colour. Remove from pan and set it aside.
  2. After it cools off mix the tomato, salt, pepper, coriander and asafoetida into it.
  3. Beat the egg into this mixture. Add the milk to make the mixture just ease to flow (not too thick and not too thin).
  4. Heat pan with 1 table spoon butter till the butter starts to sizzle. Pour the batter nicely spreading the masala all around the pan. Shake the pan slightly till the omelette leaves the bottom.
  5. Now the top should be well done to have minimal liquid. Fold the omelette in half and keep gas at minimum for about 1 minute. The omelette is ready and can be transferred to a plate

Note : If you want you can also turn the omelette and keep on sim gas till its cooked.
