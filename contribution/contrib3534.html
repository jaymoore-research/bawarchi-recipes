Potli Chicken

Ingredients

4 Chicken Breast Pieces
4 Leaves of Spinach
1 cup Orange Juice
1 Tsp Cornstarch
1Tsp Sugar
Salt according to your taste
2 Tsp Lemon Juice
Grated cheese
2-3 Sticks of Cinnamon

Method:

  1. Firstly, take the spinach leaves and wash in water and change the side of leave and take it inside.
  2. Take the chicken breast and flat it in the way that the chicken is to be opened. Give cuts on the chicken and add salt and pepper power. Put a spinach leave over the chicken.
  3. Secondly, take grated cheese and butter and make dough and put it on the chicken, after that fold the chicken and wrap the chicken in a foil paper and make it a potli. Put a potli into boiling water and boil it into it for 12 mintues

Orange Sauce

Put sugar in a fry pan and caramelized it add orange juice into the fry pan. When you put orange juice into the caramelized sugar the sugar becomes freezed, but when you cooked it becomes melted after some time. To make the souce thicken add cornstarch. Than add salt and mix it and gave a flavor of Cinnamon. Pour lemon juice for sour flavour.
