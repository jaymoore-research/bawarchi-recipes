Chicken in butter & pepper

Ingredients

Chicken- 1/2 Kg
Pepper crushed - 6 tbsp or as per taste
Salt
Juice of one Lime
Butter - 50 gm
Oil - 50 gm Green Chillies slit- 4
Coriander leaves chopped - 1 cup
Ginger juliens-1 tbsp

Method

  1. Marinate chicken in lime juice, salt & 3 tbsp of pepper.
  2. Heat oil & butter together. Fry the chicken in it.
  3. Now mix the fried chicken along with rest of ingredients & return to the pan. Fry it again for few more min before serving.
