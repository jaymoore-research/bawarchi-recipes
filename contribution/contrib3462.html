Easy Vegeteable Soup

Ingredients

8 Tomatoes (small size)
1 Beet root (small size)
5-6 Spinach leaves
8 Baby Carrots
Little bit of Ginger (optional)
Coriander Leaves (optional)
Salt (accordng to taste)

Method:

  1. Wash all the vegetables and put in the pressure cooker with around 2 cups of water and salt. Give 3-4 whistles.
  2. Now grind the boiled vegetables in the grinder.
  3. Put it to boil. For extra flavour you can put in corainder leaves while boiling.
  4. Boil it for around 7-8 mins and It's Done.
