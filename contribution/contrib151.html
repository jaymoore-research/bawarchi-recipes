Vellayappam

Ingredients

Rice Flour-3cups
Coconut milk-1can
Dry Yeast-1tsp
Sugar-1tbs
Water-3cups
Salt to taste

Method

  1. Warm up 2tbs of water, add sugar &mix; in the yeast.In a mixing bowl ,mix together the flour&coconut; milk & make it into a paste.
  2. Make sure there are no lumps.
  3. Gradually add water & make a thin batter (like the consistency of Dosa batter).
  4. Add the dissolved Yeast,salt &mix; thoroghly.
  5. Allow the batter to ferment for 6-8 hrs.
  6. When the batter has risen it's ready to use.
  7. Heat a non stick tawa.
  8. Pour in 1 ladel of batter, lift the tawa & rotate it slowly once so that the batter spreads evenly.
  9. Cover & let cook for 1 min.
  10. Remove the Appam using a spatula when done.
  11. Serve hot wuth a spicy side dish (korma).
