Chicken Keema with Zucchini

Ingredients

1 lb minced chicken
2 zucchinis peeled and chopped
2 medium tomatoes
1 tbs garlic paste
1 tsp chilli powder
1 tsp turmeric powder
salt
1 sprig of curry leaves
1 tsp mustard seeds

Method

  1. Fry oil preferably in a non-stick pan, add mustard seeds and curry leaves till they sputter.
  2. Add garlic paste ( cover the pan for a few seconds so the garlic doesn't splatter).
  3. Fry for a min. add chillie and turmeric powder fry and add the keema.
  4. Fry well for a few mins till chicken turns from pink-to- white - to light brown.
  5. Then add salt and chopped zucchini and mix well.
  6. Cover and cook till zucchini is tender.
  7. Then add chopped tomatoes from top and cover again till tomatoes are tender.
  8. Once tender fry the mixture well till water dries up. Yum! eat it with anything, rice.. roti..
