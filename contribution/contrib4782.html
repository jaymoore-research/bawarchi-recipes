Poori Kilangu

Ingredients

This is actually Potato Masala For Poori Or Dosa.

Ingredients:

onion - 1 (cut lenth wise)
potato - 2 (boiled & mashed)
green chillies - 5 or 6 (slit into two)
cumin seeds - 1/2 tsp
aniseed - 1/2 tsp
cloves - 2
cinnamom -1/2 inch
turmeric powder - 1/4 stp
oil - 4 stp
salt -to taste
tomato - 1(small)optional

Method :

  1. Put the cut onions in little water and cook until the onions are soft.
  2. Mash the potato's with the onions & let it mix well.
  3. Heat the pan with little oil,add cumin seeds,aniseed,cloves &cinnamom.
  4. When the above ingredients turn red ,add green chillies & fry for a wh ile. (tomato is optional)
  5. Then add the potato and onion mix.
  6. Put some turmetic powder & salt .cook until the raw smell of turmeric vanishes.
  7. Garnish with corriander leaves.
  8. Sever with poori on dosa.
