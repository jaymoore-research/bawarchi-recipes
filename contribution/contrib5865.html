Pineapple Kesari Bath

Ingredients

I can crushed/cubed Pineapple
3 cups soji
3 cups sugar
1 cup water
1 cup milk
Pineapple juice (from the can)
kesar and 1 cup ghee
1 tsp cardamom powder
1/4 cup fried cashew and raisins

Method

  • Soak kesar in milk.
  • Fry rave in a little ghee till you get the aroma and it starts turning light brown. Keep aside.
  • Boil water, pineapple juice from the can and milk in a different container.
  • Add kesar. Then on low flame, add soji little by little.
  • Keep stirring till you clear the clumps, if any.
  • Then add cardamom powder, sugar, ghee and pineapple cubes, mix.
  • Let cook on low fame for 5 minutes.
  • Garnish with fried cashew and raisins.
  • Serve hot.

    Serves two.
