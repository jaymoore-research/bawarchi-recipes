Falafel

Ingredients for chick pea vadas(filling)
1cup soaked urad dal
1can cooked chick peas
2ts ginger paste
1tbs sesame seeds
green chillis paste(optional)
fresh corriader paste
oil to deep fry
salt to taste

Other Ingredients
1 pack round pita pocket breads
1 letus chopped
1 pealed finely chopped cucumber
2 red bell peppers
1ts red chilli powder
salt
1.5 cup sour cream
1/2 cup ground toasted sesame seeds
1/2 ts ground black pepper
salt

Method:

  1. Grind soaked urad dal and canned boiled chick peas.
  2. Mix them with ground chillis, coriander, ginger, sesame seeds and salt to taste.
  3. Try to add as less water as possible while grinding.
  4. Make a batter tight enough to form round vadas.
  5. Deep fry them and set aside on tissue paper to soak extra oil. Mix sour cream, salt, pepper, toasted ground sesame seeds together, keep aside deseed the red bell peppers.
  6. Grind them in the mixi adding salt and red chilli powder. Make a chutney and keep aside.
  7. Now cut the pita bread into 2 halfs.
  8. On one side of the pocket spread the sour cream and on the other side apply the red hot chutney.
  9. Place 3 to 4 vadas in the pocket and cover it with letus and cucumber.
  10. And the falafel is ready to serve.
  11. The best part of the homemade falafel is you can adjust the amount of spices accorging to yur likes.
