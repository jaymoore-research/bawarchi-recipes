White Pumpkin Subji

Ingredients

White Pumpkin - half
Onion -2
Tomatoes -2
Ginger Garlic Cilantro paste
Coconut milk - 1 cup
Green Chilli - 1 (accdg to taste)
Chilli Powder - a pinch
Turmeric Powder - a pinch
Garam Masala Powder - half teaspoon
Salt accdg to taste

Method:

  1. Cut pumpkin into squares.
  2. Cut onions and tomatoes into bits.
  3. In a kadai, heat oil. Add onions, saute till golden brown. Add ginger garlic cilantro paste. Saute.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook till pulpy. Add chilli powder, turmeric powder and mix a little. Add salt. Then add pumpkin pieces and fry a bit. Add water and close so that the pumpkin gets cooked. When it is three fourth cooked add the coconut milk (first extract). You can also use ready made coconut powder to make thick milk.
  5. Let it boil a little so that the pumpkin pieces cooks fully in the milk. Add garam masala and garnish with cliantro pieces.

Tastes excellent with Chappatis and Rotis. Enjoy the dish.
