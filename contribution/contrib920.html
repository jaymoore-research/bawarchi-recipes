Sweet Potato Capsicum Rice

Ingredients

2 Cups rice (basmathi)
1 medium sweet potato diced
1 large capsicum
1 onion sliced
2 Tbl spoon peanuts

Powder:
2 TBl spoon dhania
1 teaspoon urad dal
1 teaspoon channa dal
2 tblspoon dried coconut

Method

  1. Fry the above ingredients in little oil until golden and powder.
  2. Cook rice and cool.
  3. Heat 2 Tblspoon of oil in a pan and add mustard, urad dal, channa dal, hing & curry leaves.
  4. Add peanuts and fry until golden brown.
  5. Add onions and fry
  6. Add capsicum and cook until soft
  7. Add sweet potatoes and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  8. Add the masala powder, salt & a pinch of sugar
  9. Mix well with cooked rice.
  10. Garnish with coriander leaves and fried cashewnuts.

Serve hot with onion raitha & papad.
