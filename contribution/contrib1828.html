Chicken Kurma

Ingredients

Skinless chicken 1 kg
Garlic paste 2 tsp
Ghee 1 cup
Salt 1 tsp
Small Cardimam 5
Onion (finely chopped) 1/4 Kg
Yougart 1 cup
Garam Masala 1 tsp
Red chilli 2 tsp
Corriander powder 1 tsp

Method

  1. Fry the onion in oil til brown and take out and let dry on an old newspaper.
  2. Fry cardimam, garlic, chicken and salt in the ghee left after taking out the onion.
  3. Grind the browned onion in a grinder. Mix red chilly, corriander powder, garam masal, yougart and the grinded onion. Add to the chicken in the pan.
  4. Put 1/2 cup water and let it simmer until meat is as soft as desired. Ready when the oil separates.
