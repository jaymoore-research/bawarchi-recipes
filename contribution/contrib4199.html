Moong Dal Uttappa

Ingredients

100 gms Moong dal (half dal) fotra wali dal
1" Ginger
1 small Onion (optional)
1 Green Chilli
3 cloves of Garlic
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Soak the half ground moong dal in water overnight or in warm water for 2 hours.
  2. Grind the soaked daal alongwith ginger, chilli. Add grated onion to the grinded mixture and add salt also.
  3. Put the nonstic tava on gas. Heat it put some cooking oil and spread the ready material on tava, cook it till brown on bothsides applying oil.
  4. Serve it with Tomato Ketchup.

It is a very nutritive food highly rich in proteins and good for grwoing children and people of all age low fat nutritive food for calorie conscious and diet freeks.
