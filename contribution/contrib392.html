Potato Roti

Ingredients

Potato 2-3 (big sized)
Rice flour 1 1/2 cup
Green chillies 1 (finely chopped)
Coriander (finely chopped)
Curry leaves few (finely chopped)
Cumin seeds 1 tsp
Salt to taste
Grated fresh coconut (optional) 3-tblsp
Water for mixing.

Method

  1. Boil the potatoes, then mash it well without any lumps.
  2. Then add chillies, coriander, curry leaves, salt, jeera and mix well.
  3. Then add rice flour little by little so that the mixture forms to a paste. Mixture should not be very thin.
  4. Then take a ball of mixture and pat it on a tawa in a circular pattern with your fingers.
  5. Then pour some oil around and cover it and heat until the bottom of roti turns golden brown.
  6. Serve this hot with Coconut chutney or sambhar.

Variations: You can add onions, grated carrots, capsicum to this mixture.
