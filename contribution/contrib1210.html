Baked Granola Chewra

Ingredients

Section 1

1/4 cup Margarine or Oil
A few Curry Patha
1/2 cup tightly packed Brown Sugar
1/4 tsp Red Chilli powder
1/8 tsp Ginger paste
Pinch of Hing
1/2 tsp Salt

Section 2

3 cups rolled Oats (not instant)
1 cup Rice crispies
1/4 cup roasted skinless Peanuts (optional)
1/4 cup sweetened dessicated Coconut (thick strips)
1/4 cup finely chopped hard Dates, or whole Raisins, or chopped Apricots
1/8 cup hulled Sunflower Seeds

Method :

  1. Bake the rolled oats in a 350F oven for 10 mins.
  2. When cool, mix the oats, and other ingredients in section 2 in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Separately in the oil, on low heat fry the curry patha, ginger, chilli powder, hing.
  4. Then add brown sugar and salt and stir over heat till it melts and tiny bubbles start to appear. Turn off heat.
  5. Very carefully pour the hot mixture in a thin stream over the dry ingredients in the bowl, while simultaneously stirring.
  6. If the mixture is too wet and you want a drier chewra, quickly add more rice crispies and stir.
  7. Put all the mixture on to a greased baking tray and put into the warm oven for 10 mins to get crisp.
  8. Allow to cool to room temperature before putting away in air tight container.
  9. Now enjoy guilt-free snacking!
