Ghee Rice

Ingredients

2 cups basmati rice washed and soaked for 30 minutes
3 tbsp. ghee
Handful of cashew nuts
Handful of raisins soaked in water for 1 hr
2 onions longitudinally and finely cut
4 cardamom (opened)
1" cinnamon
4 cloves
3 bays leaves
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Boil rice with salt added till it is done.
  2. Heat ghee in a kadai.
  3. Add cashew nuts and fry till light brown.
  4. Add raisins.
  5. Add cinnamon, cloves, cardamom (with its cover) and bay leaves. Fry for 2-3 seconds.
  6. Add cut onions and fry till it is tender and light brown.
  7. Now add this to the prepared rice and mix well.
  8. Serve hot with gravy/chicken curry.
