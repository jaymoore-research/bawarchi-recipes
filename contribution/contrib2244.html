Yummy Idli and Chutney

Ingredients

Long Grain Rice 3 cups
Uncle Ben's Par Boiled Rice 1/2 (half) cup
Urad Daal 1 cup
Salt to taste

Method

  1. Soak both the rice in one container and the daal in another. Soak for 4 to 6 hours.
  2. Grind the Urad Daal to a very fine paste.
  3. Grind the rice separately and add to the Daal paste.
  4. Add the salt and mix well and leave the batter in a warm place for about a day and half, till the batter rises.
  5. Pour the dough in the idli makers (if no idli maker - you can pour the dough into a flat steel plate or bowl and steam it in a pressure cooker - no whistle please).
  6. Steam for about 10 to 15 mins.
  7. Serve yummy hot idlis.

The chutney I make to go with the yummy idli's is as follows.

Ingredients for Chutney

Fresh Coconut (half a coconut)
Green Chillies - 3 or 4
Dhalia (Dried chana/ Kadala Paruppu) 1 tbsp
Oil - 1 tbsp
Mustard - 1/2 (half) tsp
Curry leaves - 5-6 leaves

Method

  1. Grind the fresh coconut , green chillies and the dhalia.
  2. Heat 1 spoon of oil, add mustard, curry leaves. When the mustard crackles add the ground paste. Add salt to taste. Do not let it boil or cook

Serve with the yummy idlis. Enjoy!
