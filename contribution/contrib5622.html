Carrot Kheer

Ingredients:

1 carrot ( 250 g )
1 liter milk
15 - 20 almonds
2 cup sugar
Elaichhi powder
A pinch of saffron soaked in little milk
1 tbsp cashew nuts and 1tsp ghee

Ā Method:

  1. Soak the almonds in warm water for 1 hour.
  2. Remove the skin and keep aside.
  3. Grate the carrot and grind it along with the peeled almonds. Keep the paste aside.
  4. Heat the milk in a thick bottomed kadhai and reduce it to 3/4 th .
  5. Add sugar and stir again till it the milk becomes little thick.
  6. Add the carrot + almond paste and keep stirring .
  7. Remove from fire. Let it cool .Add the saffron milk and the elaichhi powder..
  8. Fry cashew nuts in little ghee and add to the milk.
  9. Chill it in the fridge and serve.
