Beans with channa daal

Ingredients

Beans (fresh or frozen french beans)
1/2 cup of Channa daal
2 tbsp - fresh Coconut (dry will also be ok)

For Seasoning

1/2 tbspoon Urad daal, Mustard
1 spoon of Oil.
Curry leaves (optional)
4 - Green Chillies

Method

  1. Cook channa daal in pressure cooker with enough water.
  2. Put seasoning in a kadai with the above mentioned ingredients. After the mustard splutters add green chillies and beans. Let this cook for some time in a slow flame .
  3. Once the beans are cooked, add cooked channa (no water) and coconut and let it cook for some more time until water (if there is any) evaporates.

Tastes good with Rice or with Roti.
