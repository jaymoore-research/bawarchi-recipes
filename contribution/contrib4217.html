Brinjal Gothsu

Ingredients

Brinjal (egg plant)- 1 big size ( or 10 small size brinjal)
Tomato - 2 big size
Carrot - 1 big size
Peas - 50 grams
Moong dal - 100 grams
Tamarind - Small lemon size
Sambar Powder
Turmeric powder
Salt

For Seasoning:

Oil
Mustard seeds -1 tsp
Green Chilli - 2
Urad dal - 2 tsp
Asafoetida - 1 pinch
Curry Leaves
Cilantro

Method:

  1. Cut tomatoes and pressure cook well with moong dal and turmeric powder and water.
  2. Dice brinjal and carrot into small pieces.
  3. Soak Tamarind for 10 minutes and extract the juice and make it to 2 cups. Add diced brinjal, carrot and peas to the tamarind juice and cook till the vegetables become soft. Add salt and Sambar powder and cook for some more time.
  4. Smash the cooked moong dal and tomatoes by hand and add the same to the boiling gothsu and cook for 5 more minutes.
  5. Finally season with mustard seeds, green chilli, urad dal, curry leaves, asafoetida and add cut cilantro. Serve with ven pongal. The gothsu will give a great look! and taste yummy.

Note: The tamarind juice should be light and watery, so that on completion of gothsu, it comes to semi solid consistency and will go well with Ven Pongal. The proportion of Brinjal:Carrot:Peas should be 3:1:1.
