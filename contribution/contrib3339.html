Vegetable And Nut Pinwheel

Ingredients

2 long slices of bread,
1/2 - 3/4th mixed boiled vegetables,
Green chutney,
2 tablespoon roasted walnuts and cashewnuts,
Butter,
Chilli sauce.

Ingredients for green chutney:
11/2 cup dhania patta,
2-3 green chillies,
2 tablespoon groundsnuts,
juice of half lemon,
salt to taste,
1/2 teaspoon jeera,
1/2 cup fudina,
2-3 cloves garlic,
1 small onion.

Method for green chutney:
Grind the above ingredients to a fine paste in the mixie.

Method:

  1. Mash the vegetables roughly.
  2. Add chutney to bind the vegetables.
  3. Add salt if needed, grated coconut, paneer. Then mix in nuts.
  4. Remove the crust from the bread slices.
  5. Roll the bread and then apply butter and chilli sauce.
  6. Spread the filling.
  7. Then roll it tightly.
  8. Cover with a moist cloth and keep in the fridge for an hour.

Then cut into slices and serve as a snack.
