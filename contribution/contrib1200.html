Dal Vada

Ingredients

Moong Dal (yellow) 1.5 cups
Moong Dal (green) 2 cups
1 medium Onion
6 Green Chillies
2 inch Ginger
1 bunch Cilantro (coriander leaves)
1 pinch Soda bi-Carbonate
Salt to taste
2 tsp hot oil
Oil for frying

Method:

  1. Soak both moong dals for about 6-8 hours.
  2. Wash dal thoroughly and grind dals with green chillies and ginger. (Not very fine slightly coarse)
  3. Chop onion and cilantro very fine and add to the dal mixture.
  4. Add salt, bi-carb, and 2 tsp hot oil. Mix well.
  5. Heat oil in kadhai.
  6. Take small amounts of dal mixture and fry in hot oil till golden brown.
  7. Serve hot with fried chillies and coriander - mint chutney.

I hope everybody will enjoy this vadas.
