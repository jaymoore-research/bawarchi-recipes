Keema Kababs

Ingredients

1 lb Keema
1 Onion
1 Egg
1/2 slice of Bread
2 hot Green Chillies (optional)
Salt to taste (about 1 1/2 teaspoon)
1 teaspoon Red Pepper
1 teaspoon each Whole Jeera, Powdered Jeera, Whole Coriander and Powdered Coriander
1/2 cup Coriander Leaves
1" sliced Ginger
6-7 cloves Garlic or 1 teaspoon. minced bottled Garlic
1 teaspoon Garam Masala
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground Black Pepper

Method:

  1. In the food processor, coarsely chop onion, garlic and ginger. Add the keema and all the other ingredients and process till the mixture is coarse but not lumpy.
  2. Empty in a bowl and with oiled hands form sheesh kababs (in the shape of a sausage) and put onto bamboo skewers and shape them so that all the kababs look smooth. Put them on a foil lined cookie or baking sheets (2).
  3. Pre heat broiler and when hot, put the baking sheets under the broiler and let it broil for about 5-10 minutes till brown on side. Remove sheets from the broiler and quickly change over sides with tongs and broil till brown and done. Remove as you dont want them to get tough.
  4. Serve on a platter of onions and quartered lemons with a bowl of coriander chutney or ketchup.
  5. If out of the freezer, sprinkle with water and cover with wrap and zap in the microwave for a minuter or two, and voila they are done.
