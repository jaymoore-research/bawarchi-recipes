Daal Bati (Rajasthani dish)

Ingredients

for Dal:
1-Cup tuvar dal
1 Medium tomato chopped
2-3 Green chilies chopped
2-3 cloves of garlic chopped
1/2 tsp. Ginger chopped
2-3 bay leaves
red chili powder to taste
salt to taste
garam masala to taste
mustard seeds
cumin seeds(jeera)
pinch full of asafoetida
1 tbsp. ghee/oil.

Method:

  1. Pressure-cook tuvar dal.
  2. Put ghee/oil in a pan,heat. Now add asafoetida, mustard seeds & cumin seeds.Allow to splutter.
  3. Now add garlic, ginger & chili. Stir.
  4. Add bay leaves, tomatoes . Now add salt, red chili powder & garam masala.Stir & cover the pan with lid.
  5. After sometime pour boiled daal in to the pan & add 2-3 cups of water. Let it boil for 5-10 min.
  6. Now your daal is ready. Garnish with chopped coriander & serve hot.

Making time: 20 minutes
Makes: 4 servings
Shelflife: Best fresh

Ingredients

for Bati:
3-Cup chapati flour
1-Cup rava (sooji)
ghee/oil
salt 1/8 tsp.
cumin seeds (ajavayan)
water

Method:

  1. Mix all ingredients & make dough (tight).
  2. Make 8 round balls ( bati).
  3. Preheat the oven for 5-10 min now set the temp. of oven to 350.C.
  4. Keep all baties in a tray covered with aluminum foil.
  5. Now watch baties after every 5-min. & change the sides .
  6. After 20-25 min. take all baties out pour some ghee on it & serve hot.

Making time: 20-25 minutes
Makes: 4 servings
Shelflife: Best fresh

Tip: Serve Daal-Bati with the combination of green chutney, besan gatte & kadi.
