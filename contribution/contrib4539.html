Plain Adai

Ingredients

Par boiled Rice (idli rice)- 1 1/2 cups
Thur Dhal- 1/2 cup
Channa Dhal- 1/2 cup
Urad Dhal- 1/4th cup
Red Chillies- 4 pieces
Green Chillies- 2 pieces
Hing- little bit.

Method:

  1. Soak all the ingredients except hing in water for about 2 hours.
  2. Now drain of some water and with blender grind this stuff little by little not into fine paste but some what chorous form.
  3. Some people like to make adais then and there and some others like to ferment. Whichever the case may be now nicely mix the whole thing and take the quantity needed in another vessel, to this add hing, some coriander leaves and onions of you wish and mix every thing well and add salt. If the batter is too thick dilute it accordingly.
  4. Now take tava let it heat evenly. Take on laddle of this mixture and pour it in centre and spread evenly to the four corners.Keep in mind it should be thick and not as thin as dosa. Now lubricate the sides with oil and when one side is done turn other and process just goes on.
  5. With this propotion can make nearly 20 Adai's. Serve it with butter and jaggery, thats makes excellent combination. Have it hot.
