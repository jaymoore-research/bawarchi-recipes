Misti Doi (Sweetened Yogurt)

Ingredients

16 oz Yogurt (Plain)
14 oz Sweetened Condensed Milk
12 oz Evaporated Milk

Method:

  1. Mix the condensed milk and yogurt well. Heat the evaporated milk (only a little) remove from the stove, let it cool properly and then mix it with the condensed milk and yogurt mixture. Mix well.
  2. Pre heat oven for 5 mins Bake the mixture at 225 for 45 mins upto 1 hour. Basically keep an eye on it till the top kind of thickens and becomes red in colour. The time may vary.

Imp - Cool the evaporated milk properly or else the yogurt curdles in heat.
