Therata Paal

Ingredients

One 15 oz container of whole or skim or fat free Ricotta cheese
15 oz Carnation milk powder (fat free is fine)(use your ricotta Cheese container as your measuring can)
1Āstick (4OZ) Unsalted Butter
15 oz Sugar ( measure it with the ricota cheese container)

Ā Method

  1. Take all the ingredients in a wide mouth large microwave glass or porcelain dish ( it has to be big enough so that the mixture does not boil over)
  2. Microwave on high for 3 minutes at a time stirring well in between so that there are no lumps.
  3. After 12 minutes take care and do it one or two minutes at a time.
  4. With a new microwaves it takes around 15 minutes. with older ones it may take upto 18 minutes.
  5. Take it out when it has the right color and is semi solid therita paal consistency. It thickens as it cools .
  6. Hope you enjoy this sweet.
