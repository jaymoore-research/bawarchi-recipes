Appam

Ingredients

Coconut with shell 1 (break it and save the coconut water. Make sure it tastes fresh)
Long Grain Rice 3 cups (soak the rice 8 - 10 hours)
Sugar 1 1/2 cup
Yeast 1/2 tsp
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Wash rice and drain.
  2. Grate coconut. Blend the coconut, rice and sugar together until it becomes very creamy. Consistency of batter can be like dosa's or pancake's. Add the coconut water to blend the mixture.
  3. Add 1 tsp of hot water to yeast and mix it very well in the batter. Keep it for 8 - 10 hours to ferment. The batter will be bubbly then.
  4. Add a pinch of baking soda, salt and mix the batter.
  5. Heat skillet with oil. Make it like pancake/dosa. Cover with lid. Do not flip the appam. Remove the lid in couple of minutes to make sure it is cooked.
  6. Serve with Meat Curry or Potato Stew.
