Chichen Delight

Ingredients

Chicken (boneless thigh)1 lb
Onion (chopped) 1-big
Green chilly (chopped) 4nos
Ginger (minced) 1/2 inch piece
Tomato (chopped)1 medium
Coconut milk 1/2 cup or 2 tbsp coconut powder
Pepper 1 tsp
Fennel seed (somber) 1/2 tsp
Cinnamon 1/2 piece
Oil 1 tbsp
Curry leaves few

Method:

  1. Dry fry pepper, fennel seed, cinnamon and powder and keep it aside.
  2. Heat the oil in a kadai add onion, green chillies and ginger and fry till transparent. Add tomato and fry 5 mins.
  3. Now add the chicken pieces and fry for 10 mins. Add 1 cup water, salt and cook till the chicken is done.
  4. Add the coconut milk, powder masala and curry leaves. Cook for 5 more mins and remove. Tastes good with Dosa, Iddli and Appam. Enjoy.
