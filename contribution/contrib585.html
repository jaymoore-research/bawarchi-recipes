Vermicilli Payasam

Ingredients

Vermicilli (The Bambino one should be available at the Indian Dukans in the US ) a packet acc to the quantity you want to make
Condensed milk (sweetened) This is just like milkmaid and the essence of the taste in the kheer (Try the Borden variety,in the American grocery stores, low fat for the health conscious!) --1 TIN
Ghee -1/2 a tsp
Cashewnuts and raisins--add acc to taste
1 carton of 1% milk.(if you want the kheer to be thick, you could add half and half milk)

Method:

  1. Fry the vermicilli in ghee till it turns a beautiful brown and keep aside.
  2. Empty the contents of the condensed milk into a vessel and dilute it according to the no of servings that you want. add the 1% milk or half and half.
  3. Let this boil.
  4. Once it starts to boil add the vermicilli and keep stirring till it is cooked. It is very important to keep stirring if you want good results!!
  5. The whole process should take about 30 mins with about 15 mins of stirring time.
  6. You could roast a few cashews and raisins and then add it to the cooked kheer.
  7. It tastes the best when it is refrigerated. However, you can always eat it piping hot!

Enjoy your Kheer/Payasam :
Note: This is for about 2 servings. Pl add acc to the amount of people.
