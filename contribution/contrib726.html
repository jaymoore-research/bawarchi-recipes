Grilled Cheese Sandwiches

Ingredients

Bread slices-6
A cup finely cut vegetables-carrot, onions, tomatoes, beans
A cupful of grated mozzarella cheese
2 green chillies
salt

Method:

  1. Take a kadai and just saute the vegetables with little oil. To this add the green chillies finely cut and salt to taste. Fry for 1 minute.
  2. Take each slice and spread the vegetable mixture on top. On top of it add the grated cheese. Spread it fully in the shape of the bread.
  3. Preheat the oven on medium temperature and place this sandwich on the upper grill.
  4. When the cheese melts, it is ready. Eat it hot. Enjoy it as a breakfast or teatime snack. It takes hardly 15 minutes to prepare it.
