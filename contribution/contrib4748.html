5-Minutes Pickle

Ingredients

Zucchini one medium size
Red Chilli Powder four teaspoons
Salt according the taste
Asafoetida
1 teaspoon Lime Juice or Imli (mango) powder

Method:

Remove the skin and cut the zucchini into small pieces. Add the ingredients and mix it. Now it is ready to serve.
