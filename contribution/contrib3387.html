Cashew Burfi

Ingredients

Cashew -100 gm
Sugar -250 gm
Ghee -1/3 cup
Milk -1/2 cup
Saffron -1/4 tsp
Cardamom powder -1/2 tsp.

Method:

  1. Soak cashew in milk for an hour & grind finely along with saffron.
  2. In a casserole mix sugar with 1/2 cup of water & stir well. Microwave it on High for 4 minutes to dissolve the sugar, stirring once in between. Micro high, ghee in a cup for 1 minute.
  3. To sugar syrup, add cashew paste & hot ghee. Micro high for 12 to 15 minutes, stirring thoroughly twice in between. Test for soft ball stage (a drop of mixture when put into cold water should form a soft but firm ball). If not done micro high again for 1 or 2 minutes more.
  4. Add spices, mix well, & pour onto a greased plate & cut when slightly cool.
