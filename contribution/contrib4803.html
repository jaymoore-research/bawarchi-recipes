Rajma

Ingredients

(Total cooking time - 15 mins and cost - approx $1)
1 can Kidney Beans (rajma)
1 onion
1 tomato or more
1 tbsp chopped cilantro parsley (coriander leaves) (optional)
a pinch turmeric powder
1-2 Jalapeno peppers (green chillies) OR 1 teaspoon red chilly powder
1 tsp Coriander powder (dhania powder)
salt as per taste
1 tsp oil

Method:

  1. Chop up the onion, tomato and chillies and keep aside.
  2. heat oil in a vessel on full flame for 2 mins.
  3. Add the chopped onions and keep stirring until onions become transparent.
  4. Add the turmeric powder, chilly powder and coriander powder and stir for 1 min.
  5. Add the tomato. Keep stirring for 3 mins or until no more chunks of tomato are visible.
  6. Add the salt (do not add too much b'coz all canned products already contain salt). Add the can of rajma.
  7. Add a cup of water and stir. Close the vessel and allow it to boil for 5 mins.
  8. Sprinkle the coriander leaves on top of it. The rajma is ready to be served.

Well, this is the secret... you can use the above formula to make any dish.

Note:

Alu Palak: Use one can spinach and 1-2 boiled potatoes instead of the rajma. Rest of the ingredients and steps are the same. If you wish grind the palak in a food blender.

Brinjal curry: Use Eggplant instead of the rajma.

Egg curry: Use boiled, peeled and partly sliced eggs instead of rajma.
