Apple Pie

Ingredients

Apple 2 Nos
Sugar 1 Cup
Cinamon Powder 1 tbs

For Pastry:
White Flour 200 gms
Baking Powder 1tbs
Salt to taste
Oil / Ghee for deep frying

Method

  1. De seed Apples & chop them into small pieces ,Heat a pan add the chopped Apples & sugar ,cook till the sugar dissolves & Apples become tender.
  2. Check if more sugar is need because the apples might be sour.
  3. Add cinnamon powder & mix, take out & keep aside.
  4. Add a little ghee/oil to the flour, baking powder & salt.
  5. Kneed it to soft pliable dough adding water.
  6. Roll the dough into small squares, add spoonful of the apple mix in the center, seal the edges with little water. U can also shape in the gujiya mould.
  7. In heavy bottomed pan heat oil, fry on a low fire till crispy & done.

I hope you have a great time enjoying this wonderful pie.
