Udin Vada

Ingredients

1. 2 cups Urad dal.
2. 1 tbs Rice flour. (app)
3. 2 whole Curry leaves.
4. 4 or 5 Green Chillies.
5. Salt to taste.
6. Oil to deep-fry.

Method:

  1. Soak dal overnight.
  2. Grind the dal in a grinder or a blender (if possible a grinder). Add very little water. The dough must not be slippery.
  3. Wet your fingers to see if the dough is ready. Take a little dough in between index finger and thumb and feel. you should not feel anything coarse. It should be smooth.
  4. Transfer the dough into a container and add the salt. The dough will give out water, it will become watery.
  5. Sprinkle the rice flour to absorb the water. The quantity of rice flour depends on the texture of the dough.
  6. Add chopped green chillies and curry leaves and mix well.
  7. Heat oil in a kadai. Take a scoop of the dough on to your palm or a polythene sheet, pat it into a circle, make a hole in the center and slip it into the heated oil. (do not slip the polythene :-) fry both sides till brown.
  8. Enjoy eating the vada!

Tip: The vada should be well made. Be patient! If using a blender, you may need to keep mixing the dough or the top half will not grind.
