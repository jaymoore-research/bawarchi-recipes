Crispy Bhendi

Ingredients

200 gms Bhendi,
1/2 cup Cornflour,
1/2 cup Besan,
1 1/2 tsp Chilli Powder, 1/2 tsp haldi powder
1/2 tsp Chaat Masala,
1 tsp Pepper powder,
Oil to fry and Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Slit bhendis in the centre.
  2. Mix salt, pepper powder and apply it to the bendi.
  3. Mix besan, cornflour, chilli powder and hing (optional), salt then add water and mix to make a thick batter like pakoras.
  4. Heat oil, dip each bhindi and deep fry till crisp.
  5. Sprinkle chaat masala over the fried bhendi and serve as a snack or a side dish with rice and dal.
