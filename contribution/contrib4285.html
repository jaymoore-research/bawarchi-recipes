Aviyal with Tofu

Ingredients

Chow-Chow (cayote squash)
1 medium size Carrot
10 Green Beans
1 small Potato
1 cup non-fat plain Yogurt
1/2 packet Azumaya-extra firm Tofu
1/2 tsp Rice
4 Green Chilli
1 tsp Cumin seeds
1-1/2 tsp Salt
Curry Leaves

Method:

  1. Cut all the vegetables into 1 1/2 inches lengthwise strips. Cook them with salt till they are tender.
  2. Blend rice, chilli, cumin, tofu and the yogurt (put them in the blender in the order given) till the mixture is well blended and smooth.
  3. Add to the vegetables and bring to one boil and remove from the stove. Add curry leaves and stir. No-one will miss the coconut in this dish!

If available cooking banana and winter melon could be used instead of chow-chow and potato.
