Lauki-Tamatar Subzi

Ingredients

1) Lauki (green gourd / doodhi) - say � kg - peel and cut into 1" cubes
2) Tomatoes - 2 medium sized - chopped fine
3) Oil - 3 tsp.(yep its healthy too)
4) Cumin seeds (jeera) - 1 tsp
5) Bay leaves (tez patta) - 3 leaves
6) Asafoetida (hing) - 1 pinch
7) Salt - as per taste
8) Turmeric powder (haldi) - 1/4 tsp
9) Coriander powder (dhaniya powder) - 1 & 1/2 tsp
10) Cumin powder (Jeera powder) - 1/2 tsp.
11) Red chilli powder (mirchi powder) - 1/2 tsp - (or more as per taste)
12) Green coriander leaves (dhaniya patti) - chopped fine - for garnishing.

Method:

  1. In a pressure cooker: Heat oil. Add hing. Add jeera & tez patta. Add chopped tomatoes. Fry the tomatoes - till they are soft/wilted.
  2. Add the lauki pieces. Add salt & other masala powders. Mix well and lid the cooker. Take off the fire after 3 whistles
  3. Taste - if tastes fine let be - else add salt/masala etc. and let be on fire till 3-4 minutes.
  4. Garnish with fresh coriander.
  5. Serve with Rotis/Parathas etc.

P.S. Can be made in a pan too - takes a wee bit longer. Normally takes 15 minutes.
