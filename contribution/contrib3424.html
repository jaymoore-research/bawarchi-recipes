Curry Laksa

Ingredients

Maggi noodles one packet
Mixed vegetables one cup
Onion one (chopped finely)
Coconut milk one cup
Chilly powder one teaspoon
Salt as per taste

Method:

  1. Cook the noodles in boiling water till it is almost done. Drain it.
  2. In a frying pan pour oil and stir fry the onion first then add the mixed vegetables and cook it in low fire for some time. Then add salt and chilly powder. Mix well.
  3. Heat the coconutmilk in low fire and add the tastemaker which you get with the maggi noodles. Do not let it boil.

To serve this dish first thing to go in is the noodles top it with vegetables then pour the flavoured coconutmilk on top and have it piping hot. Try this out guys. You will love it. Happy cooking.
