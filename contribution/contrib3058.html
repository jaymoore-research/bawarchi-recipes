Spicy Dosa

Ingredients

Rice-3 cups
Toor dal-1/2 cup
Channa dal- 1/2 cup
Coarsely crushed Black Pepper
Freshly grated Coconut
1 whole bunch of finely chopped Cilantro
Finely chopped Green Chilies
Cumin
Salt
Oil

Method:

  1. Blend the rice coarsely in a blender.
  2. Blend even the dals coarsely in a blender.
  3. Soak all the 3 powders in Luke warm water adding sufficient salt. Make sure to add the water just enough to cover the mixture (make the batter a little thicker than the dosa batter).
  4. Let the mixture set for about one and half an hour.
  5. Add cumin, green chillies, coconut, coriander & crushed black pepper to the mixture & mix well.
  6. Mean while heat a griddle & grease it with a tsp. of oil.
  7. Ladle a big spoon full of batter into the center of the pan and spread it around in a circular fashion (from inside out) shaping the batter into a thick dosa.
  8. Wait for few seconds and then take a tsp. of oil and spread it over the edges of the dosa.
  9. When the edges turn golden, flip the dosa on the other side. Again add a tsp. of oil & cook it for a minute.
  10. Serve the dosa with butter.
