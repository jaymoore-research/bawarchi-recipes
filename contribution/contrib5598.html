Corn Bread

Ingredients for Cooking

1 egg,
1 cup self rising corn meal,Ā
2 tablespoon oil,Ā
1 chopped onion,
1 tablespoon chopped green chilli,Ā
1/2 cup chopped cilantro,Ā
2 tablespoon salsa,
1 cup Shredded Cheese

Method:

  1. Mix together 1 and 2 till well blended.
  2. Heat oven to 450 degree.
  3. In large bowl combine all ingredients except Shredded cheese and salsa; mix well.
  4. Grease an 8 or 9 inch square pan.
  5. Pour batter into greased pan and on the top place a layer of shredded cheese and salsa.
  6. Bake for 20-22 minutes or till golden color.
  7. Cool for 2-3 minutes and cut the bread into squares. Serve warm.
