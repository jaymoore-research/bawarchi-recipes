Cabbage Wada

Ingredients

Black Gram Dal--------- I cup
Cabbage shredded------1 cup
Coriander finely chopped-------1/2 cup
Green Chillies-----------------4-5
Salt--------------------------------To taste
Oil--------------------------------For deep frying.

Method

  1. Soak the dal for 4to 5 hours.
  2. Grind it to a coarse paste with salt and green chillies.
  3. Heat oil in a pan for deep frying.
  4. Mix shredded cabbage and coriander leaves finely chopped into the dal paste.
  5. Take a little paste at a time and spread it on a plastic cover or banana leaf in a wada shape.{ You can wet the plastic cover with water or grease it with oil}
  6. Gently drop each wada into the hot oil and fry till golden brown
  7. Serve hot with coconut chutney or coriander chutney or even pickles.
