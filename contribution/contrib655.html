Elaichi Meat

Ingredients

1 kg mutton
500gms onions
10-15 green elaichi (crushed)
2 tsps freshly ground black pepper
Coriander for garnishing

Method:

  1. Wash and put the mutton in cooker with onions and salt and pressure cook for 10 mins without water.
  2. After the cooker is cooled, open and put on large flame and dry up the water.
  3. Then add ground pepper powder and elaichi.
  4. Mix well then add 1 glass of water and keep on slow flame for 10 mins and it is ready to serve.
  5. Garnish with coriander and serve hot with rice or rotis.
