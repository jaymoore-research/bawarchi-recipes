Meat & Root Vegetable Wadas

Ingredients

1 1/2 Pound lean ground meat (beef, pork, lamb, mutton, goat, venison)
3 - 4 Cups grated, par-boiled root vegetables (potatoes, yams, carrots, parsnips, rutabegas, turnips; see note)

Mix together:

3/4 cup Besan
1 tbap ground Coriander
1 tsp ground Chilli (I used ancho)
1 tsp Salt
1 tsp ground Cumin

Wet Masala Option 1:
Rehydrate:

2 Tbsp dried, grated Onion
1 Tbsp dried, grated Garlic
1 tsp Ginger Powder

Wet Masala Option 2:
Grind together:

1 small Onion
1 inch Ginger
2-3 cloves of Garlic

Method:

Mix wet masala with meat mixture, then the dry masala. Shape into flattened oblongs and bake for 20 minutes at 375 F.

Note: I used frozen shredded hash browns (thawed). When using fresh vegetables, first grate, then cook in the microwave for 3 minutes.

Cilantro Raita

Blend in the mixie until smooth: 8 oz yoghurt, 1/2 tsp cardamom seeds, 1/2 tsp ground cardamom, 1/4 cup cilantro leaves

Stir in:

1/2 Cucumber, grated, 8 oz Yoghurt
