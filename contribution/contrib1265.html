Jhatpat Chicken

Ingredients

Chicken, cut into bite size pieces, about 850g-1kg.
Ginger- 2" piece, grated fine
Mirchi powder - 1 tsp
Haldi powder - 1/2 tsp
Dhania powder - 2 tsp
Jeera powder - 1 tsp
Kasuri methi - 2tsp.
Amchur-to taste.
Oil - 2 tblsp.
Chat masala - to taste.
Salt - to taste.
Hara dhania, chopped, for garnishing.

Method:

  1. Apply Haldi, Mirchi powder and salt to the Chicken pieces.
  2. Heat oil and fry grated ginger till fragrant.
  3. Add chicken and fry on high heat for five minutes.
  4. Then add jeera, dhania powders and amchur.
  5. Stir, reduce heat and fry till chicken is cooked.
  6. Add kasuri methi and Chat masala and stir for a minute.
  7. Finally, garnish with Hara dhania and serve hot with rotis or phulkas.
