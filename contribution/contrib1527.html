Suji Ka Seera (Semolina Halva)

Ingredients

250 gms semolina (suji or rava as known to lot of people)
3 Tbsp wheat flour
300 gms sugar
8-10 cardamoms (powdered)
10-12 strands of saffron
1 cup Ghee
10 sliced almonds for garnishing
4 cups of water

Method:

  1. Sieve the semolina. Heat the ghee and fry the semolina and the wheat flour on very low flame till it is aromatic and a shade darker than golden brown.
  2. Dissolve the sugar, powdered cardamoms and saffron in water and add this syrup to the semolina and cook it on low flame, stirring continuously till the semolina dries up.
  3. Keep on a preheated tava for 5-7 minutes and let it simmer.

Serve hot, garnished with chopped almonds.
