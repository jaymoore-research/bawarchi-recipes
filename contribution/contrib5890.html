Milk Burfi

Ingredients

Milk - 1 cup
Sugar - 2 cups
Grated coconut -1 cup
Ghee -3 cups
Besan powder -1 cup
Finely crushed almonds -1 cup
saffron a pinch

Method

  1. First fry the besan powder in a kadai for 3-5mts with 3 spoons of ghee and keep aside.
  2. Soak the almonds in the hot water for 2 hrs and peel off the layer and dry it and finely crush it.
  3. Put sugar  and milk in  in the kadai ,stir well untill the sugar gets melted,add a pinch of saffron.
  4. Now add fried besan powder,coconut,almonds,ghee 1cup to the sugar syrup and stir well for 5-10mts.
  5. Add another cup of remaining ghee to it,stir it for 10 more mts,untill it gets round like ball in the kadai.
  6. Now immediately pour  this mixture in the greased plate,allow it to cool for 5mts,and cut into small pieces.
