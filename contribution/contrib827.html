Whole Eggplant Fry

Ingredients

4 Small sized eggplants
1/4 cup coriander seeds
2 garlic cloves
4 dry red chillies
Small piece of dry coconut
Few coriander leaves
Salt to taste
5 tbspns oil

Method :

  1. Fry coriander seeds, red chillies in 1 tbspn of oil till golden brown.
  2. Cut dry coconut into very small pieces.
  3. Grind fried coriander seeds, red chillies, coconut and garlic cloves to a fine powder and keep aside.
  4. Cut eggplants into 4 sections keeping the head (green part) intact and put them in water so that they don't turn black.
  5. Mix salt and coriander leaves to the masala powder grinded before.
  6. Stuff these cut eggplants with the masala.
  7. Heat the remaining oil and fry these stuffed eggplants in it till they get cooked.
  8. While cooking, keep turning the eggplants to avoid burning.

This whole eggplant fry will taste good with hot rice and ghee and it's very easy to make. You can use the masala we grinded in other curries and fries also.
