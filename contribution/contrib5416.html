Corn Rice

(this is a very easy to cook dish)

Ingredients

1 cup cooked rice (usually leftover)
1 cup corn kernel
5-7 green chillies
1/2 tsp mustard seeds
1/4 tsp Hing (Asafoetida)
2 tbsp dry coconut powder (availabe in indo-america stores)
2 tsp oil
1-2 tsp lemon juice

Method:

  1. Run the frozen corn under hot water and strain it.
  2. Heat the oil in a pan.
  3. Once oil is hot add the mustard seeds. Let it splutter.
  4. Then add the cut green chillies and saute for few seconds
  5. Now add the corn. (frozen corn is already a little soft)
  6. Saute it for around 2 minutes
  7. Now add the cooked rice to it and saute.
  8. Add the dry coconut powder and mix it well
  9. Now add the lemon juice and mix it well.
  10. serve hot.
