Ingredients

Gram flour (Besan) 1 cup
Buttermilk 1 cup
water 2 cups
All purpose flour 1 tsp
Green Chillies 4 (finely chopped)
Shredded coconut 1/2 cup
Oil 1 tbs
Coriander 1 tbs (finely chopped)
Asafoetida 3-4 pinches
Mustard Seeds 1/2 tsp
Salt to taste

Method :

  1. In a bowl add gram flour, all purpose flour, buttermilk and water. Mix it well till it forms a smooth batter.
  2. Add green chillies and salt to taste.
  3. Mix grated coconut and chopped coriander. If you like, you can add some green chillies to this mixture.
  4. Heat the oil. Add mustard seeds to it. When Mustard seeds crackles add asfoetida. Cool this seasoning.
  5. In the meantime, on a medium flame cook the batter by stirring continuously. You have to take special care during cooking as there should not be lumps in the batter as it solidifies.
  6. Continue stirring till the batter when dropped from the ladder forms thin film.
  7. Immediately place this mixture on the half portion of a plastic sheet. Cover it with the remaining half portion. Lightly spread the mixture with the help of rolling pin to form a thin film of batter.
  8. Garnish it with seasoning and coconut-coriander mixture.
  9. Cut the spread sheet vertically into thin strips of desired width. Then roll each strip into Vadi.

Enjoy it !!!
