Potato Curry

Ingredients

2 tbsps Oil
1 tsp Mustard seeds
2 Dry Red Chillies
3-4 Curry leaves
2 big Onions, chopped
1/2 tsp ground Coriander
1/2 tsp Garam Masala Powder
1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
2 Tomatoes, quartered
400 gm Potatoes, peeled and quartered
100 ml fresh or canned Coconut Milk
100 ml Water

Method:

  1. Heat oil in saucepan. Add mustard seeds, dry red chillies and curry leaves. Stir fry for 30 seconds over medium fire.
  2. When mustard seeds begin to pop, add onions and stir fry for 1 minute.
  3. Add coriander powder, garam masala powder, turmeric powder & chilli powder.
  4. Add tomatoes & cook for 5 minutes. Then add potatoes and cook over gentle fire for 5 minutes.
  5. Pour in coconut milk and water. Cook for 15-20 minutes. Serve hot.
