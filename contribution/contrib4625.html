Idli and Chutney

Ingredients

Ponni (regular cooking rice) Rice2 1/2 cups
UncleBen's parboiled rice1 cup
Ulad dal 1 1/4 cup.
Methi seeds 1 tsp.
Salt to taste.

Method:

  1. Soak parboiled rice for 8 hrs & Ponni rice & methi seeds for 5 hrs (seperately)
  2. Soak ulad dal for 1 1/2 hrs before grinding rice. Grind parboiled rice first coarsely. Then to it add Ponni rice /methi & grind to a rough paste. Add salt to taste while grinding. If your rice batter is too thin wait for an hour, the water will float to top, take it out & use it for grinding ulad dal.
  3. Grind ulad dal to a fine paste. Mix it with rice batter, let it stay 8 hrs or overnight to ferment. You will see the batter risen, it is ready for making Idli.
  4. Mix it well in one direction only and pour in greased Idli mould, keep it in pressure cooker for 19 mts on high flame. (no whistle)
  5. Take it out of cooker and after 5-7 mts. take out from the Idli mould.

Chatni.(Type 1)

Ingredients:

One half (1/2) fresh coconut
Ginger(about 1 inch)
Little tamarind (about 5 inches)
Green chilli & salt to taste

Method:

Grind all the ingredients for the chatni with water to a fine paste. Give "Vaghar" with mustard seeds, ulad dal, red chilli, curry leaves.

Note: Fresh coconut is what makes the chatni tastes good.

Type 2

Ingredients:

1/2 of fresh coconut
11/2 tsp ulad dal.
Red chilli to taste
2 big tomatoes cut in to pieces

Method:

In a pan put little oil, add ulad dal, when it turns red, add red chilli, tomato. Roast for 5-7 mts until the raw smell of tomato in gone. Let it cool. Grind everything together with coconut & 3-5 inches of tamarind & salt to fine paste. Give "Vaghar" with mustard seeds, Jeera, red chilli & curry leaves.
