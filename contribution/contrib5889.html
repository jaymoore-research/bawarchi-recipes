Simple Veggie Noodles

Ingredients

Maggie noodles - 1 double pack (with taste maker)
Onion - 1 (cut into small pieces)
Spring onion - 1 bunch
Carrot - 2
Capsicum - 1
Beans - 15
Coriander leaves - 1 bunch (cut into small pieces)
chillie powder - 1 tsp
Taste maker(how muchever is given along with a pack)
Oil - 2 tbsp
Salt to taste

Method

  1. Cut all the vegetables into thin long pieces and keep it aside.
  2. Then take a pan,put oil and heat it for a min.
  3. In high flame add onion and fry till brown.
  4. Then add carrots and fry for 2 mins(high flame).
  5. Add beans and fry for 2 mins(high flame).
  6. Add capsicum and spring onion and fry for 2 mins in high flame.
  7. Then at last add coriander leaves, salt, chilli powder and taste maker, mix well and fry for a min and keep it aside.
  8. TakeĀaĀpan and put two cups of water and put the noodles.
  9. Allow it to boil for 5-8 mins in medium flame or till it gets cooked.
  10. Then add all the fried vegetables to it.
  11. Mix everything well in medium flame for 2 mins.Keep it aside and serve hot. Ā

Note: Very little saltĀshould be added since the tastemaker contains salt in it.You can add 1/2 tsp of salt if you wantĀwhen you mix vegetables and noodles after you taste and see.

Cooking time: 20 mins.
Serves: 2-3 servings Ā
