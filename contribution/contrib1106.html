Methi Ki Poori

Ingredients

Fresh Methi (1 measure)
Fresh Aata/wheat flour (1.5 measure)
Dhania powder(2 teaspoons)
Chilli powder(1.5 teaspoons)
Salt (to taste)
Jeera (Cumin seeds) - 2 teaspoons
Hing (asafoetida) - a pinch
Curds (or buttermilk) - as needed

Method

  1. Cut Methi leaves finely and wash it thoroughly.
  2. Mix rest of the ingredients (all masalas and aata)
  3. Knead the dough with butter milk or yogurt mixed with little water as usual for any roti.
  4. Keep it aside for 10 mnts.
  5. Roll it into small balls.
  6. Prepare rotis of circular dimensions and put on a hot pan applying oil on both sides.

The Methi Pooris should be fairly thin and the best accompaniments are Meetha (sweet) achar (pickle) and dahi (yogurt).
