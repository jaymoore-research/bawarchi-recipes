Chakunder Chicken Pulao

Ingredients

3 kg Chukander [beetroot] to make yakhni [stock] to give flavour and dark pink colour to pulao
1-1/2 kg Chicken
2 cups Rice
1 big Onion
8 Cloves
6 Cinnamon sticks - 4 cm lenght each
8 blades Javitri [mace]
3 tbsp Oil
2-1/2 tsp Salt

Method:

  1. Peel and wash beetroots, slice them and quarter each slice. Put into pot, pour just enough water to cover the beats, cook under cover 20 min.
  2. Remove beets from the dark pink water and use them in another dish for example; mithe samose stuffed with sweet beetroot halva, or paratha stuffed with beetroot masala, or beetroot curry.
  3. Remove bones from chicken, cut chicken into small pieces, put them into dark pink water, boil under cover 15 min. In another pot fry sliced onion on oil.
  4. Before making pulao of course rice sould be washed and soaked 2 hr in cold water and drained just before using.
  5. Remove chicken from pink water add to onion add masala, salt, measure 3 1/2 cups of the pink water, add and bring to boil. Throw in rice, wait till boils,cover, lower the heat, simmer 20 min. If you dont have 3 1/2 cups pink stock add normal water to the required amount.
