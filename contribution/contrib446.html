Puri Peni

Method

Puri's that have been left-over usually go un to the dustbin the next day. Try this. Add badam, pista, elachi & a pinch of saffron to milk and boil it to thick consistency. Add as much sugar as you would like. Place the puri's one by one and pour this milk over it. Your delicious peni's are ready.
