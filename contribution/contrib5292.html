Surprise Sandwich

Ingredients

Bread slices: 2
Patak's Chili Pickle: few spoons

Method:

  1. Place the bread horizontally on the table.
  2. Apply chili pickles evenly on one slice including the ends.
  3. Place second slice of bread on top of the first.
  4. This step is optional, cut the sandwich diagonally into 2 pieces.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
