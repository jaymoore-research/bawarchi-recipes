Rava Kesari

Ingredients

1. Rava - one tumbler
2. Sugar-one tumbler
3. Water- two and half tumblers
4. Kesari Yellow Colour
5. Ghee-one cup
6. Cashewnuts for garnishing

Method:

  1. Fry rava in 1 tsp of ghee till slightly brown.
  2. Heat water.
  3. Add boiling water to rava and stir for about 2 minutes.
  4. Mix a pinch if kesari yellow in water and add it to the above mixture.
  5. Add sugar and keep stirring and add the ghee little by little until the mixture doesn't stick to the sides of the pan.
  6. Roast the cashewnuts and mix it with the above mixture and Kesari is ready to serve.
