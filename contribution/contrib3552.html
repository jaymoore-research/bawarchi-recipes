Tangy Paneer Dips

Ingredients

200 gms Paneer, grated
250 gms Potatoes, boiled, peeled and mashed
250 gms. Maida
1 tbsp of Suji rawa
5 tsps Lemon Juice
Paste of 4 Green Chillies and a small piece of Ginger
Salt to taste
Finely chopped Coriander leaves
Oil to fry

Method:

  1. Mix boiled potatoes, grated paneer, chillies-ginger paste, lemon juice, coriander leaves and salt. Make small balls and flatten them slightly.
  2. Make a thick batter of maida and rava add salt to taste. Rub in two spoons of hot oil.
  3. Dip the flattened paneer-potato mix in the batter and deep fry them in oil.

This should be had hot with Tomato Ketchup or Mint Chutney.
