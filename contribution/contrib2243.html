Pathrode

Ingredients

25 Arvi leaves (Colocaesia leaves)
1 cup Rice
1 tbsp Black Gram Daal
1 tsp Channa Daal
1/2 Coconut
15 Red Chillies
1 tsp Coriander seeds
1/2 tsp Cumin seeds
1/4 tsp Turmeric
2 tsp Sugar
Gooseberry sized Tamarind
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Soak rice, black gram daal and channa daal together for 2 hours.
  2. Grind coconut, red chillies, coriander seeds, cumin seeds, turmeric, sugar, salt, tamarind together to a fine paste with little water.
  3. Add soaked rice and daal mixture to the above paste and grind coarsely. The batter should be thick.
  4. Remove thick veins and wash the arvi leaves.
  5. Place biggest leaf on a paper or working surface with the stem side up. Take some batter in hand and spread on leaf (not too thick).
  6. Keep second leaf on top and spread the batter. Prepare in the same manner for 1 or 2 more leaves.
  7. Then fold on sides to the middle of the vein and roll like swiss roll. Similarly prepare rolls with all the leaves.
  8. In the meantime heat water in pressure cooker and steam these rolls immediately for 30 minutes with weight.
  9. It can be served along with butter

Variations with Pathrode

  • Pathrode Upama: Cut Pathrode into 1 inch square pieces. Heat oil and add mustard seeds, black gram daal, groundnuts and a few curry leaves as seasonings. When it splutters add cut pathrode pieces and fry a little (If you like sweet, then you can add 1 tsp sugar while frying ). Garnish with coconut and serve.
  • Patrode dosa : Cut the Pathrode rolls into 1 inch thick pieces. Heat tawa and apply oil. Place pathrode roll pieces one by one on tawa. Add 1tbsp oil on rolls and roast on both sides. The pieces will not stick together.
  • Pathrode bonda: Cut the Pathrode rolls into 1 inch thick pieces. Prepare bonda batter with 1 cup gramflour, 1tsp red chilli powder, salt to taste. Dip the apthrode roll pieces in batter and deep fry in oil.
