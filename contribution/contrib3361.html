Sambar

Ingredients

1. Tamarind- lime size ball, soaked in 3 cups of water.
2. Dania - 2 tsp.
3. Chana Dal - 2 tsp.
4. Red Chillies - 6-8
5. Coconut - 1/4 coconut
6. Onion - 1 chopped.
7. For vegetables in Sambar (Kashnam/thaan): little Onions or Drumsticks or Ladysfinger or Brinjal.
8. Tur dal(tuvaram paruppu) - 3/4 cup, boiled in pressure cooker.
9. Curry Leaves- 6-7 chopped.
10. Mustard- 1 tsp. for garnishing.

Method:

  1. Fry dania, chana dal, red chillies in 1 tsp. of cooking oil. Let it cool.
  2. Add coconut and the onion to the above and grind it to a paste in the mixie. (do not fry the onion and coconut)
  3. Boil the vegetables (desired vegetables) in tamarind water and salt (2 tsp).
  4. Add the boiled tur dal to the tamarind water.
  5. Add the ground paste to the tamarind water. Cook for 10 mins. and then add curry leaves for flavour.
  6. Let mustard sputter in 1 tsp. oil and add to sambar.

Serving ideas:

Can be served hot with Idlis, Dosas, Rice.
