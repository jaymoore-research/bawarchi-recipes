Rasam

Ingredients

Curry leaves 10-15
Corinder leaves a small bunch of 10 stems
Big Plum tomato 1
Oil 2 tbs
Mustard 1tbs
Jeera 1tbs
Rasam powder 2-3 tbs
Salt to taste

Method

  1. Grind curry leaves,corinder leaves, tomato,rasam powder and salt with water.
  2. Then in a kadai pour the oil and put musturd and jeera let it splutter then add the above grinded mixter.
  3. allow it to boil for 4 to 5 mins (add more water, salt, rasam powder). if necessary. Then your rasam is ready.

Goes very good with cooked rice with ghee and papad.
Serves five
