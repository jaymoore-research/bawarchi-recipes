Ingredients

1Āglass rice
2 glass milk
2 tsp ghee
1 glass sugar
little kesari melt in milk
kismis & cashews

Method

  1. First fry rice in ghee & pour milk & pressure cook for 3 whistle
  2. Now put sugar to this & keep stirring untill this melts & mixes well ( note: if dry put milk)
  3. Once the sugar mixes well Then put d kesari (melted in 1 tsp milk)
  4. Fry kismis & cashews in ghee & mix this with the above it tastes well when it is cooled
