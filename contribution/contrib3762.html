Vegetable Kurma

Ingredients

Onion 2
Garlic, ginger 3 flakes, 1 inch
tomato 2 small
frozen veg., carrot, peas, corn and beans qty depends

Method:

  1. Fry onion well in the oil and add garlic, ginger and finally put tomato. Saute well. Add red chilli powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder and salt. Now, pour some half and half cream milk. Stir it and allow it to boil for 15 mts. Wait till the yellowish orange colour comes.

  2. Pressure cook the veg., without water and dont put weight. Just 7 to 8 mts is enough. Add the veg., to the above boiled mixture with salt. Leave that for 10 mts. Finally, add the fried paneer to the above and keep aside. Serve hot with chapathi.

Plantain curry :

Plantain 2
Onion 1 med
Red chilli powder 1 tsp
salt depends
turmeric powder 1/2 tsp

For seasoning : Mustard 1 tsp
Urad dhal 1 tsp
Channa dhal 1 tsp optional
Curry leaves few
Asafoetida
Oil

Method:

  1. Take oil in a vanali and when it is hot, add all the seasoning items. Once it is done, add the onion and saute well. Wait till the brown colour comes.
  2. In the meantime, boil plantain with little water, salt and turmeric. After boiling, filter the water. Once the onion colour changes, add the plantain (either mashed or not mashed) and stir well till they mixes well.
  3. Put red chilli powder, more salt(if necessary). Once the raw smell goes, keep aside. Good side dish for Sambhar rice or Rasam rice.
