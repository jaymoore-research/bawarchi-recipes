Easy Mysorepak

Ingredients

Besan Flour 1 cup
Sugar 2 cup
Oil 2 cup

Method

  1. Mix 1 cup besan with 1 cup oil with lump and keep it aside.
  2. In a non stick kadai add two cup sugar with 1/2 Cup water bring to boil 1 thread consituency.
  3. Add the besan mixed oil to the sugar syrup and stir adding the remaining oil little by little till the mixture leave the side of the pan.
  4. Pour the mixture to the greased plate and cut it into desired shape.
