Vermicelli Kesari Bath

Ingredients

1 Tbsp Butter
1 cup Vermicelli
5 Cashews
4 Tbsp Sugar
3/4 Cardamoms
Orange colour (a pinch)

Method:

  1. In a heated pan add 1 Tsp of butter and fry the cashews and vermicelli until golden brown
  2. Add 3/4 cup of water to the fried vermicelli and cashews.
  3. Add a pinch of orange colour to it
  4. Add 4 Tbsp of sugar to it and boil until the vermicelli is cooked. (if the vermicelli is not cooked properly add little more water and keep it for boiling. Taste it, if you want more sugar you can add while it boils)
  5. Add powdered cardomom at the end.
