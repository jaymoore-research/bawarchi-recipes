Beetroot Chutney

Ingredients

1 medium sized beetroot
5, 6 hot red chillies (you can add more if you want more spicy)
3 tsp moongdaal
half tsp cumin
half tsp coriander (optional)
tamarind (lollipop size of tamarind)
oil to fry
salt to taste.

Method:

  1. Peel beetroot and wash it and cut into fine pieces so that it makes you easy to grind.
  2. Take khadai with two or three spoons of oil, fry beetroot pieces in it for 5 minutes till you gets nice flavour of it.
  3. Then in the same oil fry all the above ingredients.
  4. Then you grind all of them with soaked tamarind water and you can drop tamarind pulp in it.
  5. After you finish add salt to taste.
  6. If you want it still decorative you take two spoons of oil in khadai add cumin, mustard, garlic (crushed) curry leaves and coriander and pour all this to your chutney.

You can have this in rice and for idli, dosa and chapahtis.........
