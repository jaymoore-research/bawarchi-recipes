3-in-1 Mixed Vegetable

Ingredients

Bringal (Eggplant) 6 to 7 in nos.
Cauliflower 3 to 4 nods
Green pigeon peas (toor dana) 1/2 cup
Red chilli powder 2 tea spoon
Turmeric powder 1/2 tsps.
Dhania powder 1 tsps.
Roasted Groundnut powder 2 tsps.
Ginger 1/2 tsps. (paste)
Garlic 1/2 tea sps.(paste)
Oil 1 tbsps
Musterd seeds 1/2 tsps.
Cumin seeds 1/2 tsps.
Salt As per your taste

Method:

  1. First boil Green pigeon peas (toor dana green) for 5 min. Then heat 1 table sps. oil in a pan or kadai, Then put mustard & cumin seeds, allow them to spultter for a while, then add ginger & garlic.
  2. Put Bringal (eggplant), Cauliflower, Boiled green pigeon peas allow them to cook for 6 to 7 min.
  3. Then add all masala in to it, and stir the mixture slowly. Then add salt in to it as per your taste. This vegetable is now ready to serve.
  4. Garnish it with green dhania.
  5. Serve the above vegetable with Chapati or Rice.
