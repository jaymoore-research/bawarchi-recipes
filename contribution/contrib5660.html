Plum Cake Museli

Ingredients

1 kg plumcake
1 \4 cups of boiled milk
1cup sugar
2 spoonful milk powder
1cup drinking chocolate
1 cup museli

Method

  1. Crush the plum cake into milk & beat well.
  2. Add sugar, drinking chocolate,milk powder to it. Stir well.
  3. Finally add museli & stir well.
  4. Keep in fridge. Serve it after food, preferably in icecream cups.
