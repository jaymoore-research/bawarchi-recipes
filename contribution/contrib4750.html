Bell Pepper Bonanza

Ingredients

1) Fresh Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - 6
2) Cooked Rice - 1 Cup
3) Grated Beetroot - 1/2 Cup
4) Chopped Onions - 1/2 Cup
5) Chilli Powder - 1/4 Tsp
6) Salt - 1/2 Tsp
7) Jeera - 1 Tsp
8) Garam Masala - 1 Tsp
9) Oil - 2 Tbsp

Method:

Cut the stem of the Bell Pepper and make a small slit at the top. Remove the seeds from inside and make it hollow.

For the stuffing:

  1. Heat some oil in a kadai.
  2. Add Jeera and the chopped onions. Fry till the onions turn golden brown.
  3. Add the grated beetroot, chilli powder, salt, garam masala and saute for 2 mts.
  4. Add the cooked rice to the above ingredients.
  5. Stuff each Bell pepper with the beetroot rice prepared as above.
  6. Smear some salt all over the stuffed Bell Pepper and microwave for 5 to 10 mts or till the Bell Pepper is cooked well.
  7. Serve hot with onion raitha.
