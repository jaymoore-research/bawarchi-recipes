Chicken Lotpot

Ingredients

Chicken in bone - 1/2 Kg.
Boiled Egg - 2
Frozen Green Peas - 100 gm.
Onion - 300gm.
Ginger - 20 gms
Garlic - 20 gms
1 Tomato - (chopped or puree)
Coriander Leaves Kashmiri Chilli Powder - 10 gms
Garam Masala Powder
Turmeric Powder - 10 gms
Salt - 10 gms.

Method

  1. Grind onion, ginger, garlic and coriander leaves to paste.
  2. Heat frying pan then add mustard oil.
  3. After some time add the grinded paste. Fry the paste for some time.
  4. Add Salt, Turmeric Powder & Kashmiri Chilli Powder to the paste.
  5. Add Chicken and mixed it properly with paste. Cook the chicken for some time. When the chicken is half cooked add chopped tomato, frozen green peas & chopped boiled egg. Again cook for some time.
  6. For gravy add warm water. When the chicken is done add garam masala.
  7. Serve Chicken Lotpot with Bread.
