Fried Chicken Masala

Ingredients

Chicken 1 Kg
Onion 1 cup [cut in to strips]
Cooking oil for frying

2. Preparation of the masala paste

Chilli powder 3 to 4 tsp
Coriander powder 4 tsp
Turmeric powder 1 tsp
Black pepper 15 Nos
Cloves 6 Nos
Cardamom 2 Nos
Cinnamon 5 Small pieces
Shallots 10 to 12 Nos
Garlic 4 to5 cloves
Ginger 2" piece.
Fennel 3/4 tsp
Cumin seeds 1/4 tsp
Curry leaves 2 or 3 stems
Salt to taste

3. To saute

Onions 2 medium sized finely chopped
Green chillies 4 slit in the middle
Tomatoes 1.1/2 cup finely chopped
Coriander leaves chopped for garnishing

Method

  1. Cut the chicken into pieces, wash them and keep aside.
  2. Grind the 2nd items together by adding salt in to a fine paste and divide the paste in to two equal portions.
  3. Take one porion of the paste and marinate the chicken and keep aside for 1 hour.
  4. Fry 1 cup of onions in oil till golden brown and remove from the oil. In the same oil fry the chicken pieces and when it is half fried take it out.
  5. In the remaining oil saute the onions and when it is done add the green chillies and chopped tomatoes. Stir for 2 minutes. Then add the other portion of the masala paste to it and stir till oil gets separated. Finally add the fried onions, chicken pieces and 1/2 cup of water and cook till the chicken becomes tender.
  6. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with Chappathi or Rice.

Making time: 30 minutes
Makes: 6 servings.
