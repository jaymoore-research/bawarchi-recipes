Brinjal and Peas curry

Ingredients

Brinjal 1 pound
Fresh Green peas 1/2 cup
Garlic 5 flakes
Dhaniya powder 3 tbs
Fresh grated coconut or coconut powder 6 tbs
Chilli powder 1 tbs
Salt as per requirement
Oil

Method:

  1. Take oil in a frying pan. You can either grind the garlic into a paste or cut them into thin slices.
  2. Add the garlic paste to the oil and fry for few minutes, then to this add green peas and allow them to fry.
  3. Then add the brinjals to the above and cover the lid and cook till the brinjals are soft.
  4. When the brinjals are soft add dhaniya powder, chilli powder, salt and grated coconut to it and cook it on high flame for few minutes.

Serve hot with rice and ghee. Enjoy!
