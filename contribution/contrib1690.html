Truffle

Ingredients

There are two variations to this recipe, vanilla or chocolate

Two packages of Chocolate pudding (example, fat free Jello)
Plain Chocolate cake
4 cups (1 container) Whipped Cream (example, Cool Whip)
Strawberries (sliced in fourths) (you can substitute any fruit you like)
Carmel bits (example, a Heath chocolate bar crushed)

Method:

  1. Get a tall container that is high enough to stack a couple of layers (like a casarole dish)
  2. Take the chocolate cake and slice several layers thru' the middle. In a one layer cake -slice three layers.
  3. Place the first layer on the bottom of the pan then smear a generous amount of pudding, then spoon on a layer of whipped cream, then a layer of strawberries. Do this till all the layers of cake are done.
  4. On the top layer, smear the pudding then the whipped cream and then sprinkle it with the crushed carmel bits and add two or three strawberries on top.
  5. Put in fridge to chill.

This desert is heavenly to taste and is very easy on the hips.
