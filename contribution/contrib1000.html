Raw Banana Vada

Ingredients

1. 4 medium sized raw bananas.
2. 1 cup soaked raw channa dal.
3. Small piece of ginger.
4. Finely chopped onions.
5. Coriander.
6. Oil to deep fry.
7. 3-4 green chillies.
8. Salt to taste.
9. 2 spoons of rice flour.

Method :

  1. Peel and cook the raw bananas in a pressure cooker. Mash and keep aside.
  2. Grind the soaked channadal, ginger, green chillies into a fine paste without adding much water. Add to this finely chopped onions and the mashed banana paste, 2 spoons of rice flour, salt, coriander. Mix well, shape in the form of cutlets and deep fry them till golden brown.
  3. Serve hot with tomato ketchup or any chutney.
