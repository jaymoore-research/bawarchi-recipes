I am Mrs. Latha Venkatadri from Thane. I would like to contribute to bawarchi the below mentioned Kerala dish which is simple and delicious.

E-mail : trvenkat@bgl.vsnl.net.in

Molaghashyam

Ingredients

White Pumpkin 1/4 kg
Chenai (suran) 200 gms
Beans 100 gms
Drumstick 1 no.
Raw Banana 1 no.(small)
Green Chillies 5 in no.
Pepper Corns 5/6 grated
Coconut Oil 5/6 spoons
Moong dhall 50 gms.
Karuvepilai a few leaves.

Method:

  1. Cut the above vegetables in square shape. Wash them well.
  2. Pressurecook moong dhall, vegetables, green chillies etc together. Once it is cooked, allow it to boil on the stove for 5 min mixing everything together. Remove from fire and add coconut oil and kadipatha leaves (karuveppilai).

The above dish may be had with hot rice and easy to prepare. Try and enjoy.
