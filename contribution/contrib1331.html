Spinach Rice

Ingredients

1 cup cooked rice
Spinach (frozen preffered)
1 cup green Peas
1 cup chopped Onions
Green Chillies
Corriander leaves (can also be substituted with dhaniya powder)
Ginger
Ghee and Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Pour ghee into a vessel quite generously.
  2. Cook fry the onions and then add the spinach and the peas with salt and let it cook for a while.
  3. Prepare a liquid paste of green chillies, corriander and ginger. Add the paste to the cooked spinach and later mix it with the rice. While serving it can be decorated with cashew nuts fried in ghee.
