Spicy Egg Kurma

Ingredients

Egg : 4
Tomato: 2 big
Onion : 1 big
Ginger garlic paste 2 tsp
Garam Masala 1 1/2 tsp
Green Chillies 5
Cilantro leaves 1/2 bunch
Oil, Mustard seeds and Curry Leaves for seasoning

Method:

  1. Take a pan pour little oil and do the seasoning with the mustard seeds and curry leaves.
  2. To this add chopped onions, chillies and fry till brown.
  3. To the above add the chopped tomatos, ginger garlic paste, salt, garam masala (sambar powder if preferred).
  4. After the tomatoes are finely cooked, in the pan itself make some space for the eggs. Break the eggs one by one without breaking the yolk, one should not touch the other.
  5. After some time turn it to the other side and make sure both the sides of the eggs are cooked. In the end add the chopped cilantro and ready for eating. It goes very well with plain rice or chappati.
