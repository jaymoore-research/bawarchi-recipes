Veggie Pancake

Ingredients

1. 1/2 cup soaked (for about 10 minutes) Poha.
2. 4 tablespoons Maida.
3. 1 Tomato cut into small pieces.
4. 3 tablespoons Green Peas.
5. Green Mirch Slices, Salt, Ginger Garlic paste, Cumin seeds.

Method:

  1. Making the real thing is very simple. Mix all the above ingredients well. If needed mix very little water to the ingredients so that it mixes well.
  2. Pour the mixture 1/2 inch thick in a well greased pan with oil. Cook for 10 minutes in low heat with lid covered on the pan.
  3. Reverse the pancake and cook for 5 minutes with lid on it.
  4. Hope you relish and enjoy it for the weekend.
