Radish Chutney

Ingredients

Medium radish - 3
Hot Green Chillies - 3 or 4
Garlic - 1 clove (if u r Garlic lover)
Tamarind and Salt - according to taste

Seasoning:

Oil - 1/2 tablespoon
Dry Red Chilly - 1 or 2
Bengal gram - 1/2 teaspoon
Split Black Gram - 1/2 teaspoon
Mustard seeds - 1/4 teaspoon
Cumin seeds - 1/4 teaspoon
Curry leaves

Method:

  1. Fry green chillies in 1/2 tablespoon hot oil for 3 minutes and take them out of the oil
  2. Cut radish into pieces so that it will be easy to grind. With little water grind radish pieces, fried green chillies, garlic, tamarind and salt until the radish and chillies are finely grinded in to a paste.
  3. Fry all the ingredients for seasoning in oil until the cumin seeds pop and add them to the grinded radish mix.

You can serve this with Rice, Chapati or South Indian Rice Roti.
