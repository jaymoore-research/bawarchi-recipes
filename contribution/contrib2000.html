Palak Chicken

Ingredients

Chicken 2 lbs, around 6 pcs
1 bundle Spinach
2 Medium Tomatoes
Onion Paste (1 large Onion)
Garlic Paste (6-8 flakes garlic)
4-5 Green chillies
2 tsp Turmeric Powder
2 tbsp Coriander Powder
2 tsp Chana Masala Powder
Salt
Sugar
Oil for cooking.

Method:

  1. Separate the palak leaves from the stalk and wash them
  2. Heat a pan and add palak leaves to it. Cook till the palak becomes soft. After palak is cooked, cool the mixture and blend it till it becomes a paste.
  3. Fry the onions and Garlic paste in oil till light brown, add the chicken peices some salt and cook till chicken is half done.
  4. Add some turmeric powder, coriander powder, chana masala powder and cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Add the palak paste and stir well.
  6. Make a puree of the tomatoes in a blender and add the green chillies in the blender too.
  7. Add the tomato-chillies paste to the palak- chicken mixture. Stir Well.
  8. Cover and cook for about 15-20 minutes
  9. Serve Hot with Rice or Rotis.
