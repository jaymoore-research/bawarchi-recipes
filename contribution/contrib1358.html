Quick Dhokla

Ingredients

1 cup Besan
1 cup Rice flour
3-4 Garlic cloves
2 Green Pepper
Sugar and Salt to taste
1 tea sp. Eno fruit salt
1/2 tea sp. Turmeric powder

To Garnish:

Finely chopped Cilantro
Fresh grated Coconut
Oil and Mustard seasoning.

Method:

  1. Mix besan and rice flour together.
  2. And Turmeric powder, salt and sugar.
  3. Blend ginger and garlic to a paste and add to the flour mixture.
  4. Add water to form a batter.
  5. Boil water in the cooker.
  6. Grease a container with a drop of oil pour the batter and now add one tea spoon 'Eno fruit salt.
  7. Close the lid of the cooker.
  8. Remove the top weight of the cooker. Pressure cook for ten minutes.
  9. Cool it and garnish with Cilantro, fresh coconut.
  10. Sprinkle oil and mustard seasoning on the top.

Serve hot with chutney. Total time 20-25 min
