Channa Vadas

Ingredients

1 cup Kabuli Channa soaked overnight
Green and red chillies - as per taste
salt
Finely Chopped Spring onion leaves - 1 - 1 1/2 cups

Method:

  1. Grind the first three ingredients to a not-very-soft consistency.
  2. Add the chopped greens and mix well
  3. Make vadas and deep fry till golden brown. Make sure the flame is not too hot - otherwise the vadas may not be cooked inside - but too brown on the outer side. Enjoy hot!!!
