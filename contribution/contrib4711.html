Wheat Flour Halwa

Ingredients

Wheat Flour (used for chappaties) - 1 cup
Brown Sugar (Dark Brown) - 1 cup
Butter - 1/2 stick.
Nuts (optional)

Method:

  1. Take a microwaveble container and put in 1 tsp of butter and the broken nuts and microwave it for 1 min. Remove the nuts and keep aside.
  2. Put the wheat flour and 1 tsp of butter and roast it in the microwave for 1 minute stirring it once in between.
  3. Mix 4 cups of water with 1 cup of Brown sugar in another container. Once the sugar is fully dissolved pour it over the roasted wheat flour stirring continuosly without forming lumps.
  4. Cover and cook it in the microwave for 4 mins. Stir it well and put the remaining butter in it and sprinkle with 2 tsps of water if the mixture seems very dry. Cover and cook it well in the microwave for another 5 minutes.
  5. Remove from the microwave and garnish it with the roasted nuts. Cooking time will vary with the power of the microwave. The above given time is for High powered microwave.
