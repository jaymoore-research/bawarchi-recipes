Bitter Gourd Masala Curry

Ingredients

Bitter gourd - 1/2 kg
Tomatoes - 2 or 3
Big onion - 1
Sour curds -1/4 cups
Turmeric powder -1/2 tsp.
Mustard seeds -1/2 tsp.
Garam masala powder -1/2 tsp.
Chilli Powder - tsp.
Ginger - 1inch piece
Coconut - 1/4 cup scraped
Sugar - 2tsp.
Oil -2 tsps.
Salt - as required.

Method:

  1. Cut onion, Mince ginger, chop tomatoes and keep aside. Cut Bitter gourd into small pieces, apply salt to the pieces and keep for half an hour.
  2. Grind coconut alone separately.
  3. Heat Oil in a pan tamper mustard seeds and broken red chillies then add onions and fry till transparent. Add ginger and chopped tomatoes, continue to fry till the skin of tomato comes out.
  4. Drain water if any, in the bitter gourd and fry constantly stirring for a few minutes.
  5. Add turmeric powder, chilli powder and beaten curds. When changes its colour add sugar, salt and enough water to cover the pieces.
  6. Close with a lid reduce flame and cook till the gourd becomes soft, when the gravy is thick add ground mixture of coconut, boil for one minute, remove from fire.
  7. Garnish with curry leaves and serve hot.

Varation: Tarmarind extract can be substituted in place of curds and jaggery instead of sugar.
Note : To remove bitterness from gourd soak the pieces for few minutes in rice water (water in which rice is washed) and follow the procedure.
