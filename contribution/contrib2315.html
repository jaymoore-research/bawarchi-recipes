Dosa

Ingredients

1 cup Urid Dal.
1 1/2 cup White Rice.
1 1/2 cup Boiled Rice powder.

Method:

  1. We should mix all the three items above and soak them in water for about 4 hours.
  2. Then drain the water and grind them till they become smooth.
  3. Then add some quantity of salt.
  4. Make and will get a dozen of paper dosa.
  5. Serve them HOT HOT.

Eat and enjoy.
