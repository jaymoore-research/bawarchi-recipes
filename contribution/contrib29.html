Vengaya   Thugayal (Onion Chutney)

Ingredients :

3 med. sized Onion
2 tsp. Urad Dal
2 tsp. Chana Dal
4 Dry Red Pepper
1/2 tsp. Imli (tamarind) paste
2 table spoon Oil

Method

  1. Fry the dals and the red pepper in 1/2 tsp oil until it gets roasted and keep it aside.
  2. Cut the onion into little pieces and bhoono (simmer) in the remaining oil until its wilted.
    Grind the roasted dals, onion, tamarind paste, and salt (to taste) into a paste
  3. Serve it with puri, chappathi, rice, dosa and idli.
This chutney could be made with other vegetables like egg plant, radish, cabbage etc.
