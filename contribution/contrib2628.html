Ghavachi Keer (Wheat Kheer)

Ingredients

2 cups whole Wheat
2 cups Jaggery
2-3 cups Milk
2-3 table spoons Cashews
1 tea spoon Cardamom Powder.
1 table spoon Coconut (either fresh or desiccated)
1 table spoon soaked and drained Poppy Seeds in water.
2-3 table spoons Butter/Ghee.

Method :

  1. Soak wheat Atleast for 2-3 nights in water. Leave the container you soak in the refrigerator.
  2. Drain water and coarsely powder the soaked wheat so that you can see a mixture of both grain and the powder. Do not add water or milk in it while powdering, it will easily be done because of being soaked for 2-3 days.
  3. Add a little butter in a container on a low flame and add the powdered wheat and stir it, take care that it doesn't stick to the bottom of the container.
  4. Then add milk and let it cook on low flame for 6-8 minutes.
  5. Meanwhile grate or break jaggery into pieces so that it measures to 2 cups. Add jaggery to the milk and wheat grains that is cooking. Let it cook for 15-20 minutes with constant stirring. Also add powdered cardamom and soaked- drained poppy seeds along with cashews and coconut and let it simmer on low flame. Remember to exhaust the 2-3 table spoons of butter during the entire process. You can remove it from the flame when you see butter floating on the top and nice aroma emanating.
  6. Serve it hot with some ghee on the top.

Yes, this dish is loaded with butter and is considered a delicacy. Hence you need to throw calories out of the window when eating this exquisitely prepared dish. Also you cannot replace jaggery with sugar because it spoils the taste.
