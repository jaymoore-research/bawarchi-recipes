Lemon Rice Vermicelli (RiceNoodles)

Ingredients

Rice Vermicelli (which are available in super markets)
Green Chillies chopped 3-4
Curry leaves
Coriander leaves
Musterd seeds 1 tbsp
Cummin seeds 1 tbsp
Black gram (Urid dhal) 1 tbsp
Channa Dhal 1 tbsp
Peanuts 2 tbsp
Turmeric 1 Tsp
Oil 5-6 tbsp
Lemon Juice 3- 4 tbsp (Or according to your taste)
Salt

Method:

  1. Take the rice vermicelli into a bowl and add boiling water to it and cover it with a lid, allow it to soak for 15 -20 minutes (Or untill the vermicelli has absorbed the water, do not leave for long)
  2. Drain the water by putting vermicelli into a seive and leave it for a while in the seive untill the water has completely drained.
  3. Add oil into a wok, when the oil is hot add musterd, cummin, urid channa dhal and peanuts. Let it turn brown, then add turmeric, coriander curry leaves and green chillies.
  4. Turn down the heat and add vermicelli salt and lemon juice. Mix it well and enjoy eating.
