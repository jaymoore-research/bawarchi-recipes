Butter Chicken

Ingredients

Chicken pieces- 1 kg
Tomato- 250gm.
Salt-1 Teaspoon.
Soya sauce- 1 Tablespoon.
Lime juice-1 Tablespoon.
Salted butter-1/4 Cup +1 Tablespoon.
Sugar-3/4 Teaspoon.
Garlic- 2 Tablespoon.
Ginger- 2 Tablespoon.
Chilly powder- 2 Tablespoon.

Method:

  1. Grind together garlic, ginger and chilly powder with Soya sauce. In this add salt and limejuice and mix well. Marinate chicken in this mixture for one hour. Then steam this chicken till done [or you can microwave for 3 minutes on medium power.
  2. Blend tomatoes in a mixer with little water and strain. Separate the cooked chicken pieces from the marinade.
  3. Heat 1/4-cup butter in a frying pan and put the chicken pieces in it and fry till golden brown on both sides. Add the marinade, tomato juice and sugar in this chicken. When the gravy thickens add the rest of the butter and serve immediately with Nan or Paratha.
