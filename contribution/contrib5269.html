Colaccasia Roast

Ingredients

Colaccsaia - 1/2 kg
salt - to taste
hing - to taste
chilli powder - to taste
turmeric powder - 1 tsp
besan - 2 tbsps
oil

Method :

  1. Steam the colaccasia in a cooker without a whistle till it is cooked and then peel the skin.
  2. Alternatively you can peel the skin and also steam it but you will lose all the nutrition in it.
  3. Cool it for some time and then cut into cubes.
  4. In a plate, take salt, hing, chilli powder, turmeric powder and besan and mix the powders well.
  5. Sprinkle this on top of the vegetable and leave aside for 10 minutes.
  6. You can retain little of this powder to add later on.
  7. In a non stick pan take oil, add rai and urad dal and splutter.
  8. Add these vegetables and keep on low gas and almost fry it stirring in between.
  9. Lastly you can add the leftover powder (optional) and stir for 5 mins more.
  10. You can see that the vegetable has been coated with the powder and has been roasted well as a result the slippery taste won't exist.
  11. Please try this recipe and give my feedback.

    Note : You can add any of your other masala powders like garam masala, dhania jeera powder etc depending on your taste along with the other dry powders.
