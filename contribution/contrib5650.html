Peach Halwa

Ingredients

Peach Tin -400g , cut into 1/2 inch cubes
Milk Powder-2 cups
Sugar-1/2 cup
Ghee-3-4tbls
Nestle cream -150g
Kesar
Elaichi
1 inch piece of cinnamon
4-5 almonds sliced for garnishing

Method

  1. In a pan mix milk powder, sugar ,cinnamon and ghee and cook on a low heat
  2. keep stirring for about 10 to 15 mins.
  3. Then add cream, kesar and elaichi then stir for a few mins
  4. Add the peaches, let it cook for another 5-10 mins on a low heat.
  5. Serve warm
  6. Garnish with the almond.
