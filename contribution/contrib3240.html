Kozukattai

Ingredients

1 1/2 cups Rice Flour
Required amount of Water
1 cup Jaggery
1 cup grated Coconut
Powdered Elaichi
1 tsp Oil

Method:

  1. Mix the rice flour with water and see that there are no lumps.
  2. In a kadai, pour this mix along with the oil. Keep mixing it continuously for 2 minutes. Add more oil if the dough sticks to the kadai.
  3. Remove from fire and after it cools a little, knead the dough well with your hands.
  4. In another kadai, pour a little water and add the jaggery. After the jaggery begins to boil, add the grated coconut and powdered elaichi. Keep on stirring this mixture for 2 minutes. Do not allow the mixture to become too hard.
  5. Take a small ball of dough and make a small cup with your fingers. Put a little of jaggery-coconut mixture and close the cup by pulling the dough slowly over the mixture and making a small pointed shape on top.
  6. Pressure cook these Kozukattais for about 8 minutes in a pressure cooker.

I hope you will enjoy making as much as you enjoy eating Kozukattai.
