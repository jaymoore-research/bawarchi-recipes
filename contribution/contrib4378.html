Ommadi

Ingredients

1 loaf of Sliced Bread.
2 liters of Milk
2 table spoons of Sogi
2 table spoons of Rose Water
2 table spoons of Sugar
1 tea spoon vanilla Essence
250 gms Butter
2 table spoons Corn Flour
340 gms Cream.

For Sugar syrup

1 cup Sugar
10 small Cardamoms
Pista + Badam for dressing

Method:

  1. Remove corners of the bread all the brown edges, use white only slices. Cut diagonally to look as triangles.
  2. Add water to corn-flour and make a thin paste.
  3. Add Soji, Rose Water, Sugar & vanilla essence to the Milk and boil for 15 minutes on medium flame.
  4. Then add Butter & Corn-flour paste to the mixture and boil again for another 10 minutes stirring occassionally. Put off the flame and add cream to the mixture and mix it throughly with egg-beater till it is completely dissolved. Keep it on the side to cool.
  5. Add 2 cups of water to 1 cup of sugar and boil with cardamoms for 10 mins. Then remove & keep it on the side.
  6. Take a serving bowl with flat base, Pour half of the milk mixture in it, then dip the slices into the sugar syrup, and add it to the mixture so that it makes a layer on top, keep repeating till all the slices are exhauted, add the balance mixture from the top evenly.
  7. Add Badam & Pista Slices for dressing and keep refrigerated till it is cold enough. Then serve in small bowls. It will taste delicious.
