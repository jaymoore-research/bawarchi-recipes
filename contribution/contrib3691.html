Chicken In Gravy

Ingredients

Chicken- 1/2 kg
Onion-1 medium size (finely chopped)
Tomatoes-2 medium size (finely diced)
Yogurt- 2tbspn
Ginger-Garlic paste (fresh)- 2tspn
Turmeric powder- 1 tspn
Red Chilli powder- 1 1/2 tspn
Coriander powder- 1 tspn
Garam Masala powder-1 1/2 tspn
Salt-as per taste
Oil- 2-3 tspn
Coriander leaves- 1 tbspn (finely chopped)

Method:

  1. Clean, wash the chicken. Take a bowl mix yogurt, 1/2 tspn of turmeric, red chilli, salt, ginger-garlic paste & garam masala powder. Coat the chicken with this marinade. Let it marinate for 1 hour.
  2. Heat oil in a pressure cooker saute onions to golden brown then add remaining ginger-garlic paste stir for a second then add marinated chicken to it fry for 1 min. Add all powder masalas to the chicken stir add tomatoes, salt, half glass of water pressure cook it for 1 whistle.
  3. Garnish with the coriander leaves and serve hot.
