Ingredients

1/2 -3/4 cup (or as u please) of veg. like
raw plantain, carrot, beans, peas, potato,
drumstick, yam..(any of these of your choice,
but more the variety, tastierthe avial)

1 cup curd,
1 cup fresh grated coconut,
1 1/2 tsp cumin seeds,
green chillies(as per spice reqd.),
salt(as per taste),
2 tsp raw rice soaked in water,
3-4 tbsp coconut oil,
few sprigs of curry leaves.

Method:

  1. Cut all veg into even sized, 2" pieces.
  2. Pressure cook all the cut veg together, with sufficient amount of water directly in the cooker body, till 2 whistles.
  3. Make a paste of coconut, cumin seeds, green chillies, soaked rice and salt.
  4. When the veg are cooked remove the excess water and retain just little.
  5. Add the ground paste.And cook on medium flame till raw smell is lost.
  6. Add curd. Mix well.
  7. Add curry leaves. And switch of the flame.
  8. Heat coconut oil till warm and add on top of avial.Cover.

Happy Cooking!
