Parpu Payasam

Ingredients

100 gms Channa Dal
150 gms Jaggery
25 gms Raw Rice
50 gms grated Coconut
1/4 litre Milk
2 Cardamoms
Cashewnuts for seasoning
1 teaspoon Ghee

Method

  1. Wash and Soak Channa Dal for about 1 hr in water.
  2. Wash Rice (no need to soak it) and grind it in the mixie along with coconut and cardamom.
  3. Cook Channa Dal on a Medium Flame. When Channa Dal is half cooked, add the rice-coconut-cardamom mixture. Let the mixture cook till the channa Dal becomes soft. Do not over cook the Dal.
  4. Powder the jaggery and add it to the mixture and keep stirring till the mixture attains a semi solid consistency.
  5. Pour the Milk over the moisture and wait till it just begins to boil.
  6. Remove from flame and add Cashewnuts fried in Ghee.

The Payasam is now ready to eat. This dish can be eaten either hot or cold. Either way it tastes good. Channa Dal can be substituted by Moong Dal.

Channa Dal - gives the KadalaParapu (Channa Dal in Tamil) Payasam
Moong Dal - gives the Pytamparapu (Moong Dal in Tamil) Payasam.
