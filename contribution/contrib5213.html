Low calorie veggie mix

Ingredients

Carrots : 2 medium
Peas : 100 gms
Beans : 100 gms (Cut into 2 inch pieces)
Potatoes : 1 large
Onion : 1 large
Ginger-garlic paste : 1 tbsp
Vinegar : 1 tbsp
Tandoori masala (can use tandoori chicken masala)
Tabasco (can use tomato chilli ketchup)
Salt, pepper and very little oil

Method :

  1. Mix cut vegetables with ginger garlic paste, salt and vinegar and soak overnight in refrigerator.
  2. Next day, sprinkle oil on a non-stick pan and gently toss the mixture for 2-3 mins on high flame.
  3. Add any tandoori masala to the mixture and mix well. Instead of tandoori, you may use a mixture of garam masala, coriander powder, onion powder, chilli powder and garlic flakes.
  4. Grease aluminum foil with oil/butter and place the mixture . Sprinkle with pepper and little salt(to taste). Enclose the mixture by folding in four corners of the sheet.
  5. Preheat oven to 350. Bake for 40 mins. Before taking out pour a little tabasco or ketchup.

Serves 2-3 .
