Green Peas Halwa

Ingredients

Green Peas 250 gms
Guava 100 gms
Sugar 200gms
Milk 200 ml
Ghee 100 gms
Badham, Pistha chopped : 1cup
Cardamom : 5

Method:

  1. Grind the green peas by adding little water in a mixie. Saute the paste in ghee for 15 minutes.
  2. Add guava and stir wellfor 10 minutes. Keep the gas in sim. Add sugar. Again stir it till the ghee comes out. Again pour over a greased plate.
  3. Then garnish with chopped nuts. Add cardamom powder. When it cools cut into pieces.
