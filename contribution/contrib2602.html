Sabudhana Khichdi

Ingredients

Sabudhana- 1 cup
Green Chillies- 5
Jeera- 1 tablespoon
Cumin seeds- 1 tablespoon
Corainder Leaves
Peanuts - 1 cup
Salt
Vegetable Oil- 3 tablespoons

Method:

  1. First soak sabhudhana for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat the vessel put 3 spoons of oil then put jeera, cumin seeds, red chillies and corainder leaves.
  3. Remove the sabhudhana from water by using filter after that put the soaked sabhudhana in the above hot vessel, slowly mix them and keep the lid putting it under low heat for 15 to 20 min.
  4. Then make peanuts into powder.
  5. Mix the sabhudhana for every 5 min.
  6. Put the peanut powder on it and mix it properly & eat it when it is warm preferably.
