Goolar Kabab

Ingredients

For outer Shell:

Boiled Potatoes 1 lb.(mashed)
Raw Banana (plantains) 6 nos (pealed, boiled and mashed)
Mustard Oil 4 tbsp. (smoke and bring to room temp.)
Besan (gramflour) 6-7 tbsp.(Brown On slow fire)
Saunf Powder 1 tbsp
coriander powder 1 tbsp
Red Chilli powder 1 tsp
Garam Masala 1 tsp
Salt to taste

Chutney Filling:

Amchur powder 3 tbsp
Sugar Powder 6 tbsp
Grated ginger 2 tbsp
Green Chilli paste 1 tbs
p Chopped Raisins 1 tbsp
Ground Mint 2 tbsp (can use dry mint also or grind some mint leaves without adding water)
Saunf powder 1 tbsp
garam masala 1 tbsp
Coriander Powder 1 tbsp
Salt to taste
Oil For Frying

Method:

  1. Mix well both banana and potatoes.
  2. Add besan, mustard oil, salt and all other spices for outer shell. Keep a side
  3. Mix all the ingredients for chutney. Do not use water.
  4. Take a small portion for outer shell and shape into a ball. Slightly flat it and stuff with little chutney and close it. Reshape into ball. Same way make all others too.
  5. Heat oil and deep fry till golden.

Serve hot with Tamarind Chutney.
