Sheer Khurma

Ingredients

Milk - 1 Litre
Almond - 15 Nos (sliced)
Pista - 15 Nos (sliced)
Dry Dates - 10 Nos (sliced)
Charoli - 10 Gms (Soak in water and remove skin)
White Khuskhus - 2 spoons
Roasted vermicille - 10 Gms
Cinnamon - 2 to 3 pcs
cardamon - 5 Nos
Sugar - as needed
Ghee - 2 spoon

Method:

  1. Fry the ghee, cinnamon & cardamon.
  2. When it is hot add milk and boil.
  3. Add ingredients one by one except Vermicelli.
  4. Boil till the milk is thick.
  5. Now add sugar and white Khuskhus and boil for 7 minutes.
  6. Now add vemicilli and boil for 2 minutes and serve.

Note: I don't know what is the name of Charoli in English. You can buy it in a dry fruit shop.
