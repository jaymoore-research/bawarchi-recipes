Badam Kheer

Ingredients

Almonds (Badam) - 100 gms
Milk - 1/2 litre
Sugar - 150 gms.
Boiled & chilled Milk - 1/2 litre

Method:

  1. Soak Almonds in hot water for an hour. Close it with a lid to reduce the time.
  2. Peel & grind to a smooth paste.
  3. Add 1/2 litre of milk & mix well so that you don't find any lumps at all.
  4. Add sugar & mix well.
  5. Boil the mixture till thick (very thick). Simmer it so that the mixture doesn't spill out & continue boiling till you get it really thick. It should take around 1/2 an hour. Stir it, as it's boiling.
  6. Remove & cool it. Then, refrigerate it.
  7. Then, add chilled milk with the Badam mixture & blend it for a while.
  8. Soak saffron in hot milk. Add it to the mixture & mix well.
  9. Also add rose water & Badam essence for additional flavour.

Pour in long attractive glasses while serving. It looks & tastes great, as if you're having something beyond your reach. Try it. Hope you like it.
