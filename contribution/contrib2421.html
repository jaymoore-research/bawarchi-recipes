Kootu

Ingredients

Coconut-half piece grated
Coriander seeds-2 tsp
Mustard seeds-1/4 tsp
Red chillies-5-6
Asafoetida-a pinch
Turmeric-1/2 tsp
Cummin seeds-1/2 tsp
Raw Rice-2 tsp (soaked)
Split Gram-1 cup

Vegetables:

Carrot, Cauliflower, Peas, Potato, French beans, Raw groundnut seeds (1/4cup)

Method

  1. Pressure cook the split gram and keep aside.
  2. Cook all the washed and diced vegetables.
  3. Grind all the above mentioned ingredients to a smooth paste and add to the vegetables and stir and also add the cooked split gram. Add the required amount of salt and mix well.
  4. After 10 minutes remove from fire and add garnishing of curry leaves and pinch of asafoetida in ghee.
  5. Serve hot with Rice or Chapatis.
