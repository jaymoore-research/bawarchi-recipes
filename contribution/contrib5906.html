Quick Dosakai Chutney

Ingredients

1 medium size dosakaya
1tea spoon salt
1teaspoon chilli powder
1tea spoon tamarind paste
little asafetida(Hing)
1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
1teaspoon oil.

Method

  1. Now cut the dosakaya into small pieces.
  2. To it add chilli powder, salt,Hing and tamarind paste and mix well.
  3. Then take a kadai and put the oil and when it becomes hot put the mustard seeds and when they start spluttering add to the chutney.
  4. It hardly takes 5 minutes to do it.
  5. Of course salt and tamarind can be increased or decreased to suit ones taste.
  6. It will last for 1 or 2 days.
  7. Hope you will enjoy it.
