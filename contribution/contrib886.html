Dal-Cheese Kofta Curry

Ingredients

For the kofta:

Chana dal (100 gms; soaked overnight)
Moong dal (100gms; soaked overnight)
Processed cheese (2-3 cubes)
Bread slices (2)
Ajwain
Cumin seeds
Coriander seeds
Black pepper powder, turmeric powder, asafoetida powder, chilli powder and salt to taste.
Oil to fry

For the gravy:

Tomatoes (1 kg, fully ripe)
Onions (1 large)
Green chillies (3, slit open)
Cloves (few)
Black peppercorns (few)
Bayleaves
Cumin seeds
Bada elaichi (1)
Cashewnuts, Almonds (optional)
Yogurt (1/2 cup, beaten well)
Garam Masala, Coriander powder, Jeera powder
Salt to taste
Oil to fry

Method :

For the kofta :

  1. Grind the dals, grate the cheese cubes and mix well.
  2. Crumble the bread slices and mix in.
  3. Add rest of the ingredients and knead into dough like consistency.
  4. Make oval balls of 1" size.
  5. Fry in hot oil. Keep aside.

For the gravy:

  1. Puree the tomatoes, onions and the dry fruits separately.
  2. In a wok, add oil. When hot, add cloves, bayleaves, cumin seeds, bada elaichi, peppercorns and the chillies.
  3. Add the onion paste.
  4. Fry till golden brown.
  5. Add the tomato puree.
  6. When the mixture comes to a boil, add all the masalas and salt.
  7. After it comes to a boil, add the dry fruit mixture.
  8. Let it cook over a slow flame for a few minutes.
  9. Add beaten curds and remove wok from flame immediately.
  10. Add the koftas.

Serve with hot chapatis or steamed rice. For best results, let the koftas soak in the gravy for at least an hour before serving. Please share this recipe with others for this is really yummy. I'm sure others who try it will really love it.
