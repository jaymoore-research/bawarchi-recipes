Halwa Puree

Ingredients

1 cup soji (rava)
1 cup water
1 cup sugar
1 cup wheat flour
Oil for deep frying
Water to make the dough

Method :

  1. Make the dough by mixing wheat flour and water.
  2. Knead the dough by mixing little bit oil and see that the must be soft.
  3. Keep this aside for 1/2 an hour.
  4. Boil 1 cup water with 1 cup sugar bring to boil and add soji by continuous stirring and allow the halwa to cook for about 5 min.
  5. Keep aside and allow to cool.
  6. Make small lumps of halwa out of this and keep aside.
  7. Take the dough and make small lumps and press with the palms into the thin small puree and stuff the halwa into the puree and cover the halwa with dough and take out the remaining dough.
  8. Press this into a puree with the palm on thick plastic paper or on foil and deep fry into the oil till it turns golden color. It is crispy and tastes good.
