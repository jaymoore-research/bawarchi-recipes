Bhuiya

Ingredients

Ulad Dal 2 cups
Rice 1/2 cup

For Stuffing:
Cashewnuts 1 tsp.
Coriander 3/4 cup
Coconut 1/2 cup
Red chilly 1/2 tsp.
Salt 1/2 tsp.
Green chilly 4-5
Ginger 1"
Curd 1/2 liter milk (Beat all the ingredients together)
Pepper 1 tsp.
Sugar 1 tsp.

For Chutney:
Tamarind lemon size
Jaggery lemon size
Cummin seed 1 tsp.
Garam Masal 1 tsp.
Salt 1tsp.

Method:

  1. Wash rice and ulad dal separately and soak for about 7-8 hrs.
  2. Grind them coarsely without water and salt.
  3. Grind chilies and salt separately.
  4. Scrape coconut, cut cashewnuts and charoli in small pieces. To the scraped coconut add ginger paste, coriander leaves (chopped finely), charoli and cashewnuts.
  5. Take a wet cloth or plastic sheet, put small portion of ground dal mix and spread it into a round. Then place stuffing in one side and cover it with other side to form a semicircle, slightly press it at the corner so that it sticks together.
  6. Deep fry it. Soak the fried bhuiya in water. Remove it and press it till water is removed. Put the bhuiya in curd. Prepare the rest of the dal mix in same way.

For Tamarind Cutney

  1. Soak tamarind for 1/2 an hour. Squeeze out a thick pulp from it.
  2. Add sugar or jaggery to the pulp.
  3. Bring this mix to a boil then add cumin seeds, garam masala and salt to it.
  4. Serve the bhuiyas with this sweet chutney.
