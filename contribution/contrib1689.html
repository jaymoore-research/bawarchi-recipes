Avakkai pickle

Ingredients

Mango (raw) : 2 Kgs
Salt Powder : 1/2 Kg
Chilli Powder : 1/2 Kg
Mustard Powder : 1/2 Kg
Gingely Oil : 1/2 Kg
Asafoetida : 100 gms

Method :

  1. Cut Mangoes into small pieces (but not very small) taking out the seed.
  2. Take a big vessel and a cup. With the ratio of 5 cups of Mango pieces: 3/4 cup salt : 3/4 cup chilli powder :3/4 cup mustard powder : 3/4 cup gingely oil and add the asafoetida till you get the smell of it.
  3. Mix all these together and shake well. Now transfer this to a strerilised bottle and close the lid tightly for one month. Take out the bottle everyday and shake it so that all ingredients get mixed well. After one is ready a mouth watering Avakkai pickle ready.
