Chawal ki Puri

Ingredients

1 bowl rice
3/4 bowl wheat flour
1tsp salt
1/2 tsp crushed jeera
1/2 tsp crushed cloves
1 tsp chilli powder.
1 tbsp oil
oil to fry
water for mixing

Method:

  1. Put rice in a mixing bowl add all the dry ingredients.
  2. Put 1 tbsp oil and mix.
  3. Add enough flour to hold the rice as too much flour will ruin the taste.
  4. Then add little by little water and make the dough slightly tighter than the chapati dough.
  5. Make round ball as big as chapati rounds.
  6. Roll into around chapati shape.
  7. Shallow fry on a griddle or frying pan.
  8. Serve with curd,chutney etc.
