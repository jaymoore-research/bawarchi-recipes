Jhatpat Karela Sabji

Ingredients

Karela : 2 cups Cut in medium size pieces.
Onions : same amount as karela.
Oil : 2 Table spoon.
Salt : as per taste.
Fregueek seeds : 5-6.

Method

  1. Mix Karela and Onions Together in a Bowl and keep aside.
  2. Add oil in a frying pan add fregueek seeds. fry till dark brown.
  3. Add the karela onion mixture to it. add salt and cover with lid.
  4. Cook on steam till it gets tender.

Its taste very very good without any other spices. good for digestion too.
