Peda recipe

Ingredients

1 can Condensed Milk
1 stick unsalted butter at room temperature
2 and a 1/4 cups Dry Milk Powder, like Carnation.
1 pinch elaichi powder

Method

  1. Heat a pan with the butter, add the can of condensed milk.
  2. Heat until bubbly on medium heat.
  3. Add the milk powder and elaichi powder and stir rapidly for 3 minutes until the entire mixture becomes a big lump and comes away from the sides of the pan.
  4. Remove from heat and cool for 5-10 minutes.
  5. Oil your hands and make pedas while the mixture is still hot.
  6. Cool the pedas for about 45 minutes.
  7. They are now ready to eat!
