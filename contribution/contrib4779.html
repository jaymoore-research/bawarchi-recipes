Curd Prawn Snack

Ingredients

Small prawns
thick curd
Onion - 2 (cut in to small pieces)
Tomato - 1 to 1 1/2 (cut in to small pieces)
Chilli powder- 1 spoon
2 garlic cloves
1/4 tsp ginger
salt as per taste
1/4tsp turmeric powder
2 tbsp coriander leaves
4-5 Cashews
1 tbsp Lemon juice

Gravy:

  1. Make garlic and ginger paste and mix them with cooked prawns, add required salt, chilli powder, turmeric powder and soak them for 1/2 hour.
  2. Keep little oil (very little, as you are going to fry the bread again) in tawa and fry onion and tomato till it becomes golden brown.
  3. Add the prawn mixture with this fried onion and tomato and cook in medium flame till you feel it is cooked and you don't get the raw smell.
  4. You can add roasted cashew also for taste. Add it finally so that it is crisp.
  5. Take bread slices. Remove the roasted brown edges. Soak in little water and squeeze it lightly in you palm, till the water is dry.
  6. Take two slices of bread. Now spread the prawn masala in one slice, spread thick curd on top of it, squeeze lemon if you need it and close it with the other slice. Close the sides firmly so that the masala doesnot come out.
  7. Rub the above made bread on half grinded rice flour. This will add crisp to the fry.
  8. Now Fry this bread deep in the oil in Tawa. It tastes hot, spicy with curd and lemon pinch spread on it.
  9. You can serve as week end evening dish for kids and guests with tomato or tamarind sauce or chutney.
