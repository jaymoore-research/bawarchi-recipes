I am Kamini. Onion fritters can be made very easily and quickly at home, and are enjoyed with green chutney or chilli sauce. In India, they come to mind most often when it is raining.

Onion Fritters

Ingredients

4 large Onions
4 cloves Garlic
3/4 cup Chick Pea Flour (besan)
1/2 cup Flour
1 Egg
1.5 teaspoon Bicarbonate Of Soda
1 teaspoon Chilli powder
Salt to taste
Vegetable Oil for shallow frying

Method:

  1. Peel the onions and cut in half. Slice very thinly. Finely chop the garlic.
  2. Mix the flours with the remaining ingredients and enough water to make a smooth creamy batter.
  3. Add onions and garlic and mix well.
  4. Heat oil, about 2 cm deep, in a wide flat pan.
  5. Add tablespoonfuls of the batter and press into patties. Fry on both sides until golden brown and cooked through.
  6. Remove to a rack covered with absorbent paper to drain excess oil.
  7. Serve hot with Chilli Sauce or Green Coriander Chutney.

Recipe Secret: If you do not want to use egg then you can add little corn flour to make the fritters more crispy.

Makes: 18-24 fritters
Serves: 8-10
