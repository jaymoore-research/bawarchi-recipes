Butter Chicken

Ingredients

Tomoto puree - 200 grams
Cream - 3 table spoons
Garlic Butter - 3 table spoons
Jeera - 1/2 tsp
Dried Fenugreek leaves - 1/2 tsp
Paprika- 1/2 tsp
Honey - 1/2 tsp
Chilli powder- 1 tsp
Coriander powder - 1/2 tsp
Coriender leaves - 1/4 cup
Garam Masala powder - a pinch
Chicken - 700 grams
Salt to taste
Vegetable Oil - 10 ml

Method for chicken:

  1. First wash the chicken throughly and keep aside.
  2. In a pan heat oil and add garam masala and chicken. Stir throughly after few minutes add salt, coriander powder, chilli powder and mix well. Once the chicken starts drying add two glasses of water and mix well. Boil until all the water get evaporated and also chicken became tender. Keep this chicken aside in a bowl.

For Gravy:

  1. In a pan heat oil add jeera. When it starts spultter add tomato puree and start stirring. Add little bit of salt and chilli powder (depending upon how you need hot/mild).
  2. Add paprika and stir it nicely. After few minutes add garlic butter and stir. Once the garlic butter completely melts add cream and stir it for few minutes. Add honey, dried fenugreek leaves and stir continuously.
  3. After 3 or 4 minutes, add the chicken that we kept aside in a bowl to this gravy and mix it well. Simmer for few minutes and add fresh coriender leaves to it and switch off the stove.
  4. Serve with basumati rice.
