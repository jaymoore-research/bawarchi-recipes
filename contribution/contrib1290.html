Vangibath

Ingredients

1. 3 Egg Plants
2. 2 medium sized Onions
3. 2 medium sized Tomatoes
4. 1 cup boiled Green Peas
5. 1 big green Capsicum
6. 2 table spoon Tamarind juice
7. 3 table spoon Vangibath powder (available in all Indian grocery stores. MTR brand preferred)
8. 1/2 cup grated Dry Coconut (Kobbari)
9. Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Cut all the vegetables.
  2. Heat the oil in a pan and add onions. Saute the onions till light brown and add tomatoes and vegetables.
  3. Cook vegetables in the oil and add salt to it.
  4. Once the vegetables are cooked add tamarind juice and stir for 1 minute.
  5. Add Vangibath powder and stir.
  6. Cover the pan and cook for 2 minutes.
  7. Finally, add the grated dry coconut and stir.
  8. Mix this with rice and serve.
