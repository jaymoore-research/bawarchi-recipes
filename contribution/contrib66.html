Tomato Chutney

Ingredients

1 pound of tomato
3 spoons of chillipowder & dhaniya powder
10 pieces of garlic
little mustard
little urad dhal
oil for seasoning and
salt to taste.

Method

  1. Make juice out of the tomatoes.
  2. Heat the pan and pour 10tbsp of oil.
  3. Season with mustard and urad dal.
  4. Pour the tomato juice into the pan and add the chilli powder and salt to taste.
  5. Also add the garlic (If you don't like garlic, it can be left out)
  6. Keep stirring the contents till all the water is evaporated and the chutney becomes very thick.

This chutney is very good with anything that you can think of including idli, dosa, poori (I feel it was made for it), chappati, rice, etc. According to your taste you can add or reduce the chilli powder. If cooked with more oil, it can stay fresh for even a week without keeping in fridge.
