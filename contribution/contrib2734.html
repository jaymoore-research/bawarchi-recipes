Pottukkadalai Bonda

Ingredients

Pottukkadalai powder-1 cup (no need to fry the pottukkadalai)
Rice flour-4 cups
Onions-2 or 3
Green Chillies-2
Salt to taste
Red Chilli Powder-1 spoon (if necessary)
Curry Leaves and Coriander Leaves
Oil to deepfry
Ghee-1 spoon

Method:

  1. Cut onions into very small pieces and mix with salt.
  2. Mash the onions well with hands and add pottukkadalai powder, rice flour, finely cut green chillies, curry leaves and coriander leaves, chilli powder, ghee. Mix everything well and add water a little if necessary.
  3. Make small balls out of this dough and deepfry in oil.
  4. Take it out from oil when it turns into golden colour.
  5. This will be very good with Coconut Chutney.
