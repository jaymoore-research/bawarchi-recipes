Ghee Rice

Ingredients
(Serving size 3-4 servings)

Fresh coconut half a cup
cloves 2-3 flowers
cinnamon 2 pieces (an inch long)
cardamom 2 pieces
green chillies 4-5 (or as needed)
garlic 1 clove (optional)
ginger 2-3 small pieces
poppy seeds 1 teaspoon
coriander leaves
or mint leaves a few

Grind all the above ingredients in a blender to a very fine paste with water.

Method:

  1. In a cooking pan, heat about 5-6 spoons of ghee.
  2. When it is hot, fry cashewnut pieces in it till they are golden brown.
  3. Then add a few pieces of cloves, cinnamon and bay leaves. Fry for a few seconds.
  4. Then add the finely ground masala to it and fry well for about 2-3 minutes.
  5. Also add peas. (may add potatoes also if desired at this point)
  6. Then add 1 1/2 cups of Basmati rice (washed and drained).
  7. Add the required amount of water (as suggested by the type of rice that you are using).
  8. Add salt to taste. Mix really well.
  9. Cook covered till the rice becomes tender. Serve hot along with onion and tomato raita.

Note: I cook in a pressure pan, so my cooking time is about 15 minutes. If you do not use a pressure pan, it may take longer for you.
