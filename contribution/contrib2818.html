Cashew Chicken

Ingredients

1 pound cleaned chicken breast or thigh
2 medium sized finely chopped ripe tomatoes
3-4 whole cardamoms, 1 cinnamon stick, some curry leaves, whole red pepper (optional)
Handful of roasted cashews
2 teaspoon of ginger paste,
1/4 teaspoon of garlic paste, and 1 1/2 tablespoon of onion paste
2 teaspoon coriander powder and salt to taste
1-2 tablespoon Oil
Heavy whipped cream or half and half milk

Method:

  1. Cut the chicken breast or thigh in 1 inch size.
  2. Marinate the chicken pieces in 2 teaspoon yogurt, salt, and coriander powder for 4-5 hours.
  3. Put oil in a shallow pan.
  4. Put in the curry leaves, whole red pepper, cardamoms, and cinnamon stick in the hot oil.
  5. Put in the ginger, garlic, and the onion paste in the oil.
  6. Saute for atleast 15-20 minutes on medium heat or until the paste turns little pinkish.
  7. Then put the chicken pieces and some Shaan's chicken biriyani paste for a spicy taste (optional).
  8. Saute in medium heat for atleast 20 minutes.
  9. Then put the sliced tomatoes and simmer for another 10-15 minutes with the lid on.
  10. Put 1 cup of water and some salt to taste and simmer for another 10 minutes.
  11. At the very end, when the chicken is almost cooked put the heavy cream and cook for another 10 minutes.
  12. Your chicken is ready to serve.
  13. Garnish it with roasted cashews and sprinkle few drops of lemon juice.
  14. It tastes so wonderful that you will eat the pot with it.
