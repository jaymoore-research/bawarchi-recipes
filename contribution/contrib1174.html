Plantain Vada

Ingredients

2 Plantains (Kucha kela or Vazaikkai)
2 Med sized Onions
4 green hot Peppers
1/2" piece of Ginger
1 tbs. cut Coriander leaves
Salt to taste
Oil for deep frying the vadas.

Method:

  1. Cut the whole plantains in 2, 3 pieces and cook it in the pressure cooker.
  2. Remove the skin and mash it without any lumps. To this add very finely cut onion, hot pepper, ginger, coriander leaves and mint leaves (optional), and salt.
  3. Make vada shapes out of this mashed plantain-onion mix and deep fry them till crisp.

It goes well with ketchup or any green chutney. Eat it hot.
