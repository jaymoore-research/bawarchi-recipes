This is the recipe for Paneer Kulfi

Cottage Cheese (Paneer) Kulfi

Ingredients

1. Milk - 1 litre
2. Paneer - 200 gms (finely grated)
3. Amulya Milk Powder - 200 gms
4. Cardamam - 5-6 (small)
5. Dry Fruit - cashewnuts, almonds, pista (5-6) each, finely chopped
6. Sugar to taste

Method:

  1. Boil the milk
  2. Keep aside half of the milk and cool the other half
  3. In the cool half put the amulya milk powder and mix it well in the milk
  4. Now heat the mixture of above milk and other half along with the dry fruit, sugar, grated paneer and cardamam, stir it well till the mixture becomes thick (say for 5-10 minutes). You can add green colour and rose essence.

After that cool it and pour it in the Kulfi container and keep for refrigeration.

Serves: 6 persons
