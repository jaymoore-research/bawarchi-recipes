Green Sweet Peas Pattice

Ingredients

500 gm Sweet or Petite Peas
3 cups plain flour (Maida / Wheat)
1 slice bread for bread crumbs
1 piece ginger paste
1- 2 pieces garlic paste
1 tsp Til
4-5 green chillis finely chopped
2 tsp lemon juice
2 tsp sugar
1 tsp corriander powder
1 tsp garam masala
chopped corriander
Salt to taste
Oil for deep frying
Water to knead flour

Method

  1. Boil the green peas for a very little time (only till they are a little soft)
  2. In a Mixie blend the peas (not to a very smooth paste)
  3. Take a tbsp of oil in a deep pan and add the peas paste and roast till the oil mixes well with the peas
  4. Add green chillis, ginger paste, garlic paste, til, corriander powder, corriander, sugar, lemon juice, garam masala and salt and mix well
  5. Add bread crumbs and mix well
  6. Keep the mixture aside

For the dough

  1. Take Maida / Wheat flour
  2. Add little salt as required
  3. Add 1 tsp of oil
  4. Knead the flour with water till it becomes pliable dough (The dough should not be very soft, it is good if the dough is on the harder side)
  5. Take handful of dough and roll it to a medium size poori
  6. Take a spoonfull of the prepared mixture (filling) and place it in the center of the rolled poori
  7. Cover the mixture by taking all the sides of the poori and close it by bringing all the edges in the center, flatten with very little pressure
  8. Repeat the same procedure for the remaining dough and mixture
  9. Heat Oil in a Kadhai
  10. When ready put the Pattice and fry until golden brown and little crisp
Serve hot with green chutney or tomato ketchup.
Have fun eating...
