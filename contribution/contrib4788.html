Vegetable Kurma

Ingredients

Carrot 2
Beans 20
Capsicum 2
Potato 2
Cauliflower - 1/2
Green Peas 1/2 cup
Tomato 2 - 3
Green Chilly 5-6 (for masala)
Onion 2
Coriander Leaves 1 bunch
Mint leaves 1 bunch
Cinnamon 2 pieces (half for masala)
Cloves 4 (half for masala)
Cardamom 2 (for masala)
Bay leaf 1 piece
Cashew Nut 10 (for masala)
Coconut Grated 1 cup (for masala)
Garlic cloves 2 (for masala)
Ginger 1 small piece (for masala)
Kuskus 1 spoon (for masala)
Yoghurt 1 cup

For Masala
Grind the following in to fine paste and keep aside.
Green Chilly 5-6
Cinnamon 1 piece
Cardamom 2
Cloves 2
Garlic cloves 2
Ginger 1 small piece
Coconut Grated 1 cup
Kuskus 1 spoon
Turmeric Powder 1/2 tsp

Method:

  1. Grind separately the cashew nut in to fine paste and keep aside.
  2. Cut the vegetables like beans, carrot, potato and capsicum in to long pieces.
  3. Chop the onion in to fine slices.
  4. Keep the capsicum, green peas and cauliflower separately.
  5. Keep oil in a pan and drop the cinnamon, cloves and bay leaf and drop the sliced onion and keep frying till it turns brown.
  6. Drop the hard vegetables like beans, carrot and potatoes first and keep turning then add the capsicum and green peas and fry.
  7. Add the masala paste and keep turning.
  8. Add turmeric powder and salt.
  9. Drop the sliced tomatoes and the cauliflower.
  10. Allow it to boil and then add the cashew paste.
  11. After five minutes, remove from fire and add yoghurt and stir slowly.
  12. Garnish with finely chopped coriander leaves and mint leaves.
  13. Serve with hot chapathis or rice.
