Kandi Pachadi (Tor Dal Chutney)

Ingredients

Kandi pappu (Tordal) - 1 cup
Red chillies - 10-12
Jeera (Cumin seeds) - 1 tbsp
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Fry kandi pappu, red chillies and jeera in a pan, till kandi pappu turns brown.
  2. Grind all the fried items with little water, till it becomes a paste.
  3. Add salt as per taste.

Note:
It is a very quick and easy recipe. It is nice to eat with hot rice, ghee and Mango pickle.
