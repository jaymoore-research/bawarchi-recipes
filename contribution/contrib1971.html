Mint Rice

Ingredients

Rice (Basmathi) - 2 cups
Mint (Pudina) leaves 1 bunch or 5 tbsp.
Coconut (grated), fresh or dessicated 3 tbsp.
Green Chillies 2
Ginger 3/4 inch piece
Garlic 3 pods
Onion (medium sized) 1
Cumin (Jeera) 1 tsp.
Potato (large sized) 1
Oil 2 tbsp.
Lime juice 1 tsp.

For Seasoning :

Oil 2 tbsp.
Mustard (Rai) seeds - 1/2 tsp.
Channa (Gram) dal 1 tsp.

Method:

  1. Cook Rice.
  2. Cut Potato into 1/2 x 1/2 pieces, deep fry the pieces & keep this aside.
  3. Chop the Onion into pieces. Grind the Onion, Mint (Pudina) leaves, grated Coconut, Cumin (Jeera), Chillies, Ginger & Garli into a smooth paste using very little water.
  4. Take 2 tbsp. of Oil in a pan, heat it & add mustard seeds and Channa daal. When the daal turns to light brown colour add the mint paste. Fry the paste for 3 to 4 minutes with constant stirring.
  5. Add the paste to the cooked rice. Add fried potato pieces. Mix well. Serve hot.
  6. Just before serving add lime juice to Mint Rice, mix well.
