I want to share my favourite sweet which is prepared in Andhra for the festival Ugadi and the name is called differently. We call it as "Bakshalu" and some others call it as "Bobattlu".

E-mail : joe@etaligent.com

Babattlu

Ingredients

Channa dal 1 cup
Jaggery 1 cup
Elachi 1 (Whole roughly powdered)
Maida 1 1/2 cup

Method:

  1. First make dough with maida and keep it a side untill dal cooks. Cover with wet cloth.
  2. Cook dal with double water and after removing from cooker remove the water from dal and grind the dal into paste. While grinding add jaggery. This is now called as Poornam. Add elachi and mix.
  3. Make Poornam into small round balls
  4. Make maida also into small balls which must be half the size of poornam balls.
  5. Now take maida ball into palm and spread it to your palm size and keep pooranm ball in that and close it from all sides. Now press like chapathi. You can do this by hand or stick.
  6. Heat the pan apply some oil and add fry what you made earlier.

Tips:

  • Add some Ghee to Poornam.
  • In the place of channadal you can add thoor dal which is good for health also.
