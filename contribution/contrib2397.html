Coconut Redchilly Chutney

Ingredients

Fresh coconut cut into small pieces - 1 cup
Coriender seeds (dhania) - 2 tbs spoons
Dry (sukhi) red chillies - 2 to 3 (medium size)
Cumin seeds (jeera) - 1 1/2 tbs spoon
Tamrind - 10 grms or 1 tsp
Curry leaves (kadi pattha) - 10-15
Fresh coriender bunch -1 (medium bunch)
Garlic flakes - 10 to 12 (small flakes)
Ginger chopped - 1 small spoon
Salt - 1 tsp or according
To taste oil or ghee - 1 tbs

Preparation:

Heat oil or ghee in a kadai add all the above ingredients and saute (bhunao) for 2 to 3 minutes, do not let it burn.

Then spread the ingredients over a plate and let it cool for sometime and then grind it in a mixer coarsly (do not make paste).

Note: Do not add water and see that you grind the ingredients evenly and not make it a paste by stiring the mixture with a spoon while grinding.

Enjoy with steaming hot rice and dal
