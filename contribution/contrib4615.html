Mango Pie

Ingredients

3 cups Water
1 cup Sugar
3 pockets Gelatin
8 oz. Whipping Cream
8 oz. Cheese Cream
1 tin Mango Pulp (you can get it in any Indian store)
1 canned Cherries

Method:

  1. Heat water until boil.
  2. Add sugar, gelatin. Let it melt.
  3. Add whipping cream, cheese cream and mango pulp. Cook for some time until everything dissolve. Let it cool.
  4. Put it in refrigerator for 45 min. or until its ready to cut cakes.
  5. Put one cake on the plate and serve with 1 tsp. of canned cherries.
