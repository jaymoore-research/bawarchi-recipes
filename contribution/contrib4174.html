Coconut Sambhar

Ingredients

Fresh Coconut grated-1 cup
Onions -2 small size
Tomato-1
Chutney Dhal- 1 tbsp
Tamarind paste-1 tsp
Coriander leaves

For seasoning:

Onion sliced-1
Curry leaves
Jeera

Method:

  1. Before grinding the above ingredients take one onion and fry it without oil.
  2. Grind all the above ingredients.
  3. Take kadai heat oil and add jeera and curry leaves. Add sliced onion and fry it.
  4. Add the grinded gravy to it and add salt for taste and bring it boil.
  5. Enjoy it with hot rice and desi ghee.
