Nepali Dish: Bhuta   Ko   Masu

Ingredients

1 kg mutton
150 grms onion sliced
100grms tomatoes chopped
12 sprigs spring onions
2 stalks celery
3 table spoons soya sauce
a pinch aginomoto
1" cube ginger
pinch of salt
half cup oil

Method

1. Boil meat with a pinch of salt and ginger bits till the meat is tender.
2. Slice meat into juliene strips when it is cold.
3. Heat oil, add ginger and onions, keep stirring till the onion is transperrent.
4. Add rest of the ingredient except the greens.
5. Fry lightly till all the moisture is absorbed
6. Just before serving add tomatoes and greens.
