Fish Padaa

Ingredients

1/2 lb Fish (Any fish of your liking)
Chopped ginger garlic (to equal 1 tablespoon of ginger garlic paste)
A handfull of red chillies or according to the pungency required
1 tablespoon cumin seeds
Tumeric in proportion to the chilly powder
Vinegar
Oil for frying

Method :

  1. Cut the fish into pieces of about 1/2 an inch. Deep fry the pieces.
  2. Grind all the other ingredients in vinegar. Remember do not use water to grind only small amounts of vineger. The ingredients should be ground to a 3/4 paste. You can make a full paste if you want. But remember no water at all.
  3. Heat oil (you could use the fish fry oil for better taste) in a kadai. Put the masala in & fry for about 5-7 minutes on medium heat or till it has fried properly. Remember the masala should not get burnt.
  4. Now include the fried fish in the masala & mix it well. Keep it on low heat for about 5 mins. Keep stirring occasionally.
  5. You can either serve immediately or preserve it. If you are planning to preserve it use more oil & vinegar like in pickles. If you are going to serve it for lunch or dinner, prepare it about 1-2 hrs. in advance.

Hope you enjoy it
