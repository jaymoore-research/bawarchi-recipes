Kalkandu Bath

Ingredients

Rice I cup.
Kalkandu( Lump white sugar) : 1 cup ( Misri);
Moong dal (optional).
Ghee 1 cup.;
Milk 2 cups;

For garnishing :
Sliced Almonds( six);
Split Cashew ( 25x2);
Kismish (dry gapes) 25;
Kesar a few strings

Method:

  1. Clean and wash the rice.
  2. Boil Milk and reduce it to half; Add rice (and the dal) and pressure cook til the tice is really soft.
  3. You can achiev it by giving it an extra whistle (if your pressure cooker does not whistle - like Hawking Futura- Give extra 3 minutes at medium flame).
  4. Take the rice from the pressure cooker and add 1/2 cup ghee in a thick bottomed vessal and add in the "Kalkanndu" Slowly. Mind you the flame must be simmer.
  5. Add in the Kesar Strings after dissoleving them in a little Luke warm milk.
  6. Add in thr balance of ghee slowly, while stirring the rice in.
  7. Keep string contantly and once the lump sugar is melted add the Roasted almond, cashew and Grapes.
  8. Serve Hot. If required you can add little more ghee so that the rice does not burn/ get stck at he bottom of the vessal.
