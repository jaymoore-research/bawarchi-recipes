Kadamba Chutney

Ingredients

Japanese eggplant -1
Red bell pepper -1
Onion -1(medium)
Tomato -2(big)
Coriander -1(bunch)
Mint -1(bunch)
Red chili -6 or7
Tamarind -little
Salt -to taste

For seasoning

Mustard seeds -1 teaspoon
Urad dal -1"
Curry leaves -(little)
Hing -1/4"
Oil -3"

Method

  1. Keep frying pan in the cooktop.
  2. Pore oil into the pan, when oil becomes hot put all those seasoning and red chillies.
  3. Then add one by one all those ingredients fry for few minutes.
  4. Allow it to cool.
  5. Grind them in the blender.
  6. It goes well with idli, dosa, even rice and chappati.
