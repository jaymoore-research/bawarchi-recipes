Poricha Rasam

Ingredients

channa dal - 1tbsp
urad dal - 1 tbsp
pepper + jeera - 3/4 tsp(together only)
chillies (red) -
coconut(scraped) - 1 tsp
Toor dal water - 1 cup
Curry leaves, asafoetida, mustard seeds and jeera for seasoning.
Salt to taste

Method

  1. Fry the dals and pepper, jeera in ghee and grind to a fine powder.
  2. Take dal water( boil toor dal with water and take only the water) and add tomato, asafeotida, salt.
  3. Boil this for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Then add prepared powder. Allow to boil for another 5 minutes.
  5. Finally season with roasted mustard, curry leaves and jeera (in ghee).
  6. This goes great with rice and any curry.
