Chicken Gizzards

Ingredients

1. Chicken Gizzards - 1 pack.
2. Ginger & Garlic paste - 3 tsp.
3. Onion - 1.
4. Tomato - 1.
5. Turmeric Powder - 1 pinch.
6. Red Chilly Powder - 2 or 3 tsp depending upon your hot limit.
7.Oil and Salt - As per your limit.

Method:

  1. Clean and Wash the Chicken Gizzards thoroughly in the water and apply to that a few turmeric powder, Red chillly powder (1 tsp) and salt little bit and keep it aside.
  2. Take a deep frying pan, pour oil into that and add Onions and fry till turns brown in colour.
  3. Then add Ginger and Garlic paste to it and fry for a while then add turmeric powder, Red chilly Powder, Tomatoes.
  4. Then add the Gizzards to that and keep on frying for a while till the gizzards gets well mixed with the onions, tomatoes and ginger and garlic, add salt to this.
  5. Add 3 glasses of water and cover with the lid, for every 5 min keep on stirring. Now let it get well cooked and it turns to gravy, if you feel that gizzards are not cooked properly then again you can add water to that and keep it covering. Now your Chicken Gizzards is ready to have.

Tomato is optional, For those who likes of dry type you don't need to add tomatoes, for those who likes of gravy type can add tomatoes.

You can have "Chicken Gizzards" with Chapathi, Puri, Fried Rice, With plain rice, Biryani, Naan.

Cooking Time: 15-20 min.
