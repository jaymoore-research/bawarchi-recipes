Carrot Squares

Ingredients

1. Carrot (shredded) 500 gms.
2. Ghee (purified butter) 3 tbs.
3. Sweetened Condensed Milk 1 can
4. Sugar 100 gms.
5. Cardamom, Almond, Cashewnut, Pista - for garnishing.

Method:

  1. Take 500 gms. Shredded carrot. Put Shredded Carrot in Pressure cooker for 5 to 7 minutes (3 to 4 whistle) Allow the cooker to cool down for 5 minutes. Drain water from boiled shredded carrot.
  2. In a non-stick pan, take 3-table spoonful of ghee and add boiled shredded carrot into it, and keep it stirring for 10 minutes. Add one can of condensed milk (sweetened) 300 ML. Keep on stirring the mixture for about 25 minutes, add 100 gms. of sugar if you prefer to have little sweeter, again boil it after adding sugar till such time it becomes pretty thick and it leaves the side of the pan. Add one teaspoonful of ghee if required. Put the mixture in a square pan.
  3. Garnish the square with Almond, Cashew, Pista and Cardamom. May wish to allow it to cool down to make proper squares, if you like, you may wish to keep it in fridge for frozen squares. Enjoy it. Wow, it tastes delicious.
