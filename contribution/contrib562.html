Daal

Ingredients

moong dal - 1 cup
onion (cut into small pieces) -1 cup
garlic - 2-3flakes
kasturi meethi (optional) - 1tsp
green chilies - 2-3
chilli powder - 1/2 tsp
salt - to taste

Method

  1. Cook moong dal with 2 cups of water, little salt, & garlic
  2. In a pan heat oil, add onions and fry for few minutes.
  3. Now add greenchillies, tomatoes, kasturi meethi leaves and chilli powder .
  4. After few minutes, add the mashed moong dal and little water if required.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  6. It is great with rotis.
