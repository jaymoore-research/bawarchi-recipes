Glucose Ladoo

Ingredients

Milkmaid condensed milk tin 1
Glucose biscuit 500 Gms.
Mixed dry fruits 1 cup
Coconut Powder 1/4 cup
Cardamom powder 1 TBS

Method :

  1. Make powder of glucose biscuits in the mixer. Put them in a big bowl.
  2. Add coconut powder (keep 2 TBS of c. powder aside), cardamom powder and dry fruits. Mix with light hands.
  3. Now add condensed milk into it make a smooth dough.
  4. Now make small round balls and roll them in the remained coconut powder and place them in a plate keep them in the refrigerator for half an hour. Now your ladoos are ready.

Tips:-

You can use any other sweet biscuits in place of glucose biscuits.
You can also use coconut powder in place of biscuits.
You can keep them in the fridge for a week.
