Alu Toast

Ingredients

White sliced bread(not too soft)
Boiled potatoes -Ā4 nos
Chopped onion -1 no.
Chopped green chillies - 4 nos.
Red chilli powder - 1/4 teaspoon
Chopped cilantro
Grated ginger - 1/2 teaspoon
Salt to taste

Method

  1. Mash the boiled potatoes mix the remaining ingredients except bread....
  2. Now take a bread slice & apply the potato mixture on both sides smoothly...
  3. Now heat a frying pan put little oil &Ātoast it till its nice & brown on both sides.
  4. Enjoy with ketchup...
