Ingredients

For Batter

All Purpose Flour : 2 cups
Egg (optional) : 1
Sugar : 1 tbsp
Salt : A Pinch
Water : To make a thick batter

For Filling

Grated Coconut : 2 cups
Sugar : To taste
Powdered Cardamom : 1/4 tsp

Method

  1. Mix all the ingredients for the batter to a batter the consistency. Should be similar to that of dosas.
  2. Pour a ladel full of batter on a non stick pan and make thin dosas and keep aside. Do not keep the Dosas to fill in later because they tend to stick to each other, so do the filling as soon as the dosa is made.
  3. For the filling mix all the ingredients and place a tbsp of filling in each dosa and roll it to the shape of a rod. Makes a wonderful and easy to make snack.
