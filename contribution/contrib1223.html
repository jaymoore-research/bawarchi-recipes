Vattal Kozhambu

Ingredients

Tamarind - a ball of small sized lemon or the ready paste available according to taste
Salt - to taste
Turmeric powder - a pinch
Curry Leaves - few
Gingely oil - 2 table spoons
Mustard seeds - 1/2 teasp.
Methi seeds(fenugreek) - 1/4 tsp.
Red gram dhal (thuvaramparuppu) 1/2 tsp
Asafoetida powder - a small pinch
Small onion - a handful - Peeled and cut into small pieces

To roast and powder:
3 teaspoons Coriander seeds
1/2 tsp black pepper seeds
1/2 tsp cumin seeds
1 1/2 tsp channa dhal (kadalai paruppu)
7 dry red chillies (can add acc. to taste requirements)
Roast all this with out oil and powder coarsely

Method:

  1. Soak the tamarind in hot water. Squeeze and extract the juice.
  2. The juice should be about 400 ml.
  3. Heat oil in a thick bottomed vessel or in a non stick pan.
  4. Add mustard seeds and add the methi and thuvar dhal after mustard flutters.
  5. Add the cut onions and saute for 2 mins in slow fire.
  6. Add the ground masala powder and fry for a minute.
  7. Add the tamarind juice, salt and turmeric powder. Add also the asafoetida and allow to boil.
  8. The kozhambu should thicken nicely - to half its original amount (around 200 ml) - normally a teaspoon of kozhambu should be enough for a small bowl of rice.
  9. Add curry leaves. Serve with hot rice.
