Bellpepper (Capsicum) Chutney

Ingredients

2 medium size Peppers
1 medium Tomato
1 medium Onion
2 Green Chilli Pepper
1 dry Red Chilli Pepper
4 tbsp Chana Dal (bengal gram dal)
1 tsp Mustard seeds
1/4 cup chopped Cilantro (coriander) leaves
salt to taste

Method:

  1. Chop the sweet pepper finely.
  2. Chop the onion and tomato fine.
  3. Roast the chana dal in 1/4 tsp of oil along with the dry red chilli pepper.
  4. Saute the sweet pepper, onion, tomato and the green chilli pepper in 1/2 tsp of oil, till the raw flavour goes off.
  5. First, grind the chana dal into a coarse powder and then add the rest of the sauted ingredients. Grind to a medium consistency slowly adding the cilantro while grinding. Add salt to taste, and mix them together properly.
  6. In a dutch pot or kadai, add 1/2 tsp of oil and add the mustard seeds, when it crackles, add it to the chutney. You can serve this chutney with Idlis, Dosas, Uppuma or you can use it as a spread on bread, or it can be also mixed with rice and eaten.
