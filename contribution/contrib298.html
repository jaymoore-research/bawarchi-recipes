Quick Dal with Palak

Ingredients

Tur daal: 1cup
Palak (Spinach) : 3-4 bunches
tomato : 1 cup
tamarind : a small ball
garlic flakes: 3-4
onion: 1 (small)
karipatta : 10-15 leaves
corainder (kotmeer): a bunch
asoefetida (hing): a pinch
red chilli powder: 1- 2 tsps
ghee: 1tsp
methi seeds: 5-6 seeds
zeera : 1 tsp
red chillies : 2-3

Method

  1. In a pressure cooker add water, turdaal, spinach, methi, 2 garlic flakes, a little of the corainder, one whole tomato, one onion sliced into pieces, asoefitida and close the cooker. Cook until the daal is soft (about 5-8 mins).
  2. Mash this cooked mixture lightly. Add salt and chilli powder. cook on a slow flame.
  3. Boil tamarind and half of the karipatta leaves separately, till the tamarind becomes soft - extract the pulp and add the tamarind water to the daal mixture.
  4. Cook on a slow flame for 2-3 minutes.
  5. In a sauce pan, heat a little oil - add the remaining garlic and karipatta, add red chillis (2-3) and zeera. When the oil is hot and sputtering, add it to the daal and immediately cover the daal with a tight lid. Allow to stand for a few minutes.
  6. Serve hot with rice and ghee and papad :)

(You can add any other green vegetable of your choice)
