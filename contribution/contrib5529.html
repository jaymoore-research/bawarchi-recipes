Instant Mango Gojju

Ingredients for Cooking

Mango- 1 medium size
Bengal Gram - 50 gms
Black Gram - 50 gms
Fenugreek- 2 tsp
dry red chillies-10
Asafoetida-1 pinch
grated dry coconut-1 tbsp
Tamarind-1 small ball
Oil- 3 tsp
salt to taste

Method:

  1. Heat oil in pan.Fry asafotida, bengal gram, black gram, red chillies, and fenugreek & remove from fire.
  2. Add coconut powder to the above mix.
  3. Add tamarind & salt to the above mix and grind to a fine powder.
  4. Melt jaggery in little water.Heat it for 3 mins.
  5. Add the above prepared powder to this.Remove from fire.
  6. Remove skin from mango & cut it into small pieces & add it to the above prepared mix.
  7. For seasoning add mustard seeeds, haldi & curry leaves to heated oil & mix it.

This is a side dish & can be eaten with Chapati, Dosa & rice.
Preparation Time: 15 mins
Shelf life: 1 week in refrigerator
