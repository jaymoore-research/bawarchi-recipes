Gulab   Jamoons

Ingredients for Jamoon

2 cups skim milk powder
1 cup maida/multipurpose flour
1 cup butter (unsalted)
1 cup milk
1/2 tsp Baking Soda

 Ingredients for Sugar Syrup

3-4 cups sugar
2 cups water
cardamom powder

Other Ingredients

oil for deep frying
raisins
Method

  1. Mix the ingredients for jamoon to make a very soft dough.
  2. Set aside for one hour
  3. In the meantime melt the sugar in water over low flame and prepare the sugar syrup. Put off the flame once the syrup boils.
  4. Add cardamom powder to the syrup
  5. Make small balls of the jamoon mix and put one raisin in the center of each ball
  6. Deep fry in oil on a low flame till golden yellow in color
  7. Immediately immerse the hot jamoons into hot sugar syrup.
  8. Let it soak.
  9. Serve hot either with whipped cream or with Ice cream ( in a plate)

Variations

You could use khoya / Mishri (Crystal sugar) instead of raisins in the center of each jamoon.
