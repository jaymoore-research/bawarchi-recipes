Moong Ke Khaste

Ingredients

1 cup yellow moong daal
3 cups maida (plain flour)
1 tsp coriander seeds (dhania)
1/2 tsp cumin seeds (jeera)
1 tsp garam masala
1/2 tsp amchoor
1/2 tsp red chilli powder
3-4 green chillies, finely chopped
1 inch ginger, finely chopped
Salt to taste
Oil for deep frying

Method :

  1. Soak the moong daal overnight and then grind it with as little water as possible.
  2. Dry roast coriander seeds & cumin seeds and crush them coarsely.
  3. Add coriander & cumin seeds mixture, red chilli powder, garam masala, amchoor, salt chopped green chillies and ginger to daal and mix it well.
  4. Now heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan, preferably non stick, and add the above daal mixture to it.
  5. Stir it continuously till it dries. First it will form a lump and then eventually after continuous frying it will break in a smooth mixture for filling. Take care that it doesn't stick to the pan or gets burnt. Now the filling is ready.
  6. Mix about 3 tbsp oil and a little salt to the maida and mix it well with hands.
  7. Knead a tight smooth dough using hot water.
  8. Now take small cubes from the dough and place � tsp of filling in it and close it carefully. Now press it carefully with a rolling pin with not exerting much pressure and just giving them a flat shape.
  9. Then repeat the above step for the whole dough.
  10. Now heat oil in a deep pan or kadai and deep fry the khastas on a low flame till they turn golden brown (3-4 at a time).
  11. You will have delicious khastas ready which go very well with tamarind and jaggery chutney.
  12. You can keep them for 3-4 days.
