Chicken Shaslik

Ingredients

1.. 1 kg boneless chicken
2.. soya sauce 6 tbsp
3.. sugar 1/2tsp
4.. red chilli powder 4 tbsp
5.. tomatoes 4-6( medium size)
6.. black pepper 2 tbsp
7.. onions 3-4
8.. oil 5 tbsp
9.. vinegar 4tbsp
10.. adrak lassan paste 1tbsp
11.. capsicum 4(medium sized)
12.. chillie garlic sauce 2 tbsps

Method:

  1. In a bowl of boneless chicken put 2 tbsps red chillie poder,salt(to taste),2 sps soya sauce,2 sps vinegar,1 sp black pepper and now mix thorougly.Keep aside for half an hour.
  2. In a fry pan put 2-3 tbsp oil n let it heat.Now fry the above marinated chicken in fry pan.
  3. After frying for 15 min take out in a dish.
  4. Now again put 2tbsp oil in the pan & fry the onion layers,capsicum & add 2 tbsp vinegar,2tbsp soya sauce,sugar 1\4 tsp,1tbsp black pepper,1tsp salt & fry for 5-10 min & now put the mixture on the over fried chicken in the dish.
  5. Now in the pan again put 2 tbsp oil & the tomatoes , chillie garlic sauce 2 tbsp,2 tbsp red chillie powder, 1\4 tsp sugar,2 sps soya sauce & mix it nicely to form a paste like.
  6. Now put the above sauce in the fried chicken & vegetables.
  7. Chicken shaslik is ready to serve.
