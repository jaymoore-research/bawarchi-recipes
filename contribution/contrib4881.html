Sweet and sour vegetables

Ingredients

1 green bell pepper,cut into squares
1 yellow pepper, cut into squares
8 cherry, or 4 medium, tomatoes
8 cauliflower florests
8 fresh or canned pineapple, chunks
8 cubes tofu
boiled rice, to serve (optional)

For the marinade
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
2 tablespoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon finely ground black pepper
1 tablespoon clear honey
2 tablespoon chilly sauce

Method

  1. Thread the prepared vegetables,pineapple and tofu cubes on to four skewers, alternating the ingredients.
  2. Prepare the marinade. Mix together all the ingredients. If the mixture is a little too thick, add 1 tablespoon water to dilute it a little.
  3. Brush the skewers with the seasoned oil,ready for barbecuing. Cook on a hot barbecue for 10 minutes, until the vegetables are done. Turn the vegetables often and brush the marinade everytime.
  4. Serve on a bed of plain rice, if you like.

Recipe secret: If you like you can blanch the cauliflower a little before putting in the skewers.

Serves 4
