Pot Roast Chicken

Ingredients

1 small whole chicken( 2 pounds or 1 Kg.), cut into 4 equalĀpieces horizontally & vertically.
2 big Onions finely chopped
11/2 inch ginger root
5-6 garlic (big) cloves, if small take 8-9
Ā 3/4th cup vinegar (white or brown)
1/4th cup vegetable oil
2 Dried red chillies
Salt to taste

Method

  1. Clean the chicken under flowing cold water, drain out the water completely.
  2. Grind the ginger and garlic to a paste.
  3. In a large bowl dip the chicken pieces in vinegar & evenly spread the ginger garlic paste over the chicken with a spoon.
  4. Allow to the chicken to marinate for about 45 minutes.
  5. In a pressure cooker or a cooking pot, heat the oil with dried red chillies.
  6. Put the marinated chicken in the cooker and add salt , stir it for a while then cover the cooker.Ā DONOT ADD WATER.
  7. After theĀfirst whistle, set it to low flame for 10 minutes.
  8. Pot Roast Chicken is ready, if there is water in the cooker then dry out the water in high flame.
  9. Serve hot with naan or parathas. Serves 4.
