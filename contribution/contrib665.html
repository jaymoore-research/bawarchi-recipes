Bellary Saaru

Ingredients

1/2 cup toor dal
1 small cup coconut 3-4 green chillies
1/2 cup coriander seeds
1/2 bunch cilantro
3-4 teaspoon cumin seeds
Pinch of asafoetida
1 onion
1 1/2 cup water
1/2 cup toor dal
3-4 tsp tamarind juice
salt to taste
Curry leaves (optional).

Method:

  1. Grind all to paste with water except onion.
  2. Pressure cook toor dal(1/2 cup).
  3. Make vaghar add curry leaves and turmeric, onion and fry (Golden brown).
  4. Add toor dal boil for five minutes and add tamarind juice.
  5. Then add paste boil well for ten minutes.

Serve with hot rice and chapati.
