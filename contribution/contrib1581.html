Crunchy Chicken

Ingredients

Boneless Chicken - 1 LB (cleaned and cut into pieces)
All purpose flour - 3 tbsp.
Corn Flour - 3 tbsp.
Egg - 1
Garam Masala Powder - 2 tsp.
China Salt or Ajinomoto - 1/2 tsp.
Chilli Powder - 2 tsp.
Turmeric - 1/2 tsp.
Pepper powder - 1 tsp.
Soya sauce - 1 tsp.
Ginger Garlic paste - 2 tsp.
Green Chillies - 5 (cut into long strips)
Salt - acc to taste
Oil for frying

Method :

  1. Marinate chicken with salt, turmeric, chilli powder, garam masala, 1 tsp. of ginger garlic paste, corn flour, all purpose flour, soya sauce, china salt, pepper powder and egg for 4-5 hours.
  2. Now fry the chicken pieces in hot oil until the chicken is cooked and golden brown in color. Drain on a paper towel.
  3. In a separate pan add 1 tsp. of oil and fry green chillies and the remaining 1 tsp. of ginger garlic paste for 1 min and add to the fried chicken.
  4. Garnish it with cilantro and serve immediately with onion slices, lemon juice and ketchup. Goes well as an appetizer or as a side dish.
