Chef's Salad

Ingredients

Bed -of cabbage Dressing:

Hanged Curd - 1 cup
Salad oil - 1 tbsp
Sugar Powder - 4 tsp
Black Pepper Powder - 1 tsp
Mustard Powder - 1/2 - 1 tsp (or to taste)
Salt - 1 tsp (or to taste)
Whipped cream - 1/2 cup
Tomato ketchup - 1/2 cup
Red pepper sauce - 2 tbsp
Mix all the ingredients.

Body:

  • Beat root, Cucumber, Carrot, Raddish, Onion, Red Raddish, Kakri etc.
  • Cchop and mix with dressing.
  • Pour this in a bowl with finely chopped cabbage.

Garnishing: Garnish with half cut tomato pieces.
