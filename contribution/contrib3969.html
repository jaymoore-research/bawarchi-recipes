Inippu Vadai (Sweet Wada)

Ingredients

Whole skinned Urud dal 200 gm
Iddli Rice (parboiled rice) 150 gm
Sugar 200 gm
Oil to fry

Method:

  1. Soak the Iddli rice for 30 mts and Urud dal for 15 mts separately.
  2. Grind Rice till smooth without adding much of water. Remove and keep aside.
  3. Drain dal. Grind to a paste adding the sugar little at a time. Use all the sugar. Do not add water, as the addition of sugar will not make it necessary.
  4. Now add the rice batter to the grinder and grind along with the urud dal batter till its thoroughly mixed.
  5. Heat oil in kadai. Take a little quantity of batter of lime size and flatten it as for wadas. Deep fry. 4 or 5 wadas can be made at a time depending on the kadai size.

Now the delicious wadas are ready to impress your friends and relatives.

Note: Do not add much of water as this will absorb more oil during frying. The finished batter should be thick.
