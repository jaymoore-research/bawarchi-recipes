Moon Dal halwa

Ingredients

1/4 kg moong daal(chilke wali)
1/4 kg ghee
1/2 kg whole milk
1/4 kg sugar
dry fruits,
1/4 tsp cardamom powder
2-3 drops yellow colour.

Method:

  1. First soak moong daal for 4-5 hours ,wash thoroughly & remove the covering & grind it.
  2. Fry it in ghee till brown,now add milk & sugar
  3. Keep stirring it till it leaves the sides add colour ,dryfruits, & elaichi powder & serve hot.
