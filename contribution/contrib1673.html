Instant Gajjar Halwa

Ingredients

Gajjar 500 mg
Sugar 200gms or to taste Dry fruits 1/2 cup
Milk powder 1 cup

Method:-

  1. Wash and grate the carrots.
  2. Mix carrots, sugar and ghee in a microwave bowl and cover it with a lid. Keep it in the microwave for 8 minutes.
  3. Take out and add dryfruits and milk powder with light hands. Don't mix the milkpowder fully. Leave a few balls (they will look like as if you have added a lot of khoya).
  4. Put the bowl back in the oven for 7 minutes. Serve hot.
