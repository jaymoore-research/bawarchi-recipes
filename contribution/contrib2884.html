Capsicum Rice

Ingredients

1 Full capsicum finely chopped.
1 Full onion finely chopped.
Salt and chilly powder as much required.
2 Tablespoon of oil.
2 cups of rice.

Method:

  1. Keep the rice to boil.
  2. Heat the pan with oil and add mustard in it.
  3. Add finely chopped onions in it, wait until it becomes golden brown and add chopped capsicum to it and wait for 4 minutes.
  4. Then add salt and chilli powder as much required , let it simmer for some minutes.
  5. After that mix it with rice and add some coriander to it. Now capsicum rice is ready to eat.
