Rasila Hari Mirch

Ingredients

Green Chillies 50 gms
Fenugreek seeds 10 gms
Coriander seeds 15 gms
Lime Juice from 4-5 lime
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Slit the green chillies and remove the seeds and put them in the lime juice.
  2. Powder fenugreek seeds and coriander seeds separately (raw) and mix them to the chilli-lime and add salt. Mix them well and keep it aside for a day. It gives the rich taste the next day which can be had with Makai Ka Roti or Jowari Ka Roti or even with Curd Rice. Isn't it tasty.
