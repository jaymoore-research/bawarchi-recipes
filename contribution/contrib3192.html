Spicy Crispy Corn Chicken

Ingredients

1. Chicken-1/2 boneless
2. Red chilli powder-2tspn,kashmiri red chilli powder-11/2 tspn(for colour)
3. Turmeric powder-1tspn
4. Ginger-Garlic paste-1tbspn
5. Salt-as per taste
6. Egg-1 (large)
7. Pepper powder-1tspn
8. Roasted Thin Corn flakes(plain)-3-4 cups
9. Oil-4-5 tbsp (as much for shallow fry)
10. Lemon juice- 2 tsp

Method:

  1. Clean and cut the chicken into medium size pieces, prick pieces with fork on all sides. Take a bowl add ginger-garlic, turmeric, two types of chilli powder, juice of one lemon, salt, mix it well, now smear each piece well. Let it marinate for 3-4 hrs.
  2. Now take one egg add salt & pepper to it whisk until yolk & white get mixed, coat each piece well in the egg mixture.
  3. Crush the corn flakes coarsely with your palm.
  4. Heat in the kadai or saucepan coat each piece in the corn flakes fry in the hot oil until golden brown.
  5. Serve hot with chilli-garlic sauce.

Happy cooking!!!
