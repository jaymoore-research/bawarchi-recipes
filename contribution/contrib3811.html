Omellette Curry

Ingredients

Egg - 4nos
Chopped Onion - 2 tsp
Chopped Green Chillies - 1 tsp
Salt - 1 tsp
Turmeric powder - 1 tsp

Making gravy:

Whole Garam Masala - few
Whole Jeera - 1 tsp
Bay Leaf - 2 nos
Chopped Tomatoes - 1 cup
Onion paste - 1/4 cup
Garlic-Ginger paste - 1/4 cup
Curd - 1/2 cup
Red Chilli powder - 2 tsp
Salt - as taste
Turmeric powder - 2 tsp
Water - 1 cup
Fresh chopped Coriander - 2 tsp

Method:

  1. Beat the eggs together and mix it with other ingredients. Make an omellette, and cut into six equal pieces.
  2. Heat a pan, add oil. Put whole jeera, whole garam masala and bay leaf, after crackling it add other masalas and fry together untill it removes oil. Now add water, and after boiling add the omellette pieces. Cover it for 10 minutes in a slow flame and garnish with fresh coriander.
  3. Serve hot with Rice or Paratha.
