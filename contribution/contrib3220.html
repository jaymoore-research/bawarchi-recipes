Tamarind Chutney

Ingredients

1/2 cup Tamarind Paste, preferably prepared fresh by soaking tamarind in luke warm water and removing pits.
1 tsp Black Zeera
1 tsp Sabut Dhaniya
1/2 tsp Crushed Red Chillies
Salt to and Brown Sugar to taste.

Method:

  1. Roast Zeera, Dhania and Crused Red Chillies in a non stick pan.
  2. Grind the roasted ingredients in a grinder.
  3. In a small bowl add Tamarind paste and the roasted ingredients and mix well until smooth. Add salt and brown sugar according to taste. Chutney is ready.

This Chutney tastes great with Alloo-ki-Tikki, Pakoras, Samosas, Vegetable Pulao or Khichri.
