Fulvadi

Fulvadi 2 bowl - Besan flour

1 bowl - Suji (Rava)
1- bowl - Yogurt (Curd)
3/4 to 1 bowl - Oil
3/4 bowl - Sugar
Dry toasted and coarsely grounded dry coriander seeds
Sesame seeds
Fennel seeds (variyali - little crushed)
Red pepper powder
Crushed black pepper
Salt to taste

  1. Mix all ingredients and make dough.
  2. Keep the dough overnight or for an hour before making the fulvadi.Ā
  3. Take the fistfull of dough and rub it on fulvadi zhara directly on hot oil and fry till red.
  4. Store in tight container once cool.
  5. Fulvadi used in Gujarati jaman as a side compliment and one can also enjoy it as snacks with tea. Enjoy it and send me your comments.
