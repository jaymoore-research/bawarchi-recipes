Hi, I am Vidhu from Sharjah, U.A.E. I came across this site recently & found it a very interesting way to share recipes. So here I am forwarding a recipe for a sweet date dish.

Date Delight

Ingredients

150 gram dates chopped
250 ml boiling water
1/4 tsp soda bicarbonate
60 gm butter
200 gm sugar
1 egg
1/4 tsp vanilla
225 gm all purpose flour
1/4 tsp baking powder
A pinch of Salt
50 gm nuts

Method

  1. Pour boiling water on the chopped dates & add soda bicarbonate.
  2. Beat butter & sugar together, add egg & vanilla & beat well.
  3. Sift the flour, baking powder & salt & fold alternately with the date mixture into the creamed butter, sugar & egg.
  4. Add the chopped nuts & stir well.
  5. Pour the batter into a pie dish & bake in a pre-heated oven for 30-40 minutes at 180 degree C.
  6. Watch carefully while baking. Remove & let it cool completely before making slices.

Happy cooking, Happy eating.
