Quick Gulab Jamuns

Ingredients

1. Nido milk powder-11 tsp
2. Baking powder-1 1/2 tsp
3. Veg.ghee or Dalda to fry (very little, 10 tsp)
4. Sugar-1 cup
5. Cardamom powder- 1tsp.

Method:

  1. Take 11 tsp of milk powder and add 1 tsp of baking powder mix well.
  2. Add just 3 to 4 tsp of water to it and make a dough. The dough should be tight enough to make a ball. Since it is a milk powder we should be very careful while adding water.
  3. Now in another vessel make a sugar syrup- just melt it add cordomom powder and safron optional.
  4. Now make small balls, deep fry them in the veg.ghee and immediately immerse it in the sugar syrup.

Now our delicious jamoons are done. For 11 tsp of milk powder we can make 25 small jamoons. The important point to remember is that the dough should not get dried up as it tends to crack when we make balls. Also if we add too much baking soda the jamoons will become very soft and it will loose its shape, getting dissolved in the syrup. The syrup should be little hot while we add the jamoons.
