Tomato Gozhu

Ingredients for Cooking

1 big onion
3 big tomatoes
1 small ball of tamarind (for soaking)
Pinch of turmeric
2 teaspoon sambhar powder
2 teaspoon grated coconut
2 green chillies
2 teaspoon pandharpur dal
1 teaspoon khuskhus (white poopy seeds)
2-3 garlic pieces
1 small cup coriander leaves
Salt to taste

FOR TADKA:
1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
1/2 teaspoon jeera
1/2 teaspoon urad dal
1/2 teaspoon chana dal
2 red chillies

Ā Method:

  1. Soak the tamarind for around 10min in water and keep it aside.
  2. Take a little oil for tadka in a frying pan and pour all the tadka ingredients into it and let it fry for a minute until the dal becomes red in colour.
  3. Cut the onion in small pieces and fry it once the tadka is done until it turns light brown in colour.
  4. Cut the tomatoes into small pieces and add it over the onion into the frying pan.
  5. Now add sambhar, turmeric, salt and fry it for a minute or so.
  6. Then add the tamarind water got from the soaking into the pan and let it boil for 5-10 minutes.
  7. In the meantime, grind the coconut, green chillies, coriander leaves, garlic and pandharpur dal into fine paste.
  8. Now pour the paste into the frying pan and mix for a minute or so.

You now have delicious tomato gozhu ready which can be served with hot rice,dosas,idlies and even rotis!

SERVES: 4-6 persons
