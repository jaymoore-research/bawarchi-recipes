Pudina Chutney

Ingredients

Pudina leaves 1 cup
Coconut grated 1 cup
Green Chillies 5
Tamarind quarter size of a lemon
Bengal gram 1 teaspoon
Black gram 1 teaspoon
Cooking Oil 2 teaspoon
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Wash pudina leaves and drain the water.
  2. In a kadai, add half teaspoon of oil and fry the pudina until it shrinks.
  3. Fry bengal gram, black gram and green chillies in 1 teaspoon of oil and finally fry tamarind.
  4. Cool all the fried ingredients, add salt and grind in a mixie.
  5. In a pan, add half teaspoon of oil and heat it. Add mustard seeds. When mustard splutters, remove it from fire and add it to the chutney.

SERVINGS: 3
