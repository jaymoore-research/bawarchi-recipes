Aloo Methi

Ingredients

6 medium size Potatos
1/2 bunch Methi Leaves (available in most of Indian stores) - washed, finely chopped
Finely chopped Garlic - 2 cloves
Finely chopped Chilli - 2 nos
Finely chopped Onions - 1 no
Garam Masala Powder - 1/2 tbl spoon
Chilli Powder - 1 tbl spoon
Turmeric - pinch
Salt to taste
Oil for frying

Method :

  1. Boil potatoes and peel the skin off. Cut each potato into medium size pieces (4-6).
  2. Fry the potatoes in oil till light golden brown and keep them aside.
  3. In three spoons oil, fry garlic and chilli. Add onions and fry slowly till crisp and brown. Add garam masala.
  4. Add methi leaves and fry for three minutes.
  5. Now add fried potato pieces and little chilli powder, turmeric and salt.
  6. Mix well and fry for 5-6 minutes in medium heat.
