Vegetable Fried Rice

Ingredients

2 cups Basmati rice
1 medium size potato, peeled and cut into cubes
1 big onion (or two small onions) chopped
2-3 green chillies (you may add more according to your taste)
1/2 cup fresh or frozen peas
1/2 cup fresh cauliflower
lots of coriander leaves
1/2 tsp jeera
1/2 tsp mustard seeds
1/4 tsp garam masala
1 tsp coriander powder
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1 tsp red chilli powder
salt to taste
2-3 tbsps oil

Method:

  1. Take oil in a pan and let it heat. Then add jeera and mustard seeds and let them pop for a sec.
  2. Add chopped onions and green chillies and stir it until it's golden brown.
  3. Add turmeric pd, coriander pd and leave it for a sec.
  4. Add your potatoes and mix well. cover it and cook until potatoes are partly cooked.
  5. Add cauliflower and peas and stir it well with little salt (you should add only half first time and half later).
  6. Cover the vessel and let it cook. when it is cooked, add boiled rice and mix well.
  7. Add salt(you have already added salt to vegetables so add very little for the rice), garam masala and dhania(coriander) leaves.
  8. Mix well and cover it for 4-5 min before you serve.

Can be served with raita.
