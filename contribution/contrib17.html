Leftover   Dal   Pancakes

Ingredients

LeftOver Dal ( and/or Sambhar)
1/2 cup each of Wheat flour, Rice flour, Gram flour
a clove of garlic ( grated or finely cut)
2 green chillies ( finely cut)
asaefotida, turmeric powder and salt to taste
1/2 tsp. oil AND oil for shallow fry or deep fry

Method

Add all the igredients including 1/2 tsp. oil together.
For deep frying, roll into small puris and deep fry in kadai.
For shallow frying, add water if necessary so as to make the mixture like pancakes ( or Uttappam, Dosa, etc).
Put a little oil on a griddle and spread 2 tblspns ( or as appropriate) of the mixture.
Turn and add 2-3 drops of oil at the sides. Serve with Ketchup or cocnut chutney or pickle.
