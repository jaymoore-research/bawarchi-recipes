Channa Rice

Ingredients

1 cup white whole Channa
3 cups Basmathi rice
1 big Onion (sliced)
2 tomatoes (chopped)
1/2 cinnamon stick
2 cloves
1 Bay leaves
6 cloves Garlic
1 tsp Jeera
1/4 cup Grated coconut
1 tsp Chill powder
1 tsp Dhania powder
A pinch of Turmeric

Grind:
Garlic, jeera, coconut, chilli powder, dhania powder, turmeric into a paste.

Method:

  1. Soak, clean and boil channa (don't over cook). Keep the water.
  2. In cooker add little oil and add cinnamon, cloves, bay leaves.
  3. Add onions and fry untill golden brown.
  4. Then add the tomatoes and cook for a minute.
  5. Add the ground paste and fry for 2 minutes.
  6. Put the cooked channa.
  7. Add 4 cups of water (including the saved one) or according to your rice nature.
  8. After the water starts to boil add the rice.
  9. Salt to taste.
  10. Pressure cook until one sound and remove from stove.
  11. Your tasty channa rice is ready, garnish with coriander leaves and serve it with raitha.
