Gasagase Payasa (Kannada Sweet)

Ingredients

2 tbsps long grain rice
2 tbsps poppy seeds(gasagase)
2 cups milk
1 cup water
1 block jaggery (or 10-12 tbsps brown sugar)
10 tbsps sugar
1/2 cup desicated coconut(or dry coconut-kobbari would be good)

Method:

  1. Allow the milk to boil, in a pan at low heat.
  2. In another vessel, put jaggery (or brown sugar), sugar and 1 cup water and keep it to boil at medium heat till it becomes thick like one thread consistency syrup.
  3. Take another pan and dry roast rice & poppy seeds.
  4. Grind the fried rice & poppy seeds with coconut into a smooth paste with very little water.
  5. Add this fine paste to the thickened jaggery syrup and boil for about 10-12 mins.
  6. Add this to the boiling milk and boil for about 10 mins.
  7. Remove all coarse elements from this liquid by filtering it in a drainer or colander.
  8. Serve it hot or cold dessert.
