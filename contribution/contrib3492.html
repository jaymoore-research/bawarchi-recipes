Groundnut Chutney

Ingredients

1. A small cup of Raw Groundnut.
2. A small marble of Tamarind.
3. 1 small cut Onions.
4. 1 pod Garlic (optional)
5 4 or 5 Red Chillies.(increase or decrease according to the Taste)
6. Salt to taste.
7. A table spoon of fresh Coconut scrapings

Method:

  1. In a pan with little oil fry the groundnuts till the skin comes off.
  2. Peel off the skin throughly by rubbing with your hands, when the ground nuts are taken off from the stove.
  3. In a kadai, with little oil, saute, red chillies.
  4. Grind together groundnuts, Raw cut onions, garlic, Tammarind, salt with enough water to fecilitate grinding.
