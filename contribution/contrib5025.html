Fried Rice

Ingredients

1. Cooked Basmathi or Raw Rice ... 2 cups
2. Red chillies (dry) ... 8
3. Garlic ... 6 pearls.
4. Onion -- chopped, Big ... 1
5 Green peas ... 1/2 cup
6. Onion Spring Rolls ... 1/2 bunch.
7. Salt ... to taste
8. Oil ... to fry.

Procedure:

  1. Make a paste of red chillies and garlic in a mixie. As the red chillies would take a longer time, office going people can mix 2 tsp of chilly powder with the garlic and make paste,
  2. Heat oil in a vessel, add the ground paste of red chillies and garlic and saute till the raw smell of garlic goes off.
  3. Add the chopped onion and saute for 2 or 3 minutes.
  4. Add the green peas and salt just sufficient for the onion and green peas and saute till the onion is nicely fried.
  5. Add the cooked rice and mix well.
  6. Garnish with spring onions and serve with veg. kurma or onion raitha.
