Corn Korma

Ingredients

Frozen Corn 2 cups
Cabbage 1 cup (shredded)
Green Bell pepper 1 cut into strips
Onions 2 finely chopped
Tomato 1 large chopped
Poppy seeds 3 tsp. soaked overnight with Cashews
Cashewnuts 10 soaked overnight
Haldi 1/2 tsp.
Dhania powder 1 tsp.
Lal mirch 3/4 tsp.
Salt to taste
Sugar 1/2 tsp.
Oil 4 tsp.
Garam masala 1/2 tsp.

Method :

  1. Microwave the frozen corn for 3 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile heat 1 tsp. oil in a teflon pan. Add cabbbage, shimla mirch and cooked corn, stir fry for 3-4 minutes on high heat.
  3. Grind cashew and poppy seeds to a paste.
  4. Heat the remaining oil in a pan and add onions, fry for 2-3 minutes at high heat. Reduce the heat and add rest of the masalas to it. Let it remain for medium heat for another 3 minutes, now add chopped tomatoes let the whole masala fry for another 8 minutes.
  5. Next add poppy-cashew paste, and let masala fry for 4 minutes.
  6. Add stir fried vegetables, salt, sugar and garam masala to the masala paste. Stir and take off the stove after 5 minutes. Serve hot.
