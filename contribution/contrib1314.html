Veg. Noodle Burger

Ingredients

1 cup Boiled Noodles,
1/2 cup boiled and drained Masoor daal,
1 bowl boiled and drained Spinach (Methi can be used instead),
1/2 cup Bread Crums,
1 tsp Lemon juice,
1/2 tsp Sugar,
Salt to taste

Wet masala:

1 small Onion chopped,
3 medium Green chillie,
2 tsp Ginger,
1 tsp Garlic
Grind all these ingredients together to make a thick paste.

Dry masala:

1 tsp Garam masala,
1 tsp Corriander powder
Oil for shallow fry.

Method:

  1. Mix all ingredients, wet and dry masala well to make a soft dough.
  2. Apply some oil to palm and prepare round patties.
  3. Shallow fry on drizzling oil.
  4. Place in hamburger buns and serve hot with Lettuce, Onion, Tomato and Ketchup. Also tastes good when served with Tamarind /Green chutney.

Quantity: Makes 8 to 10 patties.
Making time: 45 minutes.
