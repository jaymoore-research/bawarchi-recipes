Alu Bhat

Ingredients

1 cup Rice cooked with 1 tsp Oil and 1 tsp Salt
2 medium Potatoes cooked , peeled and cubed
2 Tomatoes chopped
1 Green Pepper cut small
2 tsp Peanuts/Cashew (optional)
1/4 tsp Turmeric

For seasoning:

1/2 tsp Mustard, 1/4 tsp Hing
Garnish: Green Coriander leaves 3 sprigs

Dry roast:

1 tsp Chana Dal
1 tsp Urad Dal
4/5 Dry Red Chilli
1 stick Curry Leaves
1 inch Cinnamon stick
2 Cloves
2 Cardamoms marble size Tamarind
3 tsp Dessicated Coconut

Method

  1. Heat 2 tsp oil add mustard, hing , nuts fry till golden brown and put in potatoes, green pepper and turmeric , fry for 3 mins.
  2. Add tomatoes , salt and cook for 2 mins. Add the ground powder mix.
  3. Add rice mix and garnish with green coriander.
