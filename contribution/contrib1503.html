Mirch Ka Salan

Ingredients

8-10 big Green Chillies (the ones you use for Pakoras/Bhajias)
Oil for frying
1 medium Onion finely chopped
2 medium Tomatoes finely chopped
1 tsp Ginger-Garlic paste
Salt to taste
2 tbsp grated Coconut roasted & powdered
2 tbsp Sesame seeds roasted & powdered
1 tsp Dhania & 1 tsp Jeera roasted & powdered
1/4 cup Tamarind Juice

Method :

  1. Cut chillies lengthwise into 2 or 4 pieces each
  2. Heat oil & fry the chillies in them till the colour changes to light green
  3. In the same oil fry ginger -garlic paste
  4. Add chopped onions & fry till brown
  5. Add chopped tomatoes & fry well.
  6. Add all the powdered ingredients and fry well.
  7. Add tamarind juice & salt to taste & let it cook for a while
  8. Finally mix in the fried chilli pieces and turn off the heat.
