Rice Kheer

Ingredients

Basmati riceĀ50 grams
Ghee(Clarified butter)
Sugar
Milk -1 litre
Cardamom
Cashewnuts and raisins

Method

  1. Wash and drain rice.
  2. Keep the rice aside for sometime till it is completely dried.
  3. Heat ghee in a pan. Add the rice and fry till light brown.
  4. Add milk to the rice and keep stirring till the mixture thickens.
  5. Add sugar.
  6. Remove from heat and sprinkle grounded cardamom.
  7. Add chopped cashews and raisins.
  8. Keep in the fridge and serve chilled.

Hope you like it.
