Wonderful Bhath in a Jiffy

Ingredients

Long grain rice 1cup (cooked)
Sambar (fresh/left over) 1 cup
Rasam (fresh/left over) 1 cup
Vegetables(fresh/left over) carrots/beans/potatoes/tomatoes (optional)
Peanuts 10-20 (optional)
Cloves 5 (powdered)
Cardamum 5 (powdered)
Cimmamon 1 stick (powdered)
Oil 1 table spoon
Salt to taste
Tamarind jiuce 1-2 spoons to taste

Method:

  1. Heat oil, add spices tamarind juice, and all the other ingradients.
  2. Stir thoroughly and pressure cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Serve with potato chips.
  4. You can also try this dish when you are lazy and desperate to clean the fridge.
