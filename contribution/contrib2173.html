Vegetable and Potato Fritters

Ingredients

2 Cups (250 g) Chick Pea Flour
1/2 Cup (60 g) Self-raising Flour
2 Tbspn Corn Flour
3 Tbspn Rice Flour
1 Tspn Baking Powder
1 Large Potato, coarsely grated
1 Large Carrot , coarsely grated
1 Large Onion , finely chopped
1/4 Tspn Chilli powder
1/4 Tspn Cummin powder
1/4 Tspn Turmeric powder
3 Spring Onion , finely chopped
1 Tbspn fresh Lemon Juice
1 1/2 Cups (375 ml) Oil
1Cup (250 ml) Water approximately

Method

  1. In a bowl, combine besan, self-raising flour, corn flour, rice flour and baking powder.
  2. Add potato, carrot, onion, chilli, cummin, turmeric, lemon juice, 1 table spoon of hot oil and enough water to a thick batter. Let it stand for 5 minutes.
  3. Heat remaining oil in a frying pan over moderate heat. Drop batter, 1 tbsp at a time, into the oil and fry both sides until brown.
  4. Drain on absorbent paper.
  5. Serve hot with Chutney, Chilli Sauce or Tomato Sauce.

Fritters should always be served hot immediately after cooking, and cannot be reheated.
