Ripe Mango Kadi (Pulichery)

Ingredients

Ripe mango - 2
Thick curds - 2 cups
Coconut grate - 3/4 cup
Red chillies - 3
Green chillies - 2 (slit)
Cumin seeds-1 tsp
Oil - for bhagar
Mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds
Salt and jaggery to taste

Method:

  1. Cut ripe mangoes and steam cook for 5 mins with the seeds. Once cooled squeeze out as much juice as possible. Add 1/2 cup water and simmer on low heat for 5-7 mins with 1/2 tsp turmeric pwdr and salt.
  2. Grind together coconut, red chillie and cumin seeds to a smooth paste.
  3. Beat the curds. Add 1/2 of the ground paste to the curd. The balance paste add to the simmering mango kadi. Mix well and simmer. Add 1 tsp jaggery and simmer further. Off the gas. Slowly add the curd-paste mixture and stir. Switch on the gas and on low heat simmer for 3 mins taking care the kadi does not curdle.
  4. Off the gas. In a kadai add 2 tsp oil (coconut oil or sunflower). Add 1 tsp mustard and 1/2 tsp fenigreek seeds. Once they sputter add to the mango kadi andserve hot with rice and pappads.
