Tiramisu

Ingredients

500 grams Mascarpone Cheese
1-1/2 cups Expresso or Strong Coffee
6 tablespoons Sugar
2 packages Lady Finger Biscuits
1 oz Liqueur of your choice (Tia Maria, Kaluha, Baileys etc )

Method:

  1. Separate egg yolks from egg whites.
  2. Beat egg yolks with sugar until creamy and smooth.
  3. Add the Mascarpone cheese and liqueur.
  4. Beat egg whites until firm and fluffy and add to the cheese mixture.
  5. Dip Lady Finger biscuits into coffee and layer the bottom of a pie dish.
  6. Cover with cheese mixture.
  7. Repeat another layer of biscuits and cheese mixture
  8. Sprinkle with semi sweet chocolate shavings and refrigerate for atleast 3 hours before serving. Serve and Enjoy
