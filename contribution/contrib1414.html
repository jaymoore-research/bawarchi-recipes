Palak Rolls

Ingredients

8-10 big leafs of Palak [spinach].
3 medium sized Potatoes boiled & mashed throughly.
1 Green Chilli chopped finely.
1/2 tsp. Red chilli pwd.
1/2 tsp. Black Pepper pwd.
1/2 tsp.roasted Cumin pwd.
1/2 tsp.dry Coriander pwd.
2 tsp.Chat masala pwd.
2 tbsp.green Coriander finely chopped.
Salt to taste, Oil to fry.

For batter :-

One cup besan.
Water 1/2 cup approximately.
Ajwain 1/4 tsp.
Little Red Chilli pwd.
1/2 tsp. Salt.
A pinch of Baking soda.

Method:-

  1. Wash the palak leafs & allow them to dry.
  2. Mix besan, water & masala's together to make a thick batter.
  3. Mix mashed potatoes & all masala's throughly, divide it into 8-10 parts.
  4. Take palak leaf & put one part of masala on it, flatten it & roll the leaf.
  5. Dip the leaf in the batter & deep fry in hot oil untill golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with Tomato Kethup.
