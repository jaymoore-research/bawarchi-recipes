Kozhukkatta With Jaggery

Ingredients

Rice Powder 2 cups
Water for mixing accordingly
Jaggery 7 to 8 spoons
Coconut shredded 1 and half cups
Cardamom powder 2 pinches(small)

Method:

  1. Heat up the very fine rice powder till the smell comes out but before turning colour. Then mix it in boiled water adding salt as if you make the dough.
  2. Mix Sharkara (Jaggery), Thenga (coconut) and Cardamom powder separately in another plate. Do not mix water in it.
  3. Then take the balls of rice paste and open it and stuff it with the other mix (Sarkara etc.). Then steam it till it is ready for use.
  4. If you do not have steaming facility, you may do as follows. Take an utensil and boil water in it. Put the balls one by one in the water when it is boiling. Then keep the flames in sim and keep it till it is ready for serving.
