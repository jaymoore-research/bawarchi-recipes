Prawn Curry

Ingredients

Prawns (cleaned and deveined) - 25 small ones
Onion -Ā 3 medium
OilĀ- 2 tblsps
Mrichi PowderĀ - 2 tsp
Tamarind paste - 1/2 tsp
Salt to taste

Method

  1. Take oil in kadhai & saute the onions till golden brown.
  2. Add mirchi powder & tamarind paste andĀfry forĀcouple ofĀseconds.
  3. Now add the prawns & fry for 2 seconds.
  4. Add water and salt and cook till done.

Serving Size: 2-3 people
