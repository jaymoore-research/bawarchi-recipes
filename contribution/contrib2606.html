Baked Rice

Ingredients

1 cup Rice
1 tin Baked Beans
1 small can Pineapple
1 glass Milk
3-4 Green Chillies
Black Pepper, Salt to taste
Cheese (grated for topping)
1 tsp Butter

Method:

  1. Soak rice for 1/2 hour and half boil them
  2. Now add milk and let it boil for few more min.
  3. Add pineapple pieces salt pepper switch off the gas
  4. Take some butter heat it add green chillies and baked beans fry for few min.
  5. Now take a baking dish grease it add one layer of rice, prepared beans, then again rice.
  6. Add grated cheese on top and bake it at moderate temperature for 5-10 min.
  7. Serve hot.
