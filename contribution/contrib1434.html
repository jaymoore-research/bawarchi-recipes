Poricha Kuzhambu

Ingredients

1- Chowchow
1/2 spoon - Turmeric powder
Little Asefotida
1/2 spoon - Urad dhal
6 to 7 Pepper whole
4 to 5 Red Chillies
1 spoon - Zeera whole
Salt - required
1/2 cup of Toor dal
1/4 cup of Coconut

For seasoning :

1 spoon - Mustard
2 to 3 Curry leaves

Grind together :

Take little oil and fry aseofodita, urad dhal, pepper, red chillies and zeera. Grind it with coconut.

Method :

  1. Take chow chow and boil it in water with little turmeric and salt.
  2. Boil the toor dhal in cooker and keep it aside.
  3. After chow chow is cooked add the ground mixture and let it cook for sometime. Then add cooked toordhal and after it is done season it with curry leaves and mustard.

Note :

Other vegetables that can be used instead of chayotte are Snake gourd, Drumstick.
You can use moong dhal instead of toordhal.
For thickening Rice Flour can also be used if necessary.
