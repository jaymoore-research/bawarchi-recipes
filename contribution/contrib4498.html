Raw Tomato Pickle

Ingredients

Raw Tomatoes : 10 nos.
Cumin Seeds, Fenugreek & Asafoetida : Powdered
Turmeric Powder : 1/4 teaspoon
Red Chilly Powder : 3 teaspoons
Sesame Oil : large qty.
Salt : to taste

Method:

  1. Chop tomatoes fine. Mix with turmeric, salt & red chilly powder, but do not keep it for a long time.
  2. In sesame oil, fry mustard & then add finely chopped tomatoes & let it boil, without adding water. After boiling, add the powdered ingredients & stop fire immediately.
  3. Its delicious with rice. Add a little bit of sugar or jaggery to serve with chapatti.

Tips: If needed, add ripe tomatoes in lieu of raw tomatoes. Do not fail to mash them if ripe tomatoes are used.
