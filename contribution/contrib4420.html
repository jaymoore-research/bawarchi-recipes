Gurkhali Chutney

Ingredients

Boiled Potato 500 gms
Roasted Sesame (til) 100 gms
Lemon Juice 2-3 tsps
10-12 chopped Green Chillies
3 medium size Onions sliced thinly
2 Radish (optional) sliced thinly
3 Tomatoes cut into cubes
4 tsp chopped Coriander
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Cube the boled potato.
  2. Powder the roasted til.
  3. Mix all the ingredients and add lemon juice. Mix well.
  4. It can be eaten as a snack. Serve with roti as a vegetable.
