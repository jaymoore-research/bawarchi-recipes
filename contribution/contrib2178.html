Left over Khichdi/Rice pakodas

Ingredients

Left over Khichdi or Rice
Rice powder or Rava 2 Tsp
Salt
Chilli powder
Amchoor Powder
Green chillies if U want
Oil for frying pakodas

Method:

  1. Mix leftover khichadi/Rice with salt, chilli powder, cut green chillies and Amchoor powder as per your taste
  2. Mix in either Rice powder or rava into it.
  3. Make small balls of it and fry in a deep kadhai
  4. Have hot with Coriander Chutney and a cup of tea!
