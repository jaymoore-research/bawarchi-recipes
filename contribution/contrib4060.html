Buttermilk Chillies

Ingredients

Long and Big Green Chillies - 250 gm.
Sour Butter Milk - 1 litre
Salt - To taste (more salt is needed)
Turmeric Powder - 1 tsp

Method:

  1. First of all thick butter milk is required. To this butter milk add turmeric powder and salt.
  2. Wash the green chillies and slit them with a safety pin.
  3. Put all the green chillies in the butter milk.
  4. Keep the green chillies in butter milk for three days covered. Daily green chillies should be mixed well for a minute.
  5. On the fourth day squees butter milk from chillies, spread them in a plate and dry in the sun.
  6. In the night put back all the greenchillies in the butter milk and cover them. Next day repeat the same procedure given in 5 and like this dry the chillies for 7 days.
  7. These well dried chillies can be stored in an air tight container and are to be deep fried before eating with rice.
  8. These chillies can also be added to chudva mixture after deep frying. Salt quantity should be more in this recipe.
