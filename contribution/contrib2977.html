Bharath Rice

Ingredients

1.. Carrot-1
2.. Cabbage-1/2
3.. Beans-5 to 6(optional)
4.. Ginger-size of a Gooseberry
5.. Garlic-5 pods
6.. Green Chillies-2
7.. Salt to taste
8.. Oil to fry
9.. Pepper
10.. Boiled Rice-1 cup
11.. Onion-1(finely chopped)

Method:

  1. Grind items 4,5&6 together in a mixie.
  2. Heat the oil in a pan
  3. Fry the onions till golden Brown
  4. Add the grounded mixture & fry till the odour evaporates
  5. Add the vegetables & cook till soft with salt
  6. Add the rice to the above mixture
  7. Add pepper & mix it well before serving
  8. Serve hot with potato chips & Onion raita.
