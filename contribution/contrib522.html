Pappad rolls

Ingredients

Pappad [Kachha] 8nos
Potato medium 3nos[peeled and half boiled]
Beans [French] 8nos[cut into small pieces]
carrot 2tbs[shredded]
cauliflower 4tbs[cut into small flowerettes]
Garlic 6pods[cut finely]
Ginger 1tea spoon[chopped finely]
Green chillies 3nos[cut into fine pieces]
Onion medium 2nos[cut into small pieces]
Pudina and Dhania 1tb spoon
patta
Salt & pepper to taste

Ingredients for frying:

Oil for frying

For batter:-

2tb spoon corn flour,
2tb spoon maida,
2 tb spoon atta,
salt and a pinch of soda Add all ingredients and mix well

Method:

  1. In a kadai pour little oil or butter.
  2. Put garlic, ginger, green chillies.
  3. Fry for 2 to 3 min.
  4. Then add onion pieces.
  5. Saute for 5 minutes (till it changes color).
  6. Add a pinch oh haldi.
  7. Then add beans, carrots cauliflower.
  8. Sprinkle little water and cook on low fire for 10 minutes. Don't overcook.
  9. Lastly add half boiled shredded potatoes.
  10. Cook till done. Let it cool.
  11. Take a polythene sheet (bigger than Lijjat Pappad)
  12. Slightly heat the pappad over gas for elasticity.
  13. Immediately Keep it over the sheet.
  14. Put vegetable stuff on one side.
  15. Then Roll it over the sheet.
  16. Seal the edges with Maida paste.
  17. Prepare all the Pappad rolls in this fashion.
  18. Dip each roll in the batter.
  19. Fry when the oil is really hot.
  20. See that the inside of the pappad is fried evenly.
  21. When crisp remove from oil.
  22. Cut each roll into 4 pieces.

Serve with Ketchep.

Enough for 5 persons.
