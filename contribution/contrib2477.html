Coconut-Mango Chutney

Ingredients

1 Coconut
3/4 Raw Mango (sour)
Red Chillies 7 to 8
Salt to taste
Turmeric little
1/2 spoon Mustard seeds
1 spoon Urad Dal
1 spoon Oil
1 pinch Asafoetida

Method

  1. Break the coconut and make small pieces.
  2. Peel the mango and make small pieces.
  3. In a frying pan put 1 spoon oil, mustard seeds, urad dal, red chillies , asafoetida. Allow the seeds to splutter. Put them aside. Put all the coconut and mango pieces in a Mixie or grinder add all the fried red chillies ,salt, turmeric. Blend them finely by adding water little by little to a fine paste. Remove contents in bowl.
  4. Add the fried mustard and urad dal seeds and mix them them well.
  5. The chutney is ready. It tastes very well with Dosa, Idlis, and with Rice too.
