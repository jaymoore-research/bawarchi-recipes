Quick Rice Kheer

Ingredients

11/2 litre milk
8 tbsps sugar (or according to taste)
6 tbsps basmati rice (soaked for at least 5 mts)
1/2 tsp of cardamom seeds crushed
Raisins (according to taste)
Almonds (according to taste crushed)
Grated or cut into thin slices dry khopra (according to taste)
1/4 cup of water

Method

  1. Take a pressure cooker, pour the milk in it, add sugar, water and set it on high heat.
  2. Keep stirring and bring it to a boil. Make sure that the milk does not burn.
  3. Add the crushed cardamom and rice.
  4. Close the lid of the pressure cooker and let the whistle blow once, then reduce the heat to between low and medium high and let cook for the next 15 minutes.
  5. Now switch off the heat and let the pressure cooker cool down on it's own, this is very important, as this is what will give the kheer its red color and consistency.
  6. After the cooker is cooled, open the lid and add the raisins, almonds and khopra (dry coconut).
  7. Serve hot or cold.

P.S If you want a thick consistency and color you can keep the cooker on heat for 5 minutes more and even after it has cooled down, let it remain closed as long as you can.
