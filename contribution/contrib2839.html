JhatPat Khana

Ingredients
(for two persons)

Milk bread-4 slices
Oil-2 tbspoon
Egg-2
Onion-one medium size
Garlic-2 cloves
Ginger-1/2 inch piece
Green chillies-as you wish
Capsicum-one small size
Coriender leaves-2 tbspoon
Tomato ketchap-1 tbspoon
Salt-as you wish

Method:

  1. First chop the onion, garlic, ginger, green chillies, coriander leaves, capsicum & cut the breads in small square pieces.
  2. Then heat oil in a pan. Now fry the onion until golden brown in color.
  3. Then mix chopped garlic, ginger & green chillies. Stir fry for sometime.
  4. Now add chpped capsicum, coriander leaves & salt. After sometime add bread pieces into it. Again stir fry it. At last add eggs in it & stir whole thing fast for one minute. Then mix tomato ketchup with it.
  5. Now your jhatpat khana is ready to serve.
