Tomato and Onion Chutney

Ingredients

1 large Onion
3-4 small Ripe Tomatoes
A few Garlic pods
A small bunch of Coriander Leaves and Curry Leaves
Half cup of freshly grated Coconut
1 tablespoon each of Channa dal and Urud dal
4 Dry Red Chillies

Method:

  1. Slice the onion in large pieces and chop the tomatoes into large pieces and set aside
  2. In a heavy bottomed kadhai pour in a tablespoon of oil and fry the dals and red chillies. When it changes colour add in the onion and garlic. Let it become transparent and not brown.
  3. Add in the tomatoes and cover it with a lid. Turn off the flame.
  4. When the above mixture turns cool add coconut, curry leaves, coriander leaves and salt. Now grind this mixture in a blender without adding water. (The tomatoes have sufficient water) You may season this with mustard.
