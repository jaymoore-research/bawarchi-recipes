Mango Pie

Ingredients

Graham Cracker pie crusts
1 can mango pulp (basically 3 cups mango pulp)
3 envelopes unflavoured gelatin (preferably knox brand)
3 cups sour cream
8 oz cream cheese
3 cups boiling water
1 cup sugar

Method

  1. Dissolve gelatin in boiling water.
  2. Blend cream cheese, sugar and gelatin mixture, fold in sour cream and blend.
  3. Add mango pulp and stir, pour the mixture into the pie crusts and refrigerate (do not freeze) for 3-4 hours.
  4. Slice it and serve.

This recipe makes 4 pies, you could use half of everything and make two pies. Enjoy your pie!
