Cornflake Umm Ali

Ingredients

1 cup cornflakes
2 tsp.pistachois-sliced
2tsp.almonds-sliced
1tbsp-raisins
1 cup sugar
1 and half cups milk
2 tbsp sweetened condensed milk
a pinch of cinnamon powder
1 tbsp glazed cherries chopped

For decoration-chopped fruits of your choice-like cherries, peaches,mango,seedless grapes etc.

Method:

  1. Take an ovenproof casserole.mix all ingredients except the milk,condensed milk and fruits for decoration.
  2. Boil the milk. Add condensed milk.
  3. Add this to the cornflake mixture.
  4. Bake in a preheated oven until it is brown on top.
  5. Decorate with fruits and serve warm.
