Cabbage Raita

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients:

Leafy Cabbage - 1 cup(Cut into Thin Medium Long pieces)
Salt To taste
Pepper powder to Taste
Yogurt/Curd - 1 1/2 cup

Method:

Mix all the above ingredients and adjust the Yogurt/Curd's Consistency as per Requirement........Leave it for 1 Hour so that the Cabbage becomes soft.........

If Needed, can even give tempering with Oil and Mustard...........

Serve it with Roti,Puri or Pulav
