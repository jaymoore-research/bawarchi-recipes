Tomato Rice

Ingredients

Green Chillies 6 -8 no's - slit in two;
Onions 1 big one sliced finely;
Coriander leaves 1/2 bundle chopped & washed;
Turmeric Powder 1/4 level tspn;
Salt to taste;
Ginger/Garlic Paste 3/4 level tbspn each;
Curry leaves few;
Mint leaves 8 - 10 no's;
Tomatoes Big ones 8 no's mashed
Rice 2 Cups - soaked for 20/25 mins. washed, drained & kept in cooker bucket.
Oil 1/4 cup.

Method

  1. Fry the onions, green chillies, curry leaves mint leaves coriander leaves till onions are brown.
  2. Add the Ginger/Garlic paste & fry till nice smell comes.
  3. Now add the the salt, mashed tomatoes, turmeric powder & mix nicely.
  4. Add this fried mixture to the Rice in the Electric Rice Cooker Bucket, add 3 cups water, cover cooker, plug on & let the Rice cook, it will cut off on it's own when Rice is done.
