Aloo Dum

Ingredients

1 lb. small aloo(potato)
1/2 green or red chilly
1/2 cup yogurt or 1/4 cup yogurt and 1/2 tomato.
1/4 cup water
salt to taste
oil to fry masala (Spices)

Spices:

1/4 cup ground onion paste.
1/4 tsp. each haldi, chilly and dhaniya-zeera powder.
3tbs. finely chopped green coriander.
1 tsp. ground ginger-garlic paste.

Method

  1. Boil potatoes and peel.
  2. Heat the oil. Fry the onion paste till brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and fry for another minute.
  3. Then add yogurt or yogurt+tomato, salt to taste, haldi, mirchi and dhaniya-zeera powder. And fry till the oil separates from the masala.
  4. Add aloo and fry aloo in this masala in medium heat with a lid on for some more time (till you feel that aloo has all the masala coating).You have Aloo-dum without gravy.
  5. Add water If you want some gravy.
