Capsicum with Peanuts

Ingredients

Big capsicum - 4
Roasted peanuts - 5 tsp
Coconut - 4 tsp (grated)
Tamarind paste - 2 tsp
Jeera - 1 tsp
Oil - 3 tsp
Garam masala - 2 tsp
Red chillie powder - 1 tsp
Turmeric - a pinch
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Soak the roasted peanuts for 10 min.
  2. Cut the capsicum in to pieces.
  3. Put the oil in a pan, add jerra and allow it to split. Then add capsicum pieces cook until the pieces are soft.
  4. Grind the peanuts with grated coconut & tamarind paste with little amount of water.
  5. Add this paste to the capsicum in the pan .
  6. Allow it to cook for 2 min.
  7. Add salt, chillie powder, garam masala & turmeric
  8. Cook until the oil floats on the gravy.
  9. Sprinkle some coriander.

Serve hot with Chapati or Fried rice. Hope you will enjoy this recipe.
