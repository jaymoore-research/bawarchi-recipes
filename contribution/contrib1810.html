Vegetable Kurma

Ingredients

2 Potatoes (diced)
1 Cauliflower (cut into flowerrets)
3 Carrots (diced)
1 cup Peas

To be roasted and ground into a paste

2 tsp Corriander seeds
1 tsp Jeera
1" Ginger
1" Cinnamon stick
4 Cloves
4 Pepper
1 tsp Turmeric powder
3 tsp Chilli powder
1 big sized Onion (roasted directly on flame)

Method:

  1. Deep fry the diced potatoes in oil.
  2. Boil cauliflower, carrots and peas, just till done.
  3. Heat oil in a pan. Fry the ground masala for 10 mins. When the oil starts showing up from the sides, add the boiled vegetables. Fry for sometime.
  4. Lastly add the fried potatoes and salt. Just sprinkle a little water but do not make the gravy to thin.

Serve it hot with chopped corriander leaves. Goes well with Puris.
