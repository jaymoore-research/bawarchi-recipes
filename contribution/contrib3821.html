Cake Toast

Ingredients

All Purpose Flour -3 cups
Butter/Margarine- 1 cup
Sugar -1 cup
Eggs -3 nos.
Vanilla Essence -1 tsp.
Baking powder -2 tsp.

Method:

  1. Preheat oven at 350 degrees.
  2. Mix flour and baking powder.
  3. Beat eggs well with sugar add butter and beat well till fluffy. Add vanilla essence. Now add flour mix. Mix well with hands.
  4. Cover the dough with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 2 hrs.
  5. Make two loaves about 7 x 3 inches. Put it in two baking dish of same size of loaves or longer. Bake at 350 degrees for 25-30 minutes or till knife inserted comes out clean.
  6. Cool it and with serrated knife cut into 1/2 inch thick slices.
  7. Reduce temperature to 300 degrees.In two baking trays put slices and bake for 30-40 minutes or till it becomes brown and crisp.
