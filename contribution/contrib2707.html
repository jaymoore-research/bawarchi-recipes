Ingredients

For Chutney

Urad daal - 3 tsp
Fenugreek (methi) seeds - 1/4 tsp
Cumin seeds - 1/2 tsp
Pepper Corns - 5-6
Red Chillies - 1-2
Green Chillies - 1-2
Turmeric - 1/4 tsp
Fresh Coriander - 1 tbsp
Fresh grated Coconut - 1 small cup
Sugar - 1/2 tsp
Salt(to taste)
Hing(a pinch)
Sour Curds - 2 -3 cups

For Seasoning:

Oil - 2 tsp
Mustard seeds - 1tsp
Cumin seeds - 1/2 tsp
Red Chilli - 1 (split into two pieces)

Method:

  1. Roast Urad daal, fenugreek, cumin, pepper corns, red chillies with a pinch of hing on medium flame till you get good aroma & dall becomes brown.
  2. Grind the above with coconut, haldi, salt, sugar, fresh coriander & green chillies to a chutney consistency.
  3. Take curds in a big bowl & beat till it blends. Then pour chutney & mix well.
  4. Season with mustard, cumin & red chillies, decorate with chopped fresh coriander.

Serve With Veg Pulao, Biryani, any mixed Rice. Goes well with plain rice too.

Cooking Time:20 min
Serves: 4
