Curd Semiya

Ingredients

100 gm Semiya
Water about 1/2 cups
2 tsp. Jeeragam
1 tsp. Mustard
1 tsp. Urad Dal
Few Curry leaves
Few Coriander
4 Green Chillies
Grated Coconut 1/4 cup
10 No. Cashew nuts
10 No. Dry grapes
2 cups of Curd
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Put Semiya in a bowl.
  2. Boil water to 100 Degrees
  3. Pour boiled hot water in the bowl so that Semiya is fully soaked.
  4. Leave it for 5 minutes.
  5. Add 2 cups of curd to it.
  6. Chop chillies into small pieces and add to semiya.
  7. Add cashew nuts and grapes.
  8. Add grated coconut to it.
  9. Heat one tbs. of oil in a shallow pan and add mustard seeds and Urad Dal. When they splutter add curry leaves to it. Then pour it into the bowl.
  10. Add two tsp. of jeeragam to it.
  11. Add salt to taste.
  12. Refrigerate it for three hours.
  13. Add cashew nuts and grapes.
  14. Add grated coconut to it.
  15. Heat one tbs. of oil in a shallow pan and add mustard seeds and Urad Dal. When they splutter add curry leaves to it. Then pour it into the bowl.
  16. Before serving garnish with coriander leaves. Hope you enjoy it.
