Dhal Laddu

Ingredients

Green gram dhal - 250 gms
Powdered sugar - 250 gms
20 cashew nuts
3 cardmums
Ghee - 250 gms
Method

  1. Slightly fry the green gram dhal in tava and powder the fried dhal.
  2. Mix the powdered dhal, powdered sugar, powdered cardmum thoroughly.
  3. For making balls, please add 1 teaspoon of slight heated ghee, 1 cashew nut to the mixture.
  4. Now tasty dhal laddu is ready for serving. This laddu is highly liked by children.

Please try and enjoy this with your family.
