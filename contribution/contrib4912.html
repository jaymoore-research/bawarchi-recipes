Chicken with Fresh Fenugreek

Ingredients

Chicken meat cut into strips - 250 grms
Garlic pulp - 1/2 tsp
Chilli powder - 1 tsp
Salt - 1/2 tsp
Tomato paste - 2 tsp
Oil - 2 tsp
Fenugreek leaves(Methi) (Only leaves) - 1 bunch
Fresh chopped coriander - 1 tbsp
Water - 1 BC

Method

  1. Bring a saucepan of water to the boil add the chicken strips and cook for about 5 to 7 minitues. Drain & set aside.
  2. In a mixing bowl, mix the garlic, chilli powder and salt with the tomato paste.
  3. Heat the oil in large saucepan. Add the tomato paste&spice mixture
  4. Add the chicken pieces & fry for 5 to 7 minitues.
  5. Add the fenugreek leaves & fresh coriander continue to fry for 5 to 7 minutes.
  6. Pour in the water, cover & cooki for about 5 minutes & serve hot with rice or chapatis
