Appu Dragon Sandwich

Ingredients

1 tbsp Shredded Cabbage (squeeze all water)
1 tbsp grated Onion
1 tbsp grated Tomato
3 tbsp Malai
Salt to taste
Pepper powder to taste
Powdered Sugar to taste
6-8 slices of Bread.

Method:

  1. Cut the edges of the bread slices.
  2. Mix all the ingredients.
  3. Fill the bread slices with the filling.
  4. Cover the top with another bread slice. Cut into triangles.
  5. Decorate with a cherry.
  6. Serve in a plate with sauce & chips.
