Aloopalak Masala

Ingredients

2 bunches Spinach
1 large Onion
1 large Tomato
4 medium cloves of Garlic
1 one inch piece Ginger
1-2 Green Chillies
1 medium Potato
1/4 tsp Turmeric powder
1/2 tsp Chilli powder
Salt as per taste
1 tsp Dhania-Jeera powder
1 tsp Garam Masala powder
2 small Elaichis
2 tbsp Oil

Method:

  1. Wash spinach in plenty of water and pressure cook it. When cool, blend in the mixie/blender to make a paste.
  2. Chop the onion and keep aside
  3. Grind tomato, elaichi, ginger, garlic and green chilli to a paste.
  4. Boil the potato and dice it.
  5. Heat oil, add onions and fry till golden brown. Add the tomato paste and cook till oil separates. After that add the spinach puree, diced potatoes, and all the remaining masalas and cover it and simmer on medium flame till oil floats on top.
