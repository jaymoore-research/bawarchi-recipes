Kofta Curry

Ingredients

For Koftas
1 block of paneer 500 gms
Boiled potatoes 3
Green chilies 3
Salt to taste
Crushed almonds 1/2 cup
Raisins 1/2 cup
Corn flour 2 tbsn

For Gravy
Onions 1 cup
Garlic/ginger paste 2 tablespoons
Tomatoes blended 1/2 cup
Chili powder 1 tbsp
Coriander powder 1 tbspn
Turmeric 1 tspn
Mawa/Milk powder 1/2 cup
Water 1-1/2 cups
Sour Cream 1/2 cup
Sugar to taste
Oil

Method:

  1. Mash boiled potatoes, paneer, chopped green chilies, almonds, salt and corn flour. Make small round balls and put a few raisins inside each kofta and flatten them.
  2. Heat oil and fry koftas till brown. Drain and keep aside in serving dish.
  3. For the gravy: Heat oil and fry onion paste for 10 minutes on medium heat. Add garlic-ginger paste and fry till dry.
  4. Add blended tomatoes and fry; add coriander powder, turmeric, salt, chili powder, and cook for 8-10 minutes, till all the water dries up.
  5. Mix mawa in water and add this to pan. Let it boil for 3 minutes. Add some sour cream till gravy is thickened.
  6. Add sugar to taste. Pour the gravy on the koftas. Whisk remaining sour cream and garnish on top.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy !!
