Pumpkin Koottu

Ingredients

White pumpkin, one slice (250 gms)
Jeera seeds (2 table spoon)
Mustard (1 teasppon)
Urad dal (1 teasppon)
Green chillies (2 or 3)
Curry leaves ( 1 arc)
Coconut (optional) 2 or 2 pieces
Chenna dal (1 tablesppon full) (kadalai paruppu)
Tamarind juice, 1 teaspoon
Turmeric powder (1 teaspoon). (Just the way you have turmeric juice for sambar or rasam)

Curry powder or Sambar powder (1 tablespoon).
Salt .

Method:

  1. Cut the pumpkin into small square pieces (not very small)
  2. Heat the Tava and dry fry Chenna dal just for a minute or so, till the dal turns from yellow to light brownish yellow.
  3. Pour 2 glasses of water over the dal in the tava.
  4. Put the cut pumpkin also into the tava.
  5. Sprinkle the turmeric powder and cover the pan. Let the flame be of medium intensity.
  6. Sprinkle the curry powder too.
  7. Also put the turmeric juice.
  8. Put the salt.
  9. In the meantime, put cut coconut, green chillies, jeera seeds in a mixer or blender and grind them into a watery pulp (Like the way you grind for a coconut chutney).
  10. Pour more water as needed in the tava.
  11. Once the pumpkin gets 3/4 th cooked (taste it, it should be soft), pour the ground mixture too and let it boil (Don't overcook, as the pumpkin gets smashed and will become pulpy).
  12. Also fry the mustard, urad dal, Asaoefoetida in oil, may be take another small pan and fry them till mustard pops and urad dal turns red.
  13. Once the punmpkin is cooked put the fried stuff on it and serve hot with fresh curry leaves floating on the top of kootu.
Tips:

The kootu should be slightly viscous.
If it is too watery, put some rice flour (1 teaspoon ) and mix it thoroughly so the rice flour doesn't form balls inside the kootu.
Also, you can add some lemon juice for taste, while being served.
