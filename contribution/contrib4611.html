Potato Snack

Ingredients

6 Medium Sized Potatoes, peeled
3 tsp Chatt Masala.

For Paste:

20 Fresh Mint Leaves
2 tbsp finely chopped fresh Coriander Leaves
5 Green Chillies
1/2 inch fresh Ginger peeled and chopped
1 tsp Lemon Juice

Method:

  1. Boil the potatoes, drain and cool then dice.
  2. Grind the ingredients for the paste with little water and add the chatt masala.
  3. Season with salt and mix. Add the potatoes and mix. Taste and adjust seasoning if need.
  4. Serve with tomato ketchup.
Serves: 7
Preparation time: 5 minutes
Cooking time: 15 minutes
