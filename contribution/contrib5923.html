Instant Dosa

Ingredients

1Cup Rava/Sooji
1/2 Cup Rice flour
2 teaspoon Urad flour
2 teaspoon Wheat flour
1/2 teaspoon Jeera
1/2 teaspoon black pepper.
1 Tomato finely chopped
1Red onion finely chopped
2-3 chilles finely chopped
Few Curry leaves chopped
Ginger (˝ inch grated)
1 Cup Curd
1 Cup water
1/2 teaspoon Hing
Salt to taste
1-2 Tablespoon oil

Method

 

  1. Mix Rava & other flours adding curd & water. Make sure no lumps are formed.
  2. Add all the remaining ingredients one by one.
  3. The final consistency should not be too thick. Add more water if needed.
  4. Finally get the tava/griddle ready.
  5. Pour the batter gradually & slowly spreading it nicely.(this is little different from the usual dosa)
  6. Put few drops of oil around the dosa, while cooking in high & turn around the other side.
  7. The crispy tasty dosa is ready to go with any kind of chutney.
