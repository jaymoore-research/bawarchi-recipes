Thengapaal Kanji (Coconut milk kanji or koozhu)

Ingredients

- 1 cup of rice
- 1 whole garlic (separate & peel off skin)
- 1 tspn fenugreek seeds (methi)
- 1/2 cup coconut milk
- Salt to taste
- 4 cups of water

Method:

  1. In a pressure cooker add rice, garlic, methi and salt cook it for 4 whistles so that the rice gets cooked very well
  2. After the steam goes off mash the rice and add coconut milk get it cooked for 3 mts.
  3. Hot milk kanji is ready to eat with lemon pickle as an easy and healthy breakfast.

Note: If you don't like coconut milk add boiled milk also.
