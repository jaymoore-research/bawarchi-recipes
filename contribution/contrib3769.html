Coconut Roll

Ingredients

Plain Flour 500 gm
Instant Yeast 6 gm
Water 250 gm
Sugar 100 gm
Butter 50 gm

For Filling:

Shredded Coconut
Sugar

Method:

  1. Make a dough and keep it to rise for half an hour. Then divide it into two parts roll it out with a rolling pin into rectangles.
  2. Mix coconut and sugar and spread this on the rectangles. Now roll up the rectangles like swissroll.
  3. Steam this in high heat for 15 minutes. Serve by cutting into slices. This sweets Malaysian name is Pau.
