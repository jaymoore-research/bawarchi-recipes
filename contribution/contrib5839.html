Pudina Chutney

Ingredients

Pudina - 1 bunch
Fresh Coconut - 1/2 cup
Tomatoes - 2 to 3
Tamarind - Lemon size ball
Sesame seeds - 2 tsps
Red chillies - 8 to 10 (depending on taste)
Cumin seeds - 1 tsp
Salt

Method

  1. First warm up sesame seeds, cumin seeds and red chillies on pan and powder them.
  2. In the same pan take two tsps oil fry pudina well and keep aside.
  3. Add 1tsp oil and fry coconut keep aside later add some more oil and put cut tomatoes and cover.
  4. Cook it until soft later put tamarind on the cut tomatoes to make to soft.
  5. Cool all the above ingredients mix the above powder and grind.
  6. Chutney is ready.
  7. Serve it with Idli, Dosa, Hot plain rice or Fried rice. Ā
