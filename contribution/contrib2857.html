Cucumber Chutney

Ingredients

Cucmber - 1 large
Red Chillies - 2
Green Chillies - 4
Coriander - some

For Seasoning

Channa daal - 1 teaspoon
Urad daal - 1/2 teaspoon
Mustard - 3/4 teaspoon
Methi seeds - 1/4 teaspoon
Oil for seasoning
Salt to taste
Tamarind - gooseberry size (or small lemon size)
Hing - a pinch

Method

  1. Peel and cut cucumber into small cubes.
  2. In a kadai put seasoning with all the above ingredients, at the end add red chillies, green chillies and tamarind. Add hing and let it cool.
  3. In a blender grind the seasoning to a coarse paste and the add diced cucumber pieces. Grind it for 5 seconds or so (do not grind it too much.it tastes good when it is not too soft). Add salt to taste.
  4. Garnish with coriander.
  5. Have it with Rice and Ghee/Oil.

Variation: Can do the same with sqush. But roast the cut squash in 1/2 spoon of oil until they become soft. Also, you don't have to peel squash.
