Baked Cauliflower With Tomato Sauce

Ingredients

Cauliflower -1 medium
Onions chopped fine -2 medium
Garlic cloves minced 5-6
Green Chillies finely chopped 2-3
Ginger root minced - a little
Tomato Sauce-1 cup
Chilli-Garlic Sauce - 2-3 tbsps (or depending on how hot you want)
Salt -to taste
Oil- 2 tbsp
Baking dish

Method

  1. Break Cauliflower into big florets and make sure to cut the stem a little short of the flower.
  2. Wash it well and boil it in water with a pinch of salt for 5 minutes. Take off the burner and drain the water.
  3. Heat oil in the kadai and add the chopped onions and garlic and ginger and chillies. Fry on high heat for about 3-4 min. And then add tomato sauce to it and also chilli garlic sauce. Fry for another 3-5 minutes.
  4. Now take the baking dish and arrange the florets on it. Spread the sauce on top of this and bake at 325oF for about 25-30 minutes.
  5. To check if its done insert a fork.
  6. Serve hot with fresh baked bread or the frozen garlic bread available in the frozen section and hot tomato soup.
