Almond Slices

Ingredients

3 medium Eggs
1/2 cup ground Almonds
1/2 cup Milk Powder
1 cup Sugar
1/2 tsp Saffron strands
100 gm Unsalted Butter
1 tbsp flaked Almonds

Method:

  1. Beat the eggs together in a bowl & set aside.
  2. Place the ground almonds, milk powder, sugar & saffron in a large mixing bowl & stir to mix well.
  3. Melt butter in a small saucepan.
  4. Pour the melted butter over the dry ingredients & mix well with a fork.
  5. Add the reserved beaten eggs to the almond mixture & stir to blend well.
  6. Spread the mixture in a shallow ovenproof dish & bake in a pre-heated oven at 160 degree C/ 325 degree F/ Gas Mark 3 for 45 minutes. Test whether the cake is cooked by piercing with the tip of the knife or a skewer- it will come out clean if it is cooked thoroughly.
  7. Once it has cooled, cut the almond cake into slices.
  8. Decorate the almond slices with flaked almonds & transfer them into serving plates. Serve hot or cold. Enjoy!

TIP: These almond slices are best eaten hot but they may also be served cold. They can be made a day or even a week in advance & re-heated. They also freeze beautifully.
