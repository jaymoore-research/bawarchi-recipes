Golden Coins

Ingredients

1. Bread slices - 24(cut in roundels)
2. Chili sauce - 2 tab sp.) Make thick batter
3. Maida - 1/2 cup ) Make thick batter
4. Salt - 1 tsp.
5. Milk - 1 cup ) Make thick batter
6. Onions - 2 big
7. Carrots - 2 big
8. Capsicum - 2 big
9. French beans - 100 gms
10. Cabbage - 100 gms
11. Green chilies - 4
12. Garlic - 3-4 flakes
13. Ginger - 1-1/4 piece
14. Soya sauce - 1 tab sp.
15. Oil - 1 cup

Method:

  1. Apply chilli sauce to each bread roundel & keep aside.
  2. Cut vegetables in Chinese style & shallow fry in oil.
  3. Make batter. Grind together ginger, garlic & green chillies. Mix this paste with fried vegetables & Soya sauce. Put this mixture on each bread roundel cover with batter.
  4. While deep frying put the batter side down.
  5. Serve hot with Tomato Sauce.
