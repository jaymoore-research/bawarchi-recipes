Rice Vermicelli Upma

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

 Ingredients

Rice vermicelli - 250gm
Oil -  2tsp
Onion  - 1/2 finely chopped
Turmeric powder - 1/2tsp
Ginger-garlic paste - 1tsp
Finely chopped capsicum - 2
Mustard seeds - 1/2tsp
Lemon juice - 3tsp
Salt  - 1tsp
Sugar  - 1/2tsp
Grated coconut  - 1/2cup
Curry leaves - 4
Chopped coriander leaves - 5

Method

  1. Boil 1 litre water and add rice vermicelli to it, close the lid and keep it for 10 min, with gas off.
  2. Take powder, onion, curry leaves, ginger-garlic paste, capsicum, fry it till onion turns golden color.
  3. Remove all the water from vermicelli which  is in the other dish, add it to the mixture.
  4. Add grated coconut, lemon juice, salt, sugar, mix them well..
  5. Decorate with  fried cashews and chopped coriander leaves

Cooking time: 30 min
Serving: 4
