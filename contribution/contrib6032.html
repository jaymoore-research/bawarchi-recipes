Brinjal in Oil

Ingredients

Brinjals – 1/2 kgs
Onions – 2 to 3 sliced
Tomato- 1 sliced
Coriander leavesfew
Ginger & garlic paste – 1 sp
Dhania powder – 1/2 sp
Chilly powder – 1/2 sp
Jeera powder – 1/2 sp
Pepper powder – 1/2 sp
Salt according to taste

Method  

  1. In the above ingredients except the brinjals fry all the items in oil & grind to a smooth paste 
  2. Slit the brinjal into 4 & stuff the masala paste inside the brinjals. 
  3. Soak a lemon size tamarind in water &  extract the juice .  
  4. Grind 4 tomatoes separately together & keep aside
  5. Now add 5 to 6 spoon of oil in a cooker & then put mustard seeds &
  6. Also add curry leaves,then whole pepper & add the stuffed brinjals
  7. Fry till the oil leaves out from the brinjal.
  8. Now add the tomato paste & stir for 2 mins & also add the tamarind juice, coriander leaves   &  salt is correct if not add little salt & chilly powder  accordingly
  9. Leave 1 stream in the cooker.
  10. Brinjal bigan is ready this will be good to eat with plain rice or chapattis , roti
