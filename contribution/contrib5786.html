Beera Kaya Curry

Ingredients

1-kgĀ Beera (Turai)
1- onions
2-table spoons of red chili powder
salt for taste(1 table spoon)
popu ginjalu - 1 table spoon and karivepaku.
4 to 5- spoons of oil
pinch of pasupu
1/2 cup of water
1/2Ācup of milk

Method

  1. Peal beera check for bitter with small piece if you taste it bitter remove that bera.
  2. And cut the pealed beeras in to small pieces.
  3. Peal onion , wash it and make it in to small pieces
  4. Put the vesselĀ on stove, dry it and put the oil ,after heating the oil add popu ginjaluĀ with karivepaku and fry it until it gets brown color.
  5. Then put the onion pieces in to it and fry it for 3 min
  6. Put all beera peace's in to it , and add pasupu, red chill powder, salt, and water.
  7. Mix it well and put the lid
  8. Cook this in medium flame ,while cooking mix it 1 or 2 times
  9. After 15 min add milk and mix it and cook until it gets smooth and soft (15 min)
  10. So your beerakaya kura is ready to eat. Ā
