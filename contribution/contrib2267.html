Bread and Mushroom Patties

Ingredients

400 gms fresh Mushrooms, chopped
3 cups Bread, crumbled
1 big Onion, chopped
3 Eggs, beaten
2 tbsp. Butter
1/2 cup Milk/stock
Oil for frying
Bread Crumbs
1/2 tsp. Salt
1/2 tsp. Pepper powder
1 tsp. Chilli powder
Chopped Coriander/Parsley

Method

  1. Saute mushrooms & onion in butter.
  2. Soak bread in milk/stock & add to mushroom mix along with eggs, salt, pepper, coriander and chilli powder.
  3. Mix well, cool and shape into patties, roll in bread crumbs and fry till golden brown on both sides.
  4. Garnish with pineapple slices, cherry tomatoes and parsley sprigs.
  5. Serve. Enjoy cooking
