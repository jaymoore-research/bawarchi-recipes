Rice Roti

Ingredients

Rice Flour 1 cup
Jeera 1 tsp
Onions 1/2 cup
Salt 1 tsp
Corriander Leaves finely cut 1/2 cup
Green Peppers 2-3 finely chopped

Method:

  1. Put the rice flour in a vessel, add chopped onions, jeera, green peppers , salt, corriander leaves, mix them well .
  2. Now add water , in little quantities to make a dough. Once the dough is prepared make balls & place them aside.
  3. Now take a pan, smear little oil on the surface & then take the ball & pat it on the pan to make it round. Pat it well so that it becomes a nice thin layer. Make hole at four places in the centre .
  4. Now put oil on all sides & in the hole so that the oil can seap in to make it nice & crisp on all sides. After putting oil, close a lid on top of the pan & cook it on medium heat. Keep checking it so that it dosent burn. When it is golden brown remove it & serve hot with pickles or chutney.
