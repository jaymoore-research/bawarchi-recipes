Coconut Halwa

Ingredients

1 cup grated Coconut
1/2 cup Jaggery
1/4 cup Water
1 tsp. Ghee
2 Cashewnuts broken into peices
1/2 tsp. Cardamom powdered.

Method :

  1. First heat ghee in slow fire in a pan for 2 minutes. Add cashewnuts and fry till it becomes slightly brown. Keep it aside.
  2. Heat jaggery & water in slow fire till jaggery melts fully. Add grated coconut and keep stirring till it leaves sides.
  3. Add the fried cashewnuts and cardamom powder and stir it.
  4. Can serve it hot or as it is.
