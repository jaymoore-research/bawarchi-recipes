I am Charu Wadhawan. I appreciate the reader contributions on Bawarchi and try them out at times. I would like to contribute a recipe for Mathri or Mathhi. This is a salty snack, eaten best with any pickle and washed down with a cup of Masaala chai. This is an anytime snack! I make this whenever we are going on a trip so we can munch on as we drive!!

Mathri

Ingredients

2 Cups Sooji or Semolina
1 Cup wheat flour (or atta that you use for making chapattis)
Salt to taste
5 teaspoons oil
Whole Black pepper corns
Oil for frying (Ouch !! This is a deep fried recipe)

Method :

  1. Combine all ingredients except the last one (which is oil for frying) and knead into a stiff dough. (Take care not to add too much oil, or else your mathris will come out like thick oily pooris).
  2. Make small balls out of the dough and roll into small round pooris.
  3. Now, before frying, pierce each mathri with fork several times. This is so that it doesnot fluff up when fried.
  4. Heat oil for frying and fry each mathri.

Enjoy with pickle.
