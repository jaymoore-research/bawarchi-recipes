Ari Payasam

Ingredients

Raw Rice 1 cup
Sugar 250 gms
Milk 1 litre
Elaichi (powdered) 1 tsp

Method:

  1. Boil the milk. Now Keep aside 1/2 the boiled milk.
  2. Clean the Rice in Fresh water.
  3. Boil water separately. When the water starts to boil, add the cleaned rice.Stir it. Half cook the rice
  4. Once the rice is half cooked, add the milk. Keep stirring for 10 mts continously.
  5. Add the sugar after 10 mts & then bring it to a boil.
  6. Pour the remaining milk kept aside & stir for another 2 mts.
  7. Switch off the gas & then add the elachi powder.

Serve Hot.
