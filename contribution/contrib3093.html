Chilli Noodle and Cashew Stir-fry

Ingredients

200 gm Thin Noodles
1/4 cup Oil
2 teaspoons chilli oil
25 grams Ginger
3 small Red Chillies, pounded
1/2 cup unsalted, roasted Cashew nuts
1 Red Capsicum, sliced diagonally
225 gm can Whole Baby Corn, drained
100 gm Bean Sprouts
1 tbsp Soya Sauce
2 tbsp Thai Sweet Chilli Sauce
2 tbsp chopped Spring Onions

Method:

  1. Cook noodles in large pan of simmering water until just tender, drain.
  2. Heat oil in wok or pan, add ginger, add chillies, cook over medium heat 1 minute. Add cashews, toss 1 minute or until golden.
  3. Add vegetables to pan, cook over meduim heat 3 minutes or until vegetables are tender. Stir in noodles and combined sauces. Toss until noodles are heated through and all ingredients are combined.
  4. Serve immediately sprinkled with spring onions.

Preparation Time: 10 min.
Cooking Time: 5 minutes
Serves 4
