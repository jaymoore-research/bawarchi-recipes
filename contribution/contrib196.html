Cornflex Chaat

Ingredients

100 gms cornflex
100 gms sweet curds
1 tbsp red chilly powder
1 tbsp cummin seed powder
2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
1 onion thinly cut
1 tomato finely cut
2 tbsp tamarind chutney
few coriander leaves
salt to taste.

Method

  1. Add to the curds red chilly pwd, cummin seed pwd,ginger garlic paste and salt.
  2. Take the cornflex in a bowl and add to it chopped onions and tomatoes.
  3. Now add the mixed curds to the cornflex and mix it well.
  4. Sprinkle some red chilly pwd and cummin seed pwd on top.Add some tamarind chutney to it.
  5. Garnish it with some coriander leaves.

Enjoy your snack.
