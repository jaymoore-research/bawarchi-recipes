Paneer Makhani

Ingredients

Paneer -200 gms.
Tomato Puree -4 tbsp.
Tomato Ketchup -4 tbsp.
Half & Half Cream -6 tbsp.
Sugar -1 tsp.(opt.)
Capsicum -1 med.(cubed deseeded)
Ginger -1" greated
Butter -1 tsp.
Salt -to taste
Pepper -1/4 tsp.
Water -1/4 cup.

Method

  1. Heat butter for 20 seconds. Add tomato puree, stir, add tomato sauce, mix well.
  2. Add water, stir then add capsicum, cook for two minutes add paneer, salt, pepper, sugar & ginger. Cook for two minutes.
  3. Add cream and stir cook for a minute.
  4. Serve hot with Naan or Tandoori Roti's.
