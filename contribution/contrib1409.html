Roasted Chicken

Ingredients

Six chicken quarter
1 tbs red chilli
1 tbs meet curry Masala
1 tbs kala Masala
1 tbs salt

Method:

  1. First remove all the skin of chicken quarter
  2. Dry it very well
  3. Then take red chill, salt & masala srinkle in one dish put the chicken on it apply the mixture on both the side of chicken marinate it for 3 hour
  4. Broil the chicken at 300 for complete 2 hour check it regularly after 15 mintues, change the side also
  5. Enjoy it when it's hot with lemon juice

Serving for three guy
Time 5 hour
