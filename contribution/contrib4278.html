Dum Ke Pasanday

Ingredients

1/2 kg flattened Mutton pieces (pasanday)
2 tsp Ginger Garlic paste
2 tsp Chilli powder
1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
1 cup Curd
1/2 cup Cream
100 gm Paneer (mashed)
1 bunch fresh Coriander (chopped)
1 bunch fresh Mint (chopped)
8 Green Chillies (chopped)
1 Onion
2 tbsp Almond powder or kaju powder
1 tsp Garam Masala
4 tbsp Oil
1/4 tsp Saffron ground in 2 tbsp water
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Rub salt ginger garlic paste to the pasanday if the meat is hard rub 1 tsp of ground papaya peel. Keep for 10-15 minutes.
  2. Mix the remaining ingredients and rub well.
  3. Arrange in a baking dish and on top arrange tomato or egg slices and bake in a moderate oven 180oC for 30 to 40 minutes.
