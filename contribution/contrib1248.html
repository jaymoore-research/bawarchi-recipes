Doi Maach

Ingredients

Fish (Grasscarp or Catfish or Buffalo fish), cut into medium sized pieces

For Masala:

(This amount of masala is good for about 4 to 5 pieces of fish)
Yogurt - 4 tablespoonful
Onion paste - 4 tblspoonful
Ginger paste - 1/2 teaspoonful
Cinnamon stick - a pinch
Small cardamom - 4 powdered
Turmeric powder - 1 teaspoonful
Chilli powder - 2 teaspoonful (or according to your taste)
Cumin powder - 1/2 teaspoonful
Salt to taste
Cooking oil.

Method :

  1. Wash the fish properly and mix all the masala (except oil, cinnamon and cardamom) in it and marinade for about 1 to 2 hours.
  2. Heat 2 tblspoonful of oil in a heavy bottomed skillet and put the cinnamom in it.
  3. Then put the fish pieces side by side.
  4. Set the flame to low and put all the mixture on top of the fish and cover the skillet with a lid. After 5-6 minutes turn the fish pieces carefully and again cover it and let them cook for few more minutes.
  5. Check whether they are cooked thouroughly and then add the ground cardamom and transfer the whole preparation in a tray. This goes well with rice.
