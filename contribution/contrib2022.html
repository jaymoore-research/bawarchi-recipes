Tomato Rice

Ingredients

4-5 large Tomatoes (ripe)
1 large Onion
Few pods of Garlic
Green Chillies
Curry Leaves, Coriander Leaves,
1/2 tea spoon Mustard
1/2 tea spoon Urad dal
1/2 tea spoon Channa dal
1/2 cup freshly grated Coconut
1/2 tea spoon Fennel Seeds (Saunf)
1/2 tea spoon of Turmeric Powder
1/2 tsp Red Chilli Powder 1/2 tsp of Garam Masala
1 1/2 cup long grain Rice cooked just till done in 2 1/2 cup water (take care that the grains are separate)

Method

  1. In a heavy bottomed pan pour in oil and season with mustard, channa dal and urad dal.
  2. When the dals turn brown add in finely chopped onion and garlic and fry for a while till transparent.
  3. Add turmeric, chilli powder and salt and then put in the finely chopped tomatoes and stir fry for a while.
  4. Grind coconut with the fennel seeds into a smooth paste and add it into the tomato. Let the mixture cook of a while and wait till the liquid gets 3/4ths dry.
  5. Take it out of the fire and let it cool. Add all the curry patta and coriander leaves and garam masala powder.
  6. Gently toss in the rice with a wooden ladle till well mixed.
  7. Serve hot with Onion Raita and Papadams.
