Banana Halwa

Ingredients

4 Ripe Bananas
2-3 tsp Ghee
4 to 5 tbsp Sugar
1/4 tsp Elaichi Powder (Cardamom)

Method:

  1. Cut the Bananas into small pieces.
  2. Cook the Bananas in medium fire and keep stirring. Add Ghee and Elaichi powder.
  3. When the Bananas are cooked (it takes about 4 to 5 mins), you get a smell of jackfruit. Add sugar.
  4. Keep stirring, till it comes to a Halwa consistency.

Have it with Bread Toast or Chappati or eat it plainly.
