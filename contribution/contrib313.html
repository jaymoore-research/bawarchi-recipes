Adai (Dhal Dosa)

Ingredients

1 cup chana dhal
1 cup tuar dhal
1/4 cup urad dhal
1/4 cup moongdhal
1/4 cup rice
2 greenchilli
4 redchilli (dry)
1" piece finely cut ginger
a pinch or two hing
salt to taste
1/2 cup (finely cut) onion
1/2 cup (finely cut) coriander
10-12 curry leaves
1/4 cup grated coconut
oil for frying dosa

Method

  1. Soak the dhals together &rice; seperately for 1 hour.
  2. Grind the rice, chillies, curry leaves & ginger together
  3. Then add the dhals &grind; .The mixture should be a thick & smooth dosa batter.
  4. Pour in a vessel &then; add hing,salt,onion,coriander & coconut. Mix well.
  5. Heat a tava or pan & pour aladleful of batter & spread in a circular motion.
  6. Spread a spoon of oil around the dosa turn & cook both sides till well done or crisp (in low flame).
  7. Serve plain or with any sweet/spicy chutney.

A TIP -- to avoid sticking of the dosa (of any kind) add a few drops of oil to the hot pan,then sprinkle salt & rub well with the cut potion of an onion. Dust the pan free of any traces of salt. Now make dosas.
