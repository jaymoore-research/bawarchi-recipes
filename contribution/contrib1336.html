Tomato Gravy

Ingredients

Onions - 2,
Tomatoes - 1/2 kg,
Green chillies - 4-5
Salt to taste,
Sugar - 1 tsp,
Roasted Gram powder - 1 tbl. sp,
Chilli powder - 1/2 tbl. sp.,
Garam masala - 1/4 tsp.,
Oil - 1 1/2 tbl. sp.,
Coriander leaves - 1/2 bunch
Cashewnuts - 2 tbl. sps (chopped)

Method :

  1. Cut onions and tomatoes finely.
  2. Slit green chillies into two.
  3. Heat oil in a pan, season with mustard, black gram dhal and bengal gram dhal.
  4. Add slit green chillies, onions, fry well, till the onion changes its colour.
  5. Add cut tomatoes and fry till the tomato skin comes off from it.
  6. Now pour little water with salt and sugar. Allow it to boil, till it thickens, add garam masala, chilli powder and gram powder.
  7. Cook for a minute and remove from fire.
  8. Garnish with coriander leaves & decorate with fried Cashewnuts.
Serve hot with Idlis or Chapathis.
