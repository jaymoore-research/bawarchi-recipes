Fondnicha Bhaat

Ingredients

2 cups Rice cooked
2/3 green chillies finely cut
2/3 onions (large) finely chopped
1 potato (cut into small pieces)
1/2 tsp mustard seeds
1/2 tsp cumin seeds
1/4 tsp turmeric powder
1 Bay leaf
Oil as required
Handful of Coriander leaves chopped finely
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Heat oil in a vessel.
  2. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds.
  3. Add green chillies, onion and salt.
  4. Once onion turns pink add potatoes and cover with lid.
  5. Once potatoes are cooked add the cooked rice and cover with lid for 2/3 mins. Sprinkle coriander leaves.
  6. Serve hot.
