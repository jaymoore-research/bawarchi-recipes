Huliavalakki

Ingredients

500 gms Chidwas (rice flakes)
50 gms Tamarind
4 tsp Coriander powder
5 whole Red Chillies
50 gms Jaggery
1 tsp. Fenugreek seeds (methi seeds)
1/2 tsp. Mustard seeds
1 tsp Cumin seeds
8 whole Black Pepper corns
A pinch of Asafoetida (Hing)
1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Clean rice flakes and powder them coarsely.
  2. Soak tamarind in 1 glass of water. Squeeze out the juice and sift it through muslin cloth. Mix jaggery in it and keep aside.
  3. Roast red chillis, fenygreek seeds, mustard seeds, cumin seeds whole black peppers, asafoetida(hing) and coriander well.
  4. Grind and mix them in turmeric powder and salt.
  5. Then add to them the tamarind juice and mix it well.
  6. Heat oil. Fry mustard seeds, red chillies, ground nut curry leaves and sesame seeds for 2-3 minutes.
  7. Mix them in rice flakes mixture and serve the end product, sprinkled with coconut powder.

Enjoy the taste !
