Venn Pongal

Ingredients

2 cups of raw rice
1 cup of split yellow moong dhal
A pinch of hing
Curry leaves
1 tbsp of whole peppercons
5 green chilles
2 tsp of chopped ginger
1 tsp of jeera [cummin seeds]
Salt to taste
6 to 7 tbsp of ghee or clarified butter.

Method:

  1. Roast rice and dhal slightly till hot to touch,wash and keep.
  2. In a pressure pan, heat 2 tbsp of ghee, throw in the peppercons and a few curry leaves and the hing (be careful as peppercons tend to splutter and can hurt, so just slightly cover the pan)
  3. Mix in the rice and dhall in the pan with 8 cups of water, after 3 whistles remove from fire.
  4. Crush ginger, chilles and jeera.
  5. Heat the remaining ghee and when hot put the crushed items and stir for a while.
  6. Open the pan mash the rice mixture with crushed items and salt to taste,

You eat this with chutney or gotzu, it is a not for calorie conscious people. My mailing add is sonyahs@uniproindia.com
