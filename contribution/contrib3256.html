Perugu Karasu

Ingredients

Rice Flour: 250 gms
Sagu dana : 50 gms
Chilli powder : as required
Asafoetida : a pinch
Sesame seeds : 1 tablespoon
Cummin seeds : 1/2 tablespoon
Sour curd (yoghurt) : 1 cup
Oil : to fry

Method:

  1. Soak sagudana in sour curd for more than 5 hours.
  2. Add soaked sagudana, rice flour and all other ingredients.
  3. In a deep fry pan heat 5 tablespoons of oil and pour the hot oil on the above mixture.
  4. Mix the above mixture into a dough with little water.
  5. Using a chakri achchu, fry the above mixture in hot oil.
  6. Good for evening snacks.
