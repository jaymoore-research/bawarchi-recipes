Shrimp Lolipop

Ingredients

Take cooked, deveined shrimp. Pat them dry.Keep aside.
1 cup all purpose flour
1/2 tsp chilli powder
1/2 tsp cumin powder
1/ 2 tsp garam masaa powder
1/2 tsp coriander powder
some turmeric
1/2 tsp baking powder
lemon juice(1 big lemon)
chopped mint(finely chopped)
salt to taste.
Mix all above ingredients with water , such that it forms perfect batter. Shouldn't be too runny.

Method

  1. Take each shrimp& roll it such that its tail is near its head .
  2. Now pierce it in a skewer.(use a wooden skewer)
  3. Now dip the shrimp end into the batter, so that it is evenly coated,then roll it in a platter of grated coconut .
  4. Dip the shrimp side in hot vegetable oil to fry it.
  5. Fry till brown.sprinkle some lemon juice if desired.
  6. This can b njoyed with tomato sauce as an appetiser.
