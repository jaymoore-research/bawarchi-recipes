Masala     Aloo

Ingredients

1/2 kg Potatoes
2 tbsp Vegetable Oil
1 tbsp Dhania powder
Salt to taste
1/2 tbsp Garam Masala
1/2 tbsp Cumin Seeds

Method

1. Cut potatoes verticaly in to long and thin pieces ( like french fries or finger chips). Wash them nicely and keep the soaked in water for 5 minutes.
2. Heat oil in a pan and add Dhania Powder , Garam Masala and salt to it.
3. Stir it properly to make a paste.
4. Now put all the potatoes into the paste and mix it well.
5. Take the potatoes out of the pan into almunim foil.
6. Keep them in a oven at 450 celcius for 20 mins. Turn the potato pieces when you are half way through.
Serving: You can serve them by inserting tooth-picks in them with tomato ketchup.
