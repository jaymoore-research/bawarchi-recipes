Chana Dal and Flat Beans Usli

Ingredients

Flat Beans (It is called Gori Kay in Kannada, Kuttavaranka in Tamil) - 100 gms
Channa Dal - 150 gms
Red Chillies - 5 to 6
Salt to taste
Oil - around 5 to 6 table spoons.
Mustard Seeds - 1/2 tea spoon
Curry Leaves - one small stalk

Method:

  1. Soak the channa dal and red chillies for about 2 hours.
  2. Cut the flat beans into small pieces.
  3. After 2 hours of soaking the channa dal and red chilies, grind it coarsely.
  4. Heat the oil in Kadai, add the mustard seeds and curry leaves. After the seeds stops spluttering add Flat Beans into the oil. Add little of salt for the flat beans to cook. After the flat beans is cooked properly add the ground channa dal paste into the oil. Cook well till the water from the channa dal dries away.
  5. Serve Usli with Rasam Rice or Mor Kolumbu.

Alternatively, if your a health freak and if you want to avoid too much oil for frying the ground channa dal paste, stream boil the channa dal paste before frying in the oil. This will consume very less oil. Thanks. Hope you enjoy making this dish.
