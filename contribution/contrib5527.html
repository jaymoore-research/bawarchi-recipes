Easy Wheat Ladoos

Ingredients for Cooking

 1     cup of wheat flour(chapati aatta)
 1     cup of fine sugar(if you want more sweet you can add more also)
 2     cup of ghee
 1/2   cup of dry fruits powdered(Cashewnuts,Badam(almonds))
 5-6   Cardamom (Elaichi) powdered

  Method:

  1. In a thick bottom vessel,add wheat flour and 1 cup of ghee  
  2. Fry till the mixture starts to give out an aroma and turns slightly to brown color.  
  3. Allow it to cool for about 5 min.
  4. While still warm, add sugar, dry fruits, Cardamom.
  5. Make ladoos by adding the rest of the ghee little at a time.

   Yummy Ladoos are ready. It is very easy to make. You can make upto 25 ladoos.
