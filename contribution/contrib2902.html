Fish Curry

Ingredients

Fresh Fish - 1 kg
Onions - 400 gm
Green Chilly - 5 pieces
Ginger paste- 2 big spoon
Garlic paste - 1 table spoon
Termeric powder- 2 table spoon
Tomatoes-100 gm
Fish Masala powder(good brand) - as desired to your palate.
Chilly powder
Coriander seed powder
Cumin powder
Garam masala
Elachi seed,Cinamon - 2 seeds
Salt - To Taste.
Coriander leaves

Method:

  1. Cut the fish into pieces & marinate them with salt, turmeric powder, chilly powder and oil. Allow them to sit for half an hour. After half an hour, fry the marinated fish pieces in oil till they get a brown colour.
  2. Chop onions & Tomatoes into very small pieces. Saute chopped onions with oil in a kadai till they become light brown in colour. Add tomatoes, ginger paste and garlic paste to it. After few minutes, add fish masala powder, coriander seed powder, cumin powder.chilly powder (little), garam masala & salt to it. Once the onion & tomatoes mixture gets a brown colour.
  3. Add the fried fish pieces to the onion and tomato mixture.
  4. Add water to it and let it to boil for 10 minutes.
  5. After 10 minutes add elaichi, cinamon and coriander leaves.

Ready to serve. Tasty fish curry. Goes well with fried rice. Happy cooking!
