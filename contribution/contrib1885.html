Quikie Fish Masala

  1. Cut the pieces of fish ( cat fish or anyother kind of fish) in to medium pieces.

  2. Marinate fish in salt, red chili powder, ginger garlic paste and vinegar (5 mins)

  3. Heat the oil in the pan.

  4. Add the pieces of fish and fry it till its 1/4 cooked,

  5. Add Butter milk (6-8 teaspoons), add garam masala / fish masala. and turmeric powder.

  6. Cook it till almonst all the butter milk evaporates.

  7. Then add Cilantro and red chiili powder...

Thats it..
