Fish Curry

Ingredients

Fish - mackrel or tuna 1 kg
Chilli powder two t/spoon
Turmeric powder - one t/spn
Onion - medium - 2nos (finely chopped)
Garlic - 25 gms
Ginger- 25 gms
Fresh grated coconut-100 gms
Lemon juice - two table spn
Salt -half t/spn
Water - 100 ml.

Method :

  1. Clean and cut the fish to a medium size and keep it aside.
  2. In the mixi add all the ingredients mentioned "except Fish" and grind them well and keep them in the bowl.
  3. In the non-stick pan / vessel add the amount of oil said above cut another two medium size onion finely chopped and fry them to golden colour and add the cleaned and cut fish and slowly heat them for two minutes and then add the ground fish curry masala in to the vessel and cook it for fifteen minutes covering it with the lid.
  4. Slowly stir the curry without breaking the fish under slow fire for five minutes.
  5. Add water and salt accordingly to suit the taste. Fish curry is ready.
