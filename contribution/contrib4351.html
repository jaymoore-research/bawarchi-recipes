Sandwich Dokla

Ingredients

3 cups Rice
1 cup Urad dal
1/2 cup Chana dal

Make this into coarse rawa in the mill (optional you get dokla atta in the market, even that can be used)

The above powder - 1 cup
Salt - to taste
Soda Bi Carb - 4 pinches
Curd - 1/2 cup
Paste of Green Chillies, Ginger - 2 tbsps
Rai - 1 tsp
Oil - 2-3 tbsp
Coriander leaves - for garnish
Coconut scrapings - 2 tbsp

For Chutney:

Coriander Leaves - 1 cup
Green Chillies - 4-5
Ginger - 1/2"
Groundnut - 1 tbsp
Salt, Lemon Juice, Sugar - to taste

Method:

  1. For dokla, mix flour, 2 pinches soda, salt and curd and keep aside till it ferments overnight.
  2. Then add the green chillies paste.
  3. Heat oil and put vaghar of rai and when it crackles, put 2 pinches of soda when it starst foaming, add it to batter and mix thoroughly.
  4. Grease a plate and make these doklas.
  5. Grind all ingredients for chutney.
  6. Before you serve, take one piece of dokla, spread chutney on it and add another piece of dokla and serve it with a tooth pick. To make it more colourful, add orange food colour to one part of dokla so that you can put one piece of white dokla first, put chutney and then top it with another orange piece of dokla piece.
