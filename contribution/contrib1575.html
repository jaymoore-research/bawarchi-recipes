Cucmber Aloo Kurma

Ingredients

Aloo 2 big
Cucumber (Kirbi) 3
Tomato 1 (big)
Green Peas few (1/4 cup)
Green Chillies 5
Mint 3 stalks
Coriander to garnish
Grated Coconut 4 tbsp
Garam Masala 1/2 tsp
Red Chilli Powder 1/2 tsp
Garam masala and chilli powder optional if you dont want to be spicy leave it else you can add.

Method:

  1. Grind mint, chillies, peas 2tbsp of coconut powder by adding little water make paste of it.
  2. Grate 2 cucumbers and cut one into big chunks.
  3. Peel potato cut into big chunks.
  4. Put tomato whole, cucumber grated and chunks and potato chunks little water and coconutpowder 2 tbsps in a pressure cooker cook for 2-3 whistles. When it is cool remove tomato skin and break with knife or spoon
  5. Put a vessel or kadai on stove add oil 2-3 spoons heat it. Add jeera, mustard seeds. Let them splutter. Add grinded mint followed by cooked vegetables stir well.
  6. Cook some time on medium heat till whatever kurma consistency you want.
  7. Add salt, chilli powder, garam masala. Cook for a minute by closing lid. Garnish with coriander leaves. Kurma is ready to serve.
