Tomato Rice

Ingredients

Cooked Rice (basmati or longrain) - 1 cup
Mustard seeds - 1/2 tsp
Urad dal - 1/2 tsp
curry leaves - 5 or 6
chopped onions - 4 tblsp.
Chilli powder - 1/2 - 1 tsp (depending on how hot you would like it to be)
Turmeric powder - a pinch
Oil - 2 tblsp.
Crushed Tomato - 3 - 4 tblsp
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Add the oil to a non-stick pan.
  2. When hot (not smoking) add mustard, urad, onions and curry leaves.
  3. When the onions are cooked, add the tomatoes, chilli poweder, turmeric powder and salt.
  4. Cook until the raw smell from the tomatoes disappears.
  5. Now add the cooked rice and stir.
  6. Serve hot with some yogurt or chips. Simple meal in less than 10 minutes.
