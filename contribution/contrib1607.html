Green Egg Korma

Ingredients

4 eggs hard boiled and shelled . Make lengthwise slits on each.
3 medium sized onions chopped fine
1/2 can thick coconut milk
3-4 cloves
1/2 stick cinnamon

Grind :
5 - 6 green chillies
1 inch ginger
4 -5 fresh garlic cloves or 2 tsp minced garlic
1 bunch cilantro leaves
1-2 medium sized shallots
1 cardamom
2 tsp aniseed

Grind into a fine paste. Set aside

Method:

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Add the cloves and cinnamon and let them pop.
  3. Add onions and fry them on medium heat till lite pink in color.
  4. Add the Ground paste and cook till the oil seperates.
  5. Add salt to taste and add the eggs into this gravy.
  6. Stir for 2 minutes very lightly and then add coconut milk on top.
  7. Let simmer for a few minutes on low - medium flame.

Serve hot with rotis / steamed rice / appams / dosas.
