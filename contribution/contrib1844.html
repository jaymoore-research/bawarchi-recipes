Tomato Pickle

Ingredients

Peeled and diced tomatoes 2 tins(any brand)
Oil 1/2 cup
Mustard
Fenugruek seeds 1/2 tsp
Hing pwd 1/4 tsp
Plain red chilli pwd 4 tbsp (or according to taste)
Salt (according to taste)

Method:

Heat oil in a heavy based or nonstick container and add mustard and fenugruek seeds and the add the hing pwd and add the tomatoes immeadietly and add the chillie pwd and salt and cook on medium heat stirring now and then until the oil floats on top.

It can be eaten with idli, dosa, chappathi, rice etc. It stays good for 1 or 2 days outside but stays longer in the fridge.
