Quick Bread Snack

Serves 4

Ingredients

10-12 bread slices
1 t.sp. Finely chopped ginger
2 t.sp. Mustard seeds
       2 tb.sp. Tomato ketchup
2-onion chopped
juice of 1 lemon
2 curry leaves
2-tb.sp. Chopped coriander
2-tomatoes chopped
        2 tb.sp.oil
4-5 green chilies chopped
salt

Method

  1. Soak the bread in a little water for few min.-squeeze out the water & crumble thoroughly
  2. Heat the oil and fry the mustard seeds
  3. Add onion and curry leaves until the onions are light pink in color.
  4. Add the tomatoes, green chilies and ginger and cook for one min.
  5. Add the crumbled bread, tom. Ketchup, lemon juice, coriander leaves. & salt & cook for 1 min.
