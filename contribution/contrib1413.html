Bell Pepper Fry

Ingredients

Bell peppers - 3 (cut into small pieces)
Onion - 1 ( cut into small pieces )
Urad daal + mustard seeds - 1 tsp
Garlic flakes - 3
Chilly powder - 2tsp
Turmeric - 1 tsp
Dry coconut powder-3tsp
Roasted chana daal powder (putnala pappu powder) - 4 tbsp
Oil - 5 tsp
Salt - acc to taste

Method :

  1. Take a pan and add oil. When the oil is heated add in urad daal and mustard seeds. When the seeds start spluttering then add in onions and garlic flakes and fry until the onions are slightly golden brown.
  2. Now add in the bell pepper pieces, salt, chilly powder, turmeric and mix.
  3. Cover the pan with a lid and after approximately 7 - 8 min when the bell peppers are cooked add in the coconut powder and puffed chana daal powder and fry on medium heat until golden brown.
  4. Garnish with cilantro and serve with hot plain rice, sambhar and ghee.
