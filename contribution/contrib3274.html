Tomato Toast

Ingredients

1. Bread (6 slices or the desired quantity)
2. Tomatoes :2
3. Onion chopped :1
4. Garlic-ginger pate : 1/2 tsp
5. Clove, cinnamon, elaichi:1 each
6. Pepper powder: 1 tsp
7. Salt to taste
8. Coriander (optional) 1/4 cup
9. Chilli powder (optional) : 1/2 tsp
10. Cheese spread ( if desired)
11. Oil to fry.

Method:

  1. Grind all the ingredients except the bread in the mixer to a fine paste
  2. Cut the bread slices into 4. Dip into the paste and fry on a heated chapathi/dosa tawa adding oil.
  3. Turn over and fry till done. Take care and fry over medium heat. Remove from fire. Spred some chesse spread or butter over it and serve with hot tea or coffee or cold juice and you'll be the star of the day!!!

Best of luck and happy cooking.
