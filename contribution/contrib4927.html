Doasa

Ingredients

1 cup Urad dhal
2 cups Rice
1 tsp menthi seeds
Oil (as needed)

Method:

  1. Soak (over night) and grind dhal with methi seeds and rice seperately to a smooth batter that can be poured easily.
  2. Heat tava add a laddle of batter to the tava and spread till it is paper thin. (the first one may not be thin).
  3. Pour a spoonfull of oil around the circumfrence of the dosa and turn around to cook on the other side.

Serve hot with Coconut chutney /Sambar / Aaloo masala curry.

Coconut chutney:
1 cup coconut
4 green chillies
1 tsp oil
1/2 tsp mustard seeds
salt ( to taste)
curry leaves

Grind coconut and green chillies and salt. Heat oil. Add mustard seeds to splutter and curry leaves and add to the chutney.
