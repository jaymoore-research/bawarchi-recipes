It is called Sunshine Rice

Sunshine Rice

Ingredients

Long grain rice : 1 cup
Tomato Juice :1 cup
Water :1 cup
Peas : 1/2 cup
Potato cut in cubes: 1/2 cup
Carrots diced: 1/2 cup
Onion : 1 large and cut into round rings.
Mushrooms ( optional) 1/4 cup and cut into slices
Broccoli: 1/2 cup and cut into florets
Cauliflower:1/2 cup and cut into florets
Corn kernel:1/2 cup
Whole masalas : cardamom ( black as well as green ), cinnamon,Black peppers cloves. This all should be around 1 teaspoon
Dry fruits : 1/4 of a cup.
2 table spoon ghee or butter
Salt according to taste
Red chilli powder according to taste
Coriander or cilantro leaves.About 1/4 cup.

Method

  1. Soak the rice for 30-45 minutes.
  2. In a heavy bottomed pan heat ghee and add whole masalas.
  3. Add onion and fry them till they turn golden brown.
  4. Add dry fruits and fry for a minute.
  5. Add all the vegetables except Broccoli.fry them for a minute or two.
  6. Add red chilli powder.
  7. Add tomato juice , rice , water and salt.
  8. Let it come to a boil and then lower down the gas and let the rice cook .
  9. Add broccoli just when rice are about to be done and after rice are almost done cover the pan with a lid.
  10. Open after a few minutes garnish it with coriander leaves and extra dry fruits if desired.

Enjoy your Rice.

Makes around 4 servings
