Kottu Kozhambhu

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients

White Kabuli Chana - 1 cup
Tamarind - small lemon size
Sambhar podi - 3 spoons
Grated coconut - 2 tbsp
Toor dhal - 1/2 cup
Kozhambhu vadam(to b fried in oil and added at the time of seasoning)
Brinjal -2-3 nos
Asafodeta
Salt to taste
Jaggery - small piece
oil

FOR SEASONING
Mustard
Chana dhal
Urad dhal
Curry leaves
Coconut

METHOD

  1. Soak Kabuli chana overnight.
  2. Cut brinjal into medium sized pieces.
  3. Pressure cook toor dhal and keep aside.
  4. Soak tamarind in some water and extract pulp.
  5. Add sambar podi,salt,brinjal and kabuli chana to the extracted tamarind pulp.
  6. Add asafodeta.Let it come to a boil.
  7. Check if brinjals and chana are cooked.
  8. After brinjals r cooked add the mashed toor dhal.
  9. Add a small piece of jaggery.let it boil for a 3-4 mins.
  10. Then remove from flame and season it.
  11. To season add 3-4 spoons of oil in a pan.
  12. To this add mustard, chana dhal(kadalai paruppu),Urad dhal(ulutham paruppu),curry leaves,Vadam(kozhambhu vadam),and grated coconut.
  13. Saute for a few mins and add to the kozhambhu.
  14. Kottu kozhambhu is ready to b served with hot rice .
