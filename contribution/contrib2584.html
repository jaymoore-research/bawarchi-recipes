Spicy Cauliflower Masala

Ingredients

1 Caulliflower (medium) washed cut into florets
Tomatoes - 4 , chopped
Green Chilli - 3 (jalapeno) chopped, optional
Red Chiili Powder - 1/2 - 1 tbsp
Turmeric Powder - 1/2 tbsp
Salt to taste
Oil - for seasoning
Onions - 2 finely chopped
Curry Leaves - 1 strand

Grind the following to a smooth paste: (adjust the following ingredients depending on how spicy you want)
Ginger - small piece
Garlic - 5-8 pods
Fresh Coconut - small piece (1/8 of coconut)
Clove - 5
Elaichi - 2
Dalchin - 1 piece
Kuskus - 2 tbsp
Dhania - 2 tbsp
Cashew - 10 nos (optional)

Method

  1. Heat oil in a pan (prefer wide bottom). Fry onions till soft and little brown. Now add the masala paste and fry in medium flame till the oil separates or the masala turns little brownish.
  2. Add tomatos, chilli powder, turmeric, salt. Fry for few more minutes.
  3. Now add cauliflower and still occationally. You can see all the masala getting into cauliflower.
  4. Add 1 cup of water and stir occationally. Cover with the lid and cook in medium flame.
  5. When cauliflower is cooked, add the curry leaves mix and transfer the contents to serving bowl.

Serve with Hot Parathas, Peas Rice, Fried Rice etc.
