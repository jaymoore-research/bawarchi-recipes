Kadhi

Ingredients

1/4 Cup Oil
1/2 tsp Haldi
1/6 tsp Salt
1/6 tsp Jeera
1/6 tsp Mustard seeds.
5-6 Green Chillies
6-7 Curry Leaves
Coriander leaves
1/7 tsp garlic
1/7 tsp ginger.
Buttermilk.

Method:

  1. Heat oil in pan.
  2. Put mustard seeds and jeera and wait till it starts spluttering.
  3. Immediately add curry leaves, green chillies cut into very small pieces.
  4. Add haldi and small amount of ginger and garlic and keep it on low flame.
  5. Add buttermilk or Curd + water.
  6. Add salt to taste and keep it on low flame.

Once the kadhi is hot then you are ready to eat. Important point is never keep prepare kadhi on high or medium flame.
