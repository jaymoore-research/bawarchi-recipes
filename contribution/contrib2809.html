This is a very easily made fish curry. Tastes really good with Mackerel, smelts or shrimp. For the veggie lover groups, this can also be used for okra or tindora vegetables. It is the native preparation from Konkan, Maharashtra and Goan coast. I have modified the real coconut to coconut milk for ease of preparing. Hope everyone enjoys it! E-mail : s_ghude@yahoo.com

Easy Fish Curry

Ingredients

1-2 lb fish
1 can Coconut Milk
3-4 dried Red Chillies
1 tsp Turmeric
1/4 tsp Dhania Powder
2 cloves Garlic
1/4 finely chopped Onion
1/4 tsp Tamarind concentrate/comparable amount of juice of fresh tamarind about size of lemon
chopped Coriander Leaves for garnish

Method:

  1. Wash the fish and rub salt. Keep aside. If making with shrimp, devein it and follow procedure. If making with okra, make vertical slits into the cut pieces before rubbing salt.
  2. In a blender, add the rest of the ingredients and grind until smooth. Add desired amount of water. (watch for the red chilli seeds, may be substituted with chilli powder 1 tbsp)
  3. In a saucepan/kadai, put 2 tbsp oil and when hot, add onions. Saute until brown.
  4. Add the blended mixture and bring to a boil.
  5. Add the tamarind juice or concentrate. Again bring to boil and then add the fish. Bring to boil and cool until fish is done.
  6. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with rice.
