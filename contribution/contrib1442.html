Eriseri

Ingredients

Pepper 1 t.spoon
Red chillies 4
Coconut grated 2 t.spoon
Mustard 1/4 t.spoon
Curry leaves
Urad dhall 1 t.spoon
Hing a small pinch
Salt 1 t.spoon (or to taste)
Coconut oil 1 t.spoon
Vegetables - Plantain, Chenai Kazanku washed, peeled and diced

Method :

  1. Boil the vegetables, add a little salt to this.
  2. Fry red chilles and pepper and wet grind this mixture with coconut and a few curry leaves.
  3. Mix the masala with the boiling vegetables and Cook until the flavour comes out.
  4. Heat some coconut oil in a pan, splutter mustard, then add urad dhall, 1 red chill, curry leaves, little hing, and add this to the above.
