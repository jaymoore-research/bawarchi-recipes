Vegetarian Mee Goreng

Ingredients

For serving 4 persons:

500 Grams Fresh Mee or Bee Hoon or Kwae Teow found in your local China Town. You may substitute the above with 4 packets of Maggi noodles.
Two strings of Spring Onions.
One Capsicum Green
Four Small strips of Ginger
Two small onions cut in small narrow strips.
Fresh Chinese Kai Lan Veg- Available with Chinese Veg stalls or substitute with Any fresh Green local Veg.
Peas 100 grams.

Method

  1. Wash & keep Fresh Mee or Boil Packet Noodles for 15 mins, keep aside.
  2. Put Chinese Wok on small fire; put 4 Table Spoons of Corn Oil to simmer, add cut onions and all the cut vegetables, stir fry only for 4 mins.
  3. Add salt, one table spoon Soya Sauce & two table spoons chilli sauce while stir frying.
  4. Add Fresh washed Mee or Boiled Maggi Noodles into the Wok. Stir fry for 8 mins on slow fire and remove.

Transfer ready to serve `Mee Goreng' to a serving dish Remove, serve hot with green chilies seasoned in Vinegar.
