Tomato Omelette

Ingredients

2 cups chana dal (besan)
Salt to taste
Tomato paste / 2 medium size tomatoes
1 medium size onion (if u like onions)
2 green chillies
1/2 teaspoon chilli powder
1/4 teaspoon of turmeric

Method:

To make the batter:

  1. Mix the besan with water to make it a bit fluid (little less fluid than dosa batter). Remove all lumps.
  2. Chop the tomatoes and onions (if any) into small pieces and mix with the batter.
  3. Chop the green chillies and add to batter
  4. Add turmeric, chilli powder and salt to taste.
  5. Add water if mixture is not fluid and mix well, slightly crushing the tomatoes.

To make omelettes:

  1. Heat pan on medium heat and pour 1/2 teaspoon of oil into it. Spread it evenly.
  2. Pour 1/2 cup (put more for thicker ones) of batter with the help of cup / ladle (like dosas) onto the pan.
  3. Cover with lid and cook on low flame for about 5 mins.
  4. Flip the omelette over, pour 1/2 teaspoon oil from sides and repeat 3

And it is done!!!!

Can make around 6-8 omelettes with above mix.
