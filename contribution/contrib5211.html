Mixed Veg Fry

Ingredients

Beans - 250 Grams
Carrots - 2 numbers
Fresh Green Peas - 100 grams
Fresh coconut (grated) - 1/4 cup
Green Chilli - 1 number
Onion (Finely chopped) - 1/2
Cooking Oil - 4 spoons
Spices - 1 spoon Chana Dal, 1 spoon Urad dal, mustered
seeds, 1/2 spoon Jeera, Curry leaves
Lime water - 1 spoon
Sugar - 1/2 spoon
Salt - to taste

Method:

  1. Cut beans into small pieces and grate carrot and keep them aside.
  2. Heat two spoons of oil in a pan.
  3. Add mustered seeds, jeera, curry leaves, chana dal, urad dal.
  4. Add finely chopped chilli.
  5. Add onions and fry for a minute.
  6. Add sugar.
  7. Add beans and fry for 4 minutes.
  8. Add grated carrot and green peas and fry.
  9. Add salt and lime water and mix well.
  10. Add Coconut in the end and mix well and serve.
