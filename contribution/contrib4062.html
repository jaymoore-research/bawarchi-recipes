Banana Pineapple Bread

Ingredients

All-purpose flour - 3 cups
Sugar - 2 cups
Eggs - 3
Oil - 1 Cup
Baking soda - 1 tsp
Baking powder - 1 tsp
Ground Cinnamon - 1 1/2 tsp
Vanilla essence - 3 tsps
Medium bananas - 3
Crushed/ Titbits pineapple - 1 can
Chopped Walnuts - 1 cup

For icing - Any cream cheese frosting

Method:

Mix all the ingredients together and pour into floured and greased pan(s). Bake for an hour at 350 degrees F. Allow the cake to fully cool down before applying the frosting.
