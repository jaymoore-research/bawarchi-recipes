Lemon Pickle

Ingredients

10 Lemons (Big Yellow ones you get in USA regular grocery stores)
1/5 teaspoon Asafoetida (Hing)
1/4 teaspoon Turmeric
1/2 teaspoon Mustard
2 Teaspoons Red Chilli powder
1/2 teaspoon Methi powder
Salt to taste
1/4 Cup any Cooking Oil (you may add more if like oilier)

Method:

  1. Cut half of the lemons (five) and extract juice from it.
  2. Cut other half of the lemons into 8-10 pieces each.
  3. Heat oil in sauce pan and add Asafoetida and then add mustard and wait for them to splutter.
  4. Add cut lemons and juices together.
  5. Add turmeric on the top. With low heat, have the mixtures boiled for 15 minutes with lid covered.
  6. Add remaining powders and salt and let it cool.
  7. Put into dried glass/plastic bottles to preserve.

Development & Run time: 25 Minutes

Output:

  • Soft lemon pieces with light red color lemon juice gravy.
  • Excellent to serve with plain upma, Curd rice and even with idly.
  • Good to taste.
  • Kids like it a lot too.
  • Not much spicy and good for health.
