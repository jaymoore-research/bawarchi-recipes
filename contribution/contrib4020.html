Mogul-E-Chicken

Ingredients

1 kg Chicken (chopped into peices)
2 1/2 Large Tomatoes
1 Large Onions
3 tbs Ginger paste
3 tbs Garlic paste
6 Green Chillies (minced)
4 tbs Red chilli powder (can vary according to how much you can handle)
3 tbs Coriander powder
3 tbs Turmeric powder
2tbs Cummin powder
2 tbs Garam Masala powder
1 small cup Curd(drain excess water)
Whole garam masala..........(cinnamon, cloves, cardamom, big cardamom, aniseed.etc)
3 Bay leaves

Method:

  1. Wash the chicken pieces thoroughly.
  2. Then mix the half the quantity of ginger paste, garlic paste, red chilli powder, cummin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder & the curd in a bowl. Add salt, minced green chillies & curd & make a consistent paste.
  3. Apply the above mixture to CHICKEN pieces & keep it aside for an hour or two.
  4. Cut the onion finely.
  5. Put some oil in a thick bottom saucepan & when the oil is hot add the whole garam masala. When it starts crackeling add onions...& saute for a while till they become golden brown in colour.
  6. Now add the remaining ginger, garlic paste & the remaining spices. Saute for a while.
  7. Now add the finely chopped tomatoes & keep stirring on high flame.
  8. Add a little bit of water & keep on cooking for atleast 15 minutes till a thick gravy is formed.
  9. Now add the chicken pieces & stir properly.
  10. Add very little water if required & cook on a high flame for 3 minutes.
  11. Then cover the lid & cook till the chicken is tender.
  12. Serve hot with Tandoori Roti or Naan.
