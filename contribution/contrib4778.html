Jeera Kuzhambu

Ingredients

Tamarind size of a lemon
Red chillies 2
Jeera 2 teaspoons
Pepper 10 seeds
Garlic flakes 7
Turmeric 1/4 teaspoon
Oil 2 teaspoon
Ghee 1/2 spoon
Mustard 1/2 spoon
Salt to taste

Method :-

  1. Soak tamarind in warm water ( 60ml ) for half an hour and strain the juice.
  2. In a kadai heat oil, add mustard. When it splutters add chopped garlic, fry for 2 minutes, add tamarind juice, turmeric powder and salt. Boil for 10-15 minutes until the kozhambu thickens
  3. Fry jeera and pepper in ghee and powder them. Add the powder after removing the boiled tamarind extract from fire. Garnish with curry leaves and coriander leaves.

Servings:3
