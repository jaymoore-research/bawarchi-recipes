Peas And Cauliflower Curry

Ingredients

Cauliflower 1 medium size
Green Peas 1/2 lbs
Potato 1 medium size
Egg plant (brinjal) 1 medium size
Coconut 1
Red Chilly 7-8
Coriander leaves - handful
Coriander powder 2 tsp
Ginger 1"
Garlic 4 pods
Onion 1 medium
Cinnamon stick
Cloves
Elaichi 2
Fennel seeds 1 tsp
Tomatoes 2 small
Salt to taste

Method

  1. Boil green peas and other vegetables with little salt in it.
  2. Take a big pan or vessel, pour oil in it and add mustard seeds and curry leaves. Once the seeds splutters, add onions and fry till brown.
  3. Grind coconut, coriander leaves, chillies, ginger, garlic, onions, cinnamon, elaichi, cloves, fennel seeds.
  4. Grind Tomato separately.
  5. After the onions in the pan turns golden brown add the grounded masala first and fry for 7-10 min. Then add Tomato paste, and fry for 5 mts. Add little water and mix it with coriander powder, turmeric powder and salt. Cook it well for another 20 to 30 min.
  6. Relish it with Rice or Chappati.
