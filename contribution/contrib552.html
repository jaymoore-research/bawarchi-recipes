Mixed Vegetable Pickle

Ingredients

1/4kg of carrots
1/4kg of tomatoes
1/4kg of beans
1/4kg of tindoli
1/4kg green chillies
1/4kg cauliflower
1/4kg brinjal
1 small size Radish
1/4kg capsicum

Masala Required :

150 gms Mustard powder
100 gms Red chilly powder
50 gms Pickle powder (ready mix)
100 gms tamarind.make the juice of the tamarind
100 gms vinegar
1/2 kg oil
salt as per taste

Method:

  1. Cut all the vegetables fine & keep it aside radish and capsicum are cut separately.
  2. Cut them into long pieces (only the radish and capsicum). Donot cook them.
  3. Soak capsicum & radish in 1/4th tsp haldi (turmeric powder) & 2 tsp rock salt.
  4. Cut the remaining vegetables and fry them in the oil.
  5. When the vegetables are half cooked add in the tamarind juice.
  6. Add in the masalas cook for about 5 mins.
  7. Then add in the capsicums and radish.

This pickle can be eaten after two days.
