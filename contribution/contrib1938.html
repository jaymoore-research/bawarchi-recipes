Lentils Vada

Ingredients

Broken Black gram (urad dhal) - 1/2 cup
Bengal gram (channa dhal) - 3/4 cup
Red gram dal (tuar dhal) - 1/4 cup
Curry leaves
Coriander leaves
Scraped Coconut - 1/2 cup
Asafoetida (Hing)- a pinch
Chilly powder
Oil(to deep fry)
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Soak all three dhals together for about 1/2 an hour.
  2. Drain all the water and grind in a mixer, adding minimum amount of water.
  3. Add salt and asafoetida to the mixture and mix well.
  4. The mixture should not be ground to a fine paste. It should be coarse. It should be made sure that the dhals are ground well, but not too finely.
  5. Chop curry leaves, coriander leaves and coconut and add to this mixture.
  6. Heat oil in a pan (on a low flame), take a small amount of the mixture, make it into small patties by placing on a plastic or aluminium foil, and add into the oil. Turn it over four or five times. When a brown layer forms on the surface, the vada is done and can be taken out.
  7. Serve hot with Coconut Chutney.
