Puliyogere

Ingredients

1/2 cup grated Coconut
8 Red Chillies
1 tsp Urad dal
1 tsp Bengal gram dal
2 tsp Coriander seeds
2 tsp Cumin seeds
1 string Curry leaves
1 tsp Asafoetida
1/4 cup Peanuts
2 tsp Tamarind powder
Jaggery pieces crushed
Rice

Method:

  1. Dry roast the coconut powder till it is brown and keep it aside.
  2. Heat a little oil, and fry the chillies, coriander, cumin, black gram dal, Bengal gram dal, curry leaves, asafoetida, curry leaves and the peanuts. After frying it for 2 minutes, grind this fried mixture to a smooth paste. Add in the roasted coconut and grind again. Don't make the mixture too watery, it should be thick in consistency.
  3. Heat oil, add in some urad dal, Bengal gram dal, peanuts and the tamarind and jaggery. Mix it well till it is of a jam like consistency. Now add in salt and the ground mixture. Mix well and fry for 2 minutes. Mix the cooked rice with this.
