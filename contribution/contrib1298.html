Poondu Kolambu

Ingredients

Poondu -- around 25 to 30 pieces
Ghee -- around 2 tsp for frying the Garlic
Black pepper -- 1 tsp (if needed very hot you can add as per your taste)
Red chilli -- a couple of them (again according to taste)
Tuvar dal -- tsp
Tamarind -- 1 small ball
Kadi Patha -- a couple of them
Salt -- as per requirement

Method:

  1. Soak the tamarind in water and make a thick paste of it.
  2. Fry Garlic in Ghee until the smell goes which adds more flavour to the Kolambu
  3. Fry all the other ingredients and along with the tamarind.
  4. In a Kadai take the mixture and boil well along with Poondu and salt according to taste.
  5. Once it thickens it is ready to be served with hot rice and gingelly oil and pappad.

Hint: This is generally taken after a good oil bath and gives good relief to the tummy and can be taken once in a week.
