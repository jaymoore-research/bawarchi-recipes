Potato Baked Dish

Ingredients

Potatoes 5
Onions 4
Garlic 1 Pod Chopped Finely
Cilantro 1/2 Bunch Cut Finely

Method :

  1. Take oil in a pan saute Onions and Garlic
  2. Cut the Potatoes into round slices and then cook the slices with salt and pepper (a pinch) in 2 glasses of Milk . After it is half cooked drain the water

For Baking:

  1. Bake it in a Pyrex Dish. Firstly arrange a layer of sliced Potatoes, secondly on top of that a layer of onion and garlic mixture, thirdly cilantro and finally cheese.
  2. Make a similar layer again on top of that and then bake it for about 45 minutes. Serve hot.
