Fruit Shake

Ingredients

1. Green grapes (seedless)
2. Kiwis
3. Strawberries
4. Red Pears
5. Red Apples
6. Milk
7. Sugar
8. Cashewnuts
9. Pinch of Pepper Powder
10. Vanilla Icecream

Method:

  1. Grind together all the fruits with milk and sugar in blender.
  2. Add cashew powder to this shake and sprinkle pepper powder on top.
  3. Serve in tall glasses with vanilla icecream as topping.

Enjoy this easy and healthy fruit shake!
