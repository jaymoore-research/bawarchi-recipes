Wheaties

Ingredients

Cream ( Malai from top of milk ) 4 cups
Wheat flour - 2 cups
Sugar one and half to two cups
Baking powder 1 tea spoon

Utensils :

  1. Heavy bottomed pan or a non stick pan
  2. Baking tray
  3. Tray for making dough

Method:

  1. Place the pan on fire and pour in the cream . Keep stirring occasionly. Soon the ghee will separate and float on top , the residue - paneer like settles at the bottom.
  2. Cool and drain of the ghee which can be used for cooking purposes.
  3. Take the residue, wheat flour, sugar and baking powder and make a smooth dough using water. ( as for making chapattis )
  4. Make small balls (about 15) . Arrange them on a greased baking tray. Bake at 150 C .
  5. Takes about 20 min to bake (Till top is light brown and a needle put in comes out clean )

So go ahead and try making Wheaties.
Let me know how you liked them.
Makes approx. 15 cookies
