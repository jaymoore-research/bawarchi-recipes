Ingredients

Rice - 3 cups
Coconut milk - 1 can
(Can also use the gratings of 1/2 fresh coconut)
Baking soda - 1 tsp
Cream of rice - 1/2 cup
Water - 2 cups
Oil to shallow fry
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Wash and soak the rice for 5-6 hours.
  2. Grind the rice to a paste using the coconut milk.
  3. Let this batter be of dosa consistency. Keep aside.
  4. Heat 1.5 cups of water in a pan, bring to a boil.
  5. In a cup mix the cream of rice with remaining 1/2 cup of water and add this to the boiling water.
  6. Keep stirring for 4-5 minutes and make sure there are no lumps.
  7. Remove from fire and cool.
  8. Add this paste, to the batter. Add salt and mix well.
  9. Let it stay for 8-10 hours (or overnight).
  10. When ready to make, add baking soda and mix well. Pour a spoonful of batter on a hot tawa, pour 1 tsp of oil and close it with a lid.
  11. Remove when done.
  12. You need not cook on the other side. Serve hot with coconut chutney or kurma.
