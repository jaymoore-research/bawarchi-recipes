Palak Rice

Ingredients

1 bunch palak
1 1/2 cups of basmati rice
Jeera
2-3 green chilies slit into two
2-3 cloves
3-4 pepper corns
1/2" dalchini
2 tej patta etc.
2-3 tbs oil
Salt to taste.

Method

  1. Wash the rice & keep aside.
  2. Wash the palak properly & churn it in mixer grinder to make a thick paste.
  3. Keep it aside.
  4. Cook the rice separately (not in the pressure cooker).
  5. Do not over cook. Let it cool.
  6. In a big pan heat the oil.
  7. Add jeera, green chilies & all the other spices.
  8. Once the spices are roasted add the palak paste.
  9. Keep stirring for1-2 min.
  10. Add salt & put the cooked rice into it.
  11. Mix well.
  12. Serve hot with curd & papad.
