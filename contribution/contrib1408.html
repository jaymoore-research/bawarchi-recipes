Vennaipoottu

Ingredients

2 cup rice
Jaggery 1/2 kg
Gram dal
Cardamoms
Coconut- scraped
Salt to tast.

Method:

  1. Soak rice in water for 2 hours and grin it.
  2. Cook rice with water add jaggery, gram dal, cardamom, and salt.
  3. Cook when it become a paste.
  4. Take plate and apply ghee and pour the paste on that plate, and Add scraped coconut on the top.
  5. Keep it become a cool. and sever it.
