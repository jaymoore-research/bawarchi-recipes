Sabudana Khichadi

Ingredients

1 cup Sabudana (take good quality one)
3-4 tbsp Roasted Groundnut Powder
2-3 Green Chillies slitted vertically
1 tsp Jeera
1-2 boiled Potatoes cut into small pieces
1 Lemon
Coriander chopped finely for garnishing
3-4 tbsp Oil or Ghee

Method:

  1. Wash sabudana 2-3 times with water.
  2. Soak 1 cup sabudana for 2-3 hrs in 1 cup water. The sabudana will soak the water.
  3. Add salt as per taste and add groundnut powder. Mix well.
  4. Heat oil or ghee in karahi. When hot add jeera then add green chillies and a little salt now add potatoes and fry for 1 minute. After this add sabudana little sugar (optional) and mix well. Sprinkle very little water and close the lid and allow it to cook for a few minutes.
  5. Garnish with coriander leaves and add a few drops of lemon juice just before serving. Sabudana Khichadi is ready for serving.
