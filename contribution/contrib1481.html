Instant Sandwhich Dhokla

Ingredients

1 1/2 cup gram flour (Besan)
1/2 cup Warm Water
1/2 cup plain Water
1 Tsp. Citric Acid
1 Tbsp. Sugar
1 Tsp. Oil
Pinch of Eno/Khana soda
1/2 cup Vinegar + 1/2 cup water mixture

For Tampering:

3/4 tsp.Turmeric powder
3/4 tsp.Chilli powder
Salt to taste
1 tsp. Mustard seeds

For Garnish:

Chopped Coriander
1 Tbsp. Grated Coconut

Method :

  1. Take besan and mix with 1/2 cup of plain water. Add a pinch of salt, chilli powder and turmeric powder.
  2. Warm 1/2 cup of water and put citric acid and sugar in it. Dissolve them completely in water.
  3. Pour the warm water in the mixture of besan.
  4. Beat the mixture nicely till there are no lumps. Add a pinch of khana soda in the mixture and beat well. The mixture will expand.
  5. Pour the mixture in the container. Put one glass of water in pressure cooker and bring it to boil.
  6. Put the container with the batter in the cooker and steam it. Cover the cooker Without the Weight on it.
  7. Slow the gas on medium and let it cook for 15 minutes.
  8. After 15 minutes open the cooker and insert a toothpick in the cooked batter. If the stick comes out clean than it is cooked.
  9. Take out the container and let it cool for 10-15 minutes.
  10. Invert the container upside down and the cake will come out.
  11. Cut the cake horizontly from the middle cutting into 2 equal halves.
  12. Mix the vinegar and water in equal portion and pour on the two cakes.
  13. Let the cake absorb all the vinegar.
  14. Apply green sauce (chutney) on one side and tomato ketchup on the other side.
  15. Put the cakes back on each other.
  16. Heat some oil in a pan and put some mustard seeds it it.
  17. Pour the mustard seed with oil on top of the cake.
  18. Garnish on top with grated coconut and chopped coriander leaves.
