Chicken Vindaloo

Ingredients

1. Chicken - 1 Kg (cut into med sz pieces)

Marinade:
1. Coriander seeds - 1 Tspn
2. Cumin seeds - 1 Tspn
3. Mustard seeds - 2 Tspn
4. Turmeric powder - 1 tspn
5. Dry red chillies - 10
6. Coconut grated fresh or (dessicated) - 1/3 cup
7. Fresh Ginger - Garlic paste - 1 tbspn
8. Oil - 5 Tbspn
9. Cinnamon - 1 inch
10. Cloves - 6 whole
11. Onions - 1 Cup (finely chopped)
12. Red paprika - 1 tbspn
13. Jaggery - 1/2 tspn
14. Water - 1 cup, juice of 1 lemon
15. Salt - as per taste

Method:

  1. Clean, wash & cut the chicken into medium sz pieces prick the chicken with fork on all sides.
  2. Combine coriander, cummin, mustard, coconut, red chillies, grind it to afine powder. Add Turmeric, lime juice and half of the giger-garlic paste & mix, then marinade the chicken in this and coat each piece well, cover and marinade for 1 hour.
  3. Heat oil in a pan or kadai over medium-high.
  4. When it is heated add cloves, cinnamom & onions, fry until light brown then add ginger-garlic paste stir in for a second and then add the marinated CHICKEN to it reseving liguid cook until the pieces are well seared.
  5. Add paprika, jaggery, salt & reserved liguid and bring the contents to boil.

Cook until CHICKEN PIECES becomes tender.
ENJOY! this spicy vindaloo with Plainor Ghee rice, Pulav or Steamed Iddiappam (Indian rice noodles) or any kind of homemade naans or Parathas.
