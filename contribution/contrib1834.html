Chocolate Peppermint Fizz

Ingredients

75g plain chocolate
2tbsp sugar
750ml Milk
1tsp Mint Essence
8 scoops of vanilla or chocolate ice-cream

Method:

  • Grate the chocolate in a pan and add the sugar and 150ml of milk.Heat gently till the sugar and the chocolate melt.
  • Remove from heat and add the rest of the milkand the mint essence. Stir well and pour into a jug and chill in the fridge for about and hour or so.
  • The just before surving pour into glasses and add 2 scoops of ice-cream into each glass.

    And then ENJOY the drink!

    (Serves 4 thirsty people)
