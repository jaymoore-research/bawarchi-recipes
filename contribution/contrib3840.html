Simple Pongal

Ingredients

Green Gram dal -- 1/2 cup
Rice -- 1 cup
Ghee or Clarified Butter -- 2 tbspns
Jeera--2 tspn
Ginger finely chopped -- 1 tspn
Freshly grated Coconut -- a handful
Green Chillies -- as per taste
Salt -- as per taste

Method:

  1. Soak rice in water for five minutes.
  2. Dry fry the dal on a heated thava till it get a nice aroma and red colour. Keep it aside.
  3. Heat ghee in a pressure pan, add mustard seeds, when it splutter add jeera, then chillies and ginger. Immediately add two cups of water. Simmer, add rice, coconut gratings, dry fried dal and salt. Simmer it. Close the lid and pressure cook. When the pressure is fully down open the lid. There is your instant pongal, mix smoothly with a spoon.
  4. Enjoy with any hot hot pickle and yoghurt while it is piping hot.
