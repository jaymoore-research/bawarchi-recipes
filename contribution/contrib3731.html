Tomato Curry

Ingredients

500 gm Red Tomatoes
1 Onion (finely chopped )
2 tsp Ginger-Garlic paste
1 tsp Jeera & Rai
1/2 tsp Haldi powder
3 to 4 Green Chillies
1 tsp Red Chilli powder
1/2 tsp Uraddal & little Methi seds
2 tsp Oil and 1/2 tsp Sambar powder
Salt and Jaggery to taste.
Boondi one cup and Green Coriender (for garnishing )

Method:

  1. First clean and slice the tomatoes and cook for 15 min.
  2. Take oil in a pan add rai, jeera, meti seeds and uraddal, then add kadipatta, onion, green chilli paste, ginger garlic paste, haldi poeder and cook for 2 to 3 min with covering lid.
  3. Now add the cooked tomatoes to the above preparation with sambar powder and salt and 1 spoon jaggery and 3 to 4 cups of water and let it boil for 5 min. Now the curry is ready while serving add boondis and hara dhania and serve hot with rice.
