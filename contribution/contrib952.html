Khatta Aloo

Ingredients

1. Potatoes 3 large
2. Tomatoes 3-4 large
3. Green Chillies 2-3 slit length wise
4. Curry leaves 10-15
5. Mustard seeds half teaspoon
6. Jeera 1 teaspoon
7. Dhania Jeera powder -2 teaspoon
8. Haldi half tea spoon
9 Salt To taste
10. Red chiily powder one and half teaspoon
11. Lime 1 large
12. Fresh corrainder leaves one fourth cup (finely chopped)
13. Cooked & mashed Green Peas - half cup (optional)

Method:

  1. Cook potatoes, peel and crumble very finely or mash
  2. Chop tomatoes, slit green chillies lengthwise
  3. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard and jeera
  4. Add green chillies, curry leaves and the chopped tomatoes. Fry well.
  5. Add Haldi powder, dhaniya, jeera powder and chilly powder.
  6. Add half cup water and let it cook for 5-8 minutes.
  7. Now add the aloo (mashed, cookedpeas can also be added)
  8. Add salt and juice of one lime.
  9. Remove from flame and add lots of cut corriander (without the stem)
