Chatpat..Instant Food

Ingredients

Onion : 1 small ( Made into small Pieces)
Tomatoes : 1 (Made into Small pieces)
Salt to Taste
Red chillies - 2 To 3 (according to your taste)
Tumeric Powder : 1/4 Tsp
Peanut Oil : 3 Tsp
Green Peas (1 cup)
Fresh Corns (1 cup)
chopped cashews (BC cup)(Optional)
1/4 tsp Garam Masala
1 tsp Jeera
1 tsp Mustard
Curry leaves - 2 - 3 leaves
Lemon Juice for taste

Serves two people

Method

  1. Heat the peanut oil in a pan
  2. Fry jeera, Mustard and curry leaves along with red chillies in the oil for 1 min (chopped cashews - optional).
  3. Now add Onions and fry for 1 more min till they become brown next add, fresh corns, tomato pieces, and Green Peas, fry it for 3 mins
  4. Add turmeric, garam masala small pinch, and salt to taste .....and sprinkle lemon juice for taste ..That's it your Instant Food
  5. CHAT PAT is ready to server your husbands who comes early in the evenings from office
