Mango Rice

Ingredients

1.Cooked rice - 2cups
2.Grated Green Mango - 1cup
3.Chopped onions - 1/2 cup (optional)
4.Salt - 2 tsp
5.Chilli powder - 2 tsp
6.Turmeric powder - 1 tsp
7.Asafoetida -1 pinch
8.Mustard -1 tsp
9.Oil - 2 to 3 tbsp
10.Curry leaves - small bunch
11.Fried peanuts - a handful

Ingredients For Raitha:
Yogurt - 1/2 cup
Baby okras - 6 to 8

Method:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard, turmeric powder and asafoetida.
  2. After the mustard splutters, add chopped onions and fry till they turn golden brown.
  3. Add the grated mango, chilli powder and salt.
  4. Cook the mango till it becomes very soft.
  5. Now, add the cooked rice . Stir well and garnish with curry leaves.
  6. Sprinkle fried peanuts on top before serving.
  7. Serve this with the "Baby Okra-Raitha".
  8. Fry the baby okras and mix with salted yogurt.

Voila! Mouth watering Mango Rice is ready to eat!

Serves - 4 to 6 . Cooking time - 15 to 20 mins
