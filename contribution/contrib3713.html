Methi Rava Dosa

Ingredients

Rava (fine one and not upma rava) - 2 cups
Rice flour - 1/2 cup
Maida - 1 table spoon
Sour Curd - 1 cup
Methi leaves, finaly chopped - 1 cup
Onion, finely chopped - 1/2 cup
Green Chillies, finely chopped - 4
Coconut, grated - 2 table spoon
Jeera - 1 teaspoon
Asafoetida - 1 pinch
Salt - as per taste

Method:

  1. In a bowl mix rava, rice flour, maida and curd.
  2. Add the jeera, asafoetida, chopped onions, methi leaves, green chillies and grated coconut.
  3. Add some water so that the batter is thick and of dosa consistency.
  4. Add salt according to the taste. Keep it for 30-40 minutes.
  5. On a medium flame heat a non stick tawa with few drops of oil. Pour 2 table spoon of batter and spread it a little. When the dosa becomes crisp and red, turn it and cook the other side for few seconds.
  6. Serve hot with Coconut Chutney and Butter/Ghee. This dosa tastes very good.
