Mexican Basket

Ingredients

FILLING
200 gms of corn
2 large chopped onion
1 chopped capsicum
3 tabs tomato sauce
1/2 cup grated cheese
Salt to taste
2 tabs oil

Method

  1. Remove the crust from the slice of bread. Roll the slice until flat & press into the cavities of a greased muffin tray.
  2. Heat the oil in a pan, fry onion till golden brown. Add capsicum,tomatoes and fry for 2 min. Add cornand salt
  3. Take 2 tabs of the mixture andspread each slice of bread. spread a little tomato sauce and sprinkle with grated cheese. Bake in a hot oven for 7-8 mins till cheese melts. serve hot.

10 slices of bread
