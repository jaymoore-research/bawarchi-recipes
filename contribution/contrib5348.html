Upmav Koyakatta

Ingredients

Grated Coconut : 1/2 glass
Boiled rice : 1 Glass
Tur Dal : 1/2 glass
Urad Dal : 1/2 glass
Mustard : 1 teaspoon
Red chilly Powder : 3 teaspoons
Sesame Oil : large qty.
Salt : to taste

Method:

  1. First soak rice, tur dal & urad dal for 3 hours & coarsely grind with green chilly, asafoetida & salt.
  2. Heat oil, fry mustard & add the ground paste & simmer in low fire for 2-3 minutes & stop fire.
  3. Add grated coconut & curry leaves.
  4. Mix well with hand & shape them into balls (each ball should be the size of a lime) without holding the mixture tight.
  5. Steam cook these balls for 10-15 minutes.
