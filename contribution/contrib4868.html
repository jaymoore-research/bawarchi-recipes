Celery Sabji

Ingredients

Celery - 1 Pound
Mung daal - 1/2 katori
Chili powder/ red chilies
Salt,
Jaggery,
Cumin seeds,
Mustard seeds,
Dhania-Jira powder,
2 spoon oil,
A pinch of turmeric powder

Method:

  1. Wash and soak mung daal in 3/4 katori water, half hour before beginning to cook.
  2. Wash and cut celery sticks into small pieces about 1/2-3/4 inch long.
  3. Add oil to the cooking pan, let it heat, then put mustard seeds, cumin seeds.
  4. When they sputter add hing, turmeric and red chilies.
  5. Curry leaves can also be added at this stage. Add soaked mung daal to the mixture in the pan.
  6. Stir for 2 min. Add celery pieces to the pan.
  7. Cover and cook for 15 min or till cooked.
  8. Stir in between to prevent charring.
  9. When done add salt, jaggery, dhania-jeera powder.
  10. Coconut powder/freshly ground, cilantro can be added for additional flavor.
