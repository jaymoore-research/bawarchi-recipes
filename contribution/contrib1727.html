Veg. Spicy Fried Rice

Ingredients

1 cup Long Grained Rice
1/2 a cup Shelled Green Peas
6 Shallots
1 small Leek
1 Carrot
2 Capsicums
1 big Onion
1 stick Celery
1 Bamboo Shoot 1 tablespoon of Bean Sprouts
1 tablespoon of Chilli Sauce
3 tablespoons of Groundnut Oil
1 tablespoon Soya Sauce
1 tablespoon of Tomato Sauce
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Chop shallots along with leaves, celery, leeks, carrots, bamboo shoots and capsicums into thin long strips.
  2. Slice the onions.
  3. Heat little oil and saute the onions and shallots. Add leeks, celery and saute.
  4. Add vegetables and shallow fry a little. Add salt, soya & chilli sauce. Fry well. Keep aside for 10 mins.
  5. In the meantime heat oil in another pan and fry washed rice for 5 mins till all the grains are covered with oil. Transfer rice to another container and add 1 1/2 cups of water. Place in pressure cooker (for 6.5, 7.5, 10 and 12 ltrs pressure cookers use 2 cups of water) cook the rice for 8 mins.
  6. Add fried vegetables and tomato sauce to the cooked rice and mix well.
  7. Garnish with a sprinkle of pepper (black) and a little vinegar.

Best eaten with Soups. Serve hot.
