Butter Chicken

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients

1/2 lbs chicken cut into small pieces
Onion 1 big
Tomato 1-1/2 cut into small pieces
Ginger & garlic paste- 1-1/2 tbsps
Chilli Powder-1 tbsp
Turmeric- 1/4 tbsp
Garam Masala - 1 tbsp
Tandoori- 1/2 tbsp
Cumin ground- 1/2 tbsp
Lemon juice- 2 tbsp
Cashew paste - 50 gms- pour water and make a thick paste
Butter- small piece

Method

  1. Marinate the chicken with tandoori powder, garam masala cumin and lemon juice. Marinate for 1/2 hr.
  2. Pour little oil in Kadhai, fry the chicken till the flesh changes color.
  3. Pour oil in kadhai, fry onions brown. Then add ginger , garlic paste. Fry well.
  4. Add Chilli powder, turmeric. Fry well, pour water and fry till onion fry's well with the masala
  5. Add tomato and fry well till oil leaves
  6. Add the cashew paste and fry well.
  7. Add small piece of butter, fry the mix well.
  8. Now add the chicken pieces with the marinade after frying.
  9. Pour little water or as per consistency and cook well.
  10. Garnish with cilantro.
