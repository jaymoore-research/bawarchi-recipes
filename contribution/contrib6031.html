Grated Lauki Curry

Ingredients

Gourd/Lauki - 1 medium
Black chana - 1/2 cup
Potato -1 (optional)
Onion - 1 medium chopped finely
Ginger paste - 1 tbsp
Grated coconut -1/3 cup
Milk - 1/2 cup
Salt to taste
Panch pooran (mixture of cumin seeds, mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, kalinji, and aniseed) - 1/2 tbsp.Ā If you don't have all of the above seeds you can substitute it with cumin seeds only.
Oil - 1tbsp

Method

  1. Soak Black chana for 8-10 hours and pressure cook it till it is done.
  2. Peel and grate lauki.
  3. Put oil in a karahi.Ā When it heats up a little, add the seeds as in no. 8.
  4. When the seed splutter, add the onion and fry till golden brown.
  5. Then add the ginger paste and fry for one minute.
  6. If you want to add potato, you can add it now, peeledĀand cut into small peices.
  7. Then add the black chana,Āgrated lauki and salt.
  8. Keep the flame in medium and cook it till the lauki is done and the water from the lauki dries up.
  9. Then add the grated coconut and milk.
  10. Let it boil for a minute and remove it from heat.
  11. Garnish it with coriander leaves.Ā It goes well with roti, paratha or rice.Ā
  12. Serve with hot rice and appalam. Ā

Preparation time: 25 Mins
