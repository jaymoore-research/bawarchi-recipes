Suhiyan

Ingredients

Chana dal 2 cups
Brown Sugar 2 cups
Cardamom 6
Scraped Coconut 1/2 cup
Raw Rice 2 cups
Urad dal 1/2 cup
Oil for frying

Method :

  1. Roast the scraped coconut & cardammom & keep aside
  2. Cook chana dal till fairly tender & drain away excess water.
  3. Add brown sugar to the dal & cook till the sugar sticks to dal.
  4. Add coconut & cardamom.
  5. Grind to a paste & make small balls of even size (size of a small lemon)
  6. Soak rice & urad dal in water for half an hour & grind smoothly to an even consistency batter that can be poured.
  7. Dip each ball in this batter & deep fry in oil till brown.
