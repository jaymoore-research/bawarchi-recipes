Ingredients

(serves 2)

4 medium sized potatoes.
1/2 lb paneer
1 green pepper- finely chopped
2 green chillies (opt)- finely chopped
a pinch of black pepper powder
1/2 tsp garam masala
salt to taste
oil for frying

Method

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into half lengthwise.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Deep fry the potatoes till they are golden brown.
  3. Scoop out from each of the fried potato halves so that they look like cups.
  4. In a Kadai heat oil. Fry the chopped green pepper pieces and green chillies lightly.
  5. Then add grated paneer, salt, black pepper powder, salt and the scooped potato ( not the cups).
  6. Mix nicely and fry for some time.
  7. Fill in the paneer mixture into the cups .
  8. Arrange them in a greased baking dish and bake them at 300o F for about 10 mins. Serve hot as an appetizer or a side dish.
