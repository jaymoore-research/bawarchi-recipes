Vegetables in Onion Masala

Ingredients

1 lb vegetables
3 medium sized onions
1 tsp red chilli powder (or as per taste)
1 tsp salt (or as per taste)
1/4 tsp turmeric
2 tbl spoons oil
1 tsp cumin (jeera) seeds
2 tsp bengal gram floor (besan) - optional

Method

  1. Cut the vegetables into 1 inch pieces.
  2. Cut onions into big pieces and grind them.
  3. Heat oil in a sauce pan or kadai. Add jeera to it. Add the onion paste, turmeric, salt and chilli powder and fry for 3 minutes.
  4. Add the vegetable, mix well and cover the pan with a lid.
  5. Let it cook in medium heat. Keep stirring in between to avoid sticking to the bottom of the pan. If required, add some more oil.
  6. When it is done, you can optionally add besan to it. It adds to the taste.

Serve with white rice.
