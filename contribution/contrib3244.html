Potato Sabzi

Ingredients

1. Potatoes 3 nos.
2. Cauliflower 8 to 10 medium size florets.
3. Green peas 100 gms.
4. Onions 3 nos. medium size.[ finely chopped ]
5. Green chillies 8 nos. [ slit in the middle ]
6. Garam masala powder 1/2 tsp.
7. Turmeric powder 1/4 tsp.
8. Tomato Ketchup 2 tbsp.
9. Cumin seeds 1 tsp.
10. Juice of one lemon
11. Oil 2 tbsp.
12. Chopped coriander leaves for garnishing.
13. Salt to taste.

Method:

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut in to cubes.
  2. Then wash the cauliflower florets. Boil these together with salt and turmeric powder. When it is properly cooked strain the water from it and keep aside.
  3. In a kadai heat oil and add the cumin seeds when it starts to splutter add onions and green chillies. Saute till it becomes slight brown in colour.
  4. Now add the garam masala powder, tomato ketchup and stir for 1 minute. Then add the green peas and 3 tbsp of water. Cover the kadai with a lid and cook for 2 minutes in low flame. Add the boiled potatoes and cauliflower. Cook in low flame till the oil separates. Finally add lemon juice and chopped coriander leaves.
  5. Serve hot with Chappathi or Puri.

Making time: 15 minutes.
Makes: 4 servings.
Shelf life: best fresh.
