Spicy Pepper Chicken

Ingredients

2 lbs Chicken breast and thigh pieces
1 tblsp Black Pepper corns
2 tsps White Vinegar
1 tsp Ginger paste
1 tsp Garlic paste
2 nos. Green Bell Peppers
1 tsp finely chopped Garlic
1 no. large Onion
6 nos. Green Chillies
1/4 cup Soya Sauce
3 tblsp Oil
3 tblsp fresh Coriander
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Cut the chicken into approximately 2 inch pieces. Wash the pieces thoroughly and pat dry with a towel.
  2. Dry roast the pepper corns in a pan and grind them coarsely.
  3. Marinate the chicken pieces with ground pepper corn, ginger/garlic paste, vinegar and salt. Allow the chicken to marinate for atleast 3 hrs.
  4. Take some oil in a wok or large heavy bottomed vessel. 5) To the partially hot oil add finely chopped garlic and slit green chillies. Keep stirring
  5. Cut the onion and bell peppers into 1/2 inch cubes and saute them in the oil. Stir fry until the vegetables appear semi-cooked.
  6. Reduce the flame to a medium intensity and add the marinated chicken. Do not cover the vessel and stir from time to to time.
  7. Once the chicken turns slightly brown on the surface cover the vessel. Allow the chicken to simmer on a low flame for approximately 30 mins. Stir occassionally.
  8. Uncover the vessel and add soy sauce to the chicken. Allow the chicken to simmer in the soy sauce for about 10 mins.
  9. Garnish with finely chopped fresh coriander and serve with hot steamed rice.
