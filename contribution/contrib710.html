Malai Kulfi

Ingredients

2 Litres Milk
2 cups sugar
2 tablespoon orange or apple juice **
10 cardomom
15 cashews (break into tiny pieces)
1 slice bread (soak in 1/2 cup of milk)

Method

  • On a heavy bottom pan heat milk stirring continuously.
  • Add orange/apple juice so that the malai comes out.
  • Do not remove malai. Keep stirring and boil the milk to half.
  • This will take about 2 hours.
  • Mash the bread and add to the content and stir for 5 minutes.
  • Turn the stove off and add ground cardomom and cashews.
  • Let it cool for a while and pour into plastic tumblers and freeze them.
  • Leave for 8 to 10 hours or till kulfi becomes solid.
  • To remove kulfi from tumbler roll the tumbler between the palm of your hands and empty the content on to a bowl.

    ** Note : adding apple or orange juice will not curdle the milk. You may increase or decrease quantity sugar depending upon your taste.
