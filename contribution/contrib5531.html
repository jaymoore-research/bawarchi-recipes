Low Fat Cheese/Peeper

Ingredients for Cooking

1Ā 500 ml container of 5% ricotta cheese
a heafty pinch of garlic powder
sprinkle of salt
1 green pepper
1 red pepper
1 med onion
1/2 tsp ginger and garlc paste each
2 tsp tandoori masala powder(preferably Raja brand)
1 tsp garam masala
1/2 tsp cayenne or chilli powder
1/2 tsp lemon pepper powder (optional) subsitute with amchur if you wish
1/2 tsp or to taste salt
1 tblsp light vegetable oil (conola)

Method

  1. Take a microwavable dinner plate
  2. Mix the garlic powder and sprinkle of saltĀ with cheese andĀ spread the cheese so that it is about 1/2 inch in height.
  3. Cover with plastic wrap such that aĀ corner is left open for steam to escape.
  4. Microwave on high for 7 - 10 mins or until cheese is firm to touch.
  5. Uncover carefully minding the steam and cut into 1 inch peices.
  6. Cut the peppers and onion in 1 inch peices. IHeat the oil, toss in the onions for 30 secs. add the masalas and chilli powder wait 15 secs
  7. Add the peppers and cheese.
  8. Stir gently lower the heat to med add salt and lemon pepper.
  9. 10 min and you have delicious colorful dish.
  10. Sprinkle with coriander leaves if desired and serve with roti, rice or toated bread.Ā It is a complete meal for four.
