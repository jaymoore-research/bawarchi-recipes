Kodbale

Ingredients

Rice flour 1 cup
Maida 2/3 cup
Shredded coconut 1/4 cup
Red chilli powder 2 table spoon
Salt 1 table spoon
Oil 6 tea spoon
Asafoetida (hing) Method

  1. Fry maida in a shallow pan for 6-8 minutes.( medium flame).
  2. Then add the rice flour, coconut powder, salt, chilli powder, hing and mix.
  3. Heat the oil and pour on the mixture.
  4. Add required quantity of water and make a dough. ( Should not be too hard or soft).
  5. Keep aside for 10-20 minutes. Then take the dough ( size of a lemon) and knead it well.
  6. Roll it with your hands till it takes the shape of a rope (don't make it thin) of about 2-3 inches and coil them.
  7. Deep fry in oil on medium. You can keep this for a month or so in an air tight box.
