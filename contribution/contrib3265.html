Cheese Onion Torti

Ingredients

More then enough for one person.
1) Time 5-10 min. max.
2) Two small Tortilla/One big Tortilla
3) Chopped Onions (1)
4) Chopped tomatoes (1)
5) Chopped green chillies (1)
6) Cheese spread
7) 2-Cheese slices
8) Ghee made from available butter preferred to oil
9) Hot salsa

Method:

  1. First of all cut the onion,tomato and chilly
  2. Then apply chhese spread on one of the Tortilla and also cut the cheese slice and apply on the edges of the same tortilla.
  3. Then spread hot salsa as per the taste you want on it
  4. Then spread chopped onions,tomatoes,chilles on it
  5. Now put second Tortilla above this one.
  6. Make sure you fill the stuff in the middle and not on the edges,else it won't stick and will remain open.This will spoil the taste.
  7. Now on Tava apply some ghee and carefully hold the tortillas and place on the tava
  8. Bake it properly on both sides,make it little brown,so that it is crispy and tastes good if it is in ghee.
  9. There you go,its ready to eat.Make pieces as per your need and enjoy it.

Well if you have a one big Tortilla, no problm just do the whole procedure on one half of the tortilla and rest of the things are the same.
