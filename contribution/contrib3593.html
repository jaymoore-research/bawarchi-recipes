Egg Layerwala

Ingredients

Egg 4
Onion 3 nos.
Oil 1-2 tblsp.
Tomatoes 2 nos.
Red chilli powder 1 tsp.
Turmeric powder (haldi) 1 tsp.
Garlic paste 3/4 tsp.
A pinch of Garam Masala
Salt according to taste
coriander leaves for garnish

Method:

  1. Cut onions and In a pan fry them to pink, add garlic paste and fry it nicely.
  2. Cut tomatoes into small pieces and put it in this nad fry, add chilli powder, garam masala, haldi powder and salt . Fry all the ingredients nicely then in the end just break the eggs and put directly on this mixture one by one so that it get covered up with eggs. Keep for 15 mins. on low flame.
  3. Garnish with coriander leaves and cut with a knife to pieces and serve it.

Note: You can make layer of eggs on any dry sabji's or kheme (minse) for egg lovers.
