Maize (Corn) Sweet

Ingredients

Maize (corn) : two pieces
Coconut : 1/2
Elaichi : two or three
Jaggery to taste
Oil for frying

Method:

  1. First remove the skin and take the seeds of maize (corn) and grind it like a paste.
  2. Great the coconut, add with the maize (corn) paste and grind it like a paste.
  3. Great the jaggery, add it to the above paste and grind it until it turns soft. And add elaichi powder also.
  4. Heat the oil in frying pan (khadai) take the above paste and make it like vadas with out hole.
  5. Deep fry it until it turns thick brown.
