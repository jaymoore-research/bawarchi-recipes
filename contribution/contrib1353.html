Avial

Ingredients

250g White Pumpkin (bhopla)
250g Red Pumpkin
150 g Carrot
100 g Brinjals
250 g Suran
200 g Beans
1-2 Drumsticks
2-3 Potatoes
(All these vegetables should be cut into Long strips)

For garnishing:

1/2 cup grated Coconut
2 cups Buttermilk
1 tsp Cumin seeds
1 tsp Turmeric powder
1 strand Curry Leaves (kadipatha)
Salt to taste
Oil

Method:

  1. Take a pan and put some oil in it.
  2. Add the cumin seeds in it and fry till brown. Also add the coconut in it at the end. Blend them with buttermilk and make a fine paste.
  3. Wash all the vegetables.
  4. Take a wide bottom Pan and Boil all the vegetables in it with water.
  5. Add turmeric pd, salt when the vegetables are being boiled. Check whether the suran is fully cooked. Then add the paste and the remaining buttermilk and bring to a boil.
  6. Garnish with curry leaves. Enjoy with rice
