Vegetarian Fried Fish

Ingredients

1 cup Milk
1 1/2 cup Semolin
1/2 cup grated Cheese
Salt, Red Chilli Powder, Ginger & Garlic paste to taste
1/2 tab.spoon Coriander powder 10 whole Black Pepper Corn
Bread Crumbs

Method:

  1. Heat the milk in a pan.
  2. Add grated cheese, coriander powder, red chilli powder, ginger-garlic and salt.
  3. Add semolina gradually and keep cooking till it forms into one
  4. Grease a dish and spread the mixture evenly to form a 1/2" thick layer. Allow to solidfy for 2 hours.
  5. Cut into diamond shapes. Cover with bread crumbs place 1 pepper corn whole to the corner of each diamond shape to resemble fish eye.
  6. Heat the oil and deep fry till golden brown.
