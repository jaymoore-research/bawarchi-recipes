Thaalatha Kozhambu

Ingredients

Vegetables:

Yam (chenai) - 1 bit
Sweet Potato - 1
Snake Gourd -1 bit
Plaintain - 1 bit
Watergourd (poosani) - 1 bit
Beans (Avaraikkai) - a little
Colacasia (arbi) 5
Pumpkin - 1 bit
Chinese beans (kothavarai) - a little
Eggplant 2
Okra a little
Capsicum 2 + any green vegetables

Other ingredients:

Toor dhal 2 tsp
Urud dhal 2 tsp
Chana dhal 2 tsp
Sesame seeds 1 tsp
Rice 1 tsp
Red chillies 1 tsp

Fry all the 6 ingredients without pouring oil in it and grind it in the form of powder. Add also alittle amount of coconut along with this when you grind.

Method:

  1. Take a little amount of tamarind in water. Add turmeric powder, salt and add all the above vegetables. Cook the vegetables in tamarind water.
  2. Add the above powder after the vegetables has been cooked. Let it be in the stove till it boils.
  3. Then season it with Mustard and Curry Leaves.

This dish will go well with Dosai, Chappati and Pongal. Its really a nutritious food.
