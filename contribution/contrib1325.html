Ingredients

2 cups Milk Powder
1 cup Water
1 Egg
4 teaspoons Sugar
1 level teaspoon Baking Powder
1 tablespooon Butter/ Margarine
A pinch of Saffron
Cardamom powder to taste
Crushed Pistachios 2 Tablespoons

Method:

  1. Mix 1 cup milk powder with 1 cup water thoroghly.
  2. Add sugar and stir till it dissolves and keep on low fire.
  3. Sieve 1 cup milk powder with 1 Tsp B.P.
  4. In a bowl break the egg and whisk with a fork lightly.
  5. Add to the sieved flour + B.P mixture.
  6. Add the butter and mix to form a soft dough. Do not knead.
  7. Shape the dough into one inch diameter balls. Do not press too hard.
  8. Slightly flatten the balls and drop into Milk-The milk should be boiling.
  9. The balls will slowly enlarge and float to the top.
  10. Slowly add all the balls, taking care not to disturb the others.
  11. Keep on low fire untill all the balls have risen. Keep for four to five minutes. Do not stirr!
  12. Add saffron and cardamom powder and garnish with crushed pistachios.

Serve chilled.
