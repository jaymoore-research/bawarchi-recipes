Potato Saung

Ingredients

5-6 medium sized Potatoes
3 large Onions-chopped into small pieces
4-5 Red Chillies or 2 tbsp red chilli powder
2 tbsp Tamarind paste
2 tbsp Oil.
1 tbsp Salt

Method:

  1. Boil the potatoes and cut into small pieces.
  2. Grind the red chillies with some water and the tamarind to make a paste.
  3. Heat oil in a kadai (Typical saung is made with coconut oil and those of you who like it can use it) and fry the chopped onions till light brown.
  4. Add the potato pieces ,salt , red chilli-tamarind paste or if you are in a hurry- red chilli powder and tamarind paste. Add a little water if too thick and bring to a boil and then let it simmer for 10-15 mins till a thick gravy is formed.
  5. Serve with Rice or hot Phulkas.
