Rava Dosa

Ingredients

Rawa - 1 cup
Rice Flour - 1 cup
Maida - 3/4 cup
Curd - 2 tbsp
Salt
Green Chillies - cut into fine pieces
Jeera - 1 tsp
Vaghar of Rai

Method:

  1. Add all ingredients and make it wattery by adding water. For every dosa you have to mix it and put on the tawa since water will stand on top.
  2. Keep the non-stick tawa on the gas, when it is warm add little oil and with the help of a cloth wipe it. Then pour the batter one ladle high so that it comes in the form of holes in between. Do not spread like normal dosa since it will stick. Put oil on the sides and turn it after it starts becoming brown and crisp.

After making these dosas, you have to run out of the kitchen since your gas will be in a mess! But its worth it since rawa dosas are supposed to be fabulous!
