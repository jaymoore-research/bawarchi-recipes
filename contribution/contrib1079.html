Sindhi Curry With Vegetables

Ingredients

4 tablespoons besan (gram flour)
3 tablespoons hot oil
1 teaspoon cumin seeds
1/2 teaspoon methi seeds
a pinch of asefotida (hing)
2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
1/2 teaspoon red chilli
1 tablespoon tamarind paste
1 tablespoon curry leaves
1 tablespoon chopped cilantro
1 teaspoon chopped mint
1 chopped green chilli
1 teaspoon chopped ginger
1 cup of your selection of chopped vegetables e.g. carrots, potatoes, cauliflower, eggplant, okra etc

Method:

  1. In pot fry cumin seeds, asefotida (hing) and methi seeds in oil till brown.
  2. Then gradually add besan powder. Continuously stir the besan till it is golden brown.
  3. Then add 4 cups of warm water.
  4. Continue to stir the besan in water till you have consistency in the liquid.
  5. Then add salt, ginger, green chilli, tamarind and spices to the liquid.
  6. Let the liquid boil and then add vegetables and, curry leaves.
  7. Set the heat low and let it slow boil for 15 minutes.
  8. Then add mint and cilantro to the liquid.
  9. Let it boil till vegetables are soft and cooked and the curry is of a soup consistency (not too thick).
  10. Remove from heat and serve immediately.
Serves : 3-4
