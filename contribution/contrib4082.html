Bhindi-Dahi Masala

Ingredients

1/2 kg Bhindi
1 Onion, chopped fine
1 Tomato, diced
1 cup Curd, beated well
1 tsp Dhania-Jeera Powder
1 tsp Garam Masala
1/2 tsp Red Chilli Powder
1/4 tsp Turmeric Powder
1 inch Ginger, chopped fine
1 clove of Garlic, chopped fine
Salt to taste
Oil for frying
Mustard, Jeera, Hing, Kadipatta (curry leaves) to season

Method:

  1. Wash, dry and chop the bhindi into 1 inch thick pieces.
  2. In a kadai, pour 2 tsps of oil, put the seasonings in it.
  3. When they splutter, put the onions, ginger, garlic   and fry till they are transparent.
  4. Add the tomatoes and fry for a minute.
  5. Add the turmeric and dhania jeera powder, fry for a minute.
  6. Add the bhindi and fry for 3-4 minutes.
  7. Add the beaten curd, salt, garam masala and mix well. Cover and simmer for 5 mins or until the bhindi is soft.
  8. Top with coriander leaves and lime juice and Serve hot with rice/roti.

Note: You can slit the bhindi lengthwise, for a different look. Adjust spices according to taste and quantity.
