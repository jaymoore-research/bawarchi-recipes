Colourful Vegetable-Fruit Salad

Ingredients

Carrot 1 medium size
Onion 1(optional)
Black Seedless Grapes a bunch
Tomatoes (2)
Fresh Lettuce a pack
Apple 1, Cherries few
Radish 1 medium,

For Seasoning:

Lemon 1
Salt to taste
Green Chillies,
Ginger small bit(optional)
Cilantro 1 tbsp

Method:

  1. Grate carrot, radish and chop the other ingredients into small pieces and mix them together.
  2. Then add the juice of one lemon and grind the green chillies, ginger and the cilantro and add it to the mixture and your salad is ready to be served.
  3. This goes well along with parathas and even as a main dish for lunch.
