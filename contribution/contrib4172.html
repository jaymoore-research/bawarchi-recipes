Orange Delight

Ingredients

Condensed milk-1 tin (400g)
Fresh cream-400 ml
Fresh orange juice- (strained)-600 ml
Gelatine-3 tbsp

Method

  1. Mix condensed milk & cream nicely. Till well blended.
  2. In a bowl take 6 tbsp of orange juice & sprinkle in the gelatine….leave it aside for 5 min to soak. Now melt it over a pan of hot water. Till the solution is clear..it should not boil.
  3. Now quickly mix in melted gelatine to remaining orange juice..& quickly mix in this orange juice-gelatine mixture to the milkmaid mixture. It should be done fast to avoid the setting of gelatine into small lumps.
  4. Pour the mixture in a flat tray & set it in refrigerator.
