Ginger Chicken

Ingredients

2 lbs chicken
2 large onions
6 green chillies
2 inch piece ginger
2 tbsp garlic paste
1 tsp Soy sauce
Salt
Cilantro
3-4 drops Red food color
2-3 drops Brown food color

Method:

  1. Grind the onion, ginger, garlic and green chillies to a smooth paste.
  2. Fry this paste in oil till it is well done...the oil should separate from the paste.
  3. Then add the soy sauce and salt.
  4. Add in the red and brown food color...very little of it...!!!
  5. Add in the chicken and cook till its done. Reduce the gravy.
  6. Garnish with cilantro
