Chicken White

Ingredients

One medium sized broiler
Handful of plain cashewnuts (not salted or roasted)
Two fresh green chillies
Mini bunch of coriander leaves
1 tsp of jeera seeds
Salt to taste
Red Pepper powder
Pinch of turmeric
1 cup of yoghurt
Quarter tsp of whole cinnamon Cardomom, black pepper
A tbs of ginger garlic paste
One onion

Method:

  1. Marinate chicken pieces in yoghurt, salt, ginger garlic paste, overnight or for 6 hours.
  2. Grind the cashewnuts, coriander, jeera seeds, green chillies, cinnamon, cardomom, black pepper to a fine paste.
  3. Chop : Onion fine
  4. Fry onions in a tbs of oil to a golden brown.
  5. Add the marinated chicken, and keep frying to seal the juices, for about 6 minutes, constantly stirring.
  6. Add the ground paste and stir till it covers all the chicken.
  7. At this point, correct the seasoning. Add a little extra red chillie powder, if a chilli fan.
  8. Add water, cook the chicken with a lid over the saucepan till chicken is tender.
I do not recommend a pressure cooker for chicken ever, it makes it stringy. Serve with hot steamed rice and onion and tomato salad.
