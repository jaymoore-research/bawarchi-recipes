Set Dosa

Ingredients

Parboiled rice - 1 1/2 cup
Ordinary rice - 3 cups
Urad dhal - 1/4 cup
Methi seeds - 1 tspn
Powha - 1 handfull
Puffed rice - 1 handfull
Rice flour - 3/4 cup
Maida - 1/2 cup
Salt - 1 tspn

Method

  1. Soak parboiled rice and ordinary rice for 3 hrs. Urad dhal and methi seeds for 1 1/2hrs and powha and puffed rice for 1/2 hr.
  2. Grind parboiled rice and ordinary rice together first then urad dhal and methi seeds and at last powha and puffed rice with enough water.
  3. Mix all the batter in one container and stir well. The batter should be smooth. Keep it in a warm place for 8 hrs for fermentation.
  4. Now Add rice flour and maida in a separate bowl and make it into a paste with water.
  5. Mix this paste to the batter and add salt.stir well, the batter is ready.
  6. Put little oil on tava.Add one ladle full of batter on tava.
  7. Make small dosa with 10cm in diameter.
  8. When dosa appears with lots of holes on top,you can turn over and cook for a minute.
  9. Make a 3-4 set of dosa and serve with chutney or sambar.

Serves 5 people.
