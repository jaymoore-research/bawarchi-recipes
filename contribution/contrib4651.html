Kozhambu (sambhar)

Ingredients

1 cup Toor Dal
1/2 tsp Mustard
2 tbsp Coriander (dhaniya)
A pinch of Turmeric
A pinch of Hing (asafoetida)
1 tsp Rice
1 cup Coconut (grated)
7-8 Red Chillies
Curry leaves

Method:

  1. Grind the above ingredients except toor dal & curry leaves by adding little amount of water.
  2. Make it into a paste (in between coarse & fine).
  3. Cut the vegetables into small pieces, cook along with toor dal & a pinch of turmeric.
  4. Add the ground paste to cooked vegetables & toor dal. Add salt to taste & mix well. Boil nicely. Add some curry leaves while boiling. Your kozhambu is ready.
  5. You can mix this with rice & eat or you can also have this as a side dish with rotis, chapatis. Hope you like this.
