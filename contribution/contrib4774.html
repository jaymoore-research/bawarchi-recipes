Chicken roast

Ingredients

Chicken 1kg (cut into big pieces)
cinnamon 2-3stick
cardamom 5
cloves 6
whole redchilly 10
ginger garlic paste 2 tbs
onion 2 big (cut in to long pieces)
tomato 2 (diced)
salt to tate
oil for frying

Method:

  1. Heat the oil in a non stick pan. Put cinnamon sticks,cardamom,cloves and red chillys over the pan.and fry for a while.
  2. Add onions and fry till golden brown.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste fry again.
  4. Add diced tomatoes fry for few minutes.
  5. Finally add the chicken pieces and fry well.
  6. Add some salt and half a cup of water in to chicken mixture.
  7. Close the pan and allowo it to boil.Keep the stove between low and medium.
  8. Stir occationally but carefully. Otherwise the chicken pieces may break.
  9. When it is done the chicken pieces will be in brown colour with thick gravy.

Your delicious roast is ready.

It goes well with friedrice or chappathi.

Enjoy!!!
