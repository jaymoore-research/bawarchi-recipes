Mixed Veggi Halwa

Ingredients

Potato - 1 cup (cut into small pieces)
White pumpkin - 1 cup (cut into small pieces)
Carrot - 1 cup (cut into small pieces)
Moong dal - 2 cups
Sugar - 4 1/2 to 5 cups
Cardamom - 4-5 pods (powdered)
Cashew - 10
Ghee - 1 cup

Method:

  1. Cook all the vegetables with very little water, till tender. Drain off all the excess water.
  2. Now grind the cooked veggis into a smooth paste. Keep aside.
  3. Fry moong dal till it produces nice aroma.
  4. Soak the fried dal for 1 hour in water then cook and grind it into a smooth paste.
  5. In a heavy bottomed vessel, add the mixed veggi paste, moong dal paste, sugar and ghee.
  6. Cook till it reaches halwa consistency. Take it off the heat.
  7. Fry cashew halves in a little ghee and add to it. Add cardamom powder to the halwa. Serve warm or cold.
