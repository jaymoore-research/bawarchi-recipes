Padi Puli Padi More

Ingredients

Fenugreek seeds 1 teaspoon
Red Chillies 3
Fresh Coconut, 1 cup
Curry Leaves
Sour Buttermilk 2 cups.
Tamrind small lemon size
Turmeric powder 1 pinch
Vegetables(Okra, Squash, Eggplant) 1 cup
Salt to taste

For seasoning

1/2 teaspoon Fenugreek seeds
1 Red Chilli

Method:

  1. Soak tamrind for half an hour and take out the juice. Boil the tamrind juice with pinch of turmeric powder and salt till tamrind smell goes off. Add vegetables to it and cook.
  2. In a kadai, take 1 teasoon of oil, fry fenugreek seeds, curry leaves and red chillies till fenugreek seeds become dark brown. Allow it to cool and grind it with coconut to a fine paste.
  3. Put this paste in the boiled tamarind juice, boil it for 2 minutes. Now add soar buttermilk to this and when it froths up remove it promptly.
  4. Seasoning with fenugreek seeds and one red chilli.
