Noorjahan Kofta Curry

Ingredients

300 g paneer, crumbled
100 g potatoes, boiled and mashed
50 g breadcrumbes
1 1/2tsp. garam masala powder
1 tsp poppy seeds
1/2 tsp cumin seeds
Salt to taste
3 tbsp oil for gravy
Oil for deep frying

Grind to paste:

300 g tomatoes
1 pod garlic
1.5 cm ginger
2 tsp chilli powder
1-1/2 tsp turmeric powder

Method :

  1. Mix paneer and mashed potatoes.
  2. Divide the mixture and roll into small balls.
  3. Roll them in breadcrumbes and deep fry in hot oil till golden brown.
  4. Remove and keep aside

To make gravy :

  1. Heat 3 tbsp oil and season with cummin seeds and poppy seeds.
  2. Add tomatoes-ginger-garlic paste and fry well.
  3. Add a little water and make a thick gravy.
  4. Add salt and fried koftas.
  5. Sprinkle garam masala powder.
  6. Serve garnished with grated coconut.
  7. Serve with chappti.
