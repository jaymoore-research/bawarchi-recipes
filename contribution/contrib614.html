Pepper Rasam

Ingredients

Pepper powder -- 2 to 4 tsp (approx)
Toor dal (Cooked) -- 1 1/2 cups
Brown sugar or jaggery -- 1 tsp
Salt to taste.
Mustard seeds and jeera -- 1 tsp each
Tamarind paste -- 1 tsp

Method:

  1. Heat a tbsp of oil in a vessel and add mustard and jeera to it.
  2. When the mustard seeds start to crackle, add the toor daal and some water.
  3. Now make a paste of tamarind, pepper powder and salt with a little hot water.
  4. Add it to the toor daal and stir well.
  5. Now, add the brown sugar and chopped cilantro and boil well.
  6. Also, if desired add lime juice.
  7. Drink as soup or mix with plain rice.
