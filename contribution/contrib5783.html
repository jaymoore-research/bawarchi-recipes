Dal Dhokli

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients

Tur Dal - 1/4 cup
urad dal - 1/4 cup
whole green moong dal - 1/4 cup
Yellow moong dal - 1/4 cup
Haldi powder - 1 tsps
Salt - to taste

For tempering :

Ghee - 2 tbsps
Chilli powder - 2 tsp
Garam masala - 2 tsp
Jeera - 1 tsp
Hing - 1 tsp
Coriander leaves - 2 tbsps

For the dokli :

Wheat flour - 3 heaped tabsps
Salt - to taste
Haldi powder - 1 tsp
Chilli powder - 1 tsp
Ghee - 2 tbsp

Method

  1. Put all the dals in a big degchi, add haldi powder and salt and start to boil.
  2. For the dokli, add all the ingredients and make a hard dough adding little water.
  3. Make small pea size balls and press with the thumb to make a slight impression.
  4. Make such doklis with the dough and add them to the dal whilst boiling.
  5. Keep adding water whenever the dal becomes thick.
  6. For tempering heat ghee in a small pan, add jeera, hing, chilli powder and garam masala and add to the dalĀ after the pulses are almost cooked.
  7. Boil for some more time till the tempering gets settled in the dal and then garnish with coriander leaves.

Note: If you feel the dal is too thick you can add water and make it to your consistency.
