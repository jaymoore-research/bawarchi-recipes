Bakka Vegetables

Ingredients

Potatoes 225 gms.
Beans 115 gms
Broad beans 225 gms
Tomatoes 225 gms
Carrot 115 gms
Onion 115 gms
Fat 50 gms
Coriander leaves - 1/4 bunch
Pomegranate seeds - 5 gms
Chilli powder 10 gms
Coriandar powder 5 gms
Turmeric a pinch
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Prepare vegatables. Then Blanch tomatoes. Slice onions.
  2. Fry the sliced onions. Add spices and fry well. Add vegeatables expect tomatoes. Mix Salt and water then cook. When vegetables are half done, add the tomatoes and pomegranate seeds. Cook till vegetables are tender and the gravy is thick.
  3. Garnish with coriander leaves. Then serve it.
