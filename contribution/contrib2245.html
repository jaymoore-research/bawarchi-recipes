Apple Saambar

Ingredients

2 Apples (sour)
Tamarind juice extracted from lemon sized ball
Sambar Powder (MTR) 4 spoons
Asafoetida
1 Onion cut into slices
1 Green Chilli
1 Cup Cooked Dhoor dhal

Method

  1. Peel off the apples and cut into small cubes.
  2. Soak the apples in lemon juice for 30 mins.
  3. With 2 spoons of oil in Tava, add musturd, cumins.
  4. Fry Onions till they turn brown. Add Green Chillis.
  5. Add Tamarind Juice, Saambar Powder, Boil this mixure for 5 mins.
  6. Add the apple cubes and continue boiling for another 5 mins
  7. Added the cooked Dhal and continue boiling for another 5 mins.
  8. Add Salt, Coriander leaves and put the stove in Sim for 10 mins.

This Samabar is delicous when taken with Rice.
