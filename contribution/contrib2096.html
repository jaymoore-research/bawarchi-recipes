Macroni Erachi

Ingredients

1 Kg boneless Meat diced (chicken or lamb)
1 tablespoon each ground Coriander and Cumin
1/4 teaspoon Turmeric Powder
1 teaspoon Chilli Powder
2 heaped teaspoons Garam Masala
1 large Onion and 2 Green Chillies sliced
1 teaspoon each ground Garlic and Ginger
1 tin Coconut Cream (450 mls) or 2 cups Coconut Milk
Oil 2 tablespoons plus 1 teaspoon
2 tablespoons White Vinegar
Salt to taste
300 gms Macroni Elbows or any small pasta
4 large Tomatoes chopped

Method

  1. In a dish combine all the dry masala powders and ginger and garlic paste with the vinegar. Add the meat and marinade for 2 hours or overnight in the fridge
  2. Heat the oil and cook the onion and chillies till onions are brown.
  3. Now add the marinaded meat and tomatoes and coconut cream.
  4. Add salt to taste. Cook for about 50 minutes till the meat is cooked . There should be about 1/2 cup to 1/4 cup gravy in the meat, if not add a little water
  5. In another dish cook the macaroni in boiling salted water till just cooked. Drain and add 1 tsp oil and mix well.
  6. Now add to the cooked meat. Stir well and leave to cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Remove from heat. Stand for 5 minutes and serve with a salad.

Enjoy.
