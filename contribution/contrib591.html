Kadagu Puli

Ingredients

Raw Cucumber - 1 (one)
Mustard Seeds - 1 tsp
Grated Fresh Coconut - 1 Cup
Green or Red Chillies - 4
Coriander Leaves(Cilantro) - Fistful
Turmeric - A pinch
Jaggery - To Taste
Tamarind Pulp - 4 tsps

Tamarind pulp:

Soak a little amount of tamarind in warm water for a few minutes and squeeze by hand to make a rich pulp. Remove the waste and keep the pulp.

Method:

  1. Cut open the Cucumber and remove the seeds.Cut the Cucumber into very small pieces.
  2. Grind the mustard seeds, grated coconut, chillies, coriander leaves (cilantro), turmeric, jaggery and tamarind pulp into a paste.
  3. Mix the cut Cucumber into the ground paste.
  4. Add salt to taste.
  5. Garnish with a tsp of mustard seeds, curry leaves and red chillies.

Serving Instructions:

Serves two (2). Serve it with steaming rice and ghee (clarified butter).

Variation:

Use any vegetable of choice instead of Cucumber. However, you need to cook the vegetable before mixing it with the ground paste.

I hope you enjoy this tasty, but quick Iyengar dish!!
