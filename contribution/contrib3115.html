Steaming Sooji Dhokla

Ingredients

Sooji - 2 cups
Curd - 1/2 cup
Water - 1 and 1/2 cup
Eno Fruit Salt - 1 tsp
Oil - 2 tbsp
Green Chillies - 2 finely chopped (optional)
Red Chillies, Hing and Kadi Patta - For Tadka
Corriander Leaves
Grated Coconut
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Mix sooji, curd, water and salt in a container and make it into a batter.
  2. Leave the batter for 1/2 an hour.
  3. Add Eno Fruit Salt and green chilles to the batter.
  4. Pour it in a greased dish and steam it for 10 to 12 minutes. Take it out in a separate dish and cut it into pieces.
  5. Prepare tadka of mustard seeds, red chillies, hing and Kadi patta.
  6. Pour tadka on it and garnish the dhokla with coriander leaves and grated cocounut.
  7. Serve with a spicy Chutney.
