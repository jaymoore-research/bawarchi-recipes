Spicy Chana Masala

Ingredients

2 cups Dried Chana
3 medium size Onions
One medium size Potato
One medium size Tomato
One spoon Ginger and Garlic paste
5 to 6 Green Chillies
1/2 spoon Garam Masala
Salt and Chilli Powder to taste

Method:

  1. Soak the dried chana about 4 to 5 hours ahead of time. (I generally keep soaked chana in my refrigerator ready anytime)
  2. Cut onions and chilli into small cubes and fry in oil until onions are golden brown in a large pan/bowl sufficient to accomodate all the ingredients (preferably non-stick)
  3. Meanwhile, boil chana in cooker for 10 minutes
  4. Peel and cut raw potato into small pieces
  5. Cut raw tomato into small pieces
  6. Add cut potato, tomatoes and bolied chana to fried onion/chilli
  7. Add 1 cup water
  8. Add salt and chilli powder to taste
  9. Cover and let cook on simmer for 10 minutes (checking regulary for water level)
  10. Once the dish is cooked to a close gravy, sprinkle 2 to 3 spoons of garam masala powder and mix well.
  11. Serve with anything - it goes with Rice, Roti, Paratha and even as pizza topping and burger filling

You may alter individual ingredients such as chilli and chilli powder to suit your taste, but as such this is a hot and spicy dish and is supposed to be enjoyed this way. Good luck.
