Sprouts Tikki

Ingredients

Sprouts (1/2 cup) par boiled
Potato (1) small boiled & peeled
Corn flour
Green chillies 2
Onion 1
Cashews 6
Corainder leaves
Saunf 1/2 tsp.
Garam masala
Haldi
Salt to taste

Method

  2. Chop onions, green chillies and coriander leaves grind it alongwith the par boiled & cut sprouts in a grinder to a paste.
  3. Cut cashews into small pieces.
  4. Peel and mash the potato and add it alongwith corn flour to the sprouts paste and cashews.
  5. Also mix together saunf, haldi, haldi and salt to the batter.
  6. Make round flat tikkis, heat a flat pan, grease it with oil or butter and place the tikkis and once both the sides of the tikkis turn golden brown. Remove and serve hot with chutney or sauce.

Not many people like sprouts especially children, though its a high source of vitamins. You can use a non stick pan and use less oil which will retain its vitamin value. Enjoy the tikkis !
