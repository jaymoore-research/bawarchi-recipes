Kadai Chicken

Ingredients

Chicken 1 kg
Red Kashmiri Chilli 20
Dhania 2 tblsp
Garlic 2 pod
Ginger 1 piece
Turmeric powder 1 tsp
Vinegar 1 tblsp
Salt
Coconut Oil
Karipatta (more)

Method:

  1. First cut and clean the chicken.
  2. Make a fine paste of chillies, dhania, ginger and garlic.
  3. Remove the excess water from the chicken & marinate it with this paste, salt and vinegar. Cook this marinated chicken in a vessel. When it is nearly cooked or half cooked also keep the vessel aside.
  4. Take a wide kadai pour some oil when it gets heated put the karipatta and add the chicken mixture to it. Cook the chicken on low flame till it becomes dry. You can prepare prawns and fish like pomfret also using the same masala.
