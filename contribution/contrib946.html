Jaggery Lemon Juice

Ingredients

1. Jaggery 100 gms
2. Ginger 10 gms
3. Pepper powder 2 teaspoons
4. 4 to 5 teaspoonful lemon juice
5. A pinch of salt

Method :

  1. Add jaggery to 4 cups of water and stir continuously to make it dissolve.
  2. Crush ginger, add it to this water, add pepper, lemon juice and salt.
  3. Then filter this to remove ginger pieces etc.
  4. Chill it and drink.

You can add pieces of fruits like Watermelon, Dharbooja to this drink. Tastes just great!! You won't believe that it has taken only these many ingredients to make this tasty and refreshing drink.
