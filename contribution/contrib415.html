Hi, I am Shefali Dhar a student in Athens, GA. The following is a recipe for Chicken Loaf given to me by my mother. What is a Chicken Loaf?? It looks like a loaf of bread. After preparation it can be refrigerated, sliced and eaten whenever the desire strikes!!

Chicken Loaf

Ingredients

1 cup chicken, boiled and shredded
3 medium sized potatoes, boiled and mashed
1 cup corn niblets, cooked
1/4 cup cheese, any brand will do. I use Kraft.
1 tbsp butter
1 tsp chaat masala
Salt, pepper to taste

Method

  1. Boil the chicken with salt and pepper. Once cooked, shred it.
  2. Boil the potatoes with a little salt. Once cooked, mash the potatoes with butter and chaat masala.
  3. Boil the corn niblets till cooked.
  4. Knead the chicken, potatoes, corn and, cheese well. Add salt and pepper to taste. This essentially is the chicken dough.
  5. Roll the "chicken dough" into the shape of a loaf of bread. Cover tightly with a plastic wrap and refrigerate for atleast 2 hours.
  6. For the best taste warm the Chicken Loaf before serving.

Can slice the loaf and serve.
Shell-life : Keeps for atleast a week - it usually gets over before the end of the week though
Making time : 30 minutes
