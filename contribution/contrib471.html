Poha Cutlets

Ingredients

1 cup poha
1boiled potato
2tbsp semolina (sooji)
2bread slice
2green chillies finely chopped
red chilli powder
salt to taste
lemon juice
turmeric powder
garam masala
fresh coriander leaves (chopped)
oil for deep fry.

Method

  1. Wash the poha in running water and then take it in a bowl and add water it as much that it can soak and poha becomes soft, then mash it.
  2. Mash the boiled potato in it and crush the bread slice in it.
  3. Add add rest of the ingredients to it keep aside little bit of sooji.
  4. Make small balls of it and flatten it then wrap it in sooji and deep fry it on med high flame.
  5. Serve hot with green chutney or tomato ketchup.

Total Preparation 1/2 hr.
