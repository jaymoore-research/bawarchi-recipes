Appam

Ingredients

Raw rice - 1 cup
Idli rice - 1 cup
Urad dhal - 1/4th cup
Cooking soda - a pinch
Salt
Sugar - 1 tspn
Fenugreek - 1/2 tablespoon
Coconut - 1/2

Method:

  1. Soak both thrice varieties and urad dhal in water for 3 to 4 hours.
  2. Then grind it separately.
  3. Add the fenugreek to the rice while grinding.
  4. You can grind both the rice together.
  5. Then keep it aside for fermentation for atleast say 7hrs before making appam add salt, cooking soda, sugar and coconut milk.
  6. Take only the first milk mix all the ingredients.
  7. The batter should be a little watery than the dosa batter consistency take a shallow non stick pan add little gingerly oil.
  8. Pour the batter and close it with a lid for 5 seconds, it will not stick at all

Your Appam is Ready

Don't turn it like u do for dosa. It goes well with coconut milk, ishtu, kurma

Serves 4
