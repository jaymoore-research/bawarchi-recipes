Milagoottal

Ingredients

Cabbage (finely chopped) - 2 cups
Moong Dal - 1 cup
Green chillies - 3
Jeera - 1 tsp
Kaali mirch (pepper (Indian, not American!!) - 1/2 tsp
Coconut (grated) - 1/4 cup
Sarson - 1/4 tsp
Udad dal - 1/4 tsp
Salt to taste

Method

  1. Bring water to boil in a heavy-bottomed vessel and boil moong dal and cabbage together, till they are well-cooked.
  2. Grind coconut, green chillies, jeera and kaali mirch to a paste, and mix it well with the boiled dal and vegetable.
  3. In order for everything to mesh well, let the koottu remain in stove in minimum heat for about 3 to 4 minutes. Add salt.
  4. In a kadai heat a tbsp oil and let the sarson and udad dal splutter well. Add it to the koottu.

This koottu goes very well with rice as well as phulkas.
