Veg Soya neggets with Onion

Ingredients

100 gms Soyabean Neggets
3 finely chopped Green Chillies
4 Big size Onion finely chopped
One cup Curd
Salt to taste
Oil

Method:

  1. Boil Soyabean neggets to make them soft and drain the water
  2. Put oil in pan. Heat oil.
  3. Put finely chopped onion and chopped green chilli and heat till brown add salt to taste
  4. Put soyabean neggets and cook it with cover for some time
  5. Add curd and cook till it is dry
  6. Serve hot. Enjoy the taste !
