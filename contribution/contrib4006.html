Chatpat Instant Noodles

Ingredients

2 Pack Top Ramen or any Instant Noodles of any flavour.
3 tbsp. Hot Salsa any flavour.
1 tbsp. Tomato Ketchup.
1 tsp. Chilli Sause.
Ground Black Pepper to sprinkle.
Water to boil

Method:

Start boiling the noodles as per instructions. When more than half done, mix salsa, ketchup, chilli sause. In last sprinkle black pepper powder. Serve hot.
