Vangi Bhareet

Ingredients

3 onions, chopped finely
3 green chillies, minced
2 large brinjals
3 cups yoghurt, beaten
1/2 cup coriander leaves, chopped
1/2 cup grated coconut
1 tsp ginger, shredded
Salt and sugar to taste

  1. Roast brinjals.
  2. Remove skin and stem. Mash well.
  3. Add onion, ginger, chillies, half the coriander, half the coconut, yoghurt, sugar and salt.
  4. Blend and chill.
  5. Garnish with remaining coriander and coconut, and serve chilled with bhakris or rotis.

Alternatively:

  1. Delete the coconut and yoghurt from the recipe above.
  2. Fry the chillies, onion and ginger in 2 tbsps hot oil.
  3. Add mashed brinjal, salt and sugar.
  4. Stir till blended.
  5. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with bhakris or rotis.

Alternately

Add 2 finely chopped tomatoes and 1 tsp chilli powder to the first recipe above. Fry onions, green chillies and Ā Ā ginger in 2 tbsps hot oil. Add tomatoes, chilli powder, mashed brinjal and blend. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve with bhakris or rotis.

ĀĀ General: To all these varieties of Bhareet, boiled green peas, roasted and pounded peanuts or pieces of Ā Ā cashewnuts can be added as variations.

Try it out, I am sure you will love it.
