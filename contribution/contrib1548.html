Spinach Milagootal

Ingredients

Spinach - 1 bunch
Red Chillies - 2
Cumin seeds - 2 teaspoons
Coconut - 1/2
Turmeric powder - 1/4 teaspoon
Toor daal - 1/2 cup

For garnishing:

Coconut Oil - 2 teaspoons
Mustard seeds - 1teaspoon
Urud daal - 1 teaspoon

Method :

  1. Pressure cook toor daal.
  2. Clean spinach thoroughly and cook it in a vessel by adding some water (very little water is enough to cook spinach) along with salt and turmeric powder
  3. Grind the spinach in a blender and keep aside
  4. Grind grated coconut, red chillies and Cumin seeds and add them to the spinach.
  5. Add the cooked Toor daal to these and cook for about 7 minutes
  6. Garnish with coconut oil, mustard seeds and urud daal

Preparation time : About 25 minutes

Can be had with Rice and Fried Potatoes.
