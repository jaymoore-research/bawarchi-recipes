Semolina Pancakes

Ingredients

a.. 2 Cups semolina
b.. 1 cup fresh curd
c.. 1 cup cooked corn or 1 teacup chopped cabbage
d.. 2 green chillies, chopped
e.. 1 teaspoon soda-bi-carb
f.. 2 teaspoon ghee/oil
g.. 2 pinch asafetida
h.. Salt to taste

Garnish :

a.. Grated carrot
b.. 2 tsp chopped coriander

Method :

  1. Mix the semolina and curd with 1 teacup of water and leave aside for 5 to 6 hrs
  2. Add the remaining ingredients and mix very well.
  3. Prepare small pancakes on a non-stick fry pan using little ghee.
  4. Sprinkle grated carrot and coriander while cooking
  5. Seve.

Serve very hot!!!!

Enjoy.
