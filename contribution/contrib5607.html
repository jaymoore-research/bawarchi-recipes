Khatti Masala Daal

Ingredients

Tur daal  -  1 cup
Oil -  2 tblsp
Onion  -  1
Ginger paste  - 1/2 tsp
Garlic paste - 1 tsp
Tomato (diced or crushed) - 3 tblsp
Tamarind -  6-8 pieces (not paste)
Red chilli powder -  1 tsp
Turmeric  -  1/2 tsp
Mustard seeds - 1 tsp
Jeera seeds  -  1 tsp
Curry leaves
Salt to taste
Coriander leaves

Method

  1. Wash tur daal and boil it. Smash it.
  2. In a kadai, put oil.  Add curry leaves, mustard seeds and jeera seeds.  Fry for a minute
  3. Add onion and fry till slightly brown. 
  4. Add ginger and garlic paste.  Fry for a minute.  Then add the tomatoes (paste or diced).  Cook it till the oil separates.
  5. Add tamarind (not paste) in it.  Add turmeric powder and red chilli powder. Cook it for 4 to 5 mins.
  6. Add the smashed tur daal.  Add water as per the thickness required.  Add salt to taste.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves.
