Stuffed Karelas

Ingredients

250 gms karelas
1 large-sized onion (grated)
1 medium-sized garlic bulb
1 large-sized tomato (chopped)
1 tbsp aniseed
2 tbsp coriander seeds
4-5 tbsp oil
1/4 tsp turmeric pd
1 tsp chilli pd
Thread for tying the karelas

Method :

  1. Scrape the karelas and make slits.
  2. Apply salt liberally in the slits and outside. Leave for 5-6 hrs.
  3. Knead them well till they are soft and the bitter salt water is released.
  4. Wash with fresh water 2-3 times and squeeze dry.
  5. Heat oil in a kadai, add a few karelas at a time and fry them at low temp till brown.
  6. Dry roast aniseed and coriander in a hot kadai and grind.
  7. Heat 2 tbsp of oil.
  8. Fry onions and garlic.
  9. When the masala leaves the oil and is dry, it is ready for filling the karelas.
  10. Cool the mixture.
  11. Fill the masala in the karelas well.
  12. Tie with the thread to avoid the filling from coming out.
  13. Fry few pieces at a time at low temp. Do not add water.
  14. Add little amchur or lemon juice, if desired.
  15. Remove the thread before serving.
