Khara Pongal

Ingredients

Rice 1 cup
Moong dal 1 cup
Black pepper, 1-1/2 table spoon. (Depending on how spicy you want).
Jeera.1-1/2 table spoon
Grated coconut dry (copra)
Salt to taste

Method :

  1. Wash rice and moong dal throughly with water.
  2. Grind black pepper and jeera in a blender (do not add water. It should be a dry mixture)
  3. Add rice, Moong dal, Black pepper & Jeera mixture, Salt, grated dry coconut to a rice cooker. Cook for 15-18 min.

Serve hot with ghee.

Cooking time 15 min
