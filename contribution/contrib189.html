Rava Ladoo

Ingredients

1 cup rava
1 cup sugar
ghee to fry cashewnuts and raisins

Method

  1. First grind both rava and sugar into fine powder.
  2. Heat ghee in a pan and fry cashews and raisins and mix this with the combined rava and sugar powder.
  3. Make ladoos out of this mixture when it is hot.
  4. Can make 15 ladoos. Enjoy eating.
