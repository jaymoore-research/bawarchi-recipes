Spicy Chicken In A Jiffy

Ingredients

Chicken 500 gms cut-up
Yogurt/Vinegar 2 tbsp.
Red Chilli powder 2 tbsp or according to taste
Turmeric powder a pinch
Dhania powder 1 tbsp
Ginger Garlic Paste 1 tsp
Tandoori powder a pinch
Oil 2 tbsps.
Salt to taste

Method

  1. Wash the chicken and make small slits with knife or fork.
  2. Mix all the ingredients and pour oil and cook on medium fire stirring occassionally. If required add water for gravy.
  3. Serve hot with Rice and Dhal.
