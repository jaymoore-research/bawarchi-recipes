Peas Masala Bath

Ingredients

1) Rice 1 cup
2) Coconut 1/2
3) Green Chillies 2
4) Ginger 1 small piece
5) Green Peas 1/2 cup
6) Oil 2 tsp
7) Cardamom 1
8) Cloves 4
9) Cinnamon 1
10) Salt to taste
11) Coriander leaves
12) Mustard seeds

Method:

  1. First grind coconut (grated), ginger, cinnamon, cloves, green chillies, cardamom to a smooth paste by adding little water.
  2. In a pressure pan, add oil.
  3. Add mustard seeds to the ground masala and saute well.
  4. Clean the rice in running water and add to the masala. Stir well for two minutes.
  5. Add the green peas, 2 1/4 cup water and salt. Close the pressure pan. Keep for 2 or 3 sounds. Remove from fire.
  6. When the pressure becomes normal, transfer the bath in a bowl and stir it.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves.
  8. Serve with Raitha.
