Kothimbir Vadi

Ingredients

1. Coriander leaves (Cilantro) 1 large bunch
2. Ginger-Garlic paste 1 tsp.
3. Turmeric powder 1/4 tsp
4. Red Chilli powder 1 tsp
5. Cumin powder 1 tsp
6. Coriander powder 1 tsp
7. A small ball of Jaggery
8. Salt (as per one's taste)
9. Gram Flour (Besan) 1.5 cups
10. Garam masala 1 tsp.
11. Oil for frying.
12. Water to make a dough.

Method:

  1. Finely chop the coriander leaves. Wash them thoroughly with water.
  2. In a bowl, add the chopped coriander leaves, the above mentioned spices, salt, gram flour, jaggery & garam masala. Mix well. Add water to make a dough. Knead the dough properly using some oil.
  3. Its time to steam cook the dough. Grease a vessel (that fits in the pressure cooker) with some oil. Now, evenly spread the dough in the greased vessel.
  4. Steam cook on low flame without producing any whistles from the pressure cooker for almost 15-20 minutes.
  5. Cut the cooked cake into rectangular pieces.
  6. Fry the rectangular pieces till golden brown in a wok containing hot oil.
  7. Serve hot with Tomato Sauce or with Coconut Chutney.
