Chicken Sukka

Ingredients

1 2-lb Chicken, cut into small pieces
1 large Onion, chopped finely
A sprig of Curry Leaves
A small lemon sized ball of Tamarind
Salt to taste
Chopped Coriander Leaves for garnish

Roast the following and powder:

6 Kashmiri Chillies (or dry red chillies)
1 tsp Coriander Seeds
1/2 tsp Methi (fenugreek) seeds
1 tsp Cumin seeds
1 tsp Black Peppercorns
1 tsp Turmeric powder
1/2 tsp Cloves
1/2 tsp Mustard seeds
4 cloves Garlic
1 small piece Ginger

Blend to a coarse paste:

1 cup Coconut, shredded
1/2 tsp Methi (fenugreek) seeds
1/2 tsp Cumin seeds
3 cloves Garlic
1 stick Cinnamon

Method:

  1. Smear salt on the chicken and marinate the chicken in the powdered masala for about 1 hr.
  2. Extract juice from the tamarind, by immersing it in hot water for about 10 minutes.
  3. Heat 2 tablespoons of the oil/ghee in a non-stick pan and fry the chicken pieces until the outsides are nicely seared. Drain and set aside.
  4. Heat some more oil if needed in the pan and add the curry leaves, mustard and onion. When the onion turns transparent, add the ground coconut masala and fry for about 10 mins until the masala is dry and the oil starts separating from the masala.
  5. Add the reserved chicken pieces and the tamarind water to the coconut. Mix well and simmer till the water content reduces and the chicken is done.
  6. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
