Masala Baingan

Ingredients to marinate brinjal

Small Brinjal 1/2 kg.
Ginger Garlic paste
Chilly powder 1/2 tsp
Turmeric powder 1/2 tsp
Jeera powder 1/2 tsp
Dhania powder 1 tsp
Lemon Juice 1/2 lemon
Tamarind extract

Method

  1. Wash and slit brinjal into four pieces vertically, the top end should be attached as it is.
  2. Add 1/2 tsp turmeric powder, 1/2 tsp chilly powder, 1/2 tsp jeera powder, 1 tsp dhaniya powder, 1/2 lemon juice. Mix it well with ginger garlic paste. And marinate for about 30 minutes.

Ingredients for gravy

Dry dessicated Coconut
Three medium size Onion
Two medium size Tomato
Cooking Oil
Mustard seeds
One stalk Curry leaves
Salt to taste
Garam Masala powder
Garam masala ingredients

Method

  1. Slightly roast 4 tbsp dry dessicated coconut with sliced one medium size onion in little oil and grind to fine paste.
  2. Take ingredients of garam masala pkt and roast in little oil and make a fine paste.
  3. In kadai, heat 4 tbsp oil for two minutes then put mustard seeds, cury leaves, sliced two medium sized onion and fry a bit. Then add 2 tsp ginger garlic paste and fry for a while.
  4. Add coconut onion paste and fry to thicken the gravy. When oil separates, add sliced tomatos and salt. Add 1 tsp turmeric powder, 1 1/2 tsp dhaniya powder, 1 tsp jeera powder and 1 1/2 tsp red chilly powder. Fry it well until all the masala is cooked properly. (Please note that the masala should not be overcooked)
  5. Add the marinated brinjal and stir well. Add 3 cups of water, add 1/2 tsp sugar and tamarind extract. Stir it well. When it starts boiling, close the lid and cook brinjal on slow fire for about 20 minutes. When brinjal is cooked, sprinkle a bit garam masala powder and coriander leaves. Close the fire and leave it for about 5 minutes. Masala Brinjal is ready.
  6. Serve it with hot roti or rice.
