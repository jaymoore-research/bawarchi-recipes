Broken Wheat Uppuma (Low Cal)

Ingredients

1 tsp Urad Dhal

1/2 tsp. each Cumin and Mustard seeds
5 tsp Oil
1 stalk Curry Leaves
3 Green Chillies
1 cup mixed Vegetables
1 small Onion (cut)
1 small Tomato (cut)
Handful Coriander Leaves chopped
2 tsp Lemon Juice
Salt to taste
2 cups Broken Wheat (Samba Gothumai in Tamil)
2 1/2 cups Water

Method

  1. Heat oil in a heavy pan.
  2. Add the seeds, dal and fry on low till light golden.
  3. Add the green chillies, curry leaves and onions. Stir and fry till the onions are light brown.
  4. Add the Tomato and mixed vegetables. Add salt to taste.
  5. Close pan for tomatoes and vegetables to cook for 5 mins.
  6. Pour in 2 cups boiling water and stir well. Bring water to a boil.
  7. Add the the broken wheat and close pan for wheat to cook on medium fire for 3 mins. Bring to low and simmer for 5 minutes.
  8. Add lemon juice and coriander and mix well.
  9. Mould into a greased cup and unmould onto serving plate.

Serve hot with Steaming Coffee or Tea. Can accompany with Green Chutney or Gotsu or Pickle.
