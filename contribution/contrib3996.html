Tomato Rice

Ingredients

Tomatoes - 6 (large)
Red chilli - 10-12
Coriander seeds - 6-7 tbsp
Cinnamon sticks - 2-3
Oil - 8 tbsp
Salt to taste
Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp
Cooked rice - 3 cups

Method:

  1. Soak red chillies, coriander seeds and cinnamon for 2 hours
  2. Grind the above to a fine paste
  3. Put oil on the kadai. Fry the paste till the oil separates.
  4. Add cut tomatoes, salt and turmeric powder. Cook on low fire till oil separates again
  5. This chutney can be stored for 1 week to 10 days in the refrigerator. When required, mix with rice. Another option is to have the tomato chutney with chapatis.
