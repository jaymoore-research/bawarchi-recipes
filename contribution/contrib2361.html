Vadas

Ingredients

200 grams Self Raising Flour
50 grams Curd
50 grams grated Coconut
3 Green Chillies finely chopped
Small Ginger finely chopped
Half tea spoon Chilli Masala

Method

  1. Mix the dough and leave for 10 minutes.
  2. Make small portions in the shape of vadas and fry in luke warm oil until golden brown.

Tastes great with Tomato and Chilly Sauce.
