Molakootal

Ingredients

Preparation time: 20-30 minutes.
Serves: 5

Fresh Cabbage 1/2 Kg.
Potato 1 (Big)
Thuvar Dhal 100Grams
Grated Coconut 100Grams.
Cummins Seeds 1teaspoon.
Dry Red Chilies 2Nos.
Turmeric Powder 1/4teaspoon.
Mustard 1/2teaspoon.
Cooking Oil 1teaspoon (for seasoning)
Curry leaves 8-10 leaves (for garnishing)
Salt to taste.

Method

  1. Chop the cabbage finely and dice the potato to small cubes and keep aside.
  2. Wash thuvar dhal and pressure cook it.
  3. Add turmeric powder and salt to the cut vegetables and cook separately till fully cooked.
  4. Make a paste of grated coconut along with cummins seeds and dry red chilies.
  5. Add pressure cooked thuvar dhal and this paste to the cooked vegetables.
  6. Half a glass of water could be added if the stew is too thick.
  7. Stir all the contents and allow this mixture to simmer for 5 minutes.
  8. Care to be taken not to allow it to boil.
  9. Garnish molakootal with curry leaves and season it with mustard and cooking oil.

Seasoning: Take a spoon of cooking oil in a small pan and heat it on low flame. Allow the mustard seeds to splutter in hot oil and pour this instantly into the molakootal.

Wish you relish this dish.
