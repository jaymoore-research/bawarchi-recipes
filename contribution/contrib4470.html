Tofu With Capsicum Veg

Ingredients

2 small or big 1 Capsicum
1 big Onion
Tofu (firm tofu) or cake tofu
1 tsp Vinegar (white)
1-2 tbsp Soya Sauce
Garlic paste
Ginger paste
Black Pepper powder

Method:

  1. First cut the onion and capsicum in length wise.
  2. Take oil in the pan, fry the onion and capsicum together till it turns little red in colour.
  3. Add garlic and ginger paste, after 5 minutes add soya sauce, vinegar and black pepper powder.
  4. At the end, add the tofu's pieces in the veg.

Note:

You can put the tofu's pieces in the soya sauce just for colouring. (3-4 hours)
