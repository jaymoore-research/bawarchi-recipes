Thai-Vegetarian Gang Gar Ree

Ingredients

2 Medium Size Potatoes (peeled but not cooked)
1 cup of Shredded Coconut (its better if its fresh)
2 tsp of Yellow Curry Paste (Its very simple, read the last line for the recipe)
1 tsp Sugar
1/2 tsp Crushed Red Chilli powder (optional)
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. First Grind the shredded coconut with water and then filter it. Keep the coconut milk aside. Remember it should be thick. Don't make it too thin.
  2. Then cut the potatoes into thin round slices and keep it aside.
  3. Now take the pan and add half of the coconut milk and add 2 teaspoons of the curry paste you have prepared (recipe at the last) then allow it to boil so until you see the oil starting to separate from the coconut milk.
  4. Then add sugar and stir for 2 minutes.
  5. Add the sliced potatoes and the rest of the coconut milk and salt. Heat it covered until the potatoes are cooked.
  6. Reduce the heat to low and and keep it for 10 minutes. You can add the red chilli if you want it to be spicy now.
  7. Before serving decorate it with sliced green chillies.

This can be served with Plain white rice. Try it and Enjoy it!

Yellow Curry:Grind Cumin Seeds, Coriander seeds, turmeric powder in the ratio of 2 : 2 : 1 in a blender with/without water.
