Chanadal With Coconut Powder

Ingredients

1 1/2 cup Chanadal
2 cups grated Coconut Powder
1 small sized Onion
3 Green Chillies
2 chopped Tomatoes
1/2 tbsp Cumin seeds
6 tbs Oil
1/2 tbsp Red Chilli Powder
1/2 tbsp Tumeric powder
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Soak Chanadal in water for 1 hour.
  2. Take cooking bowl and put oil and heat it & add cumin seeds.
  3. Add onions and green chillies and cook till soft.
  4. Add chanadal, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, salt and 2 cups of water and cook it in low flame for 20 min.
  5. Then add tomatoes and cook it for 5 min. Mix with coconut powder.
  6. Serve with Rice or Parathas.
