Paprika Chicken

Ingredients

Chicken -- 2 kg (cut into medium pieces)
Chilli powder -- 2 tspns
Paprika -- 1 tspn
Cloves powder -- 1/4 tspn
mustard powder -- 1/2 tspn
Cinnamon powder -- 1/4 tspn
Turmeric Powder -- 1 tspn
Garlic Cloves -- 4-5 (crushed)
Plain Yogurt -- 4 tbspns
Lemon juice -- 2 tbspns
Oil -- 4 tbspns
Salt -- as per taste
2 Onions, sliced in rings and soaked in lemon juice.

Method:

  1. Combine chilli, paprika, cloves, mustard, cinnamon, turmeric, garlic, yogurt and lemon juice in a bowl. Add chicken and marinate at least 8 hours, preferably overnight.
  2. Heat oil in a frying pan, add chicken in single layer, fry on all sides until lightly browned.
  3. Transfer chicken and masala to an ovenproof dish . Bake, uncovered, in moderate oven for 15mins or until chicken is tender.
  4. Serve garnished with onion rings. This serves 6 to 8. Serve this dish with rice or Phulkas.
