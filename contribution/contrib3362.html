Stuffed Green Bell Peppers

Ingredients

6 Small Green Bell Peppers.
5 Medium Size Potatoes, boiled and mashed.
1 Tsp Amchoor Powder( Dry Mango Powder)
1/2 Tsp Chilli Powder.
1 Tsp of Ground Zeera
2 Chopped Green Chillies.( If Hot Use One)
Small Bunch Of Cilantro Chopped.
1/2 tsp of Zeera.
Salt to Taste.
2 tbsp Oil.

Method:

  1. Empty Bell Peppers from the inside, save the seeds.
  2. Thoroughly mix Amchoor, Ground Zeera, Salt, Seeds that came out of Bell Peppers, chopped Cilantro and Green Chillies to mashed potaoes. Let it sit for 1/2 an hour for flavours to blend in.
  3. Stuff Bell peppers with this mixture.
  4. In a skillet add oil and Zeera. When Zeera turns light brown line up peppers in a circle, top it with left over potatoe mix. Cover and lower heat. Cook until tender. Peppers can be turned upside down gently making sure they don't break. Potatoe mixture will start to turn brown and crispy, keep an eye so it doesn't burn. When tender and crispy turn off the heat. Peppers are ready.
