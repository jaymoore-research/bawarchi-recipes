Bhindi Palleli

Ingredients

250 gm Ladies fingers (Bhindi)
2 large sized onions
50 gm groundnuts (dry roasted and cleaned)
1 tablespoon of veg. Oil to fry
1 spoon jeera
6 green chillies slit cut
Turmeric powder quarter spoon
Sabzi masala 1 teaspoon
Besan two tablespoonfuls
Salt to taste

Procedure:

  1. Wash and clean bhindi and dry with a soft cloth.
  2. Cut them into fine rounds.
  3. Fine chop the onions and slit cut the green chillies.
  4. In a thick vessel, pour the oil. When it is hot add groundnuts and cut chillies.
  5. When done add jeera, turmeric powder, salt and onions.
  6. When onions are light brown then add cut ladies fingers.
  7. Do it on high flame and keep stirring frequently.
  8. When done crisply, add sabzi masala and sprinkle besan and turn off the flame.
  9. Stir it for a couple of times and serve hot with parathas, rotis or with hot rice.

Cooking time 10 mins.

Serving for 4 people.
