Chicken Gravy

Ingredients

Chicken Thighs - 4 pieces
Onion medium - 2
Tomato big - 2
Curry Leaves - a few leaves
Red Chilli powder- 1 1/2 tea spoon
Salt - 1 tsp
Mustard Seeds, Urad dal and Channa dal -1/2 tea spoon

To Grind:

Poppy seeds - 1 1/2 tsp
Cloves - 4
Cinnamon - 1 small piece
Garlic - 6 pieces
Ginger -1 inch

Method:

  1. First peel the skin from the chicken and cut it in to medium size pieces.Wash them well.
  2. Cook the chicken in the cooker with water and a little oil. Cook until 2 whistle sounds, take out from heat and keep aside.
  3. Heat oil in a kadai. Fry mustard seeds and urad dal, when it splutter, add onions and fry until it turns brown. Then add finely chopped tomato, fry well until it leaves oil.
  4. Then add boiled chicken with water, chilli powder, salt and ground paste. Turn the switch on medium heat. Cook until the gravy thickens and leaves oil. Serve hot. It goes well with Rice and Dosa.
