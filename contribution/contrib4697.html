Maryland Bhendi

Ingredients

5 Fresh whole tender Bhendi/okra
1 Tomato, chopped
1 onion, chopped
1/2 tablespoon ginger cut in thin slices
Vinegar

Method:

  1. Cut the Bhendi/Okra at both the ends.
  2. Choose these Bhendi/Okra from an organic store or a Farmers market.
  3. Cook Bhendi/Okra with a small amount of water until tender( 6 mins is my caluclated time).
  4. If you overcook it gets sticky and mushy - mix it with Rice and have a meal with it.
  5. Drain with a coalander.
  6. Put them in layer in glass dish or micro oven dish.
  7. Pour vinegar over the Bjendi/okra and marinate in refrigerator 4 hours.
  8. Drain out all the vinegar and water.
  9. Arrange Bhendi/okra in serving dish.
  10. Spread chopped tomatoes, onions, and ginger.
  11. Use the microoven and put in for 1 minutes
