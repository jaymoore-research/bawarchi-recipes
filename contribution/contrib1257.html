Lauki Burfi

Ingredients

1 litre Milk
2 cups grated Lauki (Only the green portion and not the white one)
1 cup Mawa or Khoya
1/2 cup Sugar (Little more if necessary)
1/4 tsf Green color (optional)
4 Elaichi finely grounded

Method

  1. Keep milk to boil, meanwhile grate the lauki at the same time.
  2. When is boiling put the freshly grated lauki. When the milk reduces to half its quantity then put mawa, sugar and color.
  3. When the mixture starts leaving the sides of the vessel put it in a aluminium tray (put grounded elaichi now and decorate with dry fruits like badam and kaju.
  4. Cut into pieces after 1 hr.
