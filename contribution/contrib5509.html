Fish Fingers

Ingredients for Cooking

Fish(boneless) - 250gms(pref salmon)
Tomato sauce - 4 tsp
Soy sauce - 1 tsp
Chilly powder - 1/2tsp
Salt - to taste
Bread crumbs

Method:

  1. Microwave the fish for 4 mins.
  2. Remove off the skin & scramble the fish.
  3. Add tomato and soy sauce, chilly powder, salt.
  4. Roll the fish into fingers & coat with bread crumbs.
  5. Microwave it again or deep fry in oil or shallow fry in a tawa.
  6. Serve with tomato sauce...
