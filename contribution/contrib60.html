Vegetable Upama

Ingredients

One cup sujee (Cream of wheat)
One cup mixed vegetable (Any vegetable)
3 cup water
2 tsp vegetable oil
1 tsp towar dal (arahra dal)
1 tsp mustard seeds
1 medium size onion (chopped)
1 green pepper (chopped)
some kadhi patha (metha neeam)
lemon juice (according to your taste)
salt (according to your taste)

Method

  1. Take the pot and cook the sujee (without oil) until it gets brown. Take it out from the pot (Note stir it continously)
  2. Take a pot and warm the oil in it.
  3. Pop the mustard seeds in the oil and then put the green pepper, towar dal, kadhi patha and chopped onion in it. Cook these things until onion become brown.
  4. Put the vegetable in the pot and add three cups of water, salt, lemon juice and cover the pot for 2 minutes.
  5. Now add sujee in the pot and mix it well. Turnned off the flame, cover the pot and put aside for 1 minute. This is ready to serve.

Quantity: 6 people
Cooking time: 10 min.
