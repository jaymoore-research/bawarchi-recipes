Fish Curry in Coconut Milk

Ingredients

Fish---500g
chillies-- 100 gms corianderseeds--100gms
fenugrreek-1 spoon
salt to taste
Coconut milk -- first milk (keep aside FOR POURING LAST); second milk you have to take for cooking the fish
1/2 tsp garlic and ginger paste
1 large Onions chopped
2-3 tamarinds soak in water

Method:

  1. First you have to pour oil in a kadai and put garlic paste
  2. When it becomes brown put coriander powder, turmeric powder, chili powder salt
  3. When it becomes dark brown pour tamarind along with the water (according to your taste) and the second coconut milk.
  4. When these ingredients boil put the first milk,.till becomes a thick paste.
  5. Last you pour the first milk and put some fenugreek powder and curry leaves and sim the flame.
  6. When it becomes a paste, off the gas.

Cooking Tips:

To remove the smell of fish and to clean the fish: Put some wheat flour limewater, or vinegar,and turmeric powder in a dough put the fish and circle it with hand for 1 minute and then wash it in water the fish will look clean(wash it water for 2 or three or 4 times).

If you pour a little bit of vinegar when you cook green peas the colour and freshness won't go.

If the chicken is kept in refrigerator and you want cook fast, take the chicken from fridge and put the chicken in salt water. The ice will go fast.

If the tomatoes are too much ripe don't throw it away, the juice wont come out when you cut. put the ripe tomatoes in salt water before you want to coo
