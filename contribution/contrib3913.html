Cucumber Mandas

Ingredients

200 gms Boiled Rice
50 gms Raw rice
250 gms Jaggery
250 gms Cucumber
1/2 Coconut
4-5 Cardamom powdered

Method:

  1. Soak the rice overnight and grind it the following day into a thick batter not watery but semi solid wet (like we do for Idli). When the rice has ground add jaggery and grated cucumber and grated coconut and grind till well blend. Add Cardamom and just a pinch of salt (optional).
  2. Remove the batter, mix a tablespoon of pure ghee and either steam or bake it till done.
