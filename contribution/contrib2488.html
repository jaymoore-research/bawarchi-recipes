Spicy Tandoori Chicken Masala

Ingredients

1 whole boneless Chicken breast, cleaned and sliced in long strips about 2 inches long.
1 teaspoon of garlic paste
1 teaspoon of ginger paste
3-4 teaspoons of Shan (Its a pakistani brand) Tandoori ChickenTikka Spice Mix
Juice of 1 lime
Salt to taste
1 teaspoon of oil
1 large onion, thinly sliced in long strips
3 peppers(simla mirch) sliced in long strips.
(I use green, red, orange, yellow to make the final dish look very colorful)
1 large tomato, chopped
1 teaspoon of red chilli powder (add more if u like it really spicy)

Whole spices:
4-5 cloves, 2-3 cardamoms, piece of bay leaf, 5-6 black peppercorns, piece of cinnamon stick
Chopped coriander leaves
2-3 tablespoons of vegetable oil

Method:

    Marinate the chicken with ginger, garlic,shan tandoori chicken tikka spice mix,lime juice, salt and oil for atleast 5-6 hrs in the refrigerator.

  1. Heat oil in a karahi and add the whole spices (cloves, cardamoms, bayleaf, cinnamon stick, peppercorns)
  2. After they start spluttering, add the sliced onions. Keep stirring till they just start to change color about 4-5 mins.
  3. Add the chicken along with the marinade and keep stir frying, till all the liquid has evaporated and the chicken is cooked through. Add red chilli powder as well check the salt. Add all the colorful peppers and stir fry again for a few mins. Also add chopped tomatoes and coriander leaves. Cover the lid for a while. Don't overcook the peppers, leave them a bit crunchy.

Serve hot with naans or rotis topped with coriander leaves as well a piece of lime on the side. Enjoy!!!!
