Ingredients

Red Raw Rice 500 gm
Grated Coconut 1 no
Grated Jaggery 200 gm
Salt to taste
Yeast or toddy little
Cashewnuts and Plums as required
Baking Soda a pinch

Method:

  1. Soak the red raw rice for 6 hrs.
  2. Drain the water and grind along with the grated coconut to make it paste.
  3. Add salt to taste and yeast or toddy to ferment it. Keep aside for 6 to 8 hrs.
  4. Add grated jaggery, dices of plums and broken sauted cashewnuts and a pinch of baking soda.
  5. Stream it in moulds of medium size, as for iddlies.
  6. Serve hot.

Note: The yeast has to be dissolved in a luke warm water before adding to the batter which ferments it.
If Red raw rice is not available, Ordinary iddli rice may be substituted. Red rice adds colour to your product to some extent.
You may also add raisins and other dried fruits to make it rich.
