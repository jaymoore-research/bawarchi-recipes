Kheema Matar Fry

Ingredients

Kheema (minced meat)- 500 gms
Fresh Green Peas-1 cup (boiled)
Onions-2 medium (finely chopped)
Tomatoes-2 medium (finely chopped)
Fresh Ginger-garlic paste-2 tspn
Turmeric powder-1 tspn
Red chilli powder-1 tspn
Coriander powder-1 tspn
Garam masala powder-1 tspn
Green Chillies-2-3 (slit)
Oil-1 tbspn
Fresh Coriander leaves or mint leaves (finely chopped)-2 tbspn
Juice of 1 Lemon
Salt-as per taste.
Rings of Onions soaked in vinegar for garnishing

Method:

  1. Clean the meat add half a tspn of turmeric powder, sprinkle little water (2 TBSP), pressure cook for 1 whistle.
  2. Take a pan heat oil on medium flame,add onions fry until brown add ginger-garlic paste fry for a second add tomatoes, powder masalas stir for 1 min, then add meat,Half of the leaves(mint or coriander), salt fry until dry. Add boiled peas, remaining leaves, lemon juice adjust the seasoning.
  3. Serve hot, garnished with onion rings.
