Dosas

Ingredients

2-3 Tomatoes
1-2 Onions
Coriander leaves
Pepper/ Red Chilly powder
Cooking Oil

Method :

  1. First cut tomatoes and onions into small pieces, fry in a small quantity of oil.
  2. Then add pepper and red chilly powder, then salt.
  3. Add this mixture to dosa dough and sprinkle coriander leaves. With this dough mixture we can make crisp and tasty tomato onion dosas.

This method can also used to make chilly, onion dosas using onions and green chillies. So also Caulilflower and onions can be fried and mixed with dough to make Gobi Onion Dosas. It takes away the monotony of regular dosas while adding flavour and taste. Try it out.
