Crispy Pakora

Ingredients

Chana flour (Besan) - 2 Cups
Rice flour - 2 Tb Spoon
Corn flour - 1 Tb Spoon
Green Chillies - 4 to 8 [According to your taste]
Ginger - 2 Tea Spoon
Garlic - 1 Tea Spoon [Optional]
Baking Soda - 1/2 Tea Spoon [Optional]
Onion - 2 Medium size
Cashewnuts - 10 to 15
Ghee - 1 Tb Spoon
Salt - 2 Tea Spoon [According to your taste]
Oil - To deep fry
Coriander leaves, Curry leaves little bit.

Method:

  1. Cut Onion lengthwise.
  2. Cut ginger, garlic, coriander, Curry leaves, Green chillies, Cashews into small pieces.
  3. Mix all of the above ingredients together. Don't add water.
  4. Keep this for 20 minutes. Heat oil.
  5. Make small irregular balls using the dough and put this into the oil.
  6. Deep Fry till golden brown.
  7. Take the Pakora from the oil and put this into a bowl with tissue paper. The tissue paper will absorb the excess oil if any. Enjoy cooking!
