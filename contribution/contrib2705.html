Mushroom Curry

Ingredients

1 packet chopped Mushrooms
2 Onions
2 Tomatoes
Lots of Cinnamon (take atleast 5 -6 nos)
Cloves ( 3-4 nos)
Ginger Paste
Garlic Paste
green peas
Red Chili Powder (2 tsp)
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Heat oil in a pan . Add Onions and cinnamon and fry for a while. Then add chopped tomatoes, ginger , garlic paste and fry for a while.
  2. Add Red Chilli powder and salt and sprinkle some water.
  3. Add the chopped mushrooms and stir it well. Close the lid and let the mushroom cook.
  4. Add Green peas and let it cook along with the mushroom. Keep the lid closed for a while. Once done (Mushroom cooked) garnish with coriander leaves and Serve with Chapathis.
