Instant Chicken Crispy

Ingredients

Boneless Chicken 500 gm.
Ginger Paste 1 tbs
Garlic Paste 1 tbs
Onion Paste 1 tbs
Green Chilli Paste 1 tsp
Red chilli powder 1 tsp
Turmeric powder 1 tsp
Curd 4 tbs
Lemon Juice 1 tbs
Salt to taste
Oil for frying.

Method:

  1. Cut chicken into small pieces.
  2. Mix all the above ingredients & marinate it for 20-30 min's.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and shallow fry it till it turns golden brown. Oooh- lala yummy chicken crispy is ready.
