Adai

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients:

Rice - 1 cup
Urad Dal - 1 Handful
Arhal/Toor Dal - 1 Handful
Chana Dal - 1 Handful
Asafoetida - 1 Pinch
Curry Leaves - 15-20
Pepper(optional)
Salt to taste
Method< P>  

  1. Soak the rice in water for an hour.
  2. Soak the dals for half an hour.
  3. Grind both rice and the dals to make a thick batter. Make sure not to make a very smooth batter.Pepper, is being used, should be added at the time of grinding.
  4. Add asafetida and salt and finely chopped curry leaves.
  5. When ready to cook the adai’s , add little water if required.
  6. Heat a pan, spread a little oil. Take some batter with a serving spoon and spread on the tava like a dosa,
  7. Add half a spoon of oil and turn the adai when its golden brown and crisp.
  8. Cook on the other side similarly and serve hot with Jaggery, Sambhar or Coconut Chutney.  

Tip: Using gingely(til) oil along with the cooking oil will add flavour to the adai.
