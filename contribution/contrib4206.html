Pizza Type Snack

Ingredients

2 Slices of Bread
2 Teaspoon of Pasta Sauce/Pizza Sauce/Tomato Sauce
2 Slices of Swiss Cheese/White Mozzerella Cheese (Preferred)
1/2 Small Capsicum (Bell Pepper)
2 small Mushrooms
1 Small Onion
1 teaspoon of Butter
Red chilli Powder/ Pepper & Salt to taste.

Method:

  1. Bake the Bread, until Brown and Hard. Using Toaster is quicker.
  2. Apply butter to the bread and spread the pasta/pizza/tomato sauce.
  3. Cut thin slices of Capsicum, Mushroom and evenly spread on the bread.
  4. Cut the onions in medium pieces and spread it on the bread. Add salt and Red Chilli powder / pepper to taste.
  5. Now put the Cheese slices over the toppings on the bread.
  6. Broil the bread with toppings at 450 C, 4-5 minutes.....until cheese melts all over the bread. The dish is the ready, add black pepper over the melted cheese to taste.
