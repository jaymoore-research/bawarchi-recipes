Brinjal Chips

Ingredients

2 Big Brinjals (light green color, seedless)
Salt to taste
Red Chili Powder
Rice Flour
Oil for frying
Method :

  1. Cut Brinjal into round pieces (don't cut it very thin. Approx. 6-7 pieces of 1 Brinjal).
  2. Apply Salt and chili powder evenly to all pieces.
  3. Take rice flour (dry) in a plate and deep one piece into it.
  4. Shallow fry it on a tawa till crisp. Don't burn it.
  5. Remove it on a paper napkin.

Serve hot.

You can use Potato instead of Brinjal but cut potato thick

Time : 15 minutes max.
Serves : Two
