Dadape Pohe

Ingredients

1/4 kg Flattened Rice (thin variety)
3 Tomatoes
2 Onions
2 medium Cucumber (Khira)
1 cup grated Coconut (Fresh)
2-3 Green Chillies (as per taste)
1 Raw Mango (optional)
2-3 tbsp Lemon Juice
1 tsp Sugar
Salt to taste.
2-3 tbsp Oil
1 tsp Mustard seeds
Pinch of Hing
1 tsp Turmeric powder

Method:

  1. Finely chop tomatoes, onions, cucumber and green chillies.
  2. Grate the raw mango.
  3. Add all the ingredients to the poha and mix well.
  4. Heat oil, add mustard seeds, hing and turmeric powder. Add to the mixture and mix well.
  5. Keep this in a closed container for a few minutes for best results.
