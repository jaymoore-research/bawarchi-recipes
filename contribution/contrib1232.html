Ingredients

1 cup of Besan (Kadalamaavu)
1 cup of ghee
1 cup of corn oil
2 cups of sugar
1/2 cup of water (to make a syrup)

Method:

  1. In a bowl mix well ghee and corn oil and add besan to it and mix properly breaking all the lumps.
  2. Now make a syrup of sugar by adding 1/2 cup of water to a consistancy of 1 strand (1 Tar).
  3. When it STARTS FORMING 1 TAR immediately put the mixture and keep stirring till you see the web type consistancy and it starts to leave the pan.
  4. Then immediately transfer it to a greased plate and cut after 2 minute and let it cool.
