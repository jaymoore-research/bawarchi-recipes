Green Chilli Pickle

Ingredients

Green Chillies
Mustard Oil 150 ml
Vinegar 1 tbsp
Salt
Turmeric powder 1 tbsp
Mustard powder 2 tbsp
Black Salt 1 tsp
Methi powder 1 tsp
Saunf powder 2 tbsp
Sugar 1 tbsp

Method:

  1. Wash the green chillies and cut it into small pieces of 1 inch or 1 cm.
  2. Then add mustard powder, turmeric powder, methi powder, saunf powder, black salt, vinegar, white salt, sugar and then mix well, at the end add the mustard oil.
  3. Mix well and keep it closed, let the chillies soak well.
  4. In 2-3 days pickle is ready to eat. Keep the pickle in the Refrigerator.
