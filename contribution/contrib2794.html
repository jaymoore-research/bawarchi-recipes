Stuffed Chillies

Ingredients

Long - thick Green Chillies (normally they are less hot) 8-10 nos
Grated fresh Coconut : one half coconut
2 tsp Gram Powder (basan)
1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
1/2 tsp Asafoetida powder
Chilly powder : as required
Salt : as required
1/2 tsp. Cumin seeds
2 tbsp Groundnut Oil

Method :

  1. Wash chillies & wipe-dry them. Do not remove stems of chillies .
  2. Make a cut by knife on one side only, leaving approx. 1 cm portion on each end.
  3. Remove all seeds from each chilly.
  4. Mix grated coconut, turmeric powder , asafoetida , cuminseeds, Salt , Chilly powder , basan together without using water.
  5. Stuff this mixture in the chillies.
  6. Put oil for heating in a fry pan (tawa) & keep stuffed chillies .
  7. Sprinkle little oil on it & cover them & allow to cook on low flame for 20 - 30 minutes.
  8. Serve them with hot Chapati or Rice.
