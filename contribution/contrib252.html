Dal Fry

Ingredients

1 cup of Toor Dal
1 onion cut into long stripes.
1 green chilly chopped.
1 Tomato finely chopped.
1 tsp mixture of mustard seeds, whole coriander seeds, cumin seeds, fenugrek seeds.
1/2 tsp of tumeric
1 tsp chillie powder.
1 tsp of dry mango powder or dry anardana powder.
1/2 inch grated ginger.
2 tbsp of cooking oil.
2 tbsp of fresh chopped coriander.
salt as per taste.

Method

  1. Wash dal thoroughly and add 2 cups of water and cook the dal in a pressure cooker until done.
  2. Cool down cooker,remove dal & beat with spoon or a hand beater.
  3. Heat 2 tbsp of oil in a khadhai or a saucepan. Add tsp mixture of mustard,cumin,fenugreek & whole coriander seeds. When seeds states poping add chopped onion,tomato,and grated ginger and cook it until soft.
  4. Add dry mango powder and tsp of chillie powder and fry for a minute.
  5. Add dal mixture and bring to boil.
  6. Simmer for 5 to 10 minutes.
  7. Take Dal fry in a serving dish and garnish it with chopped fresh coriander.
  8. Serve this Dal fry with Roti,Rice or Pulao.

Enjoy eating and cooking.
