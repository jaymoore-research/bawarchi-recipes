Walnut and Lime Chicken

Ingredients

500 gm Chicken
8 to 10 Walnuts
1/2 tsp Pepper powder
4 tblsp Lime Juice
1/2 tsp Shahjeera
2 tblsp Butter

Method:

  1. Pressure-cook chicken with little salt. Shred the chicken into small bits.
  2. In a pan heat butter, add chicken add pepper and saute well.
  3. Grind the walnuts and add to the chicken. Add salt, pepper powder and lime juice. Mix well. When done, serve hot.
