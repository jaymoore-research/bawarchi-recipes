Supreme Curd Delite

Ingredients

400 gm Curd
1/2 cup Paneer
1/2 cup Cream
2 tbsp Mix Fruit Jam
1 tsp Honey
3 cup mix chopped Fruits (Banana, Grapes, Apple, Orange)
1/4 tsp Lemon Juice
1 tbsp Powred Sugar
Pinch of Nutmeg powder

Method

  1. Hang curd in muslin cloth for 1/2 an hour.
  2. Sprinkle lemon juice on chopped fruits.
  3. Mix hang curd, paneer, cream, jam and honey in mixi. Mix chopped fruits in curd delite chilled in fridge, sprinkle nutmeg powder and serve.
