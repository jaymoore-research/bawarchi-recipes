Kashmiri Damaloo

Ingredients

Medium Sized Potatoes - 5
Deghi Mirch - 2 tsp
Saunf Powder - 2 tsp
Sund powder - 1/2 tsp
Garam Masala - 1/2 tsp
Lavang - 2-3
Badi Elaichi-2
Dalchini -1 small stick
Choti Elaichi-1
Oil for frying
oil for Cooking- 2 serving spoons
Salt to taste
Water - 1 mug

Method:

  1. Boil and peel the potates leave them to cool at room temperature.
  2. Make small holes in boiled potatoes with the help of a fork from all sides.
  3. Heat oil in the kadai and deep fry these boiled potatoes on a low flame so that they get fried inside also.
  4. **Sprinke a hand full of water over these potatoes while in oil only and when they have become red.
  5. Take out potatoes in a dry dish.
  6. Take another pan (with lid) and put oil for cooking. When hot put laung, badi elaichi, dalchini, choti elaichi and let fry till the sweet aroma comes out.
  7. When done put deghi mirch and put a mug of water immediately otherwise the curry would become black. Now put rest of the masalas in this curry and bring to bil for 5 mins.
  8. Put the fried potatoes in this curry and cover the pan with a tight lid. Simmer for 1 hr. and keep on checking in between so that water does not dry.
  9. Replace from heat when the gravy has becomes thick and oil has come up.
  10. Serve with Boiled Rice or Chappatis.
**You will have to be very careful in this step. If you think you cannot do it you can skip this step.

Believe me you will enjoy this treat from the Wazawan (Kitchen of a Kashmiri Cook) of Kashmir.
