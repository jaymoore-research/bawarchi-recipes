Mutton Palak

Ingredients

1 Kilo mutton
1/2 big onion
3 tomatoes
1 1/2 green chillies

Method:

  1. Marinate mutton for 10 mins. Add a sufficient amount of ginger. Add 1 tablespoon curd and to it add yellow powder and salt.
  2. Add in the spinach - {1 bunch} , chilli & yellow powder. Boil with water.
  3. Place marinated mutton over medium heat after 10 mins, mash the spinach. Add salt. Cover. Let the water from the mutton dry, retaining a little. Grate 6 big pods of garlic . Chop onion .Grate tomatoes.
  4. In pressure cooker add 1 {one} table spoon oil, full cumin, onion. Saute till little brown. Add garlic, ginger, salt & tomatoes. Cook over high flame for 5 mins. Add mutton and its water. cover . Mix mutton until stands on oil. Add 2 1/2 big ktories of water after putting palak & mixing it well for 5 mins. 5 whistles.

Hope you like my best home-made recipe.

P.S: And for those who Don't know what "ktories", are they are metal bowls.
