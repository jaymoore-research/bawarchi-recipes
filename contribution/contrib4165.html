Ingredients

Rawa (Sooji) 1 cup
All Purpose Flour 1/2 cup
Sugar 3/4 cup (Depends on your taste)
Egg 1
Riped Banana (optional) 1/2
Cardamom 2
Cooking Soda 1/4 tsp ( pinch)
Oil to Deep Fry

Method:

  1. Beat egg to fluffy.
  2. Now add sugar, rawa, flour, soda, powdered cardamom & mix well.
  3. Mash the banana & add to the mix. The mix should be thick like vada mix, if it is so thick you can add very little water.
  4. Leave the mix to set for atleast 20 minutes.
  5. Now heat the oil in frying pan & drop small balls of mix with a spoon
  6. Fry till it turns gloden brown & drain in a paper towel.
