Sweet and Sour Noodles

Ingredients

Noodles Pack 1
Onions small 1
Green Chillies 2 to 4
Mixed Vegetables (Carrot, Beans, Corn)
Eggs 2
Tomato Souce 1 1/2 tsp
Soya Sauce 1/2 tsp
Chilli Garlic Sauce 1/2 tsp
Oil and little Salt Black Pepper

Method:

  1. Initially boil the noodles and keep them aside until they become cold.
  2. Now in a frying pan add oil and fry the chillies, onions and the mixed vegetables one after the other. When all these are boiled add the 2 eggs by breaking them up. Let this also get cooked. Then add all the sauces and stir.
  3. Add little salt and pepper for taste and then add the noddles. Let the nodles get fried more on low flame so that they become little dry which makes them more tastier. Thats it you are done. You can even avoid the eggs if you donot want them.
