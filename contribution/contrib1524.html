Vegetable Soup (Italian Style)

Ingredients

1 onion, chopped in half rings
1 tomato, chopped
12-13 flowerettes of cauliflower
1 carrot, finely chopped
Peas-1/2 cup
1/2 cup boiled macaroni
Oil
Salt and black pepper as per taste
1/4 tsp ginger garlic paste
A little cornflour mixed in water
Chilli sauce

Method :

  1. Take a cooker/covered vessel and put onion rings and ginger garlic paste to fry.
  2. Meanwhile blanch all the vegetables in salted boiling water for 3-4 mins.
  3. When onion is golden brown put half the parboiled vegetables in it, saute for 2-3 mins, add 3 cups water and then cook this mixture for 10 mins(covered) or if in cooker, put 3 whistles.
  4. If you like your soup of thin consistency you can sieve the mixture in the cooker in a vessel. If you like it thick, blend the cooker mixture in a mixi for 30 secs.
  5. Put the blended/sieved mixture in a pan on the fire. Add salt, pepper
  6. Now add the macaroni and the other half of boiled vegetables to this. Add little water if required.
  7. Simmer on low fire for 5 mins, add chilli sauce droplets on top and stir gently.
  8. 2 mins before serving add the cornflour in water.

You may like to serve Italian style in shallow plates instead of bowls.

** Note : Italian style soup is thick. You can alter the consistency of your soup by adding/reducing water content.
