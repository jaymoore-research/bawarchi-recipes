Elephant Yam Pitlai

Ingredients

1/2 Kg Elephant Yam skinned and cubed
1 cup Toor dal cooked with Termeric powder and Salt
1 tablespoon Tamarind paste dilutted with 1 cup water
Roast, cool and grind to a paste the following with a little water:
3 dry red whole Chilli
1 table spoon Dhania
1 teaspoon Channa dal, Urd dal and Methi seeds each
1 pinch of Hing
Curry leaves

Method:

  1. Boil the elephant yam pieces in water with salt till just done, drain and keep aside.
  2. In a heavy bottomed pan add a little oil and season with mustard and add the suran pieces and fry for a while.
  3. Now add the tamarind water and let it boil properly. Stir in the masala paste, curry leaves, hing and let it boil for a little while on slow fire.
  4. Mash the toor dal well with a ladle and add to the boiling gravy. Adjust seasoning and consistency with salt and water.
  5. Serve hot with plain cooked rice.
