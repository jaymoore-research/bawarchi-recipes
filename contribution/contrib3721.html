Laddu Delight

Ingredients

1. 1/2 kg. Rava (Sooji)
2. 1/2 kg. Sugar
3. 10 cashewnuts
4. 5-6 Elaichi
5. 8-10 Kismis
6. 1 cup Ghee or Dalda.

Method:

  1. Fry the Rava in a frying pan along with 1/2 cup of Ghee or Dalda till you smell a good aroma.
  2. Fry cashews in ghee lightly.
  3. Grind the Rava into a fine powder.
  4. Grind Sugar also into a fine powder.
  5. Mix Rava and Sugar, add cashews, elaichi and kismis. Pour ghee a little at a time and prepare delicious laddus. These are easy to make and tasty to eat.
