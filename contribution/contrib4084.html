Cabbage & Peas Kadi

Ingredients

1/4 kg. Cabbage
200 gms Pea
1/4 cup curds
1 tsp rice
1/4 tsp tur dal
1 cashew-nut
3 green chillies
1/4 cup grated coconut
1 tsp mustard seeds

Method

  1. Boil peas and cabbage. Add salt.
  2. Soak rice, tur dal and cashew in a little water.
  3. Grind coconut, chillies and the soaked items with curds
  4. Pour in the ground mixture in the boiled vegetables. Pour in some water, if too thick.
  5. Boil for 2 minutes.
  6. Season with mustard seeds.
