Vegetable Upma

Ingredients

Roasted Rava 1 cup
All chopped vegetables 1/2 cup like Cabbage, Green Peas (frozen), Beans, Potato, Carrot, Lima Beans (optional), Onion 1/2, Grated Ginger 3 spoons,1 Green Chilli
Lemon Juice,
1 tbs Sugar
Salt
Urad Daal 2tbs (optional)
Chopped Coriander.
3 cup Water

Method:

  1. Heat vessel. Add 4 spoon oil. Add mustard seeds. Once spultters add urad dal, pinch of hing and curry leaves.
  2. Fry onions and add chilli.
  3. Add all vegetables. Fry 2-3 mts. then add water salt and ginger.
  4. Boil well all vegetables until soft. Add lemon juice and sugar
  5. Add rava slowly by stirring. Boil for 2-3 mts.
  6. Add coriander. Cover the lid.
  7. Serve after 5mts with pickles.

You can eat with sevu also. Enjoy.
