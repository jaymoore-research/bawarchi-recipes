Mangalore Bonda

Ingredients

Maida 1 cup
Rice flour 1/4 cup
Rava 2 Tbsps
Thick sour Curd 3/4 cup
Salt to taste
Baking Soda a pinch
Green Chillies finely chopped 1 tbsp
Coriander chopped
Chopped Onions 1 medium

Method:

  1. Mix all the ingredients except baking soda & set aside for 6 hrs closed. Try not to add water at all.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Mix baking soda to the mixture, take small balls & fry this in oil till golden brown.
  3. Serve hot with chutney.

P.S: U can add coconut pieces thinly cut too if u like it. Very tasty & very easy to make esp. for parties. Doesn't take much oil either.
