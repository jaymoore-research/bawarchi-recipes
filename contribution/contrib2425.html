Tasty Butter Murukku

Ingredients

Rice flour - 2 cups
Urad dal - 1/2 cup
Sesame seeds - 1 tsp
Hing - a pinch
Cumin seeds - 1 tsp
Unsalted butter - 3 tsp
Salt - to taste
Refined oil - required quantity.

Method

  1. In a heavy bottomed pan, fry Urad dal till light brown & let it cool. Grind it into a fine powder.
  2. To this add the rice flour & the rest of the ingredients except butter.
  3. Melt the butter & add it to the dry ingredients. Mix it well & mould it into a dough (Make sure it has the right consistency to pass through the murukku presser)
  4. With the help of a murukku presser/mould, fry it in a deep pan of heated oil, until they are golden brown in colour.
  5. Drain them in a paper towel to remove excess oil, let them cool. Store in an air tight container.

Enjoy crispy & tasty homemade butter murukku. Everyone at your home will love it.

Note: The murukku presser can be coated with oil on the inside for easy pressing.
