Mooli-Gajar Paratha

Ingredients

One big Raddish peeled & grated.
Two medium Carrots peeled & grated.
1 small piece of Ginger grated.
1/2 tsp Ajwain
1/2 tsp Black Pepper
1/2 tsp Red Chilli powder
1 tsp Green Chilli finely chopped.
2 tbsp.finely chopped Green Coriander Leaves.
Salt to taste
Oil for frying.

For dough:

Take whole Wheat Flour & water & knead it as we knead for normal roti.

Method :

  1. Take grated raddish in your hand, small quantity at a time and press it with your palms to remove all water to make it dry. Do the same with remaining raddish & with carrots too.
  2. Now mix raddish, carrot, ajwain, black pepper, red chilli powder, green chilli & coriander. Don't mix salt.
  3. Make a small roll from the dough & flatten it in a way so that center of it will be little thick.
  4. Take 2 tbsp. of mixture in separate plate & mix a pinch of salt to it & promptly stuff it in that flattened roll.
  5. Flatten it with very light hand rolling & put it on hot tawa. Keep it on medium heat so that it can cook well. Turn it after 5 seconds. Cook it little from both sides, then put oil on tawa and fry it both sides till golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with Curds & Butter.
