Email: ravipras@ix.netcom.com

Gojjavalakki

Ingredients

Poha or beaten rice or avval - 1 cup ground to crystals
Rasam powder - 2 Tsp
Tamarind paste - 1 1/2 Tsp
A pinch of Sugar
A strand of curry leaves
Chana dal and Urad dal - 1 Tsp
Groundnuts or Peanuts - 1 Tsp
Mustard and Jeera seeds - 1 Tsp
A pinch of turmeric
Salt to taste

Method :

  1. Mix the Tamarind paste, rasam powder, salt and sugar well into a paste, add a little water sufficient enough to soak the poha soft.
  2. Now, heat oil in a Kadai, add Mustard and jeera seeds, allow to splutter, add Chana dal, urad dal and peanuts, When they turn golden brown, add Curry leaves and a pinch of turmeric.
  3. Now, add the poha and mix well. Its ready to be eaten. One good thing about this dish is that it tastes much better when it is not hot. So, u can eat it anytime.
