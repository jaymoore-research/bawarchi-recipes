Mixture

Ingredients

1.5 Cups Channa besan
1 Cup Poha
1 Cup Peanuts
1 Cup Split Dalia
1/4 Cup Cashew
3 Cup Oil (To fry all items)
Curry leaves
1 Table spoon Chilli Powder
Salt to taste
1 Table spoon Sugar

Method:-

  1. Take 1 cup besan and make a thick paste.
  2. Heat oil in a frying pan, when it is hot, press the paste using a presser with small round openings. (will come out like noodles/sevai). Fry it until it becomes crispy. Keep it in a bowl lined with newspaper or paper towel.
  3. Fry Poha (just put it in hot oil and take out immediately).
  4. Fry peanuts, split dalia, Cashew and Curry leaves separetly. Keep all in a bowl lined with paper towel to drain extra oil.
  5. Make a loose paste of remaining besan. Put it in Jali and make boondi as we make for ladu. Drain the oil.
  6. Mix all them in a big bowl. Add salt, sugar and chilli powder, mix well.

You can add Badam, fried Carrot or Potato. It is a good teatime snack.
