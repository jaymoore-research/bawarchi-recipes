Ingredients

1 large size cucumber
3 cups butter milk (sour butter milk will be good)
1tbs mustard for seasoning

Ingredients For Grinding:

1 cup grated coconut
4 green chilies
2 tbs. zeera
1-tbs. chenna dal soaked in water
1tbs rice soaked in water
Salt according to taste.

Method :

  1. Peel the skin of cucumber and cut into small pieces
  2. Boil the cucumber with little salt
  3. In a grinder grind all the ingredients given under ingredients for grinding
  4. For grinding use buttermilk instead of water
  5. After grinding mix this paste to a cup containing buttermilk.
  6. Once the cucumber is boiled add this grind mixture along with buttermilk.
  7. Bring to boil.
  8. Then put chopped coriander.
  9. This one goes very well with rice.

Hope you enjoy making it.
