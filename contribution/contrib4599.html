Ridge Gourd Gojju

Ingredients

Ridge gourd 1-2
Red Chilles 3 - 4
Coriander seeds 2 tbs
Tamarind small piece
Turmeric a pinch
Salt to taste
Curry leaves few
Urad dal 1 tbsp
Mustard 1 tbsp
Oil 2 tbsp

Method:

  1. Mix all the ingredients except the last three and cook with little water until the vegetable is soft.
  2. Blend in the mixer to a smooth paste with coconut (2 tbsp) using very little water.
  3. Give tadka with urad dal and mustard.
