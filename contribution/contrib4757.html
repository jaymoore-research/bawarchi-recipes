Butter Dhania Chicken

Ingredients

1 Kg Chicken (cut into small pieces).
1 bunch Coriander leaves,
2 Onions,
8-10 Green Chillies,
Cumin powder 2 tsp,
Ginger-Garlic paste 2 tbsp,
4 tsp Curd,
Salt as per taste.,
Sugar 1/2 tsp,
Garam Masla crushed 1 tsp.

Method:

  1. Marinate chicken in ginger garlic paste as well 2 tsp. curd. Grind coriander leaves and chillies. Now put half (ground paste) in chicken and half keep aside. Now put black pepper in this and turmeric powder, salt and cumin powder. Leave it about an hr.
  2. Now chop the onions. Take karahi and add butter, put crushed garam masla. Put sugar & onions also. Now fry the chicken along with all marination. Now put rest of the masla's. Add rest curd and now keep frying off and on.
  3. Now don't add water. Because it always leaves water itself. Now see when all chicken is done.
  4. Garnish with little leaves again.
  5. Serve hot with plain rice.
