Fat Free Macaroni Pasta

Ingredients

Macaroni - 2 cups
Mixed Vegetables - 1 cup (preferably beans, carrots, spring onions, broccoli)
Oregano - 1 tsp ( you can take powdered or leaves)
Salt as desired
Chilly Powder - 1/4 tsp
Oil - 1 tsp
Grated Mozzarella Cheese or any other grated cheeseĀ- 1 cup

Method

  1. Take 5 cups of water and boil macaroni.Ā
  2. Add little salt, oil and broccoli in the water.
  3. After the macaroni becomesĀlarge and soft, drain all the water.
  4. In the same vessel put all the vegetables with the oregano, chilly powder,Āsalt and fry till they are cooked.
  5. You can sprinkle some water if needed.
  6. Add the boiled macaroni and broccoli in the dish and stirĀtill they are mixed properly.
  7. While serving garnish with mozzarella cheese.Ā

Note: Take a vessel which is more like a kadai. It will be easy for stirring and it will not stick. After garnishing with mozzarella cheese, heat the macaroni in the microwaveĀoven for a minute,Āit will taste very good, as the cheese would have melted.
