Vegetable Chicken Fry

Ingredients

Chicken- 250 gms
Vegetables (Carrot, Beans, Green Peas, Potato)-chopped-1 cup
Whole Spices-2 gms
Salt to taste
Butter
Garam Masala-1/2 tsp., Chilli powder-1/2 tsp

Method:

  1. Boil chicken with whole spices and salt.
  2. Heat butter and add vegetables. Cover and cook with salt. Add chicken after it is done. Add chilli powder and garam masala. Cover and simmer this for about 15-20 minutes.
  3. Serve hot when done.
