Raw Banana Podimas

Ingredients

4 Raw bananas for cooking (which you get in American Grocery Stores too)
3 small green chillies
Kari leaves
1 teaspoon Broken Urad Dal
1/2 teaspoon Jeera
1/2 teaspoon Mustard
Oil to season (Coconut oil, if you like)
Salt to taste
Aesofoetida

Method:

  1. Cut the bananas into half and boil them just for five minutes in high flame. If cooked too much, you may not be able to grate it.
  2. Remove from fire and cool them. About 15 minutes.
  3. Remove the skin. Grate the banana, mix salt and keep aside.
  4. In a frying pan, add oil and season urad dal, jeera and mustard. When mustard pops, add chopped green chillies, kari leaves and aesofoetida. During this process, keep the flame in medium to low medium.
  5. Add the grated banana. Mix with the seasoning. Swith the flame off.
  6. Add grated coconut. Mix once more. Add lemon juice. Mix again.
  7. Curry is ready, serve with Sambhar rice, Rasam Rice or simply with curd.

Note: Take care not to mix the grated banana vigorously, to avoid sticking.
