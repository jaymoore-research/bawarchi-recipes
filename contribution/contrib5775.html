Tuna Stir-Fry

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients

1 can or packet of Tuna (in oil)
1 bag frozen stir-fry vegetables (thawed)
1 tsp Ginger Paste
1 tsp Garlic Paste
1 tbsp vegetable oil
2 Slit Green Chilies
1 tsp Hot Chili Paste (optional)
1/2 cup Stir-fry Sauce (any brand)
1/4 cup Chopped Coriander Leaves
1/4 cup Chopped Scallions  (green onion)
1 tsp Pepper
Salt to taste

Method

  1. Heat oil in a wok or a large kadai.
  2. Add ginger-garlic pastes and the  green chilies and sauté for 1 min, add the vegetables and sauté for 2-3 minutes more.
  3. Add chili paste, stir-fry sauce, pepper and tuna, mix thoroughly.
  4. Check taste and if needed add salt.
  5. Cook for 2 minutes.
  6. Remove from fire and garnish with coriander and scallions.
  7. Serve hot with rice.
  8. Tuna can be replaced with 1 cup cooked shrimp or 1 cup cooked-shredded chicken. If you are a vegetarian then just skip tuna.
