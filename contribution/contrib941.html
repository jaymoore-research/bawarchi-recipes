Palak Kabab

Ingredients

Palak 1 bunch
1/4 kg chana dal
2 small onions chopped
Few green chillies chopped
Coriander leaves chopped
Salt to taste
Bread crumbs.

Method :

  1. Soak channa dal for 2-3 hours, grind it and keep aside.
  2. Clean and chop palak leaves and cook in little oil till dry.
  3. Mix the dal paste and cooked palak.
  4. Add salt, green chillies, onion, coriander leaves. Form into cutlets, roll in bread crumbs and deep fry.
