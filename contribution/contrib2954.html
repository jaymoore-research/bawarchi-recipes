Stir-Fry Tofu

Ingredients

2 big cubes of firm tofu - cut in into small one inch cubes
1 1/2 tbsp. of corn flour mixed in 1/2 cup of water
3-4 green chillies - finely chopped
4 tbsp of dark soya sauce
2 inch ginger - finely chopped
3 cloves garlic - finely chopped
3 table spoon of Vegetable Oil/ Seasame oil
2 stalk of green onions - chopped
Salt to taste.

Method:

  1. Drain the tofu from the package. Soak it into salted water for 2-3 hrs.
  2. Heat oil in a non-stick pan/ wok. Add garlic and ginger and stir fry on high flame for 30 seconds.
  3. Add green chillies and stir fry for another 10 sec. Reduce the flame.
  4. Add the soya sauce and corn flour mix and stir continously. As the sauce starts to thicken add tofu. Boil it for 10 min on very slow flame.
  5. Transfer tofu into a serving dish and sprinkle it with green onions.
  6. Enjoy it with white steamed rice.
