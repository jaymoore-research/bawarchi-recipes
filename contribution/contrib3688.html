Bread Thayir Vada

Ingredients

Bread slices-6
Thick Curd - 2 cups
Urad Dal -1/3 cup
Red Chilli Powder and Salt according to taste.
A handful of cut Green Coriander.
Oil for deep frying.

Method:

  1. Soak the urad dal in water for an hour.
  2. Grind the dal to a paste as you do for urad vada (i) Add the required salt to the above paste and mix thoroughly.
  3. Remove the sides of the bread (that is the bread rind) and then soak the bread in small amount of water. Squeeze the extra water.
  4. Add chilli powder, required salt (if you use salt bread then do not add salt) and mash it. Now make balls from the above soaked and squeezed bread. (If you squeeze the water completely then it will be difficult to make balls) Dip each bread ball in the urad dal paste so that it is completly coated with that and deep fry the balls to golden brown colour. Put it on a plate.
  5. Now beat the curd without adding water. Pour the beaten curd over the Bread vadai. Garnish with cut corriander. This goes well with a hot and sweet chutney.
