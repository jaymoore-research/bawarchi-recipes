Rava Dosa

Ingredients

3 cups suji(rava)
2 cups maida (all purpose flour)
1 cup rice flour
sour butter milk
green chilies
coriander
salt
onions

Method:

  1. Mix suji, maida, rice flour with butter milk. (make into a dough)
  2. Cut onions, green chillies, corriander into small pieces and add to the dough
  3. Add salt, and keep the dough to side for an half hour.
  4. Make dosa on the tawa adding a little of oil or ghee.

Serve hot with corriander chutney and enjoy.
