Vegetable Pasta (Italian Dish)

Ingredients

Pasta-1 cup (any size of ur choice)
onion-1/2
tomato-1 small size
bell pepper-1/2
pasta sauce-6-8tsp
olive oil-5tsp
salt to taste

Method

  1. Cook pasta in a pan with water by adding 1tsp of oil.
  2. After pasta is fully cooked, if there is excess water remove it.
  3. Keep the pasta aside.
  4. Cut onion, tomato & bell peper into thin slices.
  5. Each slice must be cut into 2 inch so that it doesn't look odd.
  6. In a pan add olive oil. Heat it add onion fry till it is looking like glass,add tomato fry for 2 min,then add bell pepper fry for few min.
  7. Then put pasta & add salt mix together.
  8. Keep the flame in low ,then add pasta sauce. Mix everything together, keep in heat for 30sec.
  9. Serve hot.
  10. If u wish u can add chicken peices which is cooked seperatly. This can be added after bell pepper.

Enjoy ur dinner
