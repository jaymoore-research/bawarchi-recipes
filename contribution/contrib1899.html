Nutritious Gawar

Ingredients

1/2 kg gawar, cut in to small pieces
1 medium sized onion
1 small bunch of coriander leaves
4 tsp pomegranate seeds(Anardhana)
2 tsp coriander powder
3-4 green chillies
1 tsp ginger paste
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
10 flakes garlic
Pinch of garam masala powder
Salt to taste
Cooking oil

For garnishing:
A handful of cashewnuts, fried.

Method:

  1. Boil the gawar under pressure up to one whistle and kkep it aside.
  2. Grind together the onion, green chillies, coriander leaves, garlic, ginger, and anardhana to a fine paste.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and add the ground paste to it fry for five to seven minutes.
  4. Add the boiled gawar and the dry masalas to it.
  5. Keep stirring till the oil floats on top.
  6. Add 1/4 cup water and cook under pressure on a low heat for another five minutes.
  7. Garnish with cashewnuts.

Serve hot with chappatis/phulkas
