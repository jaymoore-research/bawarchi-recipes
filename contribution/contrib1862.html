Tomato Curry

Ingredients

tomato 1 (large, chopped)
onion 1/2 (chopped)
greenchilli 4 (split)
ginger 1/2 tsp (chopped)
coriander powder 1 tsp
turmeric powder 1/2 tsp
salt (to taste)

For grinding:-

coconut 2 cup (grated)
jeera 1/2 tsp
garlic 2 cloves
For seasoning:-

oil 1 tsp
mustard 1/2 tsp.
redchilli 4
curryleaves

Method:

  1. Cook tomato, onion, ginger, greenchilli with coriander pwd., turmeric pwd. in one cup of water.
  2. Grind coconut, jeera, garlic thoroughly
  3. Heat oil in a pan add mustard seeds allow to splutter, add red chilli , curry leaves and add the grinded mixture into it. Heat little.
  4. Add the seasoned mixture into the cooked tomato.
  5. Add salt and gently boil it for 2 minutes.

(This dish goes well with rice )
