Chicken Roast

Ingredients

Chicken cut into small pieces 1kg
Onion (big)finely sliced 3 nos
Garlic 5 - 6 cloves
Tomato sliced 3
Ginger cut into small pieces 1 - 1 1/2 inch
Chilli powder 2 teaspoons
Coriander powder 2 - 2 1/2 teaspoons
Turmeric powder 1/2 teaspoon
Chicken Masala Powder 2 - 2 1/2 teaspoon
Tomato sauce 1 teaspoon
Green Chillies (slit) 3 - 4
Salt to taste
Oil enough for cooking
Curry leaves

Method:

  1. Heat the oil and fry onions until it becomes slightly brownish.
  2. Add tomatoes, garlic, ginger, green chillies and currry leaves and saute it.
  3. Add chilli powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, chicken masala and saute this also.
  4. Now add the chicken pieces into this, add salt and stir fry it properly. After 5 minutes pour little water and stir it. Whenever its necessary pour water little by little.
  5. Before finshing off the cooking pour tomato sauce and stir.

Now the chicken roast is ready. You can have with rice. It tastes great!! Try it.

Note: Please do not pour too much of water. You can get chicken masala powder from any of the Indian Stores. it increases the taste!!
