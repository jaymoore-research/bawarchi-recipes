Mango Pie

Ingredients

1gm - Graham craker crust or Pie crust
1/2 can - mango pulp.
3 tsp - sour cream.
1/2 cup - sugar.
1 small packet or sashe - unflavoured gelatine.

Method:

  1. Take 1 cup of hot water and mix the gelatine in the hot water until it dissolves then keep it aside. Now mix all the ingrediants, sour cream, mango pulp, sugar and finally add the gelatine water and blend it nicely with the hand blender.
  2. Pour the blended liquid into the pie crust and put it in the freezer section for 1hr until it's done and then take it out and keep it in the refridgerator for few minutes.
  3. Then take out the mango pie crust from the refridgerator and make it into slices and u are ready to serve your desert .

Enjoy the desert.
