Coconut Crispies

Ingredients

500 gm maida
2 tea spoons chilli powder
sufficient salt
1/2 fresh coconut.

Method:

  1. First grate fresh coconut, sieve maida, mix coconut chilli powder, salt and maida together, then add grated coconut and mix it and keep it aside for one hour.
  2. Then mix the dough well, if necessary add very little water, it should be as hard as poori dough,then pour oil in kadai, after the oil is heated then reduce the flame, make small balls with maida dough and pat them on a platic cover like small puri's and deep fry in oil till golden brown in colour, store them in a air tight container.
