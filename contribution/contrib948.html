Dum Ka Chicken

Ingredients

1 chicken (cut into 8 pieces)
150 gms yoghurt
8 nos Almonds
1 tbsp Coconut (grated)
3 nos Onions
1 tbsp garam masala powder
2 tsp Red chilli powder
1 tsp Turmeric
Salt to taste
1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic paste
1/2 tsp Black pepper
1/2 tsp Nahari powder
1/2 tsp Ajinamoto
1 pinch Saffron colour
Few strands of saffron
Lemon (according to ur taste)
Fresh finely chopped coriander
3-4 tbsps Oil

Method :

  1. Wash the chicken pieces and drain the water.
  2. Fry the onions until brown, remove from oil and spread on a plate. Crush it with ur hand after it cools.
  3. Roast almonds and coconut and grind to a fine paste.
  4. Prepare a marinade of the given ingredients (except oil).
  5. Mix all these with the chicken pieces.
  6. Marinate for about 1/2 an hour.
  7. In a vessel put this mixture and the oil. Cook on medium till the chicken is done.
