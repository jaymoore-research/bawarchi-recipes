Ribbon Pakoda

Ingredients

3 cups Besan
1 cup Rice Flour
4 tsp Margarine or Butter
Salt to taste
1 tsp Chilli powder
1 tsp Hing powder (asafoetida)
Oil for frying

Method:

  1. In a mixing bowl, add the besan, rice flour, chilli powder, margarine and the salt. Add water little by little to make a medium consistency dough.
  2. Take the ribbon pakoda press and fill it up.
  3. Heat the oil and when it is hot, press the dough onto the hot oil. Fry it till it is light golden in color. Remove from the oil when the hissing sound ceases.
  4. Remove, and drain it on to a kichen paper towel.
  5. Store it in a dry airtight container.
