Idli Pakoda

Ingredients

1 cup leftover ldli batter
1/4 cup Rice flour
2 Green Chillies (chopped)
1/2 tsp Ginger Garlic paste
Little salt
Oil to deep fry
One Small Onion (chopped nicely)

Method:

  1. Mix well all the above ingredients.
  2. Allow the oil to heat enough. Take the batter with hand and drop in the oil like small pakodas.
  3. Deep fry them and serve hot with sauce.
