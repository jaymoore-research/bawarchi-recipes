Bharli Mirchi

Ingredients

Mirchi (Green peppers)- those which are long and big- 3
Besan- 1 cup
cilantro
salt
oil- 4 teaspoons

Method:

  1. Cut the mirchi such that we can fill in the masala.
  2. Keep it aside.
  3. Take besan, add salt to taste, add chopped cilantro and 2 teaspoons oil.
  4. Stuff the mirchi with the above masala.
  5. Put the stuffed mirchis in a frying pan and add just a little oil from all the sides of mirchi.
  6. Cover it, cook it on sim till done.
  7. Check frequently to see that the besan is getting cooked.
  8. Make a little bit crispy, will taste good, enjoy!
