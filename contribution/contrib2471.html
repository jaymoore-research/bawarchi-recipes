Quick Chicken Curry

Ingredients

300 grams of Boneless Chicken.
1/2 tea spoon of Turmeric
1 table spoon of Chilli Powder
125 grams of Curd
125 grams of Tomato Puree
7 pieces of Cloves
Salt to taste

Method

  1. Cut the boneless chicken in to small small pieces and keep it in a bowl.
  2. Put it on a frying pan with oil, first put the chilli powder plus turmeric powder and mix it well with the frying oil. Then put the curd again mix it well with frying chilli powder.
  3. Then boneless chicken (cut into pieces) has to be placed to the chilli powder plus curd mix and stir the contents very well so that it will get mixed. If you think the mixing and the cooking of the boneless chicken is good then put the tomato puree and mix it well.
  4. Then please add 150 ml of hot water plus the 7 pieces of cloves and mix the contents very well and Leave it for 10 to 15 minutes.
  5. Now I can say the Quick Chicken Curry (QCC) is ready. This can be served with Basmathi Rice.
