Pudding In Pie

Ingredients

One box of honey graham crackers (use only about 24 pieces of it)
One large size Chocolate Pudding mix
8 oz frozen Coolwhip
Few pinches of ground Cinnamon powder

Method

  1. Make pudding according the direction on the box. keep aside.
  2. In a 11 x 7 inch glass baking pan, arrange crackers to make layer (about 12 crackers)
  3. Then spread 1/2 amount of pudding mixture on the top of the cracker layer.
  4. Smooth the coolwhip mixing by spoon. And spread 1/2 amounts of coolwhip on the pudding layer.
  5. Sprinkle some cinnamon powder on it.
  6. To make second layer, arrange cracker top of the coolwhip layer.
  7. Then repeat pudding mixture first, then coolwhip, then cinnamon.
  8. Cover the pan with aluminum foil and keep in the refrigerator for 4-5 hours until cool and set.
  9. Cut into big pieces and serve in a dish.
