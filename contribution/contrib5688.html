Easy Bread Pizza

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients

4 slices of Plain White Bread
8 tblsp black sliced olives
1 small onion chopped finely
1 cup shredded cheese(Mexican four cheese mix will be good)
4 tblsp finely cut Bell pepper
Salt and Pepper to taste

Method:

  1. Butter the bread slices lightly and layer with 2 tblsp of olives, 1 tblspĀ of Bell Pepper and 2 tblsp of onion andĀ top with cheese.
  2. Sprinkle salt and pepper to taste
  3. In a preheated toaster oven (400 C) place the bread slices and leave itĀuntil the cheese has completely melted and starts to brown.
  4. Serve immediately as an evening snack.
