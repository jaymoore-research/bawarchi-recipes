Cat Fish Curry

Ingredients

1 pound 0.5 inch width cut Cat Fish
1 big yellow Onion finely chopped
0.5 x 0.5 Ginger
3 big flakes of Garlic
3 medium size red Tomatoes finely chopped
4 Tablespoon Plain Yoghurt
1 Teaspoon Turmeric Powder
3 Teaspoon Coriander Powder
3-5 Stems of Coriander Leaves
5 Green Chillies (As per your taste to hot food)
1 Teaspoon Red Chilli Powder
Salt as per the taste
Oil

Method:

  1. Marinade cut Cat Fish pieces in yoghurt, 1 teaspoon of turmeric powder, 1 teaspoon of red chilli powder and salt as per your taste for 30 minutes.
  2. Make a paste of garlic, ginger and coriander powder in a blender.
  3. Make a paste of tomatoes, green chillies, coriander leaves and water.
  4. In a karahi fry onions in 2 tablespoon of oil till onion turns light brown colour.
  5. Put the contents of blender having garlic, ginger and coriander paste in the karahi with fried onion. Fry this for about 10 minutes. Make sure that this mixture do not get burnt.
  6. Add blended tomato, green chilli and coriander into this karahi.
  7. Leave this karahi in a simmer heat for about 10 minutes. If you want you can add little more salt as per the taste.
  8. Add marinated fish into this karahi. Put this on flame for about 30 minutes in a simmer heat. Add water if the gravy dries down. Make sure the fish pieces do not break while you stirr it from time to time.
  9. Serve hot with Rice and Cucumber Raita.

I myself have tried this recipe many times at home. This is really tasty.
