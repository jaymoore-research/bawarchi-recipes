Sago Thalipeth

Ingredients

Soaked Sago (sabudana) - 1 cup
Boiled potatoes
Green chillies finely chopped
Curry leaves -- 5-6 finely chopped
Cilantro finely chopped
Jeera powder - 1/4 tea spoon
Oil (optional)
Salt to taste

Method

  1. Mash the potatoes to a fine paste (dough) and no lumpswhile adding all the above ingredients except oil to it.
  2. The consistency of the dough should be like that of chapati flour.
  3. Once this dough is ready, take some thick plastic cover and put some oil on it so that the dough doesn't stick to it.
  4. Take small amounts of dough (preferably size of a big lemon) and pat it on the plastic cover.
  5. Till it becomes the size of some small chapati. Turn it upside down on your palm and put it on hot tava.
  6. Put some oil on it and cook the thalipeth till done or golden brown on medium heat.
  7. Your sabudana thalipeth is now ready!
  8. Serve it hot with ketchup or a chutney of your choice or just eat it as it is!

Variations: You can add finely chopped or grated vegetables like tomato, onion, cucumber etc to this to make it more tasty.

Enjoy and send me your feedback.
