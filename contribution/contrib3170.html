Mixed Vegetable Curry

Ingredients

Potato - 2
Green Peas - handful
Carrot - 2
Beet root (small) - 1
Onion (Big) - 1
Green Chillies - 4
Coconut milk
Curry leaves
Garam Masala
Oil - 2 tb sp
Mustard
Cumin
Salt

Method:

  1. Cut the onions, potatos, carrot, beet root, green chillies and keep it aside.
  2. In the pan add the oil, mustard and cumin, let it crackle. Then add the curry leaves and cut green chillies.
  3. Add the cut onions and saute. When it becomes golden color, add the all the cut vegetables and peas, saute for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add water to this, cover and cook.
  5. When the vegetables are cooked, add the garam masala and coconut milk, stir it. (Do not let it boil after adding the coconut milk)

Time : 15 minutes
