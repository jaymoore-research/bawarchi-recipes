Spicy Red Chilli Chicken

Ingredients

Chicken- 1/2 Kg
Whole Red chilli- 5-6 nos
Garlic paste - 1 teaspoon
Lime juice- 1 teaspoon
Turmeric powder - 1/2 teaspoon
Salt as per the taste
Oil or Ghee- 1 teaspoon
Paparika chilli powder and Kashmirichilli Powder - 1/2 tbspn each

Method:

  1. Cut the chicken into medium size. Wash it prick the chicken with knife.
  2. To marinate the chicken add lime juice, turmeric powder, garlic paste, salt, paprika & kashmiri chilli powder, break the whole red chillies into small bits by hand. Mix well. Let it marinate for 3-4 hrs.
  3. Then take deep frying pan add oil or ghee to it when it is heated add the chicken fry it in medium slowly until it is golden brown on all sides.

Your spicy red chicken is ready to be eaten hot. Enjoy it!
