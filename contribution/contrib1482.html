Upma

Ingredients

Dalia (available in Indian grocery stores) 1 cup

Seasoning :

Channa dal 1 tbsp.
Urad dal 1 tbsp.
Mustard seeds 1 tsp.
Cummin seeds 1 tsp.

Vegetables:

Onion (chopped) 1/4 cup.
Beans (finely chopped) 1/4 cup.
Carrots (chopped) 1/4 cup
Peas (matar fresh or frozen) 1/4 cup.
Potato (chopped) 1/4 cup.
Green Chillies (chopped) according to taste
Ginger (chopped) according to taste
Cauliflower (small pieces) 1/4 cup
Salt to taste
Cooking Oil 2 tbsp.
Curry Leaves one or two stems
Corriander Leaves can be used instead of curry leaves according to availability and taste.

Method :

  1. Add little water to dalia to wet it and boil in the cooker (this makes it easier because in the open pan it takes a long time) Can boil it the day before and keep in the refrigerator.
  2. Take two tbsp of cooking oil, in a pan and put the seasoning in hot oil.
  3. Once it starts spluttering (mustard and cummin) add onions and fry them till translucent. Add all the other vegetables and cook for some time (2 mins). Place a cover and cook till vegetables are tender.
  4. Dalia after boiling becomes like cooked rice. Mash it with hand and put in the pan with the the vegetables. Add salt, curry leaves, green chillies and ginger and mix. Cover the pan and cook on medium heat for 3 to 5 mins.

Serve hot. Best when had fresh.
