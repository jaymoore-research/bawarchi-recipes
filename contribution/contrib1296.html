Maddur Vada and Rava Kodabale

Ingredients

Rava - 1 Cup
Cake flour - 1 Cup
Rice flour - 1 Cup
Onion - 3 Cups (Fine Chopped)
Green Chilli - 6 Nos (you may add more to your taste)
Corinder & Curry leaves - Fine Chopped
Salt to taste
Oil to fry

Method:

  1. Mix all ingredients together add little water if required, form like a Chapati dough.
  2. Take a small quantity and roll it in a rolling pin like small puries, fry till golden brown.
  3. Maddur Vada is ready to eat. Store in a air tight container, eat any time & Enjoy.

Rava Kodabale (Karnataka Fame):

Ingredients:

Rava - 1 Cup
Cake Flour - 1 Cup
Green Chilli - as required to taste
Coriender - half Cup chopped
Soda - half tea spoon
Ghee - four tea spoon
Oil to fry

Method:

  1. Heat up the Ghee and mix with all ingredients together with water to form like chapati daugh.
  2. Take small quantity of dough, roll the dough to a little finger thick rope, join the ends to form like a circle.
  3. Deep fry in a low heat till golden brown, Kadabale is ready to eat.
  4. Store in a air tight container. It can remain unspoiled for many days. Eat any time and enjoy.
