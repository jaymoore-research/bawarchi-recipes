Carrot Paniyaram

Ingredients

Rice : 1 1/2 cups
Sago : 1/2 cup
Green Chillies : 7
Curd : 1 cup
Carrot : 1 (medium)
Onion : 1 (medium)
Coriander leaves, Mint leaves : 2 Tbl.sp
Mustard seeds : 2 tsp
Urad dhal : 2 tsp
Salt
Oil - for frying

Method :

  1. Soak the rice for 2 to 3 hrs.
  2. Grind till raw consistency along with Green chillies and salt.
  3. Add sago and curd with the above and mix it well.
  4. Allow it to ferment.
  5. Chop the onions into small pieces.
  6. Scrape the carrots and chop the Green Chllies.
  7. Heat oil add Mustard seeds, Urad dhal (White).
  8. Add Onions and Green Chillies and fry till onion becomes transculant.
  9. Add Scraped carrots and fry till raw smell goes.
  10. Mix with the fermented mixture and mix well.
  11. Heat Paniyaram pan and pour.
