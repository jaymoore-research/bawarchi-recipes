Corn Curry

Cooking time: approx 45 min, prep time: 10 mins.

Ingredients

5 roma tomatoes diced
1 medium size onion chopped finely
1 can tomato puree/sauce
2 cans corn
2 potatoes, peeled and diced
ginger-garlic paste/ or freshly grated
1 green chilli
1 can coconut milk
small qty: cumin seeds, cardamom, cinnamon stick, clove
(you could dry grind these spices to make a powder)
2 tsb oil
1/4 tsp turmeric powder
salt to taste

Method of Preparation:

  1. In a deep pan, add the oil, and add all the dry spices, garlic, ginger, green chilli and onions.
  2. Sautee till golden brown.
  3. Add the tomatoes, and turmeric powder.
  4. Cover and cook till soft (about 3 mins)
  5. Add 1/2 can of coconut milk and the can of tomato puree/sauce and the potatoes along with some water.
  6. Cover and cook for about 15 mins.
  7. Add the canned corn along with some of the water in the can.
  8. Add salt to taste and the rest of the coconut milk.
  9. Let it simmer for another 10 mins and take it off the heat.
  10. Garnish with some chopped coriander.

Serve with pulao or onion parathas!
