Carrot Kurma

Ingredients

1. Carrot - 1/2 kg
2. Tomato - 2 or 3
3. Grated coconut(dry)-1/2 cup
4. Green chillies - 5
5. Ginger - 1/2" piece
6. Garlic - 5 cloves
7. Onion - 3
8. Cardamom - 2
9. Cloves - 2
10. Turmeric Powder - 1/4 tsp
11. Salt to taste
12. Oil - 1 tsp

Method:

  1. Grind coconut,chillies,ginger and garlic together.
  2. Cut carrot into small pieces.
  3. Boil it and keep aside.
  4. Heat oil in a pan
  5. Add masalas and onion and fry it.
  6. Then add tomatoes and fry for a minute.
  7. To this,add boiled carrot and grinded masala(mixture)
  8. Boil it till it becomes gravy
  9. Serve hot with poori or chapati
