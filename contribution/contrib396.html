Yoghurt Dip

Ingredients

1 cup Yoghurt
2 tbsp tahini (sesame seed paste) which you will find in any Lebanese or Middle eastern food store.
2 tbsp lemon or lime juice
1 clove garlic minced
1/4 tsp ground cumin, salt little bit (optional)
choppped parsley or coriander leaves (optional)

Method

  1. Combine yoghurt, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, ground cumin, salt and coriander.
  2. Mix well serve in deep dish bowl with pita chips.
  3. You can serve this as a vegetable deep with salad platter.

Cooking time: 10 minutes.
Serves: Four.
