Mirchi Vada

Ingredients

6 Banana peppers

For the batter:

1 cup gram flour (besan)
salt
turmeric

For the filling:

3 large potatoes
finely chopped coriander leaves
chilli powder
salt

Method:

Filling:

  1. Boil the potatoes with salt.
  2. Cool, peel and then mash them.
  3. Add cut coriander leaves, chilli powder and mix well.

Batter:

  1. Mix all the ingredients with water to make a thick batter.
  2. Slit the peppers, remove the seeds, and fill them with the potato filling.
  3. Heat the oil in a kadahi.
  4. Now dip the filled peppers in the batter till they are covered completely.
  5. Now fry them till golden brown.

Serve them hot with mint chutney or ketchup. They make a delicious evening snack!!
