Appe

Ingredients

Rice 3 Cups
Urad Dal 1 Cup
Fenugreek Seeds 1 Tablespoon
Channa Dal 4 Tablespoons
Coriander Leaves finely chopped 3 Tablespoons
Onion finely chopped 1 medium
Green Chillies 3 or 4 finely chopped
Oil
Salt to taste
Sugar 1/2 tsp

Method:

  1. Soak rice and fenugreek seeds together and dals separately for 10-12 hrs.
  2. Grind everything, add salt & sugar and keep in a warm place for 8-9 hrs to ferment.
  3. Take half of the batter in a container - add chopped onions, green chillies and coriander leaves. Mix thoroughly.
  4. Keep Appe Tawa on a medium flame.
  5. When it is hot add few drops of oil in each round of Appe Tawa.
  6. Fill the round with the prepared appe mixture.
  7. When the sides start browning, turn the appe upside down and fry on the other side.
  8. Serve hot with Coconut Chutney.
