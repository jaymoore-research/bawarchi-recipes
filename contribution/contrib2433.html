South Indian Curry (Sothi)

This "sothi" is a special receipe which can be prepared at home, when we have a party.

Ingredients (for two people)
1. Coconut - 1 (roughly 1/2 per person)
2. Vegetables - Potato, Carrot, Beans, Drumsticks
3. Garlic - 4 to 7 flakes
4. Small onions - 10
5. Moong dhal - 1/2 cup

Method:

  1. Pressure Cook all the vegetables & moong dhal.
  2. Grind coconut and take milk extract out of it.
  3. Keep the first extract separate. Take the second extract again with some amount of water.
  4. Now add garlic, onions to the second extract and boil it. After 5 min add the first extract and cooked vegetables to it. Smash moong dhal and add to the above.
  5. Fry jeera in ghee and spread to it.
  6. Sothi can be had with rice and potato curry and ginger chutney.
