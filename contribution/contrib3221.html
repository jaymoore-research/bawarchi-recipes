Black Eyed Beans Or Mocchakottai Kootu

Ingredients

1. Mocchakottai - 1/4 kg
2. Pumpkin - 1/2kg(cut into medium pieces)
3. sambar powder - 3 tspn

For Grinding:

1. coconut powder - 7 -8 tspn
2. cumin seeds - 1 -1/2 tspn
3. Red chillies - 5 -6

Method:

  1. Boil Mocchakottai in water. When this done, keep it aside.
  2. Cook pumpkin in little water with some salt and keep it aside.
  3. Now, grind the coconut, cuminseeds and chillies with some water to a fine paste. Add this mixture to the mocchakotai and pumpkin. Then add sambar powder. Add salt as per ur taste. Mix everything well.

This goes well with Rice, Chapatis. Hope you would try this kootu and relish its taste.
