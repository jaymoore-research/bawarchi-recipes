Paneer in Cream

Ingredients

Paneer (Cottage Cheese ) : 250 gms
Tomatoes : 2 medium
Onions : 2 medium
Salt to taste
Turmeric pwdr : 1/4 tsp
Cream / Half&Half; : 3 tbsp
Dry Methi leaves : 1 tsp full (Washed)

Method :

  1. Wash & Cut Paneer into long pieces. Keep aside.
  2. Pressure cook Tomato & Onion together on high till 2 whistles, with little less than 1/2 a cooker of water.
  3. Take out when pressure cooked & set aside along with water, cool & grind with water as to make soup & then strain water in sauce pan.
  4. Bring the soup to boil & pour cream, let boil for atleast 10 min.
  5. Now add salt & turmeric pwdr, stir & let boil for 1 min.
  6. Add the chunks of paneer & washed methi leaves.
  7. Boil for another 2 mins. on high.

Serve this with Parathas.
