Vegetable Stuffed Chapatti

Ingredients

For Chapatti

200 g fine whole Wheat Flour
1 tsp Oil
125 ml Water

For Filling

1 tsp Fennel powder
1 Onion, thinly sliced
2 cm Ginger, minced
2 Green Chillies, sliced
3 tsp Meat Curry Powder
350 gm Cauliflower, cut into small pieces
3 medium Potatoes, boiled, peeled & diced
100 gm Peas, softened in hot water for 1/2 minute
1/3 tsp Salt

Method:

To Make Chapatti

  1. Put flour, oil & water in a bowl. Knead to form a smooth dough; leave aside for 1/2 hour.
  2. Divide into 8 balls; roll each ball into chapattis.
  3. Place on wok and cook on both sides, about 1 minute each. Set aside.

To Make Filling

  1. Heat 2 tbsp oil and fry fennel for about 30 seconds.
  2. Add onion, ginger, garlic, chillies and fry for 30 seconds. Add all the ingredients & stir fry for 15 minutes until the mixture is quite dry.
  3. Put 2 tbsp of filling on chapatti end; roll up thightly. Serve.
