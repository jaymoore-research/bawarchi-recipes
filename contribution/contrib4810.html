7 cupcake

Ingredients

1 cup Milk
1 cup Gram flour
1 cup Ghee
1 cup Coconut flakes
3 cups sugar (try with 2 1/2)
Nuts you like

Method:

  1. Mix all the other ingredients together.
  2. Heat a pan and pour the mix into it and keep mixing till the mixture leaves the sides of ther pan (similar to Mysore paku).
  3. Pour it into a greased plate allow to cool a bit then cut into cakes and decorate with the nuts.

Kaboom! your cake is ready to eat.
