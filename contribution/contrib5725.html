Chicken with Pecans

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients:

Chicken breast - 2 nos
Cauliflower - 1
Beans -Ā3/4 cup (pacha avarai or mochai)
Onion - 1 medium
Tomato - 1 medium
Ginger - a small piece
Garlic -1
Clove - 1
Fennel seeds - Į tsp
Cinnamon - small piece
Turmeric powder-a pinch
Chili&coriander powder- 1 tsp
Pecans - 8 to 10
Olive oil - 2 - 3 tsp
Fenugreek leaves - 10 to 15 leaves (optional)
Celantro - 1 tbsp
Salt to taste
Method

  1. Chop or mince the chicken in a food processor.
  2. Cut onions ,garlic, ginger ,tomato .
  3. Cook cauliflower and beans in salted water.
  4. Heat a pan add oil then clove cinnamon 1 tbsp of onion ,garlic and ginger.
  5. Fry until onion is slightly brown . Cool and grind it to a paste along with Chili & coriander powder and pecans.
  6. Heat oil in a pan add fennel seeds,remaining onion , saute the onions until translucent and add minced chicken.
  7. When chicken becomes white add salt and turmeric powder.cook for 3-4 mts.
  8. Now add cooked vegetable ,mix well and then add the ground paste.
  9. Cook for another 10 mts or until the curry is semi-dry.
  10. Garnish with cilantro and serve hot with rice or chappathi.
  11. Note : if fenugreek leaves are available saute them and add while adding the vegetables
