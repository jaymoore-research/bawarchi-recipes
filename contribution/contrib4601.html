Puffed Rice Uppuma

Ingredients

Puffed Rice-3 cups
Peanuts-2 tbsp
Onion - 1
Green Chillies-2
Lime Juice-2 sp
Oil-1 tsp
Gram Dhal-1 tsp
Udhad dhal-1 tsp
Mustard seeds
Salt to taste
Turmeric powder- 1 pinch
Coriander and Curry Leaves.

Method:

  1. Pour oil in a pan and season it with mustard seeds, gram and udad dhal.
  2. Saute finely chopped onions and add green chillies and turmeric powder. After sometime, add the puffed rice and mix well. Then add powedered peanuts, salt and a dash of lime juice.
  3. Finally add finely chopped coriander and curry leaves.
