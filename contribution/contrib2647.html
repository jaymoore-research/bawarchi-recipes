Mix Veg Delight

Ingredients

Shelled Green Peas 50 gm
Cauliflower florets 50 gm
Broccoli florets (optional) 50 gm
Paneer cubes (cottage cheese) 50 gm
French Beans thinly sliced 50 gm
Green Cabbage shredded 25 gm
Capsicum deseeded & sliced 1 no
Spring Onions coarsely chopped 100 gm
Oil 1 tablespoonful
White Pepper Powder 1/2 teaspoon
Green Chili chopped 1-2 no
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Heat oil in a karahi or wok and cook spring onions for half a minute. Add green chillies and white pepper powder and stir.
  2. Add cauliflower and broccoli florets, mix, cover & cook till half done.
  3. Add capsicum & beans, stir, cover and cook on a medium heat for another 3-4 minutes
  4. Add cabbage & cook for another 2-3 minutes and lastly add paneer cubes and salt. Mix well and cook uncovered for 2 minutes
  5. Garnish with green coriander leaves and serve hot with paranthas.

Fruit Salad

Ingredients:

Banana (cut into small cubes) 1 no
Apple (peeled & cut into small cubes) 1 no
Green Grapes (deseeded & chopped) 50 gm
Black Grapes (deseeded & chopped) 50 gm
Orange (deseeded & chopped) 1 no
Strawberries (chopped) optional 3-4 no
Cherries (deseeded & chopped) optional 8-10 no
Raisins 10-15 no
Cream 100 gm
Yogurt fresh 125 gm
Sugar 2 -3 tablespoonful
Black Salt/Salt to taste
White Pepper (optional) a pinch or two

Method:

  1. Take yogurt in a bowl, add sugar and beat nicely.
  2. Add cream and mix to form a uniform mixture.
  3. Add all the fruits one by one while mixing well.
  4. Lastly, add salt & pepper and mix well. Decorate with strawberries or cherries & grapes and serve chilled. Be prepared for the compliments.

It tastes best when prepared freshly. One can always add or omit a fruit depending upon seasonal variations.
