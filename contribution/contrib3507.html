Three Beans Curry

Ingredients

Rajma 1/2 cup
Moong dal (green) 1/2 cup
White Peas 1/2 cup
Onion 3 cut into small pieces.
Tomato 1/2 cup cut into small pieces
Tomato Puree 1/2 cup
Turmeric powder 1/2 tspn
chillie powder 1/2 tspn
curry powder 1/2 tspn
Garam masala powder 1/2 tspn
Methi seeds 1/4 tspn
Kalongi seeds 1/4 tspn
Saunf 1/4 tspn
tamarind paste 1/2 table spon
Oil for frying
Salt for taste
coriander leaves for garnishing.

Method:

  1. Soak the dals separatelyovernight and cook it next day.
  2. In a pan heat oil and put saunf and the seeds. once it starts spluttering fry onion till light brown.
  3. Now put the tomato pieces and fry. Add the cooked dals and the dry powders one by one and fry.
  4. Put tomato puree, tamarind paste and salt and fry until it becomes thick gravy. Add little water if necessary.
  5. Finally garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with Pulkas or Parathas.

Serves: 5-6
Preparation time: Apart from soaking , 30 minutes.
Shelf life - one day.
