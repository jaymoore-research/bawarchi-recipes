Fish Fry

Ingredients

Cat Fish (3 fillets serve 6 people)
1 Large Onion(cut into small pieces)
Ginger Garlic Paste
Coriander Leaves
Garam Masala
Lemon
Coconut Powder
Red Mirchi Powder
Dhania Powder
Turmeric Powder
Salt
Oil

Method:

  1. Cut Fish into 2 inches pieces and wash them well with vinegar and water marinate the washed fish with ginger garlic paste, salt, mirchi powder, Dhania Powder and add juice of 1 lemon, mix well and marinate and keep in fridge 24 hours or more. (you can marinate fish and keep in freezer for week, this way it will be marinated well. If you want to cook after 1 week or so keep it in freezer)
  2. Whenever you want cook the fish remove from fridge and squeeze out all the water with hand . Retain this water, don't throw away. Keep a skillet on the stove and add oil into it to deep fry the fish.
  3. After the oil is hot add the fish pieces one by one, you can fry them in batches. Turn them over slowly so that they are fried till brown.while turning them do it slowly so that the fish don't break into pieces.
  4. After they are fried remove them and put in a paper towel so that it will absorb oil.
  5. Take another skillet and add oil or you can use the same oil what you used for frying.
  6. The fish add the onion pieces after few minutes add ginger garlic paste and fry them till light brown or till cooked well add mirchi powder, garam masala, turmeric, coconut powder salt and fry for few more minutes. Now add the fried fish and mix slowly for 2 mins. or so and then add lemon juice and coriander and remove from fire to serve hot.

Hope you enjoy this dish . When ever I cooked this recipe, the last people who eat will never get to eat this.

Coconut Burfi

Ingredients

1 medium glass of coconut finely grated.
1 medium glass of sugar.
1/2 tbsp of elaichi powder.

Method

  1. Add the glass of sugar to little quantity of water in a vessel.
  2. Keep it in low flame and stir it till the sugar dissolves.
  3. Slowly add the grated coconut to it and add a little ghee to it.
  4. Keep on stirring till the coconut mixture is cooked and there is no water in it.
  5. Pour the mixture in a plate which is smeared with little ghee.
  6. Slowly cut it in the shape of Burfi.

Almost 20-25 pieces can be made.
