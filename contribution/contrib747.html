Baboos Roti (buns)

Ingredients

1 Cup of Curd
1 - 1 1/2 Cups of Sugar
1/2 Banana (If small banana, use 1 or 1 1/2)
4-5 cups of Maida.
one pinch of any color powder
one pinch baking soda
Oil to Fry.

Method:

  1. Mix the curd and sugar.
  2. Add colour and the soda to the mix.
  3. Mix in the bananas, so that there is no lumps remaining.
  4. Add in Maida until the dough can be made as one whole mass. (Make the dough as if you are going to make chappathis or paratas.).
  5. Keep the dough for 4-8 hours.
  6. Take a small ball of the mixture (as big as a tennis ball) and flatten it with a rod. One can even use a bottle to flatten.
  7. While flattening if the mixture sticks, it can be soaked in Maida flour little bit. ( Flatten it only until the Mixture is just thin enough ).
  8. Fry it in oil for couple of seconds.
  9. Then turn it and fry in on the other side for few more minutes. Eat it hot.
