Chicken Alfredo with Broccoli

Ingredients

4 cups of Broccoli Florets
2 tbsp. Olive Oil
4-6 skinless, boneless Chicken breast, cut into long strips
1.5 cups Whipping Cream
1.5 cups Parmesan Cheese
1 cup of Pasta (Fettuccini/Penne/Fusili) cooked according to the package directions
1 med.sized Onion finely chopped
1 tbsp. Ginger-Garlic paste
Salt and Pepper to taste
1 Bayleaf

Method:

  1. Place the broccoli florets in a plastic bag and microwave it until tender.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet.
  3. Add chicken strips and saute until cooked (about 5 mins). Drain and keep them aside.
  4. Add chopped onion and a bayleaf to the same oil and saute for 3 minutes.
  5. Add ginger-garlic paste and saute for a couple of minutes.
  6. Add whipping cream and parmesan cheese and simmer until thick sauce forms. It will take about 5 mins.
  7. Add chicken strips, broccoli florets, salt and pepper and cooked pasta to it.
  8. Pour this mix into a buttered casserole and sprinkle grated mozzarella cheese. Broil until cheese melts.
  9. Serve hot with Salad and Baked Tomato halves.

To bake tomatoes, slit tomatoes into 4 pcs. Add olive oil, dried Italian herbs,salt and pepper and bake in 350 degrees oven for 25 mins.
