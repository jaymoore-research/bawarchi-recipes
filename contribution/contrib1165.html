Strawberry and Rhubarb Parfait

Ingredients

1 cup fresh Rhubarb, peeled and chopped
1 cup fresh Strawberries
1/2 cup Granulated sugar
2 tbsp. light corn syrup
2 large sprigs of mint
1/3 pint of heavy whipping cream
2 tbsp. dessert wine
1/3 cup of Scotch whisky (blended)
3 Eggs, separated

Method :

  1. Combine rhubarb, strawberries, sugar, mint and dessert wine in a saucepan.
  2. Bring rhubarb mixture to a boil and simmer for 10 minutes until fruit is soft.
  3. Puree rhubarb mixture in a food processor. Sieve and cool.
  4. Whisk the whisky, sugar, corn syrup and egg yolks in a double boiler until light and frothy.
  5. Whisk egg whites and whipping cream to soft peaks.
  6. Add rhubarb mixture to cooled egg yolk mixture.
  7. Gently fold in cream and egg whites.
  8. Place in terrine or individual molds and freeze.
  9. To serve, slice or turn out of molds.
