Cracked Wheat Khichdi

Makes : 2

Ingredients

2 cup wheat cracked
1/2 cup corn kernels
1/2 cup green peas
1/2 cup french beans finely chopped
1/2 cup carrot diced
peanuts (optional)
garlic paste
ginger chilli paste
salt
red chilli powder
dhaniya powder
turmeric
a pinch of hing
1 tea spoon ghee or oil

Method:

  1. First in rice cooker vessel add all the ingredients & add 3 glass of water. mix well. Allow to cook. Serve hot.

Variation:
You make this khicdi in microwave also. Follow the same procedure. Cook for 20 min. Stirring occasionally.

you can also add cauliflower if you like.

you can also make this khichdi with baingan & potato.
