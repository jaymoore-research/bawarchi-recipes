Chicken in Red sauce

Ingredients

1 pkt Chicken legs or small pieces of whole chicken
� cup Mayonnaise
� cup Yogurt
� cup Tomato ketchup
8-10 Cashews
Green Chillies to taste
3 cloves of Garlic
Oil
Soya Sauce
Ginger Garlic paste
Black ground Pepper

Method:

  1. Firstly, marinate chicken in soya sauce, ginger and garlic and little pepper for minimum 2-3 hours and than fry the chicken (only half cook it) in shallow oil. Remove the chicken and keep aside. Save the oil.
  2. Than in a blender blend together the mayonnaise, yogurt, ketchup, chillies and garlic and cashews to a smooth paste.
  3. In the left over oil - (not all the oil, you may remove some) fry the paste well (you may add corn and sliced capsicum for additional flavor) and then place the chicken pieces and let it cook with the lid shut on low flame until oil surfaces and chicken is cooked.

Eat on it's own or with Bread or Nan.
