Pineapple Love

Ingredients

Whip Cream 8 oz.
Sour Cream 8 oz.
Condensed Milk Small tin
Crushed Pineapple 2 small/ 1med. tin
Strawberry Roundels to decorate.

Method :

  1. Mix all Ingredients & refrigerate for 2 hr.
  2. Decorate with the strawberry & serve.

Making Time: 15 min.
Makes: 4 servings
Shelf Life: 3 days.

Note: You can use some other fruit for decoration. Keep in the fridge if not using same day.
