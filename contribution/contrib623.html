Palak Paneer

Ingredients

1 Cup Basmati or Long Grain
2 Cup Water
3/4 cup finely chopped onion
2-3 green chillies (make into paste)
1 tbs ginger garlic paste
2 tbs cumin seeds
1/2 cup palak paste ( To make palak paste: first boil palak with water, when it is cool make thick paste)
1 tbs lemon juice
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. In a pan put little oil when it is hot, first fry onion, ginger garlic paste, cumin seeds till onion becomes light brown.
  2. Next add green chilli paste, fry for 1-2 minutes then add palak paste
  3. In the meantime cook one cup rice long or basmati will do, when it is done and add cold mix the rice to fried mixture and keep on the stove for 1-2 minutes
  4. Later take out, add lemon juice , mix well and serve hot with Raita
