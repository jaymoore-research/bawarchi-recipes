The Konkan Thunder

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Serving Size: 2 persons
Ingredients

Chicken - 1.5 pounds
Turmeric Powder- 1 tspn
Onions: 2 medium size
Ginger - 1 tspn
Garlic paste -  1. tspn
Asafeotida - 1/2 tspn
Chillie powder - 1. tbspn
Garam masala  - 1 tspn
Oil - 2 tbspn
Onions: 2 medium size
Coconut dry - 3/4 cup
Poppy seeds - 1 tspn (optional)

Method :

 

  1. Clean wash and cut chicken into medium sized pieces.
  2. Clean and cut 1 onion into thin long slices.
  3. In a frying pan, saute the onions till dark brown. Keep aside
  4. Then Roast the poppy seeds and dry coconut till it changes its color.
  5. Grind together onion and  coconut with water and make a very fine paste of it. Keep aside.
  6. Heat Oil in a pan and saute 1 finely cut onion till golden brown.
  7. Add  ginger garlic paste, fry for a minute, then add asafeotida, turmeric   powder, chillie powder, 1 tspn garam masala powder.
  8. Saute for a minute, then add chicken and 1 cup of water.
  9. When it starts boiling cover it with the lid. When the chicken is 3/4th cooked add the paste in it and salt.
  10. Cook till the chicken is done.
  11. Garnish it with coriander leaves.

P.S :If you want more gravy add 1.5 cup water. instead of

Serve this hot with garlic & red chillies chutney, ghee or yogurt
