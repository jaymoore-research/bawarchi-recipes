Brinjal Thuvaiyal

Ingredients

1. brinjal 4 (if size is small take 8)
2. red chilli 8
3. mustard 1tbs spoon
4. urad dal 3 tbs
5. small pinch of asafoetida powder
6. oil 5 to 6 spoons for frying
7. salt
8. a small ball of tamarind

Method

  1. Cut the brinjal and boil it.
  2. Smash the boiled brinjal using vegetable smasher.
  3. Take a fry pan add 5 to 6 spoon of oil in it
  4. Add mustard when it splutters add asafoetida powder
  5. Then add urad dal and red chilli and fry it.
  6. Mix smashed brinjal and all the fried ingredients with salt and tamarind
  7. Using a blender grind every thing.
  8. This paste is called thuvaiyal
  9. It goes very well with rice.
