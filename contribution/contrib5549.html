Coriander Podi/Dry Curry

Ingredients for Cooking

1 Bunch Coriander
2 tblsp Chutney Dhal
4 or 5 green chillies
1/2 tsp coriander seeds
1/4 tsp cumin seeds
salt to taste
3 tbsp. oil
Mustard seeds
Curry leaves
Urad dhal
Garlic pods - 7 or 8

METHOD:

  1. Wash and cut coriander . 
  2. Grind  chutney dhal , coriander seeds and cumin seeds into fine powder 
  3. Add cut coriander and green chillies 
  4. Grind this mixture for about a minute with out water . 
  5. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds , curry leaves , urad dhal 
  6. Add cut garlic and fry until urad dhal turns light brown. 
  7. Add the mixture from the grinder to the oil and try to mix it with a flat laddle. 
  8. Add salt
  9. Fry the entire mixture for about 3 to 4 minutes until it is dry . 
  10. Do not roast it too much. 
  11. Enjoy with rice and ghee.

Making time: 20 minutes
Shelflife: 1 week, refrigerated
2-3 days, at room temperature
