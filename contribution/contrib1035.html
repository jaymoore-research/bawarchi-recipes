Sponge Dosa

  1. Wash 2 cups parboiled rice & 1/4 cup rice flakes (Poha or Aval) separately.
  2. Soak them together in 3-4 cups buttermilk for 10-12 hrs.
  3. Next day, grind to a fine smooth paste with 1 1/2-2 tsps salt. Add 1/4 tsp baking soda just before preparing dosa.
  4. Heat a nonstick tawa, pour thick dosa. Very Impt: Do not spread.
  5. Put little ghee or oil around & close with a lid. Cook over med flame for a few mins till there is no raw batter on top of dosa.
  6. Do not turn around as it looses its spongy texture.
  7. Serve hot with any chutney of ur choice. I feel it goes very well with coconut or onion chutney.
