Banana Custard

Ingredients

Ripe Banana 2
Apple 1
Custard Powder 15 gms
Milk 150 ml
Sugar 1/4 cup
Cashew pieces 1 tsp
Almond pieces 1 tsp
Pista pieces 1 tsp
Raisins 1 tsp
Vanilla essence few drops

Method :

  1. Mix sugar and custard powder with little milk.
  2. Boil remaining milk and pour in the above mixture.
  3. Heat the mixture till it begins to boil.
  4. Add vanilla essence.
  5. Chop the bananas and apples into small pieces and add it to this mixture.
  6. Garnish with nuts

After making the above custard, refrigerate and serve it cold. The above recipe can also be tried with oranges, seedless grapes.
