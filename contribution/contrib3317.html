Chicken Curry

Ingredients

Chicken pieces 6-8
1 cup grated coconut
10 roasted red chillies
2 onions. (1 small and 1 large)
2 tomatoes
1" ginger
6 flakes of garlic
2 green chillies
1/2 a bunch of coriander leaves
1" cinnamon stick
3 cloves
Oil for seasoning
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Boil the cleaned chicken pieces in a vessel without water (It leaves water while boiling). Add salt when half done and cook till done.
  2. Grind together roasted chillies and coconut with required amount of water.
  3. Half way through grinding, add to this, 1 small onion, ginger, garlic, green chillies and coriander leaves (all chopped) and grind to make a fine paste.
  4. Heat 2 tbsp oil in a kadai. Add finely chopped 1 large onion and fry till it is brown.
  5. Add cinnamon and cloves and fry for 2-3 seconds.
  6. Add ground masala and fry for 5 minutes.
  7. Add chopped tomatoes and fry for few more seconds.
  8. Add water to this and boil this gravy.
  9. Trasnfer this to the cooked chicken and heat on a low flame for 5 minutes.
  10. Serve hot with ghee rice.
