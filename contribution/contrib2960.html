Kanjeevaram Idli (6-7 people)

Ingredients

Rice - 1 cup,
Urad dal - 1 cup,
Coconut - 1/2 cup,
Oil 2 tablespoon,
Pepper 1/2 teaspoon,
Jeera 1/2 teaspoon,
Channadal 1/2 teaspoon,
Salt 3/4 teaspoon or according to taste,
Green chilli,
Ginger 1/2 inch,
Curry leaves a few,
A pinch of hing,
A pinch of turmeric.

Method:

  1. Soak rice and urad dal in water for a few hours.Wash and grind into a coarse batter adding enough water. Keep to ferment overnight.
  2. Hext morning heat oil in a pan add the rest of the ingrdients, fry a bit, and then mix into the batter along with the salt. The batter must be thick.
  3. Steam in idli moulds or greased plate for 10-20 minutes.
  4. Serve hot with tomato chutney or sambhar.
