Pudina Rice

Ingredients

1 cup Basmati Rice
1 bunch Mint leaves (pudina)
2 Tomotoes
2 Onion
1 inch Ginger
2 Green Chillies
4 to 5 Garlic flakes
1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
1/2 tsp Chilli powder
1 or 1 pieces of Cinnamon
2 or 3 Cloves
1 tsp Ani seeds (sombu)
2 to 3 table spoon of Oil
1 tsp of Salt (or as per taste)

Method

  1. Wash and soak rice for ten minutes.
  2. Cut onions into thin long pieces.
  3. Cut tomotoes into small pieces.
  4. Grind mint leaves, ginger, garlic, cloves, cinnamon and aniseeds into a fine paste.
  5. Heat oil in a pan and add the onions and fry till translucent. Add the ground paste and tomatoes fry till oil separates. Add the powders and fry for a minute.
  6. Transfer the above into a pressure cooker or rice cooker. Add the soaked rice and pour 2 cups of water. Add salt as per taste and cook. If in pressure cooker the maximum time is 7 minutes.

Note: Fry cashew and decorate.
