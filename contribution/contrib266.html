Vegetable Fried Rice

Ingredients

1 to 2 small carrots cut length wise thinly,
Green Beans 10 to 12 cut length wise,
Peas 1/4 cup frozen or dry soaked over night,
Cauliflower flowerts 1/2 cup,
Bell pepper 1/2 cut into lenth wise,
Onion 1 medium,
4 small cinnamon sticks,
4 cloves.
1 Cup Rice Basmati or Long Grain= 2 Cup Water.

Green Masala

Green chillies 4 to 5,
Ginger small piece,
Garlic
1 to 2 clove,
Cilantro small bunch.
Make paste of this in blender and keep aside.

Method

  1. In a pan put little ghee and little oil when it is hot, first fry onion, cloves, and cinnamon sticks till onion becomes transparent
  2. Then cut all the vegetables length wise thin and put them to onion which is in the pan and fry till all the vegetables are done nicely with out putting water.
  3. Once this is done now put Green Masala Paste to this along with salt to your taste and lemon and fry for some more time.
  4. In the meantime cook one cup rice long or basmati will do, when it is done and cold mix the rice to the fried vegetables mixture and keep on the stove for 5 to 10 mts on low.
  5. Later take out and serve with side dish like Raita or Chenna.
