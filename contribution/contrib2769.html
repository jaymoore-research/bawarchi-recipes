Spring Onion Vegetable

Ingredients

Spring Onions - 2/3 bunch
Gram Flour - 2 tbsp.
Chilli powder - 2/3 tsp.
Turmeric - 1 tsp
Oil - 3 tbsp
Salt - as per taste
Sugar - one pinch
Mustard seeds - 1 tsp

Method:

  1. Wash the spring onions and cut the green leaves into very small pieces. Do not take lot of onions as they might spoil the taste.
  2. Heat the oil in a kadai and add mustard seeds. Immediately add turmeric and then the cut spring onions. Cook this in oil for about 5-7 minutes. Do not cover the kadai. Keep stirring.
  3. Add chilli powder, stir for a while. Do not overcook the vegetable otherwise it looses its green colour.
  4. Add salt and little bit of sugar.
  5. Add gram flour 1 tbsp at a time. Keep stirring so that lumps are not formed. There should be no dry flour in kadai. Add flour only until the oil can absorb it. After that just stir fry the vegetable on low flame continously for a minute or two. The dish is ready.
