Poricha Kootu

Ingredients

Diced chow-chow pieces - 2 cups
Bengal gram dhal - 1/2cup
Salt - as required

For seasoning:

Oil - 2tsps
Mustard seeds - 1/4tsp

Grind together:

Red chillies - 6
Cumin seeds - 1tsp
Black gram dhal - 1tsp

Fry the above in little oil and grind with 1/4 cup of grated fresh Coconut.

Method:

  1. Scrape the outer skin and dice into 1 inch cubes.
  2. Cook it along with bengal gram dhal with enough water till dhal becomes soft to touch.
  3. Add the ground paste, salt and cook till the gravy becomes thick.
  4. Heat oil, add the seasonings and mix it with kootu.
  5. Garnish with curry leaves.
  6. Serve hot with cooked rice.

Variations

Other vegetables that can be used instead of chow-chow (chayotte) are White Pumpkin, Snake Gourd, Raddish, Vegetable Marrow, etc.
