Beans Paruppu Usili

Ingredients

Very tender Beans 200 gms very finely chopped.

For the Usili:

Thuvar dal - 1/2 cup
Chana dal, Pesar dal & Urad dal put together - 1/2 cup. ( all the four dals totaling to one cup)
Raw Rice 1 tea spoon.
Red Chillies 3-4
Curry Leaves one bunch
Asafoetida( hing) - 1/4 tea spoon;

For Seasoning:

Kadugu (rai) - 1/2 tea spoon
Urad dal - 1/2 tea spoon;
Chana dal - 1 tea spoon
Turmeric powder - 1/4 tea spoon.
Oil 1/4 cup or more as required.

Method:

  1. Soak all the dals and the raw rice for about 3-4 hours.
  2. Grind to very nice paste together with the red chillies. The paste should be as thick as you make for paruppu vadai (aamai vadai). Mix in hing (perungayam) powder. Leave it for about 1/2 hour.
  3. Mix the finely chopped beans in this dal paste and cook in a pressure cooker for about two / three whistles.
  4. Take it out and cool it under the fan. Loosen the cooked paste evenly. Ensure that there are no lumps.
  5. In a kadai pour oil initially 2 table spoons and allow the rai (kadugu to splutter). Then add the curry leaves, some more hing powder, turmeric podwer, urad dal, channa dal and fry them till they are golden brown.
  6. Add in the cooked paruppu usili and fry till all water is completle evaporated. Add in the required amount of oil slowly. (The most tiring part--- call your spouse --- and ask to) keep a careful watch and keep on mixing the usili lest it would burn. Ensure that the gas is very very low.
  7. Serve it with hot rice and the "red pumpkin race".

Prepartion Time: Other than soaking 15 minutes
Cooking Time: 45 minutes.
