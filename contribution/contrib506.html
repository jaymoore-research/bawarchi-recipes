Masala Cookies

Ingredients

All purpose flour - 1 cup
Margarine/butter/any fat - 1/2 cup
Sugar - 2 tsp
Salt to taste
Baking powder 3/4 tsp
Hot peppers chopped, curry leaves chopped, ginger grated, cilantro chopped, mint chopped - (total of 3 tsp)
Yogurt - 2-3 tsp

Method:

  1. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt
  2. Cut the fat/margarine/butter in the flour with sugar.
  3. Do not knead. It must be bread crumb consistency.
  4. Mix the chopped peppers, curry leaves, ginger, cilantro, mint.
  5. Add yogurt and mix
  6. Roll the dough 1/8" thickness and cut to desired shapes
  7. Place on a baking sheet 2" apart
  8. Bake at 350 degree F for 15 minutes or till done.
