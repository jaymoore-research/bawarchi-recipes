Pepper Chicken

Ingredients

1 whole chicken
coriander powder 1tbsp
cinnamon powder 1tsp
soy sauce 2tsp
rice vinegar (optional)
garam masala 1tsp
pepper powder 1tbsp
onions 3 large
green chilly 4 to 5
oil
coriander leaves for garnish
salt to taste

Method

  1. Clean the chicken and put it into a nonstick kadai with some salt, coriander powder, cinnamon powder and some water.
  2. Add water just so the chicken is covered. Cook till chicken is tender (30 min max)
  3. Take another kadai with oil and fry the onions, green chillies in them till golden. You can start frying the onions when the chicken is 3/4 done.
  4. Add the chicken & add the coriander powder, garam masala, soy sauce, vinegar, pepper powder and check for salt.
  5. Garnish with freshly chopped coriander.

And u'r set.. enjoy!
