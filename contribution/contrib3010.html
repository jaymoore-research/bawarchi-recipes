Gola-Bhat with Sar

Ingredients

For Gola:

1 1 /2 cup Besan
1 Onion finely chopped
2 tbsp Chilli Powder
1 tbsp Salt
1 tbsp Oil
1 tbsp Lemon Juice
1/2 tbsp Haldi
1/2 tbsp Garam Masala
Water to make a dough.

For rice

1 cup Rice
Hing
Currypatta
Mastard seeds
Jeera.
Salt as per taste

For Sar

Tamarind pulp 2 tbsp
1 cup Water
Salt for taste
Hing
Mustard for tadka
Oil
Currypatta

Oil

5 tbs Oil
Mustard seeds 2 tbsp
Currypatta
Garlic flakes 4-5 chopped

Method

  1. Mix all the ingredients given in gola & make dough and make small balls in the shape of egg.
  2. For rice make tadka and add rice and water and add the gola's when the rice start's boiling, cook along with the rice, now the gola-bhat is ready.
  3. For sar mix water and tamarind give tadka and just heat it don't boil it. sar is ready.
  4. For oil heat oil add mustard seeds, currypatta, garlic, hing.
  5. Serve rice with gola and sar in a bowl and 1 spoon oil on the rice.

Serves: 3 people.
