Idli Finger Fries

Ingredients for Cooking

Leftover idlies
Oil for deep frying

  Method:

  1. Take  leftover  idlies, cut   into  thin  slices.
  2. Heat  oil  in  a deep  frying  pan. 
  3. When  the  oil   is  really  hot, drop  few  pieces  at a  time. 
  4. Keep  turning  them now  and  then
  5. When  it  is  turns    into  golden   brown remove from  oil    and remove  excess  fat  using  paper  napkin.0
  6. Serve  it  hot  with  coconut chutney   or  any   sauce.
