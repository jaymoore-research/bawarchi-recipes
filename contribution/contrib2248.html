Fish fry

Ingredients

1 Fish cut into medium sized pieces
1/4 tsp Turmeric Powder
1 1/4 tsp Ginger-Garlic paste
1 tsp Red Chilli powder
3 nos. Cloves powder
2 nos. Elaichi powder
5 tsp Oil
Little Parsily
1 tsp Lemon Juice
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Wash the fish atleast 4-5 times in medium hot water with adding little salt.
  2. Take a bowl put fish pieces, turmeric, ginger garlic paste, red chillies powder, cloves, elaichi, lemon juice and salt to taste mix all the ingredients and marinate for atleast 1/2 an hour.
  3. Heat the pan with oil and put the marinated fish pieces one by one and fry till golden brown.
  4. Atlast before serving garnish with fried onions, lemon pieces and parsily.
  5. Serve hot
