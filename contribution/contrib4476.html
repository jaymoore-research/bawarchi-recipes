Mixed Vegetables

Ingredients

10-12 florets of Cauliflower, if they are big slit them into half.
10 French Beans washed & chopped in slants.
3 Carrots washed & chopped in slants.
10-12 Button Mushrooms halved.
10-12 cubes of Paneer.
1 medium bowl of Frozen Green Peas.
1 large Potato sliced.
1 large Onion sliced.
2 Green Chillies finely chopped.
1 large Tomato finely chopped.
3 tbsp. Tomato Ketchup.
1/4 tsp. Red Chilly powder
1/4 tsp. Garam Masala powder
Salt to taste.
Oil for frying & cooking.
1 tbsp. Butter or Margarine.

Method:

  1. Put water in a pan, let it boil & then put beans, carrots & peas to blanch them. Let them boil just for 2 minutes,sieve them & wash them in cold water to maintain the texture.
  2. In the mean time heat oil in a karahi & fry cauliflower & potatoes turn by turn till golden brown in colour. Keep aside the boiled & fried vegetables.
  3. Now take butter in a skillet, heat it & saute mushrooms on high heat till golden brown. Take them out & in the same skillet put 2 tbsp.oil heat it & put sliced onions, saute them till little golden brown & mix tomatoes, fry for another 2min.
  4. Then mix all the vegetables, salt, chilly powder, garam masala powder, chopped green chillies. Cook them for another 2 min. Then add ketchup cook for another minute.
  5. Serve hot with Roti or Paratha.

(YOU CAN CHANGE VEGETABLES OR PROPORTIONS ACCORDING TO YOUR TASTE). Enjoy.
