Coriander stuffed Brinjal

Ingredients

Fresh coriander : 3 bunches
Green chillies : 10
Tamarind : as needed
salt : 3 tea spoons
Small Brinjals : 10
Oil : 5 table spoons

Method:

  1. Cut the brinjals so that the pieces should remain attached at the stalk, for stuffing the brinjals.
  2. Grind coriander, chillies, tamarind and salt well as a thick paste.
  3. Stuff this mix in the brinjals.
  4. Heat oil and fry the stuffed brinjals in that oil till they get cooked up well.
