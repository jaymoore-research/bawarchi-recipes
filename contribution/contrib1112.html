Carrot Rice

Ingredients

Rice 2 cups
Half kg of carrots
Bunch of Coriander leaves
4 Garlic cloves
A small piece of Ginger
2 to 3 cloves
Green chillies (as per taste)
Cinnamon
Salt

Method:

  1. Grate the carrots.
  2. Grind a bunch of coriander leaves alongwith garlic, ginger, cloves, green chillies and cinnamon.
  3. Take oil, splutter it with bay leaves and add the ground masala with the grated carrots. Cook it over low fire without adding water.
  4. Add salt as per taste.
  5. Add the required amount of cooked rice to it and serve it hot with a salad.
  6. Add little ghee if you are not on diet.

You may preserve this for a couple of days and add it up with cooked rice when you feel like eating.
