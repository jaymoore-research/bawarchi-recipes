Namkin Chilla

Ingredients

2 cups of Besan (Chana Daal Flour)
1 medium Onion grated or chopped
2 Green Chillis chopped
1 clove of Garlic grated
1 tsp Jeera
1 tsp Turmeric Powder
1 tsp Red Chilli Powder
2 tsp Lime juice
Salt to taste
Cliantro chopped
1 cup Oil or Cooking Spray

Method :

  1. Put the besan in a big bowl, add salt, chilli powder, turmeric powder, jeera & mix it well
  2. Now add onion, green chillis, garlic, cliantro & lime juice
  3. Mix this mixture by gradually adding water to form a thick batter
  4. Heat a nonstick pan or girdle
  5. On low heat grease nicely with oil or cooking spray and pour one big spoon of batter and spread to a thin round shape (just like traditional dosa)
  6. Pour little oil around its edges and when the underside turns golden, turnover the chilla
  7. Remove from the girdle when both the sides turn golden.
  8. Serve the chilla hot, with chutney, ketchup or pickles.
