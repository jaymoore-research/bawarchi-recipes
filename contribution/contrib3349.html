Tandoori Aloo Chatpata

Ingredients

1 Lb. Potatoes
3 Table spoons Tandoori paste.
Salt to taste.
Half spoon freshly crushed garlic.
4 Table spoons Tomato paste.
Half Table spoon Red chilli powder (Can use Quantities to Taste)
2 cups Water.
2 Spoons crushed Jeera powder (Roasted).

Method:

  1. Peel potatoes. Slit half way on two differnt sides.
  2. Mix above ingeedients and pour in an oven ware tray.
  3. Place potatoes in the tray. Place in an oven at 350 Degrees till the potatoes are soft. Stir once or twice if necesary.
  4. Serve while hot.

An Excellent appetizer at parties. Try the recipe. Good luck. Jai shree Krishna.
