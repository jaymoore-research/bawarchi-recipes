Mango Lassi

Ingredients

1 part mango pulp fresh or canned
2 parts dannon fat free or low-fat vanilla yogurt.

Method

Blend the 1 part mango and 2 parts dannon vanilla yogurt in a blender for 1 min And it is ready to serve.

If you want really chilli lassi then add ice or refrigerate for an hour or so.
