Mexican Pizza

Ingredients

1/2 pound ground meat
16 ounce can of refried beans
1 cup salsa (your choice)
1/2 bunch of green onions(you could use more or less amount)
8 oz. shredded cheddar cheese
Some black olives, chopped
Some small piece of bell pepper, chopped
1 pack (10 pieces of tortillas)
1 cup oil (add more oil when needed)
1 green chili, chopped

Method

  1. Put oil in the large skillet and take one tortilla and fry until golden brown and crispy, do same with other tortillas and keep on side.
  2. Cook the ground meat, drain the fat and when it cool, crumble it with your fingers or by the spoon
  3. Take one fried tortilla on the dish and sread some refried bean by spoon then sprinkel some meat
  4. Now take other tortilla and put it on the one bean-meat tortilla to make layer
  5. Then spead some salsa on it and sprinkel some cheddar cheese on it, sprinkel some green onions and put some olive pieces and some bell peppers pieces,chili
  6. Put that dish in the microwave and heat on high for 4 Minutes
  7. Cut into four pieces and serve
