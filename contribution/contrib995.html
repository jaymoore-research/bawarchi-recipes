Fish Curry

Ingredients

Fish - 1/2 kg
Coconut (scraped) - 1/2 of a medium sized coconut
Green chillies - 4 small ones
Red chilli powder - 1.5 tsp
Coriander powder - 3 tsp
Turmeric powder - 0.5 tsp
Onion (medium sized) - 1
Tomato (medium sized) - 1
Cummin seeds - 1 tsp
Tamarind extract - 1/2 cup
Curry leaves (few)
Mustard - 1 tsp
Oil - 2/3 tbsp
Garlic - 8 flakes
Salt to taste

Method :

  1. Grind coconut, onion, tomato, cummin seeds to a fine paste. (keep aside a small portion of the onion - and slice into small pieces)
  2. Heat the oil in a thick-bottomed vessel. Put the flame in sim. Add mustard.
  3. After the mustard splutters, add the sliced onion and garlic flakes. Fry until golden brown and then add curry leaves and sliced chillies.
  4. Add chilli, turmeric and coriander powder with constant stiring. Continue until oil separates.
  5. Add the coconut paste and stir until oil separates.
  6. Pour the tamarind extract and add water. (water should be a little more than the consistency desired). Let the flame be increased.
  7. Allow to boil and then add salt and the fish.
  8. Avoid vigorous stirring after adding the fish.
  9. Cook until u get the consistency desired.

This curry is good with plain rice, idlis. Tastes better if kept for a day.
