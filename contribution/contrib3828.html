Kabargah (Tabaq Maz)

Ingredients

About 3 by 5 inch long pieces of Mutton (Ribs) with fatty layer & skin intact & 1 Kg Each piece ( 2 or 3 Rib bones)
Desi Ghee 1/2 Kg
Curd 1/2 Kg
Red Kashmiri Chilli Powder 2 tsps
Ginger Powder 1 tsp
Aniseed Powder 2 tsp
Turmeric 1/2 tsp
Asafoetida a pinch
Garam Masala 2 tsp
Cloves 5 No
Salt To Taste
Milk 1 Kg

Method:

  1. In a Patila of about 3 liter capacity, pour the milk, add Turmeric, Ginger & Aniseed powders, Cloves, a tsp Salt & Asafoetida. Stir the contents. Place the meat pieces in the Patila & boil on medium heat for half an hour when the Meat becomes tender & almost all milk has got absorbed and evaporated. Remove the Patila & let the contents cool down a little.
  2. In a bowl mix Curd, Chilli powder, Garam masala & a tsp of Salt and make it to a homogenous batter.
  3. Heat the ghee in a frying pan on slow fire and dip the Meat pieces after removing any gravy, in the batter & fry till Golden Brown. Apply Silver foil & serve hot.

Note: Tabaq Maz varies from Kabargah in that after applying the batter, the Meat pieces are baked in clay plates called TABAQ after smearing Ghee inside. Each Tabaq has 3 or 4 Meat pieces and is covered with another Tabaq. The rims are then sealed with kneaded wheat floor dough. These Tabaqs are then placed on slow charcoal fire so that Meat pieces get sauted in about 30 minutes to deep brown color This process imparts a special aroma to the Dish. Remember the Dish should be served steaming hot.

This recipe is as is being practised by Kashmiri Chefs at various occassions.
