Onion Chutney

Ingredients

2 big Onions.
1 tbsp red chilli powder.
1 tsp cumin powder
1 lemon
1 tbsp chopped coriander (optional)
salt to taste

Method:

  1. First chopped onions finely.
  2. Then add red chilli powder, cumin powder, coriander leaves and mix it.
  3. Then add salt to taste.
  4. Finally add lemon juice.
