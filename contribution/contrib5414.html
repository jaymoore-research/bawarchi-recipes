Chocolaty Carrot and Nut Cake

Ingredients

1 cup Finely grated carrots
3/4 cup dark brown sugar
1/2 cup butter(1 stick)
1 cup of mixture of almond,pecan,cashew,walnut and pista nuts
1 cup Boiling water
1 1/2 cup Wholewheat flour
1/2 cup Unsweetened cocoa powder
1 teaspoon all spice powder
1 teaspoon Baking powder
1/2 teaspoon sodium bicarbonate
1/2 teaspoon Salt

Method:

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. In a big bowl, combine carrots, sugar and butter. Pour the boiling water to this.
  3. In another bowl, mix all the other ingredients.
  4. Add this to the carrot mixture & blend well.
  5. Pour this to a greased cake pan.
  6. Bake till a toothpick inserted in the center of the cake comes out clean.
