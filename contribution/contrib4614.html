I am Payal. This is the recipe for momos can be vegetarian as well as non-vegetarian.

E-mail : dhingrapayal@hotmail.com

Momos

Ingredients

For Momos:

1/2 tbsp salt
2 cups Flour (maida)
Water (enough to knead)
1/2 (boiled, boneless) Chicken (for non veg)
1/2 (grated) Cabbage (for veg)

For Chutney:

5-6 pieces Red Chilli
5-6 pieces Garlic
Vinegar (enough to make it in liquid form)
Pinch of Baking Powder

Method:

  1. Make dough out of flour (maida), baking powder and water keep overnight.
  2. Next day take tennis ball sized dough and roll it like chapatti. Cut half of it from middle like samosas.
  3. Fill boiled chicken/cabbage with lillte bit of salt to taste.
  4. Steam the momos (for steaming idli utensil can be used). Steam till the stickiness goes away. Till about 10 minutes. (Note: you can even fry them.)

For Chutney:

In a mixer mix red chilli, vinegar and garlic together till it forms a paste. Serve hot momos with chutney.
