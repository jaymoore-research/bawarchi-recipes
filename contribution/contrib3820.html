Brinjal Puli Kozambhu

Ingredients

1 big lemon sized Tamarind
1 teaspoonful Chilli powder
1 teaspoonful Coriander powder
A pinch of Turmeric powder
1/2 a teaspoon of Mustard
1 teaspoonful of Jeera
10 seeds Fenugreek
50 grams small sized Onion
10 pearls of Garlic
200 grams Brinjal
10 Curry leaves and Coriander sliced
Salt, to taste

Method:

  1. Soak the tamarind in warm water for an hour . Prepare a thick liquid and strain the juice.
  2. Add salt, chilli powder, coriander powder and turmeric.
  3. Peel off the skin and cut the onions and tomatoes. Split the brinjal into four pieces.
  4. In a kadai, add 3 teaspoonful of oil and heat it. Add mustard seeds. When it splutters, add jeera and fenugreek. Then fry the onions and tomatoes.
  5. In another kadai, fry brinjal with a little oil for 3 minutes. Add the fried onions and brinjal to tamarind liquid. Boil until the brinjal is cooked completely.
  6. Garnish with coriander leaves.

This goes well with rice, dosai, idly and is a very tasty side dish. Along with Brinjal, thattai payaru (karamani) soaked for 6 hours can be added and boiled.
