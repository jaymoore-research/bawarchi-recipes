Chilli Garlic Rice

Ingredients

Basmathi rice 1 cup
Cabbage (cut into small stripes) 1 cup
Beans (cut into small pieces) 1/4 cup
Green bell pepper (cut into small stripes) 1/4 cup
Onions 1
Spring Onions 1 small bunch
Carrots (cut into small stripes) 1/4 cup
Oil 31/2 tbs
Salt as required

Grind together :
Green chillies 6
Garlic 4

Method:

  1. Wash and cook the rice.
  2. Allow it to cool and separate the grains.
  3. To this rice add the chilli garlic paste.
  4. Now, heat oil in a pan and fry the cut vegetables one by one as follows:
    Onions, Beans, Carrots, Bell pepper, Cabbage and in the end the spring onions.
  5. When they are cooked add salt and cooked rice.
  6. Fry in a high flame till the rice becomes hot.
