Walnut Pudding

Ingredients

150gms. walnuts
3 eggs
250 gms. bread
3/4 glass sugar
1 1/2 glass milk
100 gms. butter
breadcrumbs

Method:

  1. Soak bread in milk.Grind walnuts into paste.
  2. Beat eggyolks with sugar and add walnut paste and bread soaked in milk together with melted butter.
  3. Mix all the ingredients well.
  4. Whisk eggwhites,fold them into the mixture.
  5. Butter a baking dish and sprinkle it with breadcrumbs, then pour mixture into it and bake in a moderate oven for 30 to 40 minutes.
  6. Serve hot.
