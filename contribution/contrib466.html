Gatte ki kadi

For Gatte

Ingredients
5 tbsp gram flour
Salt and chilli powder to taste
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
Pinch of asafoetida
1/4 tsp of each cummin pow and garam masala
1 tsp ajwain (thymol)
1 green chilly cut

Method

  1. Knead all the above ingredients into a thick dough (as for puris).
  2. Divide into five parts and shape each into 1 1/2 cm thick cylindrical rolls .
  3. Steam for 5 to 6 minutes until colour is discharged..
  4. Take out , cool and cut each roll into thick rings.
  5. Keep aside.

Ingredients for kadi

Curd-1/2 cup
Water 4 cups
Gram flour 2 tbsp
Chilli powder
Turmeric powder
Sugar itsp (optional)
Salt

Method

  1. Mix all ingredients and keep seperately
  2. Heat oil, add mustard and jeera
  3. Fry gatte until golden
  4. Add the kadi mixture and simmer for five mins
  5. Serve garnished with coriander

Goes well with rice
Note- Gatte can be made into a dry curry also as with aloo.
