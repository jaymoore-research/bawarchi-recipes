Cheese Balls

Ingredients

10 Slices of pepperidge farm bread
1-2 cups mozarella cheese grated
7 to 10 green chillies
2 cloves of garlic
1/4" ginger

Method:

  1. Grind green chillies, ginger and garlic together.
  2. Soak bread in water. Squeeze out excess water, and transfer it into a clean bowl.
  3. Add 1 to 1 1/2 cups of grated mozarella cheese, and then add ginger, garlic chillie paste and salt.
  4. Make small balls and deep fry in hot oil over medium heat.

Mint chutney

Ingredients

1 cup mint leaves
1 cup coriander leaves
2 cloves of garlic
1" piece ginger
5 to 7 green chillies
2 tbsp lemon juice
1/4 tsp cumin seeds (jeera)
1 tbsp roasted bengal gram seeds (dhalia)

Method:

Grind these ingredients with 1/2 cup of water. consistency should be thick.
