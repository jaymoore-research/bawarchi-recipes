Yuca (Mogo in Africa)

Method

  1. Buy frozen Yuca and place Yuca in the microwave for 7 to 10 minutes (App. 6 to 7 pieces) till yuca is tender.
  2. Cut the yuca in small pieces (Same as French fries).
  3. Fry in oil of choice till light brown in colour.
  4. Place on paper towel so as to absorb extra oil.
  5. Sprinkle salt and pepper to taste and eat with tomato Kechup.

For Fresh Yuca:

Micro wave and remove brown skin and follow above recipe. Starch is extracted from Yuca. (Also eaten in Souh India)
