Goan Prawn/Shrimp Curry

Ingredients

Prawns/Shrimps 2 cups, peeled and deveined
Coconut 1 cup , finely grated
Tamarind size of a small lemon
Whole black pepper 5-6
Turmeric Powder 1 tsp
Chilli Powder 1/2 tsp
Green Chillies 2-3 cut into half
Corriander powder 1 tsp
Cumin powder 1tsp
Onion 1/2 cup finely chopped
salt to taste
oil
water

Method

  1. Wash prawns and marinate with salt, turmeric powder and chilli powder.
  2. Grind grated coconut with tamarind and black pepper to fine paste. Set aside
  3. In little oil (1-2 tsp) fry onions till golden brown.
  4. Add corriander and cumin powder, and add prawns.
  5. Fry for a minute and add around 1 cup of water , cut green chillies and cover.
  6. Once prawns are cooked (to check take a piece out and try to break it - if it easily does then it's cooked) add coconut paste to it.
  7. Let the curry boil, uncovered for 5-10 minutes.
  8. Adjust the consistency by adding water.
  9. Garnish with corriander leaves and serve with plain white rice

Tip: If you make this curry 2-3 hours before serving then it tastes great.
