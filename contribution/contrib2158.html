Tomato-Onion Salad

Ingredients

Tomatoes 2 (big in size )
Onion 1 (small in size)
Curd 1 tbspn
Chopped Chillies 2
Mustard Seeds 1 tspn
Oil for seasoning
Sugar 1 tspn
Salt as per one's taste
Chopped Coriander for garnishing
Powdered Peanuts (optional) 1 tspn

Method :

  1. Finely chop the tomatoes & the onion.
  2. Mix them together in a bowl.
  3. In a pan, heat some oil. When the oil is heated, add a spoonful of mustard seeds. When the mustard seeds begin to crackle, add the chopped chillies.
  4. Pour this mixture over chopped tomatoes & onions. Mix very well.
  5. To this mixture, add the mentioned amount of curd, sugar & salt as per your requirement & powder of peanuts.
  6. Garnish with chopped coriander.
