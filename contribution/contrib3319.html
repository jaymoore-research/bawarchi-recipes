Cauliflower Delight

Ingredients

1 head of a medium sized cauliflower
(break it into flowerets)
2 cups of white sauce (see bottom)
1 big tablespoon of butter
1/2cup or morzella cheese
a large pinch of cinnamon
a large pinch of paprika
1 tablespoon of dry herbs (oregano, thyme)

Method

  1. Boil cauliflower with enough water and salt till just tender.
  2. Drain and spread on a baking tray. Dot it with butter. Pour in the white sauce evenly.
  3. Spread the cheese and sprinkle with cinnamon and paprika. Keep it in a preheated oven till cheese melts and is quite bubbly. now put the herbs on top and serve hot with garlic bread.

White sauce

Fry a tablespoon of refined flour in a tablespoon of butter. Now add a cup of milk and keep stiring so that no lumps are formed. Add 1/2 a cup of grated chesse and 1/2 a cup of heavy cream and keep stiring on low flame till cheese melts and the mixture is smooth. Leave it to cool.
