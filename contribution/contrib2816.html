Chat Pata Aloo

Ingredients

Potato - 2
Lemon Juice - 2 tsp
Red Chilli Powder - 3 tsp
Hing - a pinch
Mustard seeds - 1 tsp
Jeera seeds - 1 tsp
Roasted Jeera powder - 1 tsp
Salt - to taste
Curry Leaves - Few
Chopped Corinader Leaves

Method:

  1. Wash & peel potatos. Cut lenagth wise.
  2. Apply salt to cut potatos, microwave till soft & tender
  3. In kadai, heat little oil
  4. Add Mustard & Jeera seeds.
  5. Cook for a second.
  6. Add Hing, Curry leaves
  7. Add chilli powder
  8. Stir for a second
  9. Add potatos, jeear powder, salt, lemon Juice
  10. Mix nicely , so that potatos get coated nicely
  11. Decorate with chopped green corinader leaves.

Adjust salt, lemon juice & chilli powder according to taste.
If you are not worried about calories, then potatos can be deep fried instead of microwaving.

Enjoy!!
