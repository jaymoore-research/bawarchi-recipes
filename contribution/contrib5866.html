Vazhapoo Paruppu-Usuli

Ingredients

chana dal(kadalai paruppu) - 1 cup
tur dal(thovaram paruppu) - 1/4 cup
red chillies
Ā heeng(perungayam) - a pinch
mustard seeds - 1 tsp.
chilli powder - 1 tsp.
salt - to taste
Banana flower - 1 large (for USA readers - available in Italian and MexicanĀgroceryv stores)

Ā Banana flower needs to be peeled layer by layer, and inside each tendril, there is a black stem 'kallan' which needs to be removed. Then cut the tendrils to small pieces. Pls. apply oil to your hands prior to this as banana flower tends to stick when you handle it.

Method

    Ā
  1. Soak chana dal and tur dal for an hour, and grind into a thick paste with red chillies, heeng and salt.
  2. Put this lentil paste in idli plates and cook in pressure cooker for 2 whistles.
  3. Pressure cook the banana flower also.
  4. After the lentil paste is done, cool it and with your hands/mixie powder into small pieces; Now this is called usuli.
  5. Drain the cookedĀbanana flower in a colander, fry it in oil with salt, gingerĀandĀgarnish it with mustard seeds. Keep aside.
  6. Fry the usuli in oil and add additional salt, chilli powder as required.
  7. After it turns a little brown, add the banana flower and mix well. Ā

Paruppu usuli can be prepared with beans, cabbage, broccoli and cauliflower also.
