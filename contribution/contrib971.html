Devilled Eggs

Ingredients

Eggs 4 (hard boiled)
Onions 1 (med)
Green chillies 2 (finely chopped)
Cashewnut 5-6 (finely chopped)
Raisins a few
Boiled Potatoes 5 big (mashed very well)
Coriander leaves 1 sprig (finely chopped)
Sugar 1/2 tsp
Salt as per taste
Jeera powder 1 tsp
Dhaniya powder 1 tsp
Egg 1 (for coating)
Breadcrumbs For coating
Oil for deepfrying

Method :

  1. Peel the hard boiled eggs & cut them neatly in half.
  2. Take out the yolks carefully.
  3. To the yolks add chopped onions, green chillies, chopped cashewnut, raisins, chopped coriander leaves, sugar & salt & mix well.
  4. Fill this mixture back in the egg white halves taking care not to break them.
  5. To the mashed potato add dhaniya-Jeera powder & salt.
  6. Heat kadai & put 2 tsp oil.
  7. Fry the mashed potatoes till they get little brown.
  8. Take fistful of this potato mix & cover one egg half with the stuffing taking care that the potato covering is entire.
  9. Roll in the beaten egg & coat with breadcrumbs.
  10. Prepare all the eggs in a similar manner.
  11. Deep fry till golden brown on all sides.
  12. Serve hot with lemon wedges & onion roundlets.
