Talipattu

Ingredients

Rice flour two cups
Grated coconut one cup
Onions finely chopped
Green chillies cut small
Coriander leaves chopped
Jheera one tea spoon
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Mix all the above ingredients and add water little by little and make a dough.
  2. Make equal size lumps of dough and keep aside.
  3. Grease the pan/khadai or tawa nicely with oil.
  4. Take each one of the dough lump and spread it with your four fingers so that it is not very thin or thick.
  5. Spread it to the size of the tawa etc..
  6. Make four wholes with the back of the spoon and pour oil in the wholes and also on all sides of the tawa.
  7. Cover it with a plate and keep on the medium heat.
  8. When turned slight gold colour remove with the help of the wooden spoon or the spoon used to make dosa.

Serve hot with ghee and Jheenga (Heerekai in kannada) chutney.

Jheenga (Heerekai) Chutney:

  1. Remove the skin of the heerekai and cut into thin cubes.
  2. Heat oil in a khadai. Put one tea spoon mustard after it spluttered put cut heerekai cubes, green chillies, coriander leaves, curry leaves, salt, little bit of jaggery, little but of tamarind and salt. Fry for few seconds and cook on a low fire, covered.
  3. When done remove from the fire allow it to cool and grind it in the mixie.
