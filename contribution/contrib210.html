Spicy Potatoes, Bread Pizza, Surprising Sauce

Spicy Potatoes

Ingredients:

10 Beans cut into long pieces
2 Carrots cut into long pieces
4 Onions cut into long pieces
5 Potatoes cut into long pieces
Tomato sauce , Soya bean sauce to taste
Salt , Pepper to taste
One pinch ajinomoto
Mustard seeds
Oil
Coriander leaves for garnishing

Method

  1. Fry potatoes and keep aside.
  2. After this heat 7-8 tablespoons of oil and put the mustard seeds in it.
  3. After they have started bursting, add the onions, carrots and beans.
  4. Add salt and pepper and let the vegetables soften.
  5. Then add the potatoes and the sauces.
  6. Put it into the serving bowl, garnish and serve hot.

Bread Pizza

This is a light snack made out of the normal bread bought from the market.

Ingredients

6 Slices of normal white bread
6 Tomatoes finely chopped
Tomato sauce
Salt & Pepper to taste
Garlic finely chopped
Capsicum and Onions cut into long pieces for garnishing
Grated cheese for garnishing
Butter
Oil

Method

  1. Put butter on the bread and keep aside.
  2. Then take oil in a pan and cook the garlic.
  3. After this add the tomatoes , tomato sauce, salt and pepper to it.
  4. Let it become like a puree, then spread it on the buttered slices of bread.
  5. Garnish with capsicums, onions and cheese.
  6. Cook them in the oven for 10 minutes at 250 centigrates.

Surprising Sauce

Ingredients

1 Tomato
1/2 teaspoons saunf
6-7 cloves of garlic finely chopped
1/4 cup tomato sauce
2 tablespoons red chilly sauce
2 tablespoons oil

Method

  1. Heat oil in a pan and put the saunf and garlic.
  2. After the garlic has cooked add the tomatoes and shake constantly until they become like a puree.
  3. Then add the sauces with 1/2 a cup water and mix.
  4. After the sauce has thickened, cool and serve with French fries.
