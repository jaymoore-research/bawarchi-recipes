Raagi (Nachni) Adai

Ingredients

1 cup of boiled rice
1 cup of sada rice
1 cup of raagi (nachni)
1 teaspoon of udad dal
1 teaspoon of methi
1 cup of grated coconut
15 pieces of red chilles
2 pieces of green chilles
1/2 piece of ginger
4-5 curry leaves
1 spoon of asafoetida
1/4 spoon of turmeric powder
1/2 bunch of Coriander leaves
oil for cooking
salt acc. to taste

Method :-

  1. Soak the rice, udad dal, methi and raagi ( nachni) together for about 3 to 4 hrs.
  2. While grinding add the chilles, ginger, curry leaves,turmeric powder, coconut, and grind it softly.
  3. Then add the salt and asafoetida at the end and mix well. Then cut the coriander and curry leaves and mix it with the batter.
  4. Heat the tawa on medium high and spread the batter on the tawa ( like dosa) and shallow fry with edible oil.

Serve adai hot with limboo achar or sambhar.
