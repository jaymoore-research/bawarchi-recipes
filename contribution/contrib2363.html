Curryleaves Karapodi

Ingredients

Fresh Curryleaves - 1 Cup
Coriander Seeds - 1 Cup
Black gram dal/Urad dal - 2 tsps
cummin seeds/Jeera seeds - 2 tsps
Tamarind - a small ball of the size of a lemon
Fenugreek seeds/Methi seeds - 3,4 seeds
Salt to taste
Ghee/Oil for frying - 3 tsps

Method

  1. In a small pan add 3 tsps of ghee/oil and after it gets hot add the above mentioned ingredients except salt and fry well till they turn reddish brown in colour.
  2. Remove them from heat and cool them for some time.
  3. Then add salt to the cooling items.
  4. Then grind them well into a smooth powder in a mixie.
  5. Store it in an air tight container.

This is indeed a great South Indian variety Karapodi!
