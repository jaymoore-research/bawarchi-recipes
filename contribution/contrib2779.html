Snake Gourd Cutlets

Ingredients

1 tender medium sized Snake Gourd
2-3 medium sized Potatoes
2 Green Chillies
3 cloves of Garlic
2 Shallots
A pinch of Turmeric powder
Salt to taste
Oil for frying
Rice powder

Method:

  1. Scrape the snake gourd and cut it in halves/quarters. Remove the seeds.
  2. Meanwhile boil the potatoes
  3. Heat a bit of oil in a pan. Add chopped shallots & crushed garlic. Fry till onions are soft.
  4. Mash the potatoes. Add the shallot mixture, crushed green chillies, turmeric and salt to taste.
  5. Stuff the snake gourd with the potato mixture. Cut the stuffed snake gourd in 1/2 cm thick circles. Stick the potato mixture to the inner sides of the circle if it tends to come out.
  6. Coat with rice powder and shallow fry with a bit of oil.
  7. Serve hot with Mint Chutney.
