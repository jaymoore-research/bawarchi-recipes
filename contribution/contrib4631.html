Quick Bread Pizza

Ingredients

Any type of Bread - 6 thick slices
Grated Mozarella Cheese - 6 tbsp
Pasta Sauce (tomato) - 6 tbsp
Chopped Coriander leaves - for garnishing

Method:

  1. Take each slice of bread, spread 1 tbsp. of pasta sauce and sprinkle 1 tbsp. of grated mozarella and garnish with coriander leaves.
  2. Prepare all the slices of bread in this way.
  3. Place on a baking tray and bake till the cheese melts.
  4. Serve hot.
Serves two persons.
