Fried Meat Balls in Sweet and Sour Sauce

Ingredients

1 1/2-cups Meat minced
2 tblsp Garlic minced
1 tsp Ginger paste
1 Egg beaten
1/4 tsp Salt or to taste
1 tsp Soya Sauce
1/2 tsp Ajinomoto
1/2 tsp Pepper powder
1 tsp roasted Sesame powder
1 1/2 tblsp Corn flour
Oil for deep-frying

For the sweet and sour sauce

2 tblsp Oil
2 1/2 tblsp Sugar
2 tblsp Vinegar
1/2 tsp Soya Sauce
1/4 tsp Salt to taste
1 1/2 tblsp Tomato ketchup
1/4 tsp Corn Flour
3 tblsp spring Onions chopped
1 tsp Ginger minced
1/8-cup Water

Method:

  1. Heat 2 tblsp oil, add all the sauce ingredients and boil till thick. Remove and keep aside.
  2. Combine the minced meat and all other ingredients together and mix well with the hand till soft.
  3. Make walnut size balls out of this mixture.
  4. Heat the oil, deep fry the meat balls, turning frequently for even colour. When light brown, remove from oil and drain.
  5. Before serving, reheat the oil until very hot and fry the meat balls once again until golden brown. Drain on paper and serve with the sweet and sour sauce.
