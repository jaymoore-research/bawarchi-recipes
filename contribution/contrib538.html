Spicy Tender Vegan Mix

Ingredients

Carrots - 100 gms.
French beans - 100 gms.
Cauliflower - 100 gms.
Cabbage - 100 gms.
Green Peas - 100 gms.
Tomatoes - 200 gms.
Onions - 150 gms.
Potatoes - 150 gms.
Salt - to your taste
Cilantro - a few

For the masala paste :

Coconut powder - 1/2 cup
Garam masala powder - 1 tsp.
Ginger - 2 - 3 thin slices (or to your taste)
Green chilies - 3 - 4 nos. (or to your taste)
Cardamom - 2 - 3 nos. (Pods)
Poppy seeds - 3 tsp.
Coriander seeds - 3 tsp.
Cumin seeds - 1 tsp.
Black pepper (whole) - 1 tsp.
Dry red chili - 1 - 2 nos. (or to your taste)
or Red chili powder - 1 tsp.
Garlic (optional) - 2 - 3 cloves
Cashew nuts (optional) - 1/2 cup
Water - approx. 1/2 cup

For the paste, add all the ingredients into a mixer and grind them well.

Method :

  1. Wash, cut and cook all the vegetables (except the tomatoes and onions) with a pinch of salt till tender.
  2. Slice the onions and tomatoes and keep aside them separately.
  3. Heat a little oil in a kadai and fry the onions till golden brown, then add the tomatoes and let them fry a little.
  4. Pour the paste into the kadai, add the salt and give it a good stir.
  5. Leave it on high heat for about 2 minutes. Now, add in the cooked vegetables and mix well.
  6. Let the entire mixture cook on high heat for about 3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Chop the cilantro and use as garnishing.
