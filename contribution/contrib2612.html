Kerala Kute Curry

Ingredients

1/4 Kg Kala Channa (Soaked Overnight)
1 Raw Bananna (Cut into small pieces )
1/4 Kilo Yam (Cut into small pieces )
10 leaves Karipata
1 tsp Musturd seeds
1 tsp Coconut grated (dry for frying)
3 tsp Coconut Oil
1/2 tsp Chilli Powder
1/2 tsp Pepper Powder
Salt to taste
1 tsp Jeera
1/4 cup Water
1/2 Coconut grated
1/4 tsp Turmeric Powder

Method:

  1. Put Kala Channa, Raw Bananna, Yam, in the cooker along with salt (according to taste) pepper powder, turmeric powder and chilli powder. Wait for 5 whistles.
  2. Grind (1 tsp Jeera , 1/4 cup water , 1/2 coconut grated) - till it becomes a thick paste.
  3. Fry mustard seeds in coconut oil with 10 leaves Kari pata.
  4. Put the fried ingredients in pressure cooker along with the grinded paste . Mix it well and serve with Chappati or Rice (served as side dish)
