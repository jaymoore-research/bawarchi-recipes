Chilli Chicken

Ingredients

1. Chicken 2 pounds skin peeled out and cut into pieces
2. 2 table spoon vinegar
3. 2-1/2 Table spoon Soya Sauce
4. 3 Table spoon Corn flour
5. 1 Tsp. Finely chopped ginger garlic
6. Salt per taste
7. Oil for frying
8. Green chillies 8 to 10 finely chopped

Method :

  1. Clean the chicken well!
  2. Mix all the ingredients from No.2 to 4 and add salt and marinade for 1 hr
  3. Take a pan and put some oil and fry the chicken pieces till brown in color
  4. Take a thick bottom saucepan, add oil and fry the onions till transparent, add ginger garlic paste.
  5. Then add finely cut green chillies to it and add the fried chicken, now add the marinade and keep it in slow fire till done by stirring well.

Note:

Decorate the chili chicken by salads. If you like still dark in color you can add 1 tsp. Soya sauce more. If you want more spice u add more chillies to it!
