Steamed Arvi

Ingredients

6-10 Arvi leaves (You can find it in Indian stores)
2 cups rice soaked in water for 4-5 hours
2 tblspoon coriander seeds
4-6 Red chillies
Tamarind soaked in water
Jaggery according to taste (some like it sweet)
1/2 scraped coconut
A pinch of turmeric
Salt according to taste

Method :

  1. Clean the arvi leaves in running water and remove the stem. Keep it aside.
  2. Grind together coconut, jaggery, tamarind water, red chillies, rice , coriander seeds, turmeric to form a thick paste. Make sure that you don't add much water as the paste has to be applied to leaves. Add salt according to taste to this paste.
  3. Take a arvi leaf and spread this paste on the leaf by hand and keep another leaf on this and again spread some paste. You can bind together like this 3-4 leaves.
  4. Make a roll and steam it. I usually use rice cooker for steaming.
  5. It takes atleast 30-40 mins in Rice cooker for steaming. Serve them with butter or ghee.

Variation :

  1. You can make slices of this roll when they are cooled and take a tava and sprinkle some oil and put this slices. When they start browning on the both sides, remove them and serve as it is.
  2. You can cut this rolls in to small pieces. Take a kadai and 2 tspns of oil. Add mustard seeds, when they splutter add 2 tspns urad dal, curryleaves and the cut pieces in to the kadai and fry for a while. This tastes really good.
  3. You can try this recipe with cabbage, but reduce the quantity of tamarind and jaggery.

Hope you like this recipe.
