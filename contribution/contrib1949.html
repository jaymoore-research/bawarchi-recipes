Light Bite

Ingredients

1/2 cup Semolina (sooji),
1 tsp Chopped Ginger,
3-4 Green Chillies Chopped,
2 Tomatoes chopped,
Salt to taste,
1 tsp Black Pepper Powder
Bread.

Method:

  1. Mix all the ingredients except Bread and make a smooth Paste (thick).
  2. Cut Bread slice into 4 square pieces. Cover one side of the bread with paste.
  3. Dip fry till golden brown and crisp.
  4. Serve with green chutney or Sauce as a companion of tea. Enjoy!
