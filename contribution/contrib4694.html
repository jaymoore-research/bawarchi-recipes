Chicken Katsu

Ingredients

1 cup Water
1/2 Table Spoon Soya Sauce
1/2 Table Sugar
Wisk all the above together.

Breaded Chicken (Chicken Katsu) 2 Chicken Breasts
1 cup Flour
1 cup Bread Crumbs
1/2 Table Spoon Garlic powder
1 Egg
1 cup Buttermilk
1 tsp Salt
1 tsp Baking Powder
Oil for deep frying.

Method:

  1. Take a small zip lock bag. Add 1 beaten egg, Buttermilk and garlic powder. Mix well and add the chicken breasts and shake well. Refrigerate it for 2 hrs.
  2. Take an other small zip lock bad. Add flour, salt, baking powder, bread crumbs. Shake the bag well. Now add the marinated chicken to the above bag and shake till it is properly coated with flour breadcrumb mixture. Deep fry the chicken till golden brown .

Putting together (Chicken Katsudon)

1/2 onion
1/2 cup cooked rice
1 beaten egg
1/2 cup dipping sauce
2 breaded chicken

Take a pan. Heats oil and add chopped onions and fry until it turns pink. Pour some of the dipping sauce over it. Now place one breaded chicken. Pour a little more dipping sauce. Then pour the beaten egg over the chicken and cover the pan with a lid. Cook for a while. Take hot streaming rice in a bowl . Pour the cooked chicken katsu over the rice . Pour some more sauce or just leave the sauce in a small bowl so it could be used when required. Enjoy Hot steaming Chicken Katsudon with California rolls!!
