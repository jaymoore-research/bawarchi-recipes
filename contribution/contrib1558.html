Puli Ingi

Ingredients

Ginger- 1 lb
G. chillies-1 lb
Oil- 1 cup
Mustard- 1tbs
Channa Dhal- 1tbs
Urud Dhal- 2 tbs
Methi seeds- 2tbs
Hing- 1-2 tbs (acc. your taste)
Jaggery- 4 tbs
Tamarind- 1 packet, If past 3-4 tbs
Turmeric- 1 tsp (Full)
B. Sesame- 1 1/2 tb. (Roasted & powdered coarsely without oil)
Salt- To taste.

Cooking time: 1 hour.

Instructions:

  1. Cut Ginger and G. Chilies into small pieces and keep aside.
  2. Ginger and G. Chilies should be of the same amount.
  3. Roast B. Sesame and keep aside without adding oil. Its for garnishing at last.

Method:

  1. Select a deep Kadai for the preparation of this dish. It has a tendency to splatter.
  2. Pour oil into it and once it is hot enough add Mustard, C.Dhal, U.Dhal, Methi. Let the Mustard to split.
  3. Now add Ginger and G. Chilies and fry a little.
  4. Pour the Tamarind pulp or past with water.
  5. Add Turmeric, Hing, Salt, Jaggery.
  6. All the ingredients should immerse in the Tamarind water, so do it accordingly.
  7. Keep stirring from time to time. Test for taste and add Salt and Jaggery according to your taste.
  8. This dish had a hot and sweet taste. Let it simmer down.
  9. It should be reduced nicely and look like a paste with all the moisture gone.
  10. Once this is done. Remove it from the flame and garnish with the roasted and powdered Sesame seeds.

Happy Cooking.
