Apple Pickle

Ingredients

1 green Apple (Grany Smith)
1 tsp Red Chilli Powder
1/4 tsp fenugreek powder (to be fried and then powered)
1/4 tsp Turmeric Powder
salt to taste (approx. about 1/2 tsp)

For Seasoning:
3/4 tsp Mustard
a pinch of asafoetida
Oil for seasoning.

Method:

  1. Peel off the apple skin and cut into small pieces.
  2. Add oil in a pan and season with mustard and asafoetida.
  3. Put the apple pieces and fry for 2-3 minutes. Then reduce the heat.
  4. Add turmeric powder, fenugreek powder, chilli powder, salt and mix well and fry for a minute. Then put off the heat when done.

Note:

  1. Best if used within 3 days.
  2. Apple should not be over-cooked.
  3. Don't add water while cooking apple.
