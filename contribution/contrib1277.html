Special Pooha

Ingredients

Pooha 2 cups
Mustard seeds 1 tsp
Red Chillies 7 (according to taste)
Fresh Grated Coconut 1/2 cup
Jaggery 1/2 tbsp
Tamarind juice 3 tsp
Salt

Seasoning:

Oil 2 tbsp
1/4 tsp Mustard seeds
Peanuts 1/4 cup
Curry leaves
Haldi

Method :

  1. Grind mustard seeds, coconut, redchillies & jaggery with little water
  2. Soak the pooha in water for 5 minutes & then drain & keep it aside
  3. In a kadai heat the oil add mustard seeds allow to splutter then add haldi , peanuts & curry leaves
  4. Add the tamarind juice & grinded mixture
  5. Add salt & the pooha
  6. Mix well for 3 or 4 minutes & then serve hot

Enjoy this.
