Aviyal

Vegetables: Drumstick, lentils,Red Pumpkin,Carrot, Raw Banana, French Beans, Cucumber (altogether half kg)

Method:

  1. Cut all the vegetables in thin & oblong size (half size of small finger), wash & boil with 1-2 cups of water, half tsps of saffron (haldi) powder, half tsp red chilli powder and required salt.
  2. Once it is thoroughly cooked (without much water in it), remove from the flame and keep aside.

For Grinding : Grated coconut - 1 cup, Green Chillies : 6-7 (small ones) One-Two pieces of Onions Curry Leaves - 1 string.

Method:

  1. Grind them coarsely.
  2. Mix this coconut paste with two cups of thick curd well and add to the already boiled vegetables.
  3. Cook for 1-2 mts and take out from flame.
  4. Put 2 strings of curry leaves.
  5. After 10 mts, put coconut oil and mix. A tasty Avial is ready now.

This preparation (Avial) is one of the specialties of Kerala especially at the harvest festival Onam and also any traditional functions. Try yr hand and enjoy it. Best of luck
