Potato Soup

Ingredients

5-6 medium sized potatoes,peeled and diced
1 medium sized onion, diced
1 bunch of carrots, celery, leeks, parsely
15gms butter/margarine/ghee
1 cup of milk
500ml of chicken stock
cheddar cheese(grated)

Method

  1. Saute onions and all the soup vegetables in some butter/ghee.
  2. Add the diced potatoes to it.
  3. Add the chicken stock to it and let it boil till all the vegetables are tender.
  4. With a masher or a fork, mash all the potatoes and add milk to it.
  5. Let it simmer for a good 45 mins.
  6. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot with cheddar cheese sprinkled over it.
  8. Tastes real good with some bread sticks.
