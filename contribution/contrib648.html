Mango Pulp with Peachjello.

Ingredients

2 Jello Peach flavor
1 Can Mango Pulp (Thick)
1 Cool Whip (litre)

Method:

  1. Make Jello as per the directions, let it set for overnight.
  2. In a blender put the Jello, Cool Whip, Mango Plup & just turn the blender on & off for 20 to 30 seconds.
  3. Jello will look like 'Suji', mix well by spoon.
  4. Let it set for two hours & enjoy ! Try not to put any more sugar .
