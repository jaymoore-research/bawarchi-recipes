Spicy Macaroni

Ingredients

200 gms - Macaroni
One (big) - Onion (Chopped)
One (big) - Tomato (chopped)
100 gms- Potato (diced)
1Tbsp- Oil
1tsp - Red Chilli Powder
Garam Masala - to taste
Salt - to taste

Method:

  1. Boil the Macaroni with a smear of Ghee for 2- 3 minutes. Wash it in cold water and set aside.
  2. Heat a kadai with 1tbsp of oil. Saute the onions, add tomates and saute them until they get cooked.
  3. Add the potatoes and simmer the mixture until potato gets done. Add the Red chilli powder , Garam Masala & salt.
  4. Now add the Macaroni. Cook for 2 Minutes.
  5. Serve with Tomato Sauce and Cheese.
