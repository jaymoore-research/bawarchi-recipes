Shankarpale

Ingredients

1/4 Kg. Maida,
1/3 cup Sugar
1/3 cup Water
1/3 cup Ghee
1 pinch Salt,
4 tbsp Ghee for frying.

Method

  1. Add sugar in water & mix well.
  2. Take ghee in pan and heat it.
  3. Take maida in a thali and add salt, add sugar and water solution.
  4. Add ghee in maida. Make dough. Not too loose. Keep for 1/2 an hour.
  5. Roll on and cut into small squares.
  6. Fry in ghee on low flame till it becomes pink.
  7. Keep in air tight jar. Don't keep in freeze.
