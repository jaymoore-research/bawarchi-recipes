Ingredients

cauliflower - 1 medium or small one
tomatoes - 3
ginger - 1 1/2 sp chopped
garlic - 1 1/2 sp chopped
onion -1
chilli pr - 1 1/2 sp
garam masala - 1sp
coriander seed powder - 1/2 sp
cumin seed -1 sp
cashewnuts - 10
white corn flour - 50 gm
oil for fry
salt

Method:

  1. Cut the cauliflower into medium size florettes.
  2. Boil the warer in a pan and add 1sp turmeric pr, 1sp salt and put the florettes in it .turn off the flame. Cover the pan with a lid for 5min.
  3. Make ginger, garlic paste.
  4. Grind tomatoes, onion, coriander seed powder, garam masala powder.
  5. Make a thin batter out of corn flour.add a pinch of salt, chilli powder (optional). Dip the florettes one by one and fry it in oil. Don't deep fry.
  6. Take a pan and some oil (2 or 3sp) and season it mustard seeds.
  7. Then add ginger garlic paste. Add cashewnuts (or you can fry separately and add at end). Then and grinded mixture. If you want thick means add little water or add little more. Cook till raw smell goes out. Finally add the fried florettes.

You can have it as an side dish for chappati.
