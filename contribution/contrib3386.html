Mutton Stew

Ingredients

Meat - 1.5 lbs
Eggs - 2
Flour - 6 dsp
Jeera Powder - 0.5 tsp
Cinnamon Powder - 0.5"
Cloves (powdered) - 0.5"
Pepper powder - 0.5"
Soya Bean Sauce - 3 dsp
Coriander - 3 stalks
Water - 2.5 cups
Vegetable fat (Dalda) or Ghee (clarified butter) - 4 oz.

Procedure

  1. Cut the meat into big but thin slices.
  2. Marinate the meat in a mixture of soya bean sauce and salt for about 3 hours.
  3. After blending the spices in the flour, mix one egg, the flour and the meat together well.
  4. Fry the meat well in dalda.
  5. Put the fried meat, add 2 1/2 cups of hot water and when it starts boiling, reduce the heat.
  6. Keep the lid on, till the meat is soft and well cooked.
  7. Stir now and then, so that the meat does not stick to the pan.

Serve in a dish with minced coriander leaves on top.
