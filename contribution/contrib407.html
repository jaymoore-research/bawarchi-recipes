"All Vegetarian" Omelette

Ingredients

Gram Flour 2 cups
Onion (finely chopped) 1-2
Tomato (finely chopped) 1
Green chillies (finely chopped) 4
Turmeric powder a small pinch
Red Chilli powder 3/4 teaspoon
Dry mango powder (Amchoor) - Optional 1/4 teaspoon
Cumin powder 1/2 teaspoon
Garam Masala - Optional 1/4 teaspoon
Salt to taste

Method

  1. Mix the gram flour along with the other listed ingredients and water just sufficient to make a thin (but not too watery) batter.
  2. Heat about 3-4 tablespoons of oil in a pan and pour out a small portion of the above batter in it and spread it in the pan just as one would do for the omelette.
  3. Keep it on a low flame, pour about 1/2 teaspoon of oil all around the spread batter and put a lid on the pan to cover it.
  4. After about 4-5 minutes, remove the lid and check to see if the omelette has started puffing at the bottom.
  5. Now turn around the omelette on the other side - if the pan is becoming dry, you could pour another 1/2 teaspoon of oil around the omelette again.
  6. Continue to cook on a low flame till this side is also crisp and becomes golden brown.
  7. The "All Vegetarian" Omelette is ready to be served.
  8. Make the other omelettes in the same way.
  9. Serve them hot with bread and ketchup.

Cooking Time : About 7 minutes for each omelette
Serves : 3-4
