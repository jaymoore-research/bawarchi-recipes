Panna

Ingredients

1. Two Medium Green Mangoes
2. 10 to 12 tea spoons Sugar
3. 1/4 tea spoon Salt
4. 1/2 tea spoon Black Salt
5. 1/2 tea spoon Rosted Cumin Seed
6. 3 cups Water

Method:

  1. Boil throughly the Green mangoes in Pressure Cooker with water as usually done to boil any other vegetable.
  2. Take the Pulp out of the boiled Green Mangoes and drain it with the help of Stainer. Then add 3 cups of water to it.
  3. Now after this add Salt, Black salt, Sugar, Rosted and powdered Cumin Seeds and stir well with a Spoon .
  4. Serve it cold with Ice
