Hare Chane Ki Kari (with Methi Muthia)

Ingredients

2 teacups Moong Sprouts or 2 teacups of fresh Channa
1 coconut
1 onion, chopped
1 teaspoon cornflour
oil
salt to taste

To be ground into paste

1 teacup chopped Coriander
7 Green Chillies
1/2" piece Ginger
6 cloves Garlic
2 teaspoons Lemon Juice
4 to 5 Spinach leaves

For the methi muthias

2 teacups chopped Methi
1/2 teacup Wheat Flour
a pinch of Asafoetida
1 teaspoon Sugar
1 tea spoon Chilli Powder or 1 teaspoon Green Chilli-Ginger paste
2 tablespoons hot Oil
Oil for frying

For garnish Chopped Coriander

Method:

  1. Boil the channa (or the moong sprouts).
  2. Scrape out the coconut meat, add 2 cups of water and blend in a blender. Strain the coconut milk.
  3. Heat the oil and fry the onion for one minute. Add the paste and fry for 3 to 4 minutes.
  4. Add channa or the sprouts.
  5. Mix the cornflour in the coconut milk, add salt and add to the chana mixture.
  6. Mix all the ingredients for the methi muthias including the hot oil. Shape into balls and deep fry in oil on a slow flame.
  7. Add these balls to the curry.

Serve hot decorated with fresh coriander.
