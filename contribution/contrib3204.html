Butter Chicken

Ingredients

Chicken 1 no
Butter 4 tsp
Red Chilli Powder 1 tsp
Lemon juice 1 tsp
Salt taste
Marination:

Yogurt 1 tbsp
Ginger Garlic 2 tbsp
Red Chilli Powder 1 tbsp
Lemon juice 1 tsp
Salt taste
Sause:

Garam Masala 1 tsp
Tomato Puree 2 tbsp
Sugar 1 tsp
Ginger Garlic 1 tbsp
Red Cilli Powder 1 tsp
Fresh Cream 3 tbsp
Butter 1 tbsp
Salt taste

Method:

  1. Clean chicken and cut into pieces.
  2. Apply above marination to chicken. Refrigerate for 3 hour.
  3. Put chicken in oven 10 min 200 deg .remove and keep aside
  4. Heat butter in a pan add garam masala, ginger garlic paste, chopped green chillies. Cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Add tomato puree, red chilli powder,salt,water. boil it add sugar.
  6. Add cooked chicken pieces. Simmer for 15 minutes last add cream.
