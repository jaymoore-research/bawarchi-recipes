In order to make the idli Saturday the process starts Friday morning.

Idli

Ingredients

2 cups basmati rice
1 cup urud dal

Method:

  1. Soak rice and dal together Friday morning.
  2. At or about 5.00 pm grind rice and dal to a smooth batter.
  3. Not too watery or not too doughy.
  4. Put the paste in a glass or steel bowl cover and let it sit in the oven or a warm place in your kitchen.
  5. Following morning the batter should be fermented.
  6. Add salt to the batter and steam the idili's in the idli cooker or the idli pan can be used in a steamer.

Coconut Chutney

1/2 of fresh coconut
2 clove garlic
1 small onion - chopped
1 teaspoon chopped ginger
2 tablespoon crushed pepper
1 teaspoon of Tamarind paste ( Imli )
few curry leaves
2 tablespoon oil for baghar
1 teaspoon of mustard seeds

Method:

  1. Grind together coconut, garlic, ginger, pepper, salt and tamarind.
  2. The mixture should be finely ground.
  3. The chutney can be as watery or not too watery as per your liking.
  4. Add oil to a sauce pan, add mustard onion and curry leaf, slightly brown and add the coconut chutney to the sauce pan heat it and now the chutney is ready to be served with the fresh hot idli.

Enjoy!!!!!!!!!!!
