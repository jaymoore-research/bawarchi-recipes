Doota Koora

Ingredients

Stock of banana plant (Doota in Telugu) 20cm long piece
Sesame seeds (til seeds) 1/4 cup
Curry leaves - 10 leaves
Green chillies - 5 nos
Red chillies - 5 nos
Chana dal (bengal gram) - 2 tspns
Urad dal (black gram) - 2 tspns
Mustard seeds - 1 tspns
Salt to taste, pinch of turmeric and 6 spoons of veg oil

Method

  1. Take the outer skin of stock and cut it in thin circular portions removing the fibre in between rolling it to your left index finger. Now grate these circular portions. This looks like grated coconut
  2. Add salt and turmeric to it and cook it on a low flame till done
  3. In a shallow pan pour oil and add slit green chillies red chillies mustard seeds black gram bengal gram curry leaves when the grams turn into golden brown add cooked stock and stir well
  4. Before point 3 roast sesame seeds and make powder out of it.
  5. Sprinkle this powder before removing the pan from the fire

Serve it with white rice and ghee (clarified butter)
