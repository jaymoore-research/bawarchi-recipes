Tikhat Mithachi Puri

Ingredients

1 Cup Of Plain Flour (Aata)
1 Tbsp. Oil
Salt To Taste
2 Tsp.Ajwan Seeds
1/4 Cup Coriander Leaves
1 Tbsp.Red Chilli Powder
2 Tsp.Turmeric Powder
Water For Kneading
Oil For Deep Frying

Method

  1. Mix all ingre.,except oil for deep frying.
  2. Add water little by little and make a soft dough.
  3. Cover and keep for 10 min.
  4. Break into small portions & make it like a puri.
  5. Heat oil in a frying pan & fry puri one by one (till lightly brown)
