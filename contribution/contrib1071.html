Tasty Tacso

Ingredients

6 Taco Shells (Any Brand)
1 14Oz can of Refried Beans
1 Cup of Hot Salsa or Picante Sauce
1 8Oz can of Sour Cream
1 Cup of Shredded Cheese (any kind)
1 Tomato, cored and diced
1 Small Onion shredded lengthwise
1 Cup of Shredded Lettuce

Method :

  1. In a small saucepan, heat the refried beans along with the salsa or picante sauce until the two are blended nicely and it becomes hot.
  2. Heat the taco shell upside down in the microwave oven for about 1 minute or according to the directions on the packet.
  3. Put about two-three tablespoons of refried beans in the shell first.
  4. Then follow it by shredded lettuce, onions, tomato and cheese. Top this with sour cream. Viola..the tacos are ready.

Note 1: You can interchange the contents in any order you want. If you don't like cheese and don't want to use it, the taco's still taste good.

Note 2: If you want more spicy hot, then you can add some salsa or picante sauce directly as one of the contents along with mixing it with the refried beans.

Servings: 6
