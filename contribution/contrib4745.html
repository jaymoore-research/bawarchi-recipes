Pumpkin Delight

Ingredients

1 small Pumpkin
25 ml / 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds
6 ml/ teaspoon Tumeric
6 ml/teaspoon Cardamom seeds
30 ml/ tablespoon Brown Sugar
50 ml/1/4 cup fresh chopped Ginger
30 ml/ tablespoon Ghee

Method:

  1. Cut the pumpkin up into small cubes (takes a lot of work!)
  2. Heat up the ghee in a heavy pot. Add in the ginger, fry briefly and add the pumpkin when about 1/3 cooked, add the spices and the sugar. Cover and cook on medium till finished.

Trick: To tell when the pumpkin is done: poke it with a sharp knife and lift it up. If it falls off the knife, it's cooked.
