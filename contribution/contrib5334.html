Chicken Fry

Ingredients

Chicken Legs 6 pcs.
Turmeric Powder 1/2 tsp.
Chilli Powder 2 - 3 tbsp.
Garam Masala 1/2 tbsp.
Ginger Garlic Paste 1 tbsp.
Soji (Rawa) 3 tbsp.
Vinegar 3 tbsp.
Salt as per taste
Oil to fry

Method :

  1. Wash the Chicken and make deep cuts to the pieces.
  2. Marinate the chicken with all the other ingredients except soji for about 1 to 1 1/2 hour.
  3. Before frying just roll over the pieces in soji and shallow fry it till its done.

Chicken Leg Fry is ready to eat :-

Easy haan!!
