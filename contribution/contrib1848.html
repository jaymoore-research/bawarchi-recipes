Tomato Cream Chicken

Ingredients

6 chicken leg quarters (both drumsticks and thighs).
1 small can tomato sauce or puree
1/2 pint heavy cream
2 tsp of ginger and garlic paste
2 tsp of paprika
1 tbsp of oil (corn or canola)
2 tsp mustard seeds
6 curry leaves
4 dry round red chillies (ask Indian grocery store for bor marcha)
Salt to taste
Chopped cilantro for garnish

Dhungaar (Optional but highly recommended)
1 Charcoal
1 tbsp ghee

Procedure:

  1. Marinate the chicken quarters overnight with the ginger garlic paste and paprika.
  2. Next morning place the marinated quarters in a baking dish and pour the tomato sauce and heavy cream in the chicken.
  3. Cover baking dish with a foil and let it cook in a 350 degree oven for atleast an hour.
  4. In a seperate pan add 1 tbsp of oil and add the mustard seeds, curry leaves and round red chillies to the oil. Once the ingredients start popping take them off the heat and pour it into the chicken mixture.
  5. Once again cover the baking dish and let it cook for additional 20 minutes in the oven.
  6. After the chicken is cooked do a dhungaar to make it taste even better.

Dhungaar method:

  1. Take a charcoal and heat it on the gas flame for about 10 minutes.
  2. Once part of the charcoal turns grey and red remove it from the flame add it to the chicken pan and add 1tbsp of ghee on the hot coal. Cover immediately.
  3. The smoke that generates from the coal gives a very nice smoky taste to the chicken. You can throw the coal away after your done with the dhungaar.

Garnish: With chopped cilantro
