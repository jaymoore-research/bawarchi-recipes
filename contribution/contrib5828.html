Methi Ghost

Ingredients

1 kg Goat chops or shoulder meat
1 kg onions
500 gms ripe tomatoes ( boil for 15 minutes & make paste )
250 gms fresh methi leaves ( washed & dried, no stems)
50 gms ginger
50 gms garlic
50 gms green chillies
100 gm Dhana Jeera powder
25 gms each cloves, cinnamon, peppercorns
2 tbsp oil

Method

  1. Make a coarse paste of ginger, garlic & chillies(wet mix).
  2. Grind all the dry spices to powder(dry mix).
  3. Marinate meat with the half of wet & dry mixes. Refrigerate for 1/2 day.
  4. Pressure cook meat for 20 minutes & retain all the juices.
  5. Poke a knife in the meat to check if it is semi cooked.
  6. Finely chop onions & fry over slow heat till golden brown( oil floats on top).
  7. The frying process is key to best taste & could take upto 1 hour
  8. Add methi leaves, dhana jeera powder & fry for 5 minutes
  9. Add tomato paste & meat with juices.
  10. Cook over slow heat till water separates, evaporate & finally oil floats on top. Should take 30 minutes.
  11. Add salt & lime to taste.
