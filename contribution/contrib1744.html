Pineapple Subji

Ingredients

Pineapple 1 raw (sweet and sour to taste)
Oil 3 tbsps
Salt as required.
Mustard seeds 1tsp (for seasoning)

To Grind:

Coriander seeds 1tbsp
Pepper 6-7 corns
Cumin seeds 1tsp
Red Chillies 5
Mustard seeds 1/4 tsp
Methi seeds 1/4 tsp

Method:

  1. First fry all the above (to grind) items in little oil till golden brown. Add 1/2 cup grated coconut and fry for 1 more minute. Grind together with 1 tbsp roasted gram.
  2. Peel and core pineapple and slice to small pieces or cubes.
  3. Add 1 cup water and pressure cook or microwave till cooked.
  4. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard, when it splutters add pineapple and ground mixture with salt and little jaggery if desired.
  5. Let it boil on a low flame till gravy is got.
  6. Serve with parathas.
