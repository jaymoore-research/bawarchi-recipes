Aloo Kofta

Ingredients

Medium sized potatoes - 2
Tomatoes - 2
Onion - 1
Grated garlic - 1 tablespoon
Cumin seeds -Ā 1 teaspoon
Mustard seeds - 1 teaspoon
Garam masala - 1 tablespoon
Chili powder - 1 teaspoon
Green coriander leaves - handful
Grated coconut - 2 tablespoons
Oil - 1 tablespoon
Salt - as per taste

Method

  1. Cut potatoes into small 1cm cubes.
  2. Sprinkle pinches of salt and garam masala over them and cookĀ till they become soft.
  3. Take care that they do not become a paste due to overcooking. Keep aside.
  4. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds.
  5. As the mustard seeds start spluttering, add grated onions and garlic.
  6. Fry till onions get a golden color.
  7. Add chili powder, green coriander leaves, cut tomatoes and remaining garam masala. Mix well.
  8. Fry till all the ingredients get mixed well. Remove from stove.
  9. Add grated coconut to the mixture and grind to a fine paste in a mixer, adding a little water.
  10. After grinding, pour the paste again into the pan.
  11. Once it starts boiling, add the potato pieces. Heat for 2 minutes.
  12. Ready to eat with hot chapathis or parathas.
  13. Goes well with chapathis coated with ghee.
