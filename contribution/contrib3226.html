Capsicum Rice

Ingredients

Finely chopped Capsicums - One cup
Finely chopped Onions - Half cup
Green Chillies - Two
Red Chilli powder - One tsp
Sombu - One tsp
Garam Masala powder - One tsp
Oil - To fry
Salt - To taste

Method:

  1. Pour little oil in a pan. When it becomes little hot, add sombu.
  2. Add cut onions and fry till becomes golden colour.
  3. Add cut capsicums and all other ingredients.
  4. Add little water if required. Fry till the capsicum is cooked.
  5. Mix with cooked rice.
  6. Garnish with a squeeze of lime and finely cut coriander leaves.
Millagu Koyambu

Ingredients

Tamarind - Size of a small lime
Bengalgram dhal - One tbsp
Toor dhal - Half tbsp
Coriandar seeds - Two tsp
Pepper corns - Two tsp
Aniseeds - Two tsp
Asafoetida - A pinch
Curry leaves - A small bunch
Oil - To fry
Salt - To taste
Red Chillies (Dried)- 5
Custard seeds - Half tsp

Method:

  1. Soak tamarind in a little hot water for few minutes.
  2. Fry all the ingredients other than salt till they turn golden colour.
  3. Grind the ingredients along with soaked tamarind adding required quantity of salt to a smooth paste.
  4. Mix the paste with little water .
  5. Pour a little oil in a pan, when it becomes little hot add custard seeds. Add the ground paste and fry till the paste leaves the smell of the tamarind.
  6. Add more oil if required.

This preparation will be slightly thick. If you desire to have it in a liquid form add sufficient water. It can be preserved in refrigerator for nearly a week. Just re-heat it before serving.

Usage: This can be mixed with plain cooked rice or used as a side dish for curd rice.

Medicinal values: Millagu Koyambu is one of the South Indian delicacy which is widely popular in Tamil Nadu. This has got more medicinal values. This is really good for cold and throat infection. This is considered to be a good post-delivery diet for women.
