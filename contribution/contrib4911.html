Cholley

Ingredients

1 cup safed cholley
1 tsp salt
1 pinch soda-bi-carbonate
1 tea-bag
1 bay leaf
2 glasses water
1/4 tsp chilli powder
1 tsp ground coriander
1 tsp amchur
1/2 tsp garam masala
1/2 tsp chana masala
1 pinch black pepper
3 tbsp oil

Method:

  1. Soak one cup safed cholley in water overnight or in hot water for 3 hours.
  2. In a pressure cooker add cholley, salt, soda-bi-carbonate, one tea bag, one bay leaf and 2 glasses water.
  3. Cook till tender, though not too tender.
  4. Take a sieve and remove the water.
  5. Save the water.
  6. Put cholley in a parat.
  7. Add chilli powder, ground coriander, amchur, garam masala, chana masala and black pepper.
  8. Heat a little oil in a karhai. Add cholley. Mix taking care not to break them.
  9. Add the saved water.
  10. When water has almost cried out (not completely dried out) add 3 tbsp. hot oil. Cholley are ready.
