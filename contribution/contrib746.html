Summer Thriller

Ingredients

Boiling Water 3 Cups or 24 Fl Oz
Flow Thru Tea Bags (Lipton Preferably) 3 Bags
Sugar 1/3 Cup
Chilled Cranberry Juice Cocktail 1 cup or 8 Fl Oz
Lemon Juice 1 Tablespoon

Method:

  1. Boil water.
  2. Add all Tea bags. Cover for five mins.
  3. Remove bags and add sugar.
  4. Stir and cool.
  5. In a large pitcher add the prepared tea to remaining ingredients.

Serve with ice.
May use leaf Tea if tea bags are not available.
Serves four.
