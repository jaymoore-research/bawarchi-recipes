Boli (South Indian Sweet Dish)

Ingredients

Bengalgram dal 2 cups
Sugar 2 cups
Maida 1 1/2 cups
Gingelly Oil 1/2 cup
Cardamom 8
Ghee for frying

Method:

  1. Boil the gram with plenty of water. When nicely cooked strain. Again add water, boil & drain.
  2. Add sugar & cardamom to the cooked gram & keep on low heat till the mixture becomes semi solid. Stir constantly.
  3. Remove from fire & grind to a fine paste.
  4. Add a pinch of kesari powder to the flour and with a little water, make into a dough
  5. Pour gingelly oil into the dough & knead until it becomes very soft.
  6. Make equal number of balls with the dough & the gram mixture.
  7. Take a ball of gram, cover it with maida ball & turn on to a floured board (rice flour to be used on the board) & roll out as thinly as possible.
  8. Apply a little ghee on griddle & roast the boli on it.
