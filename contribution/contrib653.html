Pav Bhaaji

Ingredients

2 Chopped onions
3 Chopped tomatoes
Flower (350 - 400 gm - 500 gm- as per your capacity to eat)
4 Potatoes
Green Matar (70 gm or to taste)
capsicum for taste
Ginger-Garlic-Green chilly- paste to taste (now-a-days readily available at food shopping centers in ethnic section) - otherwise make paste at home
Pav-Bhaji masala
Oil

Method:

  1. Chop the flower
  2. Take 3-4 potatoes and cut them to pieces and add to flower
  3. Add green matar
  4. Chop the capsicum and add to above
  5. Put this whole combination in pressure cooker and let it boil upto 4-5 whistles. Keep the pressure cooker aside to get cold.
  6. Take a pan and add oil to it. Let the oil get bit heated.
  7. Add chopped onion to the oil and fry it till it gets pink.
  8. Then add Garlic-ginger-green chilly paste to the onion. Add pinch of Haldi powder (turmeric powder) and add 1 - 1 and 1/2 table-spoon of Pav-bhaji Masala. (adjust to your taste) and fry the whole combination for a minute or two.
  9. Add chopped tomatoes (you can also use tomato puri, but taste won't be as good as natural tomato) and start frying on medium heating.
  10. Fry the the combination for about 5-7 min.
  11. Now take out the flower-matar-potato-capsicum and mash it in vessel, mash it near to the paste.
  12. Add it to above pan to the above combination of onion-tomato
  13. Add 5-6 drops of lemon juice
  14. Can also add tomato ketchup for sweet-sour taste. Take 1-1/2 table spoon of Ketchup add same amount of water and mix it with pav-bhaji.
  15. Keep the whole combination frying for 7-8 min. and your pav-bhaji ready to serve.

Try to get pav-bhaji bread (pav) if you can or its up to you, you can eat any kind of plain bread or even roti with pav-bhaji. So enjoy your wk. end night with pav-bhaji.
