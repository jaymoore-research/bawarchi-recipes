Bitter Gourd Sabzi

Ingredients

4-5 bitter Gourd medium size
Salt
Red chilli - 1tsp.
Pea nut - half catori
Coriander
Temarid - 2, 3 pod
Turmeric - 1 tsp.
Sugar - 1 tsp.
Lemon juce - 1 tsp.
Garlic - 2 , 3 pod
Ginger
Cumin seed
Oil - 3tsp.

Method

  1. Cut the bitter gourd into circular form
  2. Put some lemon juce, salt, turmeric kept aside for 15 min
  3. Then fry some peanut & grind in mixer with gralic, ginger & cumin seed
  4. Take oil in fry pan put the bittergourd by washing it add salt, tumeric, sugar, temarid water cook it at low heat for 20 min
  5. Then add red chilli & the grind peanut mixture coriander

Time required - 45 min

Servings : 3-4
