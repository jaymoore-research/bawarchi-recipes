Tandoori Chicken

Ingredients

Chicken legs -- 8 legs
Ginger garlic paste -- 2 tbhps
Red chilli pwdr -- 3-4 tsps
Turmeric pwdr -- 1 tsp
Tandoori masala pwdr -- 3-4 tsps
Thick sour yoghurt -- 1 cup
Salt to taste

For garnish:
Lemon.
If needed can be brushed with butter after roasted

Method:

  1. Marinate chicken legs with salt,yoghurt, ginger garlic paste, red chilli pwdr, turmeric and tandoori masala pwdr. Keep aside for 1 - 8 hrs. Depending on the time in hand.
  2. Then lay out the chicken legs in a greased plate and bake in an oven at 400 degree F for 10 mins, then remove and turn the pieces in their places and then bake it again for 4-5 mins. Garnish with coriander leaves.
  3. Serve hot with lemon slices and onion slices.
