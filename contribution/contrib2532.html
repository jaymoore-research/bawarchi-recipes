Kootu Curry

Ingredients

Black Chana 1/2 cup
Medley of Vegetables like Plantain, Pumpkin, Yams, White Bhopla etc- 1 cup
Grated Coconut about 1/2 cup + 2 tbsp
Coconut Oil 2tbsp
Coriander seeds 1 1/2 tbsp
Methi seeds 1/2 tsp
Red Chilli 5 to 6
Mustard seeds 1/2 tsp
Pepper powder 1/2 tsp
Turmeric powder 1/2 tsp
Jeera 1/2 tsp
Salt to taste.
Curry leaves.

Method:

  1. First, wash and soak the Chana overnight.
  2. Boil the chana in a pressure cooker.
  3. Chop the vegetables and cook it in a separate container with turmeric powder and salt. Cook it in little water by covering it with a lid so that it cooks in steam. Be sure to keep stirring and see that there is enough water.
  4. Now take about 1 tbsp coconut oil in a pan and roast the coriander seeds, Methi seeds, Red chillies and jeera. When they are golden brown in colour add the 1/2 cup of grated coconut and roast till it becomes golden brown in colour and emits a nice smell.
  5. Now blend all these in a mixer adding the pepper powder with little water so that it becomes a nice paste.
  6. Now, when the vegetables are done, add the cooked chana to this. Mix well and add the prepared paste. Cook for some time till the raw taste is gone.
  7. Take about 1 tbsp of coconut oil in a separate pan and add the mustard seeds. When they splutter, add the remaining 1 1/2 tbsp of grated coconut and fry till red in colour. Add this to the Curry. Also add some Curry leaves.
