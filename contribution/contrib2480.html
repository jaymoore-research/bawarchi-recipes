Black Eye Beans Vada (Babbarla Vada)

Ingredients

Black Eye Bean 2 cups
Green Chillies 3-4
Chopped Onion 1
Mint & Coriander Leaves
Turmeric 2 tsp
Asafoetida Powder a pinch
Salt according to your taste
Oil for frying

Method

  1. Soak the beans in water for 4 to 5 hours
  2. After it has soaked well, grind it coarsley (Don't Make a smooth paste) by adding little water.
  3. Remove it into a bowl and add chopped onion chopped chillies, chopped mint and green chillies and turmeric, asafoetida and salt
  4. Mix it well and leave it to settle for a while
  5. Meanwhile heat the oil in a wok or a pan
  6. Take a little mix at a time, make it into balls or press it like a vada and drop it into hot oil. Remove it when it turns golden brown.
  7. Serve it with Chilli Sauce or Ketchup.
