Rice Uppama

Note: All ingredient quantities are ap prox.

Ingredients

Raw rice - 250 grams
Split Red Gram - 50 grams
Coconut (scraped) - Half a cup
Green chillies - 2 to 3
Mustard seeds - Half teaspoon
Split black gram - Half teaspoon
Curry leaves - few
Red chillies - 2
Onions - 2 or 3 (finely chopped)
Coconut Oil (optional)

Method

  1. Sprinkle little water in rice and keep aside for few minutes.
  2. When it is dried grind it in a mixer into little pieces not as powder.Ā
  3. Soak Split Red Gram in water for few minutes and grind it with green chillies into paste.
  4. Heat oil add mustard seeds, split black gram and red chillies.Ā
  5. Add onions when it is fired and water twice to the quantity of rice i.e 1:2 ratio.Ā
  6. Add scraped coconut, grounded paste, salt and curry leaves.
  7. When water starts boiling add pounded rice and close the lid.
  8. After five minutes open the lid and stir again.
  9. If rice gets cooked remove from fire. Finally add coconut oil.
