Bread Kofta

Ingredients

Kofta:

4-5 slices of Bread
2 medium potatoes
Salt, Garam masala , Lemon juice

For Gravy:

2 Tomatoes or Tomato puree
1 Onion, 3-4 cloves of garlic,
2 green chillies, small piece of ginger
Salt, Turmeric powder, Chilli powder, Garam masala and some heavy cream (optional)

Method:

To Prepare Kofta, Boil the potatoes and mash it then wet the bread slice with some water and mix it with the potatoes, add salt, garam masala and little lemon juice. Then Shape them up in oval or round shape as you like. Then deep fry it in the oil and keep it aside.

For Gravy:

Heat some oil in a pan add chopped onion, garlic, green chillies and ginger in the oil, fry it until brown. Then add the cut tomato pieces or tomato puree it. Let it cook for a while. Then add all masala's i. e : Salt, Chilly powder, Turmeric powder, Garam masala etc. Then add some water may be half cup and boil the gravy for a while.

Add the Kofta to the gravy only five minutes or just before you want to eat. You can garnish this variety with corriander and grated cheese. ENJOY!
