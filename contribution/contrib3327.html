Asparagus

Ingredients

Asparagus : 1 bunch
Salt : To taste
Black Pepper : 1/2 tsp
Butter : 1 tbsp
Water : 1 medium Sized bowl
Soya Sauce : 1 1/2 tsp

Method:

  1. Wash & cut (3 pieces out of 1 stick) asparagus into diagonal shape leaving the hard bottom aside.
  2. Take bowl full of almost boiled water & put this pieces of asparagus into it for 5 mins. So as to make it crispy.
  3. Takeout asparagus from water & put under running cold water for few seconds.
  4. Take a non-stick pan & put butter , as butter melts put the asparagus in it and saute for 5 mins. Later put salt, black pepper, & soya sauce into it & saute for another 5-7 mins. Or till the veg. Is crisper....don't overcook so as to make it soft.

You can serve it with Garlic bread or keep it as an accompaniment along w/ other snacks. Asparagus is also very high in Vitamin C. I'm sure u'll love To eat it.
