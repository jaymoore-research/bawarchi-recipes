Cabbage Chutney

Ingredients

One whole cabbage (chopped and cleaned well)
1/4-1/2 cup tamarind(cleaned well)
Green chillies(3-4) or (1/2-1 tsp) red chilli powder
one cup of water
1 tsp salt
3-4 tsp Oil

for seasoning (optional but preferred):

1 tsp.oil
1/4 tsp. mustard seeds
1/4 tsp. cumin seeds
1 pinch asafoetida

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a deep bowl and fry the chopped cabbage for 10-15 min. until the color turns golden brown.
  2. Allow it to cool a bit.
  3. Add the tamarind to this along with salt, water and chopped green chillies.
  4. Grind all of these in a mixie or a stone grinder.
  5. Grind till smooth.
  6. Heat oil and add the seasoning ingredients. Pour it over the chutney.
  7. Mix gently.
  8. Serve fresh with dosas, idlis, upma, utthapams, vadas, etc.
