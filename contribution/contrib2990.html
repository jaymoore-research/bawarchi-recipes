Scrumptious Gobhi (Cauliflower) Ka Kheer

Ingredients

1. 1 cup chopped cauliflower
2. 10-12 cups whole milk
3. 1 cup sugar or according to your taste
4. 1 tsp. cardamom powder
5. 10 unsalted peeled almonds
6. 2 tbsp. of ghee

Method

  1. Fry the chopped cauliflower in ghee, with steam till its golden brown. Keep it aside.
  2. Boil milk. Once the milk starts boiling add the fried cauliflower.
  3. Keep stirring for a while. Allow it boil for 15 mints. Then add sugar, cardamom powder and the almonds.
  4. Simmer and leave it for a few minutes until the mixture thickens.
  5. Let it cool. Keep it in the fridge. Chill it and serve as a dessert.
