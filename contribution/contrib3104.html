American salad in Indian Style

Ingredients

(1) Lettuce
(2) Tomatoes
(3) Green Pepper
(4) Carrots
(5) Mushrooms
(6) Croutons-Bread Crumbs
(7) Sunflower seeds
(8) Boiled beans-kidney beans
(9) Golbanzo Beans
(10)Crushed Red Chilly powder
(11) Black Pepper
(12) Dressings:-French, Italian, Thousand Island (Yo can use any dressing you would like to have on your salad.
(13) A few drops of Lemon

Method:

  1. Take a salad bowl or a plate.
  2. Cut lettuce and try to keep the lettuce in the middle of the plate and in equal pieces.
  3. Now cut Green peppers and mushrooms and decorate them in circle (plate).
  4. After that cut tomatoes and carrots and try to mix them in the lettuce.
  5. After all this, put some Golbanzo beans, kidney beans, Croutons and sunflower seeds at the top of the salad and try to spread them across.
  6. At last put the salad dressing according to your taste and sprinkle red chilli and black pepper at the top of whole salad. You can put some green chillies and chopped cilantro. Enjoy it.

Note: Ingredients can be used as per your tatste.
