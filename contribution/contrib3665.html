Lemon Rice

Ingredients

Lime 1
Rice 1/2 cup
Turmeric powder 1/2 tsp

For seasoning:

Curry leaves, Mustard, Urad dhal, Cashewnut, Peanut, Channa dhal Asafoetida, Ginger, Garlic

Method:

  1. Heat oil in a vanali and do all the seasoning. Once it is done, keep them aside.
  2. You can mash the ginger and put it or grind ginger and garlic together and fry them in the oil.
  3. Take the cooked rice and spread it on a plate. Let it cool. Spread some salt and turmeric powder. Pour the lime juice and add the seasonings and mix well. If you want, add coriander leaves.
  4. Finally, grind coconut and roasted gram dhal (pottukadalai) well and mix them with rice. It gives some different taste. Try and enjoy.
