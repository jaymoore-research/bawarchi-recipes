Pineapple Kesaribath

Ingredients

Sooji (rave) 1 cup
Sugar 1 1/4 cup
Ghee 1/2 cup
Milk 1/2 cup
Water 1 cup
Cardamom 1 (powdered)
Clove 1 (powdered)
Cashews 1 tbsp
Raisins 1 tbsp
Pineapple grated 1/2 cup
Saffron 1 pinch + 2 tbsp milk

Method:

  1. Heat 3 tbsp ghee in a shallow frying pan add cashews and raisins fry till golden, remove from pan and keep aside.
  2. Add sooji to the remaining ghee and fry on a low heat till light brown and keep aside.
  3. Bring milk and water to rolling boil add saffron dissolved in milk and add 2 tbsp ghee(ghee will prevent burning).
  4. Then add sooji,cardamom,clove,cashews and raisins slowly stirring the mixture to prevent the formation of lumps.
  5. Keep stirring to break any lumps.
  6. Reduce heat.
  7. Cook 2-3 mins.
  8. When all the water has been absorbed, add sugar, remaining ghee, grated pineapple.
  9. Stir well till traces of ghee began to show at the sides.
  10. Turn off the heat and keep it covered for few minutes.

Serve warm or cold.
