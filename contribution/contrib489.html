Moong dal chutney

Ingredients

1 cup moong dal soaked for about one hour

For the seasoning:
Oil 1 tbsp
Mustard seeds 1/2 tbsp
Cummin seeds 1/2 tbsp
Red chillies 7 to 820
Asafoetida 1/2 teaspoon
Tamarind 2 inches
Corriander leaves
Salt to taste.

Method:

  1. In a karahi heat oil,add mustard seeds allow it to crackle
  2. Add cummin seeds, hing & red chillies.Let it cool.
  3. Put the above seasoning in the mixer with the soaked moong dal, tamarind, corriander leaves & salt.
  4. Grind it to a smooth paste.
  5. It tastes great with chappatis rice & pooris.
  6. It stays fresh for 2-3 days if refrigerated.
