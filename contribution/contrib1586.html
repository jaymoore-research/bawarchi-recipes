Sago Vada

Ingredients

1/4 th kg.Sago (Saboodana)
2 medium size Aloo
4-5 tbspns. ground Peanut Powder
1 bunch Mint leaves {or a pod of Ginger (optional)}
1 bunch Coriander leaves
4-5 Green Chillies
1/2 tspn. Turmeric Powder.
Salt as per your taste
Oil for frying.

Method:

  1. Soak sago (saboodana) for 10-15 mts. in water and then drain the water and allow the sago to become soft like sponge.
  2. Boil the aloo and mash them.
  3. Grind green chillis, coriander leaves and mint leaves (or ginger as per your option) into paste.
  4. When the sago becomes soft like sponge, put the mashed aloo, green chilli-coriander-mint leaves paste, ground peanut powder, salt as per your taste and the turmeric powder in it and mix well and make it a dough.
  5. Now convert the dough into small sized balls, press them on your palm like patties and deep fry in the oil.
  6. Your crispy sago vada are ready.

Serve them hot with Tomato Sauce. It would be a good tea time snack.
