Banana Flower Parupu Usili

Ingredients

Banana Flower : 1 (Medium size)
Channa Daal : 2 (tblsp)
Tuar Daal : 2 (tblsp)
Mustard seed : 1/4 tea spoons
Urad Daal : 1/4 tea spoons
Green Chilli : 3
Asafoetida : Very little
Salt : Accordingly.

Method:

  1. Soak Channa Daal and Tuar Daal in Hot water for one hour.
  2. Boil the banana flower sprouts.
  3. Take the soaked daal and grind it along with Chilli, little asafoetida and the required amount of salt (make it to fine paste).
  4. Take a pan, add oil, put mustard seeds and urad daal. Once the mustard seeds starts spluttering add the ground paste and fry it in the pan till the daal paste separates into solid pieces. Add the boiled banana flower sprouts and fry for 5 minutes.

Its mouth watering. Serve it hot with Saambar, Rasam or Vathal Kozhabu. Bye and have a mouth watering lunch.
