Muthiya

Ingredients

3 cups grated doodhi (bottle gourd/lauki)
1 cup semolina (suji)
1 cup wheat flour
1 cup besan (chick pea flour)
3 tea spoon ginger chilli paste
2 lime juice
Baking powder
Pinch of haldi (turmeric) & asfoetida(hing)
Salt
2 tea spoon oil

PROCEDURE

  1. Keep water in a steaming potĀto boil.
  2. While water is boiling, start mixing all the ingredients coarsely except lime juice & baking powder.
  3. Add 1 teaspoon baking soda over it add lime juice.
  4. Mix well to form stiff dough.
  5. On steaming rack keep the dough like rolls.
  6. Arrange all the rolls next to each other. even if it touches each other that is ok.
  7. Steam for 40- 50 mins till it is cooked.
  8. Open the lid, allow to cool & cut each roll horizontally into slices like bread.
  9. Serve hot with chhash (buttermilk) & chutney. OR
  10. Keep oil in pan & add mustard seeds allow to splutter add sesame seeds & hing. & fry on a slow flame till little crispy. Garnish with chopped coriander. Serve hot with chhash( buttermilk) & chutney.
