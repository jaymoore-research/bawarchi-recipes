Makai Frankie

Ingredients

For Pattice:

Corn Kernels - 1 cup
Boiled Potatoes - 2 nos
Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tbsp
Green Chillies -1 tsp
Red chilli powder - 1 tbsp
Haldi - 1 tsp
Jeera powder - 1 tsp
Salt to taste

For Frankie:

Maida - 1 cup
Water
Oil
Salt to taste

For chutney:

Mint leaves - 1/4 cup
Cilantro - 1 cup
2-3 chillies .
1/4 tsp Jeera
Salt as per taste

Method For pattice:

  1. Boil corn kernels (you can use fresh corn too, boil it and remove kernels)
  2. Mash the kernels very well some kernels remain its ok.
  3. Add boiled potatoes, paste and spices .
  4. Take the mixture and make small pattice out of it and shallow fry them and keep aside.

Method For frankie:

  1. Make a dough of maida salt and water as of chapati dough.
  2. Roll into small chapatties
  3. Roast them on a tava on both sides u can prepare them ahead of time and preserve.
  4. You can also use tortillas if u want.

Method for Chutney:

Prepare mint chutney by mixing chutney ingredients and grinding it.

To make makai frankie:

  1. Take a roti and spread mint chutney on it .
  2. Place 2 pattice in between mash a little and some chopped onion (if u like)
  3. Roll the roti by joining both ends and shallow fry them till its crisp on both sides .
  4. While serving cut the frankie in between and serve with chutney.
  5. You can also serve it with tamarind chutney and u can eat the pattice alone also.
