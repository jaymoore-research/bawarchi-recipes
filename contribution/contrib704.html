Stuffed Toasted Sandwiches

Ingredients

2 large tomatoes cut into small pieces
1/2 bunch of coriander leaves (cilantro)
2-3 green chilis (jalapeno)
Salt to taste.

Method

  1. Mix all the above.
  2. Switch on the sandwich toaster- butter both sides of bread slices
  3. Place a slice on the toaster and fill it with the above mix cover with another slice and cook till done.
  4. Serve hot with ketchup or chili sauce whatever you wish.
  5. The tomato mixture can be replaced by leftover potato curry or any curry of your choice. Hope everyone enjoys it.
