Doodhi Dal

Ingredients

1 medium sized Bottle Gourd (doodhi)
1/4 cup Tur Dal
1 large Tomato
Salt to taste
2 Green Chillies
1 medium sized Ginger
1 tsp Ghee (saturated butter)
1/4 tsp Mustard seeds
1/4 tsp Jeera

Method:

  1. Boil tur dal in a pressure cooker with ample amount of water.
  2. Cut doodhi and boil separately in a vessel.
  3. Puree the tomato along with chillies and ginger and add it to the boiled doodhi.
  4. Whip the boiled tur dal and add to the doodhi. Add salt and turmeric powder to it.
  5. Season with ghee, mustard seeds and jeera.
