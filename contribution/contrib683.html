Spicy Phoha

Ingredients

1 cup poha
1 small onion chopped
1/4 cup Fresh coriander chopped
1 medium potatoe cut into 1"cubes
1 tbs oil for seasoning
salt to taste
1/4 tsp Chilli powder
1 tsp mustard seeds
1 tsp coriander seeds powdered
1 tsp urad dal
2 green chillies
pinch of turmeric

Method:

  1. Wash poha in cold water drain and keep aside.
  2. Heat a pan on medium add oil, urad dal, mustard seeds.
  3. When mustard seeds splash put onions once onions are soft add salt, turmeric, coriander powder, chilli powder and potatoes allow it to cook.
  4. When cooked add poha.
  5. Mix well cook for 2 min on medium.
  6. Sprinkle coriander leaves.

Serve hot with a piece of fresh lemon.
