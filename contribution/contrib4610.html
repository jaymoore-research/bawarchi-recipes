Apple Kheer

Ingredients

1 litre Milk (fulll fat or fat free)
4 tbsp Sugar
4 medium Apples, peeled
1/4 tsp Cardamom powder
4 Almonds, sliced
5 Pistas

Method:

  1. Put the milk to boil in a heavy based pan. Add the sugar and cook gently until the mixture is reduced by half.
  2. Grate the apples and add to the milk. After boiled remove from the heat at once.
  3. Sprinkle cardamom, pistas and almonds on top. Serve hot or cold.

Serve: 5
Time to Prepare: 5 minutes
