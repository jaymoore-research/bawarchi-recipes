Kerala Uppuma

Ingredients

rava-3cups
small onions-4
green chillies-3
chopped ginger-1 teaspoon
curry leaves-as required
oil-3spoon

Method:

  1. Take a pan and fry the rava until it will be fried well.
  2. Remove the rava from the pan.
  3. Add oil in the same pan and after when the oil becomes hot put the mustard seeds to splutter.
  4. After that add 3 red chilles and then add onions,ginger,greenchilles and curry leaves and stir them to get lightly fried.
  5. After that add 3 cups of water and sufficient salt into the previous fried items.
  6. Allow the water to boil well.the water should start bubbling.
  7. After the water bubbles up add the fried rava to the water and immediately start stiring.continue your stiring until it comes into the final uppuma stage.
  8. Serve it with hot with bananas.
