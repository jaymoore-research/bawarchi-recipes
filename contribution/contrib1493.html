Semya Bhath

Ingredients

Bambino Vermicelli : 1/4 kg
Onions : 2 (Chop them finely)
Tomatoes : 2
Green Chillies : 4
Ginger, Garlic paste : 1 tsp
Coriander, Mint leaves - small
Curd : 2 tsp
Lemon Juice : 1 tsp
Turmeric Powder : 1/4 tsp
Red chilli powder : 1 1/4 Tsp
Pepper powder : 1/2 tsp
Garam masala powder: 1/2 tsp
Oil
Salt
Mustard seeds : 1/2 tsp
Urad dhal : 1/2 tsp (broken white)
Bengal gram dhal: 1/2tsp
Curry leaves : few

Method :

  1. In a heavy vessel pour three glasses of water and allow it to boil.
  2. Put Vermicelli into the boiling water and allow it to cook for 3 mts and separate the excess water from it. Keep it aside.
  3. Heat oil add mustard seeds, Urad and Bengal gram dhal, curry leaves and fry.
  4. Add Onions and fry till it becomes soft.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes, Chopped Green Chillies, Coriander and mint leaves and Ginger garlic paste and fry till good smell comes off.
  6. Add Turmeric powder, Red chilli powder, pepper powder and Garam masala powder.
  7. Then add Curd and lemon juice and cook in a medium flame.
  8. Add Cooked Vermicelli at the end and mix it well.
