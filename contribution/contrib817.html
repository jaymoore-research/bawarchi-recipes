Batata Song

Ingredients

2 med size potatoes boiled, peeled & cubed
lemon size tamarind ball (Soake in warm water for 10-15 min & take juice)
2 med size onions chopped finely
3-4 tsp chilli powder (depending on spice u want)
1 tsp mustard
few curry leaves
2 tbsp oil
coriander leaves to garnish

Method :

  1. Heat oil in a pan & season with mustard & curry leaves.
  2. When mustard starts to splutter, add chopped onions & fry till golden brown.
  3. When it's done add chilli powder, fry till the aroma of chilli powder comes.
  4. Now add juice of tamarind & little water.
  5. When this mix starts to boil, add cubed potatoes & salt.
  6. Let the gravy boils nicely, take it from stove & sprinkle some coriander leaves.

Enjoy with Idli, dosa, roti.
Same way we can do with prawns (shrimps) also, tastes great!
