Spinach Rice

Ingredients

Cooked riceĀ- 2 cups
Frozen spinachĀ- 1 small pack
Onion - 1
Green chilli - 2
Tamarind paste - 1 tsp
Mustard seeds -1 tsp
Fenugreek seeds - 1 tsp
asafoetida -1 pinch
oi -1tbs
Curry leaves - few
Salt - to taste

Method

Ā

  1. In aĀpressure cookerĀ , cook spinachĀ to a soft texture.
  2. In a pan dry fry the mustard and fenugreek seeds, and powder it.
  3. Now add oil to the pan fry onion curry leaves asafoetida powder.
  4. Now grind the spinach to a fine paste pour it into the onion keep it in simmer for about 5 min add salt and powdered spice, and tamarind paste
  5. Now add the cooked rice and mix well.
  6. We can eat this rice with any yogurt paccadi.
