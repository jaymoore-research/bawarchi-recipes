Ingredients

For this dish vegetables such as chaqote squash, cucumber, white pumpkin, Greens or Okra can be used. But Okra should be fried in the oil (not deep fry) to get rid of the stickiness.

To grind :
Chana dal soaked for atleast an hour in water - 3 Tbsp
Cilantro - 3-4 strands washed
Green chillies or Red chillies - 3 to 4 depending on spicyness preferred
Ginger - 1/2 inch piece
Jeera seeds - 1 Tsp
Cumin seeds - 1/2 Tsp
Fresh grated coconut - 1/4 cup

For the tadka :
Mustard seeds - 1/2 Tsp
Curry leaves - 1 strand
Oil - 1 tsp
A pinch of Asafoetida

Buttermilk - 1 cup
Salt to taste

Method :

  1. Peel the skin of the veggie u're using expcept for Okra or Greens and cut them into small cubes.
  2. Boil the vegetable till soft. Place a vessel on the cooktop with the boiled vegetable, add salt and after a minute add the ground masala and heat on a medium flame.
  3. When the raw smell of the green chillies is gone,put the vessel off the cooktop and add Buttermilk to the vessel and mix thoroughly.
  4. Heat oil in a pan,add Oil. When hot, add mustard seeds, allow to splutter, add curry leaves and a pinch of Asafoetida.
