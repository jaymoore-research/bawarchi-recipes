Spicy Fish Curry

Ingredients

1 lb Fish (King, butter or trout)
Tamarind - the size of one lemon
1/2 Coconut
1 Large Onion
2 Medium sized Tomatoes (use sour tomatoes for better taste)
10-15 Methi seeds
A few curry leaves
5-6 split Green Chillis
1 tsp. Ginger Garlic Paste
2 tblsp. Chilli Powder
1 tsp. Turmeric Powder
1 tblsp. Dhania Powder
Coriander leaves
4-5 tblsp. cooking Oil
Salt to taste

Method :

  1. Soak tamrind in warm water and squeeze out juice. Add salt, a pinch of turmeric powder and soak the fish pieces.
  2. Grind together coconut, onion and tomatoes to a fine paste.
  3. Heat oil, add curry leaves and methi seeds. Add ginger garlic paste and split green chillis. Fry.
  4. Add ground masala and spices and fry well till oil rises to the top. When boiling, slowly add fish pieces and the tamrind water. Cover and cook on simmer.
  5. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with parboiled rice.

PS : If the curry is too thick add warm water while boiling.
