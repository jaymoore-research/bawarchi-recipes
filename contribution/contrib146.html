Vegetable Khichdi

Ingredients

1 cup rice
a little over 1/2 cup yellow moong dal
1 capsicum (green pepper)
1 potato
few florettes of cauliflowere (big pieces)
1 carrot (sliced long, about 1")
7-8 beans (in 1" pieces)
3/4 cup cabbage (cut in medium pieces)
some peas
1" ginger (crushed)
1 teaspoon cummin seeds
1 tablespoon ghee
2 green chillies (in 1/2" pieces, slitted)
Salt to taste

Procedure:

  1. Cut all the vegetables a little big.
  2. Soak the rice & dal together in water.
  3. In a kadai, heat the ghee and add cummin seeds to it.
  4. When the seeds splutter add the slitted green chillies.
  5. Add first the green pepper. Fry for a minute and then add all the vegetables and fry it for about 5 minutes.
  6. Drain the water from the rice & dal and add it to the vegetables. Mix everything well and add salt.
  7. Just crush the ginger and add it to the mixture.
  8. Add about 3 1/2 cups of water. And close the kadai tightly so that no steam escapes.
  9. Check back in 10 to 15 minutes and add some more water if the rice, dal or vegetable looks a little uncooked. Cook till done and your vegetable khichadi is ready.

Note: If you like ginger then you can crush some more and put in. This tastes excellent with tomato chutney. I have a quick recipe for it.

Tomato Chutney

Ingredients:

1 can crushed tomatoes
a little sambhar powder or red chilli powder
a pinch of hing
salt to taste
1 teaspoon oil
1 teaspoon mustard seeds

Method

  1. Add the oil in a kadai and add the mustard seeds.
  2. When the seeds splutter add hing. Then add the crushed tomatoes.
  3. Add either smbhar powder or chilli powder and salt to taste.
  4. Mix everything and close the kadai with a lid for about 2 to 3 minutes.
  5. The chutney will start bubbling. Just wait till the raw smell of the sambhar or chilli powder goes off.

Note : This chutney tastes very good with sambhar powder but can be made with the chilli powder too but the taste might differ a little. The chutney takes about 5 to 7 minutes to make. So you have your khichadi and chutney ready in totally 20 to 30 minutes. Hope you enjoy this dish.
