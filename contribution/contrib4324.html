Stuffed Capsicum Chicken Seasoning

Ingredients

250 gm Prawns, peeled & minced
200 gm minced Chicken Meat
3 medium red, green or yellow Capsicums, quartered
2 thin slices Ginger

Seasoning A (Blend Together)

1 tsp Maggi Seasoning
1/4 tsp ground White Pepper
1 tsp Sesame Oil
2 tsps Cornflour

Seasoning B (Blend Together)

1 tbsp Maggi Seasoning
1 tbsp Maggi Concentrated Chicken Stock
250 ml Water
2 tbsps Cornflour

Method:

  1. Mix minced prawns and chicken meat with Seasoning A.
  2. Stuff capsicum with the marinated mixture.
  3. Heat oil in a pan fry the stuffed capsicum until cooked. Place on a serving dish.
  4. With the remaining oil, saute the ginger slices until aromatic.
  5. Add in Seasoning B bring to boil and simmer until the sauce thickens.
  6. Pour the sauce over the stuffed capsicum and serve at once.

Preparation Time: 15 minutes
Cooking Time: 10 minutes
Serves 5
