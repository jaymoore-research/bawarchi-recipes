Maida Kozhukattai

Ingredients

For the stuffing:

Scrapped Coconut - 2 cups
Powdered Jaggery - 1 cup
Cardamom powder - 1 tsp
Ghee - 1 tsp

For the dough:

Maida flour - 1 tumbler
Oil - few tsps (to knead the dough)
Oil - to deep fry

Method:

  1. Take a hard bottom pan and heat the ghee in it.
  2. Then pour little quantity of water in the pan, and when water is also heated add the powdered jaggery to the pan.
  3. When the jaggery melts in the water, immediately add the scraped coconut and keep stirring constantly keeping gas low. Do not allow jaggery to turn into a syrup before adding the scraped coconut. It is sufficient if the jaggery just melts in the water before adding coconut.
  4. When the entire mixture turns into a solid without sticking to the pan switch off gas and add cardamom powder.
  5. Knead Maida into a soft, stretchy dough with water and oil.
  6. Roll the dough into uniform balls, keep a teaspoon of the stuffing inside each ball and deep fry the balls in oil till they turn brown.

Bye! Enjoy cooking!
