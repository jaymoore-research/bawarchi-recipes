Tandoori Chicken

Ingredients

4 or 6 Leg Quarters with bones, with or without skin. As a rule I remove the skin after the chicken has cooked so as to add a little of the fat and all of the flavor of the skin.
2 Tablespoons Chili Paste
3 Tablespoons Patak Tandoori Paste
2 Tablespoons Patak Curry Paste
2 Tablespoons Garam Masala
3 Tablespoons Red Chilli Powder
Fresh, ground rosemary
Salt to taste

Method :

  1. Lime juice : Mix the pastes and powders with a 3 : 1 lime juice/water mixture to give it the consistency of a thick buttermilk and add the chicken. Marinate from 6 hours to overnight in the fridge.
  2. Preheat grill to approx. 450.
  3. Place chicken on high rack in grill for 17-20 minutes flipping once half way through.
  4. The skin of the chicken will burn slightly, but the meat will be perfect and the skin is now discarded.
