Thai Fried Rice

Ingredients

Rice 2 cups
Onion 1 medium size
tomato 1 medium size
Garlic 7 to 8 cloves finely chopped
Soya sauce 1 tbsp
Vinegar 2 teaspoon
Sugar 1 teaspoon
Red chilly pwder 1 teaspoon
Oil 2 tablespoon
Salt as required
2 cups mixed vegetables like carrot, beans, cauliflower & cabbage. (These vegetables should be cut in large pieces)
One small cucumber cut into 2 inch pieces
1/2 size lemon

Method:

The essence of Thai cuisine is to cook on a very high flame so that the nutrients of the ingredients is not lost.

  1. Wash & cook rice in such a way that one grain should not stick to other.
  2. Place a karahi with oil on a high flame
  3. Add finely chopped garlic ,stir fry.
  4. Add onions ,fry for a minute.
  5. Add tomato.
  6. Add the mixed vegetables & salt. Keep on stirring till the vegetables are done.
  7. Add rice,soyasauce ,vinegar,sugar & chillipowder.

Apart from cooking rice, this recipe takes just 10 minutes to be ready. While serving garnish it with cucumber slices & lemon wedges.

Happy cooking !
