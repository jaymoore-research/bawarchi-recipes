Salsa Rice

Ingredients

1 cup Uncooked rice
Salsa
2 tbsp Ghee/Oil/Butter (whatever is handy)
1/2 tsp Cumin seeds
Salt

Method:

  1. Wash and drain rice
  2. Heat the ghee/oil/butter
  3. When it is hot enough, add the cumin seeds. Allow them to splutter
  4. Add the rice and saute for some time.
  5. Add just enough salsa to coat the rice.
  6. Add salt to taste
  7. Add the same amount of water you would normally use to cook rice
  8. Cook in pressure cooker.

Eat hot. Tastes great with veggie dips or yogurt.
