Ingredients

(1) Red kidney beans----1 cup
(2) big onion-1
(3) tomato{big} -1
(4) garlic-4/5 palu
(5) ginger-
(6) Somph -1 tsp
(7) Cloves-1-2
(8) Cinnamon -1"
(9) Red chilly pwd -1 tsp

Method:

  1. First soak red kidney beans over night, and cook that with little salt. Cut tomato & onion
  2. Take 1/2 onion along with other ingredients fry them in a pan with 1 tsp of oil for 3 min,add red chilly pwd also to this.
  3. Allow them to cool and grind them to past
  4. In a pan add 1 tbsp of oil, fry cumin then add the grinded paste for some time now add cooked red kidney beans and salt (to taste) cook them for 10 or 15 min in sim
So the raw smell of onion will go and will get a good taste

Note:for rich taste you can add some cream before removing from stove
