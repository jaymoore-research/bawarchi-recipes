Ginger pickle

Ingredients

Ginger as per your requirement (preferably a big chunk)
Garlic 10 flakes
Salt as per requirement
Turmeric 1 tbs
Jaggery powdered 10 tbs
Chilli Powder/Red chillies 7 or 8
Tamarind lemon size
Oil

Seasoning:

Channa dal 2 tbs
Urad dal 2 tbs
Mustard seeds 1tbs

Method:

  1. Peel and grate the ginger, and also grate jaggery.
  2. Add water to tamarind and boil it till tamarind becomes soft.
  3. Take a kadai add 2 tbs oil and fry the grated ginger for few minutes or till it changes colour to light brown.
  4. Then grind together garlic, ginger, turmeric, jaggery, chilli powder, salt and tamarind pulp into a fine thick paste don't add water, grind it with tamarind pulp.
  5. Take a kadai heat 10 tbs oil to it add mustard seeds, channadal, urad dal, when the mustard seeds splutter add the ground paste to it and fry for a few seconds.

This pickle goes well with Idlis, Dosas, and also with rice.
Shelf life : 2-3 weeks
