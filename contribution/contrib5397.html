Pineapple Delight

Ingredients
4 tbsp pineapple syrup
3/4th glass water
Some rocksalt

Method:
Mix the syrup, water, and a pinchfull of rocksalti a tall glass on the empty part of the glass, apply rocksalt put 2-3 ice cubes garnish with a pine apple slice or a cherry

Note:instead of pineapple u can also use strawberry, blackcurrent and rose syrups Strawberry Soda:
4 tbsp strawberry syrup
3/4 th glass soda
Rocksalt

Method:
The procedure is the same as the above garnish with a lemon slice

Note:variations can be done with blackcurrent or by mixing strawberry and blackcurrent
