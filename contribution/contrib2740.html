Sev Shak

Ingredients

1 cup liquidised tomatoes
Red chillie to taste
1/2 tsp turmetic
1 tsp sugar
6 curry leaves
1tsp garlic,ginger,green chillies (paste)
1 cup of gram flour
1 tsp of mustard seeds
Pinch of asofedita (Hing)
1/2 tsp of fenugreek seeds
4 tbl of oil
Couple of sprigs of corriander(fresh)
Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Heat the oil in a pan.Add the mustard seeds, fenugreek seed and hing.
  2. Stir in the ginger, garlic chillie paste together with the tumeric powder.
  3. Add curry leaves and then the tomatoes.
  4. Add salt to taste and the sugar too.
  5. Let it cook till the oil seeps through. Run it in the boiling
  6. Then add 2 cups of boiling water.
  7. Make a dough of the gram flour (season to taste)and insert in a sev machine and run it in the boiling tomatoe 'soup' Cover and cook for 10 mins. Garnish with corriander. Seema
